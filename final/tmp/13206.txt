QUESTION
How can players best utilize the Champion class's unique combination of equipment and stats in combat situations?

ANSWER
Players can utilize the Champion class's equipment and stats by burning foes from a distance with Sacred Seal, using Axe for melee combat, and leveraging high Strength, Endurance, and Faith for ferocious fighting.
