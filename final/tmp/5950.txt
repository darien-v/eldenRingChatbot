\n\n Sorcery of the Crozier \n\n        Channel magic into the glintstone to activate an ancient sorcery, producing floating magic projectiles that chase enemies automatically. \n\nSorcery of the Crozier is a Skill in Elden Ring. Sorcery of the Crozier is a Unique Skill for the Colossal Weapon Watchdog's Staff.\n\nHow to get Sorcery of the Crozier in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on the Watchdog's Staff.\n\nElden Ring Sorcery of the Crozier Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 15\nDeals 12 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nGenerates a ring of projectiles that hover in place before homing in on the target you've locked on to.\n\nProjectile tracking may act oddly against bosses with large hitboxes or multiple lock-on points.\n\nProjectile damage is now enhanced by Warrior Jar Shard, Shard of Alexander (Fixed in 1.07 patch notes)\nProjectiles scale with weapon upgrade level only, not with any stats.\n\nSorcery Sentinel Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring
