\n\n Lookout Stones \n\nLocation\n Found near Evergaols, cemeteries, and specific points of interest. \n\nDrops\n 23 - 499 Runes Ruin FragmentSanctuary StoneVolcanic Stone\n\nLookout Stones are strange creatures composed of various sphere-shaped boulders and an uncanny eye that glows when standing up or changing form. They often take the form of large worms made of stone or dull groups of seemingly lifeless rocks.\n\nLocations\n\nMost commonly found around Evergaols in all regions. Almost all Evergaols are surrounded by large numbers of Lookout Stones.\nOccasionally found around other points of interest, such as groups of coffins.\nLarge amounts of Lookout Stones are found in Caelid, in canyons near the Deep Siofra Well. Those that are curled in a pile will open their eyes and explode.\n\nDrops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 23 - 499\n\nRuin Fragment\n100%\nN/A\n\nSanctuary Stone\n4.00%\nN/A\n\nVolcanic Stone\n8.00%\nOnly some Lookout Stone creatures.\n\nNotes & Tips\n\nAs they are inorganic, these creatures are immune to most status effects.\nThey have unique attacks, QUESTION
How do Lookout Stones differ in behavior based on their location and surroundings?

ANSWER
Lookout Stones act differently based on their surroundings, such as exploding when clumped together in Caelid and being immune to most status effects due to their inorganic nature.
