Juvenile Scholar Cap protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters. The location of this item can be found by following the Resurrection Painting location.\n\nCap worn by young academy scholars, the juveniles birthed anew by the amber egg of Queen Rennala, the head of Raya Lucaria Academy. Yet their rebirth is not without imperfections, and thus do they repeat the process, eventually becoming utterly dependent upon it. Rebirth is as sleep to them, and with each awakening, memory fades into oblivion. \n\nJuvenile Scholar Cap Location in Elden Ring\nThe Juvenile Scholar Cap Helm can be found as follows:\n\nCollect the Resurrection Painting at the Artist's Shack in eastern Liurnia. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nWith the painting on hand, head to the graveyard area east of Caria Manor. This location is only accessible after completing the manor and defeating Royal Knight Loretta. The item is found by touching a ghost sitting on a chair at the south section of the graveyard, surrounded by albinaurics. The ghost disappears as soon as you touch it and the Juvenile Scholar Cap will appear on the ground along with a Juvenile Scholar Robe and 1 x Larval Tear. QUESTION
What are the defensive properties and benefits of equipping the Juvenile Scholar Cap in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The defensive properties and benefits of equipping the Juvenile Scholar Cap in Elden Ring are Damage Negation, Physical 1.4, VS Strike 1.8, VS Slash 1.8, VS Pierce 1.4, Magic 4.5, Fire 4, Light 4.2, Holy 4.4, Resistance, Immunity 15, Robustness 10, Focus 26, Vitality 29, and Poise 2.
