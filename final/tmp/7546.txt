\n\n Ash of War: Mighty Shot \n\nAffinity\nStandard\n\nSkill\nMighty Shot\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Mighty Shot is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Mighty Shot provides Standard affinity and the Mighty Shot Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:\n"Mighty Shot: Archery skill performed from an oblique stance. Ready the bow, then pull the bowstring to its limit to enhance the power of the shot, penetrating the enemy's guard."\nUsable on light bows and longbows.\n\nAsh of War: Mighty Shot Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Mighty Shot:\n\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab on a road in the east of the Weeping Peninsula. This is the main road you take from Limgrave.
