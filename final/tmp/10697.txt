\n\n Star Shower \n\nSpell Type\nGlintstone Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 23\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Fires six shooting stars that pursue foes \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 24 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nStar Shower is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Star Shower spell fires six homing projectiles.\n\nOne of the glintstone sorceries of the Academy of Raya Lucaria.Fires six magic shooting stars that pursue the target.This sorcery can be cast while in motion. Charging enhances potency.The most difficult to master of the Olivinus Conspectus sorceries.\n\nWhere to find Star Shower\nWhere to find Star Shower:\n\n The spell can be purchased for 10,000 Runes after you deliver the Conspectus Scroll to Miriel Pastor of Vows, Sorceress Sellen, or Preceptor Seluvis. \n\nElden Ring Star Shower Guide\n\nGlintstone Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 26\nDeals magic damage\nDamage can be boosted by 10% by wearing Lusat's Glintstone Crown. This comes at the cost of a 15% increase in ALL sorcery costs\nA upgraded version of Glintstone Stars, with the same characteristics but inferior FP-to-damage ratio. Due to the high FP costs it may be deemed unsuitable for explorations, but useful for special and planned encounters.\nStar Shower, like many other multi-projectile spells, suffers in damage output in the early/mid game, due to the way absorption and defenses work. The higher level and scaling you reach, the better results you will get out of this spell \nStar Shower has extremely good tracking, even at long ranges.  In addition, there is a slight delay between the cast time and when the projectiles begin moving. \n\nThis makes Star Shower excellent against enemies that typically dodge spells, like NPC phantoms\nThe good tracking also makes it an excellent choice in mounted combat, allowing you hit fast moving targets\n\nThis sorcery may be charged by holding the input. This increases the cast time slightly, but also increases the damage. \n\nBest Star Shower Buffs\nUse these Buffs to maximize the performance of Star Shower. QUESTION
Where can Star Shower be purchased in Elden Ring after delivering the Conspectus Scroll to specific characters?

ANSWER
The Conspectus Scroll must be delivered to Miriel Pastor of Vows, Sorceress Sellen, or Preceptor Seluvis in order to purchase Star Shower for 10,000 Runes in Elden Ring.
