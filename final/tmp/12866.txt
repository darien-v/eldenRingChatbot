The Dragonclaw spell conjures a colossal claw to tear through hordes and overpower standalone foes. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the incantations of Dragon Communion.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to tear through foes with dragon claws. Allows one follow-up attack.\nIncantation of those who have hunted dragons and feasted upon their hearts. This is a pure and overwhelming power.\n\nWhere to find Dragonclaw\nWhere to find Dragonclaw:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Church of Dragon Communion in Limgrave for 1 Dragon Heart.[Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Dragonclaw Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 35\nDeals standard physical damage\nCan be cast twice in a row before incurring the downtime.\nWhen the sequence is finished however, this spell receives a long recovery time of 2 seconds during which the caster cannot commence any actions.\nFull combo takes on average 6 seconds to complete, with the first slam landing 2 seconds after the initial cast.\nThis spell grants increased Poise, but only during the mid-air animation.\nWhen wielding a seal in both hands, this incantation can be chain cast by alternating between the left-hand and right-hand cast buttons, doing so is superior to using the standard follow up cast, due to its shorter duration and recovery time.\nNote: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost reduced from 32 to 24, and poise damage and stamina damage against guarding enemies were both increased.The directional control on the attack was also improved.\n\nBuilds with Dragonclaw \n\n Blood Dragon Written Guide\n\nBlood Dancer Written Guide\n\nDragon Warrior Written Guide\n\nDragon Dancer Build\n\nElden Ring Dragonclaw Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
