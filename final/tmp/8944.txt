QUESTION
What is the significance of the Deathtouched Spirit in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Deathtouched Spirit in Elden Ring serves as a defeated NPC outside the Deathtouched Catacombs, expressing disgust at the violation of its resting place and the choice to live within Death, adding depth to the game's lore and world-building.
