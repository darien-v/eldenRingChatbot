It guards the bridge south of the Bestial Sanctum. This is an optional boss. This dragon is simply a stronger Agheel in a rather narrow arena.\nLesser Dragon is the normal enemy form of this boss. Other bosses with the same basic attacks are Flying Dragon Agheel, Decaying Ekzykes, Glintstone Dragon Smarag, Glintstone Dragon Adula, and Borealis the Freezing Fog. QUESTION
What is the most effective strategy for defeating Flying Dragon Greyll in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Stand on elevated areas, use Serpentbone or Rotbone Arrows from a distance, and retreat from long-range fire attacks to defeat Flying Dragon Greyll effectively.
