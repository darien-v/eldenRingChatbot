Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. \n\nA feather from a hawk that lived as one with the storms. Material used for crafting items.  These feathers, enveloped in swirling winds, are often used for arrow fletching.\n\nWhere to find Stormhawk Feather in Elden Ring\nIn the world\n\n1 by Liftside Chamber Site of Grace in Stormveil Castle: On the ground in the heavily guarded courtyard.\n1 by Liurnia Highway South Site of Grace in Liurnia of the Lakes: On a corpse by the road to the north. walkway, the other be reached from the south), and 1 more on the ground below where the others are perched.  (Note: if your damage is high enough to one-shot them, they will almost always fall from Where can Stormhawk Feather be found in Elden Ring?
