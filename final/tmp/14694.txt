Banished Knight Armor (Altered) is part of the Banished Knight Armor Set, and is an earlier, more accessible version of the Banished Knight Armor, but it lacks the unaltered version's cape. It can be obtained pretty easily in Limgrave or Caelid whereas the unaltered version only drops in Castle Sol, in the Mountaintops of the Giants. As a part of the Banished Knight Armor Set, it has high poise, defenses and weight.\n\nThick, full set of armor covering the entire body. This armor was worn by knights who, whether by misfortune or misdeed, were forced to abandon their homes.  These fierce warriors were each and all accomplished. Perhaps that is why, despite their territorial losses, they were still named knights.\n\nWhere to find Banished Knight Armor (Altered) in Elden Ring\nThe Banished Knight Armor (Altered) Chest Armor can be found at:\n\nStormveil Castle. QUESTION
Why is the Banished Knight Armor (Altered) considered an earlier, more accessible version of the Banished Knight Armor in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Banished Knight Armor (Altered) lacks the unaltered version's cape and can be easily obtained in Limgrave or Caelid, making it more accessible than the unaltered version in Castle Sol.
