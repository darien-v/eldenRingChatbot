\n\n Liurnia of the Lakes \n\nLiurnia of the Lakes Map & Fragments\nSites of Grace & Sub-Locations\nAll NPCs & Merchants\nAll Items, Equipment & Magic\nAll Enemies & Bosses\n\nLiurnia of the Lakes is one of the major continental areas in the world of Elden Ring. Found northwest of the starting area of Limgrave, this region has been the common backdrop of pre-launch images of the game, notably due to the prominent placement of The Academy of Raya Lucaria overlooking the misty lake below. It can be initially accessed via the passage beneath Godrick the Grafted's throne room in Stormveil Castle, or by following a narrow cliffside path around the east edge of the castle in northern Stormhill.  \n\nWith its shallow waters and vast wetlands, the region of Liurnia is beset with the gradual sinking of most of its landmass. With its forests perpetually blanketed in fog, eerie sounds of bells can be heard in the distance.\n\nLiurnia of the Lakes Map & Map Fragments for Elden Ring\n\nMap (Liurnia, East): At the swampy lake, on the south, there's a monument and the map shard is just by it. QUESTION
How can someone best navigate and explore the region of Liurnia of the Lakes in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To best navigate and explore Liurnia of the Lakes in Elden Ring, one can access it via the passage beneath Godrick the Grafted's throne room in Stormveil Castle or follow a narrow cliffside path around the east edge of the castle in northern Stormhill, taking in the vast wetlands, forests perpetually blanketed in fog, and eerie sounds of bells while uncovering locations like the Academy of Raya Lucaria and various sub-locations.
