QUESTION
Why is Grave Violet obtained specifically from graveyards and believed to be useful in calling forth spirits?

ANSWER
Grave Violet is obtained specifically from graveyards because it blooms there and is believed to be useful in calling forth spirits due to its hue resembling ghostflame.
