\n\n Lesser Bloodhound Knight \n\nLocation\nRaya Lucaria AcademySwamp Lookout TowerGelmir Hero’s GraveVolcano Manor\n\nDrops\n 491 - 1764 Runes\n\nLesser Bloodhound Knight is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Lesser Bloodhound Knights are Non-Boss variants of Bloodhound Knight and Bloodhound Knight Darriwil. Bloodhound Knights are large, lightly-armored humanoids, notorious for their fierce agility and an aggressive attack behavior. These Knights move by walking with their feet and hands on the ground, and wield large curved greatswords with great dexterity.\nThe spirit of a Bloodhound Knight, Floh, can be summoned for aid in battle by using Bloodhound Knight Floh Ashes.\n\n The Bloodhound Knights are trained as hunters and known to be unshakable trackers. Without the use of language, each knight chooses his own master. Once the decision has been made, the knight stays loyal for life. \n\nElden Ring Lesser Bloodhound Knight Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Bloodhound Knight:\n\nFound hanging from one of the gates to the Raya Lucaria Academy, passing the Isolated Merchant. Upon approximation from within the Academy, this Bloodhound Knight jumps in ambush to surprise-attack players.\nFound in the Gelmir Hero’s Grave, guarding a corpse holding the Gelmir Knight Set. It drops the entire Bloodhound Knight Set upon death.\nFound in Swamp Lookout Tower in Caelid.\nAnother is found in the Volcano Manor, past the hidden wall. QUESTION
How can players effectively strategize to defeat the Lesser Bloodhound Knight in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can effectively defeat the Lesser Bloodhound Knight in Elden Ring by anticipating surprise attacks, utilizing ranged attacks to maintain distance, and exploiting openings after its aggressive attacks.
