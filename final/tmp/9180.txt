\n\n Putrid Corpse \n\nLocation\nVarious\n\nDrops\n 25 - 1266 RunesMushroomMelted MushroomToxic MushroomBloodrose\n\nPutrid Corpse is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Putrid Corpses are the reanimated dead that roam the Lands Between en masse. Usually found in large groups around graves and catacombs. There are many different variants of Putrid Corpses, often taking on characteristics of their surroundings such as being imbued with poison from toxic pools of water, or developing large blisters full of cursed blood in the Mohgwyn Palace. This allows them to deal different types of damage and afflict opponents with varying status effects.\nA group of four (4) Putrid Corpse spirits can be summoned for aid by using the Putrid Corpse Ashes.\n\nTheir movements are dull and confused... True death does not come easily for them.\n\nElden Ring Putrid Corpse Locations\nPutrid Corpses can be found all around the various regions of the Lands Between. Some notable locations are as follows:\n\nCaelid\nWeeping Peninsula's beach.\nImpaler's Catacombs\nCaelid\nPath between Forsaken Ruins and Caelem Ruins\nCaelem Ruins\nBehind Fort Gael\nGaol Cave\nAltus Plateau\nEast Windmill Pasture\nSainted Hero's Grave\nThe Shaded Castle\nMt Gelmir, east of the entrance to Seethewater Cave. They spawn midair, up above, and fall to the ground.\nSubterranean Shunning-Grounds\nLeyndell Catacombs\nMohgwyn Palace\n\nElden Ring Putrid Corpse Drops & Drop Rate\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 25 - 1266\n\nMushroom\n5.00-12.00%\nIs not dropped by Poison or Burning Putrid Corpses.\n\nToxic Mushroom\n5.00-12.00%\nDropped only by Toxic and Fungal Putrid Corpses.\n\nMelted Mushroom\n5.00-12.00%\nDropped only by Burning and Fungal Putrid Corpses.\n\nBloodrose\n8.00%\nDropped only by Blistered Putrid Corpses.\n\nEnemy Type\nItem Drops\nNotes\n\n Putrid Corpse \n\n 36 - 430 Runes\nMushroom (5.00% base)\n\nFound on the southwest beach of the Weeping Peninsula, inside Impaler's Catacombs, on the Altus Plateau, in the Sainted Hero's Grave, east of the entrance to Seethewater Cave on Mt Gelmir, in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, and in Leyndell Catacombs. They can be found in the small Poison swamp northeast of the entrance to Castle Morne, in The Shaded Castle, and in the Leyndell Catacombs.\n\n Burning Putrid Corpse \n\n 25 - 98 Runes\nMelted Mushroom (5.00% - 12.00% base)\n\nThey will run at the player and explode in a ball of Fire. Found only in Caelid, specifically around the Shack of the Rotting and Rotview Balcony, on the path connecting Caelem Ruins to Forsaken Ruins, southwest of theDeep Siofra Well Site of Grace and northwest of the entrance to the Abandoned Cave entrance gathered around several stone sarcophagi organized in a rectangle, and in a large group on the road that passes the Shack of the Rotting and leads to Caelem Ruins, specifically southwest of the entrance to Minor Erdtree Catacombs.\n\n Blistered Putrid Corpse \n\n 289 Runes\nBloodrose (8.00% base)\nMushroom (5.00% base)\n\nThey explode upon death, expelling a cloud of blood flies, which inflicts rapid Hemorrhage buildup. Some will charge at the player and explode, not unlike the Burning Putrid Corpses. Found only inside and on the steps leading up to the Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum.\n\n Fungal Putrid Corpse \n\n 1266 Runes\nMushroom (5.00% base)\nMelted Mushroom (5.00% base)\nToxic Mushroom (5.00% base)\n\nAll of their attacks inflict Scarlet Rot buildup. Found only in Miquella's Haligtree.\n\nPutrid Corpse Notes & Tips\n\nThere are a few Fungal Putrid Corpses in Miquella's Haligtree that wait behind corners to shove unwary players off the edge of the platform and to their death. QUESTION
How can various characteristics of the Putrid Corpses in Elden Ring affect the way players approach combat strategies and adapt to different enemy types they encounter?

ANSWER
Players need to adapt combat strategies to account for Putrid Corpses' diverse damage types, afflictions, and unique behaviors, such as Poison buildup from Toxic Putrid Corpses, explosion attacks from Burning Putrid Corpses, Scarlet Rot buildup from Rotten Putrid Corpses, Hemorrhage buildup from Blistered Putrid Corpses, Magic Defense from Magic Putrid Corpses, and corner ambushes from Fungal Putrid Corpses.
