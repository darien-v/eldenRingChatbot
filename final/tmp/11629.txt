Leyndell Soldiers are the wardens or defenders in the Altus Plateau and the Leyndell Royal Capital. They are normally seen in groups, in pairs, or accompanying their superior, the Leyndell Knight. They are frequently found\nThe spirit of two (2) Leyndell Soldiers can be summoned for aid in battle by using Leyndell Soldier Ashes.\n\nThe soldiers of Leyndell fight their battles defensively.
