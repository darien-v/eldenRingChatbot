Olivinus Glintstone Crown protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nOne of the glintstone crowns bestowed upon Raya Lucaria scholars whose pursuits were deemed worthy. Increases intelligence to the detriment of HP. The lineage of the Olivinus Conspectus began with the sorcerer Lusat, and its adherents continue his study of meteors. \n\nWhere to find Olivinus Glintstone Crown in Elden Ring\nThe Olivinus Glintstone Crown Helm can be found at:\n\nFound at Raya Lucaria Academy [Elden Ring Map here]\nClosest Site of Grace: Schoolhouse Classroom\nLooted off a corpse that is on a narrow path. From the site of grace, go to the hallway and towards the room on the left. You'll find an illusory wall in front of the lootable corpse in the said room.\nGo to the balcony and jump over the fence on the left. Follow the path that circles back to the entrance of the Schoolhouse Classroom site of grace.
