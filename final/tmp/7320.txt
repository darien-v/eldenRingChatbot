Shadow Bait spell casts a shadow that serves as a distraction to unfocus your enemies.\n\nIncantation of the Two Fingers' servants, who once served as theassassins of the Roundtable Hold.\nCreates a pale gold shadow before the caster, luring foes of humanbuild and attracting their aggression.\nThis incantation can be cast while in motion or crouching, andwill still affect foes that are already in a combat state.\nIt is said that those beguiled by the shadow see within it a hatedfoe.\n\nWhere to find Shadow Bait\nWhere to find Shadow Bait:\n\nCan be found on a corpse at Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, after going through the pipe room to a room with the corpse sitting at the center, next to a not-very-well-hidden Omen ready to strike.
