\n\n Testu's Rise \n\nAn observation tower found in the middle of a lone isle in Liurnia. The entrance is sealed by impish magic and presents a challenge to would-be visitors.\n\nTestu's Rise is a Location in Elden Ring. Testu's Rise is found in Liurnia of the Lakes, on a small isle north of the academy. This tower is accessible on foot or horseback by following the lake's shallows to the isle's rocky shores.\n\nTestu's Rise Map\nTestu's Rise is on a small isle near the northernmost section of the lakes. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Testu's Rise\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Testu's Rise\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nBloodrose\nMushroom\nRowa Fruit\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nx3 Smithing Stone [2] (In gazebo surrounded by lobsters, South-East of Testu's Rise)\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nThere are no equipment items here\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nx1 Memory Stone\n\nElden Ring Testu's Rise Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nSkeleton (Spectral)\nTurtle (Spectral)\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses at this location\n\nTestu's Rise Walkthrough\nTestu's Rise Where to Find Three Wise Beasts\nThe entrance to Testu's Rise is initially sealed by a barrier. By the entrance, you will find an imp statue holding a glowing book that asks you to "Seek three wise beasts".\n\nThese beasts are represented as Ghostly Tortoises that spawn somewhere in the area. Each Tortoise needs to be found and attacked in order to break parts of the seal. This is the same challenge as the one at Oridys's Rise at the Weeping Peninsula except this time, a large number of Ghostly Skeletons will also spawn to hinder your progress.\n\nThe first Tortoise can be found by the cliffs northwest of the tower on the same level.\nThe second Tortoise is found high up on one of the trees east of the tower. Use a ranged or magical attack to hit it, and it will fall from the tree and die of fall damage.\nThe final Tortoise is to the southeast of the tower on a ledge halfway down the cliff.\n\nIf players rest at a Site of Grace before all of the Tortoises are killed, they may have to activate the book statue again to spawn the remaining Tortoise(s).\nOnce all three Tortoises are killed, players will get a notification that the seal has been broken.\nGo back to the now-opened door, and climb up the ladder.\n\nWhen you reach the top of the ladder, turn right, and proceed up the stairs. You'll see a room with a chest in the middle of it containing a Memory Stone which increases your Memory Slots by one, allowing for the memorization of more Sorceries or Incantations. \n\nElden Ring QUESTION
Why do players need to find and attack three wise beasts in Testu's Rise in order to access the tower?

ANSWER
Players need to find and attack three wise beasts in Testu's Rise in order to access the tower because doing so will break the seal placed by an imp statue holding a glowing book near the entrance.
