\nColossal Weapons are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring (not to be confused with Colossal Swords). Colossal Weapons are heavy weapons which require the wielder to have high Strength. They are capable of inflicting massive damage to enemies but are slow, making its wielder vulnerable to enemy attacks.  \nColossal Weapons are compatible with a range of different Ashes of War, allowing you to mix and match for whatever your needs may be.\n\nElden Ring Colossal Weapons Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Colossal Weapons in Elden Ring.
