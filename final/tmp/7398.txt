QUESTION
How can Lansseax's Glaive be effectively used in combat situations?

ANSWER
Lansseax's Glaive can be effectively used in combat situations by casting it for AoE damage and using it against groups due to its large horizontal swipe and follow-up AoE damage, while noting its mediocre damage against single targets.
