\n\n Bellum Highway \n\nOutside the East Raya Lucaria Gate\n\nBellum Highway is a Location in Elden Ring. The Bellum Highway is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. Located at the north of Liurnia of the Lakes going through the east path. The East Raya Lucaria Gate and Site of Grace are located in here. The eastern part of the highway reaches around to the Black Knife Catacombs.
