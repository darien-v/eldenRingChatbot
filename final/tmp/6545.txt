CONTEXT
\n\n Blade of Death \n\n        Unleash the power of the Rune of Death to fire off a blade-like projectile. \n       In addition to dealing immediate damage, the blade reduces the enemy's maximum HP and continues to wear down HP for a while. \n\nBlade of Death is a Skill in Elden Ring. Blade of Death is a Unique skill for the Black Knife.  It shares an animation with Blade of Gold, the unique skill for the Blade of Calling. It allows user to fire a projectile and deal additional damage. This also has a unique feature that debuffs a few enemy stats. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Blade of Death\n\nDefault Skill for the Black Knife.\n\nElden Ring Blade of Death Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 25\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried (the Destined Death projectile can be Parried with Carian Retaliation)\nReduces enemy max HP by 10% for 15 seconds.\nSaps enemy HP for 5 seconds\nDeals 10 stance damage.\nDamage type is Holy Damage and scales purely with Faith. Damage can be increased by Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear and Sacred Scorpion Charm, as well as other generic attack buffs like Golden Vow and Howl of Shabriri.\nThe debuff stacks with either the Black Blade incantation OR Destined Death but NOT both.\nThe range of Blade of Death is longer than Reduvia Blood Blade and equivalent to Sorceries and Incantations.\nStealth attacks with Blade of Death will do additional damage.\nThe burning effect from the Blade of Death weapon art, like status effects, will go through i-frames.\nBoth this and the Blade of Calling have substantial hyper-armor once the player is in the air.\nComparison to Blade of Gold, Blade of Gold values are represented first and in yellow, Blade of Death values are represented second and in red: FP Cost: 17/25, Poise damage (PVE/PVP): 180/100, 180/100, CC type: Knock back/Stagger, Total AR 421/430 (50 FTH), 443/446 (80 FTH), Holy Damage Motion Values: 145/180, Actual damage at 80 faith and Upgrade level +10: 1323/1150.\n\nIn short, Blade of Gold deals more damage that blade of Death at a cheaper FP cost, but Blade of Death applies a DoT and a debuff.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Effect duration that reduces maximum HP has been increased.\nSee Buffs & Debuffs for more information on stacking debuffs.\n\nBlack Knife Assassin Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills ANSWER
