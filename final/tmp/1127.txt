\n\n Caelem Ruins \n\nA set of perpetually burning ruins in western Caelid. The Caelid Highway cuts through these ruins which are heavily guarded by mobile flamethrowers and swarming with burning reanimated corpses.\n\nCaelem Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Caelem Ruins is found in Caelid. Players can get to this location by following the Caelid Highway on the west end of the region, near the telescope.\n\nCaelem Ruins Map\nThe Caelem Ruins is found along the Caelid Highway on the west end. The highway cuts through the ruins and you can't miss it if you are on the main road. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Caelem Ruins\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Caelem Ruins\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nSmoldering Butterfly\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials at this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nVisage Shield\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nNo Uniques, Ashes or Keys at this location\n\nElden Ring Caelem Ruins Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nFlame Chariot\nPutrid Corpse\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nMad Pumpkin Head (Duo)\n\nCaelem Ruins Walkthrough\nThese ruins are filled with burning Putrid Corpses that shamble towards targets and explode kamikaze-style. There are also several Maneuverable Flamethrowers roaming the ruins. These are incredibly dangerous and should be fought on horseback to more easily dodge their streams of fire. You can aim for their operators riding behind the flamethrowers who are easier to kill than the flamethrowers themselves. Getting rid of the operators also gets rid of the flamethrowers.\nOn the eastern side of the ruins, near the duo of Maneuverable Flamethrowers, you can find a set of stairs leading underground to the Mad Pumpkin Head duo boss fight. Defeating these two awards 6,300 Runes and allows access to the treasure room at the back, where you can find a chest containing the Visage Shield.\n\nElden Ring Caelem Ruins Gallery and Notes\n[other
