\n\n Albinauric Rise \n\nA lone Rise overlooking the narrow channel of the frozen river. The puzzle requires one to allow Imps to do battle against one another.\n\nAlbinauric Rise is a Location in Elden Ring. Albinauric Rise is found in the eastern Consecrated Snowfield, which is a secret area on the lower plains of the Mountaintops of the Giants and can be accessed by using both Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left) and Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) at the Grand Lift of Rold.\n\nAlbinauric Rise Map\nAlbinauric Rise is found on the eastern section of the Consecrated Snowfield. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Albinauric Rise\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location.\n\nAlbinauric Rise Walkthrough\nThe entrance to Albinauric Rise is initially sealed upon your first visit. There is a statue of two Imps fighting each other right outside the tower. This is a clue as to how to unlock the seal. There are two ways you can go about solving this puzzle. Choose the one which is more convenient for you:\n\nPuzzle Solution #1 - Requires killing an Imp while you have the Fanged Imp Ashes summon out. This can be purchased from the Isolated Merchant found at the Raya Lucaria Academy for 2,000  Runes.\nPuzzle Solution #2 - Requires using a Bewitching Branch  or a few Crystal Darts on one of the two imps patrolling the tower to make them kill each other.\n\nWhen successful, you are notified of the seal being broken. Head into the tower and get to the top floor to find the Graven-Mass Talisman sitting on a bed of crystal.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Albinauric Rise\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nNo Gatherables at this
