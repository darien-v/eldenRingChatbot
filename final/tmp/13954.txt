Radahn's Lion Armor is part of the General Radahn Set, and is one of the heaviest and most powerful armors in the game. Radahn's Lion Armor is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nArmor depicting the golden lion.Worn by General Radahn.\nThe golden lion is said to symbolize Godfrey, the Elden Lord, and his beast regent, Serosh. From his youngest years, Radahn was naturally captivated by the Lord of the Battlefield. \n\nWhere to find Radahn's Lion Armor in Elden Ring\nThe Radahn's Lion Armor Chest Armor can be purchased from Enia at the Roundtable Hold for 12,000 runes once Starscourge Radahn has been defeated.\n\nRadahn's Lion Armor Set in Elden Ring\n\nRadahn's Lion Armor is a piece of the General Radahn Set.\nThe rest
