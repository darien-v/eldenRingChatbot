\n\n Primal Glintstone Blade \n\nWeight\n0.6\n\nEffect \n\nReduces  \n       Spell  \n       FP costs by 25% but reduces maximum  \n       HP by 15% \n\nPrimal Glintstone Blade is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Primal Glintstone Blade reduces Spell FP costs by 25% but reduces maximum HP by 15%. Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nAn old glintstone blade that has been stained with blood.\nReduces FP consumption of sorceries and incantations at the cost of maximum HP.\nThe old sorcerers would slice open their hearts with these blades to imbue a primal glintstone with their soul, and thus did they die.\n\nPrimal Glintstone Blade Effect in Elden Ring\nPrimal Glintstone Blade reduces Spell FP costs by 25% but reduces maximum HP by 15%.\n\nPrimal Glintstone Blade Location in Elden Ring\nPrimal Glintstone Blade can be found in the following location:\n\nStargazer's Ruins in Mountaintops of the Giants: Found in a chest in an underground area near the center of the ruins. The entrance is sealed by a barrier and there is a talking Jellyfish outside who is looking for her sister. Summon your Spirit Jellyfish that you acquired at the beginning of the game to unlock the seal. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Primal Glintstone Blade Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  2000\nThe -25% spell FP roughly translates to +33.3% spell FP efficiency.\nThe increase in FP efficiency means the effective value of FP-restoring items, such as Flask of Cerulean Tears, Assassin's Cerulean Dagger, Ancestral Spirit's Horn, Sacrificial Axe, and Sword of Milos, is also increased, as you are able to cast more spells with the amount of flat FP restored.\nAt 50 Vigor (1704 HP), this talisman's -15% (1704HP*0.85=1448HP) effectively reduces Vigor to 40 (1450HP). At 60 Vigor (1900 HP) it reduces HP to 1615, the equivalent of 47 Vigor. Due to the diminishing returns on the amount of HP provided by levels of Vigor beyond its soft caps at 40 and 60, the more Vigor the character has the more equivalent levels they lose to the 15% reduction.\nAt 33 Mind (189 FP), this talisman's +33.3% efficiency (189FP*1.33=252FP) effectively increases Mind to 42 (248FP). At 50 Mind (300 FP) it increases FP to 400, the equivalent of 80 Mind (401 FP). Mind provides a relatively flat amount of FP beyond its soft cap at 60 compared to Vigor and HP, but the more Mind the character has the more equivalent levels they gain from the 33% efficiency.\nUsing the above points, this talisman will grant the highest amount of effective levels to characters who are already at high levels of Mind and lower levels of Vigor. At low levels of Mind and high levels of Vigor, this talisman will be less effective than redistributing levels from Vigor into Mind.\n\nWithout any other changes to HP and FP, you need 26 Vigor (833 HP) to accept all the HP from a Flask of Crimson Tears +12 (810 HP) and 38 Mind (221 FP) to accept all the FP from a Flask of Cerulean Tears +12 (220 FP). With a Primal Glintstone Blade equipped, you must increase your Vigor to 30 (994HP*0.85=844HP) to fully consume a crimson flask, but your effective spell FP is increased (221FP*1.33=294) to be on par with 49 Mind (293 FP). This is a 4 level investment for an 11 level return, or a net gain of 7 levels.\n\nOther notes & tips go here.\n\nDark Knight Written Guide\n\nElementalist Written Guide\n\nDragon Warrior Written QUESTION
Describe the process of acquiring the Primal Glintstone Blade in Elden Ring.

ANSWER
Primal Glintstone Blade can be found in Stargazer's Ruins in Mountaintops of the Giants, inside a chest in an underground area near the center, accessible by summoning your Spirit Jellyfish to unlock the seal.
