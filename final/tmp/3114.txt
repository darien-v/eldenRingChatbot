QUESTION
Why is Ronin's Greaves considered part of the Ronin's Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ronin's Greaves are considered part of the Ronin's Set in Elden Ring because they, along with other pieces like the Iron Kasa, Ronin's Armor, and Ronin's Gauntlets, form a cohesive armor set offering unified defensive properties and style.
