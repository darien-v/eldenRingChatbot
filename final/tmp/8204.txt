Banished Knight Gauntlets is an armor piece that protect both of the player's arms by applying various defensive properties, It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders, but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nThick, full set of armor covering the entire body.These gauntlets were worn by knights who, whether by misfortune or misdeed, were forced to abandon their homes.These fierce warriors were each and all accomplished. Perhaps that is why, despite their territorial losses, they were still named knights. \n\nWhere to find Banished Knight Gauntlets in Elden Ring\nThe Banished Knight Gauntlets Armor Gauntlets piece can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped from the Banished Knights in Stormveil Castle.\nDropped from the wandering Banished Knight at Cathedral of Dragon Communion\n\nBanished Knight Gauntlets Set in Elden Ring\n\nBanished Knight Gauntlets is a piece of the Banished Knight Set.\nThe rest of the set is composed by the Banished Knight Helm, Banished Knight Armor and Banished Knight Greaves.\n\nBuilds & Videos in Elden Ring for Banished Knight Gauntlets\n\nCarian Spellknight Written Guide\n\nCarian Cleaver Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Banished Knight Gauntlets Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value: 100 \nEven though the Banished Knight Set is a good set which the player can get early on, it is recommended to have enough points in Endurance, or equip items which increase "equip load". If you are lacking and don't want to play with "heavy load"- debuff, it would be good to change the gauntlets first, since the extra weight to gains ratio is very bad.\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
