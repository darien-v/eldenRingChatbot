QUESTION
Why is the Liurnian Lake Spirit in Elden Ring referring to Edgar, the Revenger?

ANSWER
The Liurnian Lake Spirit in Elden Ring refers to Edgar, the Revenger, because he sees Edgar as being consumed by love, revenge, and a frenzied flame that melts everything away.
