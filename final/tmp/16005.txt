Halberds strike a balance between moderate damage and long reach, combining straight thrusting attacks and wide swinging strikes. \nHalberds can be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, so play around with them and see what you like as you acquire more.\n\nElden Ring Halberds Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Whips in Elden Ring.
