CONTEXT
\n\n Lifesteal Fist \n\n        Skill that demonstrates mastery of the art of controlling vital energies. A slow, controlled punch with an energy-infused fist that renders foes unconscious and steals their HP. Only effective against foes of human build. \n\nLifesteal Fist is a Skill in Elden Ring. Lifesteal Fist is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible weapons. Updated to Patch 1.09\n\nHow to get Lifesteal Fist\n\nDefault skill on the Clinging Bone.\nAsh of War: Lifesteal Fist is dropped by a Teardrop Scarab along the Caelid Highway. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Lifesteal Fist Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Lifesteal Fist.\nFP Cost: 14. Does not use FP if it doesn't connect.\nDeals 12 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nHeals for 30% of your max hp.\nhas a motion value of 1.25 for PvP, making in entirely unique among skills for having the only positive PvP multiplier\nThe reason magic buffs increase damage so much seems to be a innate quirk of the skill scaling magic damage off your arc: it works similar to bows, where the arrow damage is added to your bows base damage before calculating scaling bonus. The magic AR added by the buff is interpreted as magic base damage and you end up with a massive scaling bonus (depending on your arc)\n\nPatch Changes\nPatch 1.09\n\nRange of the skill has been
