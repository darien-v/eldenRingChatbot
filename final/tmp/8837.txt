Aristocrat Garb is part of the Aristocrat Set Armor Set, and is a Lightweight armor that that provides a good balance of defensive properties. Aristocrat Garb is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nHigh quality garb adorned with fine golden embroidery.
