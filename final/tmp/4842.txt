\n\n Fanged Imp Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 50\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons two fanged imp spirits \n\nFanged Imp Ashes is a Spirit and Summon, as well as optional Keepsake, in Elden Ring. Fanged Imp Ashes summons two fanged imp spirits that moves nimbly and attack enemies. They are best used to distract enemies since these spirits move fast, you can summon them when you are being overwhelmed by a group of enemies. While the enemies are being distracted, recover some stamina for a while then continue your attack, coordinating with the fanged imp spirits you've summoned.\nImps can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Imp.\n\nAshes remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon two fanged imp spirits.\nThese spirits are well suited to causing a disturbance, moving nimbly like pests and hurling magic pots.The two imps seem fond of each other's company.\n\nWhere to find Fanged Imp Ashes\nWhere to Find Fanged Imp Ashes\n\nYou can select Fanged Imp Ashes as Keepsake during Character Creation\nIt can be purchased from the Isolated Merchant for 2000 Runes. He can be found by traveling to the Main Academy Gate site of grace and following the elevated road east, past the seal and several wolves.
