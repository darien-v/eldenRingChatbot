CONTEXT
\n\n Stamp (Sweep) \n\n        Brace armament and step into a low stance that prevents recoil from most enemy attacks. Follow up with a strong attack for a sweeping strike. \n\nStamp (Sweep) is a Skill in Elden Ring. Stamp (Sweep) is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to Swords, Axes, and Hammers (small and medium swords excepted). Updated to Patch 1.08.\n\nHow to get Stamp (Sweep)\n\nDropped by an invisible Teardrop Scarab located at the beaches of Western Limgrave. From where the Giant Land Octopus and Land Octopus enemies are, check the east side of the shore and you'll see some pillars on the beach. You'll notice multiple glowing prints moving around the pillars. Attack it at the right time just when it's about to approach you to reveal the scarab. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Stamp (Sweep) Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Stamp (Sweep).\nFP Cost: 5 (8)\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill can be Parried\n Usage: Press (L2/Special skill), then (R2/Heavy attack). \n\n (L2/Special skill) Will cause you to brace for impact, which absorbs one hit from an enemy \n (R1/Light attack) Performs the 2nd attack in the light attack combo. This does not cost FP. \n (R2/Heavy attack) Can be performed either immediately or after absorbing one strike, and performs an spinning Sweep which knocks back smaller enemies, or deals significant damage to bosses. Has a wider range compared to it's Upwards Cut counterpart, but hits twice, with the first hit weaker than the second.   \n\nStance damage: \n\n20 (w/ Axes)\n22 (w/ Greatswords, Curved Greatswords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, and Greataxes)\n24 (w/ Flails, Colossal Weapons (non-Hammers), Colossal Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers)\n26 (w/ Colossal Weapons (Hammers)\n\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nThe delay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) has been reduced and being able to perform the skill\nAttack Power was increased.\nTiming between the end of the skill and performing actions other than the strong attack has been reduced.\nWhile not mentioned in the patch notes, the second hit of the strong attack now knocks back enemies, and when performing the skill, inputting light attack now performs the 2nd attack in the light attack combo.\n\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, the poise value during the active part of the Stamp Weapon Art has been increased.\n\nStamp (Sweep) Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Stamp (Sweep) Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is it important to accurately time your attacks when trying to obtain the Stamp (Sweep) skill from the Teardrop Scarab in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Timing your attacks accurately when trying to obtain the Stamp (Sweep) skill from the Teardrop Scarab in Elden Ring is important to reveal the scarab, which is invisible until you attack it at the right moment.
