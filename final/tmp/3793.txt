CONTEXT
\n\n Giant Hunt \n\n        Skill developed for confronting gigantic foes. Step forward from a low stance, carrying the momentum into a sudden upward thrust. \n\nGiant Hunt is a Skill in Elden Ring. Giant Hunt is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible weapons. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Giant Hunt\n\nDropped by miniboss Night's Cavalry. Only appears at night. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Giant Hunt Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Giant Hunt.\nFP Cost: 16 on use\n30 stance damage (Twinblades, Spears, Halberds)36 stance damage on (Greatsword, Colossal sword, Heavy Thrusting Sword, Great Spear)\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill can be Parried\nUpon activation, the user crouches preparing their armament (weapon) before making a brief step forward, thrusting the armament upwards.\n\nThe direction of the thrust can be aimed at the start of the animation.\n\nThe thrust attack causes most humanoid enemies to be knocked up in the air. \n\nBecause enemies knocked in the ground are not invulnerable while standing up, this skill becomes a great setup for other attacks.\n\nThis always deals thrust damage. As such, it can benefit from the Spear Talisman.\nIf used without FP: \n\nDeals less damage on hit.\n\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nPoise Damage has been increased.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\n\nGiant Hunt Demonstration\n\nElden Ring
