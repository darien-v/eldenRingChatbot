\n\n Prelate's Charge \n\n        Slam armament into the ground to create a surge of flames, then charge in. \n       Hold to continue the charge \n\nPrelate's Charge is a Skill in Elden Ring. Prelate's Charge is a regular skill that smashes the weapon into the ground to create a surge of flames.\n\nHow to get Prelate's Charge\n\nDefault skill for the Prelate's Inferno Crozier\nLocated outside of Guardians' Garrison. The Scarab is hanging off a tree, just before the chain bridge drops this Ash of War. Two ways of getting there: \n\nHead south-west from the Whiteridge Road site of grace until you get to three twisted trees (the tallest in the camp) before the ridge. The Scarab is hanging off the east-most tree\nHead back across the chain bridge from the Giants' Gravepost site of grace and to the right of the tallest tree the scarab is hanging off the smaller one next to it.\n\nElden Ring Prelate's Charge Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Prelate's Charge.\nFP Cost: costs 7 FP to start the charge and then 7 FP continuously to keep charging\nThis Skill is not Chargeable. However, you can charge at enemies using it.\nBoth of this Skill's attacks can be Parried\nOnly the first and last hits normally apply status buildup. \n\nHowever, Bloodflame Blade's bleed effect and Black Flame Blade's damage over time are still applied by the intermediate hits.\nOther buffs like Poison Armament or Blood Grease do not work, nor do innate rot or bleed on the weapon. \n\nPrelate's Charge Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric
