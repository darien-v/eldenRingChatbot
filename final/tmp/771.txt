\n\n Golden Lineage Evergaol \n\nFound near the Grand Lift of Dectus, this prison contains a member of the Golden Lineage, imprisoned for the grotesque act of Grafting.\n\nGolden Lineage Evergaol is a Location in Elden Ring. The Golden Lineage Evergaol is found in Altus Plateau. It is in the far southwest of the region, right by Grand Lift of Dectus. It is in an area the requires you to follow a specific pathway to access, while jumping down the cliffside will usually result in death. You must spend one Stonesword Key to unlock it. \n\nGolden Lineage Evergaol Map\n[Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Golden Lineage Evergaol\n\nBoss - Godefroy the Grafted\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Golden Lineage Evergaol\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nThere are no gatherable items in this location.\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location.\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nGodfrey Icon\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no Key Items in this location.\n\nElden Ring Golden Lineage Evergaol Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nAbnormal Stone Cluster\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nGodefroy the Grafted\n\nGolden Lineage Evergaol Walkthrough\nTo get to the Evergaol, start at the Altus Plateau site of grace and head south of the Grand Lift of Dectus. Once at the edge of the cliff behind the lift, head to the far east side of the cliff, where you can jump down along a path that will lead you to the bottom. Upon reaching the arena you can go against Godefroy the Grafted who will share similar movements and attacks as one of the early bosses, Godrick the Grafted without reaching phase 2 and added fire.  Defeating Godefroy will reward players with a Godfrey Icon Talisman, which can enhance charged spells and skills, along with 26,000 Runes. \n\nElden Ring Golden Lineage Evergaol Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring
