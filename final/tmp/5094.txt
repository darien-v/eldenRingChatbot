QUESTION
How can Gostoc's Bell Bearing be used to gain access to new items in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Offer Gostoc's Bell Bearing to the Twin Maiden Husks at Roundtable Hold to access new items: Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot, Caestus, Bandit Garb, Bandit Boots, Bandit Manchettes, Buckler, Ruin Fragment, Furlcalling Finger Remedy, Stormhawk Feather, Great Arrow, Ballista Bolt.
