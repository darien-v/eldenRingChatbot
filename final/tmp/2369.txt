CONTEXT
\n\n Bloody Finger \n\nType\nReusable\n\nEffect \n\n        Attempts invasion of other player's world \n\nBloody Finger is a Multiplayer Item in Elden Ring. Multiplayer Items allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitve gameplay like PVP.\n\nItem for online play. (Can also be used from the Multiplayer menu.)\nAttempts an invasion of another player's world. If successful, you will arrive as an invader (Bloody Finger) with the objective of defeating the Host of Fingers of that world.\nGlistening blood has been siphoned into the nail of this finger. Its sickly pale skin feels nothing now, but the nail still aches with sweetest pain.\n\nBloody Finger Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Bloody Finger:\n\nTravel to Rose Church after defeating any shardbearer and talking to Enia in the Roundtable Hold for the first time.\nSpeak with White Mask Varré in front of the church.\nWhite Mask Varré will give you five Festering Bloody Fingers and ask you to invade other worlds.\nThere are multiple ways to complete this step; \n\nIf offline, invade Magnus The Beast Claw located in Writheblood Ruins least 3 times, simply killing him works too.\nIf online, invade at least 3 times. You do not need to successfully defeat the host; simply leaving with the Finger Severer is enough.\n\nReturn to the Rose Church. Varré will give you the Lord of Blood's Favor and instruct you to soak the cloth with a maiden's blood.\nThere are multiple ways to complete this step;\n\nOption #1 - Chapel of Anticipation \n\nTravel Northwest to The Four Belfries.\nThe highest belfry will have a Imbued Sword Key in a chest.\nThe second highest belfry will transport you to the Precipice of Anticipation.\nAfter defeating the Grafted Scion, travel further to the  Chapel of Anticipation. There, you will find the corpse of a maiden.\nInteract with her corpse to soak the Lord of Blood's Favor in her blood.\n\nOption #2 - Church of Inhibition\n\nTravel Northeast, shy of the Grand Lift, to the Church of Inhibition.\nIn the Church of Inhibition will be a dead maiden.\nInteract with her corpse to soak the Lord of Blood's Favor in her blood.\n\nOption #3 - Killing a Maiden \n\nFind and kill Hyetta.\nSoak the Lord of Blood's Favor in her blood.\n\nReturn to the Rose Church and speak with Varré.\nHand over the Lord of Blood’s Favor.\nVideo Location\n\nUpon completion of the questline, Varré will grant you the Bloody Finger.\n[Elden Ring Map Here]\n\nElden Ring Bloody Finger Guide\n\nYou can attempt to invade another player's world.\nIf you successfully invade, invasion multiplayer will begin, with you as a Bloody Finger. (Your goal will be to defeat the Host of Fingers). Killing the Host of Fingers will grant the invader a Rune Arc and a Furlcalling Finger Remedy. \nDuring invasion multiplayer, you are provided three Phantom Bloody Fingers. Using these will spawn you at another location in the same session.\nDoing the quest and consequently receiving this item makes your eyes slightly redder. This effect can be disabled in the mirror in Fia's room at the Roundtable Hold.\nThe maximum number of invaders is 1 unless the host is using the Taunter's Tongue.\nBy default, invasions can only occur once the host has summoned a cooperator. Solo invasions cannot happen unless the host has used the Taunter's Tongue.  \n\nElden Ring Multiplayer Items\n\nBlue Cipher Ring  ♦  Duelist's Furled Finger  ♦  Festering Bloody Finger  ♦  Finger Severer  ♦  Furlcalling Finger Remedy  ♦  Grace Mimic  ♦  Phantom Bloody Finger  ♦  Recusant Finger  ♦  Scriptstone  ♦  Small Golden Effigy  ♦  Small Red Effigy  ♦  Tarnished's Furled Finger  ♦  Tarnished's Wizened Finger  ♦  Taunter's Tongue  ♦  White Cipher Ring\n\n

QUESTION
How can I complete the questline to obtain the Bloody Finger item in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Travel to Rose Church after defeating any shardbearer and talking to Enia in the Roundtable Hold for the first time, speak with White Mask Varré, receive Festering Bloody Fingers, invade at least 3 times offline or online, return to Rose Church, choose one of three ways to soak the cloth with a maiden's blood, and speak with Varré to complete the questline and obtain the Bloody Finger item in Elden Ring.
