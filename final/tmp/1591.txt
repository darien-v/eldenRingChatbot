Godefroy the Grafted is found in Golden Lineage Evergaol. This is an optional boss, as players do not need to defeat it to advance in Elden Ring.\n\nElden Ring Godefroy the Grafted Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Altus Plateau\nMultiplayer is not allowed for this boss\nYou cannot summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Godefroy the Grafted Location\nFrom the Altus Plateau Site of Grace, head northeast into the valley, then switch back south through a tunnel under the main road. On the other side of this tunnel, you'll see the Golden Lineage Evergaol. Activate the evergaol with one Stonesword Key to fight Godefroy. QUESTION
Why is Godefroy the Grafted considered an optional boss in Elden Ring and not required to defeat to advance in the game?

ANSWER
Players do not need to defeat Godefroy the Grafted to advance in Elden Ring because it is considered an optional boss.
