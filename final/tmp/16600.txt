CONTEXT
\nAxes are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Axes are heavy weapons that can be wielded with one hand. They are best used in close combat and provide wide variety of attacks.\nAxes can be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, so play around with them and see what you like, as you acquire more.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, the speed and distance of some attacks has been increased and the recovery time has been reduced. Also the stagger damage of Axes’ guard counters has been increased. PVP's poise damage has also been reduced with this patch.\n\nElden Ring Axes Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Axes in Elden Ring.
