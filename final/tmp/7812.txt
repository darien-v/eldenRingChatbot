Agheel's Flame spell conjures head of Agheel to spew fire from above.\n\nSuperior incantation of Dragon Communion.Channels the power of the flying dragon Agheel.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to spew flaming breath fromabove.Charging extends duration. This incantation can be used while jumping.\nThe dead gazed at the skies over the lakes of Limgrave, praying that the dragons' flames would burn them to ash.\n\nWhere to find Agheel's Flame\nWhere to find Agheel's Flame :\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 2 Dragon Hearts. Players need to defeat Flying Dragon Agheel in order to unlock this Incantation. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Agheel's Flame Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 60\nDeals fire damage\nLike other named Breath incantations, this spell has long wind-up time of 2 seconds.\nDeals 18% more damage than Dragonfire, with slightly longer range but narrower spread.\nDuration can be extended to breathe again, and you can swivel your character while casting to spew in different directions.\nFull cast requires at least 78 FP but will use up to 144 FP with no additional benefit before any FP-conservating buffs.\nIt takes on average 5 seconds including initial wind-up to complete the full cast.\nCan be cast while jumping, and your character hovers slightly while using the spell.\nCannot be used on horseback.\nAs of patch Patch 1.09 , Roar Medallion now increases the damage of this incantation by approx.  10% in Player vs. Environment. 4% in Player vs. Player.\n\nDragon Kinght Written Guide\n\nMagic Dragonknight Written Guide\n\nDragon Dancer Build\n\nELDEN RING AGHEEL'S FLAME NOTES AND TIPS \n\nUpdated to Patch 1.10. See Patch Notes for details.\nIn the Colosseum, if the opponent is spacing the dragon breath out of its range or putting pressure by interrupting your dragon breath, use Swarm of Flies. Most opponents will run away from the attack, causing them to lose stamina and give you space to do a dragon breath.\nTo prevent the opponent from constantly interrupting your dragon breath, use Endure. With all dragon breaths having a 2.5-second wind-up and Endure having a 3-second duration it will give you enough time to do a dragon breath in the air. QUESTION
How can players unlock the Agheel's Flame Incantation in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can unlock Agheel's Flame Incantation by defeating Flying Dragon Agheel and purchasing it at the Dragon Communion Altar in the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 2 Dragon Hearts.
