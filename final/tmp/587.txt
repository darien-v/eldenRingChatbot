\n\n Minor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield) \n\nA Minor Erdtree enveloped in corruption, standing in the blizzards of the Consecrated Snowfield.\n\nMinor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield) is a Location in Elden Ring. The Minor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield) is found in the northeastern section of the Consecrated Snowfield. Players can reach this location by taking the Waygate found above the cliffs east of Albinauric Rise or crossing the frozen lake on foot or horseback towards Ordina and then heading southeast.\nThe Consecrated Snowfield itself is a secret area found on the lower plains of the Mountaintops of the Giants and can only be accessed by using the Grand Lift of Rold after obtaining the Haligtree Medallion (Left) and Haligtree Medallion (Right).\n\nMinor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield) Map\nThe Minor Erdtree of the Consecrated Snowfield is found across the frozen ravine, in the northeast. It can be found in the north, behind the tree. It exudes a red/orange aura from its body but is otherwise identical to the Erdtree Avatar in terms of behavior and moveset. Defeating it awards the Thorny Cracked Tear and Ruptured Crystal Tear as well as 160,000  Runes.\nThere is a corpse under a small tree southwest of the Erdtree that contains 3x Tarnished Golden Sunflower and another corpse bent over an overgrown root north of that with 4x Warming Stone. QUESTION
Describe the boss fight against the Putrid Avatar in the Minor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield).

ANSWER
The boss fight against the Putrid Avartar in the Minor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield) is a variant of the Erdtree Avatar, located behind the tree, exuding a red/orange aura, with behavior and moveset identical to the Erdtree Avatar, rewards Thorny Cracked Tear, Ruptured Crystal Tear, and 160,000 Runes upon defeat.
