\n\n Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 87\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons spirit of Dolores the Sleeping Arrow \n\nDolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet are used to summon the spirit of Dolores the Sleeping Arrow\n\nOne of Seluvis's favorite puppets. Use to summon the spirit of Dolores the Sleeping Arrow  Spirit of a handsome archer who dressed in the style of a man. Called the Silent Hunter by some, she fires St. Trina's arrows from her shortbow.  Dolores once belonged to the Roundtable Hold, where she was both a critic and a friend of Gideon the All-Knowing. It was because of her that he and Seluvis went their separate ways.\n\nWhere to find Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet\nWhere to Find Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet\n\nCan be chosen from Preceptor Seluvis after finding his puppet collection in a hidden basement within the region, and having chosen to give Seluvis's Potion to Nepheli Loux. \n\nIf you chose a different puppet at the time, then you can purchase this one from him for 5 Star Shards afterwards.This shop is still available if you interact with his body should he die.\n\nIf Seluvis's Potion is given to anyone else other than Nepheli Loux then you can find this Spirit Ashes on the body of  Pidia after progressing Ranni's questline far enough for Seluvis to perish resulting in the puppets Pidia were given by Seluvis to turn against him. His corpse can be looted for this Ash and Pidia's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet Notes and Trivia\n\nA possible reference to Pharis from Dark Souls.\nHer sleep arrows can be of good use against the Godskin Duo, as they are vulnerable to Sleep.\n\nElden Ring Spirits\n\nAlbinauric Ashes  ♦  Ancestral Follower Ashes  ♦  Ancient Dragon Knight Kristoff Ashes  ♦  Archer Ashes  ♦  Avionette Soldier Ashes  ♦  Azula Beastman Ashes  ♦  Banished Knight Engvall Ashes  ♦  Banished Knight Oleg Ashes  ♦  Battlemage Hugues Ashes  ♦  Blackflame Monk Amon Ashes  ♦  Bloodhound Knight Floh  ♦  Clayman Ashes  ♦  Cleanrot Knight Finlay Ashes  ♦  Crystalian Ashes  ♦  Demi-Human Ashes  ♦  Depraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes  ♦  Dung Eater Puppet  ♦  Fanged Imp Ashes  ♦  Finger Maiden Therolina Puppet  ♦  Fire Monk Ashes  ♦  Giant Rat Ashes  ♦  Glintstone Sorcerer Ashes  ♦  Godrick Soldier Ashes  ♦  Greatshield Soldier Ashes  ♦  Haligtree Soldier Ashes  ♦  Jarwight Puppet  ♦  Kindred of Rot Ashes  ♦  Land Squirt Ashes  ♦  Latenna The Albinauric  ♦  Leyndell Soldier Ashes  ♦  Lhutel the Headless  ♦  Lone Wolf Ashes  ♦  Mad Pumpkin Head Ashes  ♦  Man-Serpent Ashes  ♦  Marionette Soldier Ashes  ♦  Mausoleum Soldier Ashes  ♦  Mimic Tear Ashes  ♦  Miranda Sprout Ashes  ♦  Nepheli Loux Puppet  ♦  Nightmaiden & Swordstress Puppets  ♦  Noble Sorcerer Ashes  ♦  Nomad Ashes  ♦  Omenkiller Rollo  ♦  Oracle Envoy Ashes  ♦  Page Ashes  ♦  Perfumer Tricia Ashes  ♦  Putrid Corpse Ashes  ♦  Radahn Soldier Ashes  ♦  Raya Lucaria Soldier Ashes  ♦  Redmane Knight Ogha Ashes  ♦  Rotten Stray Ashes  ♦  Skeletal Bandit Ashes  ♦  Skeletal Militiaman Ashes  ♦  Soldjars of Fortune Ashes  ♦  Spirit Jellyfish Ashes  ♦  Stormhawk Deenh  ♦  Twinsage Sorcerer Ashes  ♦  Vulgar Militia Ashes  ♦  Wandering Noble Ashes  ♦  Warhawk Ashes  ♦  Winged Misbegotten Ashes\n\n

QUESTION
Where can Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet be found?

ANSWER
Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet can be found by choosing from Preceptor Seluvis's puppet collection after finding it in a hidden basement, or by purchasing it from him for 5 Star Shards if you initially chose a different puppet.
