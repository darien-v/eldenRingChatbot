QUESTION
Where can the Greatbow weapon be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Greatbow weapon can be found by chance from Redmane Knight in Fort Gael, or from a Leyndell Knight in Altus Plateau near the Altus Highway Junction, and also from a chest at the Highway Lookout Tower in Altus Plateau.
