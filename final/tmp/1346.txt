Dodge through its attacks and attack back as hard as you can, dodging forward whenever it swings or rises on its hind legs. Most of its attacks will pass over you, giving you plenty of time to deal damage. Blocking is risky since its attacks do massive Stamina damage.\nBreak its Stance: Although the Runebear has relatively high Stance, once it breaks it'll be knocked out for long enough to get in a few attacks and a critical hit. This may make it worthwhile to use charged heavy attacks, jump attacks, or other means of dealing lots of stance damage.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nSince the Runebear is safest up close, your best bet as a spellcaster is to use short-range spells and follow the same strategy as melee players. Bow users who are unwilling to switch to melee will have quite a challenge. Use a Shortbow for its attack speed and fire arrows at point-blank range.\n\nRunebear Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nClaw Maul\nRaises its claw and slashes it forward. This attack has a deceptively large hitbox and excellent tracking\nIf you're underneath the Runebear, this attack will miss. Otherwise, dodge in when the paw reaches the top its arc\n\nSmash\nRises briefly onto its hind legs with its left paw raised, then slams it into the ground\nThis attack has limited range, so back away or move around the back of the Runebear\n\nBite\nThe bear opens its mouth and lunges forward\nRoll into the bear, trying to end up in the safer area underneath it\n\nLunge\nLeaps through the air towards its target, covering a lot of distance\nRoll into the bear, trying to end up in the safer area underneath it\n\nCharge\nDucks down and charges forward. This attack has nearly no windup and covers a significant distance. \nThis is the most unpredictable attack. Once you see the bear turn around, prepare to dodge to the sides, otherwise try and block.\n\nDouble Paw Smash\nRises onto its hind legs with both paws raised, then slams them both into the ground creating a shockwave\nIf you've got some distance, you can jump over the shockwave. Otherwise, roll in once its paws are level with its face\n\nBear Hug\nRises onto its hind legs with its arms spread wide, walks forward a bit, and grabs you with both arms\nRoll once it's taken a few steps, or simply run away. If you get grabbed, mash buttons to free yourself quicker.\n\nBeast's Roar\nThe Runbear fires off a sonic projectile from its mouth. Commonly used at a distance\nDodge or block the attack. If you're up close, it will use this attack less frequently.\n\nRunebear Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nA bug may occur when the Runebear grabs the player.
