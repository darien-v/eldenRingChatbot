Taker's Flames allows the user to engulf the blade with blasphemous flames, stealing HP from those it touches. Faith.\nThis skill can heal Mimic Tear Ashes when the Blasphemous Blade is wielded by the Spirit Ashes. \nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Damage Detection has been added to
