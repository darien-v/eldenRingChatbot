\n\n Storm Blade \n\n        Lost skill of Stormveil. Surround armament with shearing storm winds that can be fired forward. Can be fired in rapid succession. \n\nStorm Blade  is a Skill in Elden Ring. Storm Blade surrounds armament with winds that can be fired forward. Can be fired in rapid succession. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Storm Blade\n\nSold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 1,800 . Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring Storm Blade Guide\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Storm Blade.\nFP Cost: 10\nInitial FP Cost: 10 (any chained casts cost 6 FP)\nDeals 10 stance damage (weapon hit)\nThis Skill not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nUsable on Swords (Colossal Weapons and Twinblades excepted).\nWhile only dealing moderate damage, it can nevertheless be an effective early game ranged tool for melee-oriented builds\nThis Skill is unaffected by the base damage and scaling of your weapon. It has its own base damage and Strength and Dexterity scaling, which increases with upgrade level and can be modified with infusions.\nDeals no additional damage if both the weapon and the projectile appear to connect.\n\nNOTES: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nMotion speed and range of the blade have been increased.\nDamage detection to the weapon part has been added.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\n\nOther notes and tips go here.\n\nStorm Blade Demonstration\n\nStormblade Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Storm Blade Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How to effectively use Storm Blade in combat?

ANSWER
Storm Blade is an Ash of War Skill in Elden Ring, surrounded by winds and fired forward, dealing moderate ranged damage. It is best suited for early game melee-oriented builds and unaffected by weapon base damage and scaling.
