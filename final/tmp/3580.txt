CONTEXT
\n\n Shield Crash \n\n        Two-hand the shield and charge forwards while maintaining guard. Weaker enemies will be shoved backwards and can even be staggered. Hold to extend the duration of the charge forwards. \n\nShield Crash is a Skill in Elden Ring. Shield Crash is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, it can be used on all Shields. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Shield Crash in Elden Ring\n\nAsh of War: Shield Crash is dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in Lux Ruins, Altus Plateau. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Shield Crash Guide\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Shield Crash.\nFP Cost: 12\nThis Skill is Chargeable.\n\nDespite this feature, it appears unaffected by the Axe Talisman\n\nWhen compared to Shield Bash, Shield Crash has a moment at the beginning of its cast that leaves the player unguarded, whereas Shield Bash sustains guard for its entire duration. Shield Crash overall deals much more damage, has greater range for chasing foes, hits multiple times while guard is sustained, and only costs 2 FP more than Shield Bash.Ultimately, Shield Bash is shorter and safer, by virtue of having guard sustained the entire time and due to being shorter therefore less of an investment if the enemy dodges. However, due to its chargeable nature, Shield Crash does have the option to invest less time into its total attack, giving the user a degree of on-demand versatility. \n\nAn uncharged use of this skill appears to deal roughly 40% more damage than Shield Bash. When fully charged and if all hits connect, Shield Crash can deal around 200% of Shield Bash's damage.\nDue to the fact that Shield Crash hits multiple times before dealing its most powerful strike last, the skill can take advantage of affinities and innate status effects of shields; for example, using a Cold Spiked Palisade Shield +20, which deals 141 Frostbite buildup and 65 Hæmorrhage buildup, the total damage output when comparing Shield Crash to Shield Bash with this setup is 5x higher with the former, due to Frostbite increasing damage vulnerability and Hæmorrhage dealing a large amount of damage.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: The amount of status buildup when used with weapons that have status effects has been reduced.\n\nShield Crash Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Shield Crash Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why should one consider using Shield Crash over Shield Bash in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Shield Crash should be considered over Shield Bash in Elden Ring due to its greater damage output, range for chasing foes, ability to hit multiple times while guarding, and increased total attack versatility despite initial unguarded moment.
