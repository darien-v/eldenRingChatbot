\n\n Carian Retaliation \n\n        Swing the shield to dispel incoming sorceries and incantations, transforming the magic into retaliatory glintblades. Can also be used in the same way as a regular parry. \n\nCarian Retaliation is a Skill in Elden Ring. Carian Retaliation dispels incoming sorceries and incantations, transforming the magic into retaliatory glintblades.\nSee also Carian Retaliation for the corresponding spell version of this skill.\n\nHow to get Carian Retaliation in Elden Ring\n\nPurchase from Pidia, Carian Servant: 3000\n\nElden Ring Carian Retaliation Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Carian Retaliation.\nFP Cost: 8 \n\nOnly costs FP when successfully parrying a spell (not a physical attack).\n\nCarian Retaliation has 4 start-up frames and 6 parry frames at 30 FPS. Double the values for data at 60 FPS.\nCreates a blue field in front of your shield. If a magical projectile enters this field, it's absorbed and converted into a glintblade arch above your head. Parrying a regular attack costs no FP, while parrying a magical projectile costs 8 FP.\nWorks on your own projectiles as well, such as the ones from Glintstone Scrap or Wraith-Calling Bell.\nThe glintblades' damage is affected by the affinity and upgrade level of the shield.\nSee Parrying for detailed information on the mechanic and a comparison of different parry skills.\n\nClick below for Carian Retaliation recovery frame data. Note that left-hand attacks are only possible after parrying with a shield if you're holding that shield in your right hand.\n\nSee Carian Retaliation
