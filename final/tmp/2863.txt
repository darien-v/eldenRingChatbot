\n\n Margit's Shackle \n\nEffect \n\n        Binds \n       Margit and  \n       Morgott two times per battle \n\nScaling \n\n        - \n\nFP Cost\n-\n\nMargit's Shackle is a Tool in Elden Ring. Tools are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.\n\nA fetish bathed in golden magic. Shackles were used to bind the accursed people called the Omen, and these ones were made to keep a particular Omen under strictest confinement. Though faint, the shackles still retain vestiges of power — enough to trap the once-bound Margit on earth, if only for a short time.\n\nWhere to find Margit's Shackle in Elden Ring\nCan be purchased from Patches for  5000 Runes in Murkwater Cave. [Map Link] After he becomes available as a merchant. Or it can be purchased from the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold, after giving them Patches's Bell Bearing. Video Location\nMargit's Shackle use in Elden Ring\nMargit's Shackle, when used during one of Margit's boss fights, causes Margit to violently slam against the floor, inflicting no damage, but stunning him for a brief moment. This effect will also interrupt anything Margit was doing at the time. The effect of this item can only be activated twice during the boss fight and only during Margit's first phase. Attempting to use the shackle otherwise will have the player go through the animation, but will have no effect whatsoever.\n\nElden Ring Margit's Shackle Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 Margit's Shackle.\nYou can store up to 600 Margit's Shackle.\nSell Value:  N/A\nMargit's Shackle will also work similarly during the boss fight with Morgott, the Omen King.\nWhile it might be an oversight, Margit's Shackle (as well as Mohg's Shackle) can be used to dispel nearby illusory walls and trigger some dungeon traps that require striking, in particular, it can be used to trigger the fire pillar trap within Auriza Hero's Grave from as far away as the small area beside a pit, just before the incline leading to the dungeon's first Chariot, allowing the three Chariots in the dungeon to collide and be destroyed with considerably little effort. Supposedly, this effect is due to the shackle releasing an invisible, large range explosion that deals no damage, but hits everything in its range. Please note, Mohg's Shackle has less range than Margit's Shackle. This is likely due to the fact that you can encounter Margit once out on the field, so they gave his shackle a greater range to compensate for this.\n\nElden Ring Reusable\n\nAcademy Magic QUESTION
Why is Margit's Shackle only able to be activated twice during the boss fight and only during Margit's first phase in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Margit's Shackle can only be activated twice during Margit's first phase in Elden Ring to stun Margit briefly without causing damage, interrupting his actions, due to its design and intended gameplay mechanics.
