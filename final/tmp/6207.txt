\n\n Kick \n\n        Push an enemy back with a high kick. \n       Effective against enemies who are guarding, and can break a foe's stance. \n\nSometimes a simple tool is the most effective.  \n\nKick is a Skill in Elden Ring. Kick is a skill that can be equipped with a weapon and can push back enemies during combat. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Kick\n\nDefault skill of the  Short Sword, Hand Ballista  Pulley Crossbow, and Whip\nDefault skill for the Hoslow's Petal Whip\nDefault skill for the Hammer\nDefault skill for the Soldier's Crossbow\nDefault skill for the Crepus's Black-Key Crossbow\nDefault skill for the Full Moon Crossbow\nAsh of War: Kick is sold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 800 . Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring Kick Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Kick.\nFP Cost: 0\nDeals 6 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nKick is also accessible by being bare-handed in both the player's left and right hand.\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Poise damage and stamina attack power against guarding enemies has been increased.\n\nKick Demonstration\n\nElden Ring QUESTION
What are the different ways to acquire the Kick skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Default skill of the Short Sword, Hand Ballista, Pulley Crossbow, and Whip, Default skill for the Hoslow's Petal Whip, Default skill for the Hammer, Default skill for the Soldier's Crossbow, Default skill for the Crepus's Black-Key Crossbow, Default skill for the Full Moon Crossbow, Sold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack, Can be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.
