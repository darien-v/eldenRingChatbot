\n\n Arsenal Charm \n\nWeight\n1.5\n\nEffect \n\nRaises maximum \n       Equip Load by 15/17% \n\nArsenal Charm is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Arsenal Charm raises maximum Equip Load by 15/17%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats. The Great-Jar's Arsenal talisman is the strongest version.\n\nAn iron charm that resembles a mass of weaponry. Boosts maximum equipment load.\nThis talisman was derived from an unusual greatsword, once wielded by a hero hungry for vengeance.\n\nArsenal Charm Effect in Elden Ring\nArsenal Charm raises maximum Equip Load by 15/17%.\n\nArsenal Charm Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Arsenal Charm (+15%):\n\nGiven by Nepheli Loux, who's at the Roundtable Hold.\nHer initial location is inside a room near the Secluded Cell Site of Grace in Stormveil Castle. It's near the golden fog door that leads to Godrick the Grafted. When you find her, you must exhaust her dialogue so that she tells you that you can call her via a summoning sign to help you battle Godrick.\nNepheli moves to the Roundtable Hold after defeating Godrick, and you can find her at the northeast side, inside the library. Simply speak to her and she'll give you the talisman.\n\nWhere to find Arsenal Charm +1 (+17%):\n\nAltus Tunnel: In the second cavern, head through the tunnel in the south to find a wooden balcony overlooking a pit. Drop down on the left side to an overgrown root below. Follow the root going upwards to reach an upper balcony above where you can loot the talisman from a corpse. [Map link]\n\nElden Ring Arsenal Charm Notes & Tips\n\nSell value: 100\n+1 Variant sell
