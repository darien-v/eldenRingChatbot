Wielded by the avatars who protect the Minor Erdtrees. \n\nWhere to Find Rotten Staff in Elden Ring\nThe Rotten Staff weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nElphael, Brace of the Haligtree: Dropped by the Putrid Avatar patrolling the ring walkway in the east. To reach this walkway, head out of the Prayer Room Site of Grace and jump onto the second buttress (concrete beam) connecting to a tower. Descend the tower and use the ledges on the bottom to reach the ring walkway. QUESTION
How can someone acquire the Rotten Staff in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Rotten Staff can be acquired by defeating the Putrid Avatar on the ring walkway in Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree area, or by obtaining it as a drop from the other Putrid Avatar near the Inner Walls Site of Grace in Elden Ring.
