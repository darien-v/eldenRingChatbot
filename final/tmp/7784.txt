QUESTION
Why is Bestial Sling considered an effective alternative to standard melee weapons in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Bestial Sling is considered an effective alternative to standard melee weapons in Elden Ring due to its low FP cost, fast cast time, and capability to flinch low poise enemies at medium range, especially when paired with the Blue Dancer Charm.
