\n\n Ailing Village \n\nA small, abandoned village nestled within a forested area north of the Castle Morne Rampart. This village is host to long-dilapidated dwellings, inhabited only by maddened fanatics gripped by the Flame of Frenzy. \n\nAiling Village is a Location in Elden Ring. Ailing Village is found in the Weeping Peninsula. Players can find this village by heading north from the Castle Morne Rampart.\n\nAiling Village Map\nAiling Village is found in a forested area north of Castle Morne at the Weeping Peninsula. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Ailing Village\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Ailing Village\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nArteria Leaf\nRowa Fruit\nEye of Yelough\nErdleaf Flower\nGolden Rune (1)\nMushroom\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSacred Tear\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nFlame Crest Wooden Shield\nThe Flame of Frenzy\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this location\n\nElden Ring Ailing Village Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nFrenzied Villager\nGiant Rat\nLesser Alabaster Lord\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses at this location\n\nAiling Village Walkthrough\nIn this village, there is a large bonfire in the center where several Frenzied Villagers are found taking on strange poses, covering their flame-tinged eyes. There is a hut in the north where the Flame Crest Wooden Shield can be looted off of a corpse.\nDirectly south of the village is the ruins of the Callu Baptismal Church where several Frenzied Rats and a Giant Rat can be found. Players can pick up The Flame of Frenzy spell by a pillar on the left. Like most churches, you can find a Sacred Tear towards the front of the church at the foot of the main statue piece. \n\nElden Ring Ailing Village Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll
