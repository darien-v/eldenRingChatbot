Crucible Knight Siluria is one of the very few normal sized Bosses, they perform quick heavy attacks but they have not too much Health. It is found in Deeproot Depths. This is an optional boss as players don't need to defeat it to advance in Elden Ring.\nOther Crucible Knights appear in the Crucible Knight, Crucible Knight and Misbegotten Warrior, Tanith's Knight, and Crucible Knight Ordovis boss fights.\n\nElden Ring Crucible Knight Siluria Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: The Nameless Eternal City\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nWhere to Find Crucible Knight Siluria In Elden Ring\nHead northwest from the Nameless Eternal City Site of Grace down into the lower wetlands, then turn west and head around the base of the building. Turn northwest again towards the Deeproot Depths West spiritspring. [Map Link] You'll land directly in front of Siluria, who is standing in front of the huge tree branch. The Silures lived south of the Ordovices, which in turn may have inspired the name of Siluria's counterpart Ordovis. \n\nHer name also may reference the Silurian Period, a prehistoic epoch of life that occurred after the end of the Ordovician, much like the Crucible.\n\nCrucible Knight Siluria Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Bosses\n\nAbductor Virgins  ♦  Adan, Thief of Fire  ♦  Alabaster
