Cookbooks are records of crafting techniques that players can use to learn how to craft a wide variety of items.\n\nA record of crafting techniques left by a depraved perfumer.Contains perilous techniques no ordinary physician would attempt.\nAcquire the knowledge to craft the following:\n- Poison Spraymist- Bloodboil Aromatic\n\nPerfumer's Cookbook [2] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Perfumer's Cookbook [2]\n\nFound on a corpse sitting in a chair on one of the rooftops at The Shaded Castle, in Altus Plateau. From the Shaded Castle Inner Gate Site of Grace, head north and climb up the ladder on your left. Enter the dark library area and look for a ladder up, climb up to find the Cookbook.
