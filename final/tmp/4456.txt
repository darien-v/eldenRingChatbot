\n\n Ash of War: Spinning Strikes \n\nAffinity\nQuality\n\nSkill\nSpinning Strikes\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament\n\nAsh of War: Spinning Strikes is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Spinning Strikes provides Quality affinity and the Spinning Strikes Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Quality affinity and the following skill:\n"Spinning Strikes: Polearm skill that performs continuous spinning attacks. Hold to continue the attack. Can be followed up with a normal or strong attack. Nullifies projectiles such as arrows while spinning."\nUsable on polearms (great spears excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Spinning Strikes Location in Elden Ring\n\nWhen killed either at Castle Morne, or when he invades  at the Revenger's Shack, Edgar drops a Banished Knight's Halberd +8 with the Spinning Strikes Ash of War attached. The ash of war can be extracted from the weapon.\n\nAsh of War: Spinning Strikes Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Spinning Strikes.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Spears, Halberds, and Reapers.\n\nSpinning Strikes Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Spinning Strikes Notes & QUESTION
When and where can Edgar be killed to obtain the Banished Knight's Halberd +8 with the Spinning Strikes Ash of War attached?

ANSWER
Edgar can be killed either at Castle Morne or when he invades at the Revenger's Shack to obtain the Banished Knight's Halberd +8 with the Spinning Strikes Ash of War attached.
