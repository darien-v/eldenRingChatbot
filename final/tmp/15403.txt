QUESTION
What is the location in Elden Ring where you can find the Cipher Pata weapon?

ANSWER
Found in the side room to the left in the same area Mad Tongue Alberich invades at the Roundtable Hold. To get there, go up the big stairs at Roundtable Hold and jump off the balcony, then enter the door to the left and advance along the left side. At the end of the final room, turn right and loot it off the burnt corpse lying on the bed at the end.
