\n\n Nox Swordstress \n\nLocation\nNokron, Eternal CityNokstella, Eternal City\n\nDrops\n 391 - 635 RunesNox Swordstress Set\n\nNox Swordstress is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Nox Swordstresses are members of a cold-blooded race, quick-moving warriors able to dodge incoming player attacks. They wear loose flowing clothes and attack with a Nox Flowing Sword. They are the personal guards of Night Maidens. You may find some riding Giant Ants as steeds in Nokstella, Eternal City.\n\n Long ago, the Nox invoked the ire of the Greater Will, and were banished deep underground. Now they live under a false night sky, in eternal anticipation of their liege. Of the coming age of the stars.
