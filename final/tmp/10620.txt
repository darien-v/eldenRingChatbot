\n\n Nox Monk \n\nLocation\n Nokstella, Eternal City \n\nDrops\n 635 RunesNox Monk Set\n\nNox Monk is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. They are part of the Nox and wield a Nox Flowing Hammer, which they use to attack foes from range.\n\n Long ago, the Nox invoked the ire of the Greater Will, and were banished deep underground. Now they live under a false night sky, in eternal anticipation of their liege. Of the coming age of the stars. And their Lord of Night.\n\nElden Ring Nox Monk Locations\nWhere to Find Nox Monk:\n\nNokstella, Eternal City\n\nElden Ring Nox Monk Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 635\n\nNox Monk Hood\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nNox Monk Armor\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nNox Bracelets\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nNox Greaves\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nNox Monk Notes & Tips\n\nNox Monks use a Nox Flowing Hammer but do not drop it.\nThey can turn their club into a different weapon, two shields with spikes in the middle, resembling the Pierce Shield from Dark Souls.\nThey can also briefly turn their Hammer into a large silver manifer when the player gets too close, slamming it down on the player.
