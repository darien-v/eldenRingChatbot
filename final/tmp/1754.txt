Bloodhound Knight Set, is a sturdy set made for the Bloodhound Knights for Damage Negation. The Bloodhound Knights are trained as hunters and known to be unshakable trackers. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Bloodhound Knight Set in Elden Ring\nDrops in Gelmir Hero's Grave - Mt. Gelmir.\nThe entire Bloodhound Knight Set drops in Gelmir Hero's Grave - Mt. Gelmir. Dropped by the Bloodhound Knight guarding the Gelmir Knight Set pickup. The set must then be picked up from the corpse of the Knight. See it on the Elden Ring Map here.\nTo reach the knight, drop down to the rafters where the second chariot can be ridden, but instead jump to the window just besides, climb the ladder down and the Knight will be in front of you.\n\nBloodhound Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nBloodhound Knight Helm\nBloodhound Knight Armor\nBloodhound Knight Gauntlets\nBloodhound Knight Greaves\n\nBloodhound Knight Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Bloodhound Knight Armor can be altered to the Bloodhound Knight Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nAs many of the comments mention, the helmet of this set is a bascinet with a "hounskull" visor attachment. As the name suggests, it is called this because it resembles the face of a hound.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
