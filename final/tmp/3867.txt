\n\n Corpse Wax Cutter \n\n        Lost skill of ancient heroes. \n       Lift the sword up high to release the power of corpse wax and launch it forwards as a blade-like projectile. \n\nCorpse Wax Cutter is a Skill in Elden Ring. Corpse Wax Cutter is a unique skill on the Gargoyle's Blackblade and grants the user the ability to launch the attached weapon as a projectile to damage foes at a further range. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Corpse Wax Cutter\n\nDefault skill on the Gargoyle's Blackblade.\n\nElden Ring Corpse Wax Cutter Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 16\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill can be Parried\nSaps enemy HP for 2 seconds\nDeals 24 stance damage.\nDamage type is Holy Damage. Damage can be increased by Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear and Sacred Scorpion Charm, as well as other generic attack buffs like Golden Vow. However, Flame Grant Me Strength does NOT do anything since this skill deals neither Physical Damage nor Fire Damage.\nAlbeit sapping enemy HP like Blade of Death, Destined Death, and Black Blade, this skill does NOT inflict -10% max HP debuff.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nFP consumption has been reduced from 20 to 16.\nMotion speed, range and speed of the blade have been increased.\nDamage detection to the weapon part has been added.\n\nBlasphemous Herald Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Corpse Wax Cutter Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
