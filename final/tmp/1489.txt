QUESTION
Where can one find the Radahn Foot Soldier Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Pieces of the Radahn Foot Soldier Set drop from Radahn Foot Soldiers found in many of the fortified areas and encampments in Caelid. The Foot Soldier Gauntlets and Foot Soldier Greaves can only drop from Godrick Foot Soldiers.
