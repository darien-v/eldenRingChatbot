CONTEXT
\n\n Barrage \n\n        Archery Skill using a bow held horizontally. Ready the bow, then fire off a rapid succession of shots faster than the eye can see. \n\nBarrage is a Skill in Elden Ring. Barrage is a special Archery skill using a bow held horizontally.\n\nHow to get Barrage\n\nAsh of War: Barrage can be found at Mt. Gelmir, dropped by a Teardrop Scarab found in the narrow channel north of Seethewater River. [Map Link]\nDefault Skill for most Light Bows, excepting the Composite Bow.\nDefault Skill for the Black Bow, notable for not being a Light Bow.\n\nBuilds with Barrage\n\nGolden Eye Written Guide\n\nBloody Bowman Written Guide\n\nBlack Arrow Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Barrage Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Barrage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nFP Cost: 0 on activation (stance), 2 per shot.\nOn activation, the user assumes a stance, preparing the bow. When inputting light or heavy attacks, the user quickly shoots the respective arrow from their slots, consuming FP (and stamina while in combat). \n\nWhen the stance is being held, stamina regen is reduced by approximately 80%.\nWhen the stance is held, the user can still move at a slower pace.\n\nProvides a 33% damage penalty per shot, compared to normal shots (at same range), in exchange for a higher fire rate (approximately 150% faster shooting speed compared to normal shots). \n\nThe falloff distance from arrows remains mostly the same, unless very large distances are taken into account.\nStamina consumption is the same as normal shots.\n\nThe status buildup from arrows also receive a slight penalty per shot when using this skill. \n\nHowever, the faster shooting speed renders this penalty mostly irrelevant.\n\nIf used without FP:\n\nThe damage penalty is higher (75% less damage compared to a normal shot).\nStatus buildup from arrows is significantly lower (takes aproximately double the amount of arrows for same buildup).\n\nBarrage Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric QUESTION
What are the benefits and drawbacks of using the Barrage skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Benefits: Higher fire rate (approximately 150% faster shooting speed compared to normal shots), same stamina consumption as normal shots, mostly unchanged arrow falloff distance.
Drawbacks: 33% damage penalty per shot, slight penalty on status buildup per shot, higher damage penalty and lower status buildup when used without FP.
