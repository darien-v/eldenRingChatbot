Traveling Maiden Set is a Lightweight armor that does not offer much in the way of physical protection, but boasts superior Focus and Vitality resistances. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Traveling Maiden Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe entire Traveling Maiden Set can be found on a corpse inside the Shack of the Lofty, in the northern Mountaintops of the Giants.
