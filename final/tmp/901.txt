\n\n Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree \n\nLocation\nMiquella's Haligtree\n\nDrops\n200,000Loretta's MasteryLoretta's War Sickle\n\nHP\n13,397\n\nStronger VS\n\nMagic\n\nFire\n\nHemorrhage\n\nFrostbite\n\nWeaker to\n\nLightning\n\nLoretta, Knight of the Haligtree is a Greater Foe Boss in Elden Ring. This proud knight was previously in service to the Carian royal family. This is an optional boss as players don't need to defeat her to advance in Elden Ring, though they do need to defeat her to reach Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree and face Malenia, Blade of Miquella.\nSee also Royal Knight Loretta.\nAt some point, Loretta set out on a journey to find a safe haven for the Albinauric people, until she finally came across Miquella's Haligtree. She is rumored to be an Albinauric herself. The blue glintstone of the war sickle she was given for being the personal guard of the Carian family has been replaced with the unalloyed gold often attributed to the young god Miquella.  \n\nLoretta, once a royal Carian Knight, went on a journey in search of a haven for Albinaurics, and determined that the Haligtree was their best chance for eventual salvation.\n\nElden Ring Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree Boss\n\nNot optional for Blessing of Despair ending, optional for other.\nClosest Site of Grace: Haligtree Town\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree Location\nThe first time you meet Royal Knight Loretta, you'll have to come from the Haligtree Town Plaza Site of Grace. Head southeast along the rooftops. After the roof with the Rotten Putrid Corpses, drop down to the lower level and head into the door in front of you. Turn east and head out across a wooden bridge, then double back northwest across another bridge to open the shortcut back to the Haligtree Town Site of Grace.\nFrom the Haligtree Town Site of Grace, head southeast to the elevator down to the 2nd lower level. Head across the wooden bridge, head around the gazebo, and cross the bridge to the northeast. Loretta is through the large gateway just past this bridge. [Map Link]\nLoretta, Knight of the Haligtree Combat information\n\nHealth: 13,397 HP \nDefense: 122\nStance: 80\nParryable: Yes\nImmune to critical attacks. Naturally, the language employed in the description is meant to make the reader wonder if there is some truth to this rumor, especially considering that Loretta's features and skin color are always obscured. She does share some similarities with them, such as being a female warrior, having proficiency with the bow, riding a steed and wearing silver/blue armor. Albinaurics are also capable of Glintstone Sorcery and are seen in the employ of the Carian Royals. However, Loretta bleeds red, not silver, in her in-person boss battle.\nMetatextually, Loretta's lore seems to be inspired by the tale of Moses, who led the persecuted Hebrews of Egypt into a promised land across the sea, similar to how Loretta lead the persecuted Albinaurics to the Consecrated Snowfield across the mountians.\nLoretta, Knight of the Haligtree makes the same grunting sounds as a Tree Sentinel. This is likely an oversight, as they share almost all of the same melee moves. It is fitting, though, considering she is guarding her own Tree.\nLoretta was a former Carian Knight, which explains her spirit version in Caria Manor. She eventually left to search for a sanctuary for the Albinaurics, and eventually entered Miquella’s service, tasked with guarding the Haligtree from intruders. She even replaced the glintstone in her weapon with unalloyed gold, further cementing her loyalty. She also adds a golden crest to the helmet of her Royal Knight armor, which bears the likeness of the Hailigtree.\n\nLoretta, Knight of the Haligtree Combo Chart\n\nLoretta, Knight of the Haligtree QUESTION
How can players reach Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree's location in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can reach Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree's location in Elden Ring by heading southeast from the Haligtree Town Site of Grace to the elevator down to the 2nd lower level, then proceeding through the large gateway just past the bridge.
