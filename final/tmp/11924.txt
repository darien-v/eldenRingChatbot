QUESTION
Why do Exile Soldiers defend the inside areas of their castles and forts in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Exile Soldiers defend the inside areas of their castles and forts in Elden Ring because they were soldiers sent to the penal colonies and were required to keep their faces hidden.
