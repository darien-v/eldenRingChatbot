QUESTION
Why are Stanching Boluses effective in alleviating impending blood loss in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Stanching Boluses in Elden Ring are effective in alleviating impending blood loss because blood loss escalates gradually in the game, causing great damage once a certain threshold is reached, and taking one of these boluses in a timely fashion can help avoid such severe damage.
