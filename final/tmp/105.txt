Upon reaching the area, there will be several Lesser Wormface roaming the ruins. There's also an underground chamber, and there are usually three Lesser Wormface guarding the entrance so you'll have to take them out. Going down the stairs will lead you to a door, open the door and then you'll see a chest inside, the chest contains the Wrath of Gold.\nRun back up the stairs, there's a smaller ruined building within the area, you will find the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [19] just laying on the ground beside a wall. You will also encounter a Teardrop Scarab spawn in the area, kill it to obtain the Ash of War: Golden Slam.\nThere's also a building nearby, beware of the enemies inside, this is where you can obtain the Icon Shield.\nThe Soft Cotton can be located near the ruins as well, guarded by snail like enemies.\n\nElden Ring Woodfolk Ruins Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring QUESTION
How can I efficiently navigate through the Woodfolk Ruins to find all the valuable items and defeat the enemies guarding them?

ANSWER
Efficiently navigate through the Woodfolk Ruins by first defeating the Lesser Wormface creatures, then obtaining the Icon Shield from the building, and obtaining the Invigorating White Cured Meat, Soft Cotton, Erdleaf Flower, and Gold-Tinged Excrement from various locations in the ruins.
