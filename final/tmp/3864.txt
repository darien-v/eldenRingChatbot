\n\n Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot \n\nEffect \n\n Temporarily boosts item discovery (+50) \n\nScaling \n\n        - \n\nFP Cost\n-\n\nSilver-Pickled Fowl Foot is a Consumable Item in Elden Ring. Most Consumables grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects or applying temporary buffs and debuffs, while others can be thrown at enemies to deal damage. When used, the foot increases Discovery by 50 for 3 minutes. The effect is not removed when resting at a site of grace, however the effect will be removed upon dying or using fast travel.\n\nFour-toed foot of a fowl, pickled in a silvery medicinal solution. Craftable item.\nTemporarily boosts item discovery.\nSince old times, the needy would scrape the meat clean even from a fowl's claw.\n\nWhere to find Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot in Elden Ring\n\nTwo can be found at the start of the game. From the First Step site of grace, turn around and go behind the building and to the right side of a large broken pillar. Look down and see a small ledge below. Carefully drop down to the ledge and a find a corpse holding two of them.\nOne can be looted from a corpse located at the west side at the top level of the wine cellar of Stormveil Castle. You must have the Rusty Key to unlock the room that has a ladder that you can climb. The corpse is resting on a wall, near a door, and is being guarded by a weak Commoner enemy.\nOne can be found at the northern side of the broken bridge that leads to Raya Lucaria Academy, near a tent. QUESTION
Where can I find Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot can be found at various locations in Elden Ring, such as in the starting area, Stormveil Castle, Raya Lucaria Academy, Altus Plateau, Sage's Cave, and can also be purchased from Gatekeeper Gostoc or Twin Maiden Husks at Roundtable Hold after offering Gostoc's Bell Bearing.
