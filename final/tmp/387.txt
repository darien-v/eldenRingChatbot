\n\n Leyndell Colosseum \n\n        The practice had died out by the age of King Consort Radagon, but remains of the arenas where ritual combat took place can still be found in every land. \n\nLeyndell Colosseum (Also known as The Royal Colosseum) is a Location in Elden Ring. The Leyndell Colosseum is located in the Altus Plateau and it is the largest Colosseum in the Lands Between.\n\nLeyndell Colosseum Map \n\nThe Colosseum in Leyndell can be seen immediately when you enter the city from East Capital Rampart, in Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nUnlocking the Leyendell Colosseum \nPlayers will need defeat a difficult Draconic Tree Sentinel on the northern side gate upon reaching the Royal Capital. Once defeated, cross the city by using the dragon's body and climbing onto it's wing on the west side, then dropping onto the ramparts. Cross the bridge to find the Colosseum sitting on top of the large hill. Duel Combat format and fighting at the Lyndell arena will be unlocked to players once claiming the Colosseum. \nIt is recommended that players should be around Level 80-90 before sieging the capital.\n\nLeyndell Colosseum PvP\nLeyndell Colosseum features Duel Mode. Two Tarnished face off in a duel to the death, and without the ability to respawn.\n\nNPCs and Merchants in Leyndell Colosseum\n\nGoldmask and Brother Corhyn can both be found near the Colosseum, on a cliff in view of the Erdtree.
