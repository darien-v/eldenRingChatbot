\n\n Lightning Ram \n\n        Skill inspired by tumbling rams. Let out a bleat, then tumble forwards, clad in lightning. Tumbles can be repeated in rapid succession. \n\nLightning Ram is a Skill in Elden Ring. Lightning Ram is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War that can be used on all melee armaments.\n\nHow to get Lightning Ram\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Ram - It can be found at Altus Plateau, just south of Sainted Hero's Grave. It drops from a Teardrop Scarab in the area. QUESTION
What are the effects and uses of the Lightning Ram skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Lightning Ram skill in Elden Ring allows for rapid tumbling forwards, decreasing character profile to dodge attacks while dealing scaled damage based on weapon level and dexterity.
