\n\n Isolated Divine Tower \n\nA Divine Tower that is inaccessible by normal means. This tower hides the secret to restoring the power of Malenia's Great Rune.\n\nIsolated Divine Tower is a Location in Elden Ring. It is one of the six Divine Towers. The Isolated Divine Tower can be reached by taking the waygate from the Divine Bridge in Leyndell Royal Capital. [Map Link] After one of the last updates, you now activate the warp by using the elevator in the Fortified Manor in Leyndell. Reaching this Site of Grace can be done in two ways, via the chest found at Tower of Return or through the Fortified Manor in the capital by going through a closed door in the main hall and taking the elevator. At the end of the game the only way is through the Tower of Return.\n\nIsolated Divine Tower Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nIsolated Divine Tower Walkthrough\nAscend the tower to restore Malenia's QUESTION
Why is it necessary to kill Malenia before being able to access the Isolated Divine Tower in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Malenia must be killed before being able to access the Isolated Divine Tower in Elden Ring because only after defeating her, the doors to the tower can be opened.
