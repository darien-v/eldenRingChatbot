QUESTION
Why is the Briar Set armor considered useful for exploring locations with undead skeletons in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Briar Set armor is considered useful for exploring locations with undead skeletons in Elden Ring because a single piece triggers Malenia's Great Rune on hit and more damage per armor piece worn, enhancing combat effectiveness against them.
