QUESTION
Why does the Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing (5) not exist despite the normal weapons only going up to 24 and being covered by the 4th level bell bearing?

ANSWER
The Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing (5) does not exist because normal weapons only go up to 24, which is within the range covered by the 4th level bell bearing.
