\n\n Recusant Henricus \n\nLocation\nEast of Stormhill Shack\n\nDrops\n 338 Runes (NG+)Hammer Talisman \n\nRecusant Henricus is an Invader enemy in Elden Ring. Recusant Henricus manifests abruptly before engaging in combat, using a large mace and wearing full body armor. Recusant Henricus is found North-East of Stormhill Shack.\n\nAn invader serving the Volcano Manor, tasked with hunting down fellow Tarnished. His large mace can make quick of any unfortunate targets.\n\nElden Ring Recusant Henricus Locations\nWhere to Find Recusant Henricus:\n\nNortheast of Stormhill Shack, when approaching the gates of a large building. [Map Link]As with most invader NPCs, he won't be able to invade you if you cleared all the area bosses in Limgrave (Tree Sentinel, Flying Dragon Agheel, Tibia Mariner in Summonwater Village, Mad Pumpkin Head in Waypoint Ruins, and Margit, the Fell Omen)\n\nElden Ring Recusant Henricus Drops\n\nHammer Talisman\n\nRecusant Henricus Notes & Tips\n\nWears the Greathelm and the Eye Surcoat, possibly alluding that he was an agent for Gideon Ofnir, tasked with spying on the Volcano Manor. His chest piece being found in Rykard’s arena would suggest that he was devoured by the serpent.\nUses a Great Mace with the Eruption Ash of War.\nWill not invade if the player has talked to Tanith,
