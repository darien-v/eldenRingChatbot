\n\n Ash of War: Parry \n\nAffinity\nStandard\n\nSkill\nParry Skill\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Parry is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Parry provides Standard affinity and the Parry Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values. When a Shield or Weapon in your left hand is equipped with Parry, it takes priority over the Ash of War on the armament in your right hand.\n\nThis Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill: \n"Parry: Use this skill in time with a foe's melee attack to deflect it and break that foe's stance. This provides an opening to perform a critical hit." \nUsable on daggers, curved swords, thrusting swords, fists, claws, and small and medium shields.\n\nAsh of War: Parry Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Parry:\n\nPurchase from Knight Bernahl: 600. Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.\n\nAsh of War: Parry Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the Parry Skill.\nProvides the Standard affinity, which respects the weapon's original scaling and damage.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Curved Swords, Thrusting Swords, Fists, Claws, Small Shields, Medium Shields\nOn Small Shields, Parry has 9 start-up frames, 11 active frames, and 31 recovery frames (at 60 FPS).\nOn Daggers, Parry has 11 start-up frames, 9 active frames, and 31 recovery frames (at 60 FPS).\nOn Medium Shields, Thrusting Swords, Curved Swords, Fists, and Claws, Parry has 11 start-up frames, 5 active frames, and 35 recovery frames (at 60 FPS).\nSee Parrying for detailed information QUESTION
What are the start-up frames, active frames, and recovery frames of the Parry skill on different weapon categories in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
On Small Shields: 9 start-up frames, 11 active frames, 31 recovery frames; On Daggers: 11 start-up frames, 9 active frames, 31 recovery frames; On Medium Shields, Thrusting Swords, Curved Swords, Fists, Claws: 11 start-up frames, 5 active frames, 35 recovery frames.
