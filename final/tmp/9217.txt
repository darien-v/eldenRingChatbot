\n\n Raya Lucaria Foot Soldier \n\nLocation\nLiurnia of the Lakes\n\nDrops\n 108 RunesRaya Lucaria Foot Soldier SetDaggerShort SwordSoldier's CrossbowBoltSmoldering Butterfly\n\nRaya Lucaria Foot Soldiers are Humanoid enemies in Elden Ring. They are located in most areas in Liurnia, and are often equipped with medium, light melee Weapons and full Body Armor. Raya Lucaria Foot Soldiers are a variation of the Foot Soldier.\n\nFoot Soldiers usually guarding camps and ruins with their fellow Soldiers, they wander around the Raya Lucaria Academy located within Liurnia of the Lakes.\n\nElden Ring Raya Lucaria Foot Soldier Locations\nRaya Lucaria Foot Soldiers can be found in the following locations:\n\nThree can be found fighting spectral enemies near the Liurnia Highway North Site of Grace.\nSeveral can be found within, and surrounding the Purified Ruins.\n\nElden Ring Raya Lucaria Foot Soldier Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 108\n\nFoot Soldier Helmet\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nFoot Soldier Tabard\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nDagger\n4.00%\nDropped only by those that wield it.\n\nShort Sword\n4.00%\nDropped only by those that wield it.\n\nSoldier's Crossbow\n4.00%\nDropped only by those that wield it.\n\n5x Bolt\n10.00%\nDropped by most variants.\n\nSmoldering Butterfly\n40.00%\nDropped by those that drop no weapons.\n\nRaya Lucaria Foot Soldier Notes & Tips\n\nThey do not drop the complete Raya Lucarian Foot Soldier Set that they wear, just the Helmet and Tabard.
