The Carian Glintblade Staff scales primarily with Intelligence and is a good Weapon for spellcasters that rely on their magic attacks.\n\nStaff embedded with a blue glintstone. One of two types of Carian staff.\nGifted to enchanted knights, it enhances Glintblade sorceries.\n\nWhere to Find Carian Glintblade Staff in Elden Ring\nThe Carian Glintblade Staff weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nHighway Lookout Tower in eastern Liurnia: Found on the top floor.
