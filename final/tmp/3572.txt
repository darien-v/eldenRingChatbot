CONTEXT
\n\n Sky Shot \n\n        Archery skill performed from a low stance. Ready the bow, then fire an arrow high up into the air, arcing so as to strike the enemy from above when it comes down. \n\nSky Shot is a Skill in Elden Ring. Sky Shot is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, the skill can be used on Light Bows and Longbows.\n\nHow to get Sky Shot in Elden Ring\n\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab, found on the cliffs northwest of the Isolated Merchant's Shack in Dragonbarrow. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Sky Shot Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Sky Shot.\nFP Cost: 0 on activation (stance), 2 per shot.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nHolding the skill button makes the user assume a low stance, preparing the bow. When inputting light or heavy attacks, the user shoots to the air the respective arrow from their slots, consuming FP (and stamina while in combat). \n\nWhen the stance is being held, stamina regen is reduced by approximately 80%.\nIf the user moves, the stance is canceled and they have to remain still to assume it again.\n\nThis shot can go through physical barriers in front of the user, because of the trajectory. \n\nThis also makes it extremely easy to score headshots to targets that remain still.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nThe arrows receive a damage penalty\n\nSky Shot QUESTION
What are the conditions for Sky Shot to be cancelled and assumed again?

ANSWER
If the user moves, the stance is canceled, and they have to remain still to assume it again.
