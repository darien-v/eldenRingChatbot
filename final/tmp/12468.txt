The Giant's Seal scales primarily with Faith and is a good Weapon for Giant's flame incantations. \n\nSacred seal depicting the one eyed god of the Fire Giants, adorned with braids of red hair.\nSacred seal wielded by Fire Monks and Prelates, this catalyst enhances Giants' Flame incantations. \n\nWhere to Find Giant's Seal in Elden Ring\nThe Giant's Seal can be found at the following location:\n\nInside Giant-Conquering Hero's Grave. [Elden Ring Map here]\nIn the large hall with the Troll, drop down from either side of the platform into a room with a Fire Prelate. Defeat it and check the right side of the room for a corpse holding the seal.\nAlternatively, you can reach this room by descending the lift and sending it back up. Pull the lever in the alcove to the right and then enter the small space in the raised lift to descend further down. In the next hall, sprint past the Lesser Burial Watchdog and turn left. Climb the ladder at the end of this path.\n\nElden Ring Giant's Seal Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: No Skill\nGiant's Seal can be upgraded by using Smithing Stones\nBoosts attack power of various Giants' Flame Incantations and Fire Monk Incantations by 20%. Wielding two Giant's Seals stacks this buff to about 44% damage increase.\nSell Value:  100\nSpellbuff of 273 at +25 with 60 Faith; 307 at +25 with 80 Faith.\nFire Giant Incantations include Burn, O Flame!, Flame of the Fell God, Flame, Fall Upon Them, and Giantsflame Take Thee. Fire Monk Incantations include O, Flame!, Surge, O Flame!, and Whirl, O Flame!. \n\nDespite also being flame-based, Catch Flame, Fire's Deadly Sin, and Flame Sling are NOT boosted by this seal.\nDespite being categorized as Giants' Flame Incantations internally, the following incantations are unaffected because they do not deal damage: Flame, Cleanse Me, Flame, Grant Me Strength, and Flame, Protect Me\n\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08 a bug that prevented the follow-up attack from occurring when the Black Sword Incantation was activated with a Sacred Seal in the left hand has been fixed.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed. QUESTION
How can the attack power of various Giants' Flame Incantations and Fire Monk Incantations be boosted using the Giant's Seal in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
By wielding two Giant's Seals, the attack power of Giants' Flame Incantations and Fire Monk Incantations can be boosted by about 44%.
