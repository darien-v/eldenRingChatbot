\n\n Sanguine Noble (NPC) \n\nLocation\nConsecrated Snowfield\n\nDrops\nFurlcalling Finger RemedySanguine Noble Set\n\nSanguine Noble is an NPC enemy invader located in the Consecrated Snowfields. By heading West of the Inner Consecrated Snowfield grace, and just North of the Yelough Anix Ruins towards the cliff edge.\nDefeat of the invader is required to unlock the blood covered waypoint to Mohgwyn Palace, located west towards the cliff area just behind the large tree next to the invasion area.\n\n Known to strike from pools of blood, these assassins are missionaries come to share the gospel of accursed blood.\n\nSanguine Noble (NPC) Location in Elden Ring\n\nLocated in the Consecrated Snowfield. By heading West of the Inner Consecrated Snowfield grace, and just North of the Yelough Anix Ruins\n\nThis NPC invader is not to be confused with Sanguine Noble, which a generic enemy which spawns in multiple locations such as the Rose Church, and is also a boss in the Writheblood Ruins.\nElden Ring Sanguine Noble (NPC) Drops\n\nFurlcalling Finger Remedy\nSanguine Noble Set\n\nSanguine Noble (NPC) Notes & Tips\n\nThis invasion, and several others, including the nearby Anastasia Tarnished-Eater invasion, have been bugged for many players, leading to many theories. However, they were always just bugged and may fail to trigger for unknown reasons on versions <1.05. Thankfully NPC invasion bugs have been fully fixed in patch 1.05.\nKill the Great Dragonflies that are around the area, as they can turn into nuisances while fighting the enemy.\nCan heal once\nWields double Reduvia\nDoes Slash and Pierce Damage. Can inflict Blood Loss.\nBest to manage distance. Frequent rushdown & uses projectile bleed attacks and will frequently parry & critical strike up close.\nThe armor set drop is missable.\nIt is essentially a stronger version of  Bloody Finger Nerijus, as they both have the same appearance, and use the same weapons.\nCan be lured to the ruins just south in order to summon spirit ashes and be within radius of a statue of marika\n\nElden Ring
