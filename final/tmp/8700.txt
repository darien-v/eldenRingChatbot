Crucible Tree Armor is part of the Crucible Tree Set, and is a Heavyweight chest piece that provides a good balance of defensive properties. Crucible Tree Armor is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters. \n\nArmor of the Crucible Knights who served Godfrey, the first Elden Lord. Worn by the knight Siluria and her men. Holds the power of the crucible of life, the primordial form of the Erdtree.
