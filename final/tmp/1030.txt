\n\n Church of Irith \n\nA peaceful church at the back of Stormveil Castle.\n\nChurch of Irith is a Location in Elden Ring. The Church of Irith is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. It can be easily accessed as it is on an open field at the south of Liurnia of the Lakes a few steps to the northwest of the Graveyard.\n\nChurch of Irith Map\nChurch of Irith is one of the first locations you can reach once you enter Liurnia of the Lakes. It is located behind Stormveil Castle and you can follow the outer walls east to reach it. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Church of Irith\n\nThops - Student at the Academy of Raya Lucaria. Sells basic sorceries.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Church of Irith\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nMushroom\nWarming Stone\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSacred Tear\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nThere are no no equipment in this location\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nAsh of War: Thops's Barrier\n\nElden Ring Church of Irith Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nTeardrop Scarab\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no Bosses at this location\n\nChurch of Irith Walkthrough\nChurch of Irith is one of the first locations you can visit once you enter Liurnia of the Lakes after completing Stormveil Castle (or using the alternate route into Liurnia). Of note in this church are the Sacred Tear in front of the Statue of Marika and the NPC Thops sitting on a bench at the side. Thops will sell you some low-level sorcery spells after donating 10 Runes to him. You can also speak to him about an Academy Glintstone Key and he will mention wanting to return to the academy. When you've obtained two such keys, you can donate one to Thops and he will move to the Raya Lucaria Academy. If you return to the church after Thops leaves, there will be a Teardrop Scarab where he used to sit. Killing it awards the Ash of War: Thops's Barrier. If it does not drop the Ash of War, double check that you do not have this specific item already.\n\nElden Ring Church of Irith Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring
