\n\n Grand Lift of Rold \n\nThe impressive Lift of Rold transports those who wield the right key. But could there be more than one destination?\n\n Grand Lift of Rold is a Location in Elden Ring. The  Grand Lift of Rold is found in the Forbidden Lands and guards the way to the Mountaintops of the Giants. It is only accessible by those who have gained entry into Leyndell, Royal Capital.\n\n Grand Lift of Rold Map\n[Elden Ring Interactive Map]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Grand Lift of Rold\n\nFinger Reader Crone - Can be found at the base of the lift.\nThere are no Merchants here\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Grand Lift of Rold\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nThere are no farm items in this location\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade items in this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nThere are no equipment items in this location\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this location\n\nThe following Key Items are needed to interact with this area.\n\nRold Medallion\nHaligtree Secret Medallion (Left)\nHaligtree Secret Medallion (Right)\n\nElden Ring Grand Lift of Rold Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no enemies in this location \n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses in this location \n\nGrand Lift of Rold Walkthrough\nThe Grand Lift of Rold is found by heading East from Leyndell, Royal Capital, past the Forbidden Lands. The closest site of grace is the Avenue Balcony site of grace. From the site of grace, head up the stairs and take a left down the road. Follow the road until you reach a large door. After opening the door, you can continue up the road where there is a lift on your left near the end. This lift brings you to the Rold Route, but it will be blocked by golden light until Morgott is defeated.  See in the interactive map. Once you arrive, you will find a Site of grace on your right side and the Finger Reader Crone on your left side. Head to the lift and use the Rold Medallion given by Melina after defeating Morgott, The Omen King. You won't be able to activate it without the Rold Medallion, should you, somehow, manage to reach the lift before facing Morgott.\nThis lift takes you to two different locations depending on the Medallion you've selected. This could take you to the Mountaintops of the Giants or the Consecrated Snowfield, an alternate secret location for which you will need Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left) and Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right).\nThe Left Medallion piece is found in Castle Sol. Found on the roof of the tower in the northeast, accessible after defeating Commander Niall. [Elden Ring Map Link] For the right medallion, you will need to head near theVillage of the Albinaurics.  Hit Albus, who is disguised as a pot past the Perfumer guarding the path up from the Village of the Albinaurics Site of Grace in the swamp caves west of Liurnia of the Lakes. Map Location here. Latenna, south of this location hints at Albus's whereabouts.  \nTo use the secondary medallion rather than the default Rold Medallion on the lift, use left or right keys to select which Medallion you want to use when you see the switch action prompt. \n\nElden Ring Grand Lift of Rold Gallery QUESTION
How can you activate the lift in the Grand Lift of Rold using the Rold Medallion or the Haligtree Secret Medallions?

ANSWER
You must defeat Morgott, The Omen King, to activate the lift in the Grand Lift of Rold using the Rold Medallion, or acquire and use the Haligtree Secret Medallions to unlock two different destinations.
