Highwaymen are hostile thug enemies that used to be Foot Soldiers and are now withdrawn from society. They are found in Murkwater Cave. Apparently they are initially led by Patches, often serving as partners.\n\n Former foot soldiers who have since turned to banditry. They ambush any who approach, wishing to take their valuables for themselves.\n\nElden Ring Highwayman Locations\nWhere to Find Highwayman:\n\nMurkwater Cave\n\nElden Ring Highwayman Drops\n\n Item \n
