Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman carried by assassins who strike unseen.Patterned on a scorpion freshly shed of its exoskeleton, its claws seizing a heart that sparks with lightning.\nRaises lightning attack power, but lowers damage negation.\n\nLightning Scorpion Charm Effect in Elden Ring\nLightning Scorpion Charm increases Lightning Damage by 12%, but increases Physical Damage taken by 10%.\n\nIncreases Lightning Damage by 8% in PvP.\nIncreases Physical Damage taken by 15% in PvP.\n\nWhere to find Lightning Scorpion Charm in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Lightning Scorpion Charm:\n\nCan be found in the Wyndham Catacombs in a room sealed by an Imp Statue, requires a Stonesword Key. [Elden Ring Map here]\nNote: This is not in the underground passage way in the ruins that also takes one Stonesword Key to enter.
