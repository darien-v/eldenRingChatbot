QUESTION
Where can the Winged Spear be found in Elden Ring and what are its strengths and weaknesses?

ANSWER
The Winged Spear can be found in Elden Ring as starting equipment for the Enchanted Knight class in the closed network test, known now as the Spear in the official release, and can't be found in the official release. Its strengths include long reach, the ability to attack from behind a shield, and effectiveness against mounted foes, while weaknesses may include limited availability and potentially lower stats compared to other weapons.
