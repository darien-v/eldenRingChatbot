Lord of Blood's Robe (Altered) is a Medium Weight chest piece embroidered with an extravagant gold emblem on black cloth. Lord of Blood's Robe (Altered) is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nAttire of Mohg, Lord of Blood, embroidered with an extravagant gold emblem on black cloth.  The dress of a Luminary, the reigning lord and hierarch of the coming dynasty of Mohgwyn. QUESTION
Why is Lord of Blood's Robe (Altered) considered a valuable armor piece in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Lord of Blood's Robe (Altered) is considered valuable in Elden Ring for its high Resistance, Immunity, Robustness, and Vitality stats, as well as its impressive Magic, Fire, and Holy defenses.
