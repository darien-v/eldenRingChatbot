Most Consumables grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects or applying temporary buffs and debuffs, while others can be thrown at enemies to deal damage.\n\nBlack boluses made of cave moss.Craftable item.\nAlleviates death blight buildup.\nBlight accumulates slowly, causing instant death once the threshold is reached. Take one of these in timely fashion to avoid such an event.\n\nWhere to find Rejuvenating Boluses in Elden Ring\n\nSage's Cave: Found on a chest behind a illusory wall in Sage's Cave [Elden Ring Map link]\nFound on a corpse by jumping on a trap to access a higher area of the Consecrated Snowfield Catacombs. [Map link]\nFound on the nearest corpse of a trio of corpses outside Seethewater Cave in Mt. Gelmir [Elden Ring Map link]\nFound at the center of the lake guarded by Lesser Wormface, at the southeast of Crumbling Farum Azula,. To reach this location, you must go down the elevator that is at the backyard of the big hall found a few rooms after the Crumbling Beast Grave Depths Site of Grace.
