\n\n Soldjars of Fortune Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost -\nHP Cost 135\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons three little living jar spirits \n\nSoldjars of Fortune Ashes  is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Soldjars of Fortune Ashes are used to summon the spirits of three little living jars.\nLiving Jars can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Living Jar.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of three little living jars. Summoning consumes HP rather than FP.  These reckless spirits sacrifice themselves to vanquish their summoner's foes, and explode upon death. Though their bravery is admirable, their explosions can harm friend as well as foe, and they will also immediately explode on contact with fire.  "Tell the others—tell them how brightly a fire jar burns."\n\nWhere to find Soldjars of Fortune Ashes\nWhere to Find Soldjars of Fortune Ashes \n\nDropped by the Grave Warden Duelist (boss) in the Auriza Side Tomb located in the eastern part of the Capital Outskirts. Where can Soldjars of Fortune Ashes be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
