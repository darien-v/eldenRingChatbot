\n\n Small Red Effigy \n\nType\nReusable\n\nEffect \n\n Sends a hostile sign to summoning pools \n\nSmall Red Effigy is a Multiplayer Item in Elden Ring. Multiplayer Items allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.\n\nItem for online play. (Can also be used from the Multiplayer menu.)\nSend a competitive summon sign to several nearby summoning pools (activated pools only).\nIn competitive multiplayer, your objective will be to defeat the Host of Fingers of the world to which you were summoned.\n\nSmall Red Effigy Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Small Red Effigy:\n\nNorthermost zone of Limgrave. In a burnt pillage corpse, next to the Frustrated Spirit.
