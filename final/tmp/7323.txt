\n\n Lost Ashes of War \n\nAffinity\nNone\n\nSkill\nUsed to duplicate Ashes of War\n\nLost Ashes of War is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Lost Ashes of War is a special item that can be exchanged to duplicate another Ash of War that you currently hold.\n\nEmpty ashes of war, devoid of any memories of battle.\nUnable to apply any kind of affinity in their current state, but can be used by Smithing Master Hewg of the Roundtable Hold to recreate other ashes.\nThus does the copy pass into becoming the genuine article.\n\nLost Ashes of War Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Lost Ashes of War:\n\nOne can be purchased from the Isolated Merchant Weeping Peninsula for  3000. He can be found in his shack at the very west of the Weeping Peninsula. [Map Link]\nOne can be purchased from the Isolated Merchant Raya Lucaria for  4000. From the Main Academy Gate site of grace, walk through the blue seal down the hill to the east, then south.\nOne can be found at the bottom of the water wheel lift on top of the cliff in Raya Lucaria Academy near the Schoolhouse Classroom Site of Grace. (After riding the lift down, jump up the crystals to the west.)\nOne can be purchased from the Hermit Merchant Ainsel River for  3000. [Map Link]\nTwo can be purchased from the Isolated Merchant Dragonbarrow for  5,000 each. [Map Link]\nFound in Sellia Hideaway, at the bottom of the first large open cavern with giant purple crystals that the player traverses on. It will be on a corpse. Be wary of the Revenant down here guarding it and other items. [Map Link]\nRuin-Strewn Precipice: Within the area with 2 Chanting Winged Dames. Look for the ledge behind the wooden platform. [Map Link]\nOne can be found in the first chest past Skeletal Militiaman in Sage's Cave in Altus Plateau.\nOne can be found by the locked northwestern gate outside Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Map Link]\nOne can be found in Leyndell slums' sewer tunnels (not Subterranean Shunning-Grounds). Past some rats and small hand enemies, at the end of a long straight otherwise empty tunnel that has the Lost Ashes of War and a wall-clung Fingercreeper enemy guarding it.\nSubterranean Shunning-Grounds: One is found on a corpse outside Dung Eater's cell beneath the Royal Capital. Beware the Fingercreeper that emerges as you collect the item. [Map Link]\nOne can be found on a corpse on the narrow ridge in the Mountaintops of The Giants after the whip enemy. [Map Link]\nOne can be purchased from the Imprisoned Merchant inside the Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum for  5,000. [Map Link]\nOne can be found on a corpse slumped over in front of a statue in Miquella's Haligtree canopy. QUESTION
How can players obtain Lost Ashes of War in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Lost Ashes of War can be obtained in Elden Ring by purchasing them from various isolated merchants, finding them in specific locations like Sellia Hideaway and Leyndell, or from certain Hermit Merchants, such as Ainsel River.
