QUESTION
How do the armor pieces of the Fur Set in Elden Ring compare in terms of damage negation and resistance stats?

ANSWER
The Fur Set pieces in Elden Ring offer varying degrees of damage negation and resistance: Great Horned Headband has the lowest stats, Fur Raiment has moderate stats, and Fur Leggings provide slightly higher stats.
