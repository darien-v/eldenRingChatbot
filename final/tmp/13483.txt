QUESTION
How do the stats of the Haligtree Knight Gauntlets compare to other gauntlets in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Haligtree Knight Gauntlets have high Dmg Negation, VS Slash, Magic, Fire, Ligt resistances, and high Resistance, Robustness, Focus, Vitality, and Poise stats, making them a strong defensive choice compared to other gauntlets in Elden Ring.
