\n\n Stars of Ruin \n\nSpell Type\nPrimeval Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 32\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Fire twelve dark shooting stars that pursue foes \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 43 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nStars of Ruin is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Stars of Ruin spell is a homing projectile spell that targets nearby foes and deals damage. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nLegendary sorcery devised by Lusat, primeval sorcerers. \nFires twelve dark shooting stars that pursue the target. This sorcery can be cast while in motion. Charging increases potency.\nWhen Lusat glimpsed into the primeval current, he beheld the final moments of a great star cluster, and upon seeing it, he too was broken. \n\nWhere to find Stars of Ruin\nWhere to find Stars of Ruin:\n\nCaelid: Given by Master Lusat when interacted with inside the Sellia Hideaway behind an arcane ward in the second pit. Requires the Sellian Sealbreaker to get through arcane ward (Acquired during Sorceress Sellen Questline). Elden Ring Map Link\n\nElden Ring Stars of Ruin Guide\n\nPrimeval Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 29\nDeals magic damage.\nThis sorcery can be charged to increase its damage by 30% at the expense of a longer cast time. Godfrey Icon would further increase its damage when charged.\nDamage can be boosted by 15% by wearing Lusat's Glintstone Crown. This comes at the cost of a 15% increase to ALL sorcery costs\nIt has significant tracking capabilities (even after the 1.06 nerf), and its stars can hit targets that run or dodge such as PvP opponents or enemies like Malenia.\nWhen faced against this spell, it is better to dodge into it, otherwise not all stars will be dodged.\nHas exactly the same range as Glintstone Stars and Star Shower.\nVersion history: patch 1.06 significantly reduced its tracking capability. Patch 1.07 reduced its FP cost from 38 to 32, and its Stamina cost from 33 to 29.\n\nBest Stars of Ruin Buffs\nUse these Buffs to maximize the performance of Stars of Ruin.
