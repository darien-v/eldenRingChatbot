Beast Champion Set, and is engraved with beasts on the metal. It is not only for aesthetics, but adds to the wearer's protection. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Beast Champion Set in Elden Ring\n\nDrops from Knight Bernahl upon death. If not killed before, he will eventually invade you at Crumbling Farum Azula, dropping the entire set with an altered version of the chest piece.
