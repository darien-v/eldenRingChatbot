\n\n Lesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit) \n\nLocation\nConsecrated Snowfield\n\nDrops\nN/A\n\nLesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit) is an Enemy in Elden Ring. They are Spirit versions of the Dragonkin Soldier Boss.\n\nAlas, the Dragonkin Soldiers never attained immortality, and perished as decrepit, pale imitations of their skyborn kin.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit) Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit):\n\nConsecrated Snowfield\n\nElden Ring Lesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit) Drops\n\nN/A\nNo Runes either\n\nLesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit) Notes & Tips\n\nThey do huge damage and offer no reward, and they disappear if the player gets out of range. However, they have a range that will catch players off guard, and are very hard to see against the white landscape of the Consecrated Snowfield, so it might be safer to dispatch them from around their legs, like how melee builds usually handle Trolls.\nThey are immune to all status effects.\nLike Inaba (Spirit) and Giant Skeleton (Spirit), they drop no Runes upon death.\nLike Giant Skeletons encountered in the cemeteries of the Mountaintops of the Giants, they have no summoner to defeat.\n\nOther notes and player tips go here\n\nIMAGE GALLERY\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor
