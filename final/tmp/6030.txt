QUESTION
Why is the Banished Knight Helm (Altered) described as a Heavyweight helmet that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense in the Elden Ring game?

ANSWER
The Banished Knight Helm (Altered) is described as a Heavyweight helmet that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense in the Elden Ring game because it offers robustness, vitality, and a combination of resistance and immunity properties.
