\n\n Warhawk Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 52\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons warhawk spirit \n\nWarhawk Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Warhawk summons warhawk spirit. The Warhawk summoned is similar to the ones in Stormveil Castle. It attacks both with the swords attached to its talons and its beak, as well as also being able to breathe fire. It can be upgraded with Grave Glovewort.\nWarhawks can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Warhawk.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirit of a warhawk.  Spirit of a Stormveil warhawk, the talons of which have been sliced off so that razor-fine swords could be grafted in their place. With its lord vanquished, and its wings wounded, the hawk perished as it solemnly gazed at its former home. The storm is a warhawk's cradle.\n\nWhere to find Warhawk Ashes\nWhere to Find Warhawk Ashes\n\nRewarded upon finding the location Prophecy Painting depicts. The location is north of the Church of Pilgrimage at the northern tip of Weeping Peninsula. (The Area in the South). QUESTION
Why do players need to use Warhawk Ashes to summon the spirit of a warhawk in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players need to use Warhawk Ashes to summon the spirit of a warhawk in Elden Ring because the Ashes contain ashen remains in which spirits yet dwell, allowing them to bring forth the warhawk spirit for assistance in battle.
