QUESTION
What are the differences between Glintstone Icecrag and Great Glintstone Shard spells in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Glintstone Icecrag applies Frostbite, shorter range, slower speed, slightly less initial damage compared to Great Glintstone Shard.
