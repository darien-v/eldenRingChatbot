QUESTION
How can I obtain the Guilty Hood in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Guilty Hood Helm can be purchased from the Nomadic Merchant in Mt. Gelmir for 500 Runes or found on a corpse in the sewers north of Avenue Balcony site in Leyndell Royal Capital in Elden Ring.
