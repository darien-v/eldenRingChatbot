The Moonlight Altar is a sub-region of Liurnia of the Lakes found in the southwest, high above the plateau. This area is accessible only after making significant progress in Ranni's questline, up to the point where you defeat Astel, Naturalborn of the Void in the Grand Cloister and gain access to the lift in the Deep Ainsel Well, which leads up to the plateau. \n\nThe Moonlight Altar is a large plateau bathed in lunar rays, found high above in the southwest of Liurnia. This plateau hosts the ruins of a civilization that held great reverence for the moon.\n\nMoonlight Altar Map & Map Fragments\n\nNo Map Fragments: There are no map fragments in this area. QUESTION
How can one access the Moonlight Altar in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Moonlight Altar in Elden Ring can be accessed by making significant progress in Ranni's questline, defeating Astel, Naturalborn of the Void in the Grand Cloister, and gaining access to the lift in the Deep Ainsel Well to reach the plateau.
