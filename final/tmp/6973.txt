QUESTION
Why is Grave Glovewort [4] mainly used to further increase the level of ashes in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Grave Glovewort [4] is mainly used to further increase the level of ashes in Elden Ring because it strengthens ashes to +4 by enhancing spirit tuning, which is an intricate process akin to tuning an instrument and enriching both parties involved.
