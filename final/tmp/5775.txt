QUESTION
Why does the Karolos Glintstone Crown in Elden Ring increase intelligence at the cost of stamina?

ANSWER
The Karolos Glintstone Crown in Elden Ring increases intelligence at the cost of stamina to reflect the artifact's lore and historical significance.
