The Mountaintops of the Giants region is located at the very north-east of The Lands Between, and showcases the powerful devastation that overcame the Giants who used to dwell here.\n\nThe fabled domain of the Giants, now in ruins. Devasted after their war against the Erdtree, their corpses lay frozen at the peak, with only the Fire Monks residing close by.\n\nMountaintops of the Giants Map & Map Fragments\n\nMap Mountaintops of the Giants, West: As you leave the Grand Lift of Rold, take the path forward until you reach a stone monument on your right, interact with it to obtain the map Fragment. Apostle & Godskin Noble\nRoundtable Knight Vyke\n\nElden Ring Mountaintops of the Giants Maps and Notes\n\nHow do I get to Valley of the Giants Elden ring?\nValley of the Giants also known as the Mountaintops of the Giants is located at the very north-east of The Lands Between map. Players need to access the Grand Lift of Rold with the required parts to operate it in order to use it as a lift to the Mountaintops. This lift takes you to two different locations depending on the Medallion you've selected. This could take you to the Mountaintops of the Giants or Consecrated Snowfield which is a secret area on the lower plains of the Mountaintops of the Giants, that can only be accessed through this lift by collecting both parts of the Haligtree Secret Medallion. \nTo take the Grand Lift of Rold to the Mountaintops of the Giants you will need the Rold Medallion. This item is given by Melina after you defeat Morgott, The Omen King. Talk to her in the Site of Grace on the battlefield, after failing to enter the Erdtree. Once you do, she will give it to you.
