\n\n Ash of War: Lightning Slash \n\nAffinity\nLightning\n\nSkill\nLightning Slash\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Slash is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Lightning Slash provides Lightning affinity and the Lightning Slash Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Lightning affinity and the following skill:\n"Lightning Slash: Call down a bolt of lightning into armament, then swing it down to create an explosive shock. The armament retains the lightning enchantment for a while."\nUsable on swords, axes, and hammers.\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Slash Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Lightning Slash:\n\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the middle of a Leyndell soldier encampment in the northeastern side of Altus Plateau. Just up the road from the Outer Wall Battleground site of grace:  [Map Link]\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Slash Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Lightning Slash.\nDeals 27 stance damage\nAdds Lightning damage and increases Dexterity scaling, decreases Base damage and Strength scaling.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Lightning, Sacred\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Colossal Weapons\n\nLightning Slash Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Lightning Slash Notes & Tips\n\nNotes and player tips go here.\nThe in-game tooltip is incorrect.
