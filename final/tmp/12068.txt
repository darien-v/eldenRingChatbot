QUESTION
Why are Basilisks weak to Fire Damage and Slash Damage in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Basilisks are weak to Fire Damage and Slash Damage in Elden Ring because they create clouds that build up Death Blightmeter and are resistant to most Status Effects.
