Royal Remains Set consists 4 pieces and provides good Robustness resistance and physical damage negation. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Royal Remains Set in Elden Ring\nRoyal Remains Set can be found at Roundtable Hold outside of Gideon Ofnir's room after defeating the invasion by Ensha of the Royal Remains.\n\nRoyal Remains Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nRoyal Remains Helm\nRoyal Remains Armor\nRoyal Remains Gauntlets\nRoyal Remains Greaves\n\nRoyal Remains Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nSlowly replenishes HP when HP is reduced. Which is 2 HP per second (per piece, for a total of 8) while under 18% HP.\nThe passive of this armor ensures the player will not die to the DoT effects of the Poison status effect or Fire's Deadly Sin incantation (if cast by the wearer). As such, it can be decent in PvE for the purpose of exploring poisonous dungeons like the Seethewater Cave or Abandoned Cave.\nIt has good protection and poise for an early- to mid-game armor set that is quite easy to get without farming, but is later overtaken by other sets such as the Scaled Set, Raging Wolf Set or Blaidd's Set.\nBecause the armor pieces will only heal the player up to 18% of max HP, they can be utilized with the Red and Blue-Feathered Branchswords and Twinbird Kite Shield to maintain the latter's effects without unintentionally healing out of their effects' range (Below 20% of max HP).\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can the Royal Remains Set be effectively utilized in a Poison status effect or Fire's Deadly Sin incantation scenario in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Royal Remains Set can effectively prevent death from Poison or Fire by providing passive healing to maintain health below 18% HP.
