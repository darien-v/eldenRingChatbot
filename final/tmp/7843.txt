QUESTION
What is the backstory and significance of the Devourer's Scepter in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Devourer's Scepter in Elden Ring is a legendary armament symbolizing the Lord of Blasphemy, seen in Rykard's final moments. It is a powerful Great Hammer that primarily scales with Strength, Dexterity, and Faith, ideal for high strength players, especially in Strength/Faith builds.
