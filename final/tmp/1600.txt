QUESTION
Why is the Iron Set armor considered lightweight yet still provides ample protection against fire and robustness resistance in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Iron Set armor in Elden Ring is lightweight yet provides ample protection against fire and robustness resistance due to its composition of individual pieces, each contributing to overall protection, with a focus on fire and robustness resistance.
