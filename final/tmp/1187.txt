The Altus Plateau region can be accessed either by:\n\nThe Grand Lift of Dectus\nThe Ruin-Strewn Precipice (after making your way through the Ravine-veiled Village, the Miner's Caves and Ruin-Strewn Precipice and defeating the Magma Wyrm)\nA third, secret route can be utilized to reach the Altus Plateau region by being intentionally swallowed and killed by the Abductor Virgin at the bottom of the Raya Lucaria waterwheel. QUESTION
Why are there multiple routes to access the Altus Plateau region in Elden Ring, including a secret route that involves being swallowed and killed by the Abductor Virgin?

ANSWER
Various routes to access the Altus Plateau in Elden Ring, including a secret route involving the Abductor Virgin, offer players diverse exploration options and challenge them to uncover hidden pathways in the game world.
