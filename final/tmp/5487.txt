\n\n Skeletal Militiaman \n\nLocation\nIn the beaches and graveyards of the Lands Between\n\nDrops\n\n 35 - 682 RunesHuman Bone Shard\n\nSkeletal Militiamen are Humanoid Enemies in Elden Ring. Skeletal Militiamen are literal bare-bone creatures, always hostile and often moving in packs. They often fight alongside Skeletal Bandits and Skeleton Mages. It is very common for them to ambush their preys, by acting as lifeless corpses or hiding underwater. Just like other Skeletons such as Skeletal Bandits and Skeleton Mages, Skeletal Militiamen will not die upon defeat, making them able to re-take their previous shape and keep on fighting. In order to avoid this, players should also attack their remains even after these creatures are defeated.\nThe spirits of two (2) Skeletal Militiamen can be summoned for aid in battle by using Skeletal Militiaman Ashes.\n\nMilitiamen who live in Death, and will continue to rise again until properly finished off.This is the grotesque fate of those who come into contact with Deathroot.\n\nSkeletal Militiaman Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Skeletal Militiamen:\n\nPlayers can encounter Skeletal Militiamen in the beaches and dungeons of Limgrave, and in the cemetery area of Stormhill.\nThey can be found in the Deathtouched Catacombs.\nLocated roaming the graves of Liurnia of The Lakes.\nCan be found in Wyndham Catacombs\nWeeping Peninsula. West of Witchbane Ruins.\nCrumbling Farum Azula. They have a different look, but otherwise act the same.\n\nSkeletal Militiaman Drops in Elden Ring\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Location \n\nRunes\n100%\n 35 - 682 Runes\n\nHuman Bone Shard\n5.00%\nLimgrave\n\nElden Ring Skeletal Militiaman Notes & Tips\n\nSkeletal Militiamen share their resurrection mechanic with all other Skeletons. Once defeated, the glowing slime they leave behind must be hit to avoid them reforming and attacking again.\nA weapon empowered by Ash of War: Sacred Blade will kill Skeletons without resurrection. You can also just poke most skeletons with a torch to kill them once the "slime" is exposed.\nThree weapons can kill Skeletons and other reviving enemies inherently, without the aid of buffs: Golden Epitaph, Golden Order Greatsword, and Inseparable Sword.\nHoly Water Pot can, on most occasions, kill a skeleton in a single throw.  This will also prevent them from resurrecting.\n\nIMAGE QUESTION
Where can players encounter Skeletal Militiamen in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can encounter Skeletal Militiamen in the beaches and dungeons of Limgrave, in the cemetery area of Stormhill, the Deathtouched Catacombs, the graves of Liurnia of The Lakes, Wyndham Catacombs, Weeping Peninsula, and Crumbling Farum Azula.
