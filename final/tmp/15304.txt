The Black Knife scales primarily with Faith and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for dealing additional Holy Damage and for its Unique Skill, Blade of Death, that allows the user to fire an additional projectile.\n\nDagger once belonging to one of the assassins who murdered Godwyn the Golden on the Night of the Black Knives. \nA ritual performed on the oddly misshapen blade imbued it with the power of the stolen Rune of Death. \n\nWhere to Find Black Knife in Elden Ring\nThe Black Knife weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nThis is a drop from defeating the Black Knife Assassin at the entrance of Sainted Hero's Grave, in Altus Plateau. This boss is directly west of the Rampartside Path site. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Black Knife Notes & Tips\n\nBlack Knife can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones.\nThis weapon can't be infused with Ashes of War, nor can it be enchanted with Magic or boosted by Consumables.\nThis weapon has a unique R2 moveset shared with the Blade of Calling and the Erdsteel Dagger, that performs quick double slashes. \nWeapon Skill: Blade of Death - This projectile will reduce the Target's Maximum HP for a period of time while also reducing their Current HP. For further notes and tips, go to the skill's page.\nWhile this weapon is primarily designed with Dex Faith builds in mind, the Blade of Death projectile's damage is only affected by Faith scaling, meaning that a Strength Faith build utilizing Maliketh's Black Blade or the Black Blade incantation can still benefit from this weapon solely for the weapon skill alone.\nThis weapon may have drawn inspiration from an African hybrid knife/axe known as the mambele.\nSell Value:   100.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, the speed and distance of some attacks has been increased and the recovery time has been reduced.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
