Guarded closely by the puppet soldiers of the Academy\n\nHeretical Rise  is a Location in Elden Ring. The Heretical Rise  is found in Mountaintops of the Giants, by traversing to the north-eastern frozen lake and then heading inland over the slopes.\n\nHeretical Rise Map\nHeretical Rise is found in the northern cliffs of Mountaintop of the Giants, overlooking the ravine leading to the northeastern frozen lake. The invisible bridge starts northeast of the Snow Valley Ruins Overlook grace. From here, make your way across the ravine and as you reach the tower look to your left. You should see very faint mist that guides you on a winding path up to enter the tower from a balcony above (the mist is significantly easier to see at night). Tip: You can use Rainbow Stones or shoot arrows with a bow to mark safe spots to walk on. Better yet, the Icerind Hatchet or another weapon with a ground-effect Ash of War will easily reveal the safe path.\n\nOnce you reach the balcony, you'll see two Avionette Soldiers: one close to your left, and another farther on the right. It's easiest to fight them one at a time.\n\nAfter defeating them, take the stairs on your left (going up) -- the stairs lead to a lift. \n\nTake the lift up, then step forward and turn right to go up winding stairs, but be weary of a final marionette soldier waiting for you as you ascend.\nThe stairs end at a room with a chest in the middle containing your reward -- Founding Rain of Stars\n\nElden QUESTION
How can one successfully navigate the invisible bridge in the Heretical Rise location in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
One can successfully navigate the invisible bridge in the Heretical Rise location in Elden Ring by using Rainbow Stones, arrows, or a weapon with a ground-effect Ash of War to mark safe spots to walk on and look for faint mist guiding a winding path up to the tower.
