\n\n Ghost Glovewort [3] \n\nEffect \n\n        Strengthen renowned ashes to +3 \n\nGhost Glovewort [3] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ghost Glovewort [3] is a special material used to upgrade renowned spirit ashes to +3. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nWhite flower that blooms in catacombs.A spirit nestles close to it.\nStrengthens renowned ashes to +3.\nAshes which keep their renown even after death cannot be strengthened by merely corporeal glovewort.\n\nGhost Glovewort [3] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ghost Glovewort [3]:\n\nFound in the middle of of a tall ruined wall next to the lower Hallowed Grounds in Siofra River. A gust of wind can be found at the edge of the cliff closest to the ruin, boosting you up to the top. [Map Link]\nCliffbottom Catacombs:  In the large multi-level hall, drop down to the very bottom via the gaps on either side of the central stair-bridge. Check around the corridors of this bottom pit for several items, one of which is a Ghost Glovewort (3). [Map Link]\nOne can be found inside the Caelid Catacombs. Continue along the dungeon until you reach a room with a small set of stairs on the left. The glovewort can be found further down after turning the corner, behind the large poison flower. QUESTION
Where can Ghost Glovewort [3] be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ghost Glovewort [3] can be found near the Hallowed Grounds in Siofra River, Below the Well Site of Grace in Siofra River, guarded by Fallen Hawks in Nokron, Eternal City, in a graveyard and on a bridge in Nokron, at Cliffbottom Catacombs, in Caelid Catacombs, and can be purchased at Roundtable Hold.
