Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs.\nSee Maps for detailed information and a list of all maps, and Interactive Map for a unified map with all items and locations marked.\n\nWhere to find About the Map in Elden Ring\n\nAutomatically added to inventory after concept introduction.\n\nElden Ring About the Map Tutorial Guide\n\nUse your map to check your current position, as well as the terrain. and surrounding structures.\nYou can update your map with new information by finding Map fragments at steles along the road.\nYou can also use the map to freely place beacons of light to serve as landmarks as you explore.\n\nElden Ring About the Map Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 About the Map.\nYou cannot store About the Map.\nYou cannot sell About the Map. \n\nElden Ring Info
