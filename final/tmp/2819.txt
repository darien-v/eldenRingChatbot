Drake Knight Set is a black iron set worn by the unmet drake knights. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Drake Knight Set in Elden Ring\n\nYou can find the Drake Knight Set in Crumbling Farum Azula. [Map Link]\nGo to the Dragon Temple Rooftop Site of Grace. From there, drop down to where the elevator shaft containing a waiting Warhawk is located (there is another Warhawk nearby, feasting on a corpse). After climbing down the nearby ladder, turn left and then face north. From here, drop onto a floating rock, and then again down to a platform on which you find the same elevator shaft from above. Before taking the elevator platform up (it will take you all the way to the top, above the level with the Warhawks; you will find a Beastman there), keep going to the end of the circular path outside the elevator and you'll find the chest containing the set.\n\nDrake Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nDrake Knight Helm\nDrake Knight Armor\nDrake Knight Gauntlets\nDrake Knight Greaves\n\nDrake Kngiht Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Drake Knight Helm can be altered to the Drake Knight Helm (Altered) to remove the hood.\nThe Drake Knight Armor can be altered to the Drake Knight Armor (Altered) to remove the wing and cape.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Drake Knight Set considered desirable in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Drake Knight Set in Elden Ring is considered desirable due to its high damage negation, impressive resistance values, immunity, robustness, focus, vitality, and poise, making it a formidable armor set overall.
