QUESTION
How can Blessing of the Erdtree be effectively used in cooperative gameplay in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Blessing of the Erdtree can be effectively used in cooperative gameplay in Elden Ring by providing a health regen buff to all players, including NPCs and spirit summons, for 90 seconds at 12 HP per second, not scaling with Faith, and not stacking with other temporary buffs.
