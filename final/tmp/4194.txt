\n\n Lance Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.7\n\nEffect \n\n Increases damage of attacks while on horseback by 15% \n\nLance Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Lance Talisman increases the damage of attacks while on horseback by 15%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a lance and a knight.Enhances attacks while on horseback.\nKnights on horseback are deadly foes. They see all below from their lofty position, meeting little meaningful resistance as they charge ahead. \n\nLance Talisman Effect\nLance Talisman increases the damage of attacks while on horseback by 15%.\n\nWhere to find Lance Talisman\n\nNortheast Stormhill: The closest site of grace is Deathtouched Catacombs (go outside then head straight northeast to the tip of the map), or up the hill north of the Saintsbridge site of grace. It is on a remains on top of a golem part. Beware the sleeping Golem nearby.
