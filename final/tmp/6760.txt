The Great Club scales primarily with Strength and is a good Weapon for dealing Physical Damage. The skill attached to this weapon also has a unique ability that unleashes a blast of energy and releases them as golden darts.\n\nAn enormous club of hardwood. Wildly hammering foes with this striking weapon requires no dexterity; only brute force.\nWhile it may seem sacrilegious, this weapon is said to be a withered branch of the Erdtree. Imbued with holy power, this weapon will never snap. \n\nWhere to Find Great Club in Elden Ring\nThe Great Club weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by the Stonedigger Troll boss in Old Altus Tunnel, The tunnel requires two Stonesword Keys.
