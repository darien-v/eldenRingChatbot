CONTEXT
\n\n Through and Through \n\n        Powerful archery skill using a greatbow held in an oblique stance. \n       Ready the greatbow, then twist the bowstring to fire a mighty greatarrow that can penetrate through enemies. \n\nThrough and Through is a Skill in Elden Ring. Through and Through is a regular skill available when you equip the Greatbow.\n\nHow to get Through and Through\n\nDefault skill for the Greatbow.\nCan be found on an Invisible Scarab just outside Volcano Manor in the Altus Plateau. Strike the Scarab when the footsteps are near and you will acquire this Ash of War.\n\nElden Ring Through and Through Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Through and Through.\nFP Cost: 0 (stance), 9 per shot.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nHolding the Skill button will prepare the user into a stance, preparing the bow. When inputting light or heavy attacks, the user quickly shoots the respective arrow from their slots, consuming FP (and stamina while in combat). \n\nWhen the stance is being held, stamina regen is reduced by approximately 80%.\n\nThe arrow fired with this skill will pass through all enemies in its path. \n\nThis is especially effective when used in tight spaces, or enemies are lined up in formation.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nShots receive a damage penalty\nDoesn't go through enemies.\n\nThrough and Through Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's QUESTION
What are the details of the Through and Through archery skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Through and Through archery skill in Elden Ring uses a greatbow in an oblique stance to fire a mighty greatarrow that can pass through enemies, with an FP cost of 9 per shot and reduced stamina regen by 80% while holding the stance.
