\n\n Windy Crystal Tear \n\nType\nCrystal Tear\n\nEffect \n\n        Enhances dodge rolls for a time in mixed physick \n\nWindy Crystal Tear is a Crystal Tear in Elden Ring. Crystal Tears can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to enhance its effect.\n\nA crystal tear formed slowly over the ages where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground.\nCan be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick.The resulting concoction increases the effectiveness of dodgerolls for a certain duration. However, damage taken is also increased during the concoction's effect.\n\nWindy Crystal Tear Effect in Elden Ring\nWindy Crystal Tear enhances dodge rolling for a certain duration. Frame data assumes 60 FPS.\n\nLight/Medium Roll Invulnerability Duration +19% (+5 iframes, 31 Total)\nHeavy (Fat) Roll Invulnerability Duration +25% (+6 iframes, 30 Total)\nDamage Negation -15%\nDuration: 3 min.\n\nDodge rolling effectiveness does not stack with the Crucible Feather Talisman.\n\nWhere to find Windy Crystal Tear\nThe item can be found in Caelid in a pit southeast of the Nomadic Merchant. One of the birds watches over it, facing south. QUESTION
Why is Windy Crystal Tear said to enhance dodge rolling but also increase damage taken during its effect?

ANSWER
Windy Crystal Tear enhances dodge rolling due to increased invulnerability frames but also raises damage taken by 15% during its effect.
