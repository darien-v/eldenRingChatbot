\n\n Ruin-Strewn Precipice \n\nA steep precipice with abundant caves in the insides all the way through\n\nRuin-Strewn Precipice is a Location in Elden Ring. The Ruin-Strewn Precipice is found in Liurnia of The Lakes. It is an alternative way to the Altus Plateau instead of the Grand Lift of Dectus. Its entrance is through the Miners' caves, accessed through the various stairs and platforms above Ravine-Vailed Village. It is full of Giant Bats and Imp Miners, with Miners in the inside of the caves and Land Octopuses towards the top.\n\nRuin-Strewn Precipice Map\n[Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nSites of Grace in Ruin-Strewn Precipice Elden Ring\n\nMagma Wyrm [Map Link]\nRuin-Strewn Precipice [Map Link]\nRuin-Strewn Precipice Overlook [Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Ruin-Strewn Precipice\n\nThere are no NPCs at this location.\nThere are no Merchants at this location.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Ruin-Strewn Precipice\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nKukri\nCracked Crystal\nGolden Rune [5]\nGolden Rune [6]\nRune Arc\nLightning Grease\nSoft Cotton\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSmithing Stone [3]\nSmithing Stone [4]\nSmithing Stone [5]\nSomber Smithing Stone [4]\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nMagma Wyrm's Scalesword\nSerpent-God's Curved Sword\n\nUnique, Ashes, and Keys\n\nLost Ashes of War\n\nElden Ring Ruin-Strewn Precipice Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nMiner\nLand Octopus\nGiant Land Octopus\nVulgar Militiamen\nGiant Bat\nChanting Winged Dame\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nMagma Wyrm Makar\n\nRuin-Strewn Precipice Walkthrough\nMoving along Ruin-Strewn Precipice is pretty much a straightforward process, as the caves on the inside will help you reach the top and important locations on the way, i.e. Sites of Grace.  Serpent-God's Curved Sword can be found a few levels below from backtracking at this location. Elden Ring Map Link. You'll find a giant Land Octopus and some of her kids along with a lootable body containing Smithing Stone (3). You don't have to clear the giant octopus to obtain the weapon. Circle her and loot the corpse to obtain it. If you hear the singing bat, you're passing the right area. \nIf you make your way towards the Singing Bat song, you can obtain the Lost Ashes of War if you drop off the wooden platform behind it. It'll be on a body at the edge of the cliff.  Elden Ring Map here.\nAfter defeating Magma Wyrm Makar, a Site of Grace appears in front of the large opening in the cavern opposite the entrance. That's down the ladder located to the left of where the Giant Bats were scavenging the corpse.\n3x Smithing Stone (4) against the walls of the area where a winged old woman is praying (one of them is a Giant Bat's drop).\n1x Smithing Stone (3) held by a corpse, on the roof with the Giant Land Octopus.\n1x Smithing Stone (3) under a tent on your right when coming across the second singing winged-old-lady.\n\nThere are also 2x Smithing Stone (5) within the location. The first one is fairly straightforward to get, as it lies on the floor in the cabin beside the Ravine-Vailed Village Site of Grace, before entering the caves (Map Link). The second one is not that exposed, as it is on a corpse hanging from a stone ledge midway up the large platform inside the caves. To get it, you need to jump down from the platform once it reaches the ledge's height or above. QUESTION
How do I obtain the Serpent-God's Curved Sword in the Ruin-Strewn Precipice in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
You can obtain the Serpent-God's Curved Sword by backtracking a few levels below at Ruin-Strewn Precipice, near a giant Land Octopus, inside a lootable body containing Smithing Stone (3), without having to clear the octopus.
