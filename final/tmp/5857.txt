CONTEXT
\n\n Stormcaller \n\n        One of the skills that channel the tempests of Stormveil. Spin armament to create surrounding storm winds. Repeated inputs allow for up to two follow-up attacks. \n\nStormcaller is a Skill in Elden Ring. Stormcaller is a regular skill that can be found on Ashes of War, it can also be used on Swords capable of slashing, Axes, Hammers, and polearms (small and colossal weapons excepted). Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Stormcaller in Elden Ring\n\nStormveil Castle: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the northern section of the castle, past the courtyard and just before the Secluded Cell Site of Grace. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Stormcaller Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Stormcaller.\nFP Cost: 9\nHeavy Attack FP Cost: 11\nDeals 24+12+12 (total 48) stance damage  \n\nThis skill can stagger most regular enemies such as knights and omens.\n\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nMotion speed, attack power and poise damage have been increased.\nSkill size and poise damage have been increased.\n\nStormcaller
