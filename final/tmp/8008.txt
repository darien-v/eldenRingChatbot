\n\n Leyndell Soldier Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 64\nHP Cost 0\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons two Leyndell soldier spirits \n\nLeyndell Soldier Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Leyndell Soldier Ashes summons two Leyndell Soldier spirits.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of two Leyndell soldiers.  Spirits of soldiers each equipped with a spear and greatshield. The soldiers of Leyndell fight their battles defensively. During the Shattering, they were the ramparts that fortified the royal capital.\n\nWhere to find Leyndell Soldier Ashes\nWhere to Find Leyndell Soldier Ashes\n\nFound in Sainted Hero's Grave in Altus Plateau. In the room with the collapsing floor. Start back from the beginning, avoid the center portion which will collapse, and retrieve from the corpse on the other side of the room.
