QUESTION
How can players effectively use the Golden Arrow in Elden Ring against Enemies and Bosses?

ANSWER
Players can effectively use the Golden Arrow in Elden Ring against Enemies and Bosses by dealing holy damage, preventing undead from reviving, and piercing enemies' armor.
