You may encounter Borealis the Freezing Fog, who lingers at the outskirts towards the entrance of the church, but despite the encounter, you can choose to not engage in combat. He is an optional boss though, and defeating him will reward players with additional Runes and a Dragon Heart.\nIf you continue in the same direction, you will eventually come across the small ruins of the church. It will be surrounded by Spirit Jellyfish. Go ahead and active the Site of Grace, then head to the centerpiece statue and obtain the Sacred Tear. At the short cliff overlooking the church, left of the entrance, head up the tip to obtain x3 Smithing Stone (7)  [Elden Ring Map Link]. In this area, players can also obtain a Key Item, Somberstone Miner's Bell Bearing (3) [Elden Ring Map Link], which unlocks Somberstone (5) and (6) upgrade materials sale at the Twin maiden Husks in the Roundtable Hold. Found in a corpse just outside the First Church of Marika. QUESTION
Why is the First Church of Marika in Elden Ring left desolated and destroyed?

ANSWER
The First Church of Marika in Elden Ring is left desolated and destroyed due to the lingering presence of Borealis the Freezing Fog and the optional combat encounters that players can choose to engage in.
