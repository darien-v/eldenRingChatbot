QUESTION
Why is it important to be near a rebirth monument in order to summon spirits from ashes in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Being near a rebirth monument when summoning spirits from ashes in Elden Ring is important because it allows you to summon the spirits only when in close proximity to the monument, as indicated by a specific icon on the screen.
