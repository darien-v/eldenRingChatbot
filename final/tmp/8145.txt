QUESTION
Why do the Drake Knight Gauntlets provide Dmg Negation and various defense properties in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Drake Knight Gauntlets provide Dmg Negation and various defense properties in Elden Ring because they are black iron gauntlets worn by drake knights, symbolizing their pride as dragon hunters and their pursuit of dragon strength for its beauty and awe-inspiring nature.
