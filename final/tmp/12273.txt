The Magma Breath spell conjures a wyrm head that spews volatile magma in front of the caster. \n\nOne of the incantations of Dragon Communion.Transforms caster into a wyrm to spew magma breath.Allows one follow-up attack.Those who have performed the Dragon Communion will findtheir humanity slowly slipping away. Once they fully succumb totheir fate, they are left no more than wyrms that crawl the earth.\n\nWhere to find Magma Breath\nWhere to find Magma Breath:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 1 Dragon Heart. Gelmir in order to unlock this Incantation. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Magma Breath Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 40\nDeals fire damage\nCan be cast twice before landing around the 2,5 second mark.\nUnlike the named Breath spells, cannot be cast while jumping.\nCasting this spell immediately after casting Catch Flame at midair changes the casting animation Why is performing the Dragon Communion necessary to unlock the Magma Breath Incantation in Elden Ring?
