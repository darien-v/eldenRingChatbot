\n\n Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nGravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing allows you to buy items from the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nBell bearing of an unfortunate gravity stone peddler who was slain by the Bell Bearing Hunter.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to the following items:\n- Gravity Stone Fan- Gravity Stone Chunk\n\nGravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing Location in Elden Ring\nThe Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing can be found in the following location:\n\nDropped by the Bell Bearing Hunter that spawns at the Isolated Merchant's Shack in Dragonbarrow. Like the other spawn locations he will only spawn at night. He will spawn right outside the shack where the merchant usually sits.[Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing Use\nGravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing can be handed in to the Twin Maiden Husks in order to gain access to the following items for purchase:\n\nGravity Stone Fan\nGravity Stone Chunk\n\nElden Ring Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing Notes & QUESTION
Why is offering the Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing to the Twin Maiden Husks important in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Offering the Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing to the Twin Maiden Husks is important in Elden Ring as it allows access to purchase the Gravity Stone Fan and Gravity Stone Chunk, which are new items essential for progression and gameplay enhancements.
