\n\n Giant Rat Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost -\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons three giant rat spirits \n\nGiant Rat Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Giant Rat Ashes summons 3 giant rat spirits. The rat spirits appear at a distance from the summoner, swarming upon their victim to start the fight.\nThe number of rats summoned increases with the Ashes upgrade level. At +6, the ashes will summon 4 giant rats. At +10, 5 rats will be summoned.\nGiant Rats can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Giant Rat.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of three giant rats.\nSpirits that do not require FP to summon.The rat spirits appear at a distance from the summoner, swarming upon their victim to start the fight.Even as spirits, these vermin seem to multiply endlessly. Enhancing these ashes can increase their numbers.\n\nWhere to find Giant Rat Ashes\nWhere to Find Giant Rat Ashes\n\nLocated at West Windmill Pasture, in the middle of the stable with the rats.
