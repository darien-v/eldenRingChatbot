QUESTION
Why is Rivers of Blood described as a cursed weapon that has felled countless men in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Rivers of Blood is described as a cursed weapon that has felled countless men in Elden Ring due to its association with Mohg, the Lord of Blood, and the demonic proposal made to its wielder Okina, resulting in an insatiable thirst for blood.
