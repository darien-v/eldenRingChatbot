QUESTION
Why is Sir Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing, encountered as a boss in Leyndell, Ashen Capital, an alternate version of the Royal Capital accessible only after making significant progress in the story in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Sir Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing, is encountered as a boss in Leyndell, Ashen Capital, an alternate version of the Royal Capital accessible only after making significant progress in the story in Elden Ring due to his association with Queen Marika's will and the fearful realization of an end that should not be.
