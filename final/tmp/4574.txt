\n\n Ash of War: Golden Slam \n\nAffinity\nSacred\n\nSkill\nGolden Slam\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament\n\nAsh of War: Golden Slam is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Golden Slam provides Sacred affinity and the Golden Slam Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Sacred affinity and the following skill:\n"Golden Slam: Skill of the avatars who protect Minor Erdtrees. Jump up high into the air and crash down on the ground ahead. The resulting pratfall sends golden shockwaves in all directions."\nUsable on all melee armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Golden Slam Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Golden Slam:\n\nAltus Plateau: There's a Teardrop Scarab that spawns inside one of the buildings of the Woodfolk Ruins that's right next to the Minor Erdtree in the area, in the same building as Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [19]. Defeat it to obtain the ashes.
