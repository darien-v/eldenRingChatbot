QUESTION
Why is it beneficial to upgrade the potency and number of uses of the Flask of Cerulean Tears in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Upgrading the potency and number of uses of the Flask of Cerulean Tears in Elden Ring is beneficial because it increases the efficiency of healing FP and the overall restoration capabilities of the flask.
