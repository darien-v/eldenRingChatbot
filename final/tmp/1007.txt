\n\n Church of Vows \n\nIt is here, at the Church of Vows, that the great houses of the Erdtree and the Moon were joined by the matrimonial bond between red-haired Radagon and Rennala of the Full Moon. That is why it holds in view the monuments of both houses, the Erdtree of the Capital and the Academy of Raya Lucaria.\n\nChurch of Vows is a Location in Elden Ring. The Church of Vows is found in Liurnia of the Lakes, atop a plateau on the eastern landmass. It can be accessed by following the roads heading north along the eastern landmass. An alternate route is available by way of a Waygate from Raya Lucaria Academy. The Church of Vows is home to Miriel, Pastor of Vows, a giant amicable turtle that can speak.\n\nChurch of Vows Map\nThe Church of Vows is found in Eastern Liurnia. It can be reached by heading northwest from the Artist's Shack. [Elden Ring Map Link].\nAn alternate route is available by way of a Waygate at the Raya Lucaria Academy. The Waygate is found in the northeast of the academy, near Moongrum, Carian Knight. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs & Merchants in Church of Vows\n\nMiriel, Pastor of Vows: Steward of the location. Upon your first meeting, Miriel will tell the story of the church and offer to share its knowledge on Sorceries, Incantations, as well as Lore related to Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon and Lord Radagon.\nCheck the right side of the church's entrance for a chest containing the Golden Tailoring Tools and Gold Sewing Needle key items.\nLocated at the center of the church, a statue of Lady Rennala stands out. Player's can interact with the statue as an "Atone" option is displayed. If you are in possession of a Celestial Dew, atoning for your sins will reverse any antagonizations you have with NPCs, whether caused by accident or on purpose, making them friendly again. However, if the NPC has been killed atonement will not revive them. Celestial Dews are key items obtained from various sources. For details, see the Celestial Dew page.\nThere is a Bell Bearing Hunter boss that can be fought at the church if you come in during night time. It drops the Meat Peddler's Bell Bearing upon defeat. If you are having trouble spawning the boss, rest at the Site of Grace outside, advance til night and then rest once more. The boss should spawn afterwards.\n\nElden Ring Church of Vows Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nThe Church of Vows can be reached through a Portal.
