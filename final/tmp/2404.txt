This was worn by Old Knight Istvan. The corroded metal is reinforced with rock-hard scales, making it highly effective against non-physical attacks. Istvan is one of a few wizened Tarnished who survive to this day. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Scaled Set in Elden Ring\n\nCan be found after killing the first of three invasion targets, Old Knight Istvan, in the volcano manor questline.
