\n\n Cathedral of Manus Celes \n\nA large church atop the southern plateau of Liurnia. Ranni awaits her lord in the depths of the church, guarded by her loyal Dragon.\n\nCathedral of Manus Celes is a Location in Elden Ring. The Cathedral of Manus Celes is found at the Moonlight Altar plateau, high above in the southwest Liurnia of the Lakes. The Moonlight Altar is accessible only after making significant progress in Ranni's questline, up to the point where you defeat Astel, Naturalborn of the Void in the Grand Cloister and gain access to the lift in the Deep Ainsel Well, which leads up to the plateau. Players can reach the Cathedral of Manus Celes by heading northeast from the Moonlight Altar site of grace.\n\nCathedral of Manus Celes Map\nThe Cathedral of Manus Celes is situated in the north of the Moonlight Altar plateau, overlooking Liurnia of the Lakes. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Cathedral of Manus Celes\n\nRanni the Witch [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Cathedral of Manus Celes\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nDragon Heart\nStarlight Shards\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials at this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nAdula's Moonblade\nDark Moon Greatsword\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nNo Uniques, Ashes or Keys at this location\n\nElden Ring Cathedral of Manus Celes Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nNo regular enemies at this location\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nGlintstone Dragon Adula\n\nCathedral of Manus Celes Walkthrough\nRegardless of whether the player fought the Glintstone Dragon Adula at the Three Sisters sub-region in the north or not, it will be present at this location. It will be found guarding the fields leading up to the Cathedral of Manus Celes and can finally be killed at this location. Adula drops 3 Dragon Hearts and the Adula's Moonblade sorcery spell upon defeat, as well as  120000 Runes.\nInside the Cathedral, you will find a total of 9 Starlight Shards scattered across the Cathedral proper. One more can be found outside in the north, by the large stone monument. There is a Site of Grace in the center of the Cathedral.\nLook towards the south for a cave amongst the rubble.\n\nDrop down into the cave and follow the linear path to find the body of Ranni sitting on the remains of the Fingers.\n\nApproach the body and there will be a prompt for you to place the Dark Moon Ring on her finger, initiating a cutscene.\n\nAfterwards, Ranni, now fully freed will appear before you. She will confirm that you are now her consort, and promises that you will meet again. She awards you the Dark Moon Greatsword before disappearing.\n\nThis will give the player the option of completing the "Age of the Stars" ending at the end of the game.\n\nElden Ring Cathedral of QUESTION
Why is the Glintstone Dragon Adula guarding the Cathedral of Manus Celes and what significance does it hold in Ranni's questline in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Glintstone Dragon Adula is guarding the Cathedral of Manus Celes as part of Ranni's questline in Elden Ring, where defeating it allows players to free Ranni and progress towards the "Age of the Stars" ending.
