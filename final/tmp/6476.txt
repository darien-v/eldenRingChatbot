\n\n Carian Greatsword (Skill) \n\n        Carian royal prestige embodied in a skill. Transform blade into a magical greatsword and swing it down. Can be charged to increase its power. \n\nCarian Greatsword (Skill) is a Skill in Elden Ring. Carian Greatsword (Skill) is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to Swords. This skill is similar to the Carian Greatsword sorcery spell, transforming the user's weapon into a magical greatsword to swing at enemies.\n\nHow to get Carian Greatsword Skill in Elden Ring\n\nSold by Sorcerer Rogier at Stormveil Castle or the Roundtable Hold for 2,500 .\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Rogier's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring Carian Greatsword Skill Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Carian Greatsword.\nFP Cost: 16\nThis Skill is Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nDeals 20 Poise damage on hit, 25 when fully charged.\nOutperforms Carian Grandeur in terms of damage if Carian Grandeur is only charged once, but charges more slowly, making it harder to use.\n\nCarian Greatsword Demonstration\n\nCarian Spellknight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's How can I effectively utilize the Carian Greatsword Skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
