The Loretta's War Sickle scales relatively equally with Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence, it is a good Weapon for Quality builds who also incorporate Intelligence, and enjoy the defense and utility offered by Loretta's Slash.\n\nIntricately crafted silver war sickle wielded by Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree. Originally given for service as a personal guard to Carian royalty, the weapon's blue glintstone has been replaced with unalloyed gold.\n\nWhere to Find Loretta's War Sickle in Elden Ring\nThe Loretta's War Sickle weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree upon defeat. [Map Link] Video Location\n\nElden Ring Loretta's War Sickle Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Loretta's Slash.\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War.\nLoretta's War Sickle can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones.\nAttack pattern is unique and faster compared to other halberds, as the normal attacks are diagonal upper cuts. \nShares the same moveset as the Guardian's Swordspear while providing more range, but poorer scaling for builds purely focused on Intelligence.\nShares the same body animations with Winged Scythe's Skill, Angel's Wings. \nLoretta's War Sickle carries a unique version of Loretta's Slash which has quality scaling instead of only scaling with Intelligence.\nDespite the boss using this weapon being able to use it as a spellcasting catalyst, this weapon has no such property in the player character's hands.\nSell Value:   500\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
