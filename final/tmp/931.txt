\n\n Divine Tower of East Altus \n\nSeen last before accessing to the Forbidden Lands.\n\nDivine Tower of East Altus is a Location in Elden Ring. It is one of the six Divine Towers. The Divine Tower of East Altus is found in Capital Outskirts. Found at the very east of the Leyndell, Royal Capital region.\n\nDivine Tower of East Altus Map\n\n[Elden Ring Map here] \n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Divine Tower of East Altus\n\nNo NPCs can be found in the vicinity of this location.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Divine Tower of East Altus\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nN/A\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nN/A\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nN/A\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nOmenkiller Rollo\nMorgott's Great Rune\nMohg's Great Rune\n\nElden Ring Divine Tower of East Altus Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nN/A\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nFell Twins\n\nHow to Reach the Divine Tower of East Altus\nStart at central Leyndell’s Avenue Balcony site of grace, then go up the stairs and turn left.  Follow the road to the large door out of the central city.  Go out the door and up the stairs in the ashy sandy area past a vicious mob.  Follow the path and a door to the left will hold a wooden court-room style elevator.   The route after the elevator leads both to another elevator to the Forbidden Lands or also to a different southeast exit with a lone Leyndell Soldier patrolling a path - approach him and you'll be ambushed by the Fell Twins. After you defeat them and reap the rewards of this battle, the way forward leads to the Divine Tower.\nThis is the only Divine Tower where you can activate two Great Runes - Morgott's and Mohg's.\n\nElden Ring Divine Tower of East Altus Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\nThere is a Two Fingers on a mound of earth at the top of this tower, in the centre of eight meteorite fragments.\nThe door entering this divine tower faces south-southeast and the entrance to the top faces east-northeast, an angle of 90 degrees off entering the tower. QUESTION
Why are there no NPCs found in the vicinity of the Divine Tower of East Altus in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
No NPCs are found near the Divine Tower of East Altus in Elden Ring because the main focus of this location is the battle against the Fell Twins and the subsequent activation of Morgott's and Mohg's Great Runes.
