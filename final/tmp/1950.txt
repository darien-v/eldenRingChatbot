\n\n INTELLIGENCE-KNOTCRYSTAL TEAR \n\nType\nCrystal Tear\n\nEffect \n\n        Temporarily boosts intelligence in mixed physick. \n\nIntelligence-knot Crystal Tear is a Crystal Tear in Elden Ring. Crystal Tears can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to enhance its effect.\n\nA crystal tear formed slowly over the ages where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground.\nCan be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick.The resulting concoction boosts one's intelligence for a certain duration.\n\nINTELLIGENCE-KNOT CRYSTAL TEAR LOCATION IN ELDEN RING\nWhere to Find Intelligence-knot Crystal Tear:\n\nFrom Road to the Manor Site of Grace, get to the bottom of the cliff (directly East) and follow the cliff's wall North. Guarded by a few Lesser Fingercreepers and one Fingercreeper. [Map Link]\nNote: Despite the map showing a road down from the Main Caria Manor Gate grace point, there is no safe path to reach the knot from here as it's actually a very tall cliff.
