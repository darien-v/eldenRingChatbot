\n\n Gavel of Haima \n\nSpell Type\nGlintstone Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 22\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Attacks using a magic greathammer \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 25 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nGavel of Haima is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Gavel of Haima spell creates a magical weapon that strikes enemies from above. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the glintstone sorceries of the Academy of Raya Lucaria.Creates a magical greathammer and slams it down.Allows one follow-up attack.Drawn from the scholarly conspectus of Haima, the adjudicator of the academy, this sorcery employs might as a means to quell conflict.\n\nWhere to find Gavel of Haima\nWhere to find Gavel of Haima:\n\n Can be found in the chest at the top of the Converted Fringe Tower in the Liurnia of the Lakes region. [Map Link] It requires the Erudition gesture to unlock: Give Thops the Academy Glintstone Key (Used to gain access to Converted Towers)   \n\nElden Ring Gavel of Haima Guide\n\nGlintstone Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 38\nDeals magic damage\nHas very good stance-breaking capabilities\nIf dual wielding staves, you can bypass the single follow-up attack and chain this repeatedly by casting from one hand and then the other, alternating each time. \nNote: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost reduced from 25 to 22, the hammer portion of the attack now deals increased damage and the attack does more stamina damage against guarding enemies.
