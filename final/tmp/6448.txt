Rennala's Full Moon spell creates a moon projectile that dispells sorceries it hits and temporarily reduces enemies magic damage negation. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSorcery associated with the Carian queen.\nUses the caster as a vessel to incarnate a full moon, then sends it floating toward foes. The full moon dispels all sorcery that touches it, and temporarily reduces magic damage negation for those it strikes.\nQueen Rennala encountered this enchanting moon when she was young, and later, it would bewitch the academy.\n\nWhere to find Rennala's Full Moon\nWhere to find Rennala's Full Moon:\n\n Obtained when unlocking the Remembrance of The Full Moon Queen's power by handing it over to Enia at Roundtable Hold. \n\nElden Ring Rennala's Full Moon Guide\n\nFull Moon Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 32\nDeals magic damage\nThe Carian Regal Scepter can boost this Sorcery\nPlayers can use Rennala's Full Moon to incarnate a full moon and launch it at foes. It dispels all sorcery that touches it, and temporarily reduces magic damage negation for those it strikes. The reduced magic damage negation on struck foes lasts for one minute and reduces the target's magic negation by 10%. \n\nThe negation reduction is multiplicative and therefore increases all Magic damage dealt to the target by 10% regardless of the target's base negation.\n\nThis is a Power Shred Debuff and will not stack with other debuffs of the same type. \n\nSee Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking debuffs.\n\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 55 to 47.\n\nBest Renalla's Full Moon Buffs\nUse these Buffs to maximize the performance of Renalla's Full Moon.
