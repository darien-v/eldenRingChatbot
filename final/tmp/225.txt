\n\n Street of Sages Ruins \n\nThe sages of Caelid are no more, and their former district is now the dwelling of perfumers and their poisonous flowers\n\nStreet of Sages Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Street of Sages Ruins is found in Caelid. It's accessible early game by taking the teleporter chest from Limgrave's Dragon-Burnt Ruins and exiting Sellia Crystal Tunnel. Alternatively, you can find this location by exploring the swamp area of Caelid\n\nStreet of Sages Ruins Map\nThe Street of Sages Ruins is found on the northwest portion of Aeonia Swamp in Caelid. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Street of Sages Ruins\n\nMillicent may be encountered in this area within the Swamp of Aeonia as an invader.  When defeated, does not effect the NPC and drops a Furlcalling Finger Remedy\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Street of Sages Ruins\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nAeonian Butterfly\nToxic Mushroom\nGolden Rune (4)\nPerfume Bottle\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials at this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nRock Sling\nMeteorite Staff\nPerfumer Traveler's Set\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nPerfume Bottle\n\nElden Ring Street of Sages Ruins Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nMiranda Sprout\nServant of Rot\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no Bosses at this location\n\nStreet of Sages Ruins Walkthrough\nOf note in these ruins are the Traveler's Set and Perfume Bottle that can be looted from a corpse in the corner of one of the ruined structures, guarded by several Poison Flowers and a Servant of Rot. Heading northwest, you can find a large group of Servants of Rot guarding a set of stairs leading to an underground area where you can loot the Rock Sling sorcery spell. In the southwest, there is another set of ruins guarded by more Poison Flowers. There is a corpse hanging from a window in this building that contains the Meteorite Staff.\n\nMeteorite Staff can be found on a corpse hanging from a window in a small room with several poison flowers.\nPerfumer Traveler's Set: Traveler's Hat, Perfumer's Traveling Garb, Traveler's Gloves, Traveler's Slops can be found on a corpse in a corner in the ruins.\n\nElden Ring Street of Sages Ruins QUESTION
How can one effectively navigate Street of Sages Ruins in Elden Ring to find all the valuable items and defeat the enemies present?

ANSWER
To effectively navigate Street of Sages Ruins in Elden Ring, loot the Traveler's Set and Perfume Bottle, defeat Miranda Sprout and Servant of Rot enemies, and explore corners for valuable items like the Rock Sling and Meteorite Staff.
