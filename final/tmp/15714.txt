QUESTION
Who can wield the Godslayer's Greatsword in Elden Ring and what are its primary scaling attributes?

ANSWER
The Godslayer's Greatsword in Elden Ring can be wielded by those with Strength 20, Dex 22, Fai 20 and its primary scaling attributes are Dexterity and Faith.
