It is a hulking humanoid with stubby wings and a massive axe. This is an optional boss as players don't have to defeat it to advance.\nMisbegotten is the normal enemy form of this boss.\n\nThe misbegotten are held to be a punishment for making contact with the Crucible, and from birth they are treated as slaves, or worse.\n\nElden Ring Scaly Misbegotten Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss.\nClosest Site of Grace: Morne Tunnel\nMultiplayer allowed for this boss\nYou can summon Spirit Ashes for this boss\n\nElden Ring Scaly Misbegotten Location\nThe boss of Morne Tunnel.\n\nScaly Misbegotten Combat information\n\nHealth: 1,360 HP \nDefense: 102.6\nStance: 65\nParryable: Yes\nIs vulnerable to a critical hit after being stance broken or parried\nCan be backstabbed\nDrops 2,000, Rusted Anchor\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nStandard: 0\nSlash: 10\nStrike: 0\nPierce: -10\n\nMagic: 0\nFire: -20\nLightning: 0\nHoly: 0\n\nThe negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. attacks are very slow, so you can easily dodge roll swings then get in a few hits.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nKeep your distance—all of its attacks are very slow, which should give you plenty of time to run away and prepare a ranged attack.\n\nScaly Misbegotten Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nSwing\nSlowly raises its axe, walks forward a bit if necessary, and swings it\nRoll
