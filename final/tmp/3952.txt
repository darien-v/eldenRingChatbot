\n\n Ancient Lightning Spear \n\n        Imbue the armament with the ancient dragons' red lightning, then throw it as a spear. Can be charged to increase its power. \n\nAncient Lightning Spear is a Skill in Elden Ring. Ancient Lightning Spear is a unique skill that can be used on the Bolt of Gransax.\n\nHow to get Ancient Lightning Spear in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on the Bolt of Gransax.\n\nElden Ring Ancient Lightning Spear Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 25\nThis Skill is Chargeable\nDeals 100% Lightning Damage and 9 stance damage\nAttack power scales with weapon upgrade level and Dexterity\nUsing this skill moves you forward slightly as you fling the spear. Be careful when firing it from the edges of cliffs or platforms.\nKnocks back enemies if fired while fully charged or flinch those tougher. It also benefits from Godfrey Icon\nDue to the high speed of the projectile, this skill can still hit enemies who sidestep in response to ranged attacks (like Malenia)\n\nLightning Dragoon Written QUESTION
Why is the Ancient Lightning Spear skill beneficial for players in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Deals 100% Lightning Damage, 9 stance damage, knocks back enemies when charged or flinches tough opponents, benefits from Godfrey Icon, can hit enemies sidestepping, and moves player slightly forward when used.
