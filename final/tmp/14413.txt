QUESTION
Why is the Exile Armor considered a good balance of defensive properties in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Exile Armor in Elden Ring is considered a good balance of defensive properties due to its Dmg Negation, high resistance to various types of damage (Phy, Strike, Slash, Pierce, Magic, Fire, Ligt, Holy), and solid Resistance stats (Immunity, Robustness, Focus, Vitality, Poise).
