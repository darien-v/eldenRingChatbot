The Champion Set is a Light Weight armor that exchanges protection for advanced mobility, catering to more agile Classes and playstyles. Armor provides our character with protection and different resistance against all types of damage and negative status effects. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor. \n\n Armor reserved for the badlands' bravest, proof that the wearer has slaughtered countless foes. \n\nWhere to find Champion Set in Elden Ring\nYou can find the Champion Set in:\n\nStarting equipment for the Hero Class\nSold by Nomadic Merchant Caelid South. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nChampion Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\nIn Elden Ring, the Champion Set is composed of 4 different armor pieces.
