\n\n Weathered Map \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        A simple map, badly weathered  \n       Examine using the corresponding button \n\nWeathered Map is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs\n\nA simple, badly weathered map.Left discarded and forgotten for some time.\nDepicts streaks of amber light.\n\nWhere to find Weathered Map in Elden Ring\nCan be bought from Pidia, Carian Servant in Caria Manor. Can be bought from Twin Maiden Husks after giving Pidia's Bell Bearing to them.\n\nElden Ring Weathered Map Tutorial Guide\n Shows a spot just north-east of Altus Highway Junction Grace where Amber Starlight can be looted. [Map Link]\n\nTravel to the Altus Highway Junction grace\nTravel East along the road towards the large stairs; do not travel up the stairs\nUpon reaching the bottom of the stairs, immediately turn North, leaving the paved road\nContinue North through a tunnel in the ridge\nCollect your reward at the statue\n\nElden Ring Weathered Map Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 Weathered Map.\nYou cannot store Weathered Map.\nYou cannot sell Weathered Map.\nThere seems to have been different conditions on the map's availability pre-launch and before patch 1.03. Currently, the map is available immediately upon talking, or after purchasing other items such as cookbook and ash of war\n\nElden Ring Info Items\n\nAbout Adding Affinities  ♦  About Adding
