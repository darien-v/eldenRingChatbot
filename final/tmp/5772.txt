CONTEXT
\n\n Viper Bite \n\n        Give the shield a quick shake to temporarily bring the bronze viper to life, inciting it to lash out and bite foes. \n\nViper Bite is a Skill in Elden Ring. Viper Bite is an exclusive skill for Coil Shield Wielders. The Shield is shaped as a snake, by using this skill, you unleash the snake that performs a bite attack from a pretty long distance and then quickly go back to the original shield form. Updated to Patch 1.09.\n\nHow to get Viper Bite in Elden Ring\n\nThis skill is exclusive for the Coil Shield. The Coil Shield is found on a body inside the Volcano Cave, northeast of the Ninth Mt. Gelmir Campsite.\n\nElden Ring Viper Bite Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 8\nDeals 12 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nDeals Physical Damage and inflicts Deadly Poison buildup\nDamage scales with Strength\n\n Patch Changes\nPatch 1.09 \n\nIncreased projectile generation speed and reduced the recovery time.\n\nPatch 1.07:\n\nAttack power and poison status buildup have been increased.\nPoison effect duration and increased damage caused by poison have been extended.\nFixed a bug where the skill Viper Bite could inflict poison instead of deadly poison.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Viper Bite Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.09. QUESTION
Why is Viper Bite considered a unique and exclusive skill for Coil Shield wielders in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Viper Bite is considered a unique and exclusive skill for Coil Shield wielders in Elden Ring because it is activated by giving the shield a quick shake to bring a bronze viper to life, unleashing it to perform a bite attack from a long distance and then reverting quickly back to the shield form, dealing physical damage and inflicting deadly poison buildup.
