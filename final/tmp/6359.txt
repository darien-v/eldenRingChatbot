\n\n Oracle Envoy \n\nLocation\nLeyndell Royal CapitalMiquella's Haligtree\n\nDrops\n 368 - 1653 RunesEnvoy's HornStrip of White Flesh\n\nOracle Envoy is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Reside in Leyndell, playing their trumpet-like instrument, will engage the player once in their sights. They blow bubbles from their instruments. They are smallest variants of the Oracle Envoy Giant and the Large Oracle Envoy.\nThe spirit of four (4) Oracle Envoys can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Oracle Envoy Ashes.\n\nMonstrous band of musicians who employ sacred arts. It is said that when Oracle Envoys appear playing their pipes, they do so to herald the arrival of a new god, or age.\n\nElden Ring Oracle Envoy Locations\nWhere to Find Oracle Envoy:\n\nLeyndell, Royal Capital\nMiquella's Haligtree\n\nElden Ring Oracle Envoy Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 368 - 1653 Runes\n\nEnvoy's Horn\n2.00%\nN/A\n\nStrip of White Flesh\n4.00%\nN/A\n\nOracle Envoy Combat information\nWherever two numbers are listed, the first is the value for the Oracle Envoys in Leyndell, the second is for the ones in Haligtree.\n\nHealth: 626, 1217 HP\nPoise: 30\n\nAbsorptions\n\nPhy (Standard): -10\nPhy (Slash): -10\nPhy (Strike): 10\nPhy (Pierce): -10\n\nMagic: 0\nFire: 20\nLightning: 20\nHoly: 20\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked.
