Worn by the Dung Eater, their form is a vision of the landscape of his mind, and his appearance as he wished to see it. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Omen Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Omen set can be found after killing the Dung Eater in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds at the Leyndell Royal Capital.\nKilling the Dung Eater right after giving him Seluvis's Potion allows you to get both the Omen Set and the Dung Eater Puppet. However, should you leave the area or quit and reload, his body would disappear, preventing you from getting his armor set.\nAlternatively, after completing his quest line his armor will be found on his body after reloading the area. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nOmen Set Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nOmen Helm\nOmen Armor\nOmen Gauntlets\nOmen Greaves\n\nOmen Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nEach individual piece of the Omen Set increases the damage of Omen Bairn and Regal Omen Bairn by 5%, for a total of 20% additional damage.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
