Stormhill is the upper half area of Limgrave, between its central river and Stormveil.\n\nStormhill is a stormy region with unrelenting north winds, its inclement weather keeping most settlements at bay, and allowing for the proliferation of wildlife and other, sturdier creatures. Its tall cliffs are favored by Eagles, but boars and wolves also roam. Since the breaking, spectral Spirit Jellyfish guard the graves on the upper cliffs.\n\nStormhill Map & Map Fragments for Elden Ring\n\nNo Map Fragments: There are no map fragments in this area\n\nSites of Grace & Sub-Locations in Stormhill Elden Ring\nSites of Grace\n\nCastleward Tunnel [Map Link]\nDeathtouched Catacombs [Map Link]\nDivine Tower of Limgrave [Map Link]\nLimgrave Tower Bridge [Map Link]\nMargit, the Fell Omen [Map Link]\nSaintsbridge [Map Link]\nStormhill Shack [Map Link]\nWarmaster's Shack [Map Link]\n\nSub-Locations\n\nStormhill Shack\nWarmaster's Shack\nDeathtouched Catacombs\nDivine Tower of Limgrave\nStormhill Evergaol\nStormgate\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Stormhill\n\nIron Fist Alexander - Found stuck in the ground west of the Saintsbridge Site of Grace.
