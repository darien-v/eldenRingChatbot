Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. \n\nFlight feather of a bird of prey.Material used for crafting items.Commonly used for arrow fletchings.\n\nFlight Pinion Location in Elden Ring\nDROPPED BY\n\nEnemy\nDrop rate\nAdditional info\n\nEagle\n62.5%\n-\n\nGuillemot\n47.5%\n-\n\nWarhawk\n15.00%\n-\n\nFARMING LOCATIONS\n\nChurch of Elleh: Travel directly west, killing Eagles along the way, until you reach a cliff. Safely drop down onto the square-shaped ruin to find six Guillemots, then continue down the cliffs to the west to reach another square-shaped ruin with seven Eagles. Continue northwest along the beach to find more birds. Use ranged attacks to prevent them from flying away.\nBelow the Well Site of Grace in Siofra River: Turn around, kill 4 eagles on the right, 2 eagles on the left, then walk back to the grace. Use ranged or AOE attacks. \n\nStarcaller Cry and Devourer of Worlds are the best weapon skill to farm here. Gravitas range is too small and deals inconsistent damage. Most other AoEs have the problem of blowing the bodies off the cliff. They also scare away the other Eagles around you.\nThe second best option is a Bow, because it doesn't scare away any other birds.
