Both feature many sharp tail barbs, large mandibles, and heads with a central cracked hole showing something glowing underneath. Both creatures also have bodies made of rock, reminiscent of meteorite. They also both use gravity magic. These beasts may be younger forms of Astel, as there are many Malformed Stars found underground, and there are two Astels fought as bosses. QUESTION
Why is the Full-Grown Fallingstar Beast considered an optional boss in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Full-Grown Fallingstar Beast is considered an optional boss in Elden Ring because players do not need to defeat it in order to advance in the game, although doing so yields useful Items and Runes.
