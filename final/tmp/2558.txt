QUESTION
Why is the Longbow considered a good weapon for long ranged combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Longbow in Elden Ring is considered a good weapon for long ranged combat due to its ability to fire arrows over great distances, reaching further and dealing more damage compared to a shortbow.
