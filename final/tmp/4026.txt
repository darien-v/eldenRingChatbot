\n\n Twinblade Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.7\n\nEffect \n\n Increases damage of final hit in attack chain by 45% \n\nTwinblade Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Twinblade Talisman increases the damage of the final hit in an attack chain by 45%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a twinblade and a confessor.Enhances the final hit ending a chain of attacks.\nThe twinblade technique is a tradition of the confessors, who closely guard the secret of how they preserve the momentum of their attacks. Thus is the final strike of their onslaught all the more deadly.\n\nTwinblade Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nTwinblade Talisman increases the damage of the final hit in an attack chain by 45%.\n\nTwinblade Talisman Location in Elden Ring\n\nCastle Morne: From the Behind the Castle Site of Grace, drop down to the western end of the castle and follow the path along the outer walls. There is a tower section of the walls in the westernmost corner. Climb up the ladder and go around the tower to find a chest where the talisman can be found. [Map link] [Video location]\n\n Twinblade Talisman Effective Damage Increase\n While the Talisman does increase the final hit damage by 45%. If Motion values are taken into consideration the effective damage increase in most weapons is Actually 55%, with the exceptions being:\n\nZamor Curved Sword with an increase of 65%\nHand Axe, Forked Hatchet and Icerind Hatchet with an increase of 53%\nColossal Weapons, Colossal Swords and Great Spears with an increase of 53%\nWhips with an Increase of 53%\nGlintstone Staffs and Sacred Seals with an increase of 47%\nTorches With an increase of 50%\nSmall Shields, Medium Shields and Greatshields with an increase of 50%\n\nElden Ring Twinblade Talisman Notes & Tips\n\nSell value:  500\nDespite the name "Twinblade Talisman" it is quite uneffective on Twinblades and other weapons with long R1 combos. However it is very useful with larger weapons with very short R1 combos (IE Colossal Weapons, Colossal Swords and Great Spears)\nWorks very effectively with both the L1 attack combos of dual Spears and dual Great Spears.\nOn a 3 hit light attack combo, the dmg bonus increase of the total combo is about 13.6%. Or ~15.4% if calculating with Motion values\nYou can start with a heavy attack, replacing the first attack in a light attack combo, and still proc the final hit. This seems good for 3 hit combo weapons giving you a Heavy + Light + Light attack combo.\nDoes not work with heavy attack combos or Ashes of war combos.\nPatch 1.09: Effect increased from 20% to
