Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring Spinning Slash Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Spinning Slash.\nFP Cost: 6 (12)\nThis Skill is not Chargeable.\nThis Skill (Ash of War variant) cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nDeals 33 stance damage\nThe Dragon Halberd version of this skill imbues the weapon with Ice Lightning (Lightning Damage and Frostbite buildup).\nThis skill performs a 360-degree attack that spins again with an additional input.\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nPoise damage against enemies when used with the following weapons has been increased: Greatsword, Curved Greatsword, Twinblade, Greataxe, Spear, Great Spear, Halberd, and Reaper.\n\nSpinning Slash Demonstration\n\nBuilds with Spinning Slash \n\nBlackflame Spellblade Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Vanquisher Build Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Spinning Slash Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How to execute the Spinning Slash Skill in Elden Ring correctly?

ANSWER
To execute the Spinning Slash Skill in Elden Ring correctly, slash foes as your body spins, connecting into a final heavy attack with the Dragon Halberd, and input an additional spin for the 360-degree attack. The Dragon Halberd version imbues the weapon with Ice Lightning.
