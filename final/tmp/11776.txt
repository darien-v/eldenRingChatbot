They are some of the most numerous defenders of these dungeons but are otherwise unimpressive in terms of combat ability.\n\nLoyal Soldiers sworn to the Haligtree. While rather unimpressive on their own, they are often found in large groups, using numbers to their advantage.\n\nElden Ring Haligtree Soldier Locations\nWhere to Find Haligtree
