CONTEXT
\n\n Spectral Lance \n\n        Skill of the headless Mausoleum Knights. Hurl a phantasmic spear at foes. \n\nSpectral Lance is a Skill in Elden Ring. Spectral Lance is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to Polearms, (Reapers excepted). Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Spectral Lance\n\nAsh of War: Spectral Lance - Raya Lucaria Academy: When you reach the end of the courtyard where it is heavily guarded by multiple Zombie Dog and Glintstone Zombies. Turn left and you'll see a path that is lit with a torch, down the slope, you'll find a Teardrop Scarab, kill it to obtain this item.\n\nElden Ring Spectral Lance Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Spectral Lance.\nFP Cost: 9\nDeals 25 stance damage\nRecieves headshot bonus\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nDeals 5x the Poise Damage of the weapon on hit, allowing the user to stagger enemies from quite far away.\nEffective range can be buffed by the Arrow's Reach Talisman. \nDamage scales based off weapon upgrade level and your arcane stat.\n\nNOTES: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nAttack power has been incrased.\nLong range damage falloff has been reduced.\n\nSpectral Lance Demonstration\n\nElden Ring QUESTION
Why should players consider using Spectral Lance in Elden Ring over other available skills?

ANSWER
Players should consider using Spectral Lance in Elden Ring over other available skills for its increased attack power, reduced long-range damage falloff, and the ability to stagger enemies from quite far away.
