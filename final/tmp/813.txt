Royal Revenant is a grotesque amalgamation of humanoid and arachnid, with its many grafted limbs. The Revenant is extremely aggressive and quick, utilizing its poison attacks to weaken prey. This is an optional boss, as players don't need to defeat it in order to advance in Elden Ring. However, doing so yields useful Items and Runes.\nRevenant is the normal enemy form of this boss.\n\n An obscene and disturbing abomination, blend of a spider-like creature and human limbs resulting in a quite resourceful fighter at close range.\n\nElden Ring Royal Revenant Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Road to the Manor\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Royal Revenant Location\nIn a hidden room underneath Kingsrealm Ruins in northwest Liurnia of the Lakes. The stairs leading down to the room are hidden by an illusory floor in the northeast section of the ruins. Attacking or rolling on the illusory floor dispels it, revealing the stairs. Keep your distance from the Royal Revenant, and circle it to avoid its many lunging attacks. When it does lunge, dodge forward and to the side and use the time it takes for it to rotate to get in a substantial hit. Consider bringing a weapon with high critical damage, like Misericorde, to take full advantage of the stance breaks.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nThe Healing Blow: Immediately cast Heal as the Royal Revenant first crawls out the ground. This will break its stance, but rather than getting a critical hit just cast Heal again to finish it off.\nThe Great Circle: Keep circling the revenant and backing away. It's easier to dodge its distance-closing attacks, and this should give you time to get off regular attacks.\n\nRoyal Revenant Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nSwing\nLunges forward a small distance and swings the arms on one side in one direction or another\nRoll in the same direction as its swing to avoid getting hit by its back arms\n\nJumping Swing\nPulls back its right arms, then jumps forward\nRoll forward and to your left through its swinging arms, then turn and attack it\n\nRetreating Swing\nPivots 270° and swings its arms in the direction it's turning, then scrambles backwards\nRoll backwards once you see it begin to pivot\n\nQuick Slam\nMove forward a small distance and slams its many hands down in front of it\nRoll forward and to either side, then turn and attack it\n\nRearing Slam\nRears back, then lunges forward and slams its many hands down in front of it\nRoll to either side once you see it begin to fall\n\nTwisting Leap\nJumps forward with its arms raised, then swipes them clockwise at you and it passes by, finally turning its body 180° as it lands\nAs it leaves the ground, roll away perpendicular to its direction of movement\n\nWild Swings\nScrambles forward, swinging its arms wildly\nRoll to either side, then follow it and prepare to strike once it's finished moving\n\nPoison Gout\nShoots a stream of poison forward that coats the floor for a time\nRoll to either side\n\nPortal Teleport\nOpens a glowing portal in the ground and crawls into it, then crawls out again elsewhere in the arena\nScan the arena to try to see where it's emerging, and stick close to walls to limit your blind spots.
