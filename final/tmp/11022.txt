The Meteorite Staff scales primarily with Intelligence and is a good Weapon for early game as it provides S scaling with only 18 Int requirement. It also boosts gravity-type Sorceries. \n\nStaff embedded with a dark purple glintstone, said to be the fragment of a meteorite. Boasts high sorcery scaling and boosts gravity sorceries as well.However, this staff cannot be strengthened in any way.\n\nWhere to Find Meteorite Staff in Elden Ring\nThe Meteorite Staff weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nAeonia Swamp in Caelid: In the southwestern building of the Street of Sages Ruins. The staff is on a corpse hanging from a ledge in a small room with poison flowers and a rune.
