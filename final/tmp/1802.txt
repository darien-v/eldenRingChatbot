These ruins are said to be the remains of a giant mausoleum enshrining an ancient dragon, guarded by chosen beastmen who wield weapons clad in lightning.\n\nWhere to find Beastman of Farum Azula in Elden Ring\nBeastman of Farum Azula can be found in:\n\nGroveside Cave [More info] [Map link]\nDragonbarrow Cave [More info] [Map link]\n\nBeastman of Farum Azula Combat information\n\nParriable: Yes\nIs vulnerable to a critical hit after being stance broken or parried\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nStandard: 0\nSlash: 0\nStrike: -10\nPierce: 0\n\nMagic: 0\nFire: -20\nLightning: 20\nHoly: 0\n\nThe negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 means the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nBeastman of Farum Azula Boss Guide for Elden Ring\n\nBeastman of Farum Azula Fight Strategy\nThe Best Tips for Beastman of Farum Azula:\n\nBait its long attack combos and counterattack once it's finished.\nTry to avoid blocking since its attacks drain so much stamina.\nUse Rotten Stray Ashes to take advantage of its relatively low resistance to Scarlet Rot.\n\nMelee Strategy\nBait and Punish: Bait the Beastman's attacks until it completes a full combo before attacking a couple of times and then backing away. Its weapon drains a lot of stamina if you block it, so after you land a few hits it's better to dodge back far enough that you're out of range of its swings. When in doubt, stay as far away as you can. This boss doesn't have a ton of stance, so charged or jumping heavy attacks will stance-break it easily. \nParrying: Most of the Beastman's attacks can be parried, except for the Two-Hand Slam and the last two attacks of the 6-Hit Combo (the uppercut and the overhead attack).\nMagic and Ranged Strategy\nKeep Your Distance: This fight is considerably easier at range. You can bait out its combos the same way you would in melee, but you can punish them from range rather than running into danger. Just keep your distance when it begins attacking and only block if you absolutely have to.\n\nAttacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nOne-Handed Double Swing\nCleaver: Grabs its cleaver with one hand and swings it twice horizontally.\nParry the first swing or roll away. Avoid blocking, since it will lose you a lot of stamina.\n\nTwo-Handed Double Swing\nCleaver: Grabs its cleaver with two hands and swings it twice horizontally. Much faster than the One-Handed Double Swing, but covers much less range.\nParry the first swing or roll away. Avoid blocking, since it will lose you a lot of stamina.\n\nCleaver Stab\nCleaver: Pulls its cleaver back and then stabs it forward\nRoll to your right to avoid the follow-up\n\nOverhead Slam\nCleaver: Pulls its cleaver over its head and slams it down in front\nRoll to your right to avoid the follow-up\n\nSideswipe\nCleaver: Steps to its left and swings its cleaver clockwise along with it\nRoll right and get in a hit while it recovers\n\nSpinning Slice\nCleaver: Spins in a full clockwise circle ending with a horizontal slice\nRoll right, since the hitbox is mostly towards the end of the attack\n\nBarbaric Roar\nCleaver: Lets loose a fearsome roar dealing damage in a small area of effect\nRoll backwards and prepare to dodge the follow-up combo\n\n6-Hit Combo\nCleaver: Roars, then attacks six times: four horizontal swings, an upwards vertical attack which launches it into the air, then a final overhead slam.\nDodge away and keep your distance during most of the combo, then roll in during the final slam and attack during its recovery animation.\n\nTwo-Hand Slam\nCleaver: Grabs its cleaver with two hands, jumps forward, and slams it into the ground.\nThere's a long delay before it jumps, so be careful not to roll too soon. Once you successfully dodge, you have time to get in a few attacks of your own.\n\n: Attack can be parried\n\nBeastman of Farum Azula (Cleaver) Combo Chart\n\n Beastman of Farum Azula Groveside Cave \n\nDrops\n1,000Flamedrake Talisman\n\nHP\n1,417\n\nBeastman of Farum Azula (Groveside Cave)\n\nOptional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Groveside Cave\nMultiplayer allowed for this boss\nYou can summon Spirit Ashes for this boss\n\nBeastman of Farum Azula Combat information\n\nHealth: 1,417 HP\nDefense: 101.3\nStance: 65\nDrops 1,000, Flamedrake Talisman\n\nResistances\n\nPoison: 226 / 268 / 366 / 656\nScarlet Rot: 226 / 268 / 366 / 656\nHemorrhage: 310 / 408 / 698 / 1155\nFrostbite: 310 / 408 / 698 / 1155\n\nSleep: 169 / 197 / 239 / 337\nMadness: Immune\n\nThe resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it.
