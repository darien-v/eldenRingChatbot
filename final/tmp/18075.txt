\n\n Miranda Sprout \n\nLocation\nAcross the Lands Between\n\nDrops\n\n 14 - 778 RunesPoisonbloomMiranda PowderMiquella's Lily\n\nMiranda Sprout is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. Miranda Sprouts are hostile at all times. They are smaller variants of the Giant Miranda Sprout and are typically seen with them.\nThe spirit of five (5) Miranda Sprouts can be summoned for aid in battle by using Miranda Sprout Ashes.\n\n Sweet little carnivorous blossoms that feed upon human flesh. They creep slowly, scattering toxic pollen. They are vulnerable to fire.\n\nMiranda Sprout Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Miranda Sprout:\n\nPlayers can encounter Toxic Miranda Sprouts within Waypoint Ruins in Limgrave.\nToxic Miranda Sprouts can be found within Stillwater Cave., in Liurnia.\nSome Magic Miranda Sprouts can be found in Liurnia of the Lakes, in the forested areas south from the Church of Vows.\nRotten Miranda Sprouts can be found in the Swamp of Aeonia in Caelid, as well as in the smaller body of Scarlet Rot liquid just outside the entrance to Gaol Cave. There are also both mobile and rooted Rotten Miranda Sprouts among the southern swamp banks of Aeonia.\nFrenzied Miranda Sprouts can be found in the forested area west the Frenzied Flame Village.\nSome Toxic Miranda Sprouts can be encountered in Leyndell, Royal Capital.\nSeveral Rotten Miranda Sprouts can be found on Miquella's Haligtree.\n\nMiranda Sprout Drops in Elden Ring\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 14 - 778\n\nPoisonbloom\n10.00%\n"A" Variant\n\nPoisonbloom\n15.00%\n"B" Variant\n\nPoisonbloom\n20.00%\n"C" Variant\n\nMiranda Powder\n5.00%\n"A" and "C" Variant\n\nMiranda Powder\n15.00%\n"B" Variant\n\nEnemy Type\nBuildup Type/Special Features\nAdditional Information\n\n Toxic Miranda Sprout \n\nEmits a green cloud of pollen that inflicts Poison buildup.\n\nCan be pink or yellow. QUESTION
How can players effectively combat the various types of Miranda Sprouts in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can combat Toxic Miranda Sprouts at a distance, use fire to deal with Frenzied Miranda Sprouts, look out for Scarlet Rot buildup with Rotten Miranda Sprouts, and rely on high Magic Damage Resistance for Magic Miranda Sprouts.
