QUESTION
Why is the Grafted Blade Greatsword considered a revenger's weapon burdened with oceans of anger and regret?

ANSWER
The Grafted Blade Greatsword is considered a revenger's weapon burdened with oceans of anger and regret due to its storied history of a lone surviving champion from a vanished country, claiming the swords of an entire clan, embodying vengeance and the weight of past conflicts.
