\n\n Bell Bearing Hunter \n\nLocations & Drops\n\n Warmaster's Shack: 2,700 Runes (NG) 675 Runes (NG, Co-op)Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing Church of Vows: 6,000 Runes (NG) 4,500 Runes (NG, Co-op Host) 1,500 Runes (NG, Co-op Phantom)Meat Peddler's Bell Bearing Hermit Merchant's Shack: 20,000 Runes (NG) 15,000 Runes (NG, Co-op Host) 5,000 Runes (NG, Co-op Phantom)Medicine Peddler's Bell Bearing Isolated Merchant's Shack (Dragonbarrow): 50,000Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing \n\nStronger VS \n\nHemorrhage\n\nFrostbite\n\nWeaker to\n\nPierce\n\nLightning\n\nBell Bearing Hunter is a recurring Field Boss in Elden Ring. He is a hunter of shopkeepers who only appears at night and features long-distance melee attacks. This is an optional boss as players don't need to defeat it to advance in Elden Ring.\nIn order for the Bell Bearing Hunter to appear, you must have already entered Stormveil Castle at least once. He will appear only at night, so if you don't see him, make sure to rest again at the nearby Site of Grace after passing time to nightfall.\nSee Elemer of the Briar for the Great Enemy version of this boss. Defeating the real Elemer will not prevent the Bell Bearing Hunters from appearing, however. Despite his fearsome reputation, the Bell Bearing Hunter cannot permanently kill merchants.\n\n Elemer murdered numerous instructors and merchants, and was known as the Bell Bearing Hunter.\n\nElden Ring Bell Bearing Hunter Locations\n\nWarmaster's Shack [More Info] [Map Link]\nChurch of Vows [More Info] [Map Link] \nHermit Merchant's Shack [More Info] [Map Link]\nIsolated Merchant's Shack (Dragonbarrow) [More Info] [Map Link]\nBell Bearing Hunter only spawns provided 3 requirements (in this order it will be faster): 1.it must be night; 2.the player needs to trigger the title of the specific location (like "Warmaster's Shack"); 3.the player needs to rest at the near site of grace.\n\nIf it isn't night, the player needs to change the time to night first, then they can start from step 1 here above, which is already done.\nThere are no other particular requirements generally speaking, like someone claims they first need to meet Margit in Stormveil.\n\nBell Bearing Hunter Combat information\n\nStance: 80\nParryable: Yes, but 2 parries are required per stance break\nIs vulnerable to a  critical hit  after being stance broken or parried\nDamage: Standard, Strike, Pierce, Magic\nSee Elemer of the Briar for a full boss guide\nIf you can climb on nearby scenery and get above the Bell Bearing Hunter's head, none of his attacks can hit you. Use spells like Night Maiden's Mist that can hit through structures to damage him. Even if it seems like he's going out of range, you can simply wait and he will be back in a moment.\nIf you have sufficient health, you can use Lion's Claw to tank his hits as the startup animation has superarmor which means you can't be staggered out of it by most attacks. Hit him enough times to stance break him, perform a critical and then heal while he is getting up. You will still need enough health to tank his hits and enough damage to kill him before you run out of flasks.\n\nNegations (or Absorption)\n\nStandard: 35\nSlash: 35\nStrike: 35\nPierce: 10\n\nMagic: 40\nFire: 40\nLightning: 20\nHoly: 40\n\nThe negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 means the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\n Bell Bearing Hunter  Warmaster's Shack  \n\n__image__\n\nDrops\n 2,700Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing\n\nHP\n2,495\n\nElden Ring Bell Bearing Hunter Boss (Warmaster's Shack)\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Warmaster's Shack\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou cannot summon  Spirit Ashes for this boss in its normal location, but if you draw it northeast to the guard post you can summon it there.\n\nBell Bearing Hunter Combat information\n\nHealth: 2,495 HP \nDefense: 103\nDrops 2,700, Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing\n\nResistances\n\nPoison: 316 / 414 / 704 / 1161\nScarlet Rot: 316 / 414 / 704 / 1161\nHemorrhage: 517 / 807 / 1264\nFrostbite: 517 / 807 / 1264\n\nSleep: 316 / 414 / 704 / 1161\nMadness: Immune\n\nThe resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it.
