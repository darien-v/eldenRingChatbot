QUESTION
How to effectively use Flowing Form in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Flowing Form in Elden Ring allows the hammer to behave like a whip, dealing 18 stance damage, triggering certain effects if it hits multiple enemies, costing 16 FP, and being a default skill of the Nox Flowing Hammer.
