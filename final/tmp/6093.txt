CONTEXT
\n\n Piercing Fang \n\n        A Skill used by the Bloody Finger hunter Yura. Starting with your blade held horizontally, you make a powerful thrust that cannot be blocked. \n\nPiercing Fang is a Skill in Elden Ring. Piercing Fang is a regular skill that can be found on Ashes of War, it is mainly equipped with large thrusting weapons. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Piercing Fang in Elden Ring\n\nCan be acquired from Nagakiba as part of Bloody Finger Hunter Yura questline. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Piercing Fang Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Piercing Fang. \nFP Cost: 16 on use.\n24 stance damage (Thrusting sword)30 stance damage (Straight swords, Katana, Twinblades, Spears, Halberds)36 stance damage on (Greatsword, Heavy Thrusting Sword, Great Spear)\nThis Skill is not Chargeable.\nThis Skill can be Parried\nOn activation, the user prepares the armament, then thrusts forward with a fast lunge in a single direction.\n\nThis direction can be aimed at the start of the animation, depending on movement input from the player.\n\nThe lunge completely bypasses the block/guard of enemies struck by it.\n\nHit enemies do not receive stamina damage if they were blocking, since the Weapon Skill hits the enemy directly.\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nMotion Speed, Attack Power and Poise Damage have been increased.\nThe delay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\nTiming between the end of the skill and being able to attack and to roll has been reduced.\n\nPiercing
