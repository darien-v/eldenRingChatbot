Bloodsoaked Set is a three piece cloth set that is worn by the NPC invader Inquisitor Ghiza. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Bloodsoaked Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Bloodsoaked Mask and Bloodsoaked Manchettes can be looted from the ground in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds.\n\nFrom the Underground Roadside site of grace, head into the main tunnel, turn left, and take out the first Omen. The Bloodsoaked Mask and Bloodsoaked Manchettes are on the ground on the right side of the tunnel shortly after the enemy.\n\nThe Official's Attire can be looted from a corpse after gaining access to the Mountaintops of the Giants. \n\nFrom the Forbidden Lands site of grace, turn back and head towards the elevator. Face southwest before activating the elevator, and then watch for an opening to jump off into. The Official's Attire is on the corpse in this hidden hallway, before the Blade of Calling. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nBloodsoaked Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nBloodsoaked Mask\nOfficial's Attire\nBloodsoaked Manchettes\n\nBloodsoaked Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
