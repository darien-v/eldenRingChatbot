QUESTION
Why is Elden Lord Set considered a lightweight armor option with light magical and moderate physical defense in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Elden Lord Set in Elden Ring is considered a lightweight armor option with light magical and moderate physical defense due to its Damage Negation values, providing moderate resistance across all damage types, while also having relatively low weight compared to heavier armor sets.
