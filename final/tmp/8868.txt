QUESTION
Where can you find Budding Horn in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Budding Horn can be found in Elden Ring by hunting herbivorous beasts like Deer, Sheep, Ancestral Follower, and Ancestral Follower Shaman, as well as at specific locations such as the Church of Pilgrimage and Craftsman's Shack.
