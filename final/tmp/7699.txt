\n\n Arrow's Reach Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.7\n\nEffect \n\n Increases range of various projectiles \n\nArrow's Reach Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Arrow's Reach Talisman increases the range of bows and a few other attacks by 65%. Players can equip talismans in Elden Ring to gain a variety of effects.\n\nA talisman depicting three arrows.Carried by hunters of beasts.\nIncreases the effective range of bows.\n\nArrow's Reach Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nArrow's Reach Talisman increases the effective range of bows (as well as some spells) by 65%.\nIncludes the following, and their Ashes of War:\n\nBows\nCrossbows\nBallistas\nGreatbows\n\nSee Notes & Tips for other, unexpected inclusions. \n\nWhere to find Arrow's Reach Talisman in Elden Ring\nFrom the Stormhill Shack Site of Grace in Limgrave, travel southeast to Stormhill Evergaol. There's a tower nearby, toward the north-east. Dispatch the guards inside, enter the doorway, and open the chest on the left side of the room for the talisman.
