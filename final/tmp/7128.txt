QUESTION
Why do Giant Crabs in Elden Ring have varying effects with their breath attacks, such as building Sleep or Death Blight?

ANSWER
Giant Crabs in Elden Ring have varying effects with their breath attacks, such as building Sleep or Death Blight, potentially due to feeding on different substances in their environments or being linked to other creatures like the Silver Crabs.
