\n\n Glintstone Sorcerer \n\nLocation\nRaya Lucaria AcademySellia, Town of SorceryHermit Village\n\nDrops\n 121 - 363 RunesRaya Lucarian Sorcerer SetAcademy Glintstone Staff\n\nGlintstone Sorcerer is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Glintstone Sorcerers are scholars of the Olivinus and Karolos Conspectus at the Raya Lucaria Academy, who have mastered the use of sorcery. These Raya Lucaria sorcerers wear a stone crown that obscures their identities, together with a distinctive Raya Lucarian Robe.\nThe spirit of a Glintstone Sorcerer, specifically a scholar of the Olivinus Conspectus, can be summoned for aid in battle by using Glintstone Sorcerer Ashes.\n\n Glintstone sorcerers are the descendants of astrologers, a fact that the Carians remain aware of. QUESTION
Why do Glintstone Sorcerers wear stone crowns that obscure their identities?

ANSWER
Glintstone Sorcerers wear stone crowns that obscure their identities due to the fact that they are descendants of astrologers, a piece of knowledge that the Carians are still mindful of.
