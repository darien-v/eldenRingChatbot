\n\n Lesser Wormface \n\nLocation\nAltus PlateauCrumbling Farum Azula\n\nDrops\n 541 - 6812 RunesGold-Tinged ExcrementSacrificial Twig\n\nLesser Wormface is a Enemy in Elden Ring. Lesser Wormfaces are regular enemies, usually seen around the Minor Erdtree in the Altus Plateau, and even in Farum Azula. Like their larger versions, they spew a cloud of Death Blight.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Wormface Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Wormface:\n\nAltus Plateau\nMt Gelmir: Disguised as a Putrid Corpse east of Road of Iniquity grace. Drops a Larval Tear.\nCrumbling Farum Azula\n\nElden Ring Lesser Wormface Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n  541 - 6812 \n\nGold-Tinged Excrement\n25.00%\nN/A\n\nSacrificial Twig\n2.50%\nN/A\n\nLesser Wormface Notes & Tips\n\nHas a Death Blight-inflicting ranged attack, which can be mitigated with the Prince of Death's Pustule or Prince of Death's Cyst talismans and cured using the Order Healing and Law of Regression incantations or Rejuvenating Boluses consumable.\nThe file names for the Wormfaces are Deracine_Male for the small ones, and Deracine_Female for the large ones. Deracine could come from a French word for uprooting trees, or it could mean “displaced.”\nIt is possible that these are corpses that were buried in Catacombs built around Greattree roots (back when "the roots of the Greattree were" purportedly "linked to those of the Erdtree," see the description for Root Resin), and were in the process of becoming a part of the Erdtree, but then displaced or "uprooted" by Deathroot. This would explain why they inflict Death Blight, and why they drop Sacrificial Twigs, which are rumored to have once been "pruned from the Erdtree."\nTheir stick they wield and the cloak they wear QUESTION
Why do Lesser Wormfaces drop Sacrificial Twigs in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Lesser Wormfaces drop Sacrificial Twigs in Elden Ring because the twigs are rumored to have once been "pruned from the Erdtree," possibly indicating their connection to becoming a part of the Erdtree before being displaced by Deathroot.
