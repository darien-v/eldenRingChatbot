CONTEXT
\n\n Lightning Defense \n\nType\nDefensive Stat\n\nEffect\nShows your current defense against Lightning damage\n\nLightning Defense is a Defensive Stat in Elden Ring. Lightning Defense shows your defense power and damage negation against lightning attacks. Stats determine the strengths and weaknesses of your character. Defensive Stats are determined by your Attributes and Equipment. \nLightning Damage is not as common as poison or fire damage, so it is recommended to only focus on Lightning Defense by using equipment at the moment you think you'll need it instead of permanently spending Runes to increase it.\n\nLightning Defense Elden Ring Guide\n\nYour defense power and damage negation against lightning attacks.\nThe former is calculated via your level, while the latter is calculated via defensive gear and active passive effects.\n\nElden Ring How to increase character's Lightning Defense\n\nVS Lightning Defense can only be increased by Rune Level and equipment. There is no stat that directly governs VS Lightning Defense.\n\nRelated Equipment & Items in Elden Ring for Lightning Negation\nYou can use the following Equipment and Items to increase your Lightning Negation:\n\nBoltdrake Talisman (13%/17%/20% depending on variant)\nPearldrake Talisman (5%/7%/9% depending on variant)\nBull-Goat Set offers the best combined Lightning Negation\nMalformed Dragon Set has second best Lightning Negation but is much lighter than Bull-Goat Set\n\nElden Ring VS Lightning Defense Notes & Tips\n\nDefense Increase by Rune Level\nThe soft caps for Rune Level are 71, 91, and 161\nThe formulas for calculating Lightning Defense on Rune Level are as follows: \n\nLevel 1 - 71 --> 40 + 60*((Lvl+79 - 1) / 149)\nLevel 72 - 91 --> 100 + 20*((Lvl+79 - 150) / 20)\nLevel 92 - 161 --> 120 + 15*((Lvl+79 - 170) / 70)\nLevel 162 - 713 --> 135 + 20*((Lvl+79 - 240) / 552)\nThe resulting number is rounded down for the UI menu\n\nStats in Elden Ring\n\nArcane  ♦  Dexterity  ♦  Discovery  ♦  Endurance  ♦  Equip Load  ♦  Faith  ♦  Fire Damage Negation  ♦  Fire Defense  ♦  Focus  ♦  Focus Resistance  ♦  FP  ♦  Holy Damage Negation  ♦  Holy Defense  ♦  HP  ♦  Immunity  ♦  Immunity Resistance  ♦  Intelligence  ♦  Level  ♦  Lightning Damage Negation  ♦  Magic Damage Negation  ♦  Magic Defense  ♦  Memory Slots  ♦  Mind  ♦  Physical Defense  ♦  Poise  ♦  Robustness  ♦  Robustness Resistance  ♦  Runes Held  ♦  Stamina  ♦  Strength  ♦  Vigor  ♦  Vitality  ♦  VS Pierce Damage Negation  ♦  VS Pierce Defense  ♦  VS Slash Damage Negation  ♦  VS Slash Defense  ♦  VS Strike Damage Negation  ♦  VS Strike Defense  ♦  Weight\n\n
