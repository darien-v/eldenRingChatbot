QUESTION
Why are the Commoner's Shoes in Elden Ring considered a lightweight leg armor that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense?

ANSWER
The Commoner's Shoes in Elden Ring are considered a lightweight leg armor that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense because they offer defensive properties such as Dmg Negation, Magic, Fire, Lightning, Holy resistances, and a combination of VS Strike, Slash, and Pierce values.
