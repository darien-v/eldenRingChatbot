\n\n Three Fingers \n\nLocation\nCathedral of the Forsaken\n\nRole\n Quest NPC\n\nVoiced by\n N/A\n\nThree Fingers is an NPC in Elden Ring. Three Fingers is an integral part of The Lord of Frenzied Flame ending and can be found near the Cathedral of the Forsaken, below the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds\n\n Seek audience with the Three Fingers and the flame of frenzy. If you inherit the flame of frenzy, your flesh will serve as kindling and the girl can be spared ...setting you on the righteous path of lordship.— Shabriri\n\nThree Fingers Location in Elden Ring\nThree Fingers can be found near the Cathedral of the Forsaken, below the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds. You will need to remove your armor to open the door.\n\nThis NPC does not move and cannot be interacted with outside of the cutscene where it gives you the Flame of Frenzy.\n\nThree Fingers Questline: How to complete Three Fingers' Quest\nFinding Three Fingers will imbue the character with the Flame of Frenzy which unlocks the Lord of the Frenzied Flame ending. See Endings page.  Interacting with the Three Fingers will also affect the character with large burn scars across their body as well as burning yellow eyes, these can be toggled on or off at Fia's cosmetic mirror in Roundtable Hold or by selecting cosmetics while speaking to Rennala in the Raya Lucaria Grand Library. \nNote: Removing the cosmetics will not remove the Flame of Frenzy nor will it affect the ending.\nNote: The player's burning yellow eyes are removed from their eye sockets when they are not the host.\nNote: Receiving the Flame of Frenzy will prompt Melina to leave your party thinking that you are no longer fit. She will also threaten to kill the player should they become the Lord of Frenzy.She does not appear in the final cutscene if she sacrificed herself to the Flame of Ruin.\n\nElden Ring Three Fingers Notes & Trivia\n\nBy completing Hyetta's questline you will be rewarded with the Frenzied Flame Seal when you speak with her after interacting with the Three Fingers and acquiring the Flame of Frenzy.  She will disappear, leaving the seal in her place as well as 5 Frenzyflame Stones.\nafter the interaction between the player and the three fingers, they will mark your character skin and eyes, it can be removed. Once removed you can't restore the yellow eyes.\nMeeting with the Three Fingers will lock you out of all other ending choices, unless Miquella's Needle is used at Dragonlord Placidusax's arena at Crumbling Farum Azula. This removes the Frenzied Flame, and is a permanent reversal.\n\nElden Ring NPCs\n\nAbandoned Merchant Siofra River  ♦  Aeonian Spirit  ♦  Aeonian Swamp Spirit  ♦  Albinauric Village QUESTION
What role does Three Fingers play in the Lord of Frenzied Flame ending in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Three Fingers imbues the character with the Flame of Frenzy, unlocking the Lord of Frenzied Flame ending in Elden Ring.
