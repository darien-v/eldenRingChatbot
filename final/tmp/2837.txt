CONTEXT
\nSpears are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Spears allow the user to engage in melee combat from a safe distance, keeping them out of harms way. Like Great Spears, Spears allow for attacking with a shield raised, letting users block and attack at the same time. They are also some of the most useful melee Weapons on horseback due to their superior range.\nSpears can be slotted with many different Ashes of War, allowing you to change up their Skills and Damage Type. Play around with them as you acquire more and see what works best for your Build.\nNote: As of Patch 1.08, poise damage of all Spears has been reduced for PVP. \nElden Ring Spear Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Spears in Elden Ring.
