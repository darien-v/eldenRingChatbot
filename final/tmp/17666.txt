CONTEXT
\nFire Giant Incantations are a group of Incantations in Elden Ring that share a specific type of buff criteria. Fire Giant Incantations revolve around drawing power from the Fire Giants and their manipulation of fire. They require Faith in order to be casted.\nThere are a total of 4 Fire Giant Incantations available for players. QUESTION
Why do Fire Giant Incantations in Elden Ring require Faith in order to be casted?

ANSWER
Fire Giant Incantations in Elden Ring require Faith in order to be casted because they revolve around drawing power from the Fire Giants and their manipulation of fire.
