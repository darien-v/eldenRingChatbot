QUESTION
Why are Ballista Bolts specifically designed for the Hand Ballista and Jar Cannon weapons and not compatible with other types of bows?

ANSWER
Ballista Bolts are designed for the Hand Ballista and Jar Cannon weapons because the ammunition for these weapons cannot be used in Greatbows, Crossbows, Longbows, or Shortbows.
