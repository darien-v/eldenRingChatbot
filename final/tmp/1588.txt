Land of Reeds Set is an Armor made from strip of iron fastened together and worn by the warriors of the Land of Reeds, made to reduce damage intake and protect warriors during battle. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Land of Reeds Set in Elden Ring\n\nStarting Equipment for the Samurai Class\nCan be found at the Isolated Merchant's Shack. Very north of Caelid in the Dragonbarrow section.
