War Surgeon Gown (Altered) is part of the War Surgeon Set Armor Set, and  is a lightweight chest armor that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense. War Surgeon Gown (Altered) is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. QUESTION
Why is the War Surgeon Gown (Altered) considered a lightweight chest armor that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The War Surgeon Gown (Altered) is considered a lightweight chest armor that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense in Elden Ring due to its stats, with high Magic, Fire, and Lightning defense, along with decent Physical defense, making it versatile for different types of attacks.
