\n\n Devourer of Worlds \n\n        Charge the scepter with magic and strike it against the ground to steal the HP of all nearby enemies. \n\nDevourer of Worlds is a Skill in Elden Ring. Devourer of Worlds is a skill that allows the user to steal HP of all nearby enemies by hitting the ground with the scepter. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Devourer of Worlds in Elden Ring\n\nDefeault skill of the Devourer's Scepter.\n\nElden Ring Devourer of Worlds Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 35\nDeals 6 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nDamage type is Fire Damage\nDamage scales with weapon upgrade level and Faith\nThe HP drain scales with the number of enemies hit by the skill. It still leeches HP from enemies immune to Fire Damage such as Man-Serpent albeit not doing any damage to it.\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: poise damage has been increased.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Devourer of Worlds Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
What are the effects and details of using the Devourer of Worlds skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Devourer of Worlds skill in Elden Ring allows the user to steal HP from all nearby enemies by hitting the ground with the scepter, dealing 6 stance damage, with a FP cost of 35, and damage scaling with weapon upgrade level and Faith, while the HP drain scales with the number of enemies hit, regardless of their immunity to Fire Damage.
