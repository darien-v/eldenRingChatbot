QUESTION
Why are the Fire Monks guardians of the flame of ruin and what significance does this hold in the lore of Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Fire Monks are guardians of the flame of ruin, forbidden by the Erdtree and said to be kept high in the Mountaintops of the Giants, signifying their role in protecting a powerful and dangerous source of fire linked to the lore of Elden Ring.
