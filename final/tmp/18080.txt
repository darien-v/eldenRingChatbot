\n\n Giant Miranda Sprout \n\nLocation\nEverywhere in the Lands Between\n\nDrops\n\n 256 - 5825 RunesPoisonbloomMiranda PowderCracked Crystal\n\nGiant Miranda Sprout is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. Giant Miranda Sprouts are large toxic flowers that are found all across the Lands Between. They are often surrounded by their smaller variants, Miranda Sprouts.\n\nGiant Miranda Sprout Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Giant Miranda Sprout:\n\nA Toxic Giant Miranda Sprout can be found in Waypoint Ruins in Limgrave.\nA Toxic Giant Miranda Sprout can be found in Stillwater Cave.\nMagic Giant Miranda Sprouts can be found in Liurnia of the Lakes.\nA Toxic Giant Miranda Sprout can be found in a large room on the ground-level of Siofra River.\nRotten Giant Miranda Sprouts can be found in Caelid.\nFrenzied Giant Miranda Sprouts can be found in the wooded area west of the Frenzied Flame Village, and one in Ainsel River Main.\nA Toxic Giant Miranda Sprout can be found in Seethewater Cave.\nRotten Giant Miranda Sprouts can be found in Miquella's Haligtree.\n\nElden Ring Giant Miranda Sprout Combat Information\n\nHealth:\n\n2,212 HP (Limgrave - Waypoint Ruins)\n2,483 HP (Weeping Peninsula - Minor Erdtree, Tombsward Catacombs Entrance, Morne Tunnel Entrance)\n4,393 HP (Siofra River; Liurnia of the Lakes - Mausoleum Compound, Temple Quarter; Stillwater Cave)\n5,212 HP (Perfumer's Grotto; Altus Plateau - Perfumer's Ruins, Unsightly Catacombs Entrance)\n6,301 HP (Seethewater Cave)\n7,998 HP (Ainsel River)\n10,633 HP (Subterranean Shunning-Grounds)\n12,968 HP (Leyndell, Ashen Capital)\n\nPoise: 100\n\nABSORPTIONS\n\nPhy (Standard): 0\nPhy (Slash): -40\nPhy (Strike): 35\nPhy (Pierce): 0\n\nMagic: 0 (The one at Temple Quarter has 40)\nFire: -40 (The one at Temple Quarter has 0)\nLightning: -40 (The one at Temple Quarter has 0)\nHoly: 40\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. Better fought at a distance to avoid clouds of toxic pollen.\nThey deal Strike Damage.\nMagic Giant Mirandas release a myriad of homing magic projectiles that seek out enemies, similar to Star Shower, dealing Magic Damage.\nAny fire damage dealt to the plant sets it aflame and negates the clouds: they get dissipated if present and the plant cannot release them for the duration of the burning animation. \n\nFlame duration can't be reset, however burn can be applied again after it wears off.\nFire source does not set sprout aflame, if it breaks their poise.\n\nThey can slam their flower bud against the ground aside them if the player gets too close.\nThe Frenzied Giant Mirandas will violently flail themselves around in an attempt to put out the fire.\nThere are several variants, but in general their most prominent weaknesses are Slash-type Physical damage, Fire, and Frostbite. Keep in mind that fire damage will "cure" active frostbite (can be followed by more frostbite). They are suspectible to Sleep as well.\nStrong against Strike damage, Lightning, and Holy.\nThey can be poise-broken, but the window to riposte is very slim, as they recover quickly.\nRiposte after poise break is done to the bloom, unlike riposte to the sleeping flower which is done to the stalk.\n\nEnemy Type\nBuildup and Damage Type\nAdditional Information\n\nToxic Giant Miranda\n\nEmits a green cloud of pollen that inflicts Poison buildup.\n\nCan be pink, yellow, or fading from purple to yellow.
