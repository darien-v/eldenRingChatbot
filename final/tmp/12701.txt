\n\n Erdtree's Favor \n\nWeight\n1.5\n\nEffect \n\n Raises maximum \n       HP, \n       Stamina and \n       Equip Load\n\nErdtree's Favor is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Erdtree's Favor raises user's maximum HP, Stamina, and Equip Load. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a special blessing of the Erdtree.Slightly raises maximum HP, stamina, and equip load.\nIt is said that when the Age of the Erdtree began, such blessings were personally bestowed upon their recipients by Queen Marika herself.\n\nErdtree's Favor Effect in Elden Ring\nErdtree's Favor increases HP (3%), Stamina (7%), and Equip Load (5%).\nErdtree's Favor +1 increases HP (3.5%), Stamina (8.5%), and Equip Load (6.5%).\nErdtree's Favor +2 increases HP (4%), Stamina (10%), and Equip Load (8.0%).\n\nWhere to find Erdtree's Favor in Elden Ring\n\nBase talisman:  Fringefolk Hero's Grave: To get there, use two Stonesword Keys on the double Imp Keystone next to the Stranded Graveyard Site of Grace. This will lead to a mini-dungeon with traps. Head down until you see the path narrow on both sides and drop drown at the edge to get access to the secret area. Proceed past the imps and a fire trap to find the item resting at an altar. Two Grafted Scions wait hanging above, so be sure to avoid them when they land and grab it really quickly. [Map link]\n+1 Variant: Subterranean Shunning-Grounds: Can be found in a chest, in the boss room after the boss fight with Mohg, the Omen. [Map link] You do not have to defeat Mohg to loot the chest. You can acquire this mid-fight.Video Location\n+2 Variant: Leyndell, Ashen Capital: Found on top of a branch jutting out of the ground in a large courtyard, guarded by three Lesser Ulcerated Tree Spirits. Fast travel to the Forbidden Lands Site of Grace, and take the elevator back up to the city. Exit the west door, then fight past a knight and few soldiers, and continue down the stairs, then take the large wooden elevator down. You will arrive at a landing overlooking the courtyard, where the beasts and this talisman is located. You can avoid the beasts by hugging the left wall. [Map link] Video Location\n\nElden Ring Erdtree's Favor Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  500\n+1 Variant Sell Value:  1,500\n+2 Variant Sell Value:
