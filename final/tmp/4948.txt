\n\n Seluvis's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nSeluvis's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Seluvis's Bell Bearing can be offered to the Twin Maiden Husks to gain access to new shop items. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story. \n\nPreceptor Seluvis's bell bearing, found upon his perished flesh.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to new items.\n\nWhere to find Seluvis's Bell Bearing in Elden Ring\n\nFound on Seluvis' body in Seluvis's Rise after reaching the end of Ranni's questline. See it on the Elden Ring Map Link.
