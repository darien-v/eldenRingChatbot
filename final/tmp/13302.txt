QUESTION
Why are the Royal Remains Gauntlets said to slowly replenish HP when HP is reduced?

ANSWER
The Royal Remains Gauntlets are said to slowly replenish HP when HP is reduced because they are worn by the unspeaking adherent of Sir Gideon the All-Knowing, and are believed to belong to an ancient lord - the soulless king Ensha, who was known as the lord of the lost and desperate.
