\n\n About United Combat and Combat Ordeals \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        To engage in combat with other players, touch the effigy of Marika in a Colosseum. \n\nUnited Combat and Combat Ordeals can be undertaken in this venue. \n\nIn United Combat two teams of fighters clash. Combat Ordeals are all-out brawls in which every combatant is out for themselves. \n\nAbout United Combat and Combat Ordeals is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs\n\nWhere to find About United Combat and Combat Ordeals in Elden Ring\nObtained automatically when you learn about this in Colosseums. Added to the game in Patch 1.08\n\nElden Ring About United Combat and Combat Ordeals Tutorial Guide\nTouch Marika's effigy at the Roundtable Hold or in a Colosseum to initiate the search for this PVP mode.\n\nUNITED COMBAT\n\nTeam event: Players can play 2v2 or 3v3 events.\nTimed event. This mode lasts 5 minutes.\n1 Flask per life, including a Flask of Wondrous Physick. Replenished upon death. Not cumulative.\nRespawn available. When a player is killed, it will respawn after a period of time.\nThe goal is to eliminate the opponents more times than they eliminate you.\nThe team with more eliminations when the time is up wins.\n\nCOMBAT ORDEAL\n\nFree-for-all event: Players have to fend from themselves against each other.\nTimed event. This mode lasts 5 minutes.\n1 Flask per life, including a Flask of Wondrous Physick. Replenished upon death. Not cumulative.\nRespawn available. When a player is killed, it will respawn after a period of time.\nThe goal is to eliminate the opponents more times than they eliminate you.\nThe player with more eliminations when the time is up wins.\n\nElden Ring About United Combat and Combat Ordeals Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 About United Combat and Combat Ordeals.\nYou cannot store About United Combat and Combat Ordeals.\nSell
