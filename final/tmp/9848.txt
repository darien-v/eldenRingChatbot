QUESTION
Why is the Elden Lord Crown considered a balanced helmet in terms of both Physical and Magical defense in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Elden Lord Crown is considered a balanced helmet in terms of both Physical and Magical defense in Elden Ring because it offers a good balance between Physical and Magical defense, with stats like Phy 3.8, Magic 2.5, Fire 3.6, and Ligt 2.1.
