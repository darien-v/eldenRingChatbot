QUESTION
Where can Miners be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Miners can be found near the bottom of the Ruin-Strewn Precipice, by a campfire north-by-northeast of Bellum Church, surrounded by a four-wheeled wagon cart at the entrance to Gael Tunnel, and in various mines/tunnels such as Limgrave Tunnels, Sealed Tunnel, Morne Tunnel, and more.
