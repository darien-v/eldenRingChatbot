He is one of the assassination targets assigned to Patches by the Volcano Manor. You can find the summon sign to invade his world in Ruin-Strewn Precipice. All NPC Invaders and invadable NPCs can be found here. He is also an NPC Summon against Draconic Tree Sentinel, Magma Wyrm Makar and Starscourge Radahn.\n\n Tragoth is a famed knight of assistance. Countless Tarnished, facing adversity in the Lands Between, have survived thanks only to the Great Horned One's aid.\n\nElden Ring Great Horned Tragoth Locations\nWhere to Find Great Horned Tragoth:\n\nYou can invade Great Horned Tragoth at Ruin-Strewn Precipice Overlook inside Magma Wyrm Makar's boss room after resting at the grace inside the boss room. QUESTION
How can players locate and defeat the Great Horned Tragoth enemy in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can locate and defeat the Great Horned Tragoth enemy in Elden Ring by finding his summon sign at Ruin-Strewn Precipice to invade his world or by summoning him to assist in specific boss battles if not assassinated as part of Patches' quest.
