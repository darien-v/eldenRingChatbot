\n\n Glintstone Dragon \n\nLocation\nMoonlight Altar,Liurnia of the Lakes\n\nDrops\nDragon Heart\n\nGlintstone Dragon is a Creature in Elden Ring. These Glintstone Dragons occupy the Moonlight Altar area in Liurnia of the Lakes. These are the lesser or unnamed versions of the Dragon bosses, Glintstone Dragon Adula and Glintstone Dragon Smarag. Three can be found on the moon-gazing platform west of the Moonlight Altar Site of Grace. When defeated, they each drop a Dragon Heart which is used to trade at the Church of Dragon Communion for Dragon-based Incantations. See also: Dragon Communion Incantations.\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dragon Locations\nWhere to Find Glintstone Dragon:\n\nLiurnia of the Lakes (Moonlight Altar West)  [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dragon Drops\n\nDragon Heart  \n\nGlintstone Dragon Notes & Tips\n\n Dragon type\nThey use the moves of Glintstone Dragon Smarag, though they lack a boss bar, and are weaker.\nGlintstone Dragons have very high Magic resistance, so Intelligence builds should use spells like Rock Sling that deal physical damage.\nMeteorite of Astel with Cerulean Hidden Tear can easily trivialize the fight by raining meteorites that deal physical damage.\nWear armor with high Magic and Physical resistance, like the Banished Knight Set.\nYou can climb the nearby rocks and fight them with ranged attacks, and you will be safe from most of the dragons attacks.\nThey do not respawn when slain.\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone
