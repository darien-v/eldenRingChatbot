\n\n Fire Monk Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 85\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons Fire Monk spirit \n\nFire Monk Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Fire Monk Ashes are used to summon the spirit of a Fire Monk.\nFire Monks can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Fire Monk.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirit of a Fire Monk.  Spirit of a stout monk who monitored the Giants' Flame. Versed in fire incantations, and himself steeled against fire. Though he was charged with keeping watch over the Flame, after many years of solemn duty, he has succumbed to its allure.\n\nWhere to find Fire Monk Ashes\nWhere to Find Fire Monk Ashes\n\nLooted from a chest in the Giants' Mountaintop Catacombs:\nDescend the first lift you find and then step on the plate again to send it back up. When fully raised, there is a second platform on the lift that will take you to the lowest levels of the catacombs. Continue through this path past two frost breath traps. The spell is found in a chest behind the second trap.
