\n\n Festering Bloody Finger \n\nType\nConsumable\n\nEffect \n\n Attempts invasion of other player's world \n\nFestering Bloody Finger is a Multiplayer Item and Consumable in Elden Ring. Multiplayer Items allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.\n\nItem for online play. (Can also be used from the Multiplayer menu.) Consumed upon use.\nAttempts an invasion of another player's world. If successful, you will arrive as an invader (Bloody Finger) with the objective of defeating the Host of Fingers of that world.\nThis furled finger is blackened with blood congestion. QUESTION
Why might someone want to repeatedly use the Festering Bloody Finger item in Elden Ring for invasion attempts?

ANSWER
To engage in competitive gameplay, defeat other players as an invader, and interact with the online community through invasions and PvP encounters.
