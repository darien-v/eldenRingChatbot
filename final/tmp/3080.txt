\n\n Corhyn's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nCorhyn's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Corhyn's Bell Bearing can be offered to the Twin Maiden Husks to gain access to new shop items. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or given by a related NPC, which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nBrother Corhyn's bell bearing, found upon his perished flesh.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to new items.\n\nWhere to find Corhyn's Bell Bearing in Elden Ring\n\nObtained from Brother Corhyn, alongside a Flail and Corhyn's Robes, in Leyndell, Ashen Capital, at the end of Goldmask's questline. Exhaust his dialogue, reload the area, and then pick up these items where he once was.\n\nIf you offered him the Tonic of Forgetfulness while at the bridge near the Stargazer's Ruins, Brother Corhyn will remain there after reaching Ashen Leyndell. Reload the area and collect this item set.\nIf you reached Crumbling Farum Azula without completing the Law of Regression puzzle in Goldmask's questline, these items will be found wherever you last saw Corhyn.\nThese items can also be found by killing Corhyn after he leaves the Roundtable Hold.\n\nMay also be found on a cliff west of the coliseum, in Leyndell, Ashen Capital, without completing Corhyn's questline.\n\nElden Ring Corhyn's Bell Bearing Use\nCorhyn's Bell Bearing can be used to can be used to can be used to gain access to the following items when offered to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold:\n\nUrgent Heal\nHeal\nGreat Heal\nCure Poison\nMagic Fortification\nFlame Fortification\nLightning Fortification\nDiscus of Light\nImmutable Shield\nRejection\nCatch Flame\nFlame Sling\n\nThe incantations you can purchase from this Bell Bearing are directly tied to whatever was available from Corhyn at the time. If you didn't finish the "Regression puzzle", then Immutable Shield will not be available. If Corhyn didn't meet Goldmask, then Discus of Light will not be available. If Corhyn never left the Roundtable Hold, then Great Heal and Lightning Fortification will not be available.\n\nElden Ring Corhyn's Bell Bearing Notes & Tips\n\nNotes & Tips go here.\n\nElden Ring
