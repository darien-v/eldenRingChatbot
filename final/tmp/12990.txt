Barrier of Gold spell increases Magic Damage negation for both user and nearby allies. \n\nOne of the incantations of Erdtree Worship. \nGreatly increases magic damage negation for the caster and nearby allies.\nHold to continue praying and delay activation.\nThis incantation was used by the champions of the Erdtree in the First and the Second Liurnian Wars, during which the red-haired Radagon joined the heroes' ranks\n\nWhere to find Barrier of Gold\nWhere to find Barrier of Gold:\n\nLeyndell, Royal Capital: Dropped by an invisible Teardrop Scarab. Can be found after heading down the stairs and taking the elevator, right after the fight with Godfrey, First Elden Lord (Golden Shade). [Map Link]\nFrom the Erdtree Sanctuary site of grace, head west. Go down the stairs, take the elevator down, and head north down the stairs. Continue northeast and up another set of stairs.
