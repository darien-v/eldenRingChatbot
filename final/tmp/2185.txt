\n\n Fire Defense \n\nType\nDefensive Stat\n\nEffect\nShows your current defense against Fire damage\n\nFire Defense is a Defensive Stat in Elden Ring. Fire Defense shows your defense power and damage negation against fire attacks. Stats determine the strengths and weaknesses of your character. Defensive Stats are determined by your Attributes and Equipment. \nFire is the most common elemental damage, that many enemies inflicts, Sorcerers, Dragons, enemies using torchs are very commonly found and they usually use the torch as a weapon. Even Dungeon traps usually perform great amounts of Fire Damage. Some fire attacks have a great AoE and some other attacks will take you by surprise. So it is wise to consider Fire Defense for PvE builds.\n\nFire Defense Elden Ring Guide\n\nYour defense power and damage negation against fire attacks.\nThe former is calculated via attributes, while the latter is calculated via defensive gear and active passive effects.\n\nElden Ring How to increase character VS Fire Resistance\n\nVS Fire Resistance can be increased by Rune Level and Vigor Level\n\nRelated Equipment & Items in Elden Ring for Fire Resistance\nYou can use the following Equipment and Items to increase your VS Fire Resistance:\n\nFlamedrake Talisman (13%/17%/20% depending on variant)\nPearldrake Talisman (5%/7%/9% depending on variant)\nFire Prelate Set has the highest combined Fire Negation\n\nElden Ring VS Fire Resistance Notes & Tips\n\nDefense Increase by Rune Level and Vigor Level\nThe soft caps for Rune Level are 71, 91, and 161\nThe soft caps for Vigor are 30, 40, and 60\nThe formulas for calculating Fire Defense on Rune Level are as follows: \n\nLevel 1 - 71 --> 40 + 60*((Lvl+79 - 1) / 149)\nLevel 72 - 91 --> 100 + 20*((Lvl+79 - 150) / 20)\nLevel 92 - 161 --> 120 + 15*((Lvl+79 - 170) / 70)\nLevel 162 - 713 --> 135 + 20*((Lvl+79 - 240) / 552)\n\nThe formulas for calculating Fire Defense on Vigor are as follows: \n\nLevel 1 - QUESTION
Why is it important to consider increasing VS Fire Resistance in Elden Ring, especially for PvE builds?

ANSWER
Increasing VS Fire Resistance in Elden Ring is important for PvE builds because fire is the most common elemental damage type in the game, inflicted by sorcerers, dragons, enemies using torches, dungeon traps, and various attacks with great area of effect or that catch players by surprise.
