\n\n Ash of War: Piercing Fang \n\nAffinity\nKeen\n\nSkill\nPiercing Fang\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament\n\nAsh of War: Piercing Fang is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Piercing Fang provides Keen affinity and the Piercing Fang Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Keen affinity and the following skill:\n"Piercing Fang: Skill used by Yura, the Bloody Finger Hunter. Starting with the blade held horizontally, make a powerful thrust that cannot be blocked."\nUsable on medium and large armaments capable of thrusting.\n\nAsh of War: Piercing Fang Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Piercing Fang:\n\nAcquired on the Nagakiba you recieve during Bloody Finger Hunter Yura's questline or by killing him outright. The ash can be removed from the katana. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nAsh of War: Piercing Fang Elden Ring Guide\n\nThis grants the skill Piercing Fang.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Straight Swords, Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds\n\nPiercing Fang
