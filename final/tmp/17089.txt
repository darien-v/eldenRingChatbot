Flame, Cleanse Me spell creates a flame that damages the player, but also alleviates buildup of and cures poison and scarlet rot.\n\nOne of the incantations of the Fire Monks.Creates a fire within that burns away toxins.Alleviates poison and scarlet rot buildup and cures these ailments.This incantation leaves the caster with subtle burns - a reminderthat they must fear the flame.\n\nWhere to find Flame, Cleanse Me\nWhere to find Flame, Cleanse Me:\n\n Found on a corpse at the Fire Monk camp, southeast of the Church of Vows. [Map Link] \nIf you cannot get to the Church of Vows yet it is Southwest of the Eastern Tableland site of grace.\n\nElden Ring Flame, Cleanse Me Guide\n\nFire Monk Incantation\nStamina Cost: 10\nDamages caster for 15 HP\nCures Poison and Scarlet Rot, and alleviates their buildup.\nRemoves Frostbite infliction. \n\nFrosbite removal works due the self inflicted damage being classified as fire damage. \n\nHowever, this only works when Frosbite has been applied to the player. Any frosbite buildup will not be removed.\n\nElden Ring Flame, Cleanse Me Notes & Tips\n\nAny starting class (except Prisoner) can make use of this spell by equipping the Two Fingers Heirloom How can Flame, Cleanse Me alleviate poison and scarlet rot buildup in Elden Ring?
