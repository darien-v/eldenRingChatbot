\n\n Gold Breaker \n\n        Leap up high and, while suspended in midair, imbue the rune shard with light before smashing it down hard onto the ground. \n       The heroic Radagon's signature attack. \n\nGold Breaker is a Skill in Elden Ring. Gold Breaker is an exclusive Skill to Marika's Hammer. Updated to Patch 1.09.\n\nHow to get Gold Breaker in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on Marika's Hammer.\n\nElden Ring Gold Breaker Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 26\nDeals 40 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable.\nDamage scales with weapon AR and deals both holy and physical damage. Holy damage increases with Faith Investment, physical damage increases with Strength and Dexterity investment.\nThe skill consists of the character jumping into the air while swinging the hammer back, levitating momentarily, and then lunging forward in the air to slam the hammer down and cause a large AoE explosion around the point of impact. The lunge portion of the attack travels significant horizontal distance and moves in an arc, bringing the character slightly higher into the air from the levitating point before slamming down at high speed.\nOnly the AoE portion of the skill requires FP; the movement aspects and the hammer strike work even with an empty bar.\nThe AoE explosion covers an area approximately the size of a Runebear.\nAs an airborne skill, it has infinite hyper armour for as long as the characters feet no longer touch the ground.\n\nPatch Changes\nPatch 1.09\n\nReduced the recovery time.\n\nPatch 1.07\n\nAttack Power increased.\n\nElden Ring Gold Breaker Build Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's
