\n\n Land Squirt \n\nLocations\nDragon-Burnt RuinsShores of LimgraveLiurnia of the LakesCapital OutskirtsGiants' Mountaintop Catacombs\n\nDrops\n 13 - 215 RunesGolden RunesStrip of White FleshPoisonbloom\n\nHP\n366 ~ 418\n\nLand Squirt is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. Land Squirts are hostile beings that release a poisonous gas as a defense mechanism. The mere presence near one of these creatures is enough for them to feel threatened, thus spraying their poison. Land Squirts do not move, as they are fixated to the ground. They are small versions of the Giant Land Squirt\nThe spirit of three (3) Land Squirts can be summoned for aid in battle by using Land Squirt Ashes.\n\n The firm, fleshy tubes of the creatures remain rooted to the spot, spraying poisonous liquid over their surroundings.\n\nLand Squirt Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Land Squirt:\n\nPlayers can encounter Land Squirts near water throughout Limgrave. Particularly Dragon-Burnt Ruins.\nLand Squirts can be found in various places across Liurnia Lake.\nGiant Land Squirts can be found in the Swamp of Aeonia sub-region within Caelid.\nGiant Land Squirts can also be found in the stream leading north out of the Lake of Liurnia to the Ravine-Veiled Village at the bottom of the Ruin-Strewn Precipice.\nBoth Land Squirts and Giant Land Squirts can be found in the Giants' Mountaintop Catacombs.\n\nLand Squirt Drops in Elden Ring\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 13 - 215\n\nGolden Runes\n2.00%\nGolden Rune [3], Golden Rune [5], Golden Rune [7] or Golden Rune [9] depending on the area.\n\nPoisonbloom\n8.00%\nN/A\n\nStrip of White Flesh\n4.00%\nN/A\n\nElden Ring Land Squirt Notes & Tips\n\nThey emit clouds of poison but you can easily melee them from the opposite side. QUESTION
Why do Land Squirts release poisonous gas as a defense mechanism?

ANSWER
Land Squirts release poisonous gas as a defense mechanism because they feel threatened by the presence of creatures nearby.
