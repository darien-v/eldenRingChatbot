CONTEXT
\nWhips are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Whips usually inflicts little to moderate damage, and perform poorly against heavily armored Enemies, but have long reach.\nWhips cannot be parried and cannot be used to perform critical attacks.\nWhips can be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, so play around with them and see what you like as you acquire more.\nCompatible Ashes of War include Ground Slam, Golden Slam, Raptor of the Mists, Beast’s Roar, Determination, Royal Knight’s Resolve, Endure, Kick, Quickstep, Bloodhound Step, Flame of the Redmanes, Hoarfrost Stomp, Storm Stomp, Lightning Bolt, Lightning Ram and Hoarah Loux’s Earthshaker. (Needs more testing to determine more compatible Ashes)\n\nElden Ring Whip Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Whips in Elden Ring.
