QUESTION
Why are the Foot Soldier Gauntlets widely issued to foot soldiers in a lord's army and what makes them a good balance of defensive properties in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Foot Soldier Gauntlets are widely issued due to being metal gauntlets that offer a medium weight and a good balance of defensive properties, including Dmg Negation, physical, magic, fire, lightning, and holy resistance, immunity, robustness, focus, vitality, and poise.
