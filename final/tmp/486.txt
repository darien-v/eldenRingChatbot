\n\n Frenzy-Flaming Tower \n\nA tower in north-east Liurnia of the Lakes, atop which is a giant flame causing madness in the surrounding area.\n\nFrenzy-Flaming Tower is a Location in Elden Ring. The Frenzy-Flaming Tower is located in Liurnia of the Lakes, in the northern section of the eastern landmass. This location can be reached by riding south from the Grand Lift of Dectus.\n\nFrenzy-Flaming Tower Map\nFrenzy-Flaming Tower is typically accessed from the north of the eastern landmass, riding south from the Grand Lift of Dectus. [Elden Ring Map here]\nAlternatively, smart use of the jagged rocks north of the Minor Erdtree (Liurnia Northeast) while riding and jumping on Torrent will allow players to reach the Frenzied Flame Village and Frenzy-Flaming Tower without having to go around through the northern section of the landmass.\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Frenzy-Flaming Tower\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Frenzy-Flaming Tower\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nNo Gatherables at this location\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials at this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nHowl of Shabriri (Incantation)\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nNo Uniques, Ashes or Keys at this location\n\nElden Ring Frenzy-Flaming Tower Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nFrenzied Villager\nGiant Rat\nRats\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no Bosses at this location\n\nFrenzy-Flaming Tower Walkthrough\nAbove the tower is a repeatedly-burning flame of frenzy, which will increase your madness levels if you're within view of it. You can use rocks as cover during your approach, or use the ravine to the left of the tower to come behind it, and use the Spiritspring to jump up next to it and quickly get inside the tower, which will protect you from increasing madness. There will be a giant rat and three rats inside, which can be difficult in the close quarters, so be prepared. Climb the ladder, and on one level you will find a chest containing the incantation Howl of Shabriri. Continue to the top to find a group of Frenzied Villagers, and quickly kill them to put an end to the flame of madness affecting the area.\n\nElden Ring Frenzy-Flaming Tower Gallery and Notes\n\nThis QUESTION
Why is Frenzy-Flaming Tower described as a reference to Tolkien's Barad-dûr, the tower of Sauron?

ANSWER
The location is likely a reference to Tolkien's Barad-dûr due to its towering structure containing a repeatedly-burning flame of frenzy, similar to the Dark Tower in the Lord of the Rings, and the presence of Frenzied Villagers akin to Sauron's influence over the land.
