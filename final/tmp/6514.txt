QUESTION
Where can Glintstone Arc be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Glintstone Arc can be found in Elden Ring as a starting item for the Astrologer, or purchased from Sorceress Sellen in Limgrave: Waypoint Ruins, Thops at the Church of Irith, or Twin Maiden Husks at Roundtable Hold after giving them Thops's Bell Bearing.
