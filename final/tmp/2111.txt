CONTEXT
\n\n Memory Slots \n\nType\nBase Stat\n\nEffect\nDetermines how many Sorceries or Incantations players can have ready to cast at one time\n\nMemory Slots is a Base Stat in Elden Ring representing the number of slots available for memorizing Sorceries and Incantations. Memorizing Spells can be done while resting at any Sites of Grace. Certain spells cost more than a single Memory Slot to memorize.\n\nHow to Increases Memory Slots in Elden Ring\nAll Classes begin the game with 2 Memory Slots.\nMemory Stone are a Key Item which, when held, passively increases Memory Slots by 1.\n\nPlayers can hold up to 8 Memory Stones.\n\n Moon of Nokstella is a Talisman which, when equipped, increases Memory Slots by 2.\n\nElden Ring Memory Slots Notes & Tips\nHow do I put stuff in my Memory Slot Elden Ring?\n\nAfter having your desired Equipped Spells or Magic, the first step is to rest at a Site of Grace where you will have a few additional options besides resting at this spot. Choose to Memorize Spell to bring up a menu with your Sorceries and Incantations. From here this will also display your current Memory Slots, Empty Slots and FP. The Slots at the top display your Memory Slots. Select one of the slots then choose which spell you will like to equip into that memory slot. Make sure that you meet the requirements and conditions for the Spells you select to memorize. You may now see these equipped spells from the quick access menu and switch your items here with the designated button depending on the platform you play on. Once selected, attack to use the spell. You may need to press and hold down your attack button until the action successfully activates. This will then successfully deploy the spell and drain some of your FP\n\nStep by Step\n\nGain Memory Slots from Memory Stones\nRest at a Site of Grace\nMemorize Spell\nEquip your spell to use Memory Slot\n\nHow to gain Memory Stones for Memory Slots Elden Ring? \nMemory Stones can be found in multiple Locations and can sometimes be purchased from Merchants in the game. QUESTION
How can players increase their Memory Slots in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can increase their Memory Slots in Elden Ring by obtaining Memory Stones and equipping Moon of Nokstella.
