QUESTION
Why would someone choose to use the Golden Vow skill in Elden Ring over other available skills?

ANSWER
It grants increased attack power and defense to self and nearby allies during combat, boosts damage by 11.5%, and all damage negation by 7.5% for 45 seconds, making it a powerful skill choice.
