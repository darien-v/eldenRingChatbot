QUESTION
Why do Radahn Foot Soldiers only drop the Helm and Tabard of their set, rather than the complete set they wear?

ANSWER
Radahn Foot Soldiers only drop the Helm and Tabard of their set because Godrick Foot Soldiers drop the Foot Soldier Gauntlets and Foot Soldier Greaves.
