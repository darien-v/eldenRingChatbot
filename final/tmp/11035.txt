The Glintstone Staff scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for Striking enemies within range and with the glintstone, is especially used to cast Sorceries.\n\nStaff with a glintstone embedded at the tip. A catalyst for casting sorceries. \nSorceries must be memorized first at a site of lost grace, and a staff must be equipped to cast them.\n\nWhere to Find Glintstone Staff in Elden Ring\nThe Glintstone Staff weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nStarting Equipment for the Prisoner Class\nHas a chance to be dropped by Noble Sorcerers at Caria Manor.\nDrops from a lone Noble Sorcerer, south of the Waypoint Ruins (and west of a small collection of graves). Behind the large broken tower ruin where the Troll-pulled Carriage's caravan starts it's journey. (map co-ordinates are:  -194.50781 : 113.69531 )\nEarly game, you can farm for this on the noble sorcerers Northeast of the Agheel Lake South grace in southern Limgrave. From where you enter Limgrave, head Southeast and look for some stones you can use as steps to drop down without dying, they'll be close to the border of the lake. Cut around the bottom of the lake, avoiding the patrolling mercenary, and then cut Southeast across the field toward the road. You should see a the Agheel Lake South grace point on a little shelf below the road. From the grace, there are at least 4 noble sorcerers nearby, but 2 are easy to reach. Wait for the mercenary patrolling the road to ride by, then head across the road and left until you're climbing the slope. One sorcerer & retinue will be coming down this slope from the camp above. After killing them, turn around, and there is another sorcerer & retinue traveling down the road. You may want to wait for the mercenary to pass again, because he takes actual effort to fight (whereas the sorcerers have bad eyesight and go down quickly). Once you've killed and looted both groups, head back to the grace to rest, because it's a rare enough drop that it'll probably take you many resets to get.\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Staff Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: No Skill\nThis weapon can't be enchanted with Magic nor infused with Ashes of War.\nGlintstone Staff can be upgraded by using Smithing Stones\nSell Value:   100\nBase drop rate is 2.04% (at 100 Discovery)\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed. QUESTION
Where can the Glintstone Staff be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Glintstone Staff can be found at the Starting Equipment for the Prisoner Class and has a chance to be dropped by Noble Sorcerers at Caria Manor, as well as from a lone Noble Sorcerer south of the Waypoint Ruins, behind the large broken tower ruins.
