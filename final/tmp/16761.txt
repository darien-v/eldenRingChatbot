In the upcoming Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree DLC players will explore a new area called the Land of Shadow. This expansion introduces a fresh map separate from the familiar Lands Between, offering a distinct and immersive experience. In Shadow of the Erdtree DLC, players are led by Empyrean Miquella to the Land of Shadow, an enigmatic realm shrouded by the Erdtree's shadow. This is where the goddess Marika first made her presence known. The Land of Shadow holds many secrets that players will uncover as they meet others who, like Miquella, tread its lands with their own agendas. According to Hidetaka Miyazaki, the new area has roughly the size of Limgrave.\n\nThe Land of Shadow. A place obscured by the Erdtree. \nWhere the goddess Marika first set foot.\nA land purged in an unsung battle. \nSet ablaze by Messmer’s flame.\nIt was to this land that Miquella departed. \nDivesting himself of his flesh, his strength, his lineage. \nOf all things Golden.\nAnd now Miquella awaits the return of his promised Lord. \n\nElden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree DLC Gameplay\n\nShadow of the Erdtree Release Date Trailer Impressions\n\nHow to access to the Land of Shadow in Shadow of the Erdtree DLC\n\nThe Elden Ring Land of Shadow has been confirmed to occupy the same space as the Lands Between but has been physically disconnected. Players will need to use a warp to access this area.\nThe entrance to the Land of Shadow is located within Mohg's blood-soaked boss arena, specifically at the iconic cocoon in the back. This entrance connects to Mohgwyn Palace, serving as the starting point for players venturing into the Land of Shadow. The Elden Ring Land of Shadow is a completely new location, different from the Lands Between, and is characterized by the unique presence of the Shadow of the Erdtree. This land is physically separated from the Lands Between, requiring a special means of travel. Players can expect a similar structure of exploration in the Shadow of the Erdtree Land of Shadow.\n\nLand of Shadow is a mysterious and dark realm obscured by the Erdtree, where the goddess Marika first set foot. It is a place filled with secrets and dangers, with vast maps, dungeons, and enemies to encounter.
