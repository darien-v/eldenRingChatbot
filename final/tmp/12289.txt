QUESTION
Where can one find the Lord's Divine Fortification spell in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
After defeating Malenia, Blade of Miquella, talk to Gideon Ofnir about the Haligtree roots. It can also be purchased at Twin Maiden Husks after defeating Maliketh, the Black Blade boss encounter in Elden Ring.
