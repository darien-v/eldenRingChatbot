Blackflame Monk Gauntlets are part of the Blackflame Monk Armor Set, and are Heavyweight Gauntlets that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense. Blackflame Monk Gauntlets is an armor piece that protect both of the player's arms by applying various defensive properties, It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nBlack iron gauntlets featuring a flame motif.Attire of the Blackflame Monks.\nThe Blackflame Monks, enthralled by the god-slaying black flame, became traitors, abandoning their posts as guardians. The seduction of a taboo is never easily spurned.\n\nWhere to find Blackflame Monk Gauntlets in Elden Ring\nThe Blackflame Monk Gauntlets drop from Blackflame Monks. They can be found at:\n\nThe Divine Tower of Caelid in Dragonbarrow. A monk can be found patrolling a corridor in the lower levels of the tower and one more can be found guarding the door to the Godskin Apostle boss' chamber. The latter one is an easy camp.
