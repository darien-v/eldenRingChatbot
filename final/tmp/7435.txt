\n\n Frenzied Burst \n\nSpell Type\nFrenzied Flame Incantations\n\nFP Cost 24\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Emits concentrated blast of yellow flame of frenzy from eyes \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 22 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nFrenzied Burst is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Frenzied Burst spell releases concentrated blasts of the flame of frenzy. When charged, the blast can easily penetrate the enemy's guard. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nIncantations originating from the maddening Three Fingers.\nReleases concentrated blasts of the yellow flame of frenzy from the caster's eyes.Charging enhances potency, enabling the blasts to penetrate the enemy's guard.\nIn times past, every single person who attempted to control the flame of frenzy succumbed to madness after a desperate internal struggle. This incantation is testament to a meager victory.\n\nWhere to find Frenzied Burst\nWhere to find Frenzied Burst:\n\n Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the Liurnia of the Lakes region, South of the Church of Inhibition near some frenzied rats. [Elden Ring Interactive Map Link] \n\nElden Ring Frenzied Burst Guide\n\nFrenzied Flame Incantation\nStamina Cost: 33\nDeals Fire damage\nCharging the blast deals 23.5% more damage, penetrates enemies, and deals massive stamina damage to shield-blocking targets.\nHigh range (similar to Glintstone Cometshard), fast travel speed, and moderate damage make this an excellent long-ranged tool in a Faith user's arsenal.\nThis spell also does not trigger enemies' auto-dodge animations (most often in NPC invaders) similar to an uncharged Lightning Spear.  Very useful in builds that are leaning more towards fire incants instead of lighting incants.\nInflicts Madness buildup (90 normal/105 charged) on opponents and also (20 normal/30 charged) on self. \n\nThe self inflicted madness is fairly negligible, but that also means it is not useful for triggering the Black Dumpling effect.\nNote that all but a handful of enemies in PVE are immune to Madness.\n\nNote: As of Patch 1.07 Poise Damage and Attack Power when charged has been increased.\n\nMad King Written Guide\n\n Frenzied Acolyte Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Frenzied Burst Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Frenzied Burst spell only dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the Liurnia of the Lakes region in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Frenzied Burst spell is only dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the Liurnia of the Lakes region in Elden Ring due to its connection to controlling the flame of frenzy which historically led individuals to madness.
