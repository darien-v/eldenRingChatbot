\n\n Perfumer's Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.6\n\nEffect \n\n Raises potency of \n       Perfume Arts by 20% \n\nPerfumer's Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Perfumer's Talisman raises potency of Perfume Arts by 15%. Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a set of perfume bottles.Raises potency of perfume items.\nThere are gardens known only to the perfumers. Whether hidden on the fringes of the highlands, or obscured by shadows inside caves, the flowers blossom in secret, waiting to impart their scent.\n\nPerfumer's Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nPerfumer's Talisman raises potency of Perfume Arts that deal damage by 20%.\n\nDoes not enhance the buffs of perfume items, only the potency of those which cause damage.\nWorks on Perfumer's Bolt.\n\nWhere to find Perfumer's Talisman in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Perfumer's Talisman:\n\nPerfumer's Ruins - In a chest underground below the stairs in the room with the yellow Giant Poison Flower. The stairs are covered by wooden planks and must be either rolled onto or attacked to reveal.
