Why is the Black Flame Ritual spell categorized as a Godskin Apostle Incantation in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Black Flame Ritual spell is categorized as a Godskin Apostle Incantation in Elden Ring due to being a superior black flame incantation closely associated with the Godskin Apostles.
