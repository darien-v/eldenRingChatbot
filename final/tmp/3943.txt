QUESTION
How can players acquire the Blue Cipher Ring in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can acquire the Blue Cipher Ring in Elden Ring by purchasing it from the Twin Maiden Husks for 1,000 Runes at the Roundtable Hold.
