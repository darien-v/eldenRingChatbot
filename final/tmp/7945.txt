QUESTION
How can one effectively utilize Omenkiller Rollo's aggressive spirit in Elden Ring battles?

ANSWER
Omenkiller Rollo's aggression can be effectively utilized in battles by summoning him near a rebirth monument, watching for the monument icon on the screen, and taking advantage of his Bleed proc ability while maintaining his aggressive follow-around behavior.
