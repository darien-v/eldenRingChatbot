Gelmir draw from faith in addition to intelligence to enhance the potency of their sorcery.\n\nWhere to Find Gelmir Glintstone Staff\n\nThe Gelmir Glintstone Staff is an uncommon drop from the Man-Serpent Sorcerer in the throne room above Volcano Manor.\nIt is recommended that players use a Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot for each run up to the throne room to boost item discovery from killing enemies as well as unlock the lift leading from the Temple of Eiglay Site of Grace to the throne room.
