\n\n Mending Rune of Perfect Order \n\nType\nGreat Rune\n\nEffect \n\n        Can be used to mend the shattered Elden Ring. \n\nMending Rune of Perfect Order is a Great Rune in Elden Ring. Unlike most other Great Runes, it can't be equipped. Its only effect is to alter the ending of the game.\n\nRune discovered by the noble Goldmask.Used to restore the fractured Elden Ring when brandished by the Elden Lord.\nA rune of transcendental ideology which will attempt to perfect the Golden Order.\nThe current imperfection of the Golden Order, or instability of ideology, can be blamed upon the fickleness of the gods no better than men. That is the fly in the ointment.\n\nWhere to find Mending Rune of Perfect Order in Elden Ring\nDropped by Goldmask after following his and Brother Corhyn's questline.\n\nElden Ring Mending Rune of Perfect Order Use\nGrants access to an alternative ending after defeating the final boss. QUESTION
What is the significance of the Mending Rune of Perfect Order in altering the ending of Elden Ring?

ANSWER
It grants access to an alternative ending after defeating the final boss.
