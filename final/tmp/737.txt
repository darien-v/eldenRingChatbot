\n\n Hallowhorn Grounds \n\nAn overgrown temple ruin occupied solely by a giant deer carcass.\n\nHallowhorn Grounds is a Location in Elden Ring. Two Hallowhorn Grounds exist in the game. One is found in the south-east of the Siofra River region in a big open space. It can be easily reached from the Siofra River Bank Site of Grace. Another is found in Nokron, Eternal City, north-east of the Ancestral Woods Site of Grace.\nDespite being in different regions, both Hallowhorn Grounds look similar and play similar roles. These run down temples are mostly empty save for the vegetation growing over the ruins and a large deer carcass further in. The stone obelisks in front of the temples are intially unlit, but will light up one by one when lighting fires up in various areas of Siofra River and Nokron.\n\nHallowhorn Grounds Map\n\nSouthern Hallowhorn Grounds \nNorthern Hallowhorn Grounds \n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Hallowhorn Grounds\n\n??\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Hallowhorn Grounds\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nDewkissed Herba\nMelted Mushroom\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nGhost Glovewort (2) (Siofra River)\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nHorn Bow (Siofra River)\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nMap (Siofra River) (Siofra River)\nAncestral Follower Ashes\n\nElden Ring Hallowhorn Grounds Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nAncestral Follower\nDeer\nClayman\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nAncestor Spirit (Siofra River)\nRegal Ancestor Spirit (Nokron, Eternal City)\n\nHallowhorn Grounds Walkthrough\nSiofra River\nWhen you first enter the temple in Siofra River, you may find that the temple is mostly empty - it's mostly barren and there's nothing to interact with. There are eight obelisks next to the stairs leading up to Hallowhorn Grounds that are unlit (one might be lit if you've lit the brazier on the way to the temple). You have to find eight braziers scattered around Siofra River and set them aflame to light these obelisks (See our Interactive Map for their location). Once all of them are lit, a message saying "Power gathers somewhere in horned remains" will pop up and the deer carcass further in the temple will start glowing. You can now head back to Hallowhorn Grounds to interact with the carcass to teleport to the Ancestor Spirit boss arena. You can find a map with the locations of these braziers on the Siofra River page.\n\nNokron, Eternal City\nThe Hallowhorn Grounds in Nokron, Eternal City works the same way, except for the fact that there are six obelisks instead. Find each brazier (the ones here are easier to find as most of them have a singing Ancestral Follower by them, see our Interactive Map if you have trouble finding them) and interact with the deer carcass when all obelisks are lit. This carcass teleports you to the Regal Ancestor Spirit boss arena.\n\nElden Ring Hallowhorn Grounds Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy QUESTION
What is the significance of the deer carcass found in the Hallowhorn Grounds in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The deer carcass in Hallowhorn Grounds serves as a teleportation point to specific boss arenas in the game.
