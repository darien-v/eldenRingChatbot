Gravekeeper Cloak (Altered) is part of the Duelist Set Armor Set, and is a three piece set worn by the gladiatorial duelists and grave wardens. Gravekeeper Cloak (Altered) is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nDefensive wear encircled by bronze snakes. Worn by gladiators who were driven from the colosseum. The wearer becomes a slightly easier target for foes.  The snake is viewed as a traitor to the Erdtree, and the audience delighted in seeing these bronze effigies beaten and battered.\n\nWhere to find Gravekeeper Cloak (Altered) in Elden Ring\nThe
