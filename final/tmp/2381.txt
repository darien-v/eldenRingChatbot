QUESTION
Where can one find the Traveler's Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Traveler's Set in Elden Ring can be found deep underground in a side room with a large orange flower adjacent to the Haligtree Roots site of grace, accessed by taking the lift from the large building in the northeast.
