Gelmir Knight Gauntlets is an armor piece that protect both of the player's arms by applying various defensive properties, It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nGauntlets worn by knights once loyal to Praetor Rykard. Time has yet to dull their luster. \n\nWhere to find Gelmir Knight Gauntlets in Elden Ring\n\nYou can loot the entire Gelmir Knight Set in the Gelmir Hero's Grave dungeon from a Gelmir Knight corpse. See it on the  Elden Ring Map here.\nTo find the corpse, head down the first Chariot hallway until you see a pit on either side of the central concrete walkway instead of lava. Drop down into the left pit onto some wooden beams and look for a small window along the walls. Head through this window and down the ladder to find a Bloodhound Knight guarding the corpse.\n\nGelmir Knight Gauntlets Set in Elden Ring\n\nGelmir Knight Gauntlets is a piece of the Gelmir Knight Set.\nThe rest of the set is composed by the Gelmir Knight Helm, Gelmir Knight Armor and Gelmir Knight Greaves.\n\nElden Ring Gelmir Knight Gauntlets Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value: 100\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
