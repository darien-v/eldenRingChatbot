QUESTION
Why is Maliketh's Armor considered an important piece in Elden Ring and what attributes make it stand out among other armor sets?

ANSWER
Maliketh's Armor is considered important in Elden Ring for its unique design, high defense values, and various resistances, such as immunity at 35, robustness at 57, focus at 24, vitality at 50, and poise at 24, making it stand out among other armor sets.
