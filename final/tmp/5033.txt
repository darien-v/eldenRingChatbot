\n\n Medicine Peddler's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nMedicine Peddler's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Medicine Peddler's Bell Bearing allows you to buy items from the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nBell bearing of an unfortunate medicine peddler who was slain by the Bell Bearing Hunter.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to the following items:\n- Neutralizing Boluses- Stanching Boluses- Thawfrost Boluses- Stimulating Boluses\n\nWhere to find Medicine Peddler's Bell Bearing in Elden Ring\nThis bell bearing can be obtained from the Bell Bearing Hunter located inside of the Hermit Merchant's Shack in Altus Plateau. Like the other spawn locations he only spawns here at night. QUESTION
Why is offering the Medicine Peddler's Bell Bearing to the Twin Maiden Husks necessary to gain access to specific items in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Offering the Medicine Peddler's Bell Bearing to the Twin Maiden Husks is necessary to unlock specific items because it allows you to gain access to Neutralizing Boluses, Stanching Boluses, Thawfrost Boluses, and Stimulating Boluses for purchase.
