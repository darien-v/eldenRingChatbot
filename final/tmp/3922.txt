\n\n Blade of Gold \n\n        Leap into the air, charging the armament with golden flames that are then shot at the enemy as a single, blade-like projectile. Inflicts holy damage. \n\nBlade of Gold is a Skill in Elden Ring. Blade of Gold is the unique skill for the Blade of Calling that allows the user to jump and fire a golden projectile that deals Holy Damage. It shares an animation with Blade of Death, the unique skill for the Black Knife.\n\nHow to get Blade of Gold\n\nDefault skill on the Blade of Calling.\n\nElden Ring Blade of Gold Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 20\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried (the holy projectile can be Parried with Carian Retaliation)\nDamage type is pure Holy QUESTION
What are the characteristics of the Blade of Gold skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Blade of Gold inflicts holy damage, has a FP Cost of 20, is not chargeable, and cannot be parried (the holy projectile can be parried with Carian Retaliation). Damage type is pure Holy Damage, scales with weapon upgrade level and Faith, and deals 10 stance damage.
