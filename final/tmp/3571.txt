QUESTION
How can you efficiently farm Volcanic Stone in Elden Ring at Mt. Gelmir?

ANSWER
Volcanic Stone at Mt. Gelmir can be efficiently farmed by running from the Seethewater Terminus Site of Grace to the Seethewater River and back, which can yield over 100 Volcanic Stones each leg.
