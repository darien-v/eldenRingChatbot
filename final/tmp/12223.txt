Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the incantations of the Golden Order fundamentalists.A gift of gratitude to the young Miquella from his father,Radagon.Produces a golden ring of light and fires it across a wide area.Charging enhances range.And yet, the young Miquella abandoned fundamentalism, for itcould do nothing to treat Malenia's accursed rot. This was thebeginning of unalloyed gold.\n\nWhere to find Radagon's Rings of Light\nWhere to find Radagon's Rings of Light:\n\nGolden Order Principia prayerbook\n\nElden Ring Radagon's Rings of Light Guide\n\nGolden Order Incantation\nStamina Cost: 37\nFP Cost: 23\nSlots Used: 1\nNotes: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost has been reduced from 25 to 21 and recovery time has been reduced.\n\nOther Info\n\nThis spell deals holy damage and 15 stance damage\nDoes very high damage over a wide horizontal area for the FP cost to enemies that aren't resistant to holy damage\nThis spell is chargeable and benefits from the Godfrey Icon. Charging this spell substantially increases damage done.\nThis spell casts at medium speed uncharged and at slow speed fully charged.\nThis spell can be further boosted by using the Golden Order seal.\nThis spell is very effective for farming the albinaurics in Mogwhyn palace due to its high damage and wide area.\n\nGolden Sword Sage Written Guide\n\nPerfect Paladin Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Radagon's Rings of Light Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
