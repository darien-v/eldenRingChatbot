\n\n Ghost-Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing [3] \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nGhost-Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing [3] is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Ghost-Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing [3] can be offered to the Twin Maiden Husks to gain access to new shop items.. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nThe bell bearing of a forager who once picked ghost glovewort flowers.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to the following items:\n- Ghost Glovewort [7]- Ghost Glovewort [8]- Ghost Glovewort [9]\n\nHow to get Ghost-Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing [3] in Elden Ring\nElphael, Brace of the Haligtree: Found on a grave in the western section of the graveyard in the northeast of the city. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nFrom the Haligtree Roots Site of Grace, go back up the northeastern elevator into the church and head out through the door in front of you. From there, turn left and make your way down, fighting your way through the many Lesser Kindred of Rot in the area. The Bell Bearing will be at the end of the area, surrounded by graves on the cliffside.\nAlternatively you can enter the same church from the Drainage Canal Site of Grace. Exit to the east and turn left, walking on the tree branch. Drop down to the right onto the brace, then to the left onto another branch, and then drop down to the balcony below. From there drop down into the church and exit northeast to the graveyard where the Bell Bearing is.\n\nVideo location, Halligtree\n\nElden Ring Ghost-Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing [3]
