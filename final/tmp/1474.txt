QUESTION
Where can you find the Raya Lucarian Foot Soldier Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Pieces of the Raya Lucarian Foot Soldier Set drop from Raya Lucaria Foot Soldiers found patrolling the roads in many areas of Liurnia of the Lakes. The Foot Soldier Gauntlets and Foot Soldier Greaves can only drop from Godrick Foot Soldiers.
