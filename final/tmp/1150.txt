\n\n Purifying Crystal Tear \n\nType\nCrystal Tear\n\nEffect \n\n        Purifies the Lord of Blood's curse in a mixed physick \n\nPurifying Crystal Tear is a Crystal Tear in Elden Ring. Crystal Tears can be used and mixed to enhance the effects of the Flask of Wondrous Physick. Like other Crystal Tears, it is not lost in New Game Plus.\n\nA crystal tear formed slowly over the age where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground.Can be mixed in the Flask of Wonderous Physick.The resulting concoction purifies the curse from Mohg, Lord of Blood's terrifying rite of blood.\n\nWhere to find Purifying Crystal Tear\n\nDropped by Eleonora, Violet Bloody Finger at the Second Church of Marika in Altus Plateau. [ Map Link]\n\nPurifying Crystal Tear Notes & Tips\n\nUsed to negate the curse effect during the fight against Mohg, Lord of Blood.\nCan be used at any point in the boss fight, before the second phase transition, to prevent all blood loss from the curse, although the boss still gets his health back.\nYou can find Crystal Tears at the bases of Minor Erdtrees, and elsewhere throughout the Lands Between.\nAt sites of grace, you can mix two Crystal Tears in the Flask of Wondrous Physick. (This will allow you to create elixirs with various custom effects)\n\nElden Ring Crystal Tears\n\nCerulean Crystal Tear  ♦  Cerulean Hidden Tear  ♦  Crimson Bubbletear  ♦  Crimson Crystal Tear  ♦  Crimsonburst Crystal Tear  ♦  Crimsonspill Crystal Tear  ♦  Crimsonwhorl Bubbletear  ♦  Dexterity-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Faith-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Greenburst Crystal Tear  ♦  Greenspill Crystal Tear  ♦  Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Intelligence-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Leaden Hardtear  ♦  Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Magic-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Opaline Bubbletear  ♦  Opaline Hardtear  ♦  Ruptured Crystal Tear  ♦  Speckled Hardtear  ♦  Spiked Cracked Tear  ♦  Stonebarb Cracked Tear  ♦  Strength-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Thorny Cracked Tear  ♦  Twiggy Cracked Tear  ♦  Windy Crystal Tear  ♦  Winged Crystal Tear\n\n

QUESTION
Why do players need to use Purifying Crystal Tear during the fight against Mohg, Lord of Blood in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To negate the curse effect, prevent all blood loss, and purify Mohg's curse.
