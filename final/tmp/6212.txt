\n\n Impaling Thrust \n\n        Skill that lets piercing armaments overcome enemy shields. Build power, then lunge forward for a long thrust that pierces an enemy's guard. \n\nImpaling Thrust is a Skill in Elden Ring. Impaling Thrust is a strong lunging action that can pierce through enemy shields and pierce their guard. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Impaling Thrust\n\nDefault skill of the Weapons, Rapier, Antspur Rapier, Short Spear, Partisan, Katar, Noble's Estoc and Estoc\nDefault skill for the Cleanrot Knight's Sword\nDefault skill for the Cross-Naginata\nSold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 1,000 . Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing. \n\nElden Ring Impaling Thrust Guide, Notes & Tips \n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Impaling Thrust.\nFP Cost: 9\nStance damage is dependent on weapon type: \n\n18 (Daggers, Claws)\n24 (Thrusting Swords)\n30 (Straight Swords, Katanas, Twinblades, Spears, Halberds)\n33 (Greatswords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Great Spears)\n\nThis Skill not Chargeable\nThis Skill can be Parried\n\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nMotion speed and attack speed have been increased.\nThe delay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animation) and being able to perform a skill has been reduced.\nThe timing between the end of the skill and being able to attack and to roll has been reduced.\n\nMoveset & Videos in Elden Ring for Death's Poker\n\nAll Ashes of War Demonstration\n\nKung Fu Katarist Written Guide\n\nHaima Hoplite Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Impaling Thrust Notes & Tips \n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
What are the different ways to obtain the Impaling Thrust skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Default skill of the Weapons, Rapier, Antspur Rapier, Short Spear, Partisan, Katar, Noble's Estoc, and Estoc, Default skill for the Cleanrot Knight's Sword, Default skill for the Cross-Naginata, Sold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 1,000, Can be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.
