\n\n Loretta's Slash \n\n        Skill of Loretta the Royal Knight. Leap forward, imbuing the blade with glintstone, then descend, accelerating into a sweeping slash. \n\nLoretta's Slash is a Skill in Elden Ring. Loretta's Slash is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War that can be used on Polearms and Twinblades. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Loretta's Slash\n\nAsh of War: Loretta's Slash - Caria Manor: Dropped by Royal Knight Loretta upon defeat. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nDefault skill of the Loretta's War Sickle\n\nElden Ring Loretta's Slash Guide\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Loretta's Slash.\nFP Cost: 14\nDeals 12+10 (Total 22 ) stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nDamage type is Physical Damage and Magic Damage\nDamage scales with weapon scaling. This used to be different, see stricken out text below. \n\nFor high INT builds (tested with 20 STR, 20 DEX, 80 INT), a Magic Cross-Naginata+25 will do more damage with Loretta's Slash than Loretta's War Sickle+10. For all other affinities of the Naginata and for other builds, LSW will do more damage with the skill.\n\nOn the Loretta's War Sickle, this skill scales with AR (see point above), on non-unique weapons with the corresponding Ash of War, it scales with Intelligence only, even if the Magic or Frost affinities have not been set on that weapon.\n\nNon-unique magic weapons will deal more damage with his skill on an 80 Intelligence build, which is the standard for mage builds, than the Loretta's War Sickle will with  a build set to optimise the LWS's AR, making LWS an inferior choice for this skill.\n\nWearing Spellblade Set increases the damage\nThe animation itself is identical to the one used by Angel's Wings. This attack deals magic instead of Holy damage, and does not apply the anti-heal status effect.\nThis Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nPoise damage for the first attack has been increased.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\n\nNOTES: The Skill specifically for Loretta's War Sickle Ash of War also changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nAttack Power, Damage of the First Attack and Poise damage have been increased.\n\nLoretta's Slash Demonstration\n\nCarian Cavalier Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Loretta's Slash Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is Loretta's Slash considered a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and can be used on Polearms and Twinblades in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Loretta's Slash is considered a regular skill in Ashes of War for Elden Ring, usable on Polearms and Twinblades, as it infuses the blade with glintstone, accelerating into a sweeping slash.
