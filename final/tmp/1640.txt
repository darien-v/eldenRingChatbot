QUESTION
Why are Godrick Soldiers some of the first enemies encountered in the open world of Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Godrick Soldiers are some of the first enemies encountered in the open world of Elden Ring because they drop all four pieces of the Godrick Soldier Set and can easily be found in Gatefront Ruins.
