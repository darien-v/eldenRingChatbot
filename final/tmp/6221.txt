\n\n Ice Lightning Sword \n\n        Call down a bolt of ice lightning into the blade and bring it down upon a foe. \n       The ice lightning effect will persist for a while. \n\nIce Lightning Sword is a Skill in Elden Ring. Ice Lightning Sword calls down a bolt of ice lightning that damages an enemy. It also leaves the blade with extra lightning damage and frostbite buildup for the next 45 seconds. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Ice Lightning Sword in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill for the Dragonscale Blade.\n\nElden Ring Ice Lightning Sword Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 25\nDeals 23 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill can be Parried\nThis skill consists of three trigger attacks (stomp, slash, then lightning bolt), then coats the blade with the Ice Lightning effect.\nAdds 160 flat Lightning Damage to weapon AR and 80 frost build up for 45 seconds.\nPreviously known as Hailstorm Sword on the Network Test.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nWeapon attack power has been increased.\nDuration has been increased from 20 to 45 seconds.\nAttack power of the effect that grants the weapon a lightning attribute has been increased from 130 to 160.\nTime between the skill activation and being able to perform actions has been reduced.\n\n Item Buffs Table\n\nData collected from a 99 Str & Dex build, with all base stats otherwise.\n\nItem\nStomp\nSlash\nLightning Bolt\nIce Lightning Effect\n\nShard of Alexander\n15%\n15%\n0%\n0%\n\nLightning Scorpion Charm\n12%\n6pts\n6pts\n6pts\n\nLightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear\n20%\n10pts\n10pts\n10pts\n\nLightning Scorpion Charm & Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear\n35%\n17pts\n17pts\n17pts\n\nOld Lord's Talisman\nNo Effect\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nDragonscale Daimyo Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Ice Lightning Sword Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is Ice Lightning Sword considered a Unique Skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ice Lightning Sword is considered a Unique Skill in Elden Ring because it consists of three trigger attacks followed by a lightning bolt and coatings the blade with the Ice Lightning effect, providing specific characteristics different from other skills.
