Banished Knight Set is a Heavyweight armor. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Banished Knight Set in Elden Ring\nBanished Knight Set pieces have a chance to drop from Banished Knight enemies. While the Gauntlets and Greaves will drop from all Banished Knights, the altered and non altered versions of the Helm and Chest will only drop from the knights wearing those pieces. These can be found at:\n\nStormveil Castle. (Altered Helm, Altered Chest, Gauntlets, and Greaves)\nCathedral of Dragon Communion. (Unaltered Helm, Altered Chest, Gauntlets, and Greaves)\nCastle Sol. (Altered Helm, Altered Chest, Unaltered Chest, Gauntlets, and Greaves) \n\nOnly two specific knights here will drop the Unaltered Chest.\n\nCrumbling Farum Azula. (Unaltered Helm, Altered Chest, Gauntlets, and Greaves)\n\nBanished Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nBanished Knight Helm.\nBanished Knight Helm (Altered)\nBanished Knight Armor.\nBanished Knight Armor (Altered)\nBanished Knight Gauntlets\nBanished Knight Greaves.\n\nBanished Knight Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nBanished Knight Gauntlets and Banished Knight Greaves can drop from all Banished Knight enemies.\nThe Banished Knight Armor (Altered) and Banished Knight Helm (Altered) can be farmed near the Rampart Tower site of grace in Stormveil Castle. A Banished Knight is located just down the stairs of the large room outside the grace location.\nAnother quick way to farm the Altered pieces is from the Church of the Eclipse site of grace at Castle Sol. A Banished Knight is located directly outside this grace location.\nThe unaltered Banished Knight Helm can be farmed from the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid. A Banished Knight is nearby in the church entryway.\nThe unaltered Banished Knight Armor only drops off of two specific Banished Knights in Castle Sol. One can be easily farmed by starting from the Castle Sol Rooftop site of grace and running south to the elevator. The Banished Knight can be seen off to the left with two wolves. He can be shot with ranged weapons from here causing it to teleport to the player alone.\nCastellan Edgar wears the unaltered Banished Knight set without the helmet, but will not drop any of the pieces when killed.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Banished Knight Set considered a heavyweight armor in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Banished Knight Set is considered a heavyweight armor in Elden Ring due to its high Robustness, Armor Set Weight, and Vitality, providing significant protection.
