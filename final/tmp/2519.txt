Page Set armor worn by pages who serve the nobility and are mindful to keep them out of harm's way. The unassuming sack-like appearance befits their unseen and unknown stature. Traveling wear tailored with exquisite precision, to avoid any undue shame for the page's master. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Page Set in Elden Ring\nPage Set pieces occasionally drop from Page enemies found in several areas of the Lands Between:\n\nThree are found part way down the stairs from the Royal Moongazing Grounds site of grace in Caria Manor.\nThey are scattered throughout Leyndell, Royal Capital. They vanish after Leyndell, Royal Capital becomes Leyndell, Ashen Capital, though.\nTombsward Ruins Underground\nGelmir Hero's Grave\n\nPage Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\nThe Page Set only has three pieces and does not have gauntlets.
