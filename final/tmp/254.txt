\n\n Revenger's Shack \n\nAn isolated shack north of the Minor Erdtree. Bodies litter the ground inside the shack.\n\nRevenger's Shack is a Location in Elden Ring. The Revenger's Shack is found in western Liurnia of the Lakes. Players can reach this location by heading north from the Minor Erdtree.\n\nRevenger's Shack Map\nRevenger's Shack is located just north of the Minor Erdtree in southwest Liurnia. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Revenger's Shack\n\nEdgar the Revenger - An invader NPC that appears when you interact with the shack's Site of Grace after completing Irina and Edgar's questline.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Revenger's Shack\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nRaw Meat Dumpling\nRaw Meat Dumpling x 5 (Edgar the Revenger Drop)\nRoot Resin: x3 West of the Site of Grace\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location.\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nBanished Knight's Halberd (+8) (Edgar the Revenger drop)\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nFurlcalling Finger Remedy (Edgar the Revenger drop)\nShabriri Grape (Edgar the Revenger drop)\n\nElden Ring Revenger's Shack Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nEdgar the Revenger\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nNo bosses at this location\n\nRevenger's Shack Walkthrough\nRevenger's Shack is a minor location found north of the Minor Erdtree in southwest Liurnia. On your first visit, Edgar the Revenger will invade as soon as you touch the Site of Grace. Edgar is a straightforward fight as his moveset only includes physical attacks and he is easily staggered with magic. Upon defeat, he drops his halberd, a +8 variant of the Banished Knight's Halberd as well as a Shabriri Grape, a Furlcalling Finger Remedy and 5 Raw Meat Dumplings.\nInspecting the 5 corpses inside the shack will give you 5 more Raw Meat Dumplings.\n\nElden Ring Revenger's Shack Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy Crystal
