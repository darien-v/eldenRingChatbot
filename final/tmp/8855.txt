QUESTION
Where can you find Cracked Crystal in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Cracked Crystal can be found in Sellia Crystal Tunnel, respawns in the overworld after resting at a Site of Grace, sold by Twin Maiden Husks, and dropped by Giant Miranda Sprout and Miner enemies in Elden Ring.
