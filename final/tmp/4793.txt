\n\n Man-Serpent Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 62\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons man-serpent spirit \n\nMan-Serpent Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Man-Serpent Ashes are used to summon the spirit of a man-serpent.\nMen-Serpents can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Man-Serpent.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirit of a man-serpent.  Spirit of a deformed man-serpent that wields a whip of magma. It is said that long ago, the elder serpent that dwelled on Mt. Gelmir devoured a demigod, and the birth of the man-serpents followed.\n\nWhere to find Man-Serpent Ashes\nWhere to Find Man-Serpent Ashes\n\nLocated by the Volcano Manor site of grace in the Volcano Manor legacy dungeon on Mt. Gelmir. The ashes can be found in a room on a corpse surrounded by red candles and three enemies inside the large open room with two large sets of stairs, spiral pillars, and a large egg guarded by a man-serpent at the top.
