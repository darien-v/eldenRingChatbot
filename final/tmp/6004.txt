\n\n Sacred Order \n\n        Skill of the Golden Order fundamentalist knights. Perform a salute and grant the armament holy essence. Highly effective against Those Who Live in Death.  \n\nSacred Order is a Skill in Elden Ring. Sacred Order is a skill of the Golden Order found on the Treespear. Can be mistaken for its aura buff counterpart, Shared Order. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Sacred Order\n\nDefault skill of the Treespear\nAsh of War: Sacred Order - Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in Altus Plateau, just north of the Abandoned Coffin Site Of Grace.\n\nElden Ring Sacred Order Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Sacred Order.\nFP Cost: 18\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nDamage +10% in PVE, QUESTION
What are the effects and benefits of using the Sacred Order skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Damage +10% in PVE, 2.5% in PVP, +100% damage to Undead enemies, prevent Skeleton reviving, duration of 60 seconds, see Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking buffs.
