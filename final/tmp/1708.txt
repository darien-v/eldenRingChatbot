The second one is around a corner, so if you stay near the entrance to the boss room you can usually avoid attracting its attention.\nClear out the other Demi-Humans first with ranged attacks or spells.\nDemi-Human Chiefs have low poise, so your attacks will usually interrupt theirs. Use this to chain multiple attacks or prevent yourself from getting hit.\n\nMelee Strategy\nSneaky: Neither boss will notice you when you first enter the arena, so you have a chance to sneak around and either get in a few early hits or pick off a few Demi-Humans before the closer one starts attacking.\nShielding: A decent shield can block most individual attacks, but you may not have enough stamina to fully block some combos. Once you've blocked the first attack, either dodge out of the way or guard counter to interrupt the next attack.\nBide Your Time: Wait for Knife Slam attack, when the Demi-Human Chief's knives will be briefly stuck in the floor. This is your opportunity to strike. Take advantage of their low poise to hit three or four times, as long as you still have enough stamina to retreat afterwards.\n\nMagic and Ranged Strategy\nKeep Your Distance: This boss can attack quickly, and you need time to cast spells or fire arrows so stay well back while you're fighting. You can circle the large stone pillar near the entrance to keep the boss far enough away to put more space between you and the Demi-Human Chief.\nShielding: Keep a in one hand and your catalyst or seal in the other so that you can block if needed. The Demi-Human Chiefs' attacks can have unexpected timings, so it's not always easy to dodge every attack.\n\nAttacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nSlash\nSlashes its dagger clockwise or counterclockwise\nEither block with a heavy enough shield to stagger it or dodge away and wait out the combo\n\nPivot Slash\nSlashes while running along the ground and turning 180°\nRoll towards where it starts attacking to end up away from it after the pivot\n\nSpinningAttack\n Tilts its left arm down, then spins around counterclockwise with its knives out, hitting once with each arm\nAs soon as you see them start to tilt, roll away\n\nHead Bash\nRears back and then quickly slams its head down towards the ground\nRoll when you see its nose turn up\n\nShout\nLets out a terrifying shout\nAttack a couple times times, but be prepared to roll once it starts attacking\n\nWild Leap\nLeaps through the air swinging its knives wildly.
