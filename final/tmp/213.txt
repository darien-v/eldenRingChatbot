\n\n Summonwater Village \n\nD, Hunter of The Dead, warns you of this nearby village taken over by death, known as Summonwater Village\n\nSummonwater Village is a Location in Elden Ring. Summonwater Village is found in the northeast part of Limgrave, flooded and in ruins. The Summonwater Village is hinted at by D, Hunter of The Dead, who warns you that death has taken over the nearby village and its inhabitants.\n\nSummonwater Village Map for Elden Ring\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Summonwater\n\nD Hunter of the Dead - Found in the outskirts of the village mourning over a body. He will be west of the Village and will warn you of Summonwater Village when spoken to. Before reaching the village however, D, Hunter of the Dead, will warn you of a malevolent force he refers to as "Those Who Live in Death" and advises you to avoid the village altogether.\nUpon reaching Summonwater Village, you can encounter the Tibia Mariner in the flooded areas of the village, and upon defeating it, collect a key item that will interest D. Visit him back at the Roundtable Hold to continue his questline.\nBe sure to explore the area around Summonwater Village for treasures and valuable materials. One such treasure is the Green Turtle Talisman, hidden away in an underground area on the outskirts of the village. You'll find the entrance just slightly to the south-east of the village, locked behind an imp statue that must be unlocked with a Stonesword Key.\n\nElden Ring Summonwater Gallery and Notes\n\nAll
