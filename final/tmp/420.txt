\n\n Highway Lookout Tower Liurnia of the Lakes \n\nA lone tower overlooking the Liurnia main road on the eastern landmass.\n\nHighway Lookout Tower (Liurnia) is a Location in Elden Ring. This Highway Lookout Tower is found in Liurnia of the Lakes, overlooking the main road on the eastern landmass. Players can reach this location by following the main road going north and then veering off to the left by the cliffs.\nFor the Highway Lookout Tower in Altus Plateau, see Highway Lookout Tower (Altus Plateau).\n\nHighway Lookout Tower Map\nThis Highway Lookout Tower is found on the left tip of the eastern landmass of Liurnia, near the center. It is highly recommended that you ride past the spectral enemies and just enter the tower immediately. Otherwise, the sorcerer will just keep taking pot shots at you as you try to defeat the enemies. You can return for them later after taking the sorcerer out.\nClimb up the ladder and loot the chest for a Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook (3). Head up two more ladders and you can kill the sorcerer. Loot the corpse next to him for a Carian Glintblade Staff.\n\nElden Ring Highway Lookout Tower Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy
