\n\n Divine Tower of West Altus \n\nThe hidden entrance to the Divine Tower.\n\nDivine Tower of West Altus is a Location in Elden Ring. It is one of the six Divine Towers. The Divine Tower of West Altus is found in Capital Outskirts between Altus Plateau and Leyndell, Royal Capital. It can only be reached by going through a series of illusory walls found in Sealed Tunnel. The path is linear, so there's no way to miss it, and illusory walls are generally at the end of each new area. You have to fight Onyx Lord which can be defeated in your first encounter if you are prudent enough.\nNOTE: You cannot access the Divine Tower of West Altus without first defeating Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy. Otherwise, you'll get a message saying the "[The door] is tightly blocked shut"\n\nDivine Tower of West Altus Map\nInteractive Map Location here. QUESTION
What is the significance of defeating Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy, in order to access the Divine Tower of West Altus in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Accessing the Divine Tower of West Altus in Elden Ring requires defeating Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy, as a prerequisite to open the tightly shut doors blocking the entrance.
