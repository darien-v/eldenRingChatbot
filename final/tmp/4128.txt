\n\n Somber Smithing Stone [3] \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce special armaments to +3 \n\nSomber Smithing Stone [3] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Somber Smithing Stone [3] is a colorless stone that is used to strengthen special equipment to a higher level. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nHard smithing stone drained of color.\nStrengthens special armaments to +3.\nSpecial armaments with unique characteristics cannot be strengthened with colored smithing stones. Number of steps and amount strengthened differ from standard equipment.\n\nSomber Smithing Stone [3] Location in Elden Ring\nSee Somber Smithing Stone [4] for upgrades past +3.In the World\n\n3x can be found inside the Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel. QUESTION
Why do special armaments with unique characteristics require a different type of smithing stone to be strengthened compared to standard equipment?

ANSWER
Special armaments with unique characteristics require a different type of smithing stone due to the number of steps and amount strengthened differing from standard equipment.
