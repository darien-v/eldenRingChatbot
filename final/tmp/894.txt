QUESTION
How can players effectively utilize the environmental structures to their advantage in the Magma Wyrm Makar boss fight in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can use the large center pillar as a barrier to stay close to it, allowing them to bait out Makar's attacks or cause it to get stuck in animations, providing a shield while they can freely use ranged attacks to deal damage.
