Mausoleum Knight Armor (Altered) is part of the Mausoleum Knight Set Armor Set, and is a Medium Weight chest protection that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense. Mausoleum Knight Armor (Altered) is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nArmor worn by headless knights who endlessly guard the Wandering Mausoleum.  The wing-shaped ornaments on its back evoke the Deathbird. A self-inflicted curse that ties the spirits of these loyal knights to the land, having willingly beheaded themselves so that they may serve their masters in death.\n\nWhere to find Mausoleum Knight Armor (Altered) in Elden Ring\nThe Mausoleum Knight Armor (Altered) Chest Armor can be found at:\n\nMausoleum Knight Set pieces can be farmed from the Mausoleum Knights, typically found in Liurnia of the Lakes.
