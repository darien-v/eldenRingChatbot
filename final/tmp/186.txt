\n\n Smoldering Church \n\nA church in northwestern Caelid, near the border with Limgrave\n\nSmoldering Church is a Location in Elden Ring. The Smoldering Church is found in Caelid, right at the border with Northern Limgrave. You'll find it by travelling down the main road to the east of Summonwater Village. A Site of Grace can be found inside the church.\n\nSmoldering Church Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Smoldering Church\n\nNo NPCs in this church\nNo merchants in this church\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Smoldering Church\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nNo Gatherable items here\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials here\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nSacred Scorpion Charm\nFurlcalling Finger Remedy\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nMissionary's Cookbook (3)\nNomadic Warrior's Cookbook (14)\n\nElden Ring Smoldering Church Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nAnastasia, Tarnished-Eater\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nAside from Anastasia, there are no field bosses in this location \n\nSmoldering Church Walkthrough\nTo claim the two cookbooks available in this area, loot the remains that are resting on the side of the church. She deals quick attacks with her greataxe and can dodge, so time your attacks well. You will gain Sacred Scorpion Charm and Furlcalling Finger Remedy.\nAfter the fight, you can rest at the Site of Grace found in the far end of the Smoldering Church and loot the two corpses next to it
