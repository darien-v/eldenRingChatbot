\n\n Champion's Song Painting \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Reminiscence of the painting "Champion's Song" \n       Examine using the corresponding button. \n\nChampion's Song Painting is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs\n\nWork of a wandering artist.Reminiscence of a painting titled "Champion's Song."\nThis painter is said to have captured the landscapes seen during the last moments of those welcomed into death's embrace.\nThe soul of the painter, and vestiges of the dead's last moments can be discovered by visiting the location depicted even now.\n\nWhere to find Champion's Song Painting in Elden Ring\n\nLocation: The Shaded Castle\nFound in a small wooden structure on the side of the walls on the east side of The Shaded Castle, it cannot be seen from atop the walls as the wooden roof obstructs the view, the player has to walk down the stone pillar to see it. [Map Link]\nFrom the Shaded Castle Ramparts Grace, head SE back down the stairs, then NE along the ramparts. On your left side there will be a broken wall and a stone ramp going down into an acid pool to the West. The painting is in the wooden building next to the stone ramp. Just drop down below the ramp and find the stairs to the North.\n\nElden Ring Champion's Song Painting Tutorial Guide\n\nGo to the pictured location in Altus Plateau, up on the cliff. The closest Site of Grace is the Rampartside Path.\n\n After the spirit disappears, you will receive the Harp Bow.\n\nElden Ring Champion's Song Painting Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 Champion's Song Painting.\nYou cannot store Champion's Song Painting.\nSell Value: This item cannot be sold.\nIf you have damaged Ancient Dragon Lansseax enough near the Abandoned Coffin Site of Grace in Altus Plateau at the start, it will be near the Rampartside Path Site of Grace you have to go to receive the reward so be careful of that.\n\nElden Ring Info Items\n\nAbout Adding
