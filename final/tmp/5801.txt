\n\n Torch Attack \n\n        Thrust your torch forward to set foes ablaze. This Skill is especially effective against foes who are weak to fire. \n\nTorch Attack is a Skill in Elden Ring. Torch Attack is a special skill only available for an individual weapon, and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War.\n\nHow to get Torch Attack in Elden Ring\n\nCan only be used while wielding the Torch.\nDefault skill for the Torch, Beast-Repellent Torch, Sentry's Torch, and Ghostflame Torch.\nThe Torch can be purchased from Kale in the Church of Elleh for 200 .
