QUESTION
Why is the Land of Reeds Helm considered a good balance of defensive properties in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Land of Reeds Helm is considered a good balance of defensive properties in Elden Ring due to its Damage Negation, Physical resistance of 3.1, high resistances against Strike, Slash, Pierce, Magic, Fire, and Light, as well as decent resistances in Holy, Immunity of 26, Robustness of 22, Focus of 22, Vitality of 23, and Poise of 5.
