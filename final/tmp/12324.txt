\n\n Law of Causality \n\nSpell Type\nGolden Order Incantations\n\nFP Cost 22\nSlots Used: 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Retaliates upon receiving a number of blows \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 29 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nLaw of Causality is an Incantation in Elden Ring. The Law of Causality spell serves as a counter-offensive after receiving multiple hits. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the incantations of the Golden Order fundamentalists.One of the key fundamentals.\nManifests a small ring of causality within that allows the caster toautomatically retaliate upon receiving a certain number of blows.The fundamentalists describe the Golden Order through thepowers of regression and causality. Causality is the pull betweenmeanings; that which links all things in a chain of relation.\n\nWhere to find Law of Causality\n\nGiven by Gideon Ofnir after beating Mohg, Lord of Blood and asking about the cocoon in the boss arena.\nIf not received from Gideon Ofnir, it can be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks after defeating Maliketh, the Black Blade.\nVideo Location\n\nLaw of Causality Guide\n\nGolden Order Incantation\n487 base holy attack that increases based on incant scaling\nStamina cost: 20\nDeals holy damage\nTriggers after receiving five hits.\nThe buff lasts for 120 seconds or until the explosion is triggered.\nOnly damaging hits will trigger the explosion. Damage-over-time effects, self-inflicted damage, and completely blocked attacks don't count.\nIf the fifth hit is a phantom hit (the damage effect is shown and status damage builds up, but you don't take damage) you will lose the spell's effect without an explosion occurring.\nRetaliates with a small area-of-effect explosion that can knock enemies back.\nSince this incantation only has Intelligence requirement, a pure Int build with less than 10 Faith, which excludes them from using most Sacred Seals, can still use this incantation by using Frenzied Flame Seal.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 Duration has been extended from 60 to 120 seconds.\n\nLaw of Causality Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
