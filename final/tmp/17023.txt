It is a good Weapon for building Frostbite against enemies.\n\nWeapon wielded by the knights of Zamor who earned greatrenown during the War against the Giants.\nIn apparent devotion to winter, the curved blade is styled after anicy wind and imbued with a powerful frost effect.\n\nWhere to Find Zamor Curved Sword in Elden Ring\nThe Zamor Curved Sword weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nGiant-Conquering Hero's Grave: Dropped by the Ancient Hero of Zamor boss found at the end of the dungeon. QUESTION
Why is the Zamor Curved Sword considered a good weapon for building Frostbite against enemies?

ANSWER
The Zamor Curved Sword is considered a good weapon for building Frostbite against enemies due to its powerful frost effect and skill, Zamor Ice Storm, which can apply frostbite effectively.
