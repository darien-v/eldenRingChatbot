CONTEXT
\n\n Endure \n\n        Assume an anchored stance to brace for incoming attacks, briefly boosting poise. Damage taken while using this skill is reduced. \n\nEndure is a Skill in Elden Ring. Endure is a is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and can be applied to all melee armaments. Endure will greatly complement playstyles that rely on blocking and tanking hits during combat. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Endure\n\nDefault skill on the Dragon Greatclaw, Spiked Caestus, Pickaxe, and Great Stars.\nSold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 600 . Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring Endure Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Endure.\nFP Cost: 9 on use\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nUpon activation, the user assumes a stance with their armament (weapon), providing them with a brief buff. \n\nThe stance assumed depends of the weapon class used. This also means the activation and recovery times are different. \n\n"Large" armaments (Great and Colossal tier, excepting great spears) need a longer recovery time before the user can perform another action.\n\nIncreases all absorptions by 45%, and provides increased poise against all attacks. \n\nThis buff lasts 3 seconds and remains the same among all armaments. \n\nThe absorption and increased poise buff start as soon as the user consumes FP, regardless of the animation.\n\nSome minor attacks can bounce off the user upon hit, such as spear attacks used behind a shield.\nThe increased poise allows the user to become immune to stagger from most attacks \n\nAttack that count as headshots or knockups can still stagger and interrupt the user.\n\nIf used without FP:\n\nAbsorption only increases by 10%\nPoise obtained is decreased, allowing heavy attacks to stagger the user.\nBuff only lasts 1 second\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nEffect duration has been extended but then Shortened again in Patch 1.07.1 as the adjustment had much more impact than expected.\nAn effect that prevents staggers caused by blood loss and frostbite status effects has been added.\nTime between skill activation and being able to perform actions other than attacking has been reduced.\n\nEndure Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric Roar  ♦  Barrage  ♦  Barricade Shield  ♦  Bear QUESTION
What are the specific benefits of using the Endure skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Absorption increase of 45%, increased poise against all attacks, immune to stagger from most attacks, and minor attacks may bounce off upon hit.
