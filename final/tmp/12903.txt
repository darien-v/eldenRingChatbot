QUESTION
Describe the characteristics and usage of Catch Flame in Elden Ring.

ANSWER
Catch Flame is a low-cost, fast-casting Incantation dealing fire damage with limited range, high damage output, and the ability to chain-cast, making it ideal for pure Faith builds or early-game combat, with potential synergies like quicker cast times with Magma Breath and similarities to Combustion from the Souls series.
