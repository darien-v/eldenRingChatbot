QUESTION
Why are Sage Trousers considered attire of the wise sages who were deemed heretical, and what evidence suggests that the wearer was expelled from the town?

ANSWER
Sage Trousers are considered attire of wise sages who were deemed heretical due to their thick burgundy material and evidence suggesting wearer was expelled, such as finding them on a poison-submerged corpse in Stillwater Cave.
