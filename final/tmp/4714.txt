\n\n Winged Misbegotten Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 37\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons winged misbegotten spirit \n\nWinged Misbegotten Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Winged Misbegotten Ashes can be used to summon the spirit of a winged misbegotten.\nMisbegotten can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Misbegotten.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirit of a winged misbegotten.\nA spirit with the aspect of wings which takes flight to loose arrows from its bow. The misbegotten are held to be a punishment for making contact with the Crucible, and from birth they are treated as slaves, or worse.\n\nWinged Misbegotten Ashes Location in Elden Ring\nWinged Misbegotten Ashes can be found in the following location:\n\nUnsightly Catacombs: Found on a corpse in a pit with 4 Misbegotten enemies, near the end of the catacombs. Why are the Misbegotten spirits considered a punishment for making contact with the Crucible?

ANSWER
