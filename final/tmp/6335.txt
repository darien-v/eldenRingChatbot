Frenzyflame Thrust is a Unique Skill for the Vyke's War Spear. It causes a maddening explosion that afflicts both the enemy and the wielder with Madness.\n\nHow to get Frenzyflame Thrust\n\nDefault Skill for the Vyke's War Spear.\n\nElden Ring Frenzyflame Thrust Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 22\nDeals 25 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nHitting the enemy with the spear will cause damage with both the explosion and spear.\nHitting the enemy within the explosion range causes a stronger explosion and is the highest damaging hit out of the three\nHitting too far away will just damage the enemy with the fire caused QUESTION
What are the effects of using Frenzyflame Thrust in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Frenzyflame Thrust afflicts both the enemy and the user with madness, causes a maddening explosion, deals 25 stance damage, and hitting the enemy within the explosion range yields the highest damaging hit.
