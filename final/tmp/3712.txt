Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. \n\nA fragment of a living jar, hardened after its death.Material used for crafting items.\nSuch fragments command a high price due to the magical power locked within. This leaves the living jars unfortunate targets for poachers.\n\nWhere to find Living Jar Shard in Elden Ring\nIN THE WORLD\n\nYou can find x3 Living Shard at the Raya Lucaria Academy in the Debate Hall door. From the Prison Town Church site of grace, run out the doors to the south, make a right and run west down the path toward the first smoking chimney, but head directly to the smoking chimney to the left of the first one and drop down to the rock ledge below. Turn left to the south-east, drop down to the rooftop below, and then turn around and drop down to the graveyard. There are a total of 12 jars (11 small and 1 large). 3 can be found on an island in the lava river, and  there are 7 more just to the west. Note that these explode, so hitting one and backing up will dispose of all or most of them quickly.
