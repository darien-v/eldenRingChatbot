QUESTION
How can players obtain the Mottled Necklace talisman in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can find the Mottled Necklace by exploring the very edge of the ruined bridge leading to Nokron, Eternal City, accessible via the waygate at The Four Belfries through the waygate labeled "night sky unceasing."
