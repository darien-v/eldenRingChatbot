\n\n Founding Rain of Stars \n\nSpell Type\nPrimeval Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 27\nSlots Used 2\n\nEffect \n\n        Releases a downpour of star rain for a while \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 52 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nFounding Rain of Stars is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. The Founding Rain of Stars spell casts a rain of star bolts for a short period. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nThe eldest primeval sorcery, said to have been discovered by anancient astrologer. A sorcery of legendary status.\nSummons a dark cloud of stars overhead. Shortly after, the cloudwill release a violent deluge of star rain. This sorcery can be castwhile in motion. Charging increases potency.\nThought to be the founding glintstone sorcery. The glimpse of theprimeval current that the astrologer saw became real, and thestars' amber rained down on this land.\n\nWhere to find Founding Rain of Stars\nWhere to find Founding Rain of Stars:\n\nInside a chest within Heretical Rise, in Mountaintops of the Giants. Players have to access the location via an invisible bridge starting from the other side of the ravine, and advance following a white mist that indicates the safe path (which is significantly easier to see at night). The safe path starts straight out then you have to go left and curve back towards the tower going upwards. The left path starts a little more than halfway from where you started. How can players locate the Founding Rain of Stars spell in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
