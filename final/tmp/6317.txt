CONTEXT
\n\n Glintblade Phalanx \n\n        Skill used by the enchanted knights who served the Carian royal family. Form an arch of magic glintblades overhead, which will attack foes automatically. Follow up with a strong attack to chain this skill into a lunging thrust. \n\nGlintblade Phalanx is a Skill in Elden Ring. Glintblade Phalanx creates glintstone blades over your head that attack enemies automatically when they get close enough.\nPlease visit Glintblade Phalanx for the Spell variant of this Skill.\n\nHow to get Glintblade Phalanx\n\nAcquired from Rogier's Rapier.\nAfter defeating Godrick the Grafted in Stormveil Castle, Rogier can be found in Roundtable Hold, by the balcony. After speaking to him, and receiving his rapier, the skill may be removed from the rapier in order to be applied to other armaments.\n\nElden Ring Glintblade Phalanx Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Glintblade Phalanx.\nFP Cost: 10 on use, 4 on follow up. \n\nThis totals to a 14 FP cost.\n\nDeals 10x4 stance damage, and an additional 12-22 from the follow-up attack depending on weapon type\nThis Skill not Chargeable\nThis Skill's second attack can be Parried (the Glinstone projectile can be Parried with Carian Retaliation) \n\nThis Skill's first attack can be Parried if performed with no Focus.\n\nUpon activation, the user raises their armament (weapon) to the air, creating an arch of homing glintblades that hovers over their head. \n\nInputting the strong attack button will make the user perform a thrust that covers a fair amount of distance in a single direction.\n\nThe glintblades created have the property of dealing unusually high Stance damage (10 per blade, for a total of 40). \n\nThe spell version of this Weapon Skill does not have this stance damage property.\n\nThe follow up thrust can be aimed to lunge in any direction. \n\nThis attack also deals Stance damage according to the weapon type used: \n\n12 for Daggers\n16 for Thrusting Swords\n20 for Straight Swords, Curved Swords, Katanas, Twinblades, Spears, and Halberds.\n22 for Greatswords, Curved Greatswords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, and Great Spears.\n\nBecause stance-breaking enemies becomes easier, it presents the opportunity to deal Criticals more often. \n\nA great talisman to offset the FP cost is the Assassin's Cerulean Dagger.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nDoes not create glintstone blades.\nFollow up thrust deals less damage.\n\nGlintblades summoned deal 100% Magic Damage, and scale with Intelligence regardless of the Infusion used (even Heavy or Keen). The follow up attack deals damage based on Attack Rating and the damage type matches the same as the weapon, including ratio of damage types.\n\nGlintblade Phalanx Demonstration\n\nMoonveil Shinobi QUESTION
How to efficiently use Glintblade Phalanx in combat?

ANSWER
Activate Glintblade Phalanx to create glintstone blades for automatic attacks, follow up with a strong thrust, aim the thrust strategically, and opt for a high stance damage weapon type for criticals and efficient combat.
