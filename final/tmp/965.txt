However, doing so yields useful items, as well as Runes\n\nGlintstone Dragon is the normal enemy form of this boss. Other bosses with the same basic attacks are Flying Dragon Agheel, Flying Dragon Greyll, Decaying Ekzykes, Glintstone Dragon Adula, and Borealis the Freezing Fog. Lesser Dragon is a normal enemy with the same basic attacks.\n\n Smarag was a devourer of sorcerers, and over time, his body became corrupted by their glintstones. \n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dragon Smarag Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Crystalline Woods\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dragon Smarag Location\nFrom the Crystalline Woods Site of Grace, head due south around the front of the craggy island in the lake. Dragon Smarag:\n\nUse Banished Knight Oleg for his high defense and health to distract the dragon\nSmarag has very high Magic resistance, so Intelligence builds should use spells like Rock Sling that QUESTION
When can players encounter Glintstone Dragon Smarag in Elden Ring and what important items does defeating it yield?

ANSWER
Players can encounter Glintstone Dragon Smarag in Elden Ring as an optional boss, and defeating it yields the Dragon Heart and unlocks Smarag's Glintstone Breath.
