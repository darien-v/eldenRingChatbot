Blood Grease coats an armament, inflicting blood loss.\n\nSolidified grease made from a mixture of blood-red materials. Craftable item. \nCoats armament, inflicting blood loss. The effect lasts only for a short time.\n\nBlood Grease Use in Elden Ring\nBlood Grease coats the right-hand armament, inflicting Blood Loss.\n\nAttacks cause buildup of Blood Loss (30)\nLasts for 60 seconds\nBleed Damage = Max HP * 0.15 + 100\n\nWhere to find Blood Grease in Elden Ring\n\nConsumable Item. Two can be found by a grave with a wooden pole next to it. [Map Link]\nLiurnia of the Lakes: Can be found alongside the road, on a corpse. Near the cart with the Jellyfish Shield.
