Aspects of the Crucible: Tail allows the caster to grow a tail, that sweeps enemies before the caster. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the ancient Erdtree incantations.\nCreates a supple tail that sweeps through foes before the caster.Charging enhances potency.\nThis is a manifestation of the Erdtree's primal vital energies - anaspect of the primordial crucible, where all life was once  blendedtogether.\n\nWhere to find Aspects of the Crucible: Tail\nWhere to find Aspects of the Crucible: Tail:\n\nDropped by the Crucible Knight boss in Stormhill Evergaol. See it on the Elden Ring Interactive Map\n\nBoss Fight Video For Crucible Knight in Elden Ring\n\nElden Ring Aspects of the Crucible: Tail Guide\n\nStamina Cost: 42\nAspects of the Crucible: Tail creates a supple tail that sweeps through foes before the caster.\nUsers can charge this Incantation before using it in order to add a second wider and more power powerful hit.\nWhen fully charged, this incantation deals more damage than Aspects of the Crucible: Horns and similarly knocks enemies down.  However, the Tail attack takes longer to execute and requires more dangerous positioning \n\nAn effective strategy for dedicated Crucible Incantation users is to rush down the enemy with Horns, and then use Tail while the enemy is recovering\n\nDeals standard physical damage\nStance Damage: 13 (first hit), 39 (second hit)\nRecieves a 3.5% damage bonus for each worn piece of the Crucible Axe Set and Crucible Tree Set.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 22 to 20, and stamina attack power against guarded enemies has been increased.\n\nBuilds with Aspects of the Crucible: Tail \n\nTemplar Written Guide\n\nCrucible Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Aspects of the Crucible: Tail Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
