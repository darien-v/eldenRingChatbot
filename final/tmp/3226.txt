\n\n Perfume Bottle \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Container required for crafting perfume items \n\nPerfume Bottle is a Key Item and Crafting Material in Elden Ring. Perfume Bottle is used to contain crafted perfume arts, consumables which can be used to apply different effects to allies and enemies. See also Cracked Pot and Ritual Pot.\n\nGlass bottles used by perfumers. Used to seal various scent compounds.\nEssential vessel for crafting perfume items.\nThe art of perfuming was once jealously guarded in the capital, but after the perfumers were drafted into service during the Shattering, the art became widely practiced throughout the Lands Between.\n\nWhere to find Perfume Bottle in Elden Ring\nIn the world\n\n2 in Leyndell, Royal Capital. From East Capital Rampart Site of Grace, go through the west door, travel south along the rampart, and take the lift down. Enter the multi-story tower, climb up the ladder, then find the bottle in a chest hidden behind a table. [Map Link] From Avenue Balcony Site of Grace, go out the door southeast, turn left and follow the main road northeast, go through the big doors, then follow the lower path to a dead end, where the bottle is guarded by a Leonine Misbegotten.\n1 in The Shaded Castle: It's on a corpse at the edge of the inner east wall. Reach this inner wall by climbing a ladder on the eastern side of the courtyard. [Map Link]\n1 in Volcano Manor: The bottle is on a corpse in a room unlocked with the Drawing-Room Key. \n\nVideo Location\n\n2 in Perfumer's Ruins: One bottle is on a table guarded by an Omenkiller. The other is on the edge of a raised platform near a Giant Miranda Sprout. [Map Link]\n1 in Street of Sages Ruins: It's guarded by Miranda Sprouts. [Map Link]\n1 in Perfumer's Grotto: After encountering the second Giant Miranda Sprout, search the nearby area. You should see two chests in a corner; the bottle is in one of them. [Map Link] \n1 by Altus Highway Junction Site of Grace in Altus Plateau: The bottle is in the camp to the north of the Site of Grace, guarded by two Omen. [Map Link]\n\n Purchase from\n\nHermit Merchant near Hermit Merchant's Shack Site of Grace in Capital Outskirts: 2,000; 1 available.\n\nElden Ring Perfume Bottle Use\nPerfume Bottles can be used to store crafted perfume arts, which apply different effects to allies and enemies. After a perfume art is used, an empty bottle is returned to the player's inventory, allowing for another perfume art to be created after leaving combat.\nPerfume Bottles can hold the following perfume arts:\n\nSpark Aromatic  (Perfumer's Cookbook [1])\nUplifting Aromatic  (Perfumer's Cookbook [1])\nBloodboil Aromatic  (Perfumer's Cookbook [2])\nIronjar Aromatic  (Perfumer's Cookbook [3])\nPoison Spraymist  (Perfumer's Cookbook [2])\nAcid Spraymist  (Perfumer's Cookbook [4])\n\nElden Ring Perfume Bottle Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 10 Perfume Bottles.\nPerfume Bottles already obtained do not respawn in NG+.\nIf perfume arts are put into storage at a Site of Grace, their Perfume Bottles will be available for crafting new items. If you have perfume arts in storage, your Perfume Bottles will automatically refill when you die or rest at a Site of Grace.\nYou can use the Perfumer's Talisman to increase the potency of perfume QUESTION
Why are Perfume Bottles an essential crafting material in Elden Ring and how do they affect gameplay?

ANSWER
Perfume Bottles are essential as they store crafted perfume arts that apply various effects to allies and enemies, enhancing gameplay by enabling strategic use of these effects during combat.
