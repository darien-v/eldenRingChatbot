CONTEXT
\n\n Shared Order \n\n        Skill of the Golden Order fundamentalist knights. Grant the armament and those of nearby allies holy essence. Highly effective against Those Who Live in Death. \n\nShared Order is a Skill in Elden Ring. Shared Order is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, this can be used on all melee armaments. Can be mistaken for its weapon buff counterpart, Sacred Order. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Shared Order in Elden Ring\n\nDropped by a Night's Cavalry in Altus Plateau, next to the Altus Highway Junction Site Of Grace. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Shared Order Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Shared Order.\nFP Cost: 20\nDuration: 60 secs\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nDamage +10% (2.5% in PvP)\nDeals +50% damage to  Undead  type enemies and prevents Skeletons from reviving\nThis is an Aura Buff and will not stack with other buffs of the same type. \n\nSee Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking buffs.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: The effect time has been extended from 45 to 60 seconds.\n\nShared Order
