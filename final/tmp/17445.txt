\n\n Necromancer \n\nLocation\nBlack Knife Catacombs\n\nDrops\n 73 Runes Glass ShardHuman Bone ShardGrave Violet\n\nNecromancer is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Necromancers are severely malnourished and skinny humanoids dressed in tattered gray garbs and hoods. They are often found in Black Knife Catacombs, and can resurrect a certain few Skeletons each continuously, until they are killed, at which point they and their respective Skeletons will perish.\n\nNecromancer Locations in Elden Ring\nNecromancers can be found in the following location(s):\n\nBlack Knife Catacombs in Liurnia.\n\nElden Ring Necromancer Drops\n\n 73 Runes\nGlass Shard\nHuman Bone Shard\nGrave Violet\n\nNecromancer Notes & Tips\n\nThey are a type of Commoner.\nThey carry a Torchpole with a black fire on the end (visually appears to be ghostflame, but does not QUESTION
How can players effectively deal with Necromancers in the Black Knife Catacombs in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can effectively deal with Necromancers in the Black Knife Catacombs in Elden Ring by focusing on killing the Necromancers first to prevent them from resurrecting Skeletons, and using fire-based attacks to deal extra damage as they are weak to fire.
