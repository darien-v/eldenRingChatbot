\n\n Ash of War: Poisonous Mist \n\nAffinity\nPoison\n\nSkill\nPoisonous Mist\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Poisonous Mist is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Poisonous Mist provides Poison affinity and the Poisonous Mist Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Poison affinity and the following skill:\n"Poisonous Mist: Bathe armament in poison, and then slash, spreading toxic mist forwards. The armament retains its poison for a while."\nUsable on melee armaments (whips, fists, and claws excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Poisonous Mist Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Poisonous Mist:\n\nDropped by a teardrop scarab at the southwest section of the Aeonia Swamp in Caelid. [Map Link]. \n\nThis teardrop scarab can teleport a  short distance if the player gets too close. If available, apply appropriate measures to kill it from range.\n\nAsh of War: Poisonous Mist Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Poisonous Mist.\nDeals 12 stance damage\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Poison, Blood, Occult\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds, Reapers, Colossal Weapons\n\nPoisonous Mist Demonstration\n\nGrave Scythe Build\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Poisonous Mist Notes & Tips\n\nOn input, coats the armament in poison while swinging out a green mist that slightly travels forward, and lingers for a short period of time. \n\nConsumes 14 FP on use. Not chargeable.\nBoth parts of the skill (swing + mist) apply poison buildup on enemies. Both apply the same poison buildup.\nThe swing also deals damage if it connects.\n\nThe mist applies poison buildup per tick, and lasts for 5 seconds. \n\nThe poison buildup of the mist changes depending on the weapon's affinity, as it scales with the Arcane stat.\n\nThe poison coat adds poison buildup on hit to the armament (counts as a weapon buff), which lasts 20 seconds. \n\nThe poison buildup from the buff changes depending on the weapon's affinity. as it scales with the Arcane stat.\n\nPoison affinity has good Arcane scaling (tied with Blood affinity), beaten only by the Occult affinity. At Arcane levels close to 80, an Occult weapon with Poison Mist active can deal even more poison buildup per hit, than that same weapon with a Poison affinity and Poison Mist active.\nAn affinity that has no Arcane scaling at all will have considerably lower poison buildup per hit when Poison Mist is active. This remains true even if the player has a very low Arcane stat to benefit from the Poison/Bleed/Occult affinities.\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric QUESTION
Why would a player want to find and use the Ash of War: Poisonous Mist in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To grant an armament the Poison affinity and the Poisonous Mist skill which coats the armament in poison, deals damage, and applies poison buildup on enemies, lasting for 20 seconds and scaling with the Arcane stat.
