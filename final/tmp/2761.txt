QUESTION
Why is the Fire Prelate Set considered a heavy armor set in Elden Ring and how does it provide good defenses against Fire?

ANSWER
The Fire Prelate Set is considered heavy due to its weight distribution across all armor pieces, providing good defenses against Fire through high Fire resistance values on each piece.
