Lionel's Set This set was worn by Lionel the Lionhearted. When this chivalrous dauntless knight met Fia, who had been driven from her home, he declared himself to be her father. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Lionel's Set in Elden Ring\nFound on the bed in the building with the Lower Capital Church grace in Leyndell, Royal Capital. QUESTION
Why is Lionel's Set known for having extremely high Poise in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Lionel's Set is known for having extremely high Poise in Elden Ring due to the high Poise values of each individual armor piece: Lionel's Helm, Lionel's Armor, Lionel's Gauntlets, and Lionel's Greaves.
