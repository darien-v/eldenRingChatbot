Blackflame Monk Hood is part of the Blackflame Monk Armor Set, and is a Light Weight helm that provides Robustness and Vitality as its main feature. Blackflame Monk Hood protects the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nMail hood of iron links covering a headband engraved with black flames.Attire of the Blackflame Monks.\nThe Blackflame Monks, enthralled by the god-slaying black flame, became traitors, abandoning their posts as guardians.
