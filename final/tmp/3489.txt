CONTEXT
\n\n Thunderstorm \n\n        Imbue the armament's wing-blade with lightning and swing it around to create a tempestuous lightning storm. Follow up with an additional input to perform up to two spinning attacks. The lightning will stay on the blade for a while. \n\nThunderstorm is a Skill in Elden Ring. Thunderstorm is a special skill that is only available for the Stormhawk Axe, and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War.This ability generates a storm of lightning while the weapon remains with Lightning Damage for a short period of time. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Thunderstorm\n\nDefault skill for the Stormhawk Axe.\n\nElden Ring Thunderstorm Guide\n\nThis is a Unqiue Skill\nAdds 120 base Lightning Damage for 40 seconds\nFP Cost: 19 ( - 12)\nDeals 16+10+10 (Total 36) stance damage\nDeals Physical Damage and some Lightning Damage from the weapon buff. Damage is extremely high if you can land all attacks.\nDoes not trigger Winged Sword Insignia, Rotten Winged Sword Insignia or Millicent's Prosthesis\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nMotion speed has been increased.\nDuration has been increased from 20 to 40 seconds.\nAttack power of the effect that grants the weapon a lightning attribute has been increased from 85 to 120.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Thunderstorm Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
