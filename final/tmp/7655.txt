\n\n Claw Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.7\n\nEffect \n\n Increases jump attack damage by 15% (7.5% in PvP) \n\nClaw Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Claw Talisman increases jump attack damage by 15%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a claw and an assassin.Enhances jump attacks.\nThe assassins of Ravenmount are killers by trade. They assail their victims while dressed as birds of prey.\n\nClaw Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nClaw Talisman increases jump attack damage by 15%. (Effect is reduced to 7.5% in PvP.)\n\nWhere to find Claw Talisman in Elden Ring\nLocated at Stormveil Castle, on a corpse situated on a watchtower. [Map link]\n\nDetailed instructions\nGo to the Rampart Tower Site of Grace and exit the door that leads outside.\n\nLook right, and jump down onto the rooftop where there's a destroyed pillar.\n\nJump on the pillar, climb up it, and then jump onto the small ledge nearest it.\n\nYou'll be on a platform of sorts; follow it around the edge of the building, and as you turn the corner you'll see a ladder.\n\nReaching it will be your goal, however note that there are three enemies standing to your left waiting to ambush you from a raised platform, and a fourth "playing dead" to your right.\n\nWith them defeated, climb the ladder in front of you. Upon reaching the top, you'll see several birds, and in the right corner, a corpse.\n\nLoot the corpse to receive the Claw Talisman. [Video location]\n\nElden Ring Claw Talisman Notes & Tips\n\nSell value: 500\nDoes not work with Bow jump attacks.\nDoes not add damage to Spells that allow jump-casting, such as some Dragon Communion Incantations, and Bestial Sling.\nStacks with the damage bonus of Raptor's Black Feathers, even though only a single icon appears in the HUD. \n\nTwinblade jump attack used on the same Godrick Soldier for testing (rested between each attack so soldier was at full health): \n\n226 damage without Raptor's Black Feathers or Claw Talisman. \n249 damage with Raptor's Black Feathers (about 10% increase in damage).\n260 damage with Claw Talisman (about 15% increase in damage).\n285 damage with both Raptor's Black Feathers and Claw Talisman (about 26% increase in damage).\n\nNote: Patch 1.09: Effect
