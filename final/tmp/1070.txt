\n\n Bloodhound Knight \n\nLocation\nLakeside Crystal Cave\n\nDrops\n 2,700Cerulean Amber Medallion\n\nHP\n1,985\n\nStrong VS\n\nStandard\n\nSlash\n\nStrike\n\nWeak to \n\nLightning\n\nBloodhound Knight is a Field Boss in Elden Ring. Bloodhound Knight is a humanoid crawling on all fours, wielding a large curved sword, and is found in Lakeside Crystal Cave. This is an optional boss.\nLesser Bloodhound Knight is the normal enemy form of this boss. Another Bloodhound Knight appears in the Bloodhound Knight Darriwil fight.\n\n The Bloodhound Knights are trained as hunters and known to be unshakable trackers. Without the use of language, each knight chooses his own master. Once the decision has been made, the knight stays loyal for life. \n\nElden Ring Bloodhound Knight Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Lakeside Crystal Cave\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Bloodhound Knight Location\nThe end boss of Lakeside Crystal Cave. [Map Link]\nBloodhound Knight Combat information\n\nHealth: 1,985 HP \nDefense: 107\nStance: 65\nParryable: Yes\nIs vulnerable to a  critical hit  after being stance broken or parried\nDamage: Standard, Slash\nInflicts: Hemorrhage\nDrops 3,600, Cerulean Amber Medallion\nSee Bloodhound Knight Darriwil for a full boss guide\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nStandard: 10\nSlash: 10\nStrike: 10\nPierce: 0\n\nMagic: 0\nFire: 0\nLightning: -20\nHoly: 0\n\nThe negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. QUESTION
How can players effectively exploit the vulnerability of the Bloodhound Knight boss in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can exploit the vulnerability of the Bloodhound Knight boss in Elden Ring by landing critical hits after breaking its stance or parrying it.
