\n\n Great Oracular Bubble \n\nSpell Type\nClaymen Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 16\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Launches a large magic bubble \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 25 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 18 \n\nGreat Oracular Bubble  is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Great Oracular Bubble spell creates a bubble projectile that moves forward until it explodes, dealing damage in a small AoE. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSorcery of the claymen who served as priests in the ancient dynasty.\nProduces a gigantic magical bubble that drifts towards foes before it naturally ruptures. Charging increases the delay before the bubble pops.\nThe claymen search for lost oracles within their bubbles.\n\nWhere to find Great Oracular Bubble\nWhere to find Great Oracular Bubble :\n\nFrom the Worshippers’ Woods site of grace in Siofra River, travel south until you find a toppled pillar you can walk up with a ghostly archer on it, at the top there will be a waygate you can use. After this, move forward into an archway to the right of another collapsed pillar, the Teardrop beetle is in a room full of claymen.
