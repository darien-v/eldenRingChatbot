\n\n Ash of War: Stamp (Upward Cut) \n\nAffinity\nHeavy\n\nSkill\nStamp (Upward Cut)\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Stamp (Upward Cut) is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Stamp (Upward Cut) provides Heavy affinity and the Stamp (Upward Cut) Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Heavy affinity and the following skill:\n"Stamp (Upward Cut): Brace armament and step into a low stance that prevents recoil from most enemy attacks. Follow up with a strong attack for an upward strike."\nUsable on swords, axes, and hammers (small and medium swords excepted). \n\nAsh of War: Stamp (Upward Cut) Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Stamp (Upward Cut):\n\nSold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 1,500 . QUESTION
How can Ash of War: Stamp (Upward Cut) be effectively utilized in combat situations in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ash of War: Stamp (Upward Cut) can be used to grant an armament the Heavy affinity and perform the Stamp (Upward Cut) Skill, advantageous for low stance attacks and recoil prevention, suitable for swords, axes, and hammers, costing 5 FP for the initial attack and an additional 8 FP for the follow up, making it effective in combat for these weapon types.
