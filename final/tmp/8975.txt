They are group of female warriors associated with Gowry.\n\n Description goes here\n\nMillicent's Sisters Location in Elden Ring\nMillicent's Sisters, as a whole, can be found at Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree, where player can either challenge them or Millicent. Polyanna, Youngest Sister can be encountered earlier at Swamp of Aeonia as a summon for Commander O'Neil boss fight\nSee Millicent page for further information on how to reach a point, where they can be met as a group.\nMillicent's Sisters Drop in Elden Ring\n\nThis NPC collective can be fought. If challenged, drops Rotten Winged Sword Insignia\n\nMilicent's Sisters Notes & Tips\nMilicent's Sisters is a collective of 4 NPC's:\n\nPolyanna, Youngest Sister uses Blade of Calling. Deals Slash and Holy damage\nMaureen, Second Sister uses Treespear. Deals Pierce Damage.\nAmy, Third Sister uses two Flowing Curved Swords. Deals Slash damage\nMary, Eldest Sister uses Halo Scythe. Deals Slash/Holy damage and inflicts Hemorrhage\nParryable: Yes (Melee)\nAll sisters can cast Pest Threads incantation dealing Standard Damage\nImmune to Scarlet Rot, but susceptible to Poison, Hemorrhage and Fire Damage.\nLow poise, therefore can be interrupted with ease.\nMillicent is a strong NPC and she can withstand 1-2 sisters.\n\nElden Ring Milicent's Sisters Trivia\n\nBecause you OR Millicent dying causes this fight to end prematurely, it is highly recommended to split their aggro immediately upon entering. Millicent herself can do incredible damage but has just as low poise as the sisters; if they gang up on her, it can be over very quickly. By equipping Shabriri's Woe and a decent shield and armor, the layer should be able to tank a lot of the sisters' attacks or otherwise distract them to allow Millicent a chance to actually kill
