\n\n Caelid Colosseum \n\n        "..." \n\nCaelid Colosseum is a Location in Elden Ring, The Caelid Colosseum can be found in Northern Caelid, North of the Deep Siofra River Well Site of Grace. [Elden Ring Map Link]. \n\nAs of December 7th, 2022, the closed Caelid Colosseum can be opened after installing the Colosseum Update.\n\nCaelid Colosseum Map\n\nFound in Northern Caelid, North of the Deep Siofra River Well Site of Grace.\n\nUnlocking the Caelid Colosseum\nTo reach it, you must go through Siofra River Well in Limgrave, then go far northeast the Deep Siofra Well. Players will need to find and climb a structure there and interact with the gargoyle pedestal to unlock the elevator, stonesword keys are required for this. Take the elevator to the surface, and there will be enemies players will need to defeat. At the Great Jar, there will be sigils you will need to interact with and it will spawn Great Jar's warriors that players will have to defeat. Upon defeating the enemies, you will be rewarded with the Great Jar Arsenal Talisman and granted access to the Colosseum doors.\nThere are no alternative paths as you can't drop from the cliff without dying.\n\nNPCs and Merchants in Caelid Colosseum\n\nThe Great-Jar can be found at the front of the Colosseum.\nKnights of Great-Jar - there are three Duelist NPC invaders here who are part of Great-Jar's quest and need to be defeated one at a time without dying. \n\nCaelid Colosseum PvP\nThe Caelid Colosseum features all three modes with the addition of being able to summon Spirit Ashes to fight by players’ sides:\n\nIn United Combat, combatants will be divided into two teams to fight in timed battles with the ability to respawn. Each death and respawn grants points to the adversaries, offering victory to the team with the higher score.\nIn Combat Ordeal, it’s every Tarnished for themselves. Combatants in this mode can also respawn until the timer hits zero, and the one with the most points will prevail as victor.\nDuel Mode, Two Tarnished face off in a duel to the death, and without the ability to respawn.\n\nAll Items in Caelid Colosseum\n\nGATHER & FARM ITEMS\n\nItems go here\n\nUPGRADE MATERIALS\n\nUpgrade Materials go here\n\nEQUIPMENT AND MAGIC\n\nEquipment goes here\n\nUNIQUE, QUESTION
What are the requirements to unlock the closed Caelid Colosseum in Elden Ring after the Colosseum Update has been installed?

ANSWER
Players need to find and climb a structure to interact with a gargoyle pedestal, using stonesword keys to unlock the elevator, defeat enemies at the Great Jar, interact with sigils to spawn and defeat Great Jar's warriors, and receive the Great Jar Arsenal Talisman to gain access to the Colosseum doors.
