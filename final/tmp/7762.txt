CONTEXT
\n\n Ash of War: Barrage \n\nAffinity\nStandard\n\nSkill\nBarrage\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Barrage is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Barrage provides Standard affinity and the Barrage Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:\n"Barrage: Archery skill using a bow held horizontally. Ready the bow, then fire off a rapid succession of shots faster than the eye can see."\nUsable on light bows.\n\nAsh of War: Barrage Location in Elden Ring\nAsh of War: Barrage can be found in the following location:\n\nMt. Gelmir: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab found in the narrow channel north of Seethewater River.
