\n\n Darkness \n\nSpell Type\nTwo Fingers Incantations\n\nFP Cost 24\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Creates area of darkness that conceals caster \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 18 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nDarkness is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Darkness spell can be used to avoid enemy detection. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nIncantation of the Two Fingers' servants, who once served as the assassins of the Roundtable Hold.\nCreates a veil of darkness that conceals the caster.This incantation can be cast while in motion or crouching.\nThe assassins were themselves once Tarnished who had strayed from guidance, and they pursued their duty in the darkness that is without grace.\n\nWhere to find Darkness\nWhere to find Darkness:\n\nPurchase from Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows after giving either the Assassin's Prayerbook.\nCost = 3500\n\nElden Ring Darkness Guide\n\nTwo Fingers Incantation\nStamina Cost: 24\nWhen tagged by the veil of darkness, an enemy's aggro is reset and they are blinded for ~7 seconds. \n\nThis time can allow you to reposition outside of their sight or go for a critical attack.\n\nDoes not work against certain enemies, notably most Bosses.\nIn PvP, Darkness does not prevent lock-on. However, it is still a viable way to mask your actions while inside the veil.\nSee Buffs & Debuffs for more information on stacking debuffs.\nNote: FP cost reduced significantly as per Patch 1.07, from 34 to 24.\n\nElden Ring Darkness Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
