QUESTION
Why is the Preceptor's Set armor in Elden Ring typically worn by magic preceptors who served the Carian royals?

ANSWER
Preceptor's Set armor in Elden Ring is typically worn by magic preceptors who served the Carian royals because it increases mind at the cost of stamina and is usually donned by glintstone sorcerers, the descendants of astrologers severed from the stars' fate.
