For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nElden Ring Putrid Tree Spirit Boss Guide\n\nPutrid Tree Spirit Fight Strategy\nThe Best Tips for Putrid Tree Spirit:\n\nIf possible, bring Scarlet Rot Talismans such as Kindred of Rot's Exultation which can increase attack power or Immunizing Horn Charm which raises Immunity\nUse Meteorite Spell to cause it to stagger\nSummon Rotten Stray Ashes\nUse Bloodhound's Step to help with the boss's quick movements\nBe ready upon entering the fog gates, as it approaches for an attack almost immediately. If considering Summons, call them after this initial action\nUse pillars in the arena if available but we wary because they can be destroyed.\nBe aware of the safe corners in between attacks to find an opportunity to drink your flask.\nAlways consider your position because this large creature can be faced in a small arena \nLighten your load to perform swift dodges and movements since this boss has quick movements\nConsider not using camera lock on since this boss can take up the whole screen.\nUse heavy weapons and spells to stagger\nAt ~50% health, does an AOE Scarlet Rot explosion. Run away and find a spot on the ground without a flame visual FX and stand still. If you attempt to keep running away, you may run into another scarlet rot explosion behind you.\n\nMelee Users\nCan be staggered, which is very effective as its head takes increased damage, and this makes it much more accessible while he is on the ground. Consider going for attacks directly on the head while it is stance broken, instead of a critical strike. Two main methods with melee is to constantly circle around its head when lowered or reach its tail and attack from there to avoid most of it's directional attacks. Hug its back side since most attacks will come from the head. When it starts to pull back, be ready to dodge and dives or spontaneous movement dashes from the boss. Bloodhound's Step could be considered if available to dodge its quick attacks faster and to help with countering. Use a combination of waiting for its dive attacks, dodging through and repositioning around the beast to get as many hits in while out of its line of sight until HP is depleted. Keep an eye on your Scarlet Rot.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nCan be burst down with Meteorite. As soon as you enter the fog door, target the boss and move right. It will try to attack. Immediately cast meteorite close to it and hold the button. It should stagger which allows you to drink a blue potion to replenish your FP. You might need enough FP for this to work. Another spell to consider is Rock Sling, which deals great physical damage and can also stagger it.  If your FP is out and it doesn't stagger, you don't have enough Mind stat. Note: meteorite needs to be cast close to the target for it to hit. Though there is not a lot of free space in the arena where this boss is found so creating distance for longer ranged attacks will be a challenge. To aid with this, it is recommended to use Spirit Ashes during this encounter. Continue your attacks in between the windows of this boss's attack until it is defeated. \n\nPutrid Tree Spirit Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nHead Slam\nThe Tree Spirit lifts its head and smashes down for a slam\nDodge roll backwards to avoid the landing zone as soon as you see its head lift upwards. \n\nTail Whip\n If on its back side, it will pull back its tail before sweeping it in a swift motion\nRoll Through this attack or run away and reposition\n\nScarlet Rot Explosion\nSends out a blast and effects a large vicinity\nIt is a good idea to have something equipped that could reduce these effects first. Once the spirit emits a glow, put as much distance between you and the Tree Spirit to minimize effects.\n\nDive Grab\nWith an open mouth, it will attempt to swallow you and this will heavily affect you with Rot\nImmediately dodge away and circle far back if you can to get out of the line of sight. \n\nSlide Dive\nSwoops in for a dive dealing massive damage\nLike the other dive attack, Immediately dodge away and circle far back if you can to get out of the line of sight. \n\nFire Breathing\nThe Tree Spirit begins to lower its head and angles itself to breath fire on its victims. \nSince this is a directional attack, step out of the line of damage, reangle yourself and this should give you an opportunity to attack during this action. \n\nPutrid Tree Spirit Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nOther Notes and Trivia Go Here\n\n Putrid Tree Spirit  War-Dead Catacombs  \n\n__image__\n\nDrops\n64,000Redmane Knight Ogha Spirit Ashes Golden Seed\n\nHP\n18,144\n\nElden Ring Putrid Tree Spirit Boss (War-Dead Catacombs)\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: War-Dead Catacombs\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nPutrid Tree Spirit Location\nFrom the War-Dead Catacombs Site of Grace, head down the stairs and straight ahead through the double doors once you've pulled the level to open them. [Map Link]\nTo open this door, move past the spirits fighting one another and head up the second flight of stairs on the north, jog east, and then head north again through the tunnel past the room with two spirits. QUESTION
What are some effective strategies for defeating the Putrid Tree Spirit in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Bring Scarlet Rot Talismans, use spells like Meteorite, summon Rotten Stray Ashes, dodge attacks efficiently, stagger the boss, aim for head for increased damage, counter its various attacks with appropriate strategies, and be mindful of boss's HP phases.
