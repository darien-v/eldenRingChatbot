QUESTION
How can Surge, O Flame! be effectively used in combat situations?

ANSWER
After obtaining Surge, O Flame! incantation, cast it continuously to create a stream of fire, dealing fire damage to enemies, while allowing you to move and maintain it until your FP runs out.
