CONTEXT
\n\n Vacuum Slice \n\n        Lost skill of ancient heroes. Hold the armament aloft to surround it with a shearin vacuum, then launch it forwards as a blade-like projectile.  \n\nVacuum Slice is a Skill in Elden Ring. Vacuum Slice is a skill that allows users to turn their wielded weapon into a projectile. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Vacuum Slice\n\nDefault skill on the Gargoyle's Greatsword. \nAsh of War: Vacuum Slice can be found inside the ruined temple surrounded by waterfalls in Deeproot Depths. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Vacuum Slice Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Vacuum Slice.\nFP Cost: 14\nDeals 9 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nThis Skill is not affected by the base damage or scaling of the weapon, but has its own base damage and Strength and Dexterity scaling, which are affected by infusions. \nNOTE: Prior to Patch 1.07, did not deal additional damage if both the physical slash and the projectile both connected, but Patch 1.07 added damage detection to the weapon part of the skill as well, also increased motion speed and the range of the blade and reduced FP consumption.\n\nVacuum Slice Demonstration\n\nElden
