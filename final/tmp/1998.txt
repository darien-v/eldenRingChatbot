QUESTION
Why are Crystal Tears in Elden Ring significant and how do they enhance the effects of the Flask of Wondrous Physick?

ANSWER
Crystal Tears in Elden Ring are significant as they can be mixed to create concoctions that restore half of one's maximum HP in mixed physick, enhancing the effects of the Flask of Wondrous Physick by providing various custom elixir effects when combined.
