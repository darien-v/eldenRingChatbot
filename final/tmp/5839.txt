Guardian Mask is part of the Guardian Set Armor Set, and is the armor worn by the Guardians found around various Minor Erdtrees. Guardian Mask protects the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nEngraved golden mask.Worn by the guardians of the Minor Erdtrees.\nIn accordance with an ancient pact with the Erdtree, it is said that  their deaths lead not to destruction, but instead to renewed. eternal life as guardians. \n\nWhere to find Guardian Mask in Elden Ring\nThe Guardian Mask Head Armor can be found at:\n\nGuardian Set pieces can be acquired as drops from Erdtree Guardians that are found near Minor Erdtrees in the Lands Between. \n\nAn area dense with Guardians can be found by starting at the Dragonbarrow Fork site of grace, and working your way uphill towards the Minor Erdtree.
