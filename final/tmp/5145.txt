\n\n Blackguard's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nBlackguard's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story. \n\nThe Blackguard's bell bearing, found upon his perished flesh.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to new items.\n\nBlackguard's Bell Bearing Location in Elden Ring\nBlackguard's Bell Bearing can be found in the following location:\n\nFound on Blackguard Big Boggart.\nBlackguard Big Boggart can be initially found in the Liurnia of the Lakes swamps at the Boilprawn Shack.\nHe then later moves to Leyndell in the outer moat near the Capital Rampart.\n\nBlackguard's Bell Bearing Use\nBlackguard's Bell Bearing can be used to gain access to the following items when offered to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold:\n\nRya's Necklace\nBoiled Prawn (if already unlocked)\nBoiled Crab (if already unlocked)\n\nBlackguard's Bell Bearing Notes & Tips\n\nThe shop unlocked is within the Bell Bearing Shop 2 submenu.\nIf you complete Blackguard Big Boggart's questline, this bell bearing will be on his body considering he dies at the end of it.\nThere is no purchase limit on Boiled Prawn or Boiled Crab. \n\nBoiled Prawn gives a 15% physical damage reduction and Boiled Crab gives a 20% physical damage reduction (before other damage mitigation factors), and both last 1 minute.\n\nElden Ring Bell Bearings\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell Bearing  ♦  Bernahl's Bell Bearing  ♦  Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing  ♦  Corhyn's Bell How can the Blackguard's Bell Bearing be obtained in Elden Ring?
