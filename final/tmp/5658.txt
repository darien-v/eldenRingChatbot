\n\n Resurrection Painting \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Reminiscence of the painting "Resurrection" \n       Examine using the corresponding button. \n\nResurrection Painting is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs\n\nWork of a wandering artist.Reminiscence of a painting titled "Resurrection."\nThis painter is said to have captured the landscapes seen during the last moments of those welcomed into death's embrace.\nThe soul of the painter, and vestiges of the dead's last moments can be discovered by visiting the location depicted even now.\n\nWhere to find Resurrection Painting in Elden Ring\n\nLocation: Artist's Shack. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Resurrection Painting Tutorial Guide\n\nStart at the "Behind Caria Manor" grace location and head slightly southeast until you arrive at a graveyard. When the path forks to the South and SE, take the South path that does not go under the jutting rock. The artist is behind the rock to the right of the South path that juts West. There are albinaurics and undead nearby. The artist will drop a Larval Tear, the Juvenile Scholar Robe and the Juvenile Scholar Cap.
