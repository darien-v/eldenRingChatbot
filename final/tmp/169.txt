Only the foolhardy would rush such against such odds\n\nStormgate is a Location in Elden Ring. The Stormgate is found in Stormhill, Limgrave, north from the Network Test starting point, just after Gatefront Ruins. It is a path that is heavily guarded and leads to the Stormhill region.\n\nStormgate Map for Elden Ring\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Stormgate\n\nThere are no NPCs in this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Stormgate\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nHerba\nMushroom\nRowa Fruit\nRuin Fragment\nPickled Turtle Neck\nGolden Rune [1]\nLump of Flesh\nLordsworn's Bolt (Archer Soldiers drop)\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSmithing Stone (1)\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nArrow's Reach Talisman\nLordsworn's Greatsword\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nNo Unique Items, Ashes, and Keys in this location.\n\nStormgate Creatures, Enemies and Bosses in Elden Ring\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nTroll\nSoldier\nTeardrop Scarab\nWandering Nobles\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses in this location\n\nWalkthrough for Stormgate in Elden Ring\nThe entrance of Stormgate can be found on the north side of Gatefront Ruins in Limgrave. There is a Site of Grace by the entrance, don't forget to activate it as your checkpoint in case you die. Right off the bat, as you walk up the slope, you'll hear a loud roar and a Troll will appear, you'll also notice a multiple guards on each corner, as well as barricades along the path. The Storgmate is a guarded narrow path that leads to the Stormhill region. If you want to avoid any combat here (which is possible), you can do so by horseback riding. Just make sure you have the Spectral Steed Whistle item before you rush through the barricades and the enemies. \nMake sure you catch the Teardrop Scarab as it will drop you Ash of War: Carian Glintsword. There should also be a chariot in the area leading out of Gatefront Ruins where you can open the chest and obtain a Lordsworn's Greatsword.\n\nElden Ring Stormgate Gallery and Notes\n[other images go
