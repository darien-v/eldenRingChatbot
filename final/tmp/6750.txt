\n\n Lantern \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Attach to waist to illuminate surroundings \n\nLantern is a Tool in Elden Ring. Lantern can be attaching to the waist of the player to illuminate surroundings. It has the advantage of freeing up the user's hands. Tools are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.\n\nA small waist-worn lantern that illuminates surroundings. \nUse once to turn on, and again to turn off.\nWhile its light is dimmer than that of a torch, it has the advantage of freeing up the user's hands. \n\nWhere to find Lantern
