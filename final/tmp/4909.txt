Turtles are not hostile and will not flee on approach. They will attempt to hide in their shells when attacked.\n\nTurtles are known as a nutritious ingredient, symbolic of inexhaustible power.\nHowever, those who hold turtles to be wise creatures consider the practice of eating their meat to be barbarous.\n\nTurtle Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Turtle:\n\nPlayers can encounter Turtles roaming the beaches and forests of Western Limgrave, often near water. i.e. The entrance to Groveside Cave\nSouth West Liurnia of the Lakes\nNorth of Chelona's Rise and the Chelona's Rise location itself; this entire plateau has many turtles, actually. Why do some consider the practice of eating turtle meat to be barbarous despite turtles being a nutritious ingredient and symbolizing inexhaustible power?

ANSWER
