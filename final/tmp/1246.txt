CONTEXT
\n\n Stamina \n\nType\nBase Stat\n\nEffect\nA resource consumed by most player actions.\n\nStamina is a Base Stat in Elden Ring. Stamina is the green bar and a stat that appears on the player's HUD and stat screen. It is used for most of the actions that the player character performs. Stamina can be increased by investing points into Endurance when leveling up or by equipping certain items. Stats determine the strengths and weaknesses of your character. Stats can be increased by spending points to them after leveling up.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08 a bug where stamina would replenish when switching to a crouching position while running hand has been fixed.\n\nStamina Elden Ring Guide\n\nStamina is a resource consumed when attacking, sprinting, blocking, dodging, and casting spells. It is one of the most active stats, as it determines the amount of energy left to perform actions. \n\nOut of combat, the user doesn't consume any stamina when performing such actions.\n\nIt is especially needed in Combat. If your Stamina gets depleted, you won't be able to dodge, block, attack. or quickly flee. \n\nDepleting your stamina during battle is always dangerous. For that reason, managing your stamina is a essential mechanic to master.\n\nStamina automatically recharges passively. \n\nHow to increase character Stamina in Elden Ring\n\nStamina can be increased by adding points to the Endurance stat when leveling up.\nUsing a Rune Arc with the effect of Radahn's Great Rune\nEquipping some Talismans or using some consumibles, stated below.\n\nRELATED
