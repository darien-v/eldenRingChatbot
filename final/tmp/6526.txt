CONTEXT
\n\n Blood Tax \n\n        Blood Oath skill granted by Lord of Blood. Twist to build power, then unleash a flurry of thrusts that rob the target of both their blood and their HP. \n\nBlood Tax is a Skill in Elden Ring. Blood Tax is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, this can be used on armaments capable of thrusting (Colossal Weapons excepted). Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Blood Tax in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill of the Varré's Bouquet.\nFound in the Mohgwyn Palace area in a small cave, at the eastern edge of the blood lake with four blood Albinaurics that appear after killing the beetle.\n\nElden Ring Blood Tax Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Blood Tax.\nFP Cost: 14 on use.\nDeals 16 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nAll 4 separate thrusts can be Parried\nUpon activation, the user prepares their armament while performing a spin, before unleashing a quick flurry of thrusts in front of them.\nDeals 88+44+44+110 MV (Motion Value) of damage for the 4 hits of the combo respectively.\nApplies 100+50+50+100 status effect MV (Motion Value) for the 4 hits of the combo respectively, averaging out to 75% of normal status effect buildup compared to 4 normal light attacks, though at a much faster rate when used on heavier and slower weapons.\nRestores 2% + 25 on the first 3 hits and 4% + 45 HP on the final hit for a total of 10% + 120 HP.\nThis weapon skill does not innately have any status buildup, but will apply respective buildup per hit if the weapon has it (either through weapon buffs, or innately).\nBecause of the possible stagger on light enemies pushing them backwards, it's suggested to perform the skill as closest to the enemy as possible to make all hits count.\n\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nIncreased motion speed and attack power.\nIncreased HP deprivation effect.\nReduced the delay between the end of various actions (such as using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill.\n\nBlood Tax Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric
