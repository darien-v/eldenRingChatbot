\n\n D's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to \n       Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nD's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. D's Bell Bearing should be offered to the Two Maiden Husks located at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to new items. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nD's bell bearing, found upon his perished flesh.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to new items.\n\nWhere to find D's Bell Bearing in Elden Ring\n\nOption 1: After you defeat the Fire Giant boss and approached the Forge of the Giants. Return to Roundtable Hold, found on D's corpse.\nOption 2: Return the Weathered Dagger as part of Fia's questline and find the bell bearing on D's corpse in the Roundtable Hold.
