QUESTION
How can Leaden Hardtear be effectively combined with other Crystal Tears in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to maximize its impact in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Leaden Hardtear can be combined with other Crystal Tears, such as Crimson Crystal Tear or Opaline Hardtear, in the Flask of Wondrous Physick at grace sites to create customized elixirs with enhanced effects in Elden Ring.
