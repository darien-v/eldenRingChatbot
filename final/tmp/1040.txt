\n\n Church of Elleh \n\nChurch of Elleh is a ruined building that hides a Site of Grace. The rooftop has caved in and offers no protection from the elements, and weeds are overtaking the walls, but this doesn't stop merchants from seeking what little shelter is on offer.\n\nChurch of Elleh is a Location in Elden Ring. The Church of Elleh is found in Western Limgrave. Players will find it by heading north from the first Site of Grace available. This is an important checkpoint for the player as it features a merchant and weapon upgrade table.\n\nChurch of Elleh Map for Elden Ring\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Church of Elleh\n\nRenna -  Teaches the player character the basics of Spirit Summoning and give them the key item required to summom\nMerchant Kalé - Sells general goods and amor [Elden Ring Map]\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Church of Elleh\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nRowa Fruit\nErdleaf Flower\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSmithing Stone [1]\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nThere are no equipment items in this location\nSee Merchant Kale for his shop stock\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nSpirit Calling Bell\nLone Wolf Ashes\n\nChurch of Elleh Creatures, Enemies and Bosses in Elden Ring\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no creatures in this location\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nTree Sentinel\n\nWalkthrough for Church of Elleh in Elden Ring\nIf you go straight from where the White Mask Varre was, from the starting point of Limgrave, you can find the Church of Elleh and discover another Site of Grace, the Merchant Kalé, as well as a Smithing Table to upgrade your Weapons. Interacting with another Site of Grace will allow you now to fast travel to any sites you've discovered. But keep in mind that there are some dungeons and other areas where fast travel cannot be done.\nAt the Church of Elleh, speak to Merchant Kalé so that you can check his wares or just talk to him to get more details or lore information. It is recommended to buy the Telescope from him so that you can scout areas from afar. However, if you ask him by choosing "Recommendation" Merchant Kalé will tell you to buy a Crafting Kit which allows you to craft basic items on your own. Next to the the merchant, on his left, you can find a Smithing Table which is used to strengthen your armaments. Approaching it will trigger a brief tutorial on what the Smithing Table is and you can also find x1 Smithing Stone Shard near it.\nAt some point during your return, Renna will be at the Church of Elleh in Limgrave. Return to the Church after getting the ability to summon your Spectral Steed from Melina. You will find Renna sitting by the ruins of the church. QUESTION
How can players utilize the offerings and NPCs at the Church of Elleh in Elden Ring to enhance their gameplay experience?

ANSWER
Players can utilize the Church of Elleh in Elden Ring by learning Spirit Summoning from Renna, purchasing items from Merchant Kalé, and upgrading weapons using the Smithing Table available at the location.
