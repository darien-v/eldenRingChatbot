\n\n Village Windmill Pasture \n\nA windmill with two stables, found near the Dominula, Windmill Village. The windmill here is still functional but the area has been taken over by Misbegotten creatures. \n\nVillage Windmill Pasture is a Location in Elden Ring. The Village Windmill Pasture is found in Altus Plateau, close to the Windmill Village. It can be reached from virtually all directions that connect to the main road stretching across the northernmost limits of the region.\n\nVillage Windmill Pasture Map\nVillage Windmill Pasture is found to the west of Windmill Village.
