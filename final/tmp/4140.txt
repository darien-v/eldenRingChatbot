Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nTalisman depicting a pearlescent ancient dragon.Boosts non-physical damage negation.\nThe ancient dragons who ruled in the prehistoric era before the Erdtree, would protect their lord as a wall of living rock.And so it is that the shape of the dragon has become symbolic of all manner of protections.\n\nPealdrake Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\n Pearldrake Talisman reduces Elemental Damage taken by 5/7/9%. (Effect reduced to 2/3/4% in PvP.)\n\nWhere to find Pearldrake Talisman in Elden Ring\n\nBase talisman: Can be found beyond the lowest ("Crumbling Lands") waygate of the Four Belfries. Go through the waygate, then jump down to the north, then jump down to the east (and if you can, use ranged attacks to kill the two beastmen below), then jump down again, then jump down (to the north) one more time. \n+1 Variation: Can be found in Wyndham Ruins in the Mt. Gelmir region. The talisman is found inside a chest at the end of the area sealed by an Imp Statue; a Stonesword Key is required. [Map link]\n+2 Variation: Can be found in Miquella's Haligtree. Travel to the Haligtree Town Site of Grace and exit through the northwestern door. Then climb the ladder and turn around to jump southeast across the gap. The talisman will be located on a corpse at the base of a statue, guarded by a Misbegotten Warrior. QUESTION
Where to find Pearldrake Talisman in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Base talisman: Can be found beyond the lowest ("Crumbling Lands") waygate of the Four Belfries. Go through the waygate, then jump down to the north, then jump down to the east (and if you can, use ranged attacks to kill the two beastmen below), then jump down again, then jump down (to the north) one more time. +1 Variation: Can be found in Wyndham Ruins in the Mt. Gelmir region. The talisman is found inside a chest at the end of the area sealed by an Imp Statue; a Stonesword Key is required. +2 Variation: Can be found in Miquella's Haligtree. Travel to the Haligtree Town Site of Grace and exit through the northwestern door. Then climb the ladder and turn around to jump southeast across the gap. The talisman will be located on a corpse at the base of a statue, guarded by a Misbegotten Warrior.
