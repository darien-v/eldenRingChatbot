The Nox Monk Armor is part of the Nox Monk Armor Set. Nox Monk Armor is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nArmor worn by the monks of the Eternal City, with a silk cape attached. Why do the Nox Monks wear the Nox Monk Armor in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
