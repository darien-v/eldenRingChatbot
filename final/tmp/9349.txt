Every single type of attack this boss does stacks bleed so it's best that you bring Stanching Boluses. Do not rush this boss and be careful with its string of stabs and slashes. Also be wary of its stance where you see the dagger on its left hand being lifted, it means it's ready to parry your incoming attacks. Do not engage in melee when you see this stance, as it will most likely parry you successfully once you try to attack. A successful parry on you will get you stabbed and also build-up your bleed meter very quickly.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nFor magic and ranged users, it's recommended that you equip a heavy hitting spell/incantation or weapon for ranged attacks to quickly finish the Sanguine Noble. Just be careful with the blood puddles and the dagger throw as those are also the boss' ranged options. You should also look out for the leaping thrust where it leaps forward and thrusts its sword, this move quickly closes the gap when you're trying to create distance or when you're far away from the boss for too long.\n\nSanguine Noble Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nBlood Puddles\nSlashes the ground, leaving blood puddles in the area. Touching the blood puddles will build-up your bleed meter.\nYou will clearly see the blood puddles on the ground, stepping on them will build up your bleed meter so avoid them at all costs.\n\nDagger Parry\nRaises the dagger in its left hand, waiting for an attack. If it successfully parries you, you will get a frontal stab that deals massive damage, and also stack up your bleed meter very quickly.\nWhen you see the dagger being lifted up or you see a different stance, do not engage in melee, because the parry will most likely connect, and you will be stabbed, which deals massive damage. You can engage in magic or ranged attack.\n\nDagger Throw\nUses the dagger on its left hand as a projectile and throws it towards you.\nWhen you create distance, be ready to dodge roll as well since the dagger throw is the ranged option for this boss.\n\nLeaping Thrust\nLeaps forward, closing the gap and thrusts its sword. Can be followed up by other attacks afterwards.\nThis is one of the boss' attacks that will quickly close the gap when you try to create distance or if you're far away for to long. It is recommended that you just dodge roll backwards or away from this attack as it can follow up with a different attack after the initial thrust.\n\nSanguine Noble Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nVoice Actor: What are the strategies and tips for defeating the Sanguine Noble in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Bring Stanching Boluses, use fast one-hand weapons, avoid blood puddles, observe its parry stance, utilize heavy hitting spells for ranged attacks, and dodge its leaping thrust to defeat the Sanguine Noble in Elden Ring.
