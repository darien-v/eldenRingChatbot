Only, without the twin tails. \nDiallos Hoslow had an older brother who was a stern, self-possessed man of a few words. His achievements made him seem out of reach. \nAnd so the younger aspired to be like the older. Yearning for the day that he would tell the tale of House Hoslow, in blood. Knowing full well that it would break his brother's heart.\n\nWhere to find Diallos's Mask in Elden Ring\nThe Diallos's Mask Helm can be found by:\n\nFound upon completion of Diallos's questline.\nCan also be gained by killing Diallos in North Liurnia of the Lakes. [Map Link]\n\nDiallos's Mask Set in Elden Ring\n\nDiallos's Mask is the second headpiece of Hoslow's Set.\nThe rest of the set is composed of Hoslow's Helm, Hoslow's Armor, Hoslow's Gauntlets, and Hoslow's Greaves.\n\nElden Ring Diallos's Mask Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  3000\nAfter you first speak with Diallos at Roundtable Hold, you can find him twice in the open world at different points in the game: North of the Academy Gate Town Site of Grace, and later on in Volcano Manor toward the end of the relevant questline (started by speaking to Raya, just south in a pagoda next to the birds eye scope). If players fight and defeat Diallos while he's mourning Lanya in Liurnia of the Lakes (Location), he will drop the mask and the Petal Whip, one of a set of two that are available.\nPoise Updated to
