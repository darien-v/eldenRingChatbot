QUESTION
Where can Sanctuary Stone be found in Elden Ring and how is it obtained?

ANSWER
Sanctuary Stone can be found in Elden Ring in Limgrave, Weeping Peninsula, and Liurnia of the Lakes, and it is obtained by purchasing from Twin Maiden Husks or looting from enemies like Mad Pumpkin Head, Azula Beastman, and Abnormal Stone Cluster.
