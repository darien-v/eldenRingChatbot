\n\n Vulgar Militia Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 70\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons three vulgar militia spirits \n\nVulgar Militia Ashes is a Spirit Summon in Elden Ring. Vulgar Militia Ashes are used to summon the spirits of three vulgar militiamen.\nVulgar Militiamen can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Vulgar Militiamen.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of three vulgar militiamen.  Spirits of the vulgar militia who wield long-hafted, serrated hatchets. These brutal weapons are particularly effective at causing blood loss to opponents. In the Lands Between, the small were scorned, and so they formed their vulgar militia as a means to make a living, albeit in ignominy.\n\nWhere to find Vulgar Militia Ashes in Elden Ring\nThe Vulgar Militia Ashes can be found in the following location:\n\nAuriza Hero's Grave: In the large Chariot Hall where two Chariots are running almost side by side with a short delay between them, head all the way down to the large pit behind some pillars. Looking down, you can find several wooden beams you can drop down onto. Navigate your way across the beams, heading north to find a hidden area on the right with a statue. Head into the corridor behind the statue and to the right to access a small room where a corpse holding the ashes can be found.
