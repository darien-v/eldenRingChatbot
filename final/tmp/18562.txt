This is a mesh of their model, which is called “Cordyceps” in the files, likely based on Ophiocordyceps unilaterus, a fungus that grows from an insect’s body and controls them, similar to a zombie. It's possible that the knights are being controlled by a fungus brought upon by the Scarlet Rot, as there have been evidence of fungi growing where Rot is plentiful.
