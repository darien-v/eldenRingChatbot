CONTEXT
\n\n Red-Hot Whetblade \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Grants choice of affinity upgrade to weapon \n\nRed-Hot Whetblade is a Whetblade in Elden Ring. Red-Hot Whetblade grants choice of affinity upgrade to weapon. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nRed-hot whetblade with a cipher engraved. Can be used as a whetstone knife.\nWhen applying an affinity using physical or flame-type ashes of war, an additional affinity of fire or flame art can be chosen.\n\nWhere to find Red-Hot Whetblade in Elden Ring\nWhen the Radahn Festival is inactive, travel to the Chamber Outside the Plaza site of grace. Once there, head out to the three flamethrower enemies and take a right. You should be face to face with another flamethrower. Go past them and towards the tower. An Abductor Virgin will attack you from the right as you're heading towards it. Once inside you'll find the whetblade on the floor and a soldier nearby on the stairs. QUESTION
Why is the Red-Hot Whetblade considered a key item in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Red-Hot Whetblade is considered a key item in Elden Ring because it grants the choice of affinity upgrade to a weapon, unlocking areas and quests and further progressing the game's story.
