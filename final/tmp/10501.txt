CONTEXT
\nBallistae are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Ballistae are ranged weapons that can be used to inflict high damage on hostile characters from afar and with great potential to break guard. Unlike Crossbows, they do not need to be loaded before firing each shot. They grant no guard protection, encouraging users to maintain distance from advancing enemies. QUESTION
Why are Ballistae in Elden Ring unable to receive new Ashes of War, be greased, spell buffed, or given new affinities?

ANSWER
Ballistae in Elden Ring cannot receive new Ashes of War, be greased, spell buffed, or given new affinities because they are restricted in their modification capabilities.
