Fortissax's Lightning Spear spell stabs from above with two red lightning spears in tandem when cast. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nIncantation that channels the power of the ancient dragon Fortissax. \ncreates two spears of red lightning and stabs them into the ground from above, one after another. On impact, each spear will burst into trails of lightning covering the area.\nDuring the War of the Anicent Dragons, these twin red lightning stakes were the hallmark of the ancient dragon who was once called the mightiest boulderstone. \n\nWhere to find Fortissax's Lightning Spear\nWhere to find Fortissax's Lightning Spear:\n\nTrade Remembrance of the Lichdragon with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Fortissax's Lightning Spear Guide\n\nStamina Cost: 52\nDeals lightning damage and 44 stance damage (22 per spear)\nOne of the Dragon Cult Incantations and is boosted by the Gravel Stone Seal\nBest used very close to the target. Has substantially higher damage than charged Lightning Spear but only if both strikes hit very close to the caster.\nDamage drops off with distance very dramatically. Not at all good for room clearing or multiple opponents.\nNotes: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost has been reduced from 45 to 35 and stamina damage against guarding enemies was increased.\n\nElden Ring Fortissax's Lightning Spear Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
