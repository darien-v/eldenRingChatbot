\n\n Ash of War: Storm Stomp \n\nAffinity\nQuality\n\nSkill\nStorm Stomp\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Storm Stomp is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Storm Stomp provides the Quality affinity and the Storm Stomp Skill. Ashes of War can be used to modify the Skill of an equipment piece, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Quality affinity and the following skill:\n"Storm Stomp: One of the skills that channel the tempests of Stormveil. Stomp hard on the ground to kick up a momentary storm."\nUsable on all melee armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Storm Stomp Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Storm Stomp:\n\nFound in the southeastern area of Gatefront Ruins, Limgrave. There is a set of stairs that leads to a small room underground where a treasure chest can be found. Inside the chest is the Ash of War: Storm Stomp and Whetstone Knife key item. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nAsh of War: Storm Stomp Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants Storm Stomp on all melee weapons.\nProvides the Quality affinity, which balances Strength and Dexterity Scaling, and decreases Base damage\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Spears, Great QUESTION
Why should players consider using the Ash of War: Storm Stomp in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players should consider using the Ash of War: Storm Stomp in Elden Ring because it grants the Quality affinity, provides the Storm Stomp skill usable on all melee armaments, and can be applied to various weapon categories for skill replacement.
