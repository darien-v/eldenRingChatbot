\n\n Meat Peddler's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to the Twin Maiden Husks for new items access \n\nMeat Peddler's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Meat Peddler's Bell Bearing can be used to offer to the Twin Maiden Husks for new items access. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story. \n\nBell bearing of an unfortunate meat peddler who was slain by the Bell Bearing Hunter.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to the following items:\n- Sliver of Meat - Lump of Flesh - Turtle Neck Meat \n\nWhere to find Meat Peddler's Bell Bearing in Elden Ring\n\nDropped by the Bell Bearing Hunter at Church of Vows.
