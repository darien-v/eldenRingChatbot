\n\n Mausoleum Knight \n\nLocation\nBellum HighwayConsecrated SnowfieldDeeproot Depths\n\nDrops\n 406 - 4056 RunesMausoleum Knight SetKnight's GreatswordPartisanGreatbowEclipse Crest Greatshield\n\nMausoleum Knight is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. These headless knights are capable of vanishing and teleporting short distances to attack their foes. They are easily recognized due to being headless and the unique Eclipse Crest Greatshield they have in their left hand. They can also be found riding headless mounts.\nThe spirit of a Mausoleum Knight can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Lhutel the Headless Spirit Ashes.\n\n A self-inflicted curse ties the spirits of these loyal knights to the land, having willingly beheaded themselves so that they may serve their masters in death.\n\nElden Ring Mausoleum Knight Locations\nWhere to Find Mausoleum Knight:\n\nOne can be found patrolling around the Walking Mausoleum northeast of the Raya Lucaria Academy.\nOne can be found in the Bellum Highway, standing outside the Black Knife Catacombs.\nSeveral can be found wandering the lands of the Consecrated Snowfield.\nThey can be found in large quantities roaming the ruins in Deeproot Depths. They may stop spawning once the Walking Mausoleum is toppled.\n\nElden Ring Mausoleum Knight Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 406 - 4056\n\nMausoleum Knight Armor\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nMausoleum Knight Gauntlets\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nMausoleum Knight Greaves\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nKnight's Greatsword\n4.00%\nGreatsword Variant.\n\nPartisan\n4.00%\nPartisan Variant.\n\nGreatbow\n4.00%\nGreatsword & Greatbow Variant.\n\nEclipse Crest Greatshield\n4.00%\nGreatsword Variant and some Partisan Variants.\n\n5x Great Arrow\n15.00%\nGreatsword & Greatbow Variant.\n\nMausoleum Knight Notes & Tips\n\nThe Mausoleum Knight Set does not include a helmet as the knights are headless.\nThe horses that are being ridden are also headless.\nThe missing head of the Mausoleum Knights is not just a model change- they completely lack a hitbox there, making them immune to headshots from projectile weapons. The projectile will fly right through the area where their head should be.\nTheir weapons are permanently enchanted with Death Blight and can inflict Death Blight buildup.\nThey seem to be immune to rot, along with their Soldier counterparts. (Upon further testing, also immune to bleed, possibly among other status effects).\nAll Mausoleum Knights are capable of teleportation, which can be highly disorienting for inexperienced players. If a Mausoleum Knight is particularly passive and is choosing to constantly teleport instead of actually attacking, it may be prudent to break your lock-on to get your bearings and re-orient yourself
