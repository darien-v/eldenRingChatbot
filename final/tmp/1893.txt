QUESTION
Why are the Prophet Trousers described as having a fabric that scrapes against the wearer's skin like a file, and why do they feature a rickety cart wheel around the neck as a warning symbol?

ANSWER
The Prophet Trousers are described that way to symbolize the exiled prophets' suffering and their warning against listening to their sermons.
