QUESTION
What are the defensive properties and effects of the Prophet Blindfold helm in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Prophet Blindfold helm provides Damage Negation against physical, strike, slash, and pierce attacks, high Magic, Fire, Ligt, Holy resistances, Immunity, Robustness, Focus, Vitality, and low Poise.
