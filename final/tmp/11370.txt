QUESTION
How do you obtain the Crafting Kit in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Crafting Kit in Elden Ring can be obtained by purchasing it from Merchant Kalé for 300 Runes in Limgrave, Church of Elleh, or from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Kale's Bell Bearing or defeating two Shardbearers.
