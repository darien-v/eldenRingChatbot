QUESTION
Why is the Rotten Crystal Staff a good weapon for those looking to inflict the Rot status effect in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Rotten Crystal Staff is a good weapon for inflicting the Rot status effect in Elden Ring because it enhances Crystalian sorceries, causes Scarlet Rot buildup on physical attacks, and increases Crystalian sorceries' damage by 10%.
