\n\n Ash of War: Stamp (Sweep) \n\nAffinity\nHeavy\n\nSkill\nStamp (Sweep)\n\nAsh of War: Stamp (Sweep) is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Stamp (Sweep) provides Heavy affinity and the Stamp (Sweep) Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Heavy affinity and the following skill:\n"Stamp (Sweep): Brace armament and step into a low stance that prevents recoil from most enemy attacks. Follow up with a strong attack for a sweeping strike."\nUsable on swords, axes, and hammers (small and medium swords excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Stamp (Sweep) Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Stamp (Sweep):\n\nDropped by an invisible Teardrop Scarab located at the beaches of Western Limgrave. From where the Giant Land Octopus and Land Octopus enemies are, check the east side of the shore and you'll see some pillars on the beach. You'll notice multiple glowing prints moving around the pillars. Attack it at the right time just when it's about to approach you to reveal the scarab. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nAsh of War: Stamp (Sweep) Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Stamp (Sweep).\nUsage: Press (L2) then (R2) \n\n(L2) initiates a blocking stance that absorbs one hit\n(R2) performs the Sweep attack, which hits twice\n\nProvides the Heavy affinity, which increases Strength scaling, decreases all other scaling types.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Greatswords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes,
