\n\n Leyndell Knight \n\nLocation\nLiurnia of The LakesAltus PlateauMt GelmirLeyndell, Royal Capital\n\nDrops\n 620 - 1310 RunesLeyndell Knight SetKnight's GreatswordPartisanGreatbowGolden GreatshieldGreat Arrow\n\nLeyndell Knights are a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Leyndell Knights are knights of Leyndell, the Royal Capital. They wield a heavy sword in their right arm and has a large shield on their left. They are entirely covered by golden armor and are highly dangerous due to their fine combat skills.\nThe spirit of a particular Leyndell Knight, Kristoff, can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Ancient Dragon Knight Kristoff Ashes.\n\nKnights sworn to defend the royal capital of Leyndell. The golden canopy atop their helmets represent the honor of standing among the tree's defenders. \n\nElden Ring Leyndell Knight Locations\nLeyndell Knights can be found at the following locations:\n\nOne knight can be found south of Artist's Shack in Liurnia of The Lakes\nAltus Plateau, on the road between the Grand Lift of Dectus and the western gate of the Capital Outskirts\nTwo knights can be found inside the Wyndham Catacombs dungeon\nLeyndell Royal Capital\n\nElden Ring Leyndell Knight Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 620 - 1310\n\nLeyndell Knight Helm\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nLeyndell Knight Armor\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nLeyndell Knight Gauntlets\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nLeyndell Knight Greaves\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nKnight's Greatsword\n4.00%\nOnly from knights using the Greatsword, including those with a Greatbow.\n\nPartisan\n4.00%\nOnly from knights using the weapon.\n\nGreatbow\n4.00%\nOnly from Knights using the weapon.\n\nGolden Greatshield\n4.00%\nOnly from knights using the Greatshield who don't also wield a bow.\n\n5x Great Arrow\n15.00%\nN/A\n\nDragon Cult Prayerbook\n100%\nOne time drop from the knight south of the Artist's Shack\n\nGravel Stone Seal\n100%\n From the West Capital Rampart site of grace in Leyndell, walk north to the next room with the single guard inside. The knight with the spear supported by an archer drops this seal.[Elden Ring Map here]. \n\nLeyndell Knight Notes & Tips\n\nLeyndell Knights wielding Partisans the typical spear-wielding knight moveset, with the addition of the Ash of War: Thunderbolt skill, which calls down a bolt of lightning (can be fired in rapid succession).\nLeyndell Knights wielding Knight's Greatswords have the typical sword-wielding knight moveset, with the addition of the Ash of War: Lightning Slash skill, which entails a harsh downward swing that calls down a bolt of lightning. While the swing itself has surprisingly limited reach, the bolt of lightning has impressive tracking and is capable of striking a player that is quite far away from the knight. \nA few Leyndell Knights wielding Knight's Greatswords close to the entrance to Leyndell, Royal Capital are also capable of using the Ash of War: Determination skill, drastically buffing the physical and stamina damage dealt by their next attack. While the buff is active, they gain a rushing sweep attack that is difficult to dodge, due to its large hitbox and peculiar timing.\nAll Leyndell Knights in Altus Plateau are capable of using the Rejection incantation, which they often do to knock away overly aggressive players.\nThe Frenzied Leyndell Knights south of the entrance of the Volcano Cave can use The Flame of Frenzy and the Howl of Shabriri Incantations.\n\nLeyndell Knight Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone Cluster  ♦  Albinauric  ♦  Albinauric Wolfback Archer  ♦  Anastasia
