Consort's Set is a three piece light cloth set worn by Lady Tanith of Volcano Manor. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Consort's Set in Elden Ring\n\nAfter defeating Rykard, returning to the Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy site of grace the player will find Lady Tanith relocated next to the head of her former lord. The player can attack and kill her, summoning her Crucible Knight body guard to fight. Upon defeating him, the Consort's Set can be looted from Lady Tanith's corpse. QUESTION
Why is Lady Tanith's Consort's Set armor lootable only after defeating her bodyguard, the Crucible Knight?

ANSWER
Lady Tanith's Consort's Set armor is lootable only after defeating her bodyguard, the Crucible Knight to add an extra challenge and ensure players fight a tough opponent before obtaining the armor set.
