\n\n Scarlet Aeonia \n\nSpell Type\nServants of Rot Incantations\n\nFP Cost 48\nSlots Used 3\n\nEffect \n\n        Creates a giant flower that explodes with scarlet rot. \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 35 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nScarlet Aeonia is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Scarlet Aeonia spell creates a giant flower that explodes with scarlet rot. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nTechnique of Malenia, the Goddess of Rot.\nCreates a gigantic flower that blooms into an explosion of scarlet rot.\nEach time the scarlet flower blooms, Malenia's rot advances. It has bloomed twice already. With the third bloom, she will become a true goddess.\n\nWhere to find Scarlet Aeonia\nWhere to find Scarlet Aeonia:\n\nTrade Remembrance of the Rot Goddess with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Scarlet Aeonia Guide\n\nServants of Rot Incantation\nStamina Cost: 50\nDeals standard physical damage and 13 stance damage, also knocks down enemies\nBuildup is 150 on initial explosion and 50 per tick in lingering area of effect\nScarlet Rot inflicted by this spell deals (0.33% Max HP) + 13 damage per second for 90 seconds, for a total of 29.7% Max HP + 1170 damage over its full duration.  \n\nScarlet Rot on weapons inflicts (0.18% Max HP) + 15 per second for 90 seconds, for a total of 16.2% Max HP + 1350 damage over its full duration.\n\nNote: Multiple buffs in Patch 1.07 - FP cost reduced from 56 to 48, attack power increased and poise damage and stamina damage to guarding enemies both increased. The range of the attack on landing was increased and recovery time increased. Poise during activation was also increased. Interestingly, although not stated in the patch notes, the stamina cost for this spell was also decreased, from 66 to 50.\n\nElden Ring Scarlet Aeonia Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
