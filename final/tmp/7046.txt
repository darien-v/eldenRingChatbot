CONTEXT
\n\n Knight of Zamor \n\nLocation\nZamor Ruins\n\nDrops\n 5039 Runes\n\nKnight of Zamor is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Knights of Zamor are the reanimated corpses of an ancient order of warriors from the Zamor Ruins, high above in the Mountaintops of the Giants. They are the non-boss variant of the Ancient Hero of Zamor and behave mostly identically.\n\n These long-lived warriors, clad in biting, freezing winds, are said to have been the mortal enemies of the Fire Giants since time immemorial. \n\nElden Ring Knight of Zamor Locations\nKnights of Zamor can be found in the following location(s):\n\nZamor Ruins in the Mountaintops of the Giants. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Knight of Zamor Drops\n\n 5039 Runes\n\nKnight of Zamor Notes & Tips\n\nFor DEX builds:They dodge arrows easily, so melee is recommended. It seems horseback hit and run tactics are the best when dealing with them, but their freeze attacks can stun you, and their jump attack can come from a disorienting angle when locked on. Their freeze attacks come in two forms: circular and cone aoe. The circular is used to stun opponents to leave them open to attack while the cone aoe is used for attack, but the cone aoe goes on for a while which leaves them open for a few hits if you can get QUESTION
What tactics are recommended for dealing with Knight of Zamor enemies in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
For DEX builds, melee combat is recommended as they dodge arrows easily, and horseback hit-and-run tactics work well, but watch out for their freeze attacks and jump attacks from disorienting angles, with their freeze attacks coming in circular and cone aoe forms.
