\n\n Great Jar Warrior \n\n__image__\n\nLocation\nCaelid Colosseum\n\nDrops\n(varies) runes\n\nGreat-Jar Warrior is a Duelist NPC in Elden Ring. He is a warrior that serves as a test for those wanting to receive the talisman from The Great-Jar\n\nDescription goes here\n\nElden Ring Great-Jar Warrior Locations\nWhere to Find Great Jar Warrior:\n\nNear etrance to Caelid Colosseum. After talking to a The Great-Jar NPC there, 3 signs with Duelists will appear. Map link\n\nElden Ring Great-Jar Warrior Drops\n\nEach drops runes, their amount depends on Duelst. \nAfter defeating 3 Duelists without reseting the arena, speak with The Great-Jar NPC to receive Great-Jar's Arsenal\n\nGreat-Jar Warrior Notes & Tips\n\nWhen online, Great-Jar Warriors build are randomised each time player. When offline, their build is set: \n\nMage with a long thrusting sword. Damage: Pierce,  Magic\nWarrior (Dexterity build) with a fast sword and buckler. Damage: Slash, Inflicts: Poison. May also try to parry Tarnished\nStrength build using Great Mace infused with Prayerful Strike Ash of War and tower shield. Damage: Strike, Holy\n\nParryable: It depends (online), Yes (offline)\nSuspectible to a critical hit after parry\nVulnerable to a backstab critical\nAround arena there is a golem, which may pose a threat. Eliminate him or reset the arena after reaching sign to de-aggro him.  \n\nGolem near The Great-Jar will always drop refill to Flask, therefore de-aggro him is a better thing to do. \n\nTarnished-type enemy, therefore is always suspectible to Madness and resistant to Sleep (stagger only). QUESTION
What are the different build types and corresponding weaknesses of the Great-Jar Warrior NPC in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Mage with a long thrusting sword – Damage: Pierce, Magic, Warrior (Dexterity build) with a fast sword and buckler – Damage: Slash, Inflicts: Poison, Strength build using Great Mace infused with Prayerful Strike Ash of War and tower shield – Damage: Strike, Holy.
