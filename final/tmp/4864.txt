\n\n The Stormhawk King \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Ashes of a hawk which heeds no summons. \n\nThe Stormhawk King is a Key Item in Elden Ring. The Stormhawk King is an ash of a hawk which heeds no summons. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nAshes of a hawk revered by all others as sovereign back in the days when Stormveil's winds still raged like no other.\nThis ancient monarch is proud however, refusing to answer anyone's summons. \n\nWhere to find The Stormhawk King in Elden Ring\n\nChapel of Anticipation: Use an Imbued Sword Key on the second imp statue of The Four Belfries next to the message "Precipice of Anticipation" to return to this area. From the Grafted Scion boss arena cross the bridge leading to the chapel you began the game in. Take the stairs up to the chapel then go through the doorway to the right of the chapel doors which was locked at the start of the game. This item is located at the top of the stairs directly across from the archway into the second floor of the chapel. [ Map Link]\n\nElden Ring The Stormhawk King Use\n\nThe Stormhawk King can be given to Nepheli Loux at the Roundtable Hold after exhausting dialogues with her and Gideon Ofnir. If the player never receives the potion and Seluvis is dead, they can still be given to Nepheli.\n\nElden Ring The Stormhawk King Notes & Tips\n\nThe Grafted Scion at the Chapel of Anticipation must
