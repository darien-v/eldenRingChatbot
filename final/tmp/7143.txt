\n\n Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon \n\nLocation\nAcademy of Raya Lucaria\n\nRole\nLast Carian Queen\n\nVoiced by\n Mali Harries\n\nRennala, Queen of the Full Moon is an NPC in Elden Ring. Rennala gives access to the Rebirth function, allowing the player to reallocate their stats by using a Larval Tear (also called respeccing). She can also be used to change the player's appearance.\nFor the boss form of this NPC, see Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon. Note that this boss must be fought before gaining access to this NPC. \n\nAh... Thou... Is it thy wish to be born anew? To become a sweeting, reborn of my beloved egg?\n\nRennala, Queen of the Full Moon Location in Elden Ring\nRennala, Queen of the Full Moon can be found in her boss room in Academy of Raya Lucaria immediately upon defeating her.\n\nRennala moves temporarily at the end of Sellen's quest.\nShe cannot be fought, as combat is disabled in her room after her fight.\n\nDetails related to Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon in Elden Ring\n\nRennala can be used to respec by exchanging a Larval Tear, which can be found in various places around the Lands Between.\nDuring Sorceress Sellen's questline, if Sellen is sided with, Rennala temporarily moves to the right side of the room behind a bookcase while Sellen stands in the center. She has additional dialogue if you speak to her at this point. However, once the questline is completed and Rennala moves back to her usual position, her dialogue will return to normal. You must speak to her before this if you want the additional dialogue. \n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon\nInitial encounter\n\nWhere did ye flee, my sweetings?Come out from whence ye hide. QUESTION
Why does Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon, offer the player the ability to reallocate their stats in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Rennala offers the player the ability to reallocate their stats in Elden Ring using a Larval Tear to allow them to be reborn anew and change their appearance.
