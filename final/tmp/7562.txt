CONTEXT
\nStraight Swords are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Straight Swords are reliable weapons that provide a balance between slashing and thrusting attacks, and they can be used to inflict great amount of damage on Enemies or Bosses. Straight Swords are some of the easiest Weapons to use in Elden Ring, and work well when combined with a Shield, allows for quick follow up attacks after Blocking.\nStraight Swords are compatible with nearly all Ashes of War, making them extremely versatile, and useable in many different Builds. If you're not sure which Weapon to use, check out the Longsword, and try out different Ashes of War with it to see what you may like.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, Straight Swords poise damage has been reduced only on PVP.\nNOTE: Patch 1.09, the running attack speed, first attack speed and speed of guard counter of straight sword has increased. QUESTION
Why are straight swords considered some of the easiest weapons to use in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Straight Swords are considered some of the easiest weapons to use in Elden Ring due to their reliable balance between slashing and thrusting attacks, quick follow-up capabilities after blocking, compatibility with nearly all Ashes of War for versatility in different builds, and effectiveness when combined with a shield.
