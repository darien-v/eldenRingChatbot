CONTEXT
\n\n Golden Tempering \n\n        Cross the two swords to grant their attacks holy essence. \n       While in effect, strong attack performs a dual-wielding combo attack. \n\nGolden Tempering is a Skill in Elden Ring. Golden Tempering allows the user to transform the strong attack into a combo attack, while the effect of Golden Tempering remains. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Golden Tempering in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill of the Ornamental Straight Sword.\n\nGolden Champion Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Golden Tempering Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 24\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nAll attacks of the Combo provided by this Skill can be Parried\n\nBuffs the weapon with 100 holy attack power\nAs of Patch 1.07, Golden Tempering now lasts for roughly 1 minute. \n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nA timing for interrupting the attack during a series of strong attacks while under the effect has been added.\nStrong attack motion speed, poise damage, and stamina attack power against guarding enemies during the effect have been increased.\nDuration has been increased.\nAttack power of the effect that grants the weapon a holy attribute have been increased from 85 to 100.\nTime between the skill activation and being able to perform actions has been reduced.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Golden Tempering Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
