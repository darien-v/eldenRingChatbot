\n\n Regal Roar \n\n        Let loose a mighty war cry, raising attack power, while sending out a shockwave that cannot be guarded against by stomping the ground. While active, strong attack becomes a lunging slash. \n\nRegal Roar is a Skill in Elden Ring. Regal Roar is a powerful roar that raises attack power and emits an unguardable shockwave. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nRegal Roar Buff Details in Elden Ring\n\nAdds 7.5% Physical Damage\nAdds +5 Strength scaling\nAltered Strong Attack\nLasts 40 seconds\n\nHow to get Regal Roar in Elden Ring\n\nUnique skill for Axe of Godfrey.\n\nElden Ring Regal Roar Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 25\nThe skill deals 21 Stance Damage.\n\nBuffed heavy attacks deal 21 Stance Damage. Second heavy attack deals 12 Stance Damage.\nBuffed charged attacks deal 30 Stance Damage. Second charged attack deals 18 Stance Damage.\n\nThe Skill itself is not Chargeable. However the buffed strong attacks can be charged, meaing they are affected by the Axe Talisman and not the Godfrey Icon.\nAffected by the Roar Medallion and Highland Axe passive effects.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nEffect duration has been increased.\nTime between strong attacks while under the effect has been reduced.\nTime between the skill activation and being able to perform actions has been reduced.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Lord Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Regal Roar Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why was Regal Roar altered in Patch 1.07 and what specific changes were made to the skill?

ANSWER
The Regal Roar was altered in Patch 1.07 to increase the effect duration, reduce the time between strong attacks, and decrease the time needed to perform actions after activation.
