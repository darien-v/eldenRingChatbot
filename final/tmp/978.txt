\n\n Corpse-Stench Shack \n\nA run-down shack amidst burning corpses past the site of a recent battle.\n\nCorpse-Stench Shack is a Location in Elden Ring. The Corpse-Stench Shack is a small run down shack found near the base of Mt. Gelmir. Players can reach this location by riding northwest from the Bridge of Iniquity site of grace past the wooden barricades. You can avoid all the enemies along the path by keeping to your left, by the mountain's edge. From the Bridge of Iniquity there is also a ladder lined with 2 torches. \n\nCorpse-Stench Shack Map\nThe Corpse-Stench Shack is located near the base of Mt. Gelmir, northwest of the Bridge of Iniquity.
