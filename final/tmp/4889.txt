Ancient Dragon Knight Kristoff Ashes are used to summon the spirit of Kristoff, wielding a greatshield and a spear imbued with lightning.\nLeyndell Knights can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. Spirit of Kristoff, an honorable knight of Leyndell who was also a devout worshipper of the ancient dragons. His skills strike down foes with thunderbolts, the dragons' weapon of choice. QUESTION
What is the significance of capturing Godefroy the Grafted to earn the hero's honor of Erdtree Burial according to the lore of Ancient Dragon Knight Kristoff Ashes in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To earn the hero's honor of Erdtree Burial, Kristoff captured Godefroy the Grafted after the First Defense of Leyndell, showcasing his valor and skill in Elden Ring lore.
