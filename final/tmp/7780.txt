QUESTION
Why is Bestial Vitality considered the most cost-efficient healing over time spell for a single player in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Bestial Vitality is considered the most cost-efficient due to its low cost, higher total healing amount than other low faith spells at the same faith level, and the fact that it regenerates 5 HP per second for 120 seconds, healing a total of 600 HP.
