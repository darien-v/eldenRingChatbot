\n\n Clayman Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 77\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons two clayman spirits \n\nClayman Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Clayman Ashes are used to summon the spirits of two claymen.\nClaymen can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Clayman.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of two claymen.  Bowed with age, these spirits are sluggish but hard to stagger. The warped remains of priests who searched for revelation in service of the ancient dynasty, they employ two sorceries that produce smaller and larger bubbles.\n\nWhere to find Clayman Ashes\nWhere to Find Clayman Ashes\n\nLocated on a corpse in Ainsel River, Lake of Liurnia which is south from the Uhl Palace Ruins. Easiest way to reach these is ashes is while doing Ranni's questline (the portal you enter from Renna's Rise will take you near the ashes). After entering Ainsel River proceed south along the tunnel entering into the next main room with enemies, proceed southeast while hugging the left edge until you reach the waterfall, it can be found on a corpse on the right side of the ledge. QUESTION
Why are Clayman Ashes needed to summon the spirits of two claymen in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Clayman Ashes are needed to summon the spirits of two claymen in Elden Ring because they contain the ashen remains in which the spirits reside, allowing players to bring forth the clayman spirits.
