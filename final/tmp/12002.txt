\n\n Colossal Fingercreeper \n\nLocation\nFlame Peak\n\nDrops\n 4627 Runes\n\nThe Colossal Fingercreeper is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. It is the largest variant of the Fingercreeper and is incredibly strong to compensate.\n\nElden Ring Colossal Fingercreeper Locations\nWhere to Find Colossal Fingercreeper:\n\nFlame Peak : There is one Colossal Fingercreeper hanging on a cliff right above a Giant Crow close to the Foot of the Forge site of grace, it will drop on the Giant Crow and instantly kill it if the player gets too close.\n\nElden Ring Colossal Fingercreeper Drops\n\n 4627 Runes\n\nColossal Fingercreeper Notes & Tips\n\nNot worth fighting, as it drops a rather meager amount of runes, and can stun-lock the player.
