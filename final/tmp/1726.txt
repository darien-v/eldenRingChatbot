Chain Set is a Lightweight armor that provides a good balance of defensive properties, with Slash being the strongest. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Chain Set in Elden Ring\nAll four pieces of this armor set may be purchased from Merchant Kalé, located at his camp by the Limgrave: Church of Elleh Site of Grace. The set costs 4500 Runes total (1500 for the chest armor and 1000 for the rest of the set). [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nChain Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nChain Coif\nChain Armor\nGauntlets\nChain Leggings\n\nChain Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Chain Set is the most easily available set of armor for a new character, being located very close to the start of the game and not too expensive to purchase, which makes it a good choice for starting classes who would otherwise lack early-game protection.\nThe Tree Surcoat and Eye Surcoat can be used as an alternative to the Chain Armor in the Chain Set.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
