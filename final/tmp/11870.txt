\n\n Monstrous Dog \n\nLocations\nCaelidDragonbarrowMountaintops of the Giants\n\nDrops\n 313 - 1808 RunesHefty Beast BoneBeast Blood\n\nMonstrous Dog is an Enemy in Elden Ring. Monstrous Dogs are massive dogs that have grown to be much larger than normal, with smaller forelegs in exchange for bigger and stronger hind legs. They often chase after anything, and are fast enough to catch up even on horseback.\n\nElden Ring Monstrous Dog Locations\nWhere to Find Monstrous Dog:\n\nFound everwhere in Caelid\nA unique variant always spawns outside of Gowry's Shack.\nA pair appear to be watching herds of hostile sheep at the Shack of the Rotting\nSome are near the Hermit Merchant’s shack in the Dragonbarrow.\nFound past the frozen lake in the Mountaintops.\n\nElden Ring Monstrous Dog Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 313 - 1808\n\nHefty Beast Bone\n8.00%\nN/A\n\nBeast Blood\n8.00%\nN/A\n\nMonstrous Dog Notes & Tips\n\nThis enemy will often lunge towards the player and attempt to bite/grab. The damage is very high, even for higher level players, but can mostly be avoided by sidestepping.\nThe one near Gowry is wearing a spiked collar and also has a unique move where it sprays a cloud of rot. It seems to act as a sort of guard dog.\nThere are white furred and red-eyed variants that howl upon noticing you. This will alert the other dogs to attack.\nThey can hit and even kill each other if they get too close.\nHas very low poise, can be stance broken with a single guard counter.\nDespite its large size, is very easy to block.\nSeems to take extra damage from most types of damage including standard, strike and fire.\nCan be poise-broken, which allows for a critical hit on its head.\nThey are highly susceptible to Sleep Pots.  \n\n1 Sleep Pot at Faith 22, Int 15 will put one Monstrous Dog to sleep.\n\nThe Beast-Repellent Torch prevents them from attacking the player until attacked, with exceptions: \n\nThe red-eyed variants in Caelid attack the player, regardless of the torch.\nThe regular and red-eyed variants in the Mountaintops of the Giants also ignore the pacifying effect.\n\nElden Ring Monstrous Dog Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone Cluster  ♦  Albinauric  ♦  Albinauric Wolfback Archer  ♦  Anastasia QUESTION
What are some strategies for dealing with Monstrous Dogs in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Sidestep to avoid bites, use Sleep Pots to put them to sleep, utilize Beast-Repellent Torch for pacification except for certain variants, exploit their low poise for single guard counter, target their head for critical hits.
