Defeating her will reward players with the Demi-Human Queen's Staff, which is excellent for beginner mages.\n\nDemi-Human Forest Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. As you reach the ruins, you'll encounter a couple of Demi-humans in the area, and in the middle of the ruins, a Demi-Human Queen is located. Defeating the Demi-Human Queen will drop the Demi-Human Queen's Staff as well as the Crystal Burst.\nThere's also a corpse in the middle of the area holding an Arteria Leaf. In the cellar area of the ruins, before the main building before you encounter the Demi-Human Queen mini boss, jump over a wall to access the stairs to the cellar and find the Shield of the Guilty.\nOutside north of the ruins by the cliff, there's an area where you'll see three Poison Flowers and an item in the middle.
