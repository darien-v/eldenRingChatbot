QUESTION
How can Night Shard be effectively used in combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Night Shard can be effectively used in combat in Elden Ring to swiftly fire semi-invisible magic projectiles without delay after performing another action, making it a powerful option against enemies who consistently dodge attacks.
