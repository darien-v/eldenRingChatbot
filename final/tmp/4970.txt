This Abandoned Merchant can be found inside one of the ruins in Central Siofra River. \n\nAbandoned Merchant Location in Elden Ring\nFound playing music by an unlit campfire in the ruins of Siofra River. The doorway to this area is high up and only accessible by climbing up and around the nearby wooden scaffolding. Take the wooden stairs at the eastern side of these ruins to start climbing the scaffolding. QUESTION
Why does Abandoned Merchant Siofra River express confusion and sadness upon being defeated or killed?

ANSWER
The Abandoned Merchant Siofra River expresses confusion and sadness upon being defeated or killed because of their surprise at the attack and the abrupt end to any potential interactions or transactions.
