CONTEXT
\nGreat Spears are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Using Great Spears allow players to deliver devastating blows from remarkable distances at the expense of attack speed and weight, as Great Spears are exceptionally heavy. Like Spears, Great Spears allow for attacking with a shield raised, letting users block and attack at the same time. These Weapons are also decent on horseback when compared to other melee Weapons, because of their extraordinarily long reach.\nOut of all Great Spears, only the Lance can be modified with many different Ashes of War, allowing you to change their Skills and Damage Type. QUESTION
What are the advantages and disadvantages of using Great Spears in Elden Ring compared to other melee Weapons?

ANSWER
Advantages: Devastating blows from remarkable distances, ability to attack with a shield raised, long reach on horseback.
Disadvantages: Slow attack speed, heavy weight, limited modification options.
