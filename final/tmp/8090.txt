QUESTION
How can one effectively utilize the Demi-Human Ashes Spirit Summon in combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
One can effectively utilize the Demi-Human Ashes Spirit Summon in combat in Elden Ring by summoning five small demi-human spirits, which have a violent disposition especially during nighttime, near a rebirth monument and controlling them strategically to take advantage of their abilities.
