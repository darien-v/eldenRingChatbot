Glintstone Scarab protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nBlue-green scarab worn directly on the head.These scarabs roll clumps of sorcery during their labors.\nSlightly reduces FP costs of sorceries, but increases damage taken.\n\nWhere to find Glintstone Scarab in Elden Ring\nThe Glintstone Scarab Helm can be found at:\n\n[Elden Ring Map here]\nLocation: Raya Lucaria Academy\nNearest Site of Grace: Raya Lucaria Grand Library\nFound inside a chest. From the garden section where you encountered the hostile NPC Moongrum Carian Knight, go through the gate to the South into the small balcony area with the Glintstone Sorcerer and Wandering Nobles, follow the eastern wall and jump over the balcony railing onto the small ledge outcropping. Follow the path into the nearby alcove, and then climb the ladder. The chest is inside the room at the top of the ladder, guarded by 3 Glintstone Sorcerers and a Lesser Mad Pumpkin Head.\nVideo Location\n\nGlintstone Scarab Set in Elden Ring\n\nGlintstone Scarab is
