CONTEXT
\nNomadic Merchants in Elden Ring refer to several NPC Merchants that can be found in multiple locations. Nomadic Merchants sell various items and equipment including Consumables, Materials, Weapons and Armor as well as notes containing hints on how to acquire certain items. Each Nomadic Merchant has a different stock and they are usually found near Sites of Grace or other easily accessed landmarks.
