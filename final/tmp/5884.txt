QUESTION
How can players acquire the Fire Monk Hood in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Pieces of the Fire Monk Set are dropped by Fire Monks found in camps in western Liurnia of the Lakes, around Fort Laiedd in Mt. Gelmir, and around Guardians' Garrison in the Mountaintops of the Giants. It can also be found 5 seconds east of Eastern Tableland site of grace in east Liurnia of the Lakes.
