Bandit Garb is part of the Bandit Armor Set, and a match for stealth and agility, but not so much for defense. Bandit Garb is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nLeather garb worn by bandits.\nLight for ease of movement, and supple for masking sound. In trade, they offer little damage negation. \n\nWhere to find Bandit Garb in Elden Ring\nThe Bandit Garb Chest Armor can be found at:\n\nStarting Equipment for the Bandit Class\nGatekeeper Gostoc QUESTION
Where can Bandit Garb be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Bandit Garb Chest Armor can be found by starting as the Bandit class, purchasing from Gatekeeper Gostoc at Stormveil Castle pre-Godrick's death, or from Twin Maiden Husks at Roundtable Hold post Gostoc's Bell Bearing offering in Elden Ring.
