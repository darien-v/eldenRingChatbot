\n\n Ancestral Follower Shaman \n\nLocation\nAncestral WoodsConsecrated Snowfield\n\nDrops\n 1049 - 4508 RunesShaman SetBudding HornNascent ButterflyThin Beast Bones\n\nAncestral Follower Shaman is an Enemy in Elden Ring. They can be heard singing throughout the Ancestral Woods in Siofra River. Their singing produces a glowing blue aura that grants a buff to nearby Ancestral Follower allies. When they are confronted alone they will stop singing and attack the player with an Ancestral Infant's Head and melee with their hand.\n\nThe ancestral followers live a distance from the Erdtree, awaiting new buds. They are certain to sprout from their very flesh, and indeed, their souls.\n\nElden Ring Ancestral Follower Shaman Locations\nWhere to Find Ancestral Follower Shaman:\n\nAncestral Woods, which is
