CONTEXT
\n\n Whetstone Knife \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Can add new battle arts and affinities to weapons \n\nWhetstone Knife is a Whetblade in Elden Ring. The Whetstone Knife can add new battle arts and affinities to weapons. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by related NPCs, which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nWhetstone with a cipher inscription, made to look like a small knifeblade.\nAllows the use of ashes of war at a site of grace to bestow new battle arts and affinities to armaments as skills.\nThe battle arts and affinities will depend on the ashes of war used.\n\nWhetstone Knife Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Whetstone Knife:\n\nFound in the southeastern area of Gatefront Ruins. There is a set of stairs leading to a small room underground where a treasure chest can be found. Inside the chest is the Whetstone Knife and Ash of War: Storm Stomp. [Map link]\nCan also be purchased from the Twin Maiden Husks for  500 Runes after beating two Shardbearers.\n\nElden Ring Whetstone Knife Use\n\nPlayers can use the Whetstone Knife to add Skills to Weapons.\nUsers need to be at a Site of Grace to use the Whetstone Knife.\n\nElden Ring Whetstone Knife Notes & Tips\n\nNotes & Tips go here\n\nElden Ring Whetblades\n\nBlack Whetblade  ♦  Glintstone Whetblade  ♦  Iron Whetblade  ♦  Red-Hot Whetblade  ♦  Sanctified Whetblade\n\n

QUESTION
How can players acquire the Whetstone Knife in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can acquire the Whetstone Knife in Elden Ring by finding it in the southeastern area of Gatefront Ruins or purchasing it from the Twin Maiden Husks for 500 Runes after beating two Shardbearers.
