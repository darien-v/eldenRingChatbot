\n\n Noble Sorcerer Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 11\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons noble sorcerer spirit. \n\nNoble Sorcerer Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Noble Sorcerer Ashes summons a Sorcerer that can move and use spells to attack enemies from a distance.\nThe summoned Noble Sorcerer walks very slow, and doesn't attack very frequently. Only serves as a temporary distraction, but don't count on it for defeating Enemies. Since bosses aggro on the last player/npc who damages it, and the Noble Sorcerer attacks from a distance, this summon can be used with a magic build to continuously change the bosse's aggro while it approaches either the player or NPC.\nNoble Sorcerers can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Wandering Nobles.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirit of a noble sorcerer.Spirit of a nobleman who once asked to be given a place at Raya Lucaria to learn glintstone sorceries.His talents were insufficient to be worthy of donning the stone crown, however, and he is only capable of using the most rudimentary sorcery.\n\nWhere to find Noble Sorcerer Ashes in Elden Ring\nThe Noble Sorcerer Ashes can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by the Erdtree Burial Watchdog, at Stormfoot Catacombs. QUESTION
Why is the Noble Sorcerer Ashes summon in Elden Ring only capable of using rudimentary sorcery despite once aspiring to learn glintstone sorceries at Raya Lucaria?

ANSWER
The Noble Sorcerer Ashes summon in Elden Ring is only capable of using rudimentary sorcery because his talents were insufficient to be worthy of donning the stone crown.
