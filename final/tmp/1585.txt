Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Lazuli Sorcerer Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Lazuli Glintstone Crown can be found from killing one of the crystal crabs in Raya Lucaria Academy.
