\n\n Tarnished Golden Sunflower \n\nEffect \n\n        Material used for crafting items \n\nObtained \n\n        Found near Minor Erdtrees \n\nTarnished Golden Sunflower is a Crafting Material in Elden Ring. Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. Tarnished Golden Sunflower was previously named "Tarnished Blossom" during the network test.\n\nA large flower that blooms facing the Erdtree.Material used for crafting items.Found near Minor Erdtrees.\nThough wilted and faded, it still retains holy essence.\n\nWhere to find Tarnished Golden Sunflower in Elden Ring\nTarnished Golden Sunflower found in the overworld respawns after resting at a Site of Grace\nIN THE WORLD\n\n3 in Castle Morne: From outside the room with the caged ghost, jump from the broken walls of the fortress where the enemies are, to the roof below. Jump again to the stones below, and circle the structure from which roof you just jumped from.
