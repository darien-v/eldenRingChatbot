QUESTION
Why would players choose to use the Spiritflame Arrow in Elden Ring over other types of arrows available in the game?

ANSWER
Players would choose to use the Spiritflame Arrow in Elden Ring over other types because it inflicts additional magic damage, causes frost buildup, and pierces enemies' armor.
