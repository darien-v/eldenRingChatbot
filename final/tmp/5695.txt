QUESTION
Why is the Magma Whip Candlestick considered a good weapon for producing fire-based attacks?

ANSWER
The Magma Whip Candlestick is considered a good weapon for producing fire-based attacks because it scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity, and Faith and has fire attribute scaling.
