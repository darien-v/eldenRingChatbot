\n\n Glintstone Stars \n\nSpell Type\nGlintstone Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 12\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Fires three shooting stars that pursue foes \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 12 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nGlintstone Stars is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. The Glintstone Stars spell produces three magic projectiles to attack enemies at range. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the glintstone sorceries of the Academy of Raya Lucaria.\nFires three magic shooting stars that pursue the target.This sorcery can be cast while in motion. Charging enhancespotency.\nA sorcery of the Olivinus Conspectus, which attracts sorcerers from Sellia, Town of Sorcery.\n\nGlintstone Stars Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Glintstone Stars:\n\nCan be purchased from Sorceress Sellen for  3000 Runes.\nCan be purchased from Gowry for  3000 Runes after completing his quest line.  \n\nElden Ring Glintstone Stars Guide\n\nGlintstone Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 21\nDeals magic damage\nDamage can be boosted by 10% by wearing Lusat's Glintstone Crown. This comes at the cost of a 15% increase in ALL sorcery costs\nGlintstone Stars, like many other multi-projectile spells, suffers in damage output in the early/mid game, due to the way absorption and defenses work. The higher level and scaling you reach, the better results you will get out of this spell\nGlintstone Stars has extremely good tracking, even at long ranges. In addition, there is a slight delay between the cast time and when the projectiles begin moving. \n\nThis makes Glintstone Stars excellent against enemies that typically dodge spells, like NPC phantoms\nThe good tracking also makes it an excellent choice in mounted combat, allowing you hit fast moving targets\n\nEffectively a lower-cost version of Star Shower.  Star Shower fires more projectiles, but the projectiles themselves behave identically.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 14 to 12, and attack power has been increased.\n\nBest Glintstone Stars Buffs\nUse these Buffs to maximize the performance of Glintstone Stars. QUESTION
How can Glintstone Stars be effectively utilized in Elden Ring combat scenarios?

ANSWER
