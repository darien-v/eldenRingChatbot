QUESTION
Why is the Soldier's Crossbow considered a good weapon for those who prefer ranged combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Soldier's Crossbow is considered a good weapon for ranged combat in Elden Ring due to its Crit 100, Rng 42, and being suitable for anyone without stat scaling.
