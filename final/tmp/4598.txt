\n\n Ash of War: Eruption \n\nAffinity\nFire\n\nSkill\nEruption\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Eruption is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Eruption provides Fire affinity and the Eruption Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Fire affinity and the following skill:\n"Eruption: Skill of the knights who serve at Volcano Manor. Slam armament into the ground, spawning roiling lava which spouts up upon release."\nUsable on large and colossal swords, axes, and hammers.\n\nAsh of War: Eruption Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Eruption:\n\nSold by Knight Bernahl in Volcano Manor for 8000 .
