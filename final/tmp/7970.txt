\n\n Miranda Sprout Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 0\nHP Cost 185\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons five miranda sprouts \n\nMiranda Sprout Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Miranda Sprout Ashes summons five Miranda Sprouts using HP instead of FP.\nMiranda Sprouts can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Miranda Sprout and Giant Miranda Sprout.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of five miranda sprouts. Summoning consumes HP rather than FP.  Spirits of sweet little miranda flowers, carnivorous blossoms that feed upon human flesh. They creep slowly, scattering toxic pollen. Vulnerable to fire—do take care of the little darlings.\n\nWhere to find Miranda Sprout Ashes\nWhere to Find Miranda Sprout Ashes\n\nCan be found behind an illusionary wall in the Caelid Catacombs, Caelid. Proceed through the Catacombs as normal from the site of grace, once you've reached the first open room, there will be a staircase for you to descend on your left, proceed down and immediately turn around to find a little open room under the stairs that you just came down. Attack the staircase wall to the right to reveal the ashes on a corpse.
