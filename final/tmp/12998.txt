Aspects of the Crucible: Horns Creates a mighty horn on the caster's shoulder to gore foes from a low stance. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the ancient Erdtree incantations.\nCreates a mighty horn on the caster's shoulder to gore foes from a low stance.Charging allows the caster to barrel into foes before delivering the final attack.\nThis is a manifestation of the Erdtree's primal vital energies - an aspect of the primordial crucible, where all life was once blended together.\n\nWhere to find Aspects of the Crucible: Horns\nWhere to find Aspects of the Crucible: Horns:\n\nDropped by Crucible Knight found in Stormveil Castle directly below the Ramparts. [Map Link] Once you reach the walkway where the three Warhawks are located, head to the first left turn you see. It should be a dead end. Once you reach this dead end jump down, and keep heading down until you find a collapsible cliff. The Crucible Knight should be waiting for you by a ruined archway. [Map Link] Video Location\nFor players having difficulty defeating the Crucible Knight, there is a way to cheese him by using the elevator in the building behind the archway he comes from. Wait for the knight to pass by and sneak behind him towards the elevator. Step on the pressure plate and then quickly hop off. Go back to the knight and lure him towards the elevator. Walk around the shaft opening until he falls in.\n\nElden Ring Aspects of the Crucible: Horns Guide\n\nCharging increases damage and range. Will also send smaller enemies flying instead of staggering them.\nAn excellent option for close range focused Faith builds (or Faith/Str builds), allowing you to quickly close the distance with foes and knocking them down, allowing an easy follow up\nDeals standard physical damage.\nStance Damage: 17 (uncharged), 30 (charged)\nRecieves a 3.5% damage bonus for each worn piece of the Crucible Axe Set and Crucible Tree Set.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 20 to 18, and stamina attack power against guarded enemies has been increased.\n\nCrucible Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Aspects of the Crucible: Horns Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
