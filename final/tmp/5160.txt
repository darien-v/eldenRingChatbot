CONTEXT
\nBell Bearings  in Elden Ring are Key Items which, when turned in to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold, expand their shop with new items. Though often collected after the death of NPCs, these bearings are occasionally dropped by Bosses or found throughout the Lands Between. This page covers their names, items they unlock, and Locations.\nAs of Patch 1.05, the following Bell Bearings' items are kept by the Twin Maiden Husks in New Game+. All other Bell Bearings are not kept in New Game+.\n\nBone Peddler's\nMeat QUESTION
Why are Bell Bearings often collected after the death of NPCs in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Bell Bearings in Elden Ring are often collected after the death of NPCs because they are occasionally dropped by Bosses or found throughout the Lands Between, expanding the shop with new items when turned in to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold.
