They are still guardians of the Minor Erdtrees, despite rotting from within. A few can also be found guarding Elphael from intruders.\n\n Guardians of the Minor Erdtrees, infested with Scarlet Rot. Their large staff can knock foes away, and their slam attack releases a cloud of rot, quickly leading to enemies’ demise.\n\nWhere to find Putrid Avatar in Elden Ring\n\nMinor Erdtree (Caelid) [More Info] [Map Link]\nMinor Erdtree (Dragonbarrow) [More Info] [Map Link]\nMinor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield) [More Info] [Map Link]\n2 in Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree [More Info] [Map Link]\n\nPutrid Avatar Combat information\n\nStance: 120\nParryable: No\nIs vulnerable to a  critical hit  after being stance broken\nDamage: Standard, Strike, Holy\nInflicts: Scarlet Rot\nExtremely weak to Fire damage. Godslayer Incantations are particularly effective, since their base damage is based on the enemy's max HP\nSee Erdtree Avatar for a full boss guide\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nStandard: 10\nSlash: 10\nStrike: 0\nPierce: 10\n\nMagic: 20\nFire: -100\nLightning: 20\nHoly: 40\n\nThe negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nPutrid Avatar Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nPutrid Avatar shares most of its attacks and counters with Erdtree Avatar\n\nRotted Slam\nJumps in the air and slams the ground with its whole body, releasing a cloud of Scarlet Rot in front of it\nMostly dodged the same as Erdtree Avatar's Erdtree Slam, but make sure you end up behind it, since the Rot only goes in front\n\nPutrid Avatar Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nNotes and tips go here\n\n Putrid Avatar  Minor Erdtree (Caelid)  \n\n__image__\n\nDrops\n9,600Greenburst Crystal TearFlame-Shrouding Cracked Tear\n\nHP\n5,671\n\nElden Ring Putrid Avatar Boss (Caelid)\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Minor Erdtree Catacombs\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nPutrid Avatar Location\nThe first time you approach it, head down the hill to the east of the the Smoldering Church Site of Grace, run past the Avatar to open the doors to the Minor Erdtree Catacombs, and activate its Site of Grace. QUESTION
How do you defeat the Putrid Avatar in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Wait for an opening after stance broken to land a critical hit, exploit extreme weakness to fire, and use Godslayer Incantations for maximum damage.
