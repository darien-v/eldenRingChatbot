QUESTION
Why does the Black Dumpling Helm boost ALL damage by 10% for 60 seconds after suffering Madness, instead of just attack power?

ANSWER
The Black Dumpling Helm boosts ALL damage by 10% for 60 seconds after suffering Madness, not just attack power, due to the intentional game design decision to provide a broader damage enhancement.
