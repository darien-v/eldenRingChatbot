QUESTION
Why is the Frustrated Spirit unable to open the door to the Colosseum despite its strong desire to fight as a warrior?

ANSWER
The Frustrated Spirit is unable to open the door to the Colosseum in Elden Ring because the Colosseum gates were already open as of Patch 1.08, indicating that there was no need for it to battle anymore.
