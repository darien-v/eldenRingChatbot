Blaidd's Set is comprised of the three pieces of armor he can be seen wearing, as well as a replica mask of his head that can be found separate from the other three pieces. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Blaidd's Set in Elden Ring\nThe three main pieces of Blaidd's Set, Blaidd's Armor, Blaidd's Gauntlets, and Blaidd's Greaves, can be found by following Ranni's questline. After she is given the Dark Moon Ring, return to Ranni's Rise and you will hear Blaidd talking to himself in front of the tower. When he finishes his dialogue he will turn hostile, and defeating him will grant the player Blaidd's Armor, Blaidd's Gauntlets, and Blaidd's Greaves (as well as the Royal Greatsword). [Elden Ring Map here]\nThe helmet,  Black Wolf Mask, can be found separately nearby to where the first three pieces are obtained. The mask can be found on a  corpse on the wall behind Seluvis's Rise. It can be reached by running and jumping up the crumbling part of the wall near the entrance to the tower. [Elden Ring Map here]\nVideo Location\n\nBlaidd's Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nBlack Wolf Mask\nBlaidd's Armor\nBlaidd's Gauntlets\nBlaidd's Greaves\n\nBlaidd's Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Blaidd's Armor can be altered to the Blaidd's Armor (Altered) to remove the cloak.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
