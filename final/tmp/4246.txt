CONTEXT
\nBows are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Bows are ranged weapons that can be used to inflict damage on hostile characters from afar. They grant no guard protection, encouraging users to maintain distance from advancing enemies. Bows require Arrows as ammunition.\nBows can be given new Ashes of War.\nBows cannot be greased, spell-buffed, or given new affinities.\n See also: Light Bows | Greatbows\nUsing Bows in Elden Ring\nBows have a different control scheme from melee weapons. You can only fire a bow if you're two-handing it.
