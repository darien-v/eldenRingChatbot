The Limgrave region is the first accessible area of the game. Players, will emerge from one of the underground dwellings, and be treated to Limgrave's lush environments.\n\nLimgrave is a lush, expansive section of the Tenebrae Demesne. Golden trees and tall grass and bushes provide plenty of sustenance for the local wildlife, that features boars, sheep, goat and rodents in addition to flying creatures such as eagles and owls. More sinister and aggressive wildlife also exists, and those venturing forth should be prepared to combat them.\n\nLimgrave Map & Map Fragments in Elden Ring\n\nFragment West Limgrave: Map (Limgrave, West) is found at a pillar in Gatefront Ruins, just south from the Gatefront Site of Grace.
