\n\n Fire Monk \n\nLocation\nLiurnia of the LakesFort LaieddMountaintops of the Giants\n\nDrops\n 205 - 1076 RunesFire Monk SetMonk's FlamemaceMonk's FlamebladeSmoldering ButterflyFire Blossom\n\nFire Monk is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Fire Monks gather at night in rituals and processions burning their way through Liurnia of the Lakes, accompanied by their Flame Chariots. They settle in folksy camps surrounded by their Thorn Sorcerers followers.\nThe spirit of a Fire Monk can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Fire Monk Ashes.\n\n The Fire Monks are guardians of the flame of ruin, forbidden by the Erdtree and said to be kept high in the Mountaintops of the Giants.\nThe monks came to the land of Liurnia in pursuit of a fugitive who stole their fire.\n\nElden Ring Fire Monk Locations\nWhere to Find Fire Monks:\n\n5 seconds east of Eastern Tableland site of grace in east Liurnia of the Lakes.\nLiurnia of the Lakes, southeast of the Church of Vows, as well as east of the Village of the Albinaurics, closest to the Lakeside Crystal Cave Site of Grace.\nFort Laiedd, west of the Seethewater Terminus Site of Grace.\nGiant-Conquering Hero's Grave\nGuardians' Garrison.\n\nElden Ring Fire Monk Drops\nOnly Fire Monks in Mountaintops of the Giants can drop Monk's Flameblade.\n[Map Link] At the camp South of Church of Vows:\n\nYou can find the Flame, Cleanse Me Incantation within a corpse facing the main campfire.\nYou can loot a Smoldering Butterfly off the dead body of one of the Thorn-Fire Sorcerers you just killed.\nFire Spur Me Gesture in the main campfire.\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 205 -
