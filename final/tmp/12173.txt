The Flame of Frenzy spell is cast to conjure multiple offensive flames that travel forward.\n\nIncantation originating from the maddening Three Fingers.\nCauses the yellow flame of frenzy to burst forth from the caster's eyes.Charging increases the range of the burst.\nThe flame of frenzy deals damage and causes buildup of madness. This incantation also causes buildup of madness in the caster, and is only effective against Tarnished.\n\nThe Flame of Frenzy Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find The Flame of Frenzy:\n\nCallu Baptismal Church in the Weeping Peninsula. Found on a corpse by a pillar on the left side of the church. See it on the Elden Ring Interactive Map Link.\n\nElden Ring The Flame of Frenzy Guide\n\nFrenzied Flame Incantation\nStamina Cost: 30\nLow Cost Incantation (16 Focus Points per cast)\nUses only 1 Memory Slot\nRequires Low Faith (16)\nDeals Fire Damage\nDeals 5 Stance damage per uncharged flame and 9 per charged flame, for a total of 20 when uncharged and 56 when charged if all flames hit.\nInflicts Madness buildup on both the caster (4 per flame) and targets (21 normal/28 charged per hit) \n\nNote that all but a handful of enemies in PVE are immune to Madness.\n\nWhen Madness bar is filled, the player will lose FP and take health damage.\nCast Speed is fast\nCan not be chain-casted.\nCharging up the skill will create more flames.\nWhile charging up the spell, the spread of the flame can be manually adjusted by locking off and moving the camera. By aiming the camera upward while charging, one can scatter the flames to a wider radius, and by aiming the camera downward while charging, the flames become more concentrated in a close QUESTION
How can the spread of the flame in The Flame of Frenzy spell be adjusted while charging?

ANSWER
By aiming the camera upward while charging, one can scatter the flames to a wider radius, and by aiming the camera downward while charging, the flames become more concentrated in a close range.
