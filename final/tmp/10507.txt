The Hand Ballista does not scale with any stat attributes but requires 30 Strength and 14 Dexterity to wield.  It is a good Weapon for ranged combat to break an enemies stance for more effective upcoming attacks targeted towards this recipient of this weapon's attacks. \n\nAn unconventional ballistic device modeled on a weapon used to besiege castles.
