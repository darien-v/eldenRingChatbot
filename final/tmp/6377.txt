\n\n Eruption \n\n        Skill of the knights who serve at Volcano Manor. Slam armament into the ground, spawning roiling lava which spouts up upon release. \n\nEruption is a Skill in Elden Ring. Eruption is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, this skill can be used on large colossal Swords, Axes, and Hammers. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Eruption in Elden Ring\n\nSold by Knight Bernahl in Volcano Manor for 8000 . [Elden Ring Map Link].\n\nElden Ring Eruption Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Eruption.\nFP Cost: 14 on use.\nDeals 22 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nBoth of this Skill's attacks can be Parried\nUpon activation, the user slams the armament to the ground in front, creating a small patch of lava at the point of impact that lingers for a few seconds.\nDamage wise, Flaming Strike deals significantly more damage even when taking into account the lava pool damage.\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nRange and duration of lava have been increased.\nHitbox to the part of the attack where the weapon is slammed has been added.\nTiming of the poise increase during activation was fixed.\n\nEruption
