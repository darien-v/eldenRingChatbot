CONTEXT
\n\n Square Off \n\n        This Skill starts with a horizontal sword stance. \n       Slash up through enemy guards with a normal attack, or run and \n       thrust with a strong attack. \n\nSquare Off is a Skill in Elden Ring. Square Off is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible weapons.\n\nHow to get Square Off in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill of Broadsword, Cane Sword, Longsword, Lordsworn's Straight Sword, Noble's Slender Sword, and Weathered Straight Sword.\nAsh of War: Square Off is dropped by a Teardrop Scarab found rolling around at the edge a the cliff in Siofra River. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Square Off Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Square Off.\nLight Attack FP Cost: 6\nHeavy Attack FP Cost: 8\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill's Light and Heavy attacks can be Parried\nUpon activation, the user will hold their armament (weapon) in a horizontal sword stance.  \n\nThis does not cost FP\nThe user must keep holding the Weapon Skill button to mantain this stance.\nStamina regeneration is reduced by approximately 80% while this stance is active.\n\nInputting a light attack while in the stance will cause your character to do a upward swing in front of them. \n\nThis attack inflicts a large amount of stance damage (30), and heavily damages stamina to enemies that are blocking, usually breaking their guard. \n\nThe attack is effective at making blocking enemies susceptible to guard breaks. The player can capitalize on this with different talisman loadouts if they wish.\n\nInputting a heavy attack while in the stance will cause your character to do a running forward thrust in a single direction. \n\nThis attack deals a considerable amount of damage, almost equivalent to two heavy attacks while two handing from the same weapon. \n\nBe wary of the windup and tracking, as it can be hard to land to enemies extremely close to you. The player does not stop moving until the actual thrust is made.\n\nThe attack also heavily damages stance(40), making stance breaking enemies extremely easy and effective.\nAs it counts as a thrust attack, this attack benefits from the Spear Talisman.\nThis can also catch people rolling away after a riposte if timed well in PVP.\n\nVideos & Builds for Square Off in Elden Ring\n\nSquare Off Why should I consider using the Square Off skill in Elden Ring instead of other available skills?

ANSWER
Square Off skill in Elden Ring is effective for breaking enemy guards, dealing high damage, benefiting from Spear Talisman, and catching opponents rolling away after a riposte in PvP.
