QUESTION
How can players obtain the Flamedrake Talisman in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can obtain the Flamedrake Talisman in Elden Ring by either having it dropped by the Beastman of Farum Azula in Dragonbarrow Cave in eastern Dragonbarrow or finding the +1 Variation at Leyndell Royal Capital, by heading up the elevator leading towards the Grand Lift of Rold and locating it on the ground against a column.
