\n\n Flame, Fall Upon Them \n\nSpell Type\nFire Giant Incantations\n\nFP Cost 16\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Hurls several balls of fire at once \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 28 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nFlame, Fall Upon Them is an Incantation in Elden Ring. The Flame, Fall Upon Them spell launches multiple balls of fire. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the incantations that draws directly from the power of theFire Giants.\nHurls several balls of fire at once.\nCharging enhances potency and causes the balls offire to explode.There is no record of this incantation in common prayerbooks.Only a select few prelates know of its existence.\n\nWhere to find Flame, Fall Upon Them\nWhere to find Flame, Fall Upon Them:\n\nPurchase from Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows after giving them the Giant's Prayerbook, for 10,000 Runes.\n\nElden Ring Flame, Fall Upon Them Guide\n\nFire Giant Incantation\nStamina Cost: 40\nDeals fire damage\nHurls 12 fireballs.\nEach fireball deals damage separately, and hitting with multiple fireballs will significantly increase overall damage.  There is a limit to this however; throwing too close to an enemy will cause the fireball hit before it has a chance to split, dealing only the damage of one fireball.  The ideal case is to use it in midrange combat where 1-3 fireballs are sure to hit the mark.   \n\nThis becomes exponentially more effective when faced with larger enemies where you might be able to hit most or all of the projectiles on a single target\n\nDamage of a single fireball is fairly significant, and can be used as a quick ranged AOE option to deal with groups of enemies, or as an alternative ranged option, given its affordable FP cost and fairly quick cast time. \nThe spread of the projectiles often means you can hit dodging enemies - a useful tool against NPC invaders\nNotes: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost reduced from 18 to 16 and this spell now does bonus stamina damage to guarding enemies.\n\nFlame Dancer Written Guide\n\nPyromancer Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Flame, Fall Upon Them Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
