\n\n Flightless Bird Painting \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Reminiscence of the painting "Flightless Bird" \n       Examine using the corresponding button \n\nFlightless Bird Painting is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advice, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies and Bosses, or purchased from NPCs.\n\nWork of a wandering artist.Reminiscence of a painting titled "Flightless Bird."\nThis painter is said to have captured the landscapes seen during the last moments of those welcomed into death's embrace.\nThe soul of the painter, and vestiges of the dead's last moments can be discovered by visiting the location depicted even now.\n\nWhere to find Flightless Bird Painting in Elden Ring\n\nIn the Fortified Manor at Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Map Link]\nFound on the lower level, in the same room as the Site of Grace.\nNote: It is possible to miss this painting if the player kills Maliketh, the Black Blade, causing the Leyndell, Ashen Capital instance to appear.\n\nElden Ring Flightless Bird Painting Tutorial Guide\nTravel to Windmill Heights and head south-by-southeast from there. You will find the painter on a cliff left of the shack, at the end of the road.\n\nAfter the spirit disappears, you will receive the Fire's Deadly Sin Incantation.\n\nElden Ring Flightless Bird Painting Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 Flightless Bird Painting.\nYou cannot store Flightless Bird Painting.\nThis item cannot be sold.\n\nElden Ring Info Items\n\nAbout Adding
