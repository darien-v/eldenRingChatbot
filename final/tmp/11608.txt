\n\n Living Jar \n\nLocation\nMultiple Locations\n\nDrops\n 50 - 518 Runes (Small) 353 - 1831 Runes (Large)Living Jar ShardRaw Meat Dumpling (Large)\n\nLiving Jar is an enemy in Elden Ring. They are animate jars that often hide out of place, usually only attacking when provoked or approached.\nThe spirit of (3) Living Jars can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Soldjars of Fortune Ashes.\n\n Though the jars are brought to life by human flesh and blood, they are all rather kindly folk. Perhaps they were made to be better than their innards.\nTheir fragments command a high price due to the magical power locked within. This leaves the living jars unfortunate targets for poachers.\n\nElden Ring Living Jar Locations\nWhere to Find Living Jar:\n\nStormveil Castle\nRaya Lucaria Academy\nCaria Manor\nGiants' Mountaintop Catacombs\nAuriza Side Tomb\nJarburg (non-hostile Jars are found here)\nSubterranean Shunning-Grounds\n\nElden Ring Living Jar Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n\nSmall Living Jar:  50 - 518\nLarge Living Jar:  353 - 1831\n\nLiving Jar Shard\n100%\nLarge Living Jars\n\nRaw Meat Dumpling\n100%\nLarge Living Jars\n\nLiving Jar Shard\n20.00%\nChance of a second Living Jar Shard drop (Large Living Jars only).\n\nLiving Jar Shard\n1.00% ~ 5.00%\nSmall Living Jar\n\nLiving Jar Shard*\n10.00%\nLarge Living Jar.*This variant is only found at the Raya Lucaria Academy. 90.00% of the times won't drop any items.\n\nLiving Jar Notes & Tips\n\nThese creatures (small variation) can be summoned with the Soldjars of Fortune Ashes.\nA few of the creatures are capable of speech such as Alexander and Jar Bairn.\nDeals Standard damage.\nWeak against Strike damage.\nParryable: No\nCan be poise broken, but not riposted afterwards.\nIt is most likely that the insides of a Living Jar are made up of human body parts, mostly from warriors, seeking to become one.\nThe ones in the Mountaintops Catacombs can bolster themselves, making them sturdier and causing weapons bounce off, similar to an Ironjar Aromatic. QUESTION
Why are Living Jars considered unfortunate targets for poachers even though they are rather kindly folk?

ANSWER
Living Jars are unfortunate targets for poachers due to the high price their fragments command, stemming from the magical power locked within.
