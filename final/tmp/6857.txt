\n\n Amber Starlight \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Ingredient used in special draught \n\nAmber Starlight is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Amber Starlight is used in Preceptor Seluvis's questline after he mentions his scheme to you. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nAn ephemeral sliver that gives off a pale amber glow.What remains of a passing flash of starlight.\nIf the stars command our fates, then amber-hued stars must command the fates of the gods. Such is the belief that inspired the use of these shards to prepare a most special draught.\nCannot be consumed by mere humans.\n\nWhere to find Amber Starlight in Elden Ring\n\nAmber Starlight can be found closest to the Altus Highway Junction Site of Grace in the Altus Plateau region. Directly east from the Altus Highway Junction Site of Grace, the road will break off north into an open cave, in the cave the Amber Starlight will be sitting below a statue surrounded by several Land Octopuses. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Amber Starlight Use\nAmber Starlight can be used in Prospector Seluvis's questline after purchasing a puppet spirit from him. He will fill you in on a scheme he has been planning, but requires Amber Starlight to begin this scheme. \n\nElden Ring Amber Starlight Notes & Tips\n\nThe amber starlight is located in front of a
