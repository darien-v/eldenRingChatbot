All-Knowing Helm protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nHelm set with countless eyes and ears. At the end that should not be. \n\nAll-Knowing Helm Location in Elden Ring\nThe entire All-Knowing Set is dropped by Sir Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing upon defeat. Sir Gideon is encountered as a boss in Leyndell, Ashen Capital, an alternate version of the Royal Capital accessible only after making significant progress in the story.
