It is a larger version of the Land Squirt, and spews out a poisonous mist or scarlet rot when threatened, depending on its location.\n\nElden Ring Giant Land Squirt Locations\nWhere to Find Giant Land Squirt:\n\nLiurnia of the Lakes: Along the Ravine leading north out of Liurnia Lake to the Ravine-Veiled Village.\nSwamp of Aeonia: There are several that spew scarlet rot instead of poison mist along the beaches of the swamp.\nGiants' Mountaintop Catacombs: deep in the Catacombs, there are two hallways with Land Squirts in them. The second hallway has a Giant Land Squirt in the end.\n\nElden Ring Giant Land Squirt Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 367 - 1678\n\nGolden Rune
