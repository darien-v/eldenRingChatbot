CONTEXT
\nHermit Merchants in Elden Ring refer to several NPC Merchants that can be found in multiple locations. Hermit Merchants sell various items and equipment including Consumables, Materials, Weapons and Armor as well as notes containing hints for the player. Each Hermit Merchant has a different stock and they can be found in or around a Hermit Merchant's Shack, complete with their own Sites of Grace. QUESTION
Why are Hermit Merchants in Elden Ring important for players to locate and interact with?

ANSWER
Hermit Merchants in Elden Ring are important for players to locate and interact with because they sell various items and equipment, provide hints for the player through notes, and dropping a Bell Bearing upon death, allowing players to restock at the Roundtable Hold.
