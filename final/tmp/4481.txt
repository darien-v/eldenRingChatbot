\n\n Ash of War: Sacred Ring of Light \n\nAffinity\nSacred\n\nSkill\nSacred Ring of Light\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Sacred Ring of Light is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Sacred Ring of Light provides Sacred affinity and the Sacred Ring of Light Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Sacred affinity and the following skill:\n"Sacred Ring of Light: Skill used by the commanders of the Cleanrot Knights. Gather a sacred ring of light in the armament, and then fire it forwards. Can be fired in rapid succession."\nUsable on polearms (great spears excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Sacred Ring of Light Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Sacred Ring of Light:\n\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab found in the Aeonian Swamps in Caelid, east from the Heart of Aeonia site of grace.
