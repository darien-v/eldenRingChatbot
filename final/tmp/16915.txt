The Albinauric Staff scales primarily with Intelligence and Arcane, with minor scaling in Strength, and is a potent catalyst Weapon for Arcane-heavy characters willing to incorporate Sorceries into their toolkit.\n\nShort staff with a blue glintstone embedded. Wielded by the albinaurics of old.\nThe albinaurics harbor a secret; they cast sorcery with their innate arcaneness.\n\nWhere to Find Albinauric Staff in Elden Ring\nThe Albinauric Staff weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nFound in Volcano Manor near the Guest Hall grace. Head left from the grace, then left again, taking the stairs to the next floor. Head outside and take the ladder to your left onto the roof. Head south to the back of the roof and jump off the west side onto a ledge. The Albinauric Staff, along with the Albinauric Mask, is inside a room with an Omenkiller.
