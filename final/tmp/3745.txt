\n\n Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker \n\n        Slam both hands onto the ground to violently shake the earth and unleash a shockwave. Follow up with an additional input to slam the ground again. \n\nHoarah Loux's Earthshaker is a Skill in Elden Ring. Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War. It can also be applied to all melee armaments.\n\nHow to get Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker in Elden Ring\n\nThe Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker grants the Heavy affinity and Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker skill where slamming both hands to the ground will unleash a shockwave, following up with another input will enable you to slam the ground again.\nAsh of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker can be obtained by trading the Remembrance of Hoarah Loux with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker.\nFP Cost: 16 per use, has a follow up. \n\nThis totals to a 32 FP cost.\n\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nUpon activation, the user raises their arms into the air, shouting briefly before slamming them into the ground, producing a large shockwave around them. \n\nUsing the Skill button again allows the user to follow up, slamming their arms into the ground once more, this time with reduced area of effect on the shockwave.\n\nThe shout can stagger minor enemies if they're near the player.\nDeals high Stance damage on both attacks. \n\n15 stance damage on the initial shockwave, 20 on the slam, and 20 on the follow up (either slam or shockwave)\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nThe shout does not stagger.\nThe ground slam does not produce a shockwave\nThe follow up slam does not produce a shockwave either.\n\nEach shout tick and at least the initial slam triggers the following: \n\nBloodflame Blade's bleed debuff effect\nGodskin Swaddling Cloth\n\nSeemingly full 3 shout ticks and slam on individual enemy triggers this effect.\n\nRotten Winged Sword Insignia, \n\nA full 3 shout ticks and slam on single mob seems to buff you the potency of Shard of Alexander, and double that used on two mobs\n\nWinged Sword Insignia,\nand Millicent's+Prosthesis\nAdditional Notes: \n\ncurrently only tested using Crescent Moon Axe\n\ntick requirements may vary per weapon\nMay not work on all weapons\n\nCan't confirm if effects proc on second slam or not.\nA stagger is not required for effects to proc.\n\nHoarah Loux's QUESTION
What are the effects of using Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker skill without FP in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The shout does not stagger, the ground slam does not produce a shockwave, the follow-up slam does not produce a shockwave.
