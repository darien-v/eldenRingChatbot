\n\n Armorer's Cookbook [5] \n\nEffect \n\n        Expands crafting repertoire \n\nRequired for\nImmunizing Cured MeatImmunizing White Cured Meat\n\nArmorer's Cookbook [5] is a Cookbook in Elden Ring. Cookbooks are records of crafting techniques that players can use to learn how to craft a wide variety of items.\n\nA record of crafting techniques left by an armorer who served the great general Radahn.Contains knowledge of dealing with rot; the application of fire in particular.\nAcquire the knowledge to craft the following:\n- Immunizing Cured Meat - Immunizing White Cured Meat \n\nArmorer's Cookbook [5] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Armorer's Cookbook [5]\n\nCan be found in Redmane Castle. Through a door in the southwest, past a room with an assassin enemy is an outdoors area with a ladder and a door to the right. The cookbook is in the room to the right. Please note that the doors in Redmane Castle open only after defeating the Misbegotten Warrior & Crucible Knight bosses.
