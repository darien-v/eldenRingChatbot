Rainbow Stone is an Consumable item which can be used for marking a place for other players for a short time.\n\nRuin Fragment that has undergone some simple processing. Craftable item.\nStones such as these shine with the colors of the rainbow, making them useful markers when placed on the ground.\nCan also be dropped to gauge the distance of a fall. The higher the pitch of the sound, the higher the likelihood of the fall being fatal. Once used to entertain children on the fringes of the Lands Between.\n\nRainbow Stone Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Rainbow Stone:\n\nAt Academy Gate Town, can be found at the top of the structure. \nFound on a corpse among some sacks and wooden objects in Weeping Peninsula.
