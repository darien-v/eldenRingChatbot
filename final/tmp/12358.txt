Greyoll's Roar spell conjures a thundering shriek of Greyoll that debuffs enemies, temporarily decreasing their defenses and attack power. Updated to Patch 1.10.\n\nSuperior incantation of Dragon Communion.Channels the power of Greyoll, the Elder Dragon.An incantation of legendary status.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to emit a terrible roar, reducing the attack power and defense of nearby enemies.\nGreyoll was the mother of all dragons, dwarfing all who stood before her like a looming mountain.\n\nGreyoll's Roar Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Greyoll's Roar:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 3 Dragon Hearts. Players need to defeat Elder Dragon Greyoll to unlock this Incantation. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Greyoll's Roar Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 72\nDeals standard physical damage.\n17 stance damage.\nApplies hard knockback if the opponent's stance is broken. \nThe full cast takes on average 4 seconds, with the roar being registered around the 2.0-second mark.\nEmits a 360° AoE roar that deals significant damage and also applies a debuff.\nThis is a Power Shred Debuff and will not stack with other debuffs of the same type. \n\nThe debuff decreases the enemy's attack by 15% and defense by 10% for 60 seconds.\nThe debuff visual effect of this spell will mark invisible enemies, similar to Shriek of Milos weapon skill.\nSee Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking debuffs.\n\nNote: FP cost significantly reduced as of Patch 1.07, from 62 to 50.\n As of Patch 1.09, Roar Medallion increases the damage of this incantation by approx 10% in Player vs. Environment and 4% in Player vs. Player.\nAs of Patch 1.09, Greyoll's Roar decreases the enemy's attack by 15% instead of the previous 20%.\n\nDragon Dancer Build\n\nElden Ring Greyoll's Roar Note and Tips\n\nUpdated to Patch 1.10. See Patch Notes for details.\nIf your opponent gets hard-knocked in front of you, use Greyoll's Roar. The range of the attack and active frames are long enough to make it almost impossible for the opponent to get out of range of the attack when they recover.\nUse Greyoll's Roar as a follow-up attack with Stone of Gurranq, when your opponent is point-blank. If you hit your enemy point-blank with Stone of Gurranq, it will true-combo into itself. Afterward, immediately follow up with Greyoll's Roar as the hit stun from Stone of Gurranq makes it almost impossible to dodge.\nThe high cost, slow speed, and short duration make this spell best on builds specializing in stacking buffs before quickly bursting the boss down.\nIt's also useful for stealthy ambushes against foes (PvE and PvP), especially since it hits them through walls, and can knock most enemies off ledges for an instant kill.\nDue to latency, the AoE roar will sometimes do no damage to the enemy, regardless if the opponent dodges. \nThe Power Shred Debuff can sometimes QUESTION
Why is Greyoll's Roar considered a superior incantation in Dragon Communion in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Greyoll's Roar is considered a superior incantation in Dragon Communion due to its ability to transform the caster into a dragon to emit a powerful AoE roar, reducing enemy attack power and defense significantly, along with its legendary status and connection to the Elder Dragon Greyoll.
