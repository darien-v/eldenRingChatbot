\n\n The Two Fingers \n\nLocation\nRoundtable Hold\n\nRole\nMessenger of The Greater Will\n\nThe Two Fingers are an NPC in Elden Ring. They are large, shriveled hands with only two fingers that are said to deliver messages of The Greater Will that only the finger readers can interpret.\n\nLet the words of the fingers guide you, Tarnished.\n\nThe Two Fingers Location in Elden Ring\nThe Two Fingers can be found atop the Divine Towers and the basement of the Cathedral of Manus Celes, however these versions are all seemingly dead with the only living version being found in the Roundtable Hold.\n\nThis NPC does not move\nThis NPC can't be fought\nThis NPC can not be killed\n\nElden Ring The Two Fingers Notes &
