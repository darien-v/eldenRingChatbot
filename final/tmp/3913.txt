\n\n Bloodblade Dance \n\n        Leap at foe to perform a flurry of tornado-like attacks. \n       Follow up with an additional input to perform an attack that ends in an evasive maneuver. \n\nBloodblade Dance is a Skill in Elden Ring. Bloodblade Dance is a unique skill that can be used on Eleonora's Poleblade. It allows the player to perform a flurry of tornado-like attacks while leaping into the air. Following up with an attack will perform an attack that ends in an evasive maneuver, perfect for getting that last hit before the enemy strikes again. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Bloodblade Dance in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on Eleonora's Poleblade.\n\nElden Ring Bloodblade Dance Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 11\nDeals 45 stance damage.\nAnimation has significant resistance to staggering. \nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Damage detection immediately after activating the skill has been increased.\n\nBuilds with Bloodblade Dance\n\nBlood Dancer Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Bloodblade Dance Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
