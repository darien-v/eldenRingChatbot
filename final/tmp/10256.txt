CONTEXT
\nFire Monk Incantations are a group of Incantations in Elden Ring that share a specific type of buff criteria. Fire Monk Incantations revolve around drawing power from the Fire Monks teachings, regarding Fire and are one of the two Fire-based Incantations available for players, the other being Fire Giant Incantations. Fire Monk Incantations require Faith in order to be casted.\nThere are a total of 9 Fire Monk Incantations available for players. Fire Monk Incantations can be found throughout the Lands Between.\n\nBestial\n\nBlood\n\nDragon Communion\n\nDragon Cult\n\nErdtree\n\nFire Giant\n\nFire Monks\n\nFrenzied Flame\n\nGodskin Apostles\n\nGolden Order\n\nServants of Rot\n\nTwo Fingers\n\nFire Monk Incantations info\n\nObtaining Fire Monk Incantations\n\nAfter giving the Fire Monks' Prayerbook to Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows,  players unlock both O, Flame! and Surge, O Flame! this way.\nFire Monks' Prayerbook can be found on a corpse in a Fire Monk outpost in the southern landmass, in Liurnia of the Lakes. [Elden Ring Interactive Map Link]\n\nFire Monk Incantations Requirements\n\nAs is the case with all Incantations, you must have a Sacred Seal equipped in order to be able to cast Fire Monk Incantations.\nFire Monk Incantations require Faith to use.\n\nBoosting Fire Monk Incantations\n\nDespite belonging to different "schools", Fire Monk Incantations are buffed by the Giant's Seal (+20%), though this buff does not apply to Flame Sling or Catch Flame.\nDamage-dealing Fire Monk Incantations all deal fire damage and receive damage buffs from the Fire Scorpion Charm (12%) and the Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear (20%).\n\nAll Fire Monk Incantations\n\nFlame Sling\n\nFlame, Cleanse Me\n\nFlame, Grant Me Strength\n\nFlame, Protect Me\n\nCatch Flame\n\nO, Flame!\n\nSurge, O Flame!\n\nWhirl, O Flame!\n\nFire's Deadly Sin\n\n

QUESTION
Why do Fire Monk Incantations require Faith in order to be casted?

ANSWER
Fire Monk Incantations require Faith in order to be casted because they draw power from the Fire Monks' teachings regarding Fire.
