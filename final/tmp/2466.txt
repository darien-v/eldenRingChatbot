\n\n Radagon Icon \n\nWeight\n0.7\n\nEffect \n\n Shortens the casting time of  \n       Spells\n\nRadagon Icon is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Radagon Icon shortens the casting time of Spells. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA legendary talisman depicting the Elden Lord Radagon.Shortens the casting time of sorceries and incantations.\nAs the husband of Rennala of Caria, the red-haired Radagon studied sorcery, and as the husband of Queen Marika, he studied incantations. Thus did the hero aspire to be complete.\n\nRadagon Icon Effect in Elden Ring\nRadagon Icon shortens the casting time of Spells.\n\nGives 30 virtual Dexterity, exclusively towards spellcasting.\n\nWhere to find Radagon Icon in Elden Ring\nIn Raya Lucaria Academy, the talisman is found inside a treasure chest on the second floor near the Debate Parlor Site of Grace. From the Site of Grace, head north into the courtyard.\n\nMake two hard rights, hugging the building's wall, and then jump over the fence -- you should see a ladder in front of you.\n\nClimb up the ladder and head through the broken glass window.\n\nThe treasure chest is in the north section.
