QUESTION
Why does the Rotten Crystal Sword in Elden Ring scale primarily with strength and intelligence rather than dexterity?

ANSWER
The Rotten Crystal Sword in Elden Ring scales primarily with strength and intelligence rather than dexterity because it is fashioned from pure crystal, enwreathed with powerful magic, causing its attack to scale with intelligence.
