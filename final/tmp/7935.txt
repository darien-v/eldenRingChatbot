Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nBracers reserved for the bandlands' bravest. Proof that the wearer has slaughtered countless foes. Following the example of their chieftain Hoarah Loux, the brave warriors of the badlands shun excess adornment. \n\nWhere to find Champion Bracers in Elden Ring\nThe Champion Bracers Armor Gauntlets piece can be found at the following location:\n\nStarting Equipment for the Hero Class\nCan be purchased from the Nomadic Merchant in south Caelid for  1,000 Runes.
