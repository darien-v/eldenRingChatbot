The corresponding map is tilted 90 degrees clockwise. On it, the locations of all three mirages are shown:\n\nThe first is immediately to the right of the Imp Statue.\nThe second is south-south-west in an alcove.\nThe third is further east  near the broken bridge, hidden underneath an illusory rock with a feint glow that disappears when attacked.\n\nTouching all three will cause the tower to appear. When looking for the last crest, shown closest to the Bower of Bounty Site of Grace, just below the ruined bridge on the map,  there will be a boulder that will radiate a slight glow. QUESTION
How can someone locate and interact with all three Phantom Crests in the Mirage Rise location in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Touch the first crest to the right of the Imp Statue, find the second crest in an alcove to the south-southwest, and hit a glow that appears near a boulder below the broken bridge to reveal the last crest.
