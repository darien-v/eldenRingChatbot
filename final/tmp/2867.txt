QUESTION
Why is the Cleanrot Set in Elden Ring considered to have a strong balance between immunity and robustness to withstand damaging ailments?

ANSWER
The Cleanrot Set in Elden Ring is considered to have a strong balance between immunity and robustness to withstand damaging ailments due to its high immunity and robustness resistance, combined with good physical and elemental damage absorption.
