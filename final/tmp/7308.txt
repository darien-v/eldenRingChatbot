\n\n Swarm of Flies \n\nSpell Type\nBlood Incantations\n\nFP Cost 14\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Releases a swarm of bloodflies before the caster \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 11 \n\nArcane 16 \n\nSwarm of Flies is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Swarm of Flies spell releases a swarm of frenzied bloodflies that seek their target and inflict Hemorrhage. \n\nA Blood Oath incantation, shared from the Lord of Blood's power. Releases a swarm of bloodflies before the caster to assault foes. The vile swarm deals damage and builds up onset of blood loss. This incantation can be cast while in motion. The new palace of the Lord of Blood lies in a swamp of festering blood, and these flies are said to spawn from excrement in that land. \n\nWhere to find Swarm of Flies\nWhere to find Swarm of Flies:\n\nFound on a corpse along the east wall (in a shallow cave) of the Mohgwyn Palace blood marsh, directly north from the Palace Approach Ledge-Road Site of Grace. QUESTION
Why should players use Swarm of Flies as a zoning tool in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players should use Swarm of Flies as a zoning tool in Elden Ring to force opponents to either run away or toward them and to reset frostbite or ignite certain enemies.
