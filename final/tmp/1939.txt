QUESTION
How can one effectively utilize the Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear in Elden Ring during PvP encounters?

ANSWER
Mix Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear in the Flask of Wondrous Physick, resulting in a 12.5% boost to lightning attacks during PvP in Elden Ring.
