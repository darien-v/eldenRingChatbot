\n\n Shabriri Grape \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Yellow, oozing eyeball of the infirm \n\nShabriri Grape is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nA yellowing, oozing eyeball of the infirm. The surface is shriveled, and the inside is squishy, not unlike a large, overly-ripe grape.\nGive to the blind maiden to guide her to the distant light.\n\nWhere to find Shabriri Grape in Elden Ring\n\nOne can be found near a spirit NPC at Godrick's throne room at Stormveil Castle. Using the broken bridge route to enter Stormveil Castle will allow the player to obtain the Shabriri Grape without defeating Godrick. [Map Link]\nOne can be found on the lower level of the Purified Ruins. The entrance to this area is blocked by a large plank of wood that can be destroyed with rolls or attacks. [Map Link]\nOne is dropped by Edgar the Revenger, an NPC invader found at the Revenger's Shack in western Liurnia. [Map Link]\n\nHe automatically gives you a +8 WEAPON when defeated, which can affect and potentially ruin your matchmaking, so if you play multiplayer, check the Summon Range Calculator page for the details of what it affects.\nThis grape can be obtained earlier by killing Edgar at Castle Morne, if the player returns to where he was killed after reloading the area. [Map Link]\nIf you kill Edgar while he's at Irina's body following completion of the quest, reload the area and return to his location to retrieve his grape.\n\nElden Ring Shabriri Grape Use\n\nShabriri Grape can be used to progress Hyetta's NPC questline. Hyetta roams The Lands Between in search of the distant light, drawn to her destiny by a guiding shine she feels in the back of her eyes when eating Shabriri Grapes. See Hyetta's page for details.\n\nElden Ring Shabriri Grape Notes & Tips\n\nShabriri is the demon of blindness in Jewish lore. His name in Hebrew translates to mean "fragile"\nIt seems that one who is meant to become a finger maiden for The Three Fingers, such as Hyetta, must consume a number of Shabriri Grapes, which guide them to their ultimate destination.\n\nElden Ring Key Items\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell Bearing  ♦  Academy Glintstone Key  ♦  Academy Scroll  ♦  Alexander's Innards  ♦  Amber Draught  ♦  Amber QUESTION
Where can Shabriri Grape be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
One can be found near a spirit NPC at Godrick's throne room at Stormveil Castle, on the lower level of the Purified Ruins, dropped by Edgar the Revenger at the Revenger's Shack in Liurnia, or by killing Edgar at Castle Morne.
