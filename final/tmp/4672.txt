The Rotten Crystal Spear scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence and is a good Weapon for medium range attacks and inflicting magic damage. \n\nSpear fashioned from pure crystal; a deed impossible for a human.It festers with scarlet rot.Enwreathed with powerful magic, its attack scales with intelligence.\n\nWhere to Find Rotten Crystal Spear in Elden Ring\nThe Rotten Crystal Spear weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nCan be dropped by Lesser Crystalian enemies located within Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree\n\n2 of them can be found in the room just next to the Elphael Inner Wall site of grace. \n\nA consistent strategy is to lure one of them out with any throwable item, projectile weapon or spell, then use a striking-type weapon of your choice to fish for backstabs, followed with a charged heavy attack as they rise from the ground. After several critical or heavy blows the Lesser Crystalian will crack and become easy to stunlock with regular attacks.\n\nBest way to farm is to go to the one at the Elphael Inner Wall, use the Fallingstar Beast Jaw +9 Weapon Art and kill it without passing over the tree root and aggroing the spearmen. QUESTION
How can one effectively farm for the Rotten Crystal Spear in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
A consistent strategy is to lure a Lesser Crystalian out with a throwable item, then fish for backstabs and use charged heavy attacks in Elphael. The best spot is near the Elphael Inner Wall, using the Fallingstar Beast Jaw +9 Weapon Art.
