Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Fingerprint Set in Elden Ring\nThe Fingerprint Set can be obtained by defeating Roundtable Knight Vyke, who can be found at the Lord Contender's Evergaol, which can be found in the Mountaintops of the Giants just southwest on top of a cliff next to the ice lake in the northeast of the region.  The Evergaol can be reached by taking a Spiritspring right next to a graveyard up a small hill past the Giant Crayfish. QUESTION
Why is the Fingerprint Set in Elden Ring associated with Vyke and what significance does the connection have to the lore of the game?

ANSWER
The Fingerprint Set in Elden Ring is associated with Vyke, hinting at his Knight of the Roundtable past and involvement with Shabriri, adding depth to the lore by revealing his corruption and previous alliances.
