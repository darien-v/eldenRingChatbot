\n\n Glintstone Dart \n\n        Skill that builds from the Glintstone Pebble sorcery. \n       Charge the skill to upgrade it to Glintstone Dart. \n       Follow up with a strong attack to chain this skill into a lunging thrust. \n\nGlintstone Dart is a Skill in Elden Ring. Glintstone Dart is a unique skill to the Glintstone Kris dagger, it allows the player to cast Glintstone Pebble and Glintstone Dart as well as a follow-up thrust attack. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Glintstone Dart in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on the Glintstone Kris Dagger.\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dart Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 10\nThe initial projectile of the skill is Chargeable. However, the follow-up thrust attack is not.\nThis Skill's second attack can be Parried (the Glinstone projectile can be Parried with Carian Retaliation) \n\nThis Skill's first attack can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nWearing Spellblade Set increases the damage.\nThe initial projectile deals pure Magic Damage and only scales with Intelligence.\nThe follow-up thrust attack deals both Physical Damage and Magic Damage and scales with Intelligence and Dexterity (and Strength to a much lesser extent).\nSince Patch 1.06, the range of Glintstone Dart has been increased, but the damage and stagger have been decreased.\nDeals 30 stance damage on the weapon hit. The projectile deals 0 stance damage\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nRange, speed and attack power of magic attacks has been increased.\nMagic attacks now penetrate enemies when not charged.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dart Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
