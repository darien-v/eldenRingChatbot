\n\n Parry Skill \n\n        Skill: Parry \n       Use this Skill in time with a foe's melee attack to deflect it and break that foe's stance. \n       This gives you a chance for a critical hit. \n\nParry Skill is a Skill in Elden Ring. Parry Skill is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible shields.\n\nHow to get Parry Skill in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill for the Rift Shield,  Scripture Wooden Shield, Rickety Shield, Red Thorn Roundshield\nDefault skill for the Parrying Dagger\nDefault skill for the Red Thorn Roundshield\nDefault skill for the Leather Shield\nDefault skill for the Rickety Shield\nDefault skill for the Medium Shields: Albinauric Shield, Sun Realm Shield, Round Shield, Scorpion Kite Shield, Blue-Gold Kite Shield, Large Leather Shield, Candletree Wooden Shield, Flame Crest Wooden Shield, Hawk Crest Wooden Shield, Beast Crest Heater Shield, Red Crest Heater Shield, Blue Crest Heater Shield, Inverted Hawk Heater Shield and Heater Shield.\nPurchase from Knight Bernahl: 600\n\nElden Ring Parry Skill Guide, Notes & Tips\nFAQ: How do you put Parry on a Shield Elden Ring?\nThe Ash of War: Parry can be applied to a Shield in order to grant it the Parry Skill and allow players to parry in combat. This can only be done if the skill on the equipment allows skill replacement. \n\nA Number of shields will already have the Parry Skill so that players can perform a Parry which is is one of the main Combat maneuvers players can use when going against difficult Bosses or even in PvP and can be used as an effective offensive action combination when followed up with an attack. This gives players a chance at dealing a critical hit. It requires timing and patience to master, but provides more rewarding results than regular Dodging and guard countering, giving players a chance at a critical hit.\nA Shield with the Parry Skill is regured to be able to perform this action. The skill can be purchased on its own as an Ash of War. These are special items which enables you to replace Weapon Skills and Affinities of your equipment with new ones or even one at all.They can be applied to most Weapons and Shields, this allows players to adapt each type of armament to their respective Build and playstyle.\nThe Ash of War: Parry Skill can be purchased from Knight Bernahl. It provides Standard affinity and the Parry Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values. When a Shield or Weapon in your left hand is equipped with Parry, it takes priority over the Ash of War on the armament in your right hand.\n\nParry Skill Other Notes\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Parry.\nFP Cost: 0\nOn Small Shields, Parry has 5 start-up frames and 6 parry frames at 30 FPS.
