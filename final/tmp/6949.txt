\n\n Man-Serpent \n\nLocation\nVolcano Manor\n\nDrops\n 728 - 741 RunesGelmir Glintstone StaffMagma BladeMan-Serpent's ShieldVolcanic Stone\n\nMan-Serpent is an Enemy in Elden Ring. Man-Serpents are large humanoid lizards patrolling the lava-submerged town at Volcano Manor. Most Man-Serpents you'll find wield a sword and shield, but there is also a variation that attacks with a whip.\nThe spirit of a Man-Serpent can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Man-Serpent Ashes.\nIf you would like to learn more on the Man-Serpent Sorcerer, click the link.\n\nIt is said that long ago, the elder serpent that dwelled on Mt. Gelmir devoured a demigod, and the birth of the man-serpents followed.\n\nElden Ring Man-Serpent Locations\nWhere to Find Man-Serpent:\n\nVolcano Manor\n\nElden Ring Man-Serpent Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 728 - 741\n\nMagma Blade\n1.00%\nDropped only by those who wield it.\n\nMan-Serpent's Shield\n3.03% - 4.00%\nDropped only by those who wield it.\n\nVolcanic Stone\n20.00%\nN/A\n\nMan-Serpent Notes & Tips\n\nDeals Slash (sword), Strike (whip), Standard (sword) and Fire (both) damage.
