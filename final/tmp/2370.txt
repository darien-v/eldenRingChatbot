Twinned Set is a Medium Weight Armor that depicts the entwined twins of gold and silver. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Twinned Set in Elden Ring\n\nEarliest location: Limgrave East upon killing D, Hunter of the Dead. [Elden Ring Map here]\nObtained at end of D, Hunter of the Dead's questline. [Elden Ring Map here]\nVideo Location\n\nTwinned Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nTwinned Helm\nTwinned Armor\nTwinned Gauntlets\nTwinned Greaves\n\nTwinned Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Twinned Armor can be altered to the Twinned Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nCan kill D, Hunter of the Dead as soon as you meet him to get his armor set and D's Bell Bearing\nCan be given to D's brother (found in Nokstella, Eternal City) as an optional part of Fia's questline. In order to do this, you must have the entire set in your inventory (not your chest), and the armor cannot currently be altered. Otherwise, D's brother will not accept the armor. \nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
