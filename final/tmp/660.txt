\n\n Capital Outskirts \n\nCapital Outskirts Map & Fragments\nSites of Grace & Sub-Locations\nAll NPCs & Merchants\nAll Items, Equipment & Magic\nAll Enemies & Bosses\n\nCapital Outskirts is a Location in Elden Ring. The Capital Outskirts sub-region lies in the Eastern area of the Altus Plateau and is the region just outside Leyndell, Royal Capital but still within the capital's outer walls. It also covers the bridges located between the lift southeast of East Capital Rampart and Forbidden Lands (this part of Capital Outskirts is initially sealed by Morgott, the Omen King and will be unlocked after defeating him).\nBeware of Skeletons rising from their graves and rocks flying out of the capital toward the battlegrounds when exploring the area.\n\nCapital Outskirts Map & Map Fragments\n\nFragment Leyndell: Map (Leyndell, Royal Capital) is found on a monolith near the Outer Wall Phantom Tree Site of Grace.
