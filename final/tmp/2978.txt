QUESTION
Why is the All-Knowing Set in Elden Ring considered a medium weight armor that strikes a good balance between Physical and Magical defense?

ANSWER
The All-Knowing Set is considered a medium weight armor with a good balance between Physical and Magical defense due to the distribution of its Damage Negation stats across different damage types.
