He can be seen worshipping the Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum.\n\nLord of Blood, your eminence. I beg you, grant me a seat at the table of the dynasty. Long live the Mohgwyn Dynasty. Long live! The Mohgwyn Dynasty!\n\nSiofra River Spirit Location in Elden Ring\nThe Siofra River Spirit can be found under the bridge you cross to get to the northern Hollowhorn Grounds. He is facing the Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum that can be seen in the distance. \n\nThis NPC does not move and can only be talked to.\nThis spirit can't be fought\n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: Siofra River Spirit\n\n "...
