CONTEXT
\n\n Troll's Roar \n\n        Look into the distance and let out an intense roar, generating a powerful shockwave that blows back surrounding foes. Follow up with a strong attack to slam the armament down.  \n\nTroll's Roar is a Skill in Elden Ring. Troll's Roar is a skill found on the Troll's Hammer, Troll's Golden Sword, and Troll Knight's Sword. This allows the user to distance himself from nearby enemies by generating a shockwave that blows them back. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Troll's Roar\n\nDefault skill on the Troll's Hammer, Troll's Golden Sword, and Troll Knight's Sword\nCan be found on the top of the big rock just south east the Church of Repose (in Mountaintops of the Giants).\n\nElden Ring Troll's Roar Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Troll's Roar.\nFP Cost: 22 on use.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nUpon activation, the user winds up before unleashing an almighty roar that sends a shockwave in a sphere surrounding the user. \n\nUsing the Strong Attack button after the roar allows for a follow up, where the user slams their weapon into the ground. \n\nThis follow up does not consume any FP.\nThis attack is extremely slow and telegraphed, but deals significantly more damage than the roar.\n\nThe skill deals 40 Stance Damage on a close-range hit, and 11 Stance Damage on the outer part of the roar. \n\nThe follow-up attack deals 33 to 45 Stance Damage, depending on the weapon type. Greatswords deal the least, and Colossal Weapons deal the most.\n\nThe roar consists of two components. The inner roar deals strike damage while the outer roar deals standard damage. \n\nLike all roar type attacks, they deal much lower damage than most skills.\n\nDamage increases with Base Damage and Strength Scaling \n\nRoar Medallion will increase its damage by 15%.\nThis stacks with Highlander Axe.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nNo spheric shockwave comes out.\nFollow up slam deals reduced damage.\n\nThe reduced damage applies even though the slam itself does not consume FP.\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nAttack power has been increased.\nTiming of the poise increase during activation has been fixed.\n\nTroll's Roar Demonstration\n\nColossal Crusher Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's
