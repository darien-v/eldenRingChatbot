QUESTION
Why is the House Marais Set considered a lightweight armor set that focuses on increasing Arcane and Magical defense in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The House Marais Set is considered lightweight and focuses on Arcane and Magical defense due to its properties of low weight, high Magic, Fire, and Lightning defense, and increased Resistance, Robustness, and Vitality stats.
