\n\n Explosive Ghostflame \n\nSpell Type\nDeath Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 29\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Causes ghostflame explosion that burns wide area \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 42 \n\nFaith 30 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nExplosive Ghostflame is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Explosive Ghostflame spell causes burning to surrounding area when cast. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSorcery of the servants of Death.\nStrike the ground with the staff, triggering an explosion of ghostflame that burns the surrounding area.\nIn the time when there was no Erdtree, death was burned in ghostflame. Deathbirds were the keepers of that fire.\n\nWhere to find Explosive Ghostflame\nWhere to find Explosive Ghostflame:\n\nDrops from a Death Rite Bird southeast Apostate Derelict in the Consecrated Snowfield. It's a clearing in the frozen river, and you must go there at night. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Explosive Ghostflame Guide\n\nDeath Sorcery\nCan be boosted by Prince of Death's Staff\nStamina Cost: 45\nDeals magic damage\nInflicts Frostbite on opponents. Initial explosion deals 130 and flames deal 38 per tick\nWhen casting, the caster holds their staff in two hands and thrusts it into the ground, generating an explosion and leaving patches of ghostflame on the ground. The initial explosion has good knockback, and the fire may be good for zoning enemy players in PvP, but overall this spell deals weak damage for the FP cost and leaves the caster vulnerable.\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:  \n\nThe Frostbite buildup of this spell was significantly increased - the initial explosion frost buildup was increased from 50 to 130 and the lingering ghostflame was increased from 28 frost buildup per tick to 38 per tick.\nThe attack power of the spell has been increased.\nThe stamina damage done to guarding enemies has been increased.\nThe range of the residual ghostflame left after the explosion has been increased. \nThe damage detection time of the lingering ghostflame has been reduced.\n\nExplosive Ghostflame Builds\n\nDeath Mage Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Explosive Ghostflame Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
