\n\n Page Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 81\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons page spirit \n\nPage Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Page Ashes can be used to summon the spirit of a page that uses a piercing sword and a crossbow to defend the player.\nPage servants can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Page.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirit of a page.\nSpirit of a page who traveled at the side of the noble he served.Uses a piercing sword and crossbow to defend his master.One becomes a page merely by accident of being born into obscurity; nothing is asked of ability, talent, or volition.\n\nWhere to find Page Ashes\nWhere to Find Page Ashes\n\nCan be found in Cliffbottom Catacombs, on the tomb being watched by an Omen in the final room of the catacomb after climbing up the ladder. monument icon will appear on the left side of the screen.\nUses a normal crossbow and not the pulley crossbow that High Pages uses, and will therefore can only fire one shot before reloading.\nWalks very slowly and will often fire at targets behind walls or other objects.\nLower than average hp.\nVery low DPS, struggles to kill trash mobs, especially when the crossbow misses.\n\nElden Ring
