QUESTION
How can players acquire St. Trina's Arrow in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can acquire St. Trina's Arrow in Elden Ring by looting a Commoner's corpse at Stormveil Castle, buying from Nomadic Merchant in East Limgrave, buying from Isolated Merchant after entering Academy of Raya Lucaria, or it can drop from Albinauric Archers.
