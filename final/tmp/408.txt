\n\n Rose Church \n\nThe ruins of a former church aptly named Rose Church, for its walls are now festering with scarlet rot and overgrown with bloodroses.\n\nRose Church is a Location in Elden Ring. The Rose Church is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. This location is accessible on foot or on horseback by following the Liurnia marshes to a large island in the southwest.\n\nRose Church Map\nThe Rose Church is situated atop an isle in the southwest portion of the lake. He will give players a Festering Bloody Finger. Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook (12) can be found on the ground along the southern walls and the area is rich in Bloodroses. Beware of the single Lesser Sanguine Noble assassin that emerges from the center of the church when you get close, as well as the multiple Albinaurics outside its walls. QUESTION
Why is the Rose Church in Elden Ring known for being overgrown with bloodroses and scarlet rot?

ANSWER
The Rose Church in Elden Ring is known for being overgrown with bloodroses and scarlet rot due to the festering walls and abundance of Bloodroses.
