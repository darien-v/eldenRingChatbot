\n\n Sorcerer Painting \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Reminiscence of the painting "Homing Instinct" \n       Examine using the corresponding button. \n\nSorcerer Painting is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs\n\nWork of a wandering artist.Reminiscence of a painting titled "Sorcerer."\nThis painter is said to have captured the landscapes seen during the last moments of those welcomed into death's embrace.\nThe soul of the painter, and vestiges of the dead's last moments can be discovered by visiting the location depicted even now.\n\nWhere to find Sorcerer Painting in Elden Ring\n\nLocation: Castle Sol. Inside the central building in the main courtyard. [Map Link]\nTo get the painting, you will need to access the central building from an opening on the roof as the main door is locked from the inside. From the Main Gate, head inside the courtyard and hug the western wall as you make your way through wooden barricades. Look for a building in the northwest with Slugs and a ladder that will allow you to climb on top of the ramparts. Follow the ramparts west until you find a wooden bridge connecting to the central building. Beware the spectral Exile enemies mounted on ballistae up here. Once you reach the central building, descend the ladder from an opening in the roof to collect the painting.\n\nElden Ring Sorcerer Painting Tutorial Guide\n\nOn the bridge Northwest of the Stargazers' Ruins, go on the small platform jutting out on the eastern side of the bridge to find the painter. He will drop the Greathood.\n\nAfter the spirit disappears, you will receive the Greathood helm.\n\nElden Ring Sorcerer Painting Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 Sorcerer Painting.\nYou cannot
