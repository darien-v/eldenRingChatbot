\n\n Unsheathe \n\n        Skill of swordsmen from the Land of Reeds. Sheathe blade, holding it at the hip in a composed stance. Follow up with a normal or strong attack to perform a swift slash attack. \n\nUnsheathe is a Skill in Elden Ring. Unsheathe is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to Katanas.\n\nHow to get Unsheathe\n\nDefault Skill of the Uchigatana.\nAsh of War: Unsheathe is dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the water in the South East portion of Agheel Lake, North West of the Agheel Lake South Site of Grace. QUESTION
How to effectively use Unsheathe in combat strategies?

ANSWER
Unsheathe can be effectively used by unsheathing the Uchigatana, following up with a normal or strong attack to perform a swift slash, utilizing its Light and Heavy stance damage, and its cancelation feature at no FP cost.
