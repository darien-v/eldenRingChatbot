QUESTION
Why is the Meteorite of Astel spell particularly effective against large enemies such as Glintstone Dragon and Placidusax?

ANSWER
The Meteorite of Astel spell is particularly effective against large enemies such as Glintstone Dragon and Placidusax due to its ability to hit them with multiple meteors at once and its effectiveness against enemies resistant to magic.
