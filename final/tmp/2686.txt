High Page Set is a three-piece light armor set and a variation on the Page Set. It shares the Page Trousers with the base set, but has a unique head and chest piece. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are mostly composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find High Page Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe High Page Hood and High Page Clothes can be farmed from High Pages. \n\nThe earliest one that can be found is in Raya Lucaria Academy. From the Church of the Cuckoo site, enter the church and take the shortcut ladder up. On the other side of the room is a long hallway with a High Page at the end.\nThey can also be found in Leyndell, Royal Capital in various buildings. They vanish however after Leyndell, Royal Capital becomes Leyndell, Ashen Capital\n\nPage Trouser can be farmed from Pages or High Pages\n\nHigh Page Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nHigh Page Hood\nHigh Page Clothes\nPage Trousers\n\nHigh Page Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe High Page Clothes can be altered to the High Page Clothes (Altered) to remove the cape.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
