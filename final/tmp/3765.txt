\n\n Golden Rowa \n\nEffect \n\n        Material used for crafting items \n\nScaling \n\n        - \n\nObtained \n\n        Easily found near the Erdtree \n\nGolden Rowa is a Crafting Material in Elden Ring. Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. \n\nA Rowa fruit tinted with gold.Material used for crafting items.\nEasily found near the Erdtree.\n\nWhere to find Golden Rowa in Elden Ring\nGolden Rowa found in the overworld respawns after resting at a Site of Grace.\nIN THE WORLD\n\nGrows in Altus Plateau.\n\nFARMING LOCATIONS\n\nBower of Bounty Site of Grace in Altus Plateau: Four in the immediate vicinity, and a lot more between the grace and Mirage Rise.\nOuter Wall Phantom Tree Site of Grace in Altus Plateau: Ride up the slope to the left of the stairs (towards the North East) and continue until you get to the wall, then make a right and follow the cliff down. Cover the entire area as far south as the fort (not quite to the church) keep curving right and following the cliff, and then follow the wall until you are back at the grace. This route grants 22 Golden Rowa as well as several Mushrooms, Altus Blooms and Smoldering Butterflies. Additionally there are some sheep here. A full run takes around 2 minutes. Be aware that in the grove to the West of the stairs there is one Golden Rowa that cannot be picked up.\n\nGolden Rowa Guide for Elden Ring\nGolden Rowa can be used to craft the following items:\n\nInvigorating Cured Meat\nDappled Cured Meat\nInvigorating White Cured Meat\nDappled White Cured Meat\nHolyproof Dried Liver\nSweet Raisin\n\nElden Ring Golden Rowa Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 999 Golden Rowa.\nYou can store up to 999 Golden Rowa.\nSell Value:  10\n\nElden Ring Crafting Materials\n\nAeonian Butterfly  ♦  Albinauric Bloodclot  ♦  Altus Bloom  ♦  Arteria Leaf  ♦  Beast Blood  ♦  Beast Liver  ♦  Blood-Tainted Excrement  ♦  Bloodrose  ♦  Budding Cave Moss  ♦  Budding Horn  ♦  Cave Moss  ♦  Crab Eggs  ♦  Cracked Crystal  ♦  Cracked Pot  ♦  Crystal Bud  ♦  Crystal Cave Moss  ♦  Dewkissed Herba  ♦  Erdleaf Flower  ♦  Eye of Yelough  ♦  Faded Erdleaf Flower  ♦  Fire Blossom  ♦  Flight Pinion  ♦  Formic Rock  ♦  Four-Toed Fowl Foot  ♦  Fulgurbloom  ♦  Glintstone Firefly  ♦  Glintstone Scrap  ♦  Gold Firefly  ♦  Gold-Tinged Excrement  ♦  Golden Centipede  ♦  Golden Dung  ♦  Golden Sunflower  ♦  Grave Violet  ♦  Gravel Stone  ♦  Great Dragonfly Head  ♦  Hefty Beast Bone  ♦  Herba  ♦  Hidden Plumage  ♦  Human Bone Shard  ♦  Land Octopus Ovary  ♦  Living Jar Shard  ♦  Lump of Flesh  ♦  Melted Mushroom  ♦  Miquella's Lily  ♦  Miranda Powder  ♦  Mushroom  ♦  Nascent Butterfly  ♦  Old Fang  ♦  Poisonbloom  ♦  Rimed Crystal Bud  ♦  Rimed Rowa  ♦  Root Resin  ♦  Rowa Fruit  ♦  Ruin Fragment  ♦  Sacramental Bud  ♦  Sanctuary Stone  ♦  Silver Firefly  ♦  Silver Tear Husk  ♦  Sliver of Meat  ♦  Slumbering Egg  ♦  Stormhawk Feather  ♦  String  ♦  Strip of White Flesh  ♦  Thin Beast Bones  ♦  Toxic Mushroom  ♦  Trina's Lily  ♦  Turtle Neck Meat  ♦  Volcanic Stone  ♦  Yellow Ember\n\n
