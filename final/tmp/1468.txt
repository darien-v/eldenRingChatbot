Redmane Knight Set is a Heavy Weight set that strikes a good balance between physical and magical defense specifically Fire Resistance. Redmane Knight set dons the Lion on the chest of the armor and long cloak along with the red plume on the helm to signify Radahn's pedigree as Lord Radagon's son as the proud Red Lion. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Redmane Knight Set in Elden Ring\nPieces of the Redmane Knight Set have a chance to drop from any Redmane Knight found in Caelid.\n\nOne knight can be found with a few Redmane soldiers in a small camp southwest of the Smoldering Wall site of grace.\nTwo knights can be found at Fort Gael. One has a greatbow that shoots barrages of magic arrows as you approach the fort. Another can be found inside the fort near the top. [Map Link]\nOne knight on horseback can be found between the Caelid Catacombs and the Impassable Greatbridge site of grace.\nThree knights can be found separately in Redmane Castle. One located outside at the base of the wooden siege tower containing the ballista. One on top of a building connected to the southern ramparts. One found on a building on top of the entrance to the plaza, reached by entering the tower past the Abductor Virgin.\n\nRedmane Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nRedmane Knight Helm\nRedmane Knight Armor\nRedmane Knight Gauntlets\nRedmane Knight Greaves\n\nRedmane Knight Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Redmane Knight Armor can be altered to the Redmane Knight Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Where can someone find the Redmane Knight Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Pieces of the Redmane Knight Set can be found by defeating Redmane Knights in different locations in Caelid, such as near the Smoldering Wall, Fort Gael, Redmane Castle, between Caelid Catacombs and the Impassable Greatbridge.
