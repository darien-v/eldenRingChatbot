They wear a ragged garb and a cangue that has never been taken off, causing them to slouch. Although they appear to be slender, tall, and weak, these commoners are still able to wield a weapon and use it against intruders.\n\n The boards that hang from their necks depicts a sprawling tree, its roots and branches forming two holes. A self imposed shackle, that displayed their allegiance to the Erdtree.\n...Only, there are no commoners remaining with their wits about them.\n\nElden Ring Commoner Locations\nWhere to Find Commoner:\n\nCan be found within Stormveil Castle.\nFrenzied Villagers (a type of Commoner) can be found within the Ailing Village on the Weeping Peninsula.\nFrenzied Villagers can also be found in the Frenzied Flame Village in Liurnia of the Lakes, south of the Grand Lift of Dectus.\nNecromancers can be found within the Black Knife Catacombs in Liurnia of the Lakes, north of the Ainsel River Well.\nCommoners can also be found within Redmane Castle.\nIn the Altus Plateau there is a Spear-wielding Commoner south of Craftsman's Shack, \nDeath Cultist Commoners can be found gathered just south of the base of the Minor Erdtree in the Capital Outskirts of the Altus Plateau.\nCan be encountered within Leyndell, Royal Capital.\nFrenzied Villagers can also be found within the Yelough Anix Ruins in the Consecrated Snowfield.\n\nElden Ring Commoner Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 40 - 1020\n\nCommoner's Headband (Altered)\n1.50%\nN/A\n\nCommoner's Garb\n1.50%\nN/A\n\nCommoner's Shoes\n1.50%\nN/A\n\nWeathered Straight Sword\n2.00%\nN/A\n\nGlass Shard\n5.00%\nN/A\n\nYellow Ember\n1.50%\nOnly drops from Frenzied Villagers\n\nHuman Bone Shard\n10.0%\nOnly drops from Necromancers\n\nGrave Violet\n5.00%\nOnly drops from Necromancers\n\nEnemy Type\nItem Drops\nNotes\n\n Commoner \n\n 67 - 973 Runes\nCommoner's Headband (Altered)\nCommoner's Garb\nCommoner's Shoes\nWeathered Straight Sword\nGlass Shard\n\nCommoners are found in most regions of the Lands Between. Necromancers carry what looks like a Torchpole lit with what seems to be ghostflame (though it inflicts no buildup) instead of the normal fire of the Torchpole. They do not drop this Torchpole.\n\n Naked Commoner \n\n 351 Runes\nWeathered Straight Sword\nGlass Shard\n\nNaked Commoners can only be found within Volcano Manor. Some wield Torchpoles and Spears, but do not drop them. They can throw Poison Pots, Fire Pots, Oil Pots, and Ruin Fragments.\n\n Death Cultist \n\n 227 Runes\nWeathered Straight Sword\nGlass Shard\n\nDeath Cultist Commoners can cast Fia's Mist. They are found at the base of the Minor Erdtree found in the Capital Outskirts, along with an Omen that can also cast Fia's Mist. They are near the Twiggy Cracked Tear and the Crimson Crystal Tear.\n\nCommoner Notes & Tips\n\nNaked Commoners in the Volcano Manor throw poison pots, fire pots, oil pots, and ruin fragments.\nSeveral Commoner types carry Torchpoles and Spears, but none drop them. Both of these weapons are only dropped by the Exile Soldiers that use them.\nA certain Commoner in the Capital Outskirts, near the craters with many giant arrows, will transform into Margit upon approach. After Margit is defeated, this Commoner will still spawn. QUESTION
How can players locate the Commoners in the game Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Commoners can be found in Stormveil Castle, Ailing Village, Black Knife Catacombs, Frenzied Flame Village, Redmane Castle, Altus Plateau, Capital Outskirts, Leyndell Royal Capital, Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, Yelough Anix Ruins.
