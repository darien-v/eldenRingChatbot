CONTEXT
\n\n Hoarfrost Stomp \n\n        Stomp hard to spread a trail of freezing mist on the ground. The mist applies the frost status effect. \n\nHoarfrost Stomp is a Skill in Elden Ring. Hoarfrost Stomp is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War that can be used on all melee armaments.\n\nHow to get Hoarfrost Stomp\n\nAsh of War: Hoarfrost Stomp - Liurnia of the Lakes: Dropped by an invisible Teardrop Scarab found roaming the shallow body of water southeast of Caria Manor. Look for its glowing footprints and attack as they move towards you. [Map Link]\nDefault skill of the Icerind Hatchet.\n\nElden Ring Hoarfrost Stomp Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Hoarfrost Stomp.\nFP Cost: 10\nDeals 0 stance damage\nDeals 100% Magic Damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nFast cast time, high damage, and large AOE make this a very useful tool for any type of player\nBlue Dancer Charm does not increase the damage of this Skill\nDue to it being a Skill, opponents guarding the initial spreading trail may Guard Counter and reliably knock the user out of the animation.\nDeals 70 Frostbite on initial hit, after which the ice crystals explode applying 110 each. \n\nWill nearly always inflict frostbite on enemies vulnerable to it\n\nHoarfrost Stomp QUESTION
What are the specifics of the Hoarfrost Stomp skill and its application in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
