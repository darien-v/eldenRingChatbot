\n\n Oridys's Rise \n\nA lone observation tower atop a plateau in the east of the Weeping Peninsula. Long since abandoned, the entrance is barred by a seal, presenting travelers with a small challenge.\n\nOridys's Rise is a Location in Elden Ring. The Oridys's Rise is found in the Weeping Peninsula. Players can reach this location by jumping from the Spiritspring east of the Castle Morne Rampart Site of Grace. This leads up to a tower on the rampart from which the eastern plateau can be reached. Oridys's Rise can be found on this plateau.\n\nOridys's Rise Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Oridys's Rise\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Oridys's Rise\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nThere are no gather materials in this location\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no Upgrade materials in this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nThere are no equipment and magic in this location \n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nMemory Stone\n\nElden Ring Oridys's Rise Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no Creatures or Enemies at this location.\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no Bosses at this location.\n\nOridys's Rise Walkthrough\nThe entrance to Oridys's Rise is initially sealed by a barrier. There is a statue holding a glowing book by the entrance. Inspecting this statue presents players with a challenge to "Seek three wise beasts".\n\nThese beasts are represented as Ghostly Tortoises found nearby. These Tortoises need to be attacked to break parts of the seal.\n\nThe first Tortoise can be found just a few steps to the north, opposite the tower's entrance.\n\n2. The second Tortoise is hiding in the bushes to the left of the steps leading up to the tower's entrance. If you pay close attention you'll see a blue shade emanating through the bushes.\n\n3. The final Tortoise can be found in a small pond southwest of the tower. This Tortoise is invisible until struck but will make splashes on the water. Attack where the splashes are to reveal the Tortoise.\n\nOnce all three Tortoises are killed, players will get a notification that the seal has been broken. Go back to the tower, climb the ladder, then turn right to proceed up the stairs to reach a chest containing a Memory Stone which grants increases your Memory Slots by one, allowing for the memorization of more Sorceries or Incantations. Aside from this, there isn't much else to do at the tower.\n\nElden Ring Oridys's Rise Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy Crystal QUESTION
Why do players need to seek the three wise beasts in order to access Oridys's Rise in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players need to seek the three wise beasts in order to access Oridys's Rise in Elden Ring because these Ghostly Tortoises, when attacked, break parts of the seal at the tower's entrance, ultimately allowing access to the location.
