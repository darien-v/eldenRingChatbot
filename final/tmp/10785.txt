QUESTION
Why is the boost from the Magic Scorpion Charm on the Meteorite spell barely noticeable?

ANSWER
The boost from the Magic Scorpion Charm on the Meteorite spell is barely noticeable because it affects the magic portion of the spell, which is not the primary damage component of the spell.
