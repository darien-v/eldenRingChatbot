\n\n Troll Knight \n\nLocation\nCaria ManorThe Four BelfriesLiurnia of the Lakes\n\nDrops\n 1320 - 1342 RunesTroll Knight's Sword\n\nTroll Knight is a Humanoid enemy in Elden Ring. Troll Knights are a variant of the standard Troll, clad in knightly armor, and capable of casting powerful sorcery.\n\n Called into service when the Queen invoked an oath they swore, the trolls are treated as true knights of Caria, and fight arm in arm with their human comrades.\n\nTroll Knight Locations in Elden Ring\nTroll Knights can be found in the following locations:\n\nCaria Manor: Guarding the steps towards the boss.\nA blue Troll Knight puppet can be found north of the Liurnia Highway North Site of Grace alongside many puppet Noble Sorcerers and puppet soldiers. It will engage the nearby Raya Lucaria Footsoldiers  and Soldiers\nThree headless spectral Troll Knights can be found on the path leading down from The Four Belfries.\nOne can also be found guarding the Converted Fringe Tower.\n\nElden Ring Fallen Hawk Soldier Drops and Drop Rates\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 1320 - 1342\n\nTroll Knight's Sword\n8.00%\nN/A\n\nTroll Knight Notes & Tips\n\nBehaves identically to its regular variant, now with the ability to cast three giant glintblades to chase down foes.Headless variants near the Four Belfries gain the ability to teleport short distances.\nLike regular trolls, they can damage and kill their allies.\nStrong attacks directed towards their legs will easily topple them and allow for a critical hit on their head. QUESTION
Why do Troll Knights have the ability to cast powerful sorcery in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Troll Knights have the ability to cast powerful sorcery in Elden Ring because they are a variant of the standard Troll, clad in knightly armor, and were called into service to fight alongside their human comrades, being treated as true knights of Caria.
