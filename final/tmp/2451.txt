\n\n Rotten Winged Sword Insignia \n\nWeight\n1.4\n\nEffect \n\nGreatly raises attack power with successive attacks (6%,8%,13%) \n\nRotten Winged Sword Insignia is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Rotten Winged Sword Insignia greatly raises attack power with successive attacks. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a raised prosthetic blade. An honor bestowed upon the valkyries who serve the goddess of rot. \nGreatly raises attack power with successive attacks.\nThe Four sisters were born in the swamp of Aeonia, and came to the Haligtree under the aegis of Gowry. And yet, those buds were doomed to never blossom.\n\nRotten Winged Sword Insignia Effect in Elden Ring\nRotten Winged Sword Insignia greatly raises attack power with successive attacks.\n\nIncreases attack power relative to the number of attacks you land. Has three tiers of attack power increase. Tiers decay to lower tiers when not attacking fast enough. Number of hits needed to activate effect varies based on attacks used.\nDecay duration: 1.5 seconds.\nFor each tier, attack power increases by 6%, 8%, and then 13%.\nThe attack power boost also affects the damage of certain Spells.\n\nWhere to find Rotten Winged Sword Insignia in Elden Ring\nEarned after choosing to assist Millicent and successfully defeat her sisters.
