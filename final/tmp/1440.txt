QUESTION
Why is the Shaman Set worn by the Ancestral Follower Shaman found singing in the Ancestral Woods of Siofra River in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Shaman Set is worn by the Ancestral Follower Shaman in Elden Ring due to its association with the Shamanic practices and beliefs of the characters inhabiting the Ancestral Woods of Siofra River.
