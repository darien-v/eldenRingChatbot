Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman given by the jars to their friends.Raises potency of thrown jars.\nThough the jars are brought to life by human flesh and blood, they are all rather kindly folk. Perhaps they were made to be better than their innards.\n\nCompanion Jar Effect in Elden Ring\nCompanion Jar raises potency of Throwing Pots by 20%.\n\nHas no effect on Status Buildup or other debuffs.\n\nWhere to find Companion Jar in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Companion Jar:\n\nGiven by Jar Bairn upon completing his questline. After he mentions going on a journey to become a Warrior Jar, give him Alexander's Innards, exhaust his dialogue, then reload the area. The talisman is found where he was once seated.\n\nElden Ring Companion Jar Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  500\nFor even greater potency, consider pairing this with the Jar helmet.\nThis content was
