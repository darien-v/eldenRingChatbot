\n\n Mimic's Veil \n\nEffect \n\n        Uses FP to mimic nearby objects. \n\nScaling \n\n        - \n\nFP Cost\n6\n\nMimic's Veil is a Tool and Multiplayer Item in Elden Ring. Tools are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.\n\nGolden veil of intricate design.\nUses FP to mimic nearby objects.\nWhen Godrick was hounded from Leyndell, the Royal Capital, this was one of a multitude of treasures he took with him. Also known as "Marika's Mischief".\n\nWhere to find Mimic's Veil in Elden Ring\n\nLocation: Stormveil Castle. [Map Link]\nLocated on the second floor of the fortress of the Grafted Scion. Inside the room that looks like an assembly room, there is a Banished Knight. You'll find it inside the chest of the same room. The chest is on the podium.\nThe nearest checkpoint is the Rampart Tower site of grace. From this site, turn back and take the lift on the left to get back to the room we're you fought the Grafted Scion. Go across this room, take a right up the stairs. The chest will be in the next room. \nVideo Location for the image below,\n\nMimic's Veil use in Elden Ring\nMimic's Veil allows the player to mimic nearby objects by spending 6 FP.\n\nElden Ring Mimic's Veil Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 Mimic's Veil.\nYou can store up to 600 Mimic's Veil.\nThis item cannot be sold.\nAI enemies will not notice a disguised player as long as the player is not moving.\nThere is a visual bug regarding this item. Equipping either Crepus's Vial or the Black Knife Armor (unaltered) creates a visual overlapping of the model of the player and the object of transformation. This bug appears to be only visual. It may be that the bug is tied into the visual effect of the silent movement status produced by the talisman and armor piece themselves, as the bug does not occur while under the effects of the Assassin's Approach incantation by itself.\nAnother potential bug is that one of the field transformations for Limgrave is a knocked over Land Squirt.\n The frenzy tower in North East Liurnia will not affect you if you are disguised and stationery. QUESTION
What is the purpose of Mimic's Veil in Elden Ring and how is it obtained?

ANSWER
The purpose of Mimic's Veil in Elden Ring is to mimic nearby objects using 6 FP, and it is obtained in Stormveil Castle on the second floor of the fortress of the Grafted Scion inside a chest in an assembly room.
