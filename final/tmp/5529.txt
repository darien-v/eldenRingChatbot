\n\n Cuckoo Knight \n\nLocation\nLiurnia of the Lakes\n\nDrops\n 406 - ? RunesPartisanKnight's GreatswordCuckoo GreatshieldCuckoo Knight Set\n\nCuckoo Knight is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. They serve the Raya Lucaria Academy and utilize sorcery alongside their combat prowess.\n\nKnights, named for the cuckoo feather on their helmets, who faithfully serve the Academy of Raya Lucaria, and were given free rein to wage war as they please. Like their masters, they use various sorceries for combat, but they are more skilled in melee combat.\n\nElden Ring Cuckoo Knight Locations\nCuckoo Knights can be found in Liurnia of the Lakes at the following locations:\n\nFive knights can be found northeast of the East Raya Lucaria Gate site of grace in Liurnia of the Lakes\n\nThe mounted knight on the main path does not spawn at nighttime, due to being replaced by a Night's Cavalry\n\nThree mounted knights can be found guarding the checkpoint near the Grand Lift of Dectus\nOne knight can be found by taking the ramp down beneath the Bellum Church\nOne mounted knight can be found, patrolling the road between Northern Liurnia Lake Shore Grace and The Four Belfries Grace\nOne mounted knight can be found in a camp west of The Four Belfries\n\nElden Ring Cuckoo Knight Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 406 - ?\n\nKnight's Greatsword\n4.00%\nGreatsword Variant\n\nPartisan\n4.00%\nPartisan Variant\n\nCuckoo Greatshield\n4.00%\nAll Variants\n\nCuckoo Knight Helm\n3.00%\nAll Variants\n\nCuckoo Knight Armor\n3.00%\nAll Variants\n\nCuckoo Knight Gauntlets\n3.00%\nAll Variants\n\nCuckoo Knight Greaves\n3.00%\nAll Variants\n\nSliver of Meat\n10.00%\nHorse (Mounted Cuckoo Knight)\n\nBeast Liver\n10.00%\nHorse (Mounted Cuckoo Knight)\n\nCuckoo Knight Notes & Tips\n\nMounted Cuckoo Knights use a variant of the Glintblade Phalanx spell, which creates three magic glintblades overhead that automatically seek targets once in range. QUESTION
Why do Cuckoo Knights in Elden Ring utilize both sorcery and combat prowess in their service to the Raya Lucaria Academy?

ANSWER
Cuckoo Knights utilize both sorcery and combat prowess due to being named for the cuckoo feather on their helmets, faithfully serving the Raya Lucaria Academy and having free rein to wage war as they please.
