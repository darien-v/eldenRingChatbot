Somberstone Miner's Bell Bearing [1] Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nThe bell bearing of a miner who once dug for smithing stone.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to the following items:\n- Somber Smithing Stone (1)- Somber Smithing Stone (2)- Glintstone Scrap\n\nSomberstone Miner's Bell Bearing [1] Location in Elden Ring\nSomberstone Miner's Bell Bearing [1] can be found in the following location:\n\nSellia
