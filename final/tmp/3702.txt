QUESTION
Why are Melted Mushrooms found specifically in the overworld of the Eternal City in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Melted Mushrooms are found specifically in the overworld of the Eternal City in Elden Ring due to their growth in the false night, where they drip with a viscous fluid that behaves like oil.
