\n\n Carriage \n\nCarriages are Locations in Elden Ring. Carriages are found throughout the Lands Between. They are sometimes slowly towed by two Trolls from one place to another, and often have a treasure chest in the back. QUESTION
How can a player stop a Carriage in Elden Ring in order to loot the chest in the back?

ANSWER
