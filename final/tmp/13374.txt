QUESTION
Why are the Nox Bracelets considered an important armor piece in Elden Ring and what defensive properties do they possess?

ANSWER
The Nox Bracelets in Elden Ring are important due to their defensive properties such as Damage Negation, Physical 2.1, VS Strike 2.3, VS Slash 2.1, VS Pierce 1.9, Magic 2.9, Fire 2.8, Lightning 2.8, Holy 2.3, Resistance, Immunity 20, Robustness 13, Focus 15, Vitality 15, and Poise 1.
