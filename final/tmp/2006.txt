CONTEXT
\n\n CERULEAN HIDDEN TEAR \n\nType\nCrystal Tear\n\nEffect \n\n         Eliminates all FP consumption in mixed physick \n\nCerulean Hidden Tear is a Crystal Tear in Elden Ring. Crystal Tears can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to determine its effect.\n\nA crystal tear formed slowly over the ages where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground.\nCan be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick.The resulting concoction removes all FP consumption.\nHowever, this effect is only brief and will quickly expire.\n\nCERULEAN HIDDEN TEAR LOCATION IN ELDEN RING\nMt. Gelmir: Drops from the Ulcerated Tree Spirit at the Minor Erdtree, east of the Road of Iniquity site of grace. [Map link]\n\nCerulean Hidden Tear Notes & Tips\n\nFP is not consumed by any actions that normally consume FP for 15 seconds.\nYou can perform actions that would normally consume more FP than your max (e.g. summoning expensive Spirit Ashes that you cannot afford normally).\nWeapon skills and spells that only consume FP, such as Comet Azur, Crystal Torrent, Meteorite, Meteorite of Astel, Prelate's Charge, Rock Blaster, Spinning Wheel, Surge, O Flame! and Unendurable Frenzy, can be used continuously.\nHas no effect on Tongues of Fire, the weapon skill still consumes FP even when the tear is used.\nYou can find Crystal Tears at the bases of Minor Erdtrees and elsewhere throughout the Lands Between.\nAt sites of grace, you can mix two Crystal Tears in the Flask of Wondrous Physick. (This will allow you to create elixirs with various custom effects.)\n\nBlack Blade Build\n\nPyromancer Build Guide\n\nElden Ring Crystal Tears\n\nCerulean Crystal Tear  ♦  Crimson Bubbletear  ♦  Crimson Crystal Tear  ♦  Crimsonburst Crystal Tear  ♦  Crimsonspill Crystal Tear  ♦  Crimsonwhorl Bubbletear  ♦  Dexterity-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Faith-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Greenburst Crystal Tear  ♦  Greenspill Crystal Tear  ♦  Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Intelligence-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Leaden Hardtear  ♦  Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Magic-Shrouding Cracked Tear  ♦  Opaline Bubbletear  ♦  Opaline Hardtear  ♦  Purifying Crystal Tear  ♦  Ruptured Crystal Tear  ♦  Speckled Hardtear  ♦  Spiked Cracked Tear  ♦  Stonebarb Cracked Tear  ♦  Strength-knot Crystal Tear  ♦  Thorny Cracked Tear  ♦  Twiggy Cracked Tear  ♦  Windy Crystal Tear  ♦  Winged Crystal Tear\n\n
