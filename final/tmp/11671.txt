\n\n Lesser Omenkiller \n\nLocation\nVolcano ManorPerfumer’s RuinsLeyndell, Royal Capital\n\nDrops\nOmenkiller SetGreat Omenkiller Cleaver\n\nLesser Omenkiller is an Enemy in Elden Ring. These brutal warriors hunt Omens, who are seen as impure and outside the Erdtree's grace.\nOmenkiller is the boss form of this enemy. It also appears in the Omenkiller and Miranda, the Blighted Bloom fight.\nThe spirit of a Lesser Omenkiller can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Omenkiller Rollo Spirit Ashes.\n\nTheir visage is carved in the image of the evil spirits that haunt the Omen in their nightmares.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Omenkiller Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Omenkiller:\n\nTwo Lesser Omenkiller can be found in Volcano Manor, one among the dwellings down the stairs from the Prison Town Church Site of Grace and the other in the building above the Guest Hall Site of Grace. Each one drops Great Omenkiller Cleaver the first time it's defeated.\nA Lesser Omenkiller can be found in Perfumer's Ruins. It drops Omenkiller Long Gloves, Omenkiller Robe and Omenkiller Boots the first time it's defeated.\nA Lesser Omenkiller can be found outside the Lower Capital Church Site of Grace in Leyndell Royal Capital. It drops Omensmirk Mask the first time it's defeated.\n\nLesser Omenkiller Notes & Tips\n\nSee Omenkiller for a full guide\n\nLesser Omenkiller Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures &
