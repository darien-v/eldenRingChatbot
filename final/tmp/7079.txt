\n\n Guardian \n\nLocation\nMinor Erdtrees across the Lands Between\n\nDrops\n 63 - 1609 RunesGuardian SetGuardian's SwordspearTarnished Golden Sunflower\n\nGuardian is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. They are found around several of the Minor Erdtrees, acting as the trees protectors. Guardians are hostile at close proximity and will rush forward to attack with their Swordspears.\n\n In accordance with an ancient pact with the Erdtree, it is said that  their deaths lead not to destruction, but instead to renewed. eternal life as guardians. \n\nElden Ring Guardian Locations\nWhere to Find Guardians:\n\nWeeping Peninsula near the Minor Erdtree (Weeping Peninsula)\nLiurnia of the Lakes near the Minor Erdtree (Liurnia Southwest).\nCapital Outskirts near the Minor Erdtree Church.\nLeyndell Royal Capital on the roots of the Erdtree.\nConsecrated Snowfield, near the Minor Erdtree (Consecrated Snowfield)\nGreyoll's Dragonbarrow. 29 can be found at Minor Erdtree (Dragonbarrow) starting from the Dragonbarrow Fork site of grace.
