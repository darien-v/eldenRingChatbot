Spirit Jellyfish are usually non-hostile, but if aggroed, they will fire poison and will try to headbutt their opponents.\nA Spirit Jellyfish can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Spirit Jellyfish Ashes.\n\nEthereal beings usually found near places of rest, acting as protectors. They are usually peaceful, but if angered, they attack with deadly poison fired from a distance.\n\nSpirit Jellyfish Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Spirit Jellyfish:\n\nSpirit Jellyfish are found all over the Lands Between, with the exception of the underground. They often appear near graveyards or cemetaries, sometimes at nighttime.\n\nSpirit Jellyfish Drops in Elden Ring\n\n 14 - 569 Runes\nGrave Glovewort [1]\n\nElden Ring Spirit Jellyfish Notes & Tips\n\nSpirit Jellyfish are blue in color unless provoked, after which they will turn red and start attacking. Blue Jellyfishes are safe to approach. \nTheir ranged jet attack causes Poison buildup.\nOnce hostile (turned red) they will attack the player on sight, and will make other Spirit Jellyfish aggressive as well.\nIn a few places, such as northeast of Castle Morne and southwest of the Four Belfries, there is a small group of Spirit Jellyfish that spawn red and are always aggro.\nParticularly weak to
