QUESTION
Why is the Pulley Bow considered the best choice for ledge snipers in Elden Ring, especially when combined with Arrow talismans?

ANSWER
The Pulley Bow is considered the best choice for ledge snipers in Elden Ring, especially when combined with Arrow talismans, due to its superior performance in damage output, lowest requirements, and best range compared to other bows like the Longbow and Albinauric Bow.
