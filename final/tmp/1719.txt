Commoner's Set is a three piece cloth set worn by the tall slender Commoners found across the game. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are typicall composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Commoner's Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Commoner's Garb and Commoner's Shoes pieces have a chance to drop from Commoner enemies. They can be easily found throughout Stormveil Castle and Leyndell, Royal Capital, as well as other areas of the game. These enemies can also be found in variant armor pieces that will drop Commoner's Simple Garb, and Commoner's Headband (Altered).\nThe Commoner's Headband is found on a corpse in Mt. Gelmir. After the Temple of Eiglay site of grace, take the elevator up and jump off the outside balcony into the area below. Follow the path past the Abductor Virgin into the window of the manor. Continue until you are on a walkway overlooking the lake the lava lake with Serpent statues on the right side.
