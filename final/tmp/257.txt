\n\n Renna's Rise \n\nA Tower of the Three Sisters, belonging to Renna. It houses Ranni's Snow Witch Armor Set.\n\nRenna's Rise is a Location in Elden Ring. Renna's Rise is one of three towers found in the Three Sisters sub-region of Liurnia of the Lakes. It is accessible after giving Ranni the Fingerslayer Blade. A waygate to the northern part of Ainsel River is found at the very top.\n\nRenna's Rise Map\nRenna's Rise is the northernmost tower at the Three Sisters sub-region. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Renna's Rise\n\nNPC Name - Role and Location\nMerchant Name - Role and Location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Renna's Rise\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nList Gatherable items in this location\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nInsert Upgrade Materials here\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nSnow Witch Set\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this location\n\nElden Ring Renna's Rise Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no enemies in this location\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses in this location\n\nRenna's Rise Walkthrough\nThis tower can be accessed by progressing through the Ranni the Witch questline. This Tower can be accessed once players reach the Divine Tower of Liurnia as part of Ranni's quest. This occurs after defeating Radahn and giving the Fingerslayer Blade to Ranni. After this, players can make their way and take the ladders to the top of the interior tower. There will be a chest that will contain the Snow Witch Set.
