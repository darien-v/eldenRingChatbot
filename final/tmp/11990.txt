\n\n Crab \n\nLocation\nFound near bodies of water in the Lands Between.\n\nDrops\n\n 10 - 395 Runes\nCrab Eggs\nStrip of White Flesh\n\nCrab is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. These crustaceans are often foraging, looking for anything in the mud to eat. They are often too busy eating to notice anything around them. They are not hostile unless provoked.\n\n Only those of comfort and means know the truth:a crab's eggs are more delectable than its meat.\n\nCrab Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Crabs:\n\nPlayers can encounter Crabs roaming in places of shallow water such as the beaches of Western Limgrave, and even in the courtyard of Raya Lucaria.\nNon-respawnable Crystal Crabs are also found in Raya Lucaria, each dropping a Glintstone Crown. They boast high defenses as most weapons bounce off of them. \nCan also be found in many Catacombs.\nThey are also found in Siofra River.\n\nElden Ring Crab Combat information\n\nHealth: \n\n31 HP (Limgrave)\n35 HP (Weeping Peninsula - Isolated Merchant's Shack)\n46 HP (Weeping Peninsula - Morne Moangrave)\n75 HP (Altus Plateau - Second Church of Marika)\n67 HP (Minor Erdtree Catacombs)\n63 - 91 HP (Siofra River; Liurnia of the Lakes; Capital Outskirts)\n192 HP (Consecrated Snowfield - South of Ordina)\n589 HP (Academy of Raya Lucaria)\n\nPoise: 30\nImmunities: Madness, Death Blight.\n\nAbsorptions\n\nPhy (Standard): 10 - 11\nPhy (Slash): 10 - 11\nPhy (Strike): (-40) - (-45)\nPhy (Pierce): 10 - 11\n\nMagic: 0\nFire: 0\nLightning: 0\nHoly: 0\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if an absorption is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% is absorbed. Bigger number = less damage. An absorption of 100 means no damage goes through, and a resistance of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nResistances\n\nPoison: 169 - 181\nScarlet Rot: 169 - 181\nHemorrhage: 226 - 241\nFrostbite: 127 - 135\n\nThe resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it. For example, if a resistance is 100 you must deal 100 points of whatever aux buildup to trigger it. Note that these go down over time, and increase each time the effect procs.\n\nCrab Drops in Elden Ring\n\nCrab Eggs\nStrip of White Flesh\nRunes:\n\n10  (Limgrave)\n11 ~ 12  (Weeping Peninsula)\n23 QUESTION
Why do non-respawnable Crystal Crabs in Raya Lucaria drop Glintstone Crown and have high defenses against most weapons?

ANSWER
Non-respawnable Crystal Crabs in Raya Lucaria drop Glintstone Crown due to their high defenses against most weapons.
