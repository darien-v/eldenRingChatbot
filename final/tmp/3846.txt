\n\n Dynast's Finesse \n\n        Nimbly avoid an attack, securing some distance from foes. Follow up with strong attack to perform a sudden lunge, and press strong attack again to perform a sweeping slice. \n\nDynast's Finesse is a Skill in Elden Ring. Dynast's Finesse is a Unique skill found on the Bloody Helice which can add extra damage to foes. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Dynast's Finesse\n\nDefault skill of the Bloody Helice.\n\nElden Ring Dynast's Finesse Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 5 ( -7)\nDeals 33 +11 +11 (Total 55) stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill can be Parried\nYou are immune to damage while in the first portion of the skill, the spin. \n\nDamage immunity lasts for 20 iFrames (30 FPS)\nThe spin costs no FP if one has not met the stat requirement to use the weapon, which can be used by pure caster builds to evade attacks right after casting spells, however, it would not have the damage immunity.\n\nSee Dodging for detailed information on the mechanic and a comparison of different dodging skills.\nPressing R2 after using this skill will follow up the dodge with a long range forward thrust.  This attack can be additionally followed up with a 2nd R2 for 2 additional hits (one thrust and one slash) \n\nEach subsequent attack in the chain will consume additional FP\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: The directional control of the follow up strong attack has been improved.\n\nClick below for Dynast's Finesse recovery frame data.\n\nSee Dynast's Finesse recovery frames at 30 FPS (click to reveal)\n\nRoll: 14\nAsh of War: 9\nRight-hand attack: 19\nLeft-hand attack: 22\nBlock: 22\nQuick item use: 20\nItem use: 22\nWalk: 23\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Dynast's Finesse Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
