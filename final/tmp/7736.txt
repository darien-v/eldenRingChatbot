\n\n Ash of War: Blood Tax \n\nAffinity\nBlood\n\nSkill\nBlood Tax\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Blood Tax is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Blood Tax provides and the Blood Tax Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Blood affinity and the following skill:\n"Blood Tax: Blood Oath skill granted by the Lord of Blood. Twist to build power, then unleash a flurry of thrusts that rob the target of both their blood and their HP."\nUsable on armaments capable of thrusting (colossal weapons excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Blood Tax Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Blood Tax:\n\nFound in the Mohgwyn Dynasty area in a small cave, at the eastern edge of the blood lake with four blood albinaurics that appear after killing the beetle. Elden Ring Interactive Map Link\nVideo Location\n\n Ash of War: Blood Tax Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Blood Tax.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Poison, Blood, Occult\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds\nThe skill causes you to hit 4 times, a stronger thrust followed by two weaker thrusts, then a final stronger thrust. \nDeals 88+44+44+110 MV (Motion Value) of damage for the 4 hits of the combo respectively.\nApplies 100+50+50+100 status effect MV (Motion Value) for the 4 hits of the combo respectively, averaging out to 75% of normal status effect buildup compared to 4 normal light attacks, though at a much faster rate when used on heavier and slower weapons.\nPatch 1.07 buffed this skill in a few ways - the attack was sped up and now does more damage, the health restoration effect was improved and the attack can be used sooner after the end of a different animation (such as attacking or using an item). \n\nThe health regeneration of the skill is only dependent on your max health and does not correlate to the damage done or the health of the enemy.\nThe first and last hits will always regenerate 45 HP + 4% of your max HP\nThe second and third hits will regenerate 25 HP + 2% of your max HP.\nThis means that if all hits connect, you will regenerate a total of 12% of your max health plus an additional 140 HP.\n\nBlood Tax Demonstration\n\nAsh of War: Blood Tax Tips\n\nThe Mohgwyn Dynasty can be reached early by completing White Mask Varre's questline and using the Pureblood Knight's Medal given by him.\nThis ash of war as surprisingly good stagger, able to make an enemy like a Leyndell Knight flinch from every hit regardless of poise. However, it does not boost one's own poise, thus it can easily be interrupted. \n\nIf you use Endure from a weapon in the left hand, you can nullify being interrupted. Keep in mind of your FP usage if Mind is low.\n\nCombine this with the incantation Bloodflame Blade and you'll accumulate Bleed build-up more easily than dual-wielding two Bleed weapons on top of being healed per hit (more potent at lower levels).\nThis skill costs 14 FP to use.\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric Roar  ♦  Ash of War: QUESTION
How can one maximize the effectiveness of the Blood Tax skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Equip it on weapon categories allowing skill replacement, complete the Mohgwyn Dynasty area early, use Endure from a weapon in the left hand, combine with Bloodflame Blade incantation for Bleed buildup, keep an eye on FP usage.
