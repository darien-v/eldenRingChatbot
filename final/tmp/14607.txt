Brave's Battlewear (Altered) is part of the Brave's Set Armor Set, and is currently unobtainable. Brave's Battlewear (Altered) is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nHelm wrapped in thick leather. Garb worn by warriors of the frigid uplands.\n\nWhere to find Brave's Battlewear (Altered) in Elden Ring\n\nCurrently unobtainable in-game.\n\nBrave's Battlewear (Altered) Set in Elden Ring\n\nBrave's Battlewear (Altered) is a piece of the Brave's Set\nThe rest of the set is composed by the Brave's Leather Helm, Brave's Bracer, and Brave's Legwraps\nSee also: Brave's Battlewear\n\nElden Ring Brave's Battlewear (Altered) Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value: 500\nThe unaltered piece is red, while the altered is blue. QUESTION
How does Brave's Battlewear (Altered) compare to other chest armors in Elden Ring in terms of stats and capabilities?

ANSWER
Brave's Battlewear (Altered) offers high Magic, Fire, and Lightning resistance, but lower Physical resistance compared to other chest armors in Elden Ring.
