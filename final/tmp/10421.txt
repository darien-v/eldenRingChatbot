QUESTION
How can players efficiently navigate and make the most out of the resources available at the Minor Erdtree in Capital Outskirts in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can efficiently navigate and make the most out of the resources available at the Minor Erdtree in Capital Outskirts by gathering items such as Altus Bloom, Crystal Bud, Golden Rowa, Golden Sunflower, Mushroom, and Trina's Lily, while also being aware of the Commoner and Omen enemies guarding the Crimson Crystal Tear and Twiggy Cracked Tear, with no bosses present at that location.
