\n\n Lux Ruins \n\nLong forgotten ruins among golden trees, swarmed with demi-humans protecting the underground chamber.\n\nLux Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Lux Ruins can be reached by exploring the western cliffs of Altus Plateau.\n\nLux Ruins Map\nFound on western cliffs of Altus Plateau, north-west from the Altus Plateau Site of Grace. The area surrounding the ruins will be teeming with Demi-Human enemies with chances to drop Falchion, String, Rickety Shield and other Materials. Be wary of the bushes and shrubs as you pass. In this area you can obtain Ash of War: Shield Crash dropped by a Teardrop Scarab amongst the ruins. Elden Ring Map here.\nIf you're ready to face the boss, head down towards the fog gate to face the Demi-human Queen Gilika. [Elden Ring Map] \n\nAim for her legs and it should be an easy boss fight. After her defeat you can go ahead and open the gate to reveal that chest that obtains the Ritual Sword Talisman, [Elden Ring Map here] which raises attack power when HP is at maximum.\n\nNote that some of Lux Ruins are on the bottom of the cliff, at Erdtree-Gazing Hill site of grace, this part of the ruins has no items and you cannot access Lux Ruins proper from this area on foot -- you must
