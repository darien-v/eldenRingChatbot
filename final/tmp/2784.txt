[Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Beast-Repellent Torch Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Torch Attack\nThis weapon can be infused with the Ash of War: No Skill\nThis weapon can not be enchanted with Magic or boosted with Consumables\nWhile held in either hand (or on the back while two-handing something else), certain creatures will not attack the player. The affected creatures are: Lone Wolves, Dogs, Giant Dogs (only those that do not have glowing red eyes), Boars, Sheep, Bears (not Lesser Runebears), Rats, Giant Rats, Basilisks, and Crystal Snails. If spotted by a creature, the creature will stay focused on the player (as if they are readying to attack), but will keep their distance and not attack. If the player attacks the creature or approaches too closely for too long, the creature will attack the player as normal. Putting the torch away and pulling it back out again will cause the creature to cease attacking the player once more.\nSome Basilisks will attack with Death Blight breath upon first seeing the player, even if the player has the Beast-Repellent Torch equipped. After this initial attack, they will cease attacking while the player has the Beast-Repellent Torch out.\nThe torch's effect remains active while riding Torrent, regardless of the hand that is holding it.\nSell Value:   100 Runes\nIt has the same model as the Torch, but a different flame.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed. QUESTION
How can the Beast-Repellent Torch in Elden Ring be used effectively to keep certain creatures away while exploring the game world?

ANSWER
Hold the Beast-Repellent Torch in hand to prevent attacks from certain creatures such as Lone Wolves, Dogs, Bears, and Basilisks while exploring Elden Ring.
