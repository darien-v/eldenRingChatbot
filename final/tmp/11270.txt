\n\n Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Join medallions to operate Grand Lift of Rold \n\nHaligtree Secret Medallion (Right) is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right)  is used to operate Grand Lift of Rold, granting access to a hidden path to the Consecrated Snowfield. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nRight half of a split medallion depicting the Haligtree.\nBrandishing the medallion with both halves conjoined will activate the Grand Lift of Rold, granting access to a hidden path.\nIn other words, this secret medallion will guide the bearer to the land of Miquella's Haligtree.\n\nWhere to find Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) in Elden Ring\nHit Albus, who is disguised as a pot past the Perfumer guarding the path up from the Village of the Albinaurics Site of Grace in the swamp caves west of Liurnia of the Lakes. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) Use\nHaligtree Secret Medallion (Right) can be used to operate Grand Lift of Rold. This medallion opens the path to a secret location past Lyndell, called the Consecrated Snowfield.\nIn order to use it you must be at the bottom of the Grand Lift of Rold and switch actions from "Hoist Medallion" to "Hoist Secret Medallion". This will not work if you are at the top of the Lift.\nSee Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left) for the other part.\n\nElden Ring Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) Notes & Tips\n\nHaving this item in your inventory triggers the event in the Roundtable Hold where the player is invaded by a now hostile NPC Ensha of the Royal Remains\n\nElden Ring Key Items\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell QUESTION
Why is it necessary to combine both halves of the Haligtree Secret Medallion to activate the Grand Lift of Rold?

ANSWER
