\n\n Site of Grace \n\n        This tiny golden aura is the grace of the Erdtree. But now it is all that guides you, or so I hear. You can see them, Can't you? The rays of grace, that guide you through your burden \n\nSites of Lost Grace are the Checkpoints of Elden Ring. These special locations feature a luminous remnant of Grace, enshrined by a small stump of roots, and allow a moment of respite in The Lands Between. Sites of Lost Grace function pretty much the same way that Bonfires worked in Dark Souls series.\n\nSite of Lost Grace Effects in Elden Ring\nResting at a site of Lost Grace will have the following effects:\n\nRestore: Resting at a Site of Lost Grace will restore players HP and FP and heal any status ailments.\nRefill: Players Flasks of Crimson/Cerulean Tears and Flask of Wondrous Physick will be refilled.\nRespawn: Most enemies will respawn when player rests at a Site of Lost Grace.\nResurrection: When player dies, they will be resurrected at the last Site of Lost Grace they rested at.\n\nSite of Lost Grace Menu Options in Elden Ring\nFrom a Site of Lost Grace you select the following menu options:\n\nPass Time: Allows you to pass time for day/night events.\nLevel Up: Allows you to spend Runes to increase your character Level.\nFlasks: Allows you to add an extra charge if enough Golden Seeds are available, increase the potency of your flasks if you have Sacred Tears in your inventory, and distribute the number of uses in each of your flasks.\nMemorize Spell: This menu allows you to allocate Sorceries and Incantations. Your memory slots determine the number of Sorceries and Incantations you can memorize. More memory slots can be acquired with Memory Stones.\nMix Wondrous Physick: You can combine two different Crystal Tears inside the Flask of Wondrous Physick to create various custom dual effects.\nSort Chest: Here you can browse and sort your items. If you are already carrying the maximum amount of items when you pick up a new item, it will be sorted in the chest automatically.\nGreat Runes: Allows you equip a restored Great Rune.\nAshes of War: Here you can use Ashes of War to grant affinities and Skills to your armaments. Please check Upgrades for more information.\nAlter garments: Allows you to alter the appearance of a select few pieces of armor at a cost of 500 Runes. See Tailoring Tools.\nTalk to Miniature Ranni: Interact with the miniature Ranni Key Item.\nAbout Melina: You can learn more about the world of Elden Ring by engaging in conversations with her as they QUESTION
What menu options are available at a Site of Lost Grace in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Pass Time, Level Up, Flasks, Memorize Spell, Mix Wondrous Physick, Sort Chest, Great Runes, Ashes of War, Alter garments, Talk to Miniature Ranni, About Melina, Leave.
