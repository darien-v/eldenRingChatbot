QUESTION
Why are the Confessor Boots considered a good balance between physical and magical defense in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Confessor Boots are considered a good balance between physical and magical defense in Elden Ring because they provide Dmg Negation, high defenses against various damage types like Strike, Slash, Pierce, Magic, Fire, Ligt, and Holy, along with decent Resistance stats such as Immunity, Robustness, Focus, Vitality, and Poise.
