Albinauric Ashes summons two Second-Generation Albinauric spirits who wield ripple swords.\nAlbinaurics can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Second-Generation Albinauric.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirits of two Albinaurics.\nA strapping duo of cartwheeling spirits who wield ripple swords and spew freezing breath.Both are second-generation Albinaurics, with dumpy heads that resemble those of frogs.\n\nAlbinauric Ashes Location in Elden Ring\nAlbinauric Ashes can be found in the following location:\n\nFound on a corpse in the graveyard area east of Caria Manor. QUESTION
How can you effectively summon the spirits of two Albinaurics using Albinauric Ashes in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To summon the spirits of two Albinaurics using Albinauric Ashes in Elden Ring, consume low FP (53), be near a rebirth monument, and look for the monument icon on the left side of the screen to summon.
