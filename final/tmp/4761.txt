CONTEXT
\nMessages allow players to place messages that have a random chance (?) to be seen on the ground by other players where they are placed. The text in messages can only be selected from premade words and phrases, but the format of a message being written can be toggled by pressing a button (default is the Y button on an Xbox controller) for more complex fields. The hard formatting of messages allows them to be automatically translated and displayed in different regions around the world.\nThey have some unique mechanics compared to their counterparts in FromSoftware titles. \nAppraisals\nA player reading a message can rate it Good or Bad as an Appraisal. When a message is rated Good, the author will receive 1 Flask of Crimson Tears' worth of health at no cost.\nA player can see Good vs Bad ratings on their messages, but only the number of Appraisals QUESTION
How can players effectively utilize the unique mechanics of Messages in this game to receive healing benefits?

ANSWER
Players can effectively utilize the unique mechanics of Messages in the game by creating messages likely to receive Good ratings, which provide healing benefits.
