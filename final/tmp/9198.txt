\n\n Redmane Knight \n\nLocation\nCaelid\n\nDrops\n 578 - 2882 RunesRedmane Knight SetKnight's GreatswordPartisanGreatbowRedmane GreatshieldGreat Arrow\n\nThe Redmane Knight is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring who serves under the regime of General Radahn. Redmane Knights are encountered within the region of Caelid, equipped with their signature Redmane Knight Set.\nThe spirit of the longest-serving Redmane Knight can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Redmane Knight Ogha Ashes.\n\nKnights who fought by General Radahn's side. Their red plumes is a symbol of Radahn's pedigree as Lord Radagon's son - the mane of the proud Red Lion.\n\nElden Ring Redmane Knight Locations\nRedmane Knights can be found in Caelid at the following locations:\n\nOne knight can be found with a few Redmane Soldiers in a small camp southwest of the Smoldering Wall site of grace.\nTwo knights can be found at Fort Gael. One has a Greatbow that shoots barrages of magic arrows as you approach the fort. Another can be found inside the fort near the top. [Map Link]\nOne knight on horseback can be found between the Caelid Catacombs and the Impassable Greatbridge site of grace.\nThree knights can be found separately in Castle Redmane. One located outside at the base of the wooden siege tower containing the ballista. One on top of a building connected to the southern ramparts. One found on a building on top of the entrance to the plaza, reached by entering the tower past the Abductor Virgin.\nA spectral version of the knight can be found in War-Dead Catacombs\n\nElden Ring Redmane Knight Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 578 - 2882\n\nRedmane Knight Helm\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nRedmane Knight Armor\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nRedmane Knight Gauntlets\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nRedmane Knight Greaves\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nKnight's Greatsword\n4.00%\nGreatsword Variant.\n\nPartisan\n4.00%\nPartisan Variant.\n\nGreatbow\n4.00%\nGreatbow Variant.\n\nRedmane Greatshield\n4.00%\nAll except the Greatbow Variant.\n\n5x Great Arrow\n15.00%\nGreatbow Variant.\n\nRedmane Knight Notes & Tips\n\nRedmane Knights have the typical knight moveset, but with a few additional tactics: \n\nRedmane Knights equipped with Knight's Greatswords will use the Ash of War: Lion's Claw skill, which performs a forward somersault into an overhead strike that slams the weapon on the ground.\nRedmane Knights equipped with Partisans are capable of using the Ash of War: Giant Hunt skill, which performs a powerful lunging thrust capable of launching a vulnerable player, or violently shoving a guarding player backwards.\nAll Redmane Knights are capable of using the Ash of War: War Cry skill while on foot, which boosts their physical damage output for a short time. The shout they produce while initiating this buff deals no physical damage, but can deal stamina damage if the player has their shield up close to the knight, which can lead to the player's guard being broken.\nIf equipped with a Greatbow: \n\nFire a charged gravitational arrow.\nThe knight at Fort Gael can fire a rain of gravitational arrows.\n\nMounted Redmane Knights are capable of using an ability similar to the Ash of War: Flame of the Redmanes. Instead of couching their spear for a thrust like most mounted knights, they will sometimes hold it in both hands and fling a large explosive fireball at range. They lose this ability once they have been dismounted.\n\nThe Partisans wielded by Redmane Knights in and around Redmane Castle are permanently set aflame, dealing bonus fire damage.\n\nIMAGE
