CONTEXT
\n\n Rain of Arrows \n\n        Archery skill performed from a low stance. Ready the bow, then fire a burst of arrows into the sky to shower the enemy with projectiles. \n\nRain of Arrows is a Skill in Elden Ring. Rain of Arrows is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, it can be used on all Bows.\n\nHow to get Rain of Arrows in Elden Ring\n\nCaelid: Ash of War: Rain of Arrows appears at edge of a cliff after you've looted the "Redmane" Painting in Sellia, Town of Sorcery. You'll find it down the cliff on a plateau south east of the Minor Erdtree in Caelid. Once you've made your way down, walk to the edge and you'll briefly see the spirit of a painter, he'll vanish and the Ash of War will appear in his place. (For example, this skill paired with this talisman can be used to easily aggro the Giant Crow that can be locked on to from the Palace Approach Ledge-Road in Mohgwyn Palace. )\nIf Rainbow Stone Arrow is fired using this weapon skill, the arrows' Rainbow Stone effect almost immediate disappears upon contact, although the arrows still produce the impact sound.\n\nRain of Arrows QUESTION
What strategy is recommended for effectively using Rain of Arrows in combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Utilize the Arrow's Reach Talisman to increase lock-on range and pair the skill with Rainbow Stone Arrow to aggro enemies effectively.
