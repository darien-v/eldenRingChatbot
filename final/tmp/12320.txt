\n\n Law of Regression \n\nSpell Type\nGolden Order Incantations\n\nFP Cost: 55\nSlots Used: 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Heals all ailments and dispels all special effects \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 37 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nLaw of Regression is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Law of Regression dispels most Sorcery and Incantation effects, standard Status Effects, unique effects (such as Death Flare), and effects from Consumables and other items, from all nearby targets, both friendly and hostile. This spell also dispels illusory walls, and has other uses outside of combat (spoiler, see below).\n\nIncantation of the Golden Order fundamentalists.One of the key fundamentals.\nHeals all negative statuses, dispels special effects, and reveals mimicry in all its forms.\nThe fundamentalists describe the Golden Order through the powers of regression and causality. Regression is the pull of meaning; that all things yearn eternally to converge.\n\nWhere to find Law of Regression\n\nPurchase from Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows, after giving either the Golden Order Principia prayerbook.\n\nLaw of Regression Guide\n\nGolden Order Incantation\nStamina cost: 50\nPart of Brother Corhyn's and Goldmask's quest.\nRemoves buildup and full Status Effects (such as Scarlet Rot and Frostbite).\nNullifies any Sorceries or Incantations that are already active, like a Lightning Spear that's mid-flight.\nSince this incantation only has Intelligence requirement, a pure Int build with less than 10 Faith, which excludes them from using most Sacred Seals, can still use this incantation by using Frenzied Flame Seal.\nThis incantation has an additional use (puzzle and story spoiler ahead, highlight to read): At the statue of Radagon in Leyndell, Royal Capital, there is a message that reads "Regression Alone Reveals Secrets." Casting this incantation near the message will cause the statue to change into a statue of Marika, and reveal another message that reads "Radagon is Marika."\n\nGoldmask Progression Guide for Low-Intelligence Characters\nIf your character has a low Intelligence, you may still be able to use the Law of Regression spell to progress Goldmask's quest, by using the following stat-boosting items:\n\nTwinsage Glintstone Crown (+6 Intelligence)\nIntelligence-knot Crystal Tear (+10 Intelligence)\nStargazer Heirloom (+5 Intelligence)\nGodrick's Great Rune (+5 Intelligence, requires Rune Arc)\nGrafted Blade Greatsword skill Oath of Vengeance (+5 Intelligence)\nMarika's Soreseal (+5 Intelligence)\n\nThat adds up to +36 Intelligence, so even if you somehow managed to start with 1 Intelligence, it is still possible to meet the requirement. QUESTION
What are the effects and uses of the Law of Regression incantation in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Heals all ailments, dispels special effects & sorceries, removes illusory walls, and reveals hidden messages at the statue of Radagon in Leyndell.
