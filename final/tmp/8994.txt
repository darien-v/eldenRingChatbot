\n\n Master Lusat \n\nLocation\nSellia Hideaway\n\nDrops\nLusat's Set\n\nMaster Lusat is an NPC in Elden Ring. Master Lusat can be found at Sellia Hideaway.\n\n Once a grand master at the Academy of Raya Lucaria, now exiled and residing near the town of Sellia.\n\nMaster Lusat Location in Elden Ring\n\nMaster Lusat can be found at Sellia Hideaway, The path that leads to him is sealed and can only be opened with a key recevied from Sorceress Sellen: the Sellian Sealbreaker.  Dropping down the crystal holes in Sellia Hideaway will eventually lead you to a tunnel that leads to the Boss. Early in this tunnel the player finds a Stake of Marika. The hole the player has to drop down, is right before this Stake of Marika. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nThis NPC does not move.\nMaster Lusat can not be fought, all attempts at physical and magical attacks simply will not register. As such, he cannot be killed.\n\nMaster Lusat Questline: How to complete Master Lusat's Quest\n\nMaster Lusat is part of Sorceress Sellen Questline.\nMaster Lusat can be interacted with, but he does not speak. He simply hands over the Stars of Ruin sorcery and the prompt to interact with him disappears.\n\nElden Ring Master Lusat Notes & Trivia\n\nAfter Sorceress Sellen transforms (by completing her questline and siding with her in the fight against Witch-Hunter Jerren) you can return to Master Lusat's location where Lusat's Set will be in place of his body.\nWhat is most likely Lusat's face can be seen in portraits found throughout Raya Lucaria Academy, as well as the Olivinus Glintstone Crown, worn by the Glintstone Sorcerers that are a part of the Olivinus Conspectus, which Lusat
