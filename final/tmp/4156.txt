\n\n Millicent's Prosthesis \n\nWeight\n1.6\n\nEffect \n\nBoosts dexterity (+5), raises attack power with successive attacks (4%,6%, 11%) \n\nMillicent's Prosthesis is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Millicent's Prosthesis increases Dexterity by 5, and raises attack power with successive attacks. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nPart of the golden prosthesis used by Millicent.The hand is locked into a fist that once raised a sword aloft.\nBoosts dexterity and raises attack power with successive attacks.\nThe despair of sweet betrayal transformed Millicent from a mere bud into a magnificent flower. And one day, she will be reborn - as a beautiful scarlet valkyrie.\n\nMillicent's Prosthesis Effect in Elden Ring\nMillicent's Prosthesis increases Dexterity by 5, and raises attack power with successive attacks.\n\nIncreases attack power relative to the number of attacks you land. Has three tiers of attack power increase. Tiers decay to lower tiers when not attacking fast enough. Number of hits needed to activate effect varies based on attacks used.\nDecay duration: 1.5 seconds.\nFor each tier, attack power increases by 4%, 6%, and then 11%.\nThe attack power boost also affects the damage of certain Spells.\n\nWhere to find Millicent's Prosthesis in Elden Ring\n\nCan be obtained by killing Millicent at the Windmill Heights Site of Grace after killing the Godskin Apostle there. This is the absolute earliest it can be obtained.\nKilling her at any point after this will also make her drop this talisman, including when you are given the choice at the end of her questline between aiding or challenging her, and choose to challenge her. Upon killing her, you will automatically receive this talisman. during this playthrough until a new NG+ cycle is started.\n\nElden Ring Millicent's Prosthesis Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  1,000\nCan be equipped at the same time as the Prosthesis-Wearer Heirloom
