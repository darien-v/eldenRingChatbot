\n\n Ash of War: Spectral Lance \n\nAffinity\nOccult\n\nSkill\nSpectral Lance\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Spectral Lance is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Spectral Lance provides Occult affinity and the Spectral Lance Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Occult affinity and the following skill:\n"Spectral Lance: Skill of the headless Mausoleum Knights. Hurl a phantasmic spear at foes."\nUsable on polearms (reapers not included).\n\nAsh of War: Spectral Lance Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Spectral Lance:\n\nLocation: Raya Lucaria Academy\nNearest Site of Grace: Church of the Cuckoo\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the large graveyard east of the Church of the Cuckoo site of grace. From the site of grace, exit east into the graveyard and head northeast to find a wooden bridge. Just past the bridge, head slightly southeast towards the cliff edge behind a cluster of gravestones. There should be a lower path you can jump down onto and the scarab should be in view at this point. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nVideo Location\n\nAsh of War: Spectral Lance Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Spectral Lance.\nProvides the Occult affinity, which adds Arcane scaling, decreases all other scaling types. \nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Poison, Blood, Occult\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Spears, Great Spears, Halberds\nDoes not include weapon scaling in its damage calculation, making it deal poor damage in general\nDeals exceptional Stance Damage and has very long range, with damage dropping off at longer ranges\nIs affected by the Arrow's Reach Talisman, but not the Arrow's Sting Talisman\nTracking on this Skill is exceptional, making it very hard to miss, even at very long ranges.\nDoes not apply weapon buffs when cast, but does apply the HP damage of Black Flame Blade if used before cast\n\nSpectral Lance Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Spectral Lance Notes & Tips\n\nThis skill deals significant damage to an enemy’s stance, to the point of breaking the stance of many common enemies in a single hit.\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh QUESTION
How can players obtain the Ash of War: Spectral Lance in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can obtain the Ash of War: Spectral Lance in Elden Ring by defeating a Teardrop Scarab in the large graveyard east of the Church of the Cuckoo site of grace in Raya Lucaria Academy.
