Astrologer Robe is part of the Astrologer Set, and is a Light Weight Chest Armor that boost Physical, Magical defenses and superior Focus Resistance. Chest Armor is worn on the chest slot and provides various types of defensive Stats, as well as changing the appearance of the character while equipped.\n\nRobe fashioned from supple cloth.
