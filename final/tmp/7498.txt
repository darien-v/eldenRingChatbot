\n\n Ash of War: Rain of Arrows \n\nAffinity\nStandard\n\nSkill\nRain of Arrows\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament\n\nAsh of War: Rain of Arrows is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Rain of Arrows provides Standard affinity and the Rain of Arrows Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:\n"Rain of Arrows: Archery skill performed from a low stance. Ready the bow, then fire a burst of arrows into the sky to shower the enemy with projectiles."\nUsable on all bows.\n\nAsh of War: Rain of Arrows Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Rain of Arrows:\n\nCaelid: Appears at edge of a cliff after you've looted the "Redmane" Painting in Sellia, Town of Sorcery. You'll find it down the cliff on a plateau south east of the Eastern Minor Erdtree in Caelid. This is right below Fort Faroth. The Erdtree is located in the Caelid subregion called Dragonbarrow. Once you've made your way down, walk to the edge (without the pillars) and you'll briefly see the spirit of a painter, he'll vanish and the Ash of War will appear in his place. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nIf you use the spiritspring here and jump <just past> the spiritspring (on the very edge of its radius), then double jump just before you hit the little jar on the edge of the cliff, you can safely land at the bottom or close to it.\nFrom the Fort Faroth Grace. Head NE and jump down onto the spiritspring at the base of the Erdtree. If you walk SE to the cliff to the right of the pot and broken pot next to each other, there are a series of roots you can jump down to reach this ledge.\nThere is no need to aggro the strong golem at the West to get this drop, although it can be safely engaged from the top of the last tree before dropping onto the platform. It only drops about 5000 runes and is guarding a corpse with a Rune Arc.\n\nAsh of War: Rain of Arrows Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Rain of Arrows.\nProvides the Standard affinity, which respects the weapon's original scaling and damage.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Light Bows, Bows, Greatbows\nCan be cast even if you don't have 20 FP\n\nRain of Arrows Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Rain of Arrows Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  300 Runes\nThis
