QUESTION
How can Golden Runes be obtained in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Golden Runes in Elden Ring can be obtained by looting corpses across The Lands Between, finding them in randomly spawning skulls with glowing eye sockets, defeating specific enemies like Giant Bats, Land Squirts, Giant Land Squirts, Rats, Giant Rats, Celebrants, Silver Tears, Spirit Jellyfish, Treasurers Nobles, and Chanting Winged Dames, or by shooting down Balloons that appear in specific locations.
