CONTEXT
\n\n Charge Forth \n\n        Quickly charge forward with the armament at the hip, carrying the momentum into a thrust. Hold to cover a greater distance. \n\nCharge Forth is a Skill in Elden Ring. Charge Forth is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible weapons. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Charge Forth in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill of the Golem's Halberd\nDefault skill of the Halberd and Pike\nDefault skill of the Banished Knight's Halberd\nDefault skill of the Winged Spear\nDefault skill of the Lance\nDefault skill of the Inquisitor's Girandole\nDefault skill of the Torchpole\nDefault skill of the Iron Spear\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab that is attached to a ceiling under some Land Squirts. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Charge Forth Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Charge Forth.\nFP Cost: 16 \nDeals 24 stance damage\nThis Skill is Chargeable (charging only increases the range, not the damage, however equipping Godfrey Icon does increase the damage)\nThis Skill can be Parried (only the final thrust can be parried)\nThis skill helps your character perform a piercing charge forward, that can also be guided to keep track of enemies. It is not easy to direct, but can have devastating effects if it connects. \nThe heavy attack version of the charge goes further than regular attacks.\n\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nDirectional control and motion speed have been increased.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able perform the skill has been
