Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are usually composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor. Lusat's Set features prominent dark brilliant blue glintstones on the shoulders attached to a ragged silk cloak, and a huge blue orb atop the crown.\n\nWhere to find Lusat's Set in Elden Ring\nAll four armor pieces can be found after completing Sorceress Sellen's questline and siding with Sellen, return to Master Lusat location in the Sellia Hideaway.
