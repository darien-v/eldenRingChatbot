CONTEXT
\n\n Bloody Slash \n\n        Blood Oath skill granted by the Lord of Blood. From a low stance, coat the blade in your own blood to unleash a rending blood slash in a wide arc. \n\nBloody Slash is a Skill in Elden Ring. Bloody Slash is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and can be applied to swords. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Bloody Slash\n\nDropped by a Godrick Knight fought at the top of the ramparts of Fort Haight. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Bloody Slash Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Bloody Slash.\nFP cost: 6 FP.\nDeals 12 stance damage\nHP cost: Each use damages the player for 0.05 × Max HP + 50 rounded down.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nIf the player uses this skill without sufficient FP, the attack will not be performed and the player will still take damage.\nDespite what the animation may suggest, this is not a melee attack with a buff like flaming strike but a ranged skill with very short range that deals standard damage and inflicts 60 bleed build up. \n\nThis means weapon attack power is ignored, and the damage scales purely via weapon level and arcane. The weapon's status effects, if any, are also ignored.\nIf wanting to apply bleed build up, firing blood blades at close range with a weapon that has bleed build up will apply more bleed build up as long as the weapon hits the target as well.\n\nNote: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nStatus buildup and attack power have been increased.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\n\nBloody Slash Demonstration\n\nBuilds with Bloody Slash\n\nBlackflame Apostle Written
