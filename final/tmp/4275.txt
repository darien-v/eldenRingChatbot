In times of old, when faith and battle went hand in hand, this weapon was created in tandem with the Golden Arrow.\nScales all arrow damage with faith, revealing its true worth when used with holy-infused arrows.\n\nWhere to Find Erdtree Bow in Elden Ring\nThe Erdtree Bow weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nChest on the second floor of the building containing the Erdtree Sanctuary site in Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Elden Ring Map here] From the Erdtree Sanctuary site in Leyndell, Royal Capital, exit the east side of the room. Once outside, climb up the root to the left that circles back into the building's 2nd floor. Once inside, exit the only door and immediately turn left to jump down to a nearby roof. Once on the roof, a broken window can be used to get back inside. QUESTION
How can the Erdtree Bow be obtained in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Erdtree Bow can be obtained by finding a chest on the second floor of the building containing the Erdtree Sanctuary site in Leyndell, Royal Capital.
