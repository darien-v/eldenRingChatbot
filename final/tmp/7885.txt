Stormhawk Deenh summons the spirit of Stormhawk Deenh.\nWarhawks can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Warhawk.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirit of Deenh the Stormhawk.\nSpirit of a fierce hawk that faithfully rendered lifelong service to the old king of Stormveil long ago, when the true storm raged. Its cries embolden its fellows in battle, and the tempestuous winds that encircle it shred through foes.\n\nWhere to find Stormhawk Deenh\nWhere to Find Stormhawk Deenh\n\nChapel of Anticipation: Upon returning to this location later in the game, head to the south wing of the Chapel, onto the roof to find a door that leads to the wooden platforms above the main hall of this building. There is a chest on this platform containing the ashes. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Stormhawk Deenh Guide\n\nConsumes Low FP (47)\nYou must be near a rebirth monument to summon spirits from ashes.
