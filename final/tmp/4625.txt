\n\n Ash of War: Carian Retaliation \n\nAffinity\nMagic\n\nSkill\nCarian Retaliation\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nCarian Retaliation is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Carian Retaliation provides magic affinity and the Carian Retaliation skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\nSee Carian Retaliation for the corresponding incantation.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Magic affinity and the following skill: \n"Carian Retaliation: Swing the shield to dispel incoming sorceries and incantations, transforming the magic into retaliatory glintblades. Can also be used in the same way as a regular parry." \nUsable on small and medium shields.\n\nAsh of War: Carian Retaliation Location in Elden Ring\n\nPurchase from Pidia, Carian Servant: 3000\n\nAsh of War: Carian Retaliation Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Carian Retaliation.\nProvides the Magic affinity, which adds Magic damage and Intelligence scaling, reduces Base damage and Strength / Dexterity scaling.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Magic, Cold\nCan be applied to Small Shields or Medium Shields,\nOnly costs FP when successfully parrying a spell (not a physical attack).\nCarian Retaliation has 4 start-up frames, 6 active frames, and 30 recovery frames (at 30 FPS).\nCreates a blue field in front of your shield. If a magical projectile enters this field, it's absorbed and converted into a glintblade arch above your head. Parrying a regular attack costs no FP, while parrying a magical projectile costs 8 FP.\nWorks on your own projectiles as well, such as the ones from Glintstone Scrap or Wraith-Calling Bell.\nThe damage is fixed at 150 per Glintblade regardless of Affinity or upgraded level\nSee Parrying for detailed information on the mechanic and a comparison of different parry skills.\n\nCarian Retaliation
