\n\n Royal House Scroll \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Give to a learned sorcerer to acquire new sorceries \n\nRoyal House Scroll is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Royal House Scroll Give to a learned sorcerer to acquire new sorceries. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nSorcery scroll of the Carian royal family, the heads of the Academy of Raya Lucaria.\nCan be given to a learned sorcerer to gain access to the following sorceries:\n-Glintblade Phalanx-Carian Slicer\n\nWhere to find Royal House Scroll in Elden Ring\nFrom the Agheel Lake South site of grace, head due east. There should be a minor camp of enemies up a small hill, and among these enemies are sorcerers that stand atop two ruins while pelting you with magic. Climb up the ruin furthest to the east, and right beside the sorcerer there should be a dead body containing the Royal House Scroll. QUESTION
Why do players need to give the Royal House Scroll to specific characters in order to unlock new sorceries in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players need to give the Royal House Scroll to specific characters in order to unlock new sorceries in Elden Ring because these characters, such as Sorceress Sellen, Miriel Pastor of Vows, and Preceptor Seluvis, have the ability to provide access to the sorceries listed on the scroll.
