\n\n Ruinous Ghostflame \n\n        Swing the sword to bathe its blade in ghostflame. \n       The ghostflame adds magic damage to attacks, and also has a bitterly cold bite. \n\nRuinous Ghostflame is a Skill in Elden Ring. Ruinous Ghostflame is a unique skill to the Helphen's Steeple. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Ruinous Ghostflame in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on the Helphen's Steeple.\n\nElden Ring Ruinous Ghostflame Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 20\nAs of Patch 1.07, the ghostflame weapon buff lasts 60 seconds.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nAdds 110 Magic Damage and 80 Frostbite build up to attacks.\n"Ghostflame" does not deal any Fire Damage.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nDuration has been increased from 30 to 60 seconds.\nAttack power has been increased.\nPoise damage of the effect that gives the weapon a magic attribute has been increased from 50 to 110.\nTime between various actions and the activation of the skill has been shortened.\nTime between the skill activation and being able to perform actions has been reduced.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nDark Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Ruinous Ghostflame Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
