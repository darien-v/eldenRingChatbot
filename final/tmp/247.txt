\n\n Road's End Catacombs \n\nCatacombs at the end of the world...\n\nRoad's End Catacombs is a Location in Elden Ring. The Road's End Catacombs is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. South of Revenger's Shack, take a pathway past the Ghost Jellyfishes, keep going until the end and you will reach a door that leads to the Road's End Catacombs.\n\nRoad's End Catacombs Map\nThe Road's End Catacombs are on a pathway by the cliffs west of the southwestern Minor Erdtree in Liurnia. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Road's End Catacombs\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Road's End Catacombs\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nRoot Resin\nGrave Violet\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nGrave Glovewort (2)\nGrave Glovewort (3)\nGhost Glovewort (2)\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nRune Arc\nWatchdog's Staff\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nRaya Lucaria Soldier Ashes\nGlintstone Sorcerer Ashes\n\nElden Ring Road's End Catacombs Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nFanged Imp\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nSpirit-Caller Snail\n\nRoad's End Catacombs Walkthrough\nRoad's End Catacombs is a pretty straightforward dungeon with 7 Illusory Walls. As soon as you enter, you will find the Site of grace. Keep going, and after facing a few Fanged Imps, you will find a lever. Pull it, then keep going, facing the little resistance you will find.\nYou will find several Grave Glovewort (2) to grab. When you reach the end, you'll notice that there is not much to do. Hit the wall at the very end to reveal a secret door. Beware of the Fanged Imps that are hiding on the back of the wall as soon as you enter. \nLoot the final chest at the end of the tunnel to obtain a new Spirit Ashes called Raya Lucaria Soldier Ashes.\nAnother Secret Door\nAs you are making your way back to the entrance, at the bottom of the two sets of stairs leading to the exit, hit the archway next to the stairs to reveal a secret door with some Fanged Imps and the Watchdog's staff and Grave Glovewort (3). Exit the hidden doorway and proceed up the first set of stairs. Once you are before the last set of stairs, head right and hit the second and fourth archway to reveal two secret doors. There is a single Fanged Imp as well as a Ghost Glovewort (2) and a Rune Arc to loot in the room on the left. Continue through the narrow path until you loot the Root Resin. Hit the wall in front of you to reveal another secret door. Keep going to obtain Grave Glovewort (2). Hit the wall to unveil ANOTHER secret door, which leads to a boss fight, the Spirit-Caller Snail. Defeat it to obtain the Glintstone Sorcerer Ashes and 3000 Runes.\n\nElden Ring Road's End Catacombs Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy
