Its denizens know nothing but the fiery frenzy burning within them.\n\nFrenzied Flame Village is a Location in Elden Ring. The Frenzied Flame Village is found in the north east part of the Liurnia of the Lakes region. To get to the village, head south from the Grand Lift of Dectus Site of Grace and through the mountain pass.\n\nFrenzied Flame Village Map\nFrenzied Flame Village is typically accessed from the north of the eastern landmass, riding south from the Grand Lift of Dectus.  [Elden Ring Map Village Walkthrough\nDirectly to the north of the Village is a crumbling tower with six Frenzied Villagers at the top.  Every few seconds, a ball of fire appears above them and begins to inflict Madness on you if there is nothing solid blocking your line of sight to it.  Killing the villagers at the top of the tower will stop this flame from appearing.  In order to reach the top of the tower, you can either make your way to the base of the tower and climb the ladders to the top floor, or ride the wind gust that is on a lower cliff directly to the north of the tower.  The path leading to this wind is west of the tower, tucked between two small cliffs.  Note that Madness will continue to be applied to you while you are on top of the tower as long as any of the villagers are still alive.  This can be avoided by taking cover one floor down from the top floor, then climbing the ladder as the flame begins to fade.  \nNearby, players can obtain an incantation Dropped by a Teardrop
