QUESTION
Why is Giantsflame Take Thee considered one of the highest damage ranged attacks in Elden Ring despite its very high FP cost?

ANSWER
Giantsflame Take Thee is considered one of the highest damage ranged attacks in Elden Ring due to its powerful knockback against medium-sized enemies, large AoE when charged, increased damage when charged, and its ability to make quick work of large groups of enemies.
