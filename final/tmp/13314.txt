QUESTION
Why are Ronin's Gauntlets considered part of the Ronin's Set Armor Set and what specific defensive properties do they provide in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ronin's Gauntlets are part of the Ronin's Set Armor Set because they provide Dmg Negation, Physical defense against Strike, Slash, and Pierce, Magic, Fire, Lightning, and Holy resistances, as well as Immunity, Robustness, Focus, Vitality, and Poise in Elden Ring.
