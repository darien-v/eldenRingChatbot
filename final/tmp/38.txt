CONTEXT
\n\n Mending Rune of the Fell Curse \n\nType\nGreat Rune\n\nEffect \n\n        Can be used to mend the shattered Elden Ring. \n\nMending Rune of the Fell Curse is a Great Rune in Elden Ring. Unlike most other Great Runes, it can't be equipped. Its only effect is to alter the ending of the game.\n\nLoathsome rune gestated by the Dung Eater.Used to restore the fractured Elden Ring when brandished by the Elden Lord.\nThe reviled curse will last eternally, and the world's children, grandchildren, and every generation hence, will be its pustules.\nIf Order is defiled entirely, defilement is defilement no more, and for every curse, a cursed blessing.\n\nWhere to Find Mending Rune of the Fell Curse\nAwarded to the player by Dung Eater after following his questline and giving him 5 Seedbed Curse.\n\nMending Rune of the Fell Curse Use\nGrants access to an alternative ending after defeating the final boss. QUESTION
How does the use of the Mending Rune of the Fell Curse alter the ending of Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Grants access to an alternative ending after defeating the final boss.
