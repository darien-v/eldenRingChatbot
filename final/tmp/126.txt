\n\n Swamp Lookout Tower \n\nA long-abandoned lookout tower, that now only oversees the poisonous swamp that plagues Caelid\n\nSwamp Lookout Tower is a Location in Elden Ring. The Swamp Lookout Tower is found in Caelid, southwest of Sellia, Town of Sorcery. It can only be reached after unlocking the seal to the large doorway in the very back of Sellia. Once this is achieved, you can follow the Sellia Backstreets towards the hills east of the town where many graveyards and the Church of the Plague can be found. Continue along south on these hills until you reach the Sellia Gateway. You can cross through the upper portion of the gateway towards the western hill where the tower can be found. \nAt the tower, you can find the Eternal Darkness spell on a corpse inside the jail cell.
