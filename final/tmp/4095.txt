QUESTION
Why is the Ritual Sword Talisman specifically designed to raise attack power by 10% only when HP is at maximum in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Ritual Sword Talisman is designed to raise attack power by 10% only when HP is at maximum in Elden Ring to incentivize players to maintain full health for a strategic advantage in combat.
