\n\n Unalloyed Gold Needle \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Snapped intricate needle of unalloyed gold \n\nUnalloyed Gold Needle is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Unalloyed Gold Needle is an intricately crafted needle, snapped in half, but it can be repaired by Sage Gowry. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nAn intricately crafted needle of unalloyed gold. Once snapped in half, it has been repaired by Sage Gowry.\nA ritual implement crafted to ward away the meddling of outer gods, it is thought capable of forestalling the incurable rotting sickness.\n"Now, all you need to do is deliver the needle to Millicent, convalescing in the church atop the cliff just beyond Sellia." \n\nWhere to find Unalloyed Gold Needle in Elden Ring\nThe Unalloyed Gold Needle can be found in the following location:\n\nDropped by the mini-boss Commander O’Neil located in a clearing of Aeonia Swamp in eastern Caelid. This location is southwest of Sellia. [Elden Ring Map Link] \n\nElden Ring Unalloyed Gold Needle Use\n\nUnalloyed Gold Needle can be used to ward away the meddling of Outer Gods\nReturning the Unalloyed Gold Needle to the Scarlet Aeonia left after Malenia, Blade of Miquella's fight will grant the player Miquella's Needle and a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. \n\nThe Unalloyed Gold Needle that grants Miquella's Needle only works after finishing Millicent's questline. Trying to return the repaired needle to the Scarlet Aeonia without giving it to Millicent (and thus starting her questline proper) won't work.\nAfter finishing Millicent's questline, and getting the Unalloyed Gold Needle back, the description changes to: An intricately crafted needle of unalloyed gold. Removed by Millicent from her flesh. Bears no trace of befouled blood, but is faintly moist with dew."There is something I must return to Malenia. The dignity, the sense of self, that allowed her to resist the call of the scarlet rot."\n\nElden Ring Unalloyed Gold Needle Trivia\n\nThe repair carried out by Gowry is not perfect, since a reddish material is used to reattach it.\n\nUnalloyed Gold Needle Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Key Items\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell Bearing  ♦  Academy Glintstone Key  ♦  Academy Scroll  ♦  Alexander's QUESTION
What is the significance of the Unalloyed Gold Needle in Elden Ring and how does it impact the gameplay and story progression?

ANSWER
The Unalloyed Gold Needle in Elden Ring is a ritual implement crafted to ward off outer gods, capable of forestalling sickness, and when returned, grants Miquella's Needle and a Smithing Stone, impacting gameplay through these effects and progression by being part of Millicent's questline.
