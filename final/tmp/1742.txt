Blue Silver Set is a medium weight set that is worn by the Albinauric Wolfback Archers in the Consecrated Snowfield. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Blue Silver Set in Elden Ring\n\nAlbinauric Wolfback Archers found in the Consecrated Snowfield, as well as the Ordina Gaol, drop the Blue Silver Set pieces.\nOne that is easily farmed can be found shortly north of the Consecrated Snowfield site of grace. When you hear the wolf howl, go towards it and past the wolf, and you'll find the archer.\nThree unmounted archers can be found south-west of the Ordina, Liturgical Town site of grace, on the cliffs and rock formation. They cover each other with arrow fire, but are susceptible to any mist type spell such as Night Maiden's Mist as they cannot move out of the cloud, while the player hides from the arrow behind cover.\nLatenna drops the full set if she is killed at the Slumbering Wolf's Shack.\n\nBlue Silver Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nBlue Silver Mail Hood\nBlue Silver Mail Armor\nBlue Silver Bracelets\nBlue Silver Mail Skirt\n\nBlue Silver Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Blue Silver Mail Armor can be altered to the Blue Silver Mail Armor (Altered) to remove the cloak.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Blue Silver Set important or sought after in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Blue Silver Set in Elden Ring is important due to its balanced damage negation attributes, high immunity and robustness, making it a valuable armor set for players seeking effective defense and survivability.
