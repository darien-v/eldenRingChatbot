Goldmask's Rags is part of Goldmask's Set Armor Set, and leaves little to the imagination. Goldmask's Rags is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nWorn rags that just barely cover the body.\nLord Goldmask felt no draw to the vain excess of clothing; what use is finery to one who seeks greater brilliance?\n\nWhere to find Goldmask's Rags in Elden Ring\nThe Goldmask's Rags Chest Armor can be found at:\n\nGoldmask's Rags can all be found on Goldmask's body after the completion of Brother Corhyn's questline.
