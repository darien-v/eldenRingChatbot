\n\n Golden Seed \n\nEffect \n\n        Increases Sacred Flask's number of uses \n\nScaling \n\n        - \n\nFP Cost\n-\n\nGolden Seed is an Upgrade Material and optional Keepsake in Elden Ring. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting from specific areas, dropped by an Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nA golden seed, found at the base of an illusory tree.\nIncreases a Sacred Flask's number of uses.(Can be used after resting at a site of grace.)\nWhen the Elden Ring was shattered, these seeds flew from the Erdtree, scattering across the various lands, as if life itself knew that its end has come.\n\nElden Ring Golden Seed Guide\nGolden Seeds are very special items that allow the user to upgrade the number of Crimson and Cerulean Flask charges (but not the amount of Flask of Wondrous Physick uses).\n\nYou need 1 Seed for upgrades increasing to 6 total Flasks.\nYou need 2 Seeds for upgrades increasing to 8 total Flasks.\nYou need 3 Seeds for upgrades increasing to 10 total Flasks.\nYou need 4 Seeds for upgrades increasing to 12 total Flasks.\nYou need 5 Seeds for upgrades increasing to 14 total Flasks.\n\nTherefore, a total of 30 Seeds are needed to reach the maximum number of Flasks.\nYou can get far more than 30 Seeds in your first play-through (without going into New Game Plus) so you don't have to find all of them to upgrade your flasks to max level.\n\nWhere to find Golden Seed in Elden Ring: All Locations Guide\nGolden Seed locations: Golden Seeds are found near errant golden branches of the Erdtree:\n\nLimgrave\n\nOne (1) can be found In Stormhill, on the path from Stormgate to the Stormhill Shack. [Map link]\nOne (1) can be found in Stormhill Shack where Roderika was sitting if the player rests at any site of grace in Liurnia of the Lakes, or by giving her Chrysalids' Memento.\nOne (1) can be found in Mistwood, right in front of the entrance to Fort Haight. QUESTION
Where can Golden Seeds be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Golden Seeds can be found in various locations such as Stormhill, Mistwood, Weeping Peninsula, Stormveil Castle, Academy Gate Town, Caelid, Altus Plateau, Lake of Rot, Mountaintops of the Giants, Crumbling Farum Azula, Mohgwyn Palace, and many more, totaling to over 30 locations.
