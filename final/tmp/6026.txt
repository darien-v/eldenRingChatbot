QUESTION
How can the defensive properties of the Beast Champion Helm in Elden Ring protect a player in various combat scenarios?

ANSWER
The Beast Champion Helm in Elden Ring provides damage negation and resistance stats like VS Strike, VS Slash, VS Pierce, Magic, Fire, and Ligt, along with immunity, robustness, focus, vitality, and poise, which help protect the player's head in combat scenarios.
