\n\n Mistwood Ruins \n\nConcealed by the thriving growth of vegetation, these ruins are kept out of sight by the veil of nature itself. But there's more to it than what you see.\n\nMistwood Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Mistwood Ruins are found in Mistwood, Eastern Limgrave, hidden among vast vegetation. The remnants look shattered and forgotten and feature an underground cellar.\n\nMistwood Ruins Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Mistwood Ruins  \n\nBlaidd - His howling can be heard around Mistwood Ruins. Speak to Merchant Kale to begin his storyline.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Mistwood Ruins\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nList Gatherable items here\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSmithing Stone [2]\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nAxe Talisman\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this location\n\nElden Ring Mistwood Ruins Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nLesser Runebear\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses found in this location.\n\nMistwood Ruins Walkthrough\nYou can discover the path to Mistwood Ruins by interacting with an antique spectral tree located south of Third Church of Marika, alongside the main road. Upon examination, grace will manifest as a golden human spirit that will wander without a clear destination. The spirit often disappears, but its footsteps can be traced not to lose track of it. Finally, the grace manifestation will arrive to the hidden ruins and fade away.\nOne questline begins in Mistwood Ruins. Players may come across some howling. If you look up at the taller parts of the ruins, you may come across Blaidd. You will have to speak to Merchant Kale in the Church of Elleh to continue this questline. When interacting with Blaidd, make sure you have no wolf attire on as this will make him hostile towards you and may affect the progress of your quest. \n\nElden Ring Mistwood Ruins Gallery and
