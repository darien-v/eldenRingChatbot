\n\n Slug \n\nLocation\nThe Shaded CastleAltus PlateauRoyal OutskirtsSubterranean Shunning-GroundsCastle Sol\n\nDrops\n 88 - 215 RunesStrip of White Flesh\n\nSlug is a wildlife creature in Elden Ring. Slugs are large larvae-like creatures that can spit slime to slow down players. Slugs are slow moving and can be found all around the poison covered grounds of The Shaded Castle.\n\nElden Ring Slug Locations\nWhere to find Slugs:\n\nThe Shaded Castle\nAltus Plateau: in the forested basin with the Minor Erdtree (Altus Plateau) in the middle, there are Slugs in almost half of the structures that make up Woodfolk Ruins, as well as a shallow pond directly south of the Road of Iniquity Side Path Site of Grace, in-between broken bridge segments, with a Lesser Wormface sitting nearby.\nRoyal Outskirts: Slugs drop from the walls pf\nSubterranean Shunning-Grounds: in the sewer pipes that lead to and away from the Revenant, there are many Slugs stuck all over the insides of the pipes.\nCastle Sol: in the first building when keeping to the left wall after entering the Castle.\n\nElden Ring Slug Drops\n\n 88 - 215 Runes\nStrip of White Flesh (4% base chance)\n\nSlug Notes & Tips\n\nIn the basin around the Minor Erdtree (Altus Plateau), the slug's spit attack is off-white in color and has the effect of slowing the player, as though they were traversing a swamp. The player can not run, nor jump a long distance, and can only roll as they would when burdened with a Heavy Equip Load.\nLike when traversing a swamp, however, riding Torrent makes the slowing effect irrelevant.\nWhilst the slowing effect is active, spittle can be seen dripping and rolling down the torso of the player. Unlike accumulations, however, Soap can not remove the slowing slimey spittle.\nIn every other area, the Slug's spit attack is a light, cloudy maroon-red color and just does physical damage.\nThere is another type of slug called a Burning Slug.\n\nElden Ring Slug Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone Cluster  ♦  Albinauric  ♦  Albinauric Wolfback QUESTION
Why do Slugs in Elden Ring have different effects with their spit attack depending on the area they are encountered in?

ANSWER
Slugs in Elden Ring have different effects with their spit attack based on the area due to environmental factors affecting the slowing or physical damage they inflict on players.
