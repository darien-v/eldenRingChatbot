\n\n Land Squirt Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 0\nHP Cost 240\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons three land squirts \n\nLand Squirt Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Land Squirt Ashes summons three land squirts using HP instead of FP.\nLand Squirts can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Land Squirt.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of three land squirts Summoning consumes HP rather than FP.  These spirits appear some distance from the summoner. The firm, fleshy tubes of the creatures remain rooted to the spot, spraying poisonous liquid over their surroundings.\n\nWhere to find Land Squirt Ashes\nWhere to Find Land Squirt Ashes\n\nFound at a grave with several groups of land squirts nearby on the east shore of Liurnia Lake. To reach it, start at the Gate Town Bridge site of grace, and travel north along the shore.
