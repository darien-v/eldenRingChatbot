\n\n Siluria's Woe \n\n        Thrust the weapon in a spiraling motion, surrounding it in a vortex of wind. \n       Charged attacks have the power to blow away enemies and can fire the tornado forwards.  \n\nSiluria's Woe is a Skill in Elden Ring. Siluria's Woe is a special skill only available for an individual weapon, and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Siluria's Woe in Elden Ring\n\nThis Skill can only be acquired by equipping Siluria's Tree.\n\nElden Ring Siluria's Woe Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill.\nFP Cost: 25\nDeals 27 stance damage\nThis Skill is Chargeable\nDamage type is Physical Damage and Holy Damage.\nDamage scales with weapon upgrade level and AR (which for Silura's Tree scales with Strength, Dexterity and Faith).\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nMotion speed, attack power and poise during activation have been increased.\nDamage detection to the weapon part has been added.\nThe projectile now penetrates enemies and some objects when charged.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Siluria's Woe Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
