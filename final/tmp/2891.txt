\n\n Kukri \n\nEffect \n\n        Throw at enemies to inflict damage and build up onset of blood loss \n\nScaling \n\nStr A \n\nDex S \n\nArc C \n\nFP Cost\n-\n\nKukri is a Consumable Item in Elden Ring. Most Consumables grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects or applying temporary buffs and debuffs, while others can be thrown at enemies to deal damage.\n\nA large knife used for throwing. Its curved blade is sharp enough to slice flesh cleanly.\nThrow at enemies to inflict damage and build up onset of blood loss.\nThis heavy throwing weapon is unsuitable for swift attacks, but is able to inflict heavy damage.\n\nWhere to find Kukri in Elden Ring\n\nCan be bought from the Nomadic Merchant at the eastern side of Weeping Peninsula, for  60 Runes. [Map Link] Video Location\nLiurnia of the Lakes: x6 can be looted off a corpse behind the Glintstone Dragon Smarag. [Map Link]\nStormveil Castle. 5x from Vulgar Militiamen.\n\nElden Ring Kukri Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 30 Kukri.\nYou can store up to 600 Kukri.\nSell Value:  10 Runes\nOnly bleed buildup is affected by the Arcane scaling, not physical damage.\nThe throwing animation is slower than all other similarly thrown items.\nThe projectile travels considerably farther than all other similarly thrown items.\nThis is one of a handful of unexpected things whose range is increased from wearing the Arrow's Reach Talisman.\nThe kukri does respectable bleed buildup and a bit of stance damage, making it a good option for bleed builds to keep pressure up on a boss that's hard to approach, though the slow throwing speed makes it harder to use.\n\nELDEN RING KUKRI DAMAGE\nDamage values tested on an enemy with low QUESTION
Where can you find the Kukri in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
