\n\n Lesser Kindred of Rot (Pests) \n\nLocation\nSellia Crystal TunnelCaelid Waypoint RuinsGrand CloisterSeethewater Cave\n\nDrops\n 612 - 6270 RunesPest's GlaiveAeonian ButterflyFaded Erdleaf Flower\n\nLesser Kindred of Rot (Pests) is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. Lesser Kindred of Rot are beings that resemble a large, humanoid centipedes wielding large glaives. They are lethal in combat and proficient in melee combat. Kindred of Rot are the servants of the Goddess of Rot, and due to their appearance and nature, they have been forsaken.\nThe spirit of a Kindred of Rot can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Kindred of Rot Ashes.\n\n Pale pests who crawl through the lands afflicted by scarlet rot; the abandoned children of the goddess.\n"Rot for the scarlet goddess. O scarlet blossoms, flourish in distant lands, and return to us, the unwanted children."\n\nElden Ring Lesser Kindred of Rot (Pests) Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Kindred of Rot (Pests):\n\nSellia Crystal Tunnel\nCaelid Waypoint Ruins\nChurch of the Plague\nGrand Cloister\nSeethewater Cave\nElphael, Brace of the Haligtree\n\nElden Ring Lesser Kindred of Rot (Pests) Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 612 - 6270\n\nPest's Glaive\n4.00%\nN/A\n\nFaded Erdleaf Flower\n15.00%\nN/A\n\nAeonian Butterfly\n1.50%\nN/A\n\nLesser Kindred of Rot (Pests) Notes & Tips\n\nFor more info on the Kindred of Rot Bossfight see here.\nTheir Pest Threads attack have VERY high tracking, and can quite literally turn on a dime to hit you.\nSuspectible to Fire, Hemorrhage and Frostbite.\nDeals Standard and Pierce Damage.\nCan be buffed, if someone in their vicinity is inflicted by either Scarlet Rot or Poison.\nMany of them are found in the Grand Cloister, praying at the shrine there.
