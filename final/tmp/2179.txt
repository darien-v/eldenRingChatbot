Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. \n\nFirefly that gives off a silvery light. Material used in crafting items. Found near bodies of cold water untouched by sunlight. The light of fireflies is believed to have an alluring magic.
