QUESTION
Why are Smithing Stone [7] upgrade materials considered rare and uncommon in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Smithing Stone [7] upgrade materials are considered rare and uncommon in Elden Ring due to their scarcity and the special abilities they possess, such as reinforcing armaments up to +21, which is a higher level upgrade compared to other materials.
