\n\n Maleigh Marais, Shaded Castle Castellan \n\nLocation\nFields west of The Shaded Castle\n\nDrops\n4,800  RunesAntspur RapierMarais MaskMarais Robe\n\nMaleigh Marais, Shaded Castle Castellan is a Hostile NPC in Elden Ring. Maleigh Marais can be found while roaming outside The Shaded Castle. As a Hostile NPC, Maleigh Marais will chase and attack you on sight. Their name is displayed in bright red above their health bar, similar to NPC Invaders.\n\n The sons of House Marais are all sickly born. Little wonder that Maleigh Marais would be so beguiled by the beautiful and fierce goddess who was born into rot.\n\nElden Ring Maleigh Marais, Shaded Castle Castellan Locations\nMaleigh Marais, Shaded Castle Castellan can be found at the following location:\n\nThe Shaded Castle: In the rocky fields west of the castle.
