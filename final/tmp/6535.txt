QUESTION
Why is Fia's Mist spell effective only against the Tarnished and not other enemies or NPCs?

ANSWER
Fia's Mist spell is effective only against the Tarnished because every enemy, NPC, and boss, except NPC invading phantoms, have protection against death status effects.
