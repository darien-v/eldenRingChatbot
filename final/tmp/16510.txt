QUESTION
How do you effectively combat a Deer in Elden Ring considering its health, poise, absorptions, and resistances?

ANSWER
Deer in Elden Ring have 55 HP, 0 poise, and high % absorptions to physical and elemental damage, with resistance to various status effects; use elemental damage, avoid physical attacks, and be mindful of status buildup to effectively combat them.
