CONTEXT
\n\n Flaming Strike \n\n        Skill that emits flame in a wide frontward arc. Follow up with a strong attack to perform a lunging, sweeping strike. This will also coat the armament in fire. \n\nFlaming Strike is a Skill in Elden Ring. Flaming Strike is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War that can be used on many melee armaments. Updated to Patch 1.08\n\nHow to get Flaming Strike\n\nRedmane Castle, dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in a small graveyard along the south cliffside behind the castle. From the front gate, hug right along the cliff towards the ballista, going up and past the vampire bats. There will be a small gap in the cliff that must be jumped across to get to the graveyard.\n\nElden Ring Flaming Strike Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Flaming Strike.\nFP Cost: 4 on use, 10 on follow up. \n\nThis totals to a 14 FP cost.\n\nDeals 15 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill's second attack can be Parried\nUpon activation, the user spreads flames with their left hand in front of them. \n\nInputting the strong attack button allows a follow up, where the user swings his armament (weapon) from left to right and coats it in fire. This counts as a weapon buff.\nThe buff lasts 40 seconds, and adds 90 flat fire damage to the weapon.\n\nThe weapon buff from the follow up can be applied regardless of the affinity of the weapon.\nIf used without FP: \n\nNo flames come out. However, some lesser enemies can still be staggered if hit by your left hand.\nFollow up does not coat weapon in fire.\nFollow up slash deals reduced damage.\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nDuration has been increased from 20 to 40 seconds.\nAttack power that grants the weapon a fire attribute has been increased from 65 to 90.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\n\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, hitbox duration of the flame portion of the Flaming Strike Weapon Art has been reduced.\n\nBlasphemous Herald Written Guide\n\nFlaming Strike Demonstration\n\nDragonscale Daimyo Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's QUESTION
How can one successfully perform the Flaming Strike skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To successfully perform the Flaming Strike skill in Elden Ring, one must first activate the skill by spreading flames with the left hand in front of them, followed by inputting the strong attack button to swing the weapon from left to right, coating it in fire as a weapon buff.
