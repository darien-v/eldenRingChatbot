CONTEXT
\nTools, or Reusable Tools, in Elden Ring can be used to damage Enemies, provide simple communication in Multiplayer, and even weaken Bosses. In-game, Consumables are also referred to as tools, but this wiki chose to refer to reusable tools simply as "Tools" to avoid the less recognizable name of "Reusables."
