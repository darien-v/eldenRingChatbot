[Elden Ring Interactive Map Link]\n\nFrenzyflame Stone Use in Elden Ring\nFrenzyflame Stone uses FP to continuously heal chaos followers.\n\nHeals any frenzied flame alignment creatures, including enemies, in the radius of the stone by 35 health per second. \n\nFrenzied Flame creatures include player characters who have become the Lord of Frenzied Flame and any non-human enemies able to use Frenzied Flame Incantations.\nHeals Nomad Ashes, but not Mimic Tear Ashes.\nUsing Miquella's Needle removes this alignment from player characters.\n\nAny humans in the radius not afflicted by the flame of frenzy, including sane players and all human NPCs, gain a slight buildup of  Madness while in the stone's radius. \n\nMadness damage does not reveal your position.\nDoes not cause madness in friendly NPCs.\n\nHealing effects stack with Warming Stones for a total of 60 health per second.\nLasts for 30 seconds.\n\nElden Ring Frenzyflame Stone Crafting Guide\nTo craft Frenzyflame Stone you
