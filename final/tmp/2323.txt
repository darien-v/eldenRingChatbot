\n\n Vargram the Raging Wolf \n\nLocation\nLeyndell, Royal Capital\n\nDrops\n Raging Wolf HelmRaging Wolf ArmorRaging Wolf GauntletsRaging Wolf Greaves \n\nVargram the Raging Wolf is an Enemy in Elden Ring. He is one of the assassination targets assigned to you by the Volcano Manor. You can find the summon sign to invade his world in Leyndell, Royal Capital. Other Invadable NPCs can be found here.\n\n One of the first Tarnished to arrive at the Roundtable, Vargram was named after the white wolf pelt on his helm. \nAccording to the old legends, wolves are the shadows of the Empyrean. Vargram aspired to such a state himself.\n\nElden Ring Vargram the Raging Wolf Locations\nWhere to Find Vargram the Raging Wolf:\n\nHis invasion sign can be found close to the Fortified Manor, First Floor Site of Grace. Elden Ring Map here\nHe will be assisted by Errant Sorcerer Wilhelm.\nYou will be assisted by Recusant Bernahl.\nMake sure to invade him before completing Crumbling Farum Azula or eliminating Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy, or his sign will be inaccessible. \n\nElden Ring Vargram the Raging Wolf Drops\n\nRaging Wolf Helm\nRaging Wolf Armor\nRaging Wolf Gauntlets\nRaging Wolf Greaves\n\nVargram the
