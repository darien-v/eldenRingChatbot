Godrick Foot Soldier Set is one of six variations of the Foot Soldier armor, and is worn by the foot soldiers loyal to Godrick. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Godrick Foot Soldier Set in Elden Ring\n\nAll pieces of the Godrick Foot Soldier Set drop from Godrick Foot Soldiers found in most areas of Limgrave.\nThe Foot Soldier Gauntlets and Foot Soldier Greaves drop from only Godrick Foot Soldiers, even though they are worn by every Foot Soldiers.\nTwo Godrick Foot Soldiers can be found by a campfire on the west side of the bridge near Agheel Lake North Site of Grace, where the Night's Cavalry is found in Limgrave.\n\nGodrick Foot Soldier Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nFoot Soldier Cap\nChain-Draped Tabard\nFoot Soldier Gauntlets\nFoot Soldier Greaves\n\nGodrick Foot Soldier Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
