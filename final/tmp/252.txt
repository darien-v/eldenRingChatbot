\n\n Ringleader's Evergaol \n\nFound on the Moonlight Altar. Inside awaits a notorious assassin, imprisoned for the act of slaying a demigod.\n\nRingleader's Evergaol is a Location in Elden Ring. The Ringleader's Evergaol is found at the Moonlight Altar plateau, high above in the southwest Liurnia of the Lakes. The Moonlight Altar is accessible only after making significant progress in Ranni's questline, up to the point where you defeat Astel, Naturalborn of the Void in the Grand Cloister and gain access to the lift in the Deep Ainsel Well, which leads up to the plateau. Players can reach the Ringleader's Evergaol by traveling northwest from the Moonlight Altar site of grace, past the Moonfolk Ruins and down some cliffs.\n\nRingleader's Evergaol Map\nRingleader's Evergaol's is on the northwestern tip of the Moonlight Altar sub-region in southwest Liurnia. [Elden Ring Map Link].\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Ringleader's Evergaol\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Ringleader's Evergaol\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nNo Gatherables at this location\n\nUpgrade Materials\nOn the pathway towards Chelona's Rise:\n\nSmithing Stone (7)\nSmithing Stone (8)\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nNo Equipment or Magic at this location\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nBlack Knife Tiche (Alecto, Black Knife Ringleader drop)\n\nElden Ring Ringleader's Evergaol Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nBear\nDeer\nCrystalian\nLesser Red Wolf of Radagon\nTurtle\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nAlecto, Black Knife Ringleader\n\nRingleader's Evergaol Walkthrough\nThis Evergaol does not require a Stonesword Key to unlock as the portal mechanism in the center is active as soon as you enter the area. Within, you can challenge Alecto, Black Knife Ringleader, a remarkably agile and aggressive assassin. Upon defeat, Alecto drops the Black Knife Tiche spirit ashes as well as 80,000  Runes.\nIf you have inspected the statue's book outside of Chelona's Rise in the south, one of the three Giant Ghostly Turtles required to unlock the seal can be found flying high above in the east of the Evergaol. \nAlong the long stretch of land between Ringleader's Evergaol and Chelona's Rise, you will find a path laden with crystals and several non-boss variants of Crystalians guarding the area. Past this pathway is a large open field with three gazebos by the cliffs. There is a Lesser Red Wolf of Radagon to be fought here which drops 4,518  Runes upon death and does not respawn.
