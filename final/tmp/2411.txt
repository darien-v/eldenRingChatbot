Viridian Amber Medallion raises maximum Stamina by 11/13/15%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA medallion with viridian amber inlaid. Boosts maximum stamina.\nThe Erdtree's old sap becomes amber, treasured as the most precious of jewels in the age of Godfrey, the first Elden Lord.\nA primordial life energy resides inside. \n\nViridian Amber Medallion Effect in Elden Ring\nViridian Amber Medallion raises maximum Stamina by 11/13/15%.\n\nViridian Amber Medallion Location in Elden Ring\nBase talisman: Tombsward Cave, a small dungeon in the Weeping Peninsula: Located southeast of the Fourth Church of Marika. The medallion is obtained after defeating the boss, Miranda the Blighted Bloom. [Map link]\n+1 Variant: Altus Plateau, south of the Outer Wall Battleground Site of Grace: You will hear Margit whisper, "I see you, Tarnished..."  to you before appearing on the road. Defeat him again and it will drop. [Map link] \n +2 Variant: Miquella's Haligtree: The closest site of grace is the Haligtree Town Plaza. Go south towards the roof with a dead body on it. For reference, the dead body is two stories above the talisman. Continue southeast, and jump down once. Jump across the gap, and then do an immediate 180 turn and make a running jump into the building below. It'll be in a chest guarded by Scarlet Rot Zombies.
