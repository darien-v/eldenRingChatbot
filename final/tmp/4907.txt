Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nThe bell bearing of a miner who once dug for somber smithing stone.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to the following items:\n- Somber Smithing Stone [5]- Somber Smithing Stone [6]\n\nWhere to find Somberstone Miner's Bell Bearing [3] in Elden Ring\nFound in a corpse just outside the First Church of Marika in Mountaintops of the Giants.
