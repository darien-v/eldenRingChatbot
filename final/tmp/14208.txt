Land of Reeds Armor is part of the Land of Reeds Set, and is a Heavy Weight armor that that provides a good balance of defensive properties and boosts Immunity and Vitality Resistances. Land of Reeds Armor is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nArmor made from strips of iron fastened together. Worn by warriors of the Land of Reeds.
