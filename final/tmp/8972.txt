\n\n Albus \n\nLocation\nVillage of the Albinaurics, Liurnia of the Lakes\n\nRole\nGives Key Item\n\nVoiced by\nMartyn Ellis\n\nAlbus is an NPC in Elden Ring. Albus is an Albinauric who is trying to hide from the calamity that befell Village of the Albinaurics in Liurnia of the Lakes. He has an important item that he would like you to protect\n\nPlease, no. Dear me! I haven't a clue. No secrets lie with me, not a one.\n\nAlbus Location in Elden Ring\nAlbus can be found disguised as a pot by following the path up from the Village of the Albinaurics site of grace in western Liurnia of the Lakes. You must hit the pot located past the Depraved Perfumer to get him to show up. Then talk to him twice. Map Link\n\nThis NPC does not move\nThis NPC can be fought\nIf defeated, drops: Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right)\n\nAlbus Questline: How to complete Albus' Quest\nThis is an extremely important NPC. After you reveal his true form, talk to him again to trigger a second dialogue and obtain Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right). This item will create a pair with Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left) found in Mountaintops of the Giants and give you access to an entire secret area in the northern lands.\n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: Albus\nIntroduction\n\nPlease, no. Dear me!. I haven't a clue. No secrets lie with me, not a one. Oh please, leave me be.\n\nTalk Again\n\nWait, then, you're not one of them? Well, what a relief... Oh, goodness me.\nI am Albus. An Albinauric, as you can see. We are finished. The whole village is finished. The cursemongers have destroyed everything. Not one that remains has their wits about them\nI beg you. Would you look after this medallion?\nYou must keep it out of the cursemonger's hands.\nAnd if you should meet the young Albinauric Latenna, then please, give it to her.\n\nSmall Talk\n\nA chosen land awaits us Albinaurics. The medallion is the key that leads to the city. It's only a quaint treasure, for we who cannot make the journey. But for dear Latenna, it is needed. To fulfill her purpose.\nMy legs will soon fade, and with them, my life. Alas, this is the immovable fate of all Albinaurics...\n\nWhen attacked\n\nWe are Albinaurics. We have caused you no harm!\n(On player kill) I will never give this up. Not to your kind.\nYou merciless brutes.
