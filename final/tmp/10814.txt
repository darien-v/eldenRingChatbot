\n\n Loretta's Greatbow \n\nSpell Type\nLoretta's Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 24\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Fires great arrow from a magic greatbow \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 26 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nLoretta's Greatbow is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Loretta's Greatbow spell creates a bow that fires a projectile. Can be charged to further increase damage. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSorcery used by Royal Knight Loretta.Creates a magic greatbow and fires a great arrow.Charging enhances potency. Hold to keep the great arrow nocked.It is said that the bow was Loretta's favored weapon.\n\nWhere to find Loretta's Greatbow\nWhere to find Loretta's Greatbow:\n\n Caria Manor: Dropped by Royal Knight Loretta upon defeat.
