QUESTION
How can players obtain the Mausoleum Gauntlets in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Mausoleum Gauntlets can be obtained by farming Mausoleum Soldiers who patrol areas around Wandering Mausoleums in East Liurnia of the Lakes near the East Gate Bridge Trestle site.
