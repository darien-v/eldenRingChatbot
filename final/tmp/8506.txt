QUESTION
Where can Fire Arrows be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Fire Arrows can be found:
- As starting equipment for the Samurai Class
- East of Stormhill Shack, over the corpse of a dead woman
- On a corpse atop a siege tower in Mt. Gelmir
- In a cave after fighting the giant crab near Castle Morne
- Purchased from the Nomadic Merchant east of Astray from Caelid Highway North Site of Grace.
