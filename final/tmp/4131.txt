\n\n Somber Smithing Stone [2] \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce special armaments to +2 \n\nSomber Smithing Stone [2] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Somber Smithing Stone [2] is a large shard that is mainly used to reinforce special equipment to a high level. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nLarge shard of smithing stone drained of color.\nStrengthens special armaments to +2.\nSpecial armaments with unique characteristics cannot be strengthened with colored smithing stones. QUESTION
Why are special armaments with unique characteristics unable to be strengthened with colored smithing stones in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Special armaments with unique characteristics cannot be strengthened with colored smithing stones because they require Somber Smithing Stones to reinforce to a high level.
