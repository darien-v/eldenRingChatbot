\n\n Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker \n\nAffinity\nHeavy\n\nSkill\nHoarah Loux's Earthshaker\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker provides Heavy affinity and the Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Heavy and the following skill:\n"Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker: Slam both hands onto the ground to violently shake the earth and unleash a shockwave. Follow up with an additional input to slam the ground again."\nUsable on all melee armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker:\n\nTrade Remembrance of Hoarah Loux with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nAsh of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds, Reapers, Fists, Claws, Whips, Colossal Weapons\n\nHoarah Loux's Earthshaker Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker Notes & Tips\n\nThe Skill is not chargeable, but a second input results in a second slam
