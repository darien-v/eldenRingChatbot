CONTEXT
\nLegacy Dungeons in Elden Ring are special fortresses and areas with significant challenge and exploration rewards. Legacy Dungeons are home to Lords, and players will access them to challenge the Bosses that dwell within and obtain pieces of the Elden Ring. You cannot ride Torrent within these locations.\n\nSee Locations for walkthroughs for larger open-world areas\nSee Minor Dungeons for Caves, Catacombs, etc\nSee Walkthrough and Game Progress Route for progression help\nSee Elden Ring Map and Maps for detailed maps of all areas\n\nList of Legacy Dungeons in Elden Ring\n\nThese are the main Legacy Dungeons in Elden Ring:\n\nStormveil Castle\nRaya
