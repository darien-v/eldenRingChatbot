QUESTION
Why are Skeletal Slimes particularly resistant to physical-type damage but weak to frostbite/fire damage in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Skeletal Slimes are resistant to physical-type damage as they can be poise-broken, but weak to frostbite/fire damage due to their nature in Elden Ring.
