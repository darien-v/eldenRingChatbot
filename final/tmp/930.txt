\n\n Divine Tower of Limgrave \n\nClosed with a giant set of doors. It looks like you need some pieces to interact with the tower. \n\nDivine Tower of Limgrave is a Location in Elden Ring. It is one of the six Divine Towers. The Divine Tower of Limgrave is found in Limgrave. Enroute to Stormveil, a side route will have a small purple portal. Interact with the portal and it will send you across Limgrave to the Divine Tower of Limgrave.\n\nDivine Tower of Limgrave Map for Elden Ring\nSee the location in the Elden Ring Map.\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Divine Tower of Limgrave\n\nThere are no NPCs here\nThere are no Merchants here\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Divine Tower of Limgrave\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nStormhawk Feather - run backwards out the tower\nFlight Pinion\nSilver-Pickled Fowl Foot\nSoft Cotton\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location.\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nBoltdrake Talisman\nGolem Greatbow\nGolem's Halberd\nAsh-of-War Scarab\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nCan unlock Godrick's Great Rune by channeling at the top of the tower.\n\nDivine Tower of Limgrave Creatures, Enemies and Bosses in Elden Ring\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nGolem\nEagles\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses in this location.\n\nWalkthrough for Divine Tower of Limgrave in Elden Ring\nThis is the location where you can restore Godrick's Great rune to full strength and have it grant you its power. To reach the location, take the long bridge east of Stormveil castle from the Limgrave Tower Bridge Site of Grace.  Many players won't have this Site of Grace initially, as it requires you to have navigated the ballistae gauntlet from Stormveil Main Gate or from having navigated most of Stormveil Castle and approaching from the opposite direction. \nSome tips, if coming from the Stormveil Main Gate Site of Grace:\n\nSummoning just as you enter the area is helpful as it will provide additional targets for the ballistae\nRun forward about 50 yards until there is an opening to the right\nSprint for the large open arch, but beware: a tough enemy will try to stop you\nOnce through, the Limgrave Tower Bridge Site of Grace is a short sprint away!\n\nYou can loot Boltdrake Talisman from a body ascending the stairs toward the tower. This leads to a long Stormhill bridge covered with Giant Golems. You can choose to fight them or run through them. Killing the Giant Archers will give you a chance to gain Golem Greatbow. Before using the waygate, you can safely drop down behind the ledge to find a Soft Cotton and the Ash-of-War Scarab helm.\nInteract with the waygate at the end of the bridge to reach the other side. This will take you to the front steps of the Divine Tower of Limgrave. Step to the center to activate the lift. Continue forward and there will be another Site of Grace. Contnue heading up the stairs and you'll eventually reach a site where you can Restore the power of he Great Rune. This is only available after defeating Godrick the Grafted at Stormveil Castle. You will then obtain Godrick's Great Rune.\nYou must have defeated Godrick, otherwise, the gate of the tower will be locked. \n\nElden Ring Divine Tower of Limgrave Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\nThere is a Two Fingers on a mound of earth at the top of this tower, in the centre of eight meteorite fragments.\nThe door entering this divine tower faces east-northeast and the entrance to the top faces north-northwest, an angle of ninety degrees off entering the tower.
