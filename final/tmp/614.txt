\n\n Church of Pilgrimage \n\nThe Church of Pilgrimage is a broken building situated at the top of the cliff in the north of the Weeping Peninsula. Even with the walls and roof destroyed pilgrims still make the journey to visit this church.\n\nChurch of Pilgrimage is a Location in Elden Ring. The Church of Pilgrimage is found in the northern peak of Weeping Peninsula. Players can reach the Church of Pilgrimage by following the path up the slope after crossing the rope bridge. \n\nChurch of Pilgrimage Map\nSee the location of Church of Pilgrimage on our interactive map by clicking on [Elden Ring Map here].\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Church of Pilgrimage\n\nMelina - Shares unknown lore on Queen Marika. A group of pilgrims can be seen leaving the Church. Outside the entrance of the Church players will find a spirit ghost NPC sitting on a chair with a book around their neck. Inside the Church players can find a Site of Grace and a large statue where they will find a body with a Sacred Tear. There is a Graveyard connected to the church where players can find multiple tombs with some loot as well as skeleton enemies to face.\nFurther to the north and in the surroundings around the Church players can find Eagles perched on the cliffs and sheep grazing the fields as well as a variety of gathering options.\nWarhawk Ashes can be obtained close to this location. Rewarded upon finding the location Prophecy Painting depicts.
