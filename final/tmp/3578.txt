QUESTION
Where can Yellow Ember be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Yellow Ember can be found in the Weeping Peninsula, Liurnia of the Lakes, and Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, as well as dropped by Frenzied Giant Rats, Frenzied Rats, and Frenzied Villagers and farmed in various locations like Cathedral of the Forsaken, Yelough Anix Ruins, and Mt. Gelmir.
