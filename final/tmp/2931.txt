\n\n Stonesword Key \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Use to break one imp statue seal \n\nStonesword Key is a Key Item and optional Keepsake in Elden Ring. Stonesword Key can be used to break one imp statue seal. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nA sword-shaped stone key.\nBreaks the seal on imp statues, but remains embedded in the statue after use, meaning it can only be used once as an item.\nThink well before using one.\n\nWhere to find and purchase Stonesword Keys in Elden Ring\n\nLimgrave\nWhere to purchase\n\nSold by Patches at Murkwater Cave after he sets up his shop. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Stonesword Key Use\nStoneword Keys can be used to open Imp Statues which contain loot. Stonesword Keys are consumed upon use.\nHere is a complete list of all Imp Statues that can be opened with Stonesword Keys:\n\nLimgrave\n\nFringefolk Hero's Grave: To access it you need two Stonesword Keys. It can be found near the Stranded Graveyard Site of Grace.There is a pool full of poison. Run forward and at the end turn right to the stone platform. From there, keep going but you will see a giant mechanical monstrosity coming at you, hide behind the columns on the side to prevent being trampled. In order to get down, use the small rooms or cubbies to advance surely but slowly, notice that there are enemies there. Once you reach the bottom, there's a statue Marika to make things easier while descending, because many enemies will spawn. Once you reach the end. you will enter a boss fight with a Ulcerated Tree Spirit. Defeat it to obtain a Golden Seed and a Banished Knight Oleg. There is a hidden location if you jump down at the beginning of the bridge, there the Erdtree's Favor Talisman can be found. A Stoneword Key can also be found, effectively thus costing you only a single key to access this area.\nTombsward Catacombs requires a Stonesword key to access, players can find a Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [9] past the seal.\nUsed to unlock an underground cavern within Summonwater Village that houses the Green Turtle Talisman.\nCan be used to open two locked doors at the Roundtable Hold, the first door will require one Key to open, and the second door behind the first requires two keys to open (You'll need 3 keys in total). You'll find the Assassin's Prayerbook behind the second gate.\n\nStormveil Castle\n\nIn a cellar room accessible from the main courtyard.
