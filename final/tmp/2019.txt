CONTEXT
\nCrystal Tears in Elden Ring are Key Items that can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick for various special effects. This page covers their names, effects, durations, and Locations.\nCrystal Tears, and the Flask of Wondrous Physick, are kept in New Game+. They are also unique, meaning that they cannot be found in subsequent Journeys.\n\nAmmunition\n\nBell Bearings\n\nCookbooks\n\nConsumables\n\nCrafting Mats.\n\nCrystal Tears\n\nGreat Runes\n\nInfo Items\n\nKey Items\n\nMultiplayer\n\nRemembrances\n\nTools\n\nUpgrade Mats.\n\nWhetblades\n\nElden Ring Crystal Tears\n\nCombining Crystal Tears\nTwo Crystal Tears can be mixed into the flask at a time when visiting Sites of Grace. QUESTION
How can Crystal Tears be used in conjunction with the Flask of Wondrous Physick to gain special effects in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Crystal Tears can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to gain various special effects, such as restoring HP, FP, boosting stats, and enhancing offensive or defensive capabilities.
