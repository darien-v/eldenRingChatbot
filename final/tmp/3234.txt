QUESTION
Why is the Mausoleum Knight Greaves Leg Armor considered an important piece in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Mausoleum Knight Greaves Leg Armor is considered important in Elden Ring due to its defensive properties, part of the Mausoleum Knight Set, farmed from Mausoleum Knights, offering resistance and immunity, and its set synergy with the Mausoleum Knight Armor and Gauntlets.
