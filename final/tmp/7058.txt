CONTEXT
\n\n Inaba, Disciple of Okina \n\nLocation\nSpiritcaller Cave\n\nDrops\nN/A\n\nInaba, Disciple of Okina are hostile NPCs in Elden Ring. They are disciples of Bloody Finger Okina, who had abandoned them, and appear to be deceased in the current time. They appear as spirits summoned by a Lesser Spirit-Caller Snail, that resides at Spiritcaller Cave, rushing down players with their katanas.\n\n The Inaba traveled in pursuit of not only the master who abandoned them, but also proper deaths, won honorably through hard-fought combat.\nTo be a White Reed is to seek death; fitting for the Inaba, who desired nothing more than to die by the sword\n\nElden Ring Inaba, Disciple of Okina Locations\nWhere to Find Inaba, Disciple of Okina:\n\nSpirits of the Inaba can be encountered only at Spiritcaller Cave [Map link], where he can be summoned by 2 Lesser Spirit-Caller Snails\n\nElden Ring Inaba, Disciple of Okina Drops\n\nN/A\n\nInaba, Disciple of Okina Notes & Tips\n\nWears White Reed Set and uses Uchigatana infused with Seppuku Ash of War.\nDamage: Slash, Pierce\nInflicts: Hemorrhage\nParryable: Yes\nAfter meeting the player, Inaba will start with Seppuku and then begins to fight. This allows player to locate Snail and eliminate it, as killing Lesser Spirit-Caller Snail will make Inaba vanish.\nThis specific NPC is uniquely referred to as a group, and not as individuals.
