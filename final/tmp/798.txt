However, player can leave the Stranded Graveyard through the exit door instead of jumping down, effectively skipping the entire tutorial. \nSoldier of Godrick serves as an introduction to Boss fight mechanics.\nHe wears the Godrick Soldier Set and wields the Lordsworn's Greatsword.\nIt is not possible to use spirit summons or co-op for this boss fight.\nUses "Formidable Foe I" boss theme.\n\nWhere to find Soldier of Godrick in Elden Ring\nSoldier of Godrick can be found in the Stranded Graveyard. Jump down next to where you wake up instead of going through the exit door and progress through several rooms to will eventually find a fog door. Enter the door to find the boss in a large arena. The only tricky thing about him is that he looks like he is going to strike once, but he may suddenly perform a 3 hit combo.\nGo Berserk: If you have enough health, simply attacking him repeatedly will be enough to defeat him even if you take several hits in the process. This strategy isn't advised, though, since this boss is a great chance to practice more advanced moves like guard counters, parrying, and dodging.  \nCharge and Crit: A charged heavy attack from any weapon will break his stance, allowing a critical hit that will deal about 30% of his total HP. You can prepare another heavy attack while he's getting up to break his posture again.\nGuard Counters: The most foolproof way to defeat him is to hold up your shield and guard counter once he hits you. A single guard counter will break his stance, letting you get a critical hit.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nResort to Melee: Since Soldier of Godrick runs towards you and early spells take a long time to cast, it may be best to just equip your melee weapon for now and use one of the strategies above.\nBait and Blast: If you want to stick to magic, keep your distance as best as possible and bait him into using slow attacks. After you dodge, you'll have a chance to cast a single spell before repeating the process.\n\nAttacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nOverhead Slash\nA charge attack where he performs an QUESTION
When is Soldier of Godrick found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Soldier of Godrick is found in the Stranded Graveyard in Elden Ring, shortly after starting the game, by jumping down next to where you wake up and progressing through several rooms to reach a fog door leading to the boss arena.
