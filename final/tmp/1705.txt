The Crucible Tree Set is a Heavy Weight armor that boasts superior Physical and Magical defenses, catering to Strength-focused characters and defensive playstyles. Armor provides our character with protection and different resistance against all types of damage and negative status effects. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nArmor of one of sixteen ancient knights who served Godfrey, the first Elden Lord.\n\nWhere to find Crucible Tree Set in Elden Ring\nYou can find the Crucible Tree Set in:\n\nThe whole set is found in a chest within Deeproot Depths behind a Boss: Crucible Knight Siluria.
