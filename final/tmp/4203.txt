\n\n Ghost Glovewort [7] \n\nEffect \n\n        Strengthen renowned ashes to +7 \n\nGhost Glovewort [7] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ghost Glovewort [7] is a special material used to upgrade renowned spirit ashes to +7. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nWhite flower that blooms in catacombs.A spirit nestles close to it.\nStrengthens renowned ashes to +7.\nAshes which keep their renown even after death cannot be strengthened by merely corporeal glovewort.\n\nGhost Glovewort [7] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ghost Glovewort [7]:\n\nFound in a ruins in northern Uhl Palace Ruins with two large statues at the entrance. [Map Link]\nGiants' Mountaintop Catacombs: Follow the main path until you reach the first lift. Descend it and then send it back up to reveal a second platform in the lift that will take you to the lowest levels of the catacombs. From here, continue past the room with the Lesser Burial Watchdog to a corridor with a frost breath trap. Continue past this trap to a second corridor with another frost breath trap at the end. Halfway through this second corridor, hit the wall on the right to reveal an alcove where the ghost glovewort can be found.
