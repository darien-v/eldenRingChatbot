\n\n Prince of Death's Pustule \n\nWeight\n0.6\n\nEffect \n\nRaises \n       Vitality by 90 \n\nPrince of Death's Pustule is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Prince of Death's Pustule raises Vitality by 90, improving resistance to Death Blight. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA fetid pustule taken from facial flesh.Raises vitality.(Vitality governs resistance to the effects of Death.)\nIt is said that this pustule came from the visage of the Prince of Death, he who used to be called Godwyn. As First Dead of the demigods, it's said he's buried deep under the capital, at the Erdtree's roots.\n\nPrince of Death's Pustule Effect in Elden Ring\nPrince of Death's Pustule raises Vitality by 90, improving resistance to Death Blight.\n\nWhere to find Prince of Death's Pustule in Elden Ring\n\nCan be located at Stormveil Castle.\nNearest Site of Grace: Liftside Chamber.\nLooted off a corpse that can be found at the pit of the castle's crypt. The body can be found next to a giant face that is structured from roots. It is guarded by an Ulcerated Tree Spirit.
