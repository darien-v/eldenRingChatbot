It is one of the first weapons the player can acquire that scales with Arcane and is exceptional at inflicting Blood Loss on targets.\n\nJagged dagger with a distinctive curled blade.Carried by the noble servants of the Lord of Blood.\nThis dagger rips the flesh as it enters, inflicting blood loss with sickening efficacy. QUESTION
Why is the Reduvia Dagger exceptional at inflicting Blood Loss on targets in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Reduvia Dagger is exceptional at inflicting Blood Loss due to its weapon skill, Reduvia Blood Blade, which inflicts more Blood Loss buildup per hit than its melee strikes.
