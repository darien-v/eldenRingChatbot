The Magma Wyrm's Scalesword scales primarily with Strength and Faith, with reduced scaling in Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for dealing additional Fire Damage during combat. This weapon's sharp-toothed edge can deal great damage to foes. Like its title suggests, it is a heavy-weight sword covered in hard scales and resembles the dragon's jaw. \n\nCurved greatsword wielded by Magma Wyrms. The shape resembles a dragon's jaw and is covered in hard scales.\nIt's said these land-bound dragons were once humans heroes who partook in dragon communion, a grave transgression for which they were cursed to crawl the earth upon their bellies, shadows of their former selves.\n\nWhere to Find Magma Wyrm's Scalesword in Elden Ring\nThe Magma Wyrm's Scalesword weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDrops from Magma Wyrm Makar in Ruin-Strewn Precipice in northern Liurnia of the Lakes. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring Magma Wyrm's Scalesword Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Magma Guillotine\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War\nCannot be enchanted with Magic nor buffed by Consumables\nMagma Wyrm's Scalesword can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones\nThis weapon has a unique R2 shared with Omen Cleaver.\nSell Value:   500\nMagma Guillotine is almost impossible to interrupt and the first part of the skill makes the character crouch so you can avoid most horizontal swipes. The full combo has a really high motion value and can easily deal over 3000 damage. The first attack is not affected by Claw Talisman.\nSince the physical damage of this weapon has bigger base damage portion, it's best to level Strength up to 60 before you start investing in Faith. However, it is advisable to level faith up to 25 in order to use Golden Vow and Flame, Grant Me Strength.\nIts moveset has very small range when used with both hands, since its mostly used akin to a guillotine for jump attacks and heavy attacks. If you want more range, consider using it one-handed. \n\nThe two-handed moveset, however, has a much faster R2 than would be expected of a weapon of its size - a useful tool in many situations\n\nYou can also swing this weapon slightly faster when attacking while crouching.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed. QUESTION
What are the best stats to focus on when using Magma Wyrm's Scalesword in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Focus on leveling Strength up to 60 before investing in Faith, but it's advisable to level Faith up to 25 to use Golden Vow and Flame, Grant Me Strength.
