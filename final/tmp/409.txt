The easiest way to spawn him is to rest at the Site of Grace, advance time til night and then rest once more. When you step out of the shack, the boss should spawn. This particular iteration of the Hunter is significantly stronger than others you may have faced before. Upon defeat, he drops the Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing.\nThere is also a Teardrop Scarab at the cliffs just northwest of the shack.
