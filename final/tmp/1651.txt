Gelmir Knight Set an Armor piece set that still has not lost its luster. It bears an emblem that none wear any longer, standing as it does for a lord that fell from loft ambition into gluttonous depravity. As the lord lost his dignity, so too did these knights lose their master. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Gelmir Knight Set in Elden Ring\n\nYou can loot the entire Gelmir Knight Set in the Gelmir Hero's Grave dungeon from a Gelmir Knight corpse. See it on the  Elden Ring Map here.\nTo find the corpse, head down the first Chariot hallway until you see a pit on either side of the central concrete walkway instead of lava. Drop down into the left pit onto some wooden beams and look for a small window along the walls. Head through this window and down the ladder to find a Bloodhound Knight guarding the corpse.\n\nGelmir Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nGelmir Knight Helm\nGelmir Knight Armor\nGelmir Knight Gauntlets\nGelmir Knight Greaves\n\nGelmir Knight Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Gelmir Knight Armor can be altered to the Gelmir Knight Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nThere are no Gelmir Knights alive, thus only a corpse located in the Gelmir Hero's Grave yields the armor. The Spirit found in the Volcano Manor wears the same armor and may very well be a former Gelmir Knight.\nThe Gelmir Knight's corpse is guarded by a single Bloodhound Knight, who also drops its full armor set.\nThe crest of the cape is identical to the one on the Distinguished Greatshield.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
