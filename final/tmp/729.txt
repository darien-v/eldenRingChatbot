\n\n Hermit's Shack \n\nAn abandoned shack along the southwestern edge of Mt. Gelmir.\n\nHermit's Shack is a Location in Elden Ring. The Hermit's Shack is found in Mt. Gelmir on the southwestern tip of the volcano. Players can reach this location by first traveling around the volcano starting from the ravine west of Wyndham Ruins, following it all the way around to Fort Laiedd and then heading south. The shack can be found on top of a small hill on the left.\n\nHermit's Shack Map\nHermit's Shack can be found southwest of Mt. Gelmir. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Hermit's Shack\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Hermit's Shack\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nNo Gatherables at this location\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nx3 Smithing Stone (6)\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nRoiling Magma\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nNo Uniques, Ashes or Keys at this location\n\nElden Ring Hermit's Shack Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nDemi-Human\nLesser Runebear\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no Bosses at this location\n\nHermit's Shack Walkthrough\nThere is little of note at this shack other than the Roiling Magma sorcery found on a corpse inside. There are a couple of Demi-Humans in the shack as well. Heading further west, a Lesser Runebear will descend down the cliffs above to attack. Lure it to attack the shining statue near the shack to loot x3 Smithing Stone (6).\n\nElden Ring Hermit's Shack QUESTION
How can players reach the Hermit's Shack in Mt. Gelmir in Elden Ring?
