\n\n Crucible Scale Talisman \n\nWeight\n1.1\n\nEffect \n\nReduces  \n       Critical Damage taken by 30% \n\nCrucible Scale Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Crucible Scale Talisman reduces Critical Damage taken by 30%. Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman fashioned from a scale that embodies the aspects of various creatures.Said to have grown on the human body long ago.\nReduces damage taken from critical hits.\nA vestige of the crucible of primordial life. Born partially of devolution, it was considered a signifier of the divine in ancient times, but is now increasingly disdained as an impurity as civilization has advanced.\n\nCrucible Scale Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nCrucible Scale Talisman reduces Critical Damage taken by 30%.\n\nWhere to find Crucible Scale Talisman in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Crucible Scale Talisman:\n\nAltus Plateau: Inside Leyndell Catacombs, Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, under Leyndell. From the Underground Roadside site of grace, turn left and climb down the ladder on the right into a room full of imps and pipes. On the pipe at the right end from where you enter, you can drop down onto another pipe then down below onto the ground with two Giant Crayfish and the Mohg's Shackle. One door leads into the Leyndell Catacomb site of grace. From the Leyndell Catacombs site of grace, head into the catacomb until you find a room with the flame-blowing stone imp column. Make your way into the room with the column, and hit it to make it rise up or lower down, then ride it up into a room with an Omen. The talisman is at the altar table behind it. QUESTION
Why is the Crucible Scale Talisman increasingly disdained as an impurity as civilization has advanced?

ANSWER
The Crucible Scale Talisman is increasingly disdained as an impurity as civilization has advanced due to its origin partially from devolution, which was once considered divine but now seen as impure.
