\n\n Academy Gate Town \n\nThe sunken remains of the academy town.\n\nAcademy Gate Town is a Location in Elden Ring. The Academy Gate Town is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. Players can get to the Academy Gate Town through multiple avenues, the half-submerged town can be seen from many angles around the lake.
