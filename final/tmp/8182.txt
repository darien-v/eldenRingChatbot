Bloodsoaked Manchettes is an armor piece that protect both of the player's arms by applying various defensive properties, It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nManchettes formed from tightly-wound bandages soaked through with blood. Any protection is welcome for the festering arms beneath. \n\nWhere to find Bloodsoaked Manchettes in Elden Ring\nThe Bloodsoaked Manchettes Armor Gauntlets piece can be found at the following location:\n\nThe Bloodsoaked Manchettes can be looted from the ground in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds.\nFrom the Underground Roadside site of grace, head into the main tunnel, turn left, and take out the first Omen. The Bloodsoaked Mask and Bloodsoaked Manchettes are on the ground on the right side of the tunnel shortly after the enemy.
