QUESTION
Where can the Perfumer Set be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Perfumer Set can be found by farming the Perfumer enemies throughout Leyndell, Royal Capital, or by defeating the Perfumers in Perfumer's Ruins and Perfumer's Grotto in Elden Ring.
