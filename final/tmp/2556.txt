Night Maiden Set is a cloth set worn by the Night Maiden enemies found in Nokron and Nokstella. It shares the Nox Bracelets and Nox Greaves with the Nox Monk Set and Nox Swordstress Set. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Night Maiden Set in Elden Ring\n\nPieces of the Night Maiden Set are dropped by Night Maiden enemies found in Nokron and Nokstella.\nTwo Night Maiden can be found in the Night's Sacred Ground area in the church with the large Sphere Silver Tear. Once this building's shortcut is opened, these two can be easily farmed by resetting at the Night's Sacred Ground site of grace. QUESTION
Why is the Night Maiden Set armor pieces specifically dropped by Night Maiden enemies in certain locations in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Night Maiden Set armor pieces are dropped by Night Maiden enemies in specific locations in Elden Ring to reflect the lore and thematic connection between the armor set, the enemies, and the areas where they are found.
