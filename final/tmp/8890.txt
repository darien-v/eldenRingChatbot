QUESTION
When can Beast Liver be found in Elden Ring and what are the crafting options for this material?

ANSWER
Beast Liver can be found in Elden Ring by hunting herbivorous beasts or purchased from Nomadic Merchant East Limgrave, and it can be used to craft Fireproof Dried Liver, Spellproof Dried Liver, Lightningproof Dried Liver, and Holyproof Dried Liver.
