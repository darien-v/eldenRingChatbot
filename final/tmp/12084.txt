Azula Beastmen are feral biped creatures with a hyena-like aspect, featuring a prominent hump and a seemingly skinned animal face. These creatures are often found eating from dead bodies, which makes them vulnerable to surprise or stealth attacks, but they are quick and skilled in wielding a wide array of melee weapons.\nBeastman of Farum Azula are the Boss variants of these creatures.\nTwo (2) Azula Beastman can be summoned for aid as spirits by using the Azula Beastman Ashes.\n\nBeastmen from doomed Farum Azula, the slowly crumbling ruins in the skies. These ruins are said to be the remains of a giant mausoleum enshrining an ancient dragon, guarded by chosen beastmen who wield weapons clad in lightning.\n\nElden Ring Azula Beastman Locations\nWhere to Find Azula Beastman:\n\nCrumbling Farum Azula\n\nElden Ring Azula Beastman Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 1284 - 1584\n\nBeastman's Cleaver\n2.00%\nDropped by those that wield it.\n\nBeastman's Curved Sword\n2.00%\nDropped by those that wield it.\n\nBeastman's Jar-Shield\n2.00%\nDropped by those that wield it.\n\nGravel Stone\n3.00%\nDrops only from Beastmages, which wield only a Clawmark Seal.\n\nSanctuary Stone\n8.00%\nN/A\n\nAzula Beastman Notes & Tips\n\nDeals Standard Damage (ranged), Slash Damage (melee), Pierce Damage (undead, variation with spear), Magic Damage (undead ranged) and Ligthning Damage (excluding undead variation).\nAttacking one of these creatures from a distance won't trigger all the surrounding horde, use this an advantage to attack them one by one. QUESTION
Why are Azula Beastmen weak to Slash Damage and Fire Damage, while the skeleton variation is only resistant to Slash Damage?

ANSWER
Azula Beastmen are weak to Slash Damage and Fire Damage due to their non-skeleton form, while the skeleton variation is only resistant to Slash Damage, likely due to their different compositions and vulnerabilities.
