\n\n Stormcaller Church \n\nA lonely church on a steppe.\n\nStormcaller Church is a Location in Elden Ring. The Stormcaller Church is found in Altus Plateau. It can be reached from Rampartside Path site of grace by following the road west towards the lightning strikes. \n\nStormcaller Church Map\nThe Stormcaller Church is found in Altus Plateau.
