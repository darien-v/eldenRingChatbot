\n\n Minor Erdtree (Liurnia Southwest) \n\nThis ancient Erdtree overlooks the lakes from the southwest.\n\nMinor Erdtree (Liurnia Southwest) is a Location in Elden Ring. The Minor Erdtree (Liurnia Southwest) is found in Liurnia of the Lakes in the southwest landmass. This location can be accessed by following the southwest marshes, leading up into the hills.\n\nMinor Erdtree (Liurnia Southwest) Map\nThis Minor Erdtree is located on a plateau in the southwest of Liurnia. QUESTION
Why is the Minor Erdtree in Liurnia Southwest easily accessible by entering through the southwest marshes, and what significance does this location hold in Elden Ring?
