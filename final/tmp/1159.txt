\n\n Bellum Church \n\nLocated past the ruins and the forested area guarded by Cuckoo Knights.\n\nBellum Church is a Location in Elden Ring. The Bellum Church is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. Following the east path to the north, avoiding the gate to East Raya Lucaria Gate there's a forested area with ruins guarded by Cuckoo Knights. Players can reach the Bellum Church either by going up the ramp on the west of the church, going through the valley and the watery areas of the north part of the lakes then up the ramp, or by following the east path atop the cliffs. Around the outskirts of the Church are many Cuckoo Knight enemies and even a Night's Cavalry that roams once the sun has set. Come in the evening time to defeat the Night's Calvary and obtain Ash of War: Giant Hunt as a boss drop. Pick up the Sacred Tear found in the front of the church amongst the flowers directly in front of the main statue piece. \n\nElden Ring Bellum Church Gallery and Notes\nBellum Church is very clearly built on top of a prior structure, which you can see as the Church foundation is sitting on top of a larger foundation which you can descend through on the left side of the Church.
