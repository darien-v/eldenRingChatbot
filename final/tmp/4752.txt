\n\n Zamor Ice Storm \n\n        Plunge the curved sword into the ground, building power before unleashing a freezing storm that batters the surroundings. \n\nZamor Ice Storm is a Skill in Elden Ring. Zamor Ice Storm is a skill that allows the user to unleash a freezing storm. Updated to Patch 1.07.\nThis Skill should not be confused with the spell of the same name: Zamor Ice Storm (Spell).\n\nHow to get Zamor Ice Storm in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill for the Zamor Curved Sword.\n\nElden Ring Zamor Ice Storm Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 15\nDeals 39 stance damage.\nDeals 100% Magic Damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill can be Parried\nSeems to do roughly 630 frost buildup with the initial hit of the sword.\n\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nAttack power and frostbite status buildup has been increased.\nCasting speed has been increased.\nRecovery time has been shortened.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nFreezing Battlemage Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Zamor Ice Storm Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
