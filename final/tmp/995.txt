This large, headless creature looks like a tree stump wielding a large staff, and is usually found guarding Minor Erdtrees. Most avatars drop materials for improving the Flask of Wondrous Physick. This is an optional boss as players are not required to defeat it to advance in Elden Ring.\nSee also Putrid Avatar, the variant of this boss infected with Scarlet Rot.\nWhere to Find Elden Ring Erdtree Avatar\n\nMinor Erdtree (Weeping Peninsula) [More Info] [Map Link]\nMinor Erdtree (Liurnia Southwest) [More Info] [Map Link]\nMinor Erdtree (Liurnia Northeast) [More Info] [Map Link]\nLeyndell, Royal Capital [More Info] [Map Link]\nDeeproot Depths [More Info] [Map Link]\nMinor Erdtree (Mountaintops East) [More Info] [Map Link]\n\nErdtree Avatar Combat information\n\nStance: 120\nParryable: No\nIs vulnerable to a  critical hit  after being stance broken\nDamage: Standard, Strike, Holy\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nStandard: 10\nSlash: 10\nStrike: 0\nPierce: 10\n\nMagic: 20\nFire: -40\nLightning: 20\nHoly: 40\n\nThe negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nElden Ring Erdtree Avatar Boss Guide\n\nErdtree Avatar Boss Video Guide\n[video coming soon]\n\nErdtree Avatar Fight Strategy\nThe Best Tips for Erdtree Avatar:\n\nTry to end up behind it whenever you can, as it will always turn to face you before it attacks\nIt's extremely weak to Fire damage, so Fire incantations, arrows, or Grease\nVolcano Pot and Redmane Fire Pot also do the job, if you dont use spells\nFist type weapon users should attack by either jumping attacks or on the back/side. Attacking the front may cause the issue of "missing hits".\n\nMelee Strategy\nAvoid Ashes: Since most summons can't dodge, they'll die quickly to the Erdtree Avatar's heavy attacks. They'll also make it harder for you to predict where its attacks will go and thus harder to dodge them. It has such a small stable of moves, it's better to focus on dodging them one-on-one than trying to distract it with a summon.\nPony Ride: Because the Avatar's attacks and movement are both relatively slow, it's particularly vulnerable to mounted combat. Make sure you've got enough Rowa Raisins to keep Torrent happy and you've got enough armor to survive a single staff swipe, and start circling it clockwise. Slash its back until it starts winding up for an attack. If it does an overhead swing, you can keep circling and attacking it as long as you don't end up beneath the staff when it falls. Otherwise, ride away when the attack starts and come back once it's done. You can jump over the shockwave from Erdtree Slam if you're struggling to escape its radius in time.\nSticking Close: If you're fighting the avatar on foot, it's better to stick in close and learn the timings of its relatively few attacks so you can dodge through them and keep hitting in between. Its windups are slow and telegraphed, so this shouldn't be too hard. Try to get behind it when you can to make it waste time turning to face you, and if you can't stick to its right side where you can roll behind when it tries to stomp. The only time you should leave close quarters is when it does Golden Land.\nMagic and Ranged Strategy\nTank of Ash: Choose a summon that's good at surviving, because Erdtree Avatar hits hard. The Lone Wolf Ashes, Mad Pumpkin Head Ashes, or Spirit Jellyfish Ashes are all good choices, since the Wolves summon three, and the Mad Pumpkin and Jellyfish are both very tanky and can keep the boss distracted for quite a while.\nCircle and Strike: Keep your distance, using Torrent if possible, and keep moving around it so you don't get backed into a corner. Almost all of its attacks are close quarters, but you'll need to be wary of its hopping downward swing and its Golden Land attack. Don't get greedy: make sure you're always keeping your distance while you patiently whittle down its health.\n\nErdtree Avatar Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nSwing\nSwings its staff in various directions\nThese swings are all very slow and have poor tracking, so you'll usually have plenty of time to roll or just run out of the way\n\nStomp\nRaises its right leg and stomps down\nRoll behind it and get in a few hits while it turns to face you\n\nSide Sweep\nSweeps its staff counterclockwise along its left flank\nRoll clockwise into the sweep. You back be able to get in a hit before it follows up with a clockwise swing if your weapon is quick enough\n\nErdtree Slam\nJumps in the air and slams the ground with its whole body, dealing Holy damage in a small area around it\nYou can roll back to avoid this, but if you roll towards its back just as it starts falling you can avoid the damage, get in a few hits, and position yourself well for future attacks. You can also jump over the shockwave and land a jumping attack at the same time, but be careful to start from far enough away that you don't connect with its body as it falls\n\nGolden Land\nHolds staff in the air and then slams it vertically into the ground, creating a small burst of Holy damage. A large orb of light then grows above its head and shoots Holy projectiles at you. Only used when it's below 60% health\nGet some distance so its melee attacks won't hit you, and then run in circles around it until the projectiles stop firing\n\nErdtree Avatar Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nThe attacks and behaviors are very similar to the Asylum and Stray Demon bosses in Dark Souls, including the body slam and staff attacks.\n\nErdtree Avatar Combo Chart\n\n Erdtree Avatar  Minor Erdtree (Weeping Peninsula)  \n\nDrops\n3,600 (NG) 930 (NG, Co-op)Opaline BubbletearCrimsonburst Crystal Tear\n\nHP\n3,163\n\nElden Ring Erdtree Avatar Boss (Weeping Peninsula)\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Tombsward Catacombs\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nErdtree Avatar Location\nFrom the entrance to Tombsward Catacombs, head east and then south up the hill.
