\n\n Waves of Darkness \n\n        Plunge armament into the ground to release three waves of darkness. Follow up with a strong attack to swing the armament in a sweeping strike. \n\nWaves of Darkness is a Skill in Elden Ring. Waves of Darkness is a skill that plunges armament into the ground to release three waves of darkness. \n\nHow to get Waves of Darkness in Elden Ring\n\nAsh of War: Waves of Darkness - Trade Remembrance of the Naturalborn with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Waves of Darkness Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Waves of Darkness.\nFP Cost: 16\nHeavy Attack follow-up FP Cost: 5\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nDeals 34 stance damage\nTesting across Heavy, Cold, and Magic affinities indicates that although affinity affects base damage for each wave in the ash of war, it has no effect on scaling. Magic affinity increased base skill damage the most, followed by Cold and then Heavy. Scaling appears to be intelligence based with a 5 point increase from 29 to 34 adding approximately .97% damage to each wave regardless of affinity.\n\nWaves of Darkness Demonstration\n\nElden Ring
