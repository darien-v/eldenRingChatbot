QUESTION
What is the significance of the first flower of Aeonia blooming at the spot where the Aeonian Spirit stands?

ANSWER
The first flower of Aeonia blooming at the spot where the Aeonian Spirit stands signifies a sublime moment of beauty and potential divine transformation for Malenia.
