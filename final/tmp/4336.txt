QUESTION
Why is the Icon Shield considered a shield that works best at reducing damage and guarding instead of parrying in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Icon Shield is considered best for reducing damage and guarding in Elden Ring because it is a Greatshield that provides the best protection among shields, weighs a lot, and scales primarily with Strength, emphasizing stability and defense over parrying.
