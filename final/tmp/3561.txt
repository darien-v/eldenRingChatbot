QUESTION
Why is the Warming Stone in Elden Ring a valuable consumable for healing allies in the game?

ANSWER
The Warming Stone in Elden Ring is valuable for healing allies because it uses FP to continuously heal nearby creatures by 25 health per second, stacks with Frenzyflame Stones for a total of 60 health per second, and lasts for 30 seconds.
