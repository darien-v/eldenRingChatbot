QUESTION
Why are the archer spirits summoned by Archer Ashes imbued with cold ghostflame?

ANSWER
Their arrows are imbued with cold ghostflame due to their origins as spirits who belonged to the Fallen Hawks, a band of soldiers in the Eternal City.
