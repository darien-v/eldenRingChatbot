QUESTION
How can players effectively defeat Inquisitor Ghiza in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can effectively defeat Inquisitor Ghiza in Elden Ring by utilizing ranged combat, exploiting his long attack end lag for backstabs, stunlocking with non-light weapons due to his low poise, and inflicting heavy blood loss.
