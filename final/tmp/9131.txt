QUESTION
Where can I find the Skeletal Snail in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
You can find the Skeletal Snail in Elden Ring at the Capital Outskirts near the Hermit Merchant's Shack, Sage's Cave during the Necromancer Garris encounter, and at the Mountaintops of the Giants near where the Tibia Mariner is fought.
