CONTEXT
\n\n War Cry \n\n        Give a war cry to rally the spirit and increase attack power. While active, strong attacks change to charging attacks.  \n\nWar Cry is a Skill in Elden Ring. War Cry is a temporary attack boost to give players an extra advantage during combat. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nWar Cry Buff Details in Elden Ring\n\nAdds 7.5% Physical Damage\nAdds +5 Strength scaling (This adds points to the scaling modifier of the weapon rather than your strength stat, though it's not exactly clear when in the damage formula it's added)\nAltered Strong Attack\n\nHow to get War Cry\n\nDefault skill on the Highland Axe\nDefault skill on the Warped Axe\nDefault skill on the Gargoyle's Great Axe\nSold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 800 . Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring War Cry Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: War Cry.\nFP Cost: 16\nThe Skill's effect lasts 40 seconds.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThe charging attack provided by this Skill can be Parried\n\n Roar Medallion will increase heavy attack damage by 15% for the duration.\nMoveset Changes (note that in most cases, charging the attack will cause the character to run forward several steps, regardless of any other aspects of the attack as listed below) \n\nSword, Greatsword, Colossal Sword, Curved Sword, Curved Greatsword, Katana, Axe, Greataxe, Flail, Hammer, Great Hammer, Colossal Weapon, and Reaper: Initial attack is an heavy overhead smash.  Follow up attack is an upward diagonal slash \n\nSome weapons attack slightly faster than others, but the difference is minimal, making bigger weapons much more effective with this AoW than smaller ones.\n\nTwinblade: Jumping forward thrust.  Follow up attack is an upward slash \n\nNote that this is a significant change from the default twinblade heavy attack, even when uncharged.  This can make it a useful situational tool even without the added range from charging.\n\nSpear, Greatspear, and Halberd: Initial attack is a jumping forward thrust.  Follow up attack is a forward thrust\nFists and Claws: Initial attack is a double cross, left fist then right fist.  Follow up attack causes your character to spin in a circle and hit with both fists outstretched\nHas status multiplier on charged heavy attacks ranging from 1.5x-3.0x, generally 2 handing the weapon has more multiplier and bigger weapons have higher multipliers\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nEffect duration has been extended from 20 to 40 seconds.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\nIncreased the power of strong attacks during the duration of the effect when using the following weapons: Straight Sword, Curved Sword, Katana, Axe, Hammer, Flail, Spear, Great Spear, Halberd, Reaper, Fist (one-handed), Claw (one-handed)\n\nBuilds with War Cry\n\nWar Cry Demonstration\n\nColossal Knight Written QUESTION
How does the War Cry skill in Elden Ring affect different weapons' movesets and attack mechanics during combat?

ANSWER
The War Cry skill in Elden Ring affects different weapons' movesets and attack mechanics by altering strong attacks into charging attacks, each with unique animations and increased damage multipliers.
