QUESTION
Why are the Cleanrot Gauntlets considered a good balance between Physical and Magical defense in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Cleanrot Gauntlets are considered a good balance between Physical and Magical defense in Elden Ring due to their high resistance values against various types of damage such as Strike, Slash, Pierce, Magic, Fire, and Holy.
