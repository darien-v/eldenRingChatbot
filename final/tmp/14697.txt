\n\n Banished Knight Armor \n\nDmg Negation \n\nPhy 18.7 \n\nVS Strike 15 \n\nVS Slash 19.2 \n\nVS Pierce 17.5 \n\nMagic 13.5 \n\nFire 13.5 \n\nLigt 13 \n\nHoly 13.3 \n\nResistance \n\nImmunity 60 \n\nRobustness 83 \n\nFocus 38 \n\nVitality 42 \n\nPoise 34 \n\nChest Armor\nWgt. 17.5\n\nBanished Knight Armor is a Chest Armor in Elden Ring. Banished Knight Armor is part of the Banished Knight Set, and is a Heavyweight armor that boosts superior Robustness Resistance. Banished Knight Armor is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nThick, full set of armor covering the entire body. This armor was worn by knights who, whether by misfortune or misdeed, were forced to abandon their homes. These fierce warriors were each and all accomplished. Perhaps that is why, despite their territorial losses, they were still named knights. \n\nWhere to find Banished Knight Armor in Elden Ring\nBanished Knight Armor can be found at:\n\nChance to drop from two specific spectral Banished Knights located in Castle Sol that can be seen wearing the unaltered cloaked armor.\n\nThe northern one can be easily farmed by starting from the Castle Sol Rooftop site of grace and running south to the elevator. The Banished Knight can be shot with ranged weapons from here causing it to teleport to the player. The same can be achieved by shooting an arrow through the arrow slit in the grace platform (west side), although the knight doesn't always teleport when shooting him from this spot.\nThe southern one can be reached from the Church of the Eclipse site of grace by exiting through the church to the left, turning right outside, and heading up the ladder to the catwalks. After following the catwalk past the exile soldier and around the small tower, the Banished Knight can be found just past the ladder leading down. It is recommended that the player jump onto the lower roof next to the catwalk and tower, and bait the red-eyed Banished Knight into teleporting onto the small wooden catwalk extension of the roof; then the player can roll or jump to the walkway the Banished Knight was just standing on. The Banished Knight will slowly walk towards the player, then fall to its death onto the ground below, saving the player a good deal of trouble. Then the player walk down the walkway and make their way over to the loot, which will be in a corner, behind a tombstone.\n\nThe Banished Knight Armor (Altered) can be found at:\n\nDropped from all other Banished Knights. They can be found in Stormveil Castle, Cathedral of Dragon Communion, Crumbling Farum Azula, and Castle Sol.\n\nBanished Knight Armor Set in Elden Ring\n\nBanished Knight Armor is a piece of the Banished Knight Set.\nThe rest of the set is composed of the Banished Knight Helm, Banished Knight Gauntlets and Banished Knight Greaves.\nAltered set pieces that can be acquired are the Banished Knight Armor (Altered) and Banished Knight Helm (Altered).\n\nElden Ring Banished Knight Armor Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  100 Runes\nBanished Knight Armor and Banished Knight Armor (Altered) can not Chest, there are no enemies in game that wear the fully unaltered set.\nPoise Updated to patch 1.07\n\nAll Chest Armor in Elden Ring\n\nAlberich's Robe  ♦  Alberich's Robe (Altered)  ♦  All-Knowing
