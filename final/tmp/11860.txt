\n\n Giant Land Octopus \n\nLocation\nCommonly found in watery areas\n\nDrops\n\n 187 - 4995 RunesLand Octopus OvaryStrip of White FleshOctopus Head\n\nThe Giant Land Octopus is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. These creatures are hostile when approached, and are quite tanky and unpredictable.\nGiant Land Octopuses and the smaller Land Octopuses drop Land Octopus Ovaries; this item can be used in crafting several higher tier consumables and utility items.\n\nElden Ring Giant Land Octopus Locations\n\nMost commonly found near any bodies of water\nLimgrave  \n\n2 on the western beaches, north of the Coastal Cave Site of Grace.\n1 in a pond, south of the Saintsbridge Site of Grace.\nCommonly scattered across Agheel Lake, the large body of water in the center of Limgrave.\n\nSiofra River\n\nFound in north-western edges of the map, on the ground levels of the river.\n\nLiurnia of the Lakes\n\nSouth-west of Temple Quarter Site of Grace.\n\nMany are found in the Hidden Path to the Haligtree\nWeeping Peninsula\n\nSouth-west of the Minor Erdtree in the area\n\nConsecrated Snowfield\n\nSome are found near the frozen river, and be lured to fight against Theodorix.\n\nElden Ring Giant Land Octopus Combat Information\n\nHealth: \n\n2,655 HP (Highroad Cave)\n2,980 HP (Weeping Peninsula - North of Fourth Church of Marika)\n5,272 HP (Liurnia of the Lakes - Revenger's Shack; Siofra Rive)\n5,598 HP (Ruin-Strewn Precipice; Gael Tunnel)\n15,998 HP (Hidden Path to the Haligtree)\n\nPoise: 65\n\nABSORPTIONS\n\nPhy (Standard): 80\nPhy (Slash): 80\nPhy (Strike): 80\nPhy (Pierce): 80\n\nMagic: 40\nFire: 40\nLightning: 20\nHoly: 40\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. The tentacles can also be cut off, reducing its range of attack, but it can regenerate them. Beware of the long reach of its forward attacks.\nLand Octopuses will sometimes eat one of their tentacles to heal themselves, and staggering them before the end of the animation will prevent the healing.\nA similar enemy, the Kraken, is found in the game King's Field, also made by From. QUESTION
How can players effectively stagger the Giant Land Octopus in combat?

ANSWER
The Giant Land Octopus can be staggered with a single charged heavy attack from a sufficiently heavy weapon, such as a Pickaxe.
