CONTEXT
\n\n Braggart's Roar \n\n        Declare your presence with a boastful roar. Raises attack power, defense and stamina recover speed. \n\nBraggart's Roar is a Skill in Elden Ring. Braggart's Roar is a well-rounded skill that raises a players stats and can change the direction of the battle. This skill is found attached to weapons. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Braggart's Roar\n\nFound on Blackguard Big Boggart's Iron Ball weapon after killing him or continuing Dung Eater's questline and looting the body.\nDefault skill on the Battle Hammer and Rotten Battle Hammer.\n\nElden Ring Braggart's Roar Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Braggart's Roar.\nFP Cost: 16\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nOn activation, the user briefly gathers their strength before unleashing a mighty roar that provides various buffs. \n\nThese buffs are removed as soon as the user swaps out their weapons.\nCounts as a weapon buff. Using another weapon buff will replace the effects.\nAdds 10% Physical Attack\nDamage Negation +10% for all types\nStamina Recovery Speed +10/s (20%)\n\nLasts 60 seconds.\nWhen these buffs are active, the two-handed heavy and charged attacks of the weapon change their moveset.\n\nIn the case of fists and claws, having one in each hand is considered two handing for this purpose.\nNote that this change in attack pattern is identical to the one used by War Cry, with charged attacks incorporating additional running or jumping components (see the War Cry page for specifics on the individual weapon changes)\nRunning animation itself deals 0 damage, but inflicts stance and poise damage \n\nIs affected by Roar Medallion\nIs affected by Axe Talisman\nIs NOT affected by Shard of Alexander\nIf used without FP: \n\nLasts only 6 seconds\nHeavy attacks do not change their moveset\nDoes not receive stamina regeneration or defense\n\nThe 10% attack boost only applies to the weapon that has the Ash of War used since it's a weapon buff.\nSince it is a weapon buff, if you use it on both of the weapons your powerstancing, both of them get a 10% attack boost.\nThe 10% Damage Negation and Stamina Regen parts of this Ash of War are also considered weapon buffs, not body/regen buffs respectively. The Negation multiplies all incoming damage by 0.9x. Because of this, the damage negation does not change on your status page. \n\nApplying Braggart's Roar to two separate weapons does stack the damage negation and stamina regen. However, when a weapon is sheathed the buff will disappear on that weapon.\nThe Stamina Regen stacks with other Regen Buffs and does not get overridden or negate them\n\nAdding any other weapon buff will cancel every part of this Ash of War\nCan be a good way to add a weapon buff to special affinities, such as Occult or Bleed\nThe damage of the charged attacks is based on the total AR of the weapon, innate motion values do NOT get amplified.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nDuration has been extended from 30 to 60 seconds.\nPhysical Attack Buff has been increased from 7.5% to 10%.\nAll Types of Negations have been increased from 5% to 10%\nStamina Recovery has been increased from 7 to 10.\n\nBraggart's Roar Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning
