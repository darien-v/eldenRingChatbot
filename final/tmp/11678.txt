\n\n Lesser Misbegotten Warrior \n\n__image__\n\nLocation\nLeyndell, Royal CapitalMiquella's Haligtree\n\nDrops\nIron GreatswordOld Fang\n\nLesser Misbegotten Warrior is an Enemy in Elden Ring. These ferocious feline fighters are strong and quick, putting up a serious fight for anyone who crosses their path.\nSee Leonine Misbegotten, Perfumer Tricia and Misbegotten Warrior, Crucible Knight and Misbegotten Warrior, and Misbegotten Crusader for boss forms of this enemy.\n\nAggressive and feral, the Misbegotten sees nothing but rage\n\nElden Ring Lesser Misbegotten Warrior Locations\nWhere to find Lesser Misbegotten Warrior:\n\nBy Avenue Balcony Site of Grace in Leyndell, Royal Capital: Head northeast along the main road, through the large archway, and into the ash-covered section of the capital. Go up the hill to find the Lesser Misbegotten Warrior.\nBy Haligtree Town Site of Grace in Miquella's Haligtree: Go out the northwest exit from the room with the grace, then head straight. After you go down a short ramp, you'll see a ladder; head up it. Once you're at the top, double back to the southeast and jump over a small gap. You'll see the Lesser Misbegotten Warrior in front of you guarding Pearldrake Talisman +2.\nBy Haligtree Town Plaza Site of Grace in Miquella's Haligtree: Head north across the bridge, through the small building, and you'll see the Lesser Misbegotten Warrior in an arena guarding an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Misbegotten Warrior Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Location
