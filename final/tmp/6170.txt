\n\n Frenzied's Cookbook [2] \n\nEffect \n\n        Expands crafting repertoire \n\nRequired for    \nFrenzyflame Stone\n\nFrenzied’s Cookbook [2] is a Cookbook in Elden Ring. Cookbooks are records of crafting techniques that players can use to learn how to craft a wide variety of items.\n\nA record of crafting techniques left by those afflicted by the madness of the flame of frenzy.Contains techniques discovered in desperation.\nAcquire the knowledge to craft the following:\n- Frenzyflame Stone\n\nFrenzied’s Cookbook [2] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Frenzied’s Cookbook [2]:\nFrenzied’s Cookbook [2] Is found nearest to the Cathedral of the Forsaken Site of Grace in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds below Leyndell, Royal Capital. Behind the altar in Mohg, the Omen's boss room is a hidden path which can be opened by attacking the altar behind the chest after the boss fight. Following the the hidden path, there are stairs down and will lead to collapsed rafters that you can cross; you can cross the path and follow the stairs down for a safer route, or drop directly below on second set of collapsed rafters. On the second set of rafters, you will need to drop down to the lowest set of them, and you will find Frenzied's Cookbook [2] on a sitting corpse. QUESTION
How do players acquire Frenzied's Cookbook [2] in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players acquire Frenzied's Cookbook [2] by finding it on a sitting corpse located in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds below Leyndell, Royal Capital, nearest to the Cathedral of the Forsaken Site of Grace.
