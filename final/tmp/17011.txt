CONTEXT
\nCurved Greatswords  are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Curved Greatswords are capable of slash-based attacks, allowing users to deal a large amount of damage against enemies, but sacrifice mobility, making the wielder vulnerable to Enemy and Bosses' attacks.\nCurved Greatswords are compatible with a range of different Ashes of War, allowing you to mix and match for whatever your needs may be.\nNote: Patch 1.09, speed, range, recovery time and guard counter speed of Curved Greatsword weapons has increased.\nElden Ring Curved Greatsword Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Curved Greatswords in Elden Ring.
