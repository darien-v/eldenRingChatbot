The Fallingstar Beast Jaw scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence and is a good Weapon for skewering, piercing and striking foes. It has a unique Skill, the Gravity Bolt that can Imbue the jaw of a falling star beast with gravitational lightning, sending a bolt crashing down. \n\nPart of a Falling star Beast's jaw, hard and shining black, fashioned into a weapon. \nWith its sharp point, this colossal weapon can skewer foes. \n\nWhere to Find Fallingstar Beast Jaw in Elden Ring\nThe Fallingstar Beast Jaw weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by the Full-Grown Fallingstar Beast in Mt.
