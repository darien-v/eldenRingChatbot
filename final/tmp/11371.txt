\n\n Conspectus Scroll \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Give to Sorceress Sellen to unlock Glintstone Cometshard and Star Shower \n\nConspectus Scroll is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Conspectus Scroll can be used to unlock Glintstone Cometshard and Star Shower for purchase. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nA sorcery scroll from the Academy of Raya Lucaria. Details superior sorceries taught to scholars worthy of donning the stone crown and studying a certain conspectus. \nCan be given to a learned sorcerer to gain access to the following sorceries: \n- Glintstone Cometshard - Star Shower\n\nWhere to find Conspectus Scroll in Elden Ring\n\nLocation: Raya Lucaria Academy\nNearest Site of Grace: Schoolhouse Classroom\nCan be found near the Great Hall of the Raya Lucaria Academy. From the doorway, go left and you'll find a room, there is a body that you can loot to find the scroll. [Map Link]\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Conspectus Scroll Use\n\nConspectus Scroll can be used to Give it to Sorceress Sellen to unlock Glintstone Cometshard and Star Shower for purchase.\nCan also be given to Miriel, Pastor of Vows in the Church of Vows to unlock the same spells for purchase.\nCan also be given to Thops in Church of Irith before completing his quest\nCan also be given to Preceptor Seluvis after completing his potion task.\n\nElden Ring Conspectus Scroll Notes & Tips\nFAQ: Who should I give the Conspectus Scroll to Elden Ring?\n\nPlayers have questioned who you can give the scroll to and what the differences are to giving it to each character. After obtaining the Conspectus Scroll from the body near the Great Hall of the Raya Lucaria Academy this can either be given to Sorceress Sellen, Miriel, Pastor of Vows, Preceptor Seluvis or Thops. Giving it to either NPCs will unlock the ability to purchase additional Spells from them: Glintstone Cometshard and Star Shower. Make sure you remember who you gave this scroll to since you will need to return this character in order to purchase or obtain these specific spells. Once you have given the scroll, these spells will only be available from that character. Choosing to give it to either character will not affect any questlines or have any additional consequences. There are no additional benefits to giving them to any particular character.\nThe available characters you can give it to are learned sorcerers which are capable of unlocking special Sorceries. Sorceress Sellen has a number of different locations Depending on your progress in her questline. Thops can be found at Church of Irith and later moves to Liurnia of the Lakes. Miriel, Pastor of Vows can be found  in the Church of Vows and Preceptor Seluvis is found at Seluvis's Rise after his encounter at Ranni's Rise. Choose your character and which location you prefer to obtain your spells if you choose to pursue a Sorcery Build.\nRefer to the location instructions above or the map coordinates to assist in locating the scroll. Each NPC's locations can also be found on the Elden Ring Interactive Map. \n\nConspectus Scroll Other Notes\n\nNotes & Tips go here\n\nElden Ring Key Items\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell QUESTION
Why do players need to carefully consider which character to give the Conspectus Scroll to in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players need to carefully consider which character to give the Conspectus Scroll to in Elden Ring because the character they choose will determine from whom they can unlock the special spells Glintstone Cometshard and Star Shower for purchase, and this decision will dictate where they can obtain these specific spells in the game.
