\n\n Fingerprint Grape \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Eyeball of the knight Vyke, inflamed yellow \n\nFingerprint Grape is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Fingerprint Grape Eyeball of the knight Vyke, inflamed yellow. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nEyeball of the knight Vyke, inflamed yellow. Seared with a repulsive fingerprint burn akin to those that marred his entire body.\nIf offered to the blind maiden, it will lead her to the source of the distant light, allowing her to become a Finger Maiden.\n\nWhere to find Fingerprint Grape in Elden Ring\n\nCan be dropped by Festering Fingerprint Vyke. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Fingerprint Grape Use\nFingerprint Grape can be used to lead the blind maiden to become a Finger Maiden. This is used for Hyetta's questline. See her page for details.\n\nElden Ring Fingerprint Grape Notes & Tips\n\nMissable: If you kill the Erdtree Avatar, Fingerprint Vyke will not invade and thus will not drop Fingerprint Grape. This will make Hyetta's quest incompletable this playthrough.
