The Digger's Staff scales primarily with Intelligence and Strength and is a good Weapon for boosting the power of Stonedigger sorceries. The stats of this weapon include Sorcery Scaling which shows the degree which sorceries increase in power.\n\nStaff of a sorcerer-miner who extracts glintstone from crystal tunnels. The staff itself is a tool used to mine, and the ferrule is also embedded with a glintstone. \nBoosts the power of Stonedigger sorceries\n\nWhere to Find Digger's Staff in Elden Ring\nThe Digger's Staff weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by the Glintstone Miner enemies in the Sellia Crystal Tunnel. \n\nNote: If you enter this area via the teleport trap in the Dragon-Burnt Ruins, you cannot leave it until fighting or sneaking your way out entirely.\n\nDrops from Glintstone Miners in the Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel in Liurnia of the Lakes Region.
