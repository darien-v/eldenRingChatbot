\n\n Black Whetblade \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Grants choice of affinity upgrade to weapon \n\nBlack Whetblade is a Whetblade in Elden Ring. Black Whetblade grants choice of affinity upgrade to a weapon. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nBlack whetblade with a cipher engraved. Can be used as a whetstone knife.\nWhen applying an affinity using physical or occult-type ashes of war, an additional affinity of poison, blood, or occult can be chosen.\n\nWhere to find Black Whetblade in Elden Ring\n\nFound on a corpse slumped in front of an altar in Night's Sacred Ground. QUESTION
How can Black Whetblade be used to upgrade a weapon in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Black Whetblade can be used to add Poison, Blood, and Occult Affinities to a weapon by selecting compatible Ashes of War.
