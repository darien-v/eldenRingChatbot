CONTEXT
\n\n Wolf's Assault \n\nInfuse the greatsword with frost, then perform a forward somersault to plunge it into the ground. Then, pull it out to release a cold blast.\n\nWolf's Assault is a skill in Elden Ring that is unique to the Royal Greatsword. It allows the player to infuse the weapon with frost then perform a forward somersault to plunge it into the ground, pulling it out will release a cold blast. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow To Get Wolf's Assault\nWolf's Assault is the default ash of war for the Royal Greatsword Weapon.\n\nElden Ring Wolf's Assault Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 20\nDeals 23 stance damage.\nFrost build up is 100 on the weapon strike and 60 on the explosion.\nThe weapon hit deals pierce damage while the explosion deals standard damage.\nThis Skill is NOT Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nWearing Spellblade Set increases the damage\nAI enemies do not attempt to dodge this skill.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nPoise during casting has been increased.\n\nOther notes and tips go here\n\nRoyal Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Wolf's Assault Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
What are the effects and details of Wolf's Assault skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Infuse with frost, perform a somersault, hit for pierce damage, explosion for standard damage, 23 stance damage, frost build up 100 on strike, 60 on explosion, costs 20 FP, not chargeable, not parryable, increases with Spellblade Set, improved poise in Patch 1.07.
