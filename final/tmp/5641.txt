Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs.\nSee Skills for detailed information and a list of all skills.\n\nWhere to find About Adding Skills in Elden Ring\n\nAutomatically added to inventory after concept introduction.\n\nElden Ring About Adding Skills Tutorial Guide\n\nWith a Whetstone Knife, you can use ashes of war to grant your armaments new Skills at Sites of Grace.\nAn armament can only have one Skill.\nAny Skill it previously had will be removed.\nAn armament's type determines what Skills it can have.
