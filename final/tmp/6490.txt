Greatblade Phalanx spell summons greatblades that float around the caster and attack enemies when they move close enough. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the sorceries of the Carian royal family.Creates a defensive arch of larger magic glintblades overhead, which automatically attack nearby foes.Used by the enchanted troll knights.They were the comrades of the young Rennala, bound by oath.\n\nGreatblade Phalanx Location in Elden Ring\nGreatblade Phalanx is found in the following location:\n\n Cuckoo's Evergaol: Dropped by Bols, Carian Knight upon defeat. [Elden Ring Map Link] \n\nElden Ring Greatblade Phalanx Guide\n\nCarian Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 20\nDeals (Sorcery Scaling x 1.00) Magic Damage per blade\nEach blade deals 10.175 Stance damage (30.525 total)\nHowever, the Glintblade Phalanx Ash of War will do 10x4 stance damage, outperforming this spell, but of course taking up the skill slot of a non-staff weapon.\nThe blades will break upon coming into contact with any geometry, including enemies.
