\n\n Lesser Black Knife Assassin \n\nLocation\nLeyndell, Royal CapitalOrdina Liturgical Town\n\nDrops\nGhost Glovewort (9)\n\nLesser Black Knife Assassin is an Enemy in Elden Ring. These are non-Boss variants of Black Knife Assassins. They are relentless and agile dark figures. In some cases they're even invisible, requiring a certain item in order to be seen.\n\n The assassins that carried out the deeds of the Night of the Black Knives were all women, and rumored to be Numen who had close ties with Marika herself. \n\nElden Ring Lesser Black Knife Assassin Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Black Knife Assassin:\n\nBy the Queen's Bedchamber Site of Grace in Leyndell, Royal Capital: One sitting outside the west entrance to the room that doesn't respawn when killed. It does not drop anything.\nIn Ordina, Liturgical Town (Evergaol): Four invisible assassins patrol inside the evergaol. These will NOT respawn after being killed. \n\nElden Ring Lesser Black Knife Assassin Drops\n\nGhost Glovewort (9), only in the Ordina, Liturgical Town evergaol.\n\nLesser Black Knife Assassin Notes & Tips\n\nSentry's Torch makes all of the assassins visible, even if you don't meet the stat requirements. It is recommended to get this as soon as possible, as their grab attacks can be can deal massive damage and are near impossible to dodge without it. \nThe Ordina assassins are immune to Frostbite, but only while they're invisible.\n\nLesser Black Knife Assassin Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone Cluster  ♦  Albinauric  ♦  Albinauric Wolfback
