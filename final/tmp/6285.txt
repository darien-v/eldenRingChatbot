CONTEXT
\n\n Golden Slam \n\n        Skill of the avatars who protect Minor Erdtrees. Jump up high into the air and crash down on the ground ahead. The resulting pratfall sends golden shockwaves in all directions. \n\nGolden Slam is a Skill in Elden Ring. Golden Slam is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, it can be used on all melee armaments. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Golden Slam in Elden Ring\n\nGained by killing a Teardrop Scarab northwest of Woodfolk Ruins, near the Altus Plateau Erdtree. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Golden Slam Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Golden Slam.\nFP Cost: 22 on use\nDeals 46 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable.\nUpon activation, the user briefly crouches before leaping into the air with holy power, falling down and creating a shockwave at the point of impact. \n\nFrom activation until landing (assuming terrain is completely flat), it takes 2 seconds for the Weapon Skill to deal damage.\nThe skill has two hitboxes, the slam itself and the shockwave. \n\nThe slam deals physical damage and is boosted by physical damage-boosting buffs.\nThe shockwave deals holy damage and is boosted by holy damage-boosting buffs.\n\nCompared to Ground Slam, it does about 20% more damage, but about 42% of the damage is holy damage.\n\nConsider, however, that Ground Slam deals physical damage, while Golden Slam deals Holy damage.  Many bosses are highly resistant to Holy damage, and it may be more effective to use the "weaker" Ground Slam instead\n\nBoth the Physical hit and Holy Shockwave scale with Faith.\nHas a high poise modifier, allowing the player to not be staggered by most attacks.\nIf used without FP: \n\nProduces no shockwave on impact.\nDeals less damage on hit\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed in Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nThe time between using the skill and being able to roll have been reduced.\nAttack Power has been increased.\n\nGolden Slam Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric
