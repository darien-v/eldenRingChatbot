QUESTION
Why is the Prophet class in Elden Ring best suited for players focusing on Incantations and pure Faith builds?

ANSWER
The Prophet class in Elden Ring is best suited for players focusing on Incantations and pure Faith builds due to its high Faith, mind, and some arcane and strength stats, making it ideal for casting spells and utilizing faith-based abilities.
