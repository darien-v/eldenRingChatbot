\n\n Night-and-Flame Stance \n\n        Hold the sword level and prepare to cast a sorcery. Follow up with a normal attack to cast the Night Comet sorcery, or a strong attack to sweep forward with a burst of flames.  \n\nNight-and-Flame Stance is a Skill in Elden Ring. Night-and-Flame Stance is a unique skill on the Sword of Night and Flame that allows the user to hold the sword level and then have the option to follow up with a normal attack to cast the Comet Azur sorcery or follow up with a sweep flame attack causing Fire Damage. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Night-and-Flame Stance\n\nDefault skill on the Sword of Night and Flame.\n\nElden Ring Night-and-Flame Stance Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: - (26 32)\nComet stance deals 33 stance damageFlame stance deals 25 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried (both Light and Heavy attacks can be Parried with Carian Retaliation) \n\nBoth Light and Heavy attacks can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nThe "Night" stance deals Magic Damage and scales with Intelligence. It also receives a damage boost of 2% per worn piece of the Spellblade Set.\nThe "Flame" stance deals Fire Damage and scales with Faith.\nDespite the weapon's description, the "Night" stance actually casts Comet Azur instead of Night Comet.\nThe flame attack is exceptional at dealing with crowds, while the beam attack deals great single target damage.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nAttack power has been increased.\nThe attack direction may now be adjusted up and down when using a normal attack.\nDamage detection to the weapon part has been added.\n\nBuilds with Night-and-Flame Stance\n\nSword Sage Written Guide\n\nDeathblade Written Guide\n\nDeath Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nDeath Mage Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Night-and-Flame Stance Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can players effectively utilize Night-and-Flame Stance in combat situations?

ANSWER
Players can utilize Night-and-Flame Stance in combat by casting Comet Azur sorcery with normal attacks for single target damage, and using sweep flame attacks to deal with crowds effectively.
