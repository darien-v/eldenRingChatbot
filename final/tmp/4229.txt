\n\n Iron Whetblade \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Grants choice of affinity upgrade to weapon \n\nIron Whetblade is a Whetblade in Elden Ring. Iron Whetblade grants choice of affinity upgrade to weapon. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nIron whetblade with a cipher engraved. Can be used as a whetstone knife.\nWhen applying an affinity using ashes of war, an additional affinity of heavy, keen, or quality can be chosen.\n\nWhere to find Iron Whetblade in Elden Ring\n\nLocation: Stormveil Castle. See it on the Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be looted off a corpse that can be found in the large armory room, through the north-western door in the hall with the Grafted Scion.\n\nYou will need to use the Stonesword Key on the imp statue to dispel the white fog that's blocking the doorway.\n\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Iron Whetblade Use\nIron Whetblade adds the Heavy, Keen, and Quality Affinities to the menu when selecting compatible Ashes of War.\n\nElden Ring Iron Whetblade Notes & Tips\n\nIf you've missed this item: From the Rampart Tower Grace, head south all the way down the hall and take the lift on the left (east side) down and head north into the great hall with all the hanging bodies and the Grafted Scion, where you must immediately turn left (north west) and take the door out too a courtyard. Here you will see the imp statue locked door mentioned above \n\nElden Ring Whetblades\n\nBlack Whetblade  ♦  Glintstone Whetblade  ♦  Red-Hot Whetblade  ♦  Sanctified Whetblade  ♦  Whetstone Knife\n\n

QUESTION
Where can Iron Whetblade be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Iron Whetblade can be found in Stormveil Castle, looted off a corpse in the large armory room through the north-western door in the hall with the Grafted Scion.
