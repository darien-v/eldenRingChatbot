Instead, aim for its legs and try to stay underneath it or towards its back legs. Attack its back legs to try to bait out Kick or its front legs to try to bait out Hoof Stomp, then punish those attacks. Stay patient, and avoid getting in unnecessary hits; there are plenty of changes to do damage safely.\nMagic and Ranged Strategy\nKeep a moderate distance from the boss and you should have plenty of opportunities to get off shots. Roll back whenever it starts a combo with Antler Swing, then back up while you shoot it. You'll have plenty of time for hits during Spirit Vapor attacks and in the recovery for moves like Prancing Drop.\n\nAncestor Spirit Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nAntler Swing\nSwings its antlers along the ground in one direction or another\nRoll through the swings\n\nKick\nBriefly rears up on its hind legs, then puts its front legs down again and kicks behind it\nStart walking left or right when you see it rear up and this will miss you\n\nAntler Launch\nPlunges its antlers into the ground, then lifts them abruptly upwards\nRoll forward as it hunches down, then get in some hits as it recovers\n\nHoof Slam\nRises up on its hind legs and slams down its hooves, creating a shockwave\nRoll forward as it begins to descend, then get in a few hits during its long recovery\n\nPrancing Drop\nLeaps into the air and bounces twice in midair, hovers in the air for a second, then drops to the ground creating a shockwave\nAfter the second midair bounce, count to three then roll to either side to dodge the attack; or just run forward to end up behind the boss\n\nAirborne Spirit Vapor\nJumps into the air and runs in a semicircle around you, breathing damaging vapor that lingers on the ground\nKeep your distance and avoid going in the vapor\n\nGrounded Spirit Vapor\nWalks forward and breathes damaging vapor that lingers on the ground counterclockwise in front of it\nThis attack moves slowly, so just run away and avoid going in the vapor\n\nAncestor Spirit Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nOnce the boss is defeated, you can leave by interacting with a white light in the middle of its arena. QUESTION
What are the best strategies for defeating the Ancestor Spirit boss in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Use quick spirit ashes, utilize Rock Sling for its Magic resistance, bring a shield with 100% Physical defense as a last resort, wait to heal during Airborne Spirit Vapor, attack legs, roll back during Antler Swing, dodge Kick by moving left or right, roll forward when Antler Launch hunches down, roll forward during Hoof Slam's descent, dodge Prancing Drop by counting and rolling, keep distance during Spirit Vapor attacks.
