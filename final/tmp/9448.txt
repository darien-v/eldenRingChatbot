The Moonveil scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence and is a good Weapon for dealing melee Slash and Pierce Damage in Combat. Its accompanying unique skill can also allow the user to draw the weapon at great speed for an instant slash attack and fires off a wave of light.\n\nKatana forged of glintstone.Masterpiece of a Sellian swordsmith. \nLight enwreathes the blade when sheathed, explaining its Moonveil moniker.\n\nWhere to Find Moonveil in Elden Ring\nThe Moonveil weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nObtained upon defeating the Magma Wyrm located in Gael Tunnel - Caelid. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Moonveil Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Transient Moonlight - Sheathe blade, holding it at the hip in a composed stance. Follow up with either a normal or a strong attack to draw the blade at great speed for an instant slash attack. Both attacks fire off a wave of light, matching the orientation of the blade, with normal attacks firing a horizontal & short-range wave, and strong attacks firing a vertical & long-range wave that travels along the ground. The wave of light and melee swipe deal damage independently, allowing enemies to be hit by both for increased damage when in melee range. \nThis weapon can't be infused with Ashes of War\nCan't be enchanted with Magic nor boosted by Consumables\nCan be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones\nCauses Blood loss Buildup:  (50)\nSell Value:   500.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, Katanas poise damage has been reduced only on PVP.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
