Traveling Maiden Robe is part of the Traveling Maiden Set, and is a simple cloth robe garment worn by traveling maidens. Traveling Maiden Robe is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nRobe worn by traveling maidens. Maidens traversed the Lands Between for different reasons. Some to seek audience with the Fingers. Others, to find the Tarnished who they were destined to guide. \n\nTraveling Maiden Robe Location in Elden Ring\nThe Traveling Maiden Robe can be found in the following location:\n\nOn a corpse inside the Shack of The Lofty in the northern Mountaintops of the Giants. The rest of the set can also be looted here.
