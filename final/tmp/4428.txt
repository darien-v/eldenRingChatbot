QUESTION
What is the significance of obtaining and using the Remembrance of the Blasphemous item in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Remembrance of the Blasphemous item in Elden Ring can either be sold for 50,000 Runes, consumed for a great bounty of runes, or handed to Enia in Roundtable Hold to unlock powerful weapons associated with Rykard.
