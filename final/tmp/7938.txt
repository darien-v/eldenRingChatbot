\n\n Oracle Envoy Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 72\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons four Oracle Envoy spirits \n\nOracle Envoy Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Oracle Envoy Ashes are used to summon the spirits of four Oracle Envoys.\nOracle Envoys can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Oracle Envoy and Oracle Envoy Giant.\n\nAshen Remains in which spirits yet dwell. \nUse to summon the spirits of four Oracle Envoys.\nSpirits of a monstrous band of musicians who employ sacred arts. It is said that when Oracle Envoys appear, playing their pipes, they do so to herald the arrival of a new god, or age.\n\nWhere to find Oracle Envoy Ashes\nWhere to Find Oracle Envoy Ashes\n\nMiquella's Haligtree near Haligtree Town site of grace [Elden Ring Map]\nFrom the Haligtree Town site of grace, ascend the eastern ladder and run up the large branch past the scarlet rot flowers and avoiding the bubbles from the Giant Envoy behind you. Jump down to the left to a small branch from some fungi.  From there approach and kill the Giant Envoy that was above you.  Then continue upwards to the east where one Giant Envoy, one large Envoy, and two envoys guard the ashes.\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Oracle Envoy Ashes Guide\nUnless you want to watch your back when you run from the Haligtree Town site of grace, I suggest running to the ashes from the start of the area.  When you are running towards the second grace, you will start getting attacked from the big Oracle Envoy. Before the first attack reaches you, you can fall or jump to a lower branch on the right side. Follow that branch going upwards and you will find the ashes to the right being guarded by four Oracle Envoys. Two small ones, one medium, and a large one.\n\nElden Ring Spirits\n\nAlbinauric Ashes  ♦  Ancestral Follower Ashes  ♦  Ancient Dragon Knight Kristoff Ashes  ♦  Archer Ashes  ♦  Avionette Soldier Ashes  ♦  Azula Beastman Ashes  ♦  Banished Knight Engvall Ashes  ♦  Banished Knight Oleg Ashes  ♦  Battlemage Hugues Ashes  ♦  Blackflame Monk Amon Ashes  ♦  Bloodhound Knight Floh  ♦  Clayman Ashes  ♦  Cleanrot Knight Finlay Ashes  ♦  Crystalian Ashes  ♦  Demi-Human Ashes  ♦  Depraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes  ♦  Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet  ♦  Dung Eater Puppet  ♦  Fanged Imp Ashes  ♦  Finger Maiden Therolina Puppet  ♦  Fire Monk Ashes  ♦  Giant Rat Ashes  ♦  Glintstone Sorcerer Ashes  ♦  Godrick Soldier Ashes  ♦  Greatshield Soldier Ashes  ♦  Haligtree Soldier Ashes  ♦  Jarwight Puppet  ♦  Kindred of Rot Ashes  ♦  Land Squirt Ashes  ♦  Latenna The Albinauric  ♦  Leyndell Soldier Ashes  ♦  Lhutel the Headless  ♦  Lone Wolf Ashes  ♦  Mad Pumpkin Head Ashes  ♦  Man-Serpent Ashes  ♦  Marionette Soldier Ashes  ♦  Mausoleum Soldier Ashes  ♦  Mimic Tear Ashes  ♦  Miranda Sprout Ashes  ♦  Nepheli Loux Puppet  ♦  Nightmaiden & Swordstress Puppets  ♦  Noble Sorcerer Ashes  ♦  Nomad Ashes  ♦  Omenkiller Rollo  ♦  Page Ashes  ♦  Perfumer Tricia Ashes  ♦  Putrid Corpse Ashes  ♦  Radahn Soldier Ashes  ♦  Raya Lucaria Soldier Ashes  ♦  Redmane Knight Ogha Ashes  ♦  Rotten Stray Ashes  ♦  Skeletal Bandit Ashes  ♦  Skeletal Militiaman Ashes  ♦  Soldjars of Fortune Ashes  ♦  Spirit Jellyfish Ashes  ♦  Stormhawk Deenh  ♦  Twinsage Sorcerer Ashes  ♦  Vulgar Militia Ashes  ♦  Wandering Noble Ashes  ♦  Warhawk Ashes  ♦  Winged Misbegotten Ashes\n\n
