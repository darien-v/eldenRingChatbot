QUESTION
Where can Crystal Dart be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Crystal Dart can be found on a gravestone in Caria Manor, the Altus Tunnel, the Raya Lucaria Academy, Sellia Hideaway, the Consecrated Snowfield, the Converted Fringe Tower, the Liurnia Highway North, and the gorepile near Ainsel River Downstream.
