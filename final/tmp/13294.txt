Sorcerer Manchettes are an armor piece that protect both of the player's arms by applying various defensive properties, It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nManchettes worn by Raya Lucaria's magic scholars. Made by wrapping a simple cloth around the forearms.\n\nWhere to Find Sorcerer Manchettes in Elden Ring\nThe Sorcerer Manchettes Gauntlets can be found at the following location:\n\nThey are dropped by Glintstone Sorcerers and Twinsage Sorcerers in Raya Lucaria Academy.\n\nSorcerer Manchettes Armor Set in Elden Ring\n\nSorcerer Manchettes are a piece of the Raya Lucarian Sorcerer Set.\nThe rest of the set is composed of the Raya Lucarian Robe and Sorcerer Leggings.\nSorcerer Manchettes are also a piece of the Lazuli Sorcerer Set.\nThe rest of the Lazuli Sorcerer Set is composed of the Lazuli Glintstone Crown, Lazuli Robe, and Sorcerer Leggings.\n\nElden Ring Sorcerer Manchettes Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value: 100\nSorcerer Manchettes grant the lowest defenses to Physical Defense, VS Strike Defense, VS Slash Defense, VS Pierce Defense, Magic Defense, Fire Defense, Lightning Defense and Holy Defense of all Gauntlets available in the Network test. \nSorcerer Manchettes grant the highest Immunity of the Network test, alongside the Champion Bracers, Carian Knight Gauntlets and Raging Wolf Gauntlets.\nSorcerer Manchettes are the lightest Gauntlets available in the Network Test.\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
