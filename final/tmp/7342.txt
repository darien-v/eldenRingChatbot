\n\n Ash of War: Vow of the Indomitable \n\nAffinity\nSacred\n\nSkill\nVow of the Indomitable\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Vow of the Indomitable is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Vow of the Indomitable provides Sacred affinity and the Vow of the Indomitable Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Sacred affinity and the following skill:\n"Vow of the Indomitable: Skill of the ancient warriors of the Erdtree. Hold shield aloft to imbue yourself with golden power, granting momentary invincibility."\nUsable on all shields.\n\nAsh of War: Vow of the Indomitable Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Vow of the Indomitable:\n\nLiurnia of the Lakes: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab, in the Southeast poisonous swamp area of the map, south from the Folly on the Lake Site of Grace. The scarab is located in the center of an island, near an opening in the rocky overpass. [Map link]\n\nAsh of War: Vow of the Indomitable Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Vow of the Indomitable.\nProvides the Sacred affinity, which adds Holy Damage and Faith scaling, decreases Physical Damage, and decreases most damage negation.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Lightning, Sacred.\nCan be applied to the following shield categories, so long as the shield allows skill replacement: Small Shields, Medium Shields, and Greatshields.\n\nVow of the Indomitable Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Vow of the Indomitable Notes & Tips\n\nInvincibility begins are 4 start-up frames and lasts for 30 iFrames with 2
