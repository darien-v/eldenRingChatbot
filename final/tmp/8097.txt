Guardian Bracers is an armor piece that protect both of the player's arms by applying various defensive properties, It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nEngraved golden bracers.Worn by the guardians of the Minor Erdtree.\nIn accordance with an ancient pact with the Erdtree, it is said that their deaths led not to destruction, but instead to renewed, eternal life as guardians.\n\nWhere to find Guardian Bracers in Elden Ring\nThe Guardian Bracers Armor Gauntlets piece can be found at the following location:\n\nGuardian Set pieces can be acquired as drops from Erdtree Guardians that are found near Minor Erdtrees in the Lands Between. \n\nAn area dense with Guardians can be found by starting at the Dragonbarrow Fork site of grace, and working your way uphill towards the Minor Erdtree. Guardians are encountered in small groups and more come up from the ground, making it a good place to farm the set.\nChance it drops from one of the multiple Erdtree Guardians at the minor tree in Weeping Peninsula.\n\nGuardian Bracers Set in Elden Ring\n\nGuardian Bracers is a piece of the Guardian Set\nThe rest of the set is composed by the Guardian Mask, Guardian Garb, and Guardian Greaves.\n\nElden Ring Guardian Bracers Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value: 100\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Where can Guardian Bracers be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Guardian Bracers can be found by starting at the Dragonbarrow Fork site of grace and heading uphill towards the Minor Erdtree, where they drop from Erdtree Guardians near Minor Erdtrees in the Lands Between, including the Weeping Peninsula.
