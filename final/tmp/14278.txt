Guardian Garb is part of the Guardian Set Armor Set, and is the armor worn by the Guardians found around various Minor Erdtrees. Guardian Garb is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nEngraved golden garb.Worn by guardians of the Minor Erdtree.\nIn accordance with an ancient pact with the Erdtree, it is said that their deaths led not to destruction, but instead to renewed, eternal life as guardians.\n\nWhere to find Guardian Garb in Elden Ring\nThe Guardian Garb Chest Armor can be found at:\n\nGuardian Set pieces can be acquired as drops from Erdtree Guardians that are found near Minor Erdtrees in the Lands Between.\nAn area dense with Guardians can be found by starting at the Dragonbarrow Fork site of grace, and working your way uphill towards the Minor Erdtree. Guardians are encountered in small groups and more come up from the ground, making it a good place to farm the set.\nChance it drops from one of the multiple Erdtree Guardians at the minor tree in QUESTION
Where can I find the Guardian Garb in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Guardian Garb in Elden Ring can be found as drops from the Erdtree Guardians near Minor Erdtrees in the Lands Between, specifically starting at the Dragonbarrow Fork site of grace and moving uphill towards the Minor Erdtree, as well as a chance it drops from Erdtree Guardians at the minor tree in Weeping Peninsula.
