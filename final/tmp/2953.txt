QUESTION
What is the significance of the Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing (5) not existing in relation to the level of stone usage in normal weapons?

ANSWER
The significance is that normal weapons go only up to level 24 using stones up to 8, which is fulfilled by the 4th level bell bearing.
