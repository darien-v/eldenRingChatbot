\n\n Magma Guillotine \n\n        Grab the scalesword with both hands, as a wyrm would hold it in its mouth, and leap forwards, smashing it into the ground and triggering a blast of magma. Follow up with an additional input to perform a chopping attack. \n\nMagma Guillotine is a Skill in Elden Ring. Magma Guillotine is a Damaging skill accompanied by Fire Damage and is unique to the Magma Wyrm's Scalesword. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Magma Guillotine\n\nDefault skill on the Magma Wyrm's Scalesword.\n\nElden Ring Magma Guillotine Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 20 ( - 10)\nDeals 47 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nThis Skill is not affected by the Claw Talisman despite appearing to be a jump attack\nThis Skill scales with weapon upgrade level, strength and faith.\nThe damage of this Skill is increased by Shard of Alexander and Fire Scorpion talismans, although the fact that the majority of the damage is physical reduces the latter's efficacy.\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Poise damage and stamina attack power against guarding enemies for the first attack have been increased.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nDrake Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Magma Guillotine Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
What are the specific details and effects of the Magma Guillotine skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Magma Guillotine in Elden Ring is a damaging skill that costs 20 FP, deals 47 stance damage, is not chargeable, cannot be parried, scales with weapon upgrade level, strength, and faith, and its damage is increased by specific talismans.
