\n\n Lightning Spear \n\nSpell Type\nDragon Cult Incantations\n\nFP Cost 18\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Hurls lightning spear before caster \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 17 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nLightning Spear  is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Lightning Spear spell creates a lightning projectile and throws it forward. Gravel Stone Seal increases its damage. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the incantations of the capital's ancient dragon cult. Summons a lightning spear and hurls it before the caster.Charging causes a lightning bolt to strike the point of impact.Long ago, Godwyn the Golden defeated the ancient dragon Fortissax, and befriended his fallen foe — an event that gave rise to the ancient dragon cult in the capital.\n\nWhere to find Lightning Spear\nWhere to find Lightning Spear :\n\nPurchase from Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows after giving either the Dragon Cult Prayerbook which is dropped by a Leyndell Knight patrolling south of Artist's Shack.\n\nElden Ring Lightning Spear Guide\n\nStamina Cost: 28\nDeals Lightning Damage, therefore is very useful against knight-type enemies/bosses.\nOne of the Dragon Cult Incantations and is boosted by the Gravel Stone Seal\nEnemies seem to ignore this incantation in terms of dodging, meaning under most circumstances Lightning Spear will generally make a successful hit against the enemy. This applies to both charged and uncharged variations.\nCharging allows you to add a small AOE to this single target spell.
