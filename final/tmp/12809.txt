\n\n Axe Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.8\n\nEffect \n\n Increases Charge Attack damage by 10% \n\nAxe Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Axe Talisman increases charged attack damage by 10%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting an axe and a warrior.Enhances charge attacks.\nThe Lord who led the Long March bore an axe, and his loyal warriors honored him by wielding axes of their own, making them very effective at dealing decisive blows.\n\nAxe Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nAxe Talisman increases charged attack damage by 10%.\n\nWhere to find Axe Talisman in Elden Ring\n\nFound in a cellar underneath the Mistwood Ruins in eastern Limgrave.
