Rotten Duelist Set is a three piece set worn by Rotten Duelists. It is a Scarlet Rot corrupted variant of the Duelist Set. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Rotten Duelist Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Rotten Duelist Set pieces are each earned by killing a different Rotten Duelist.\nThe Rotten Duelist Helm is acquired by killing the Rotten Duelist northwest of the Consecrated Snowfield site of grace. [Elden Ring Map here]\nThe Rotten Gravekeeper Cloak is acquired by killing the dungeon boss of the Consecrated Snowfield Catacombs, Putrid Grave Warden Duelist. [Elden Ring Map here]\nThe Rotten Duelist Greaves are acquired by killing the Rotten Duelist northwest of Ordina, Lirtugical Town on the cliffside. [Elden Ring Map here]\nEach enemy only needs to killed once to be awarded the gear.\n\nRotten Duelist Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nRotten Duelist Helm\nRotten Gravekeeper Cloak\nRotten Duelist Greaves\n\nRotten Duelist Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Rotten Gravekeeper Cloak (Altered) removes the cloak, leaving only the snake bracelets, giving the set the appearance of the Rotten Duelists found in the overworld.\nIt has particularly high Immunity and decent Robustness for its weight.\nSlightly lighter, with slightly better resistances but weaker damage negations than its counterpart, Duelist Set.\nEach piece of this set provides the effect similar to Shabriri's Woe, drawing the aggro of nearby enemies. \n\nThe Rotten Gravekeeper Cloak only has the effect while altered.\nThe Greaves increase Target Priority by 0,04, the Altered Cloak by 0,03. The value of the helmet is unknown but it is safe to assume it is 0,03. This will then become a total of 0,1 increase in Target Priority - becoming equal to Shabriri's Woe.\n\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Where can the Rotten Duelist Set pieces be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Rotten Duelist Set pieces are earned by killing specific Rotten Duelists; Helm northwest of Consecrated Snowfield site, Cloak from Putrid Grave Warden Duelist in Catacombs, and Greaves northwest of Ordina in Lirtugical Town.
