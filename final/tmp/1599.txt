Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are typically composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Juvenile Scholar Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Juvenile Scholar Set can be obtained by first obtaining the Resurrection Painting at the Artist's Shack in eastern Liurnia. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nWith the painting on hand, head to the graveyard area east of Caria Manor. This location is only accessible after completing the manor and defeating Royal Knight Loretta. The set is found by touching a ghost sitting on a chair at the graveyard.
