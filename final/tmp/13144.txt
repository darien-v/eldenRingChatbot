QUESTION
How can players effectively utilize the Unsheathe ability to enhance the power of the Samurai class in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Unsheathe is an extremely powerful ability for Samurai in Elden Ring; players should learn to use it effectively, thinning out enemies with arrows and finishing them with the Uchigatana, and consider dual wielding Uchigatanas found in Deathtouched Catacombs for increased power.
