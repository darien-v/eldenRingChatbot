Edgar appears as the player arrives at the Revenger's Shack, after interacting with the Site of Grace and completing Irina and Edgar's questline.\n\n...Ahh the poor fellow... Gone perfectly mad. Love... Revenge....the frenzied flame melts it all away...\n- Spirit\n\nEdgar the Revenger Location in Elden Ring\nEdgar the Revenger can be found in the following location:\n\nRevenger's Shack in western Liurnia. This shack is found slightly north of the Minor Erdtree. [Elden Ring Map here]Interacting with the Site of Grace by the shack will cause Edgar to invade the player.\n\nElden Ring Edgar the Revenger Drops\nEdgar the Revenger drops the following upon defeat:\n\n316  Runes\nFurlcalling Finger Remedy\nShabriri Grape\nBanished Knight's Halberd (+8)\nRaw Meat Dumpling x 5\n\nEdgar the Revenger Notes & Tips\n\nYou may miss this NPC if you rest at the Site of Grace and then relocate, as he will not re-invade if you go back.This has been fixed in the recent Patch 1.04\nUses a Banished Knight’s Halberd with the Spinning Strikes skill, along with a Frenzied Flame Seal to cast Golden Vow and Wrath of Gold.\nNote that this is the only way to obtain the Spinning Strikes Ash of War.\nThis is the same Edgar  of Castle Morne. \nIt is confirmed that he has gone mad due to frenzy, since he drops a Shabriri Grape upon defeat, which are human eyes of those corrupted by the Frenzied Flame, along with a spirit saying that he had “gone perfectly mad.”\nHis weapon drop is already upgraded at +8. This will affect multiplayer matchmaking if you didn't own a weapon with that high of an upgrade level, since it remembers the highest upgrade level weapon you ever owned. Discarding or storing doesn't influence this, so keep this in mind before defeating this NPC. Also Note, the drops aren't dropped on the ground like in some cases, but automatically placed in inventory\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone QUESTION
How can players trigger Edgar the Revenger to invade them in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Interacting with the Site of Grace by the Revenger's Shack in Liurnia of the Lakes triggers Edgar the Revenger to invade players in Elden Ring.
