Vyke will invade the player on the way up the path to the church. QUESTION
Why does Festering Fingerprint Vyke use The Flame of Frenzy, Frenzied Burst, and Howl of Shabriri in battle?

ANSWER
Festering Fingerprint Vyke uses The Flame of Frenzy, Frenzied Burst, and Howl of Shabriri in battle to deal Pierce Damage, Fire Damage, and inflict Madness.
