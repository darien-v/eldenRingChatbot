QUESTION
What are the optimal builds for the Bandit class in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Optimal builds for the Bandit class in Elden Ring are Dexterity/Arcane hybrid builds needing less than 12 Strength or a dedicated pure Arcane Reduvia build.
