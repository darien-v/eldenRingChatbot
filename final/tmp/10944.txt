QUESTION
Why is Carian Slicer considered one of the highest DPS outputs in the game despite its weaknesses in poise damage and moveset variety?

ANSWER
Carian Slicer is considered one of the highest DPS outputs due to having the highest magic damage per FP ratio compared to other spells, offering swift startup, and being able to be used without delay after other actions.
