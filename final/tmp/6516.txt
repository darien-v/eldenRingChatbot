\n\n Bloodboon Ritual \n\n        Raise the sacred spear and pierce the body of the Formless Mother. Stab up to three times, creating explosions of blood with each thrust. \n       This skill will coat the armament with bloodflame for a while. \n\nBloodboon Ritual is a Skill in Elden Ring. Bloodboon Ritual is a special skill only available for an individual weapon, and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Bloodboon Ritual in Elden Ring\n\nThis Skill can only be acquired by equipping Mohgwyn's Sacred Spear.\n\nElden Ring Bloodboon Ritual Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 20 (-20)\nDeals 21 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\n"Bloodflame" deals Fire Damage and inflicts Blood Loss buildup\nAdds a flat 30 AR of fire damage to the weapon, before modifiers. \nEach of the three inputs of the weapon art consist of multiple individual hits.  The third thrust will deal increase damage and has a longer recovery.\nDespite its wide AoE, its single target damage is quite high, meaning it is not limited to just crowd clearing\nDamage scales with weapon upgrade level and Arcane.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nThe range of the damage animation trigger on other players has been reduced.\nDamage is unchanged.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Bloodboon Ritual Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why would one need to equip Mohgwyn's Sacred Spear in order to acquire the Bloodboon Ritual skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
