\n\n Collapsing Stars \n\nSpell Type\nGravity Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 18\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Pulls foes toward caster with gravity projectile volley \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 36 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nCollapsing Stars  is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Collapsing Stars spell is a gravity manipulating spell that directly affects foes in range.\n\nOne of the glintstone sorceries that manipulates gravitational forces.\nFires numerous gravitational projectiles. Any foes struck will be pulled toward the caster. Charging enhances potency.\nA gravitational technique mastered by the young Radahn. "I thank you for your tutelage, for now I can challenge the stars."\n\nWhere to find Collapsing Stars\nWhere to find Collapsing Stars :\n\nLooted from a chest located at the War-Dead Catacombs. In the initial room with the enemies fighting each other, drop down to the lower level filled with scarlet rot and look for the chest, it will be in the center of the floor of the West most wall. \nThis catacomb is located in the northmost edge of Starscourge Radahn arena after beating him, just south of Lenne's Rise.
