How do you obtain all pieces of the Bandit Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Bandit Mask can be purchased from Nomadic Merchant, and Bandit Garb, Manchettes, and Boots are sold by Gatekeeper Gostoc and Twin Maiden Husks in Elden Ring.
