QUESTION
How can one acquire the Page Trousers Leg Armor in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Page Trousers Leg Armor can be obtained by defeating Page enemies at various locations such as the Royal Moongazing Grounds, Leyndell, Tombsward Ruins Underground, and Gelmir Hero's Grave in Elden Ring.
