CONTEXT
\n\n Corpse Piler \n\n        Forms a blade of cursed blood for repeated, interweaving successive attacks. Follow up with an additional input for further successive attacks. \n\nCorpse Piler is a Skill in Elden Ring. Corpse Piler is a special skill that is only available for the Rivers of Blood, and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Corpse Piler in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill for the Rivers of Blood.\n\nElden Ring Corpse Piler Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Weapon Skill\nFP Cost: 17 on use \n\n9 FP cost for the next two following inputs.\nUsing all the inputs totals to a 35 FP cost (17 + 9 + 9).\n\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nAll 6 separate slashes can be Parried\nUpon activation, the user slashes twice to the front in an X motion from a low stance. \n\nInputting the Skill button again allows for another follow up, with the user making a brief spin before slashing thrice side-to-side.\nInputting the Skill button after this allows for one last follow up, where the user makes an overhead slash all the way to the ground.\nThe total amount of skill inputs are 3, for a total of 6 hits.\nThe moveset is very similar to that of Double Slash, however, each slash leaves a trail of blood.\nThe trail of blood can go through walls and pillars and hit enemies hiding behind.\n\nEach and every single slash of this Weapon Skill leave behind trails of blood, which increases its effective range.\nDeals 9 stance damage per slash, 32 total on a full combo.\nDeals Physical + Fire damage with blood buildup per hit. \n\nThe Fire damage is significantly higher than the physical portion of the damage.\nThe Fire damage and bleed buildup scale with Arcane stat.\n\nThis weapon Skill can be parried as long as the parry tool meets the weapon itself.\nPatch 1.06 decreased the damage and bleed build-up of this weapon skill when hit with the blood attack. When hit by the blade, the damage is only decreased slightly.\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Attack power has been slightly increased.\n\nSanguine Samurai Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Corpse Piler Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Fire damage significantly higher than the physical portion of the damage in the Corpse Piler Skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Fire damage in Corpse Piler is significantly higher than physical damage due to scaling with the Arcane stat.
