\n\n Greenburst Crystal Tear \n\nType\nCrystal Tear\n\nEffect \n\n Temporarily boosts stamina recovery speed in mixed physick. \n\nGreenburst Crystal Tear is a Crystal Tear in Elden Ring. Crystal Tears can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to enhance its effect.\n\nA crystal tear formed slowly over the ages where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground.\nCan be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick.The resulting concoction temporarily boosts stamina recovery speed. \n\nGreenburst Crystal Tear Use in Elden Ring\nGreenburst Crystal Tear increases Stamina recovery speed by 15 per second for 3 minutes.\n\nGreenburst Crystal Tear Location in Elden Ring\nThe Greenburst Crystal Tear can be found in the following location:\n\nDropped by the Putrid Avatar found at the Minor Erdtree in Caelid, east of Smoldering Church.
