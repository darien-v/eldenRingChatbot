\n\n Dominula, Windmill Village \n\nLocated in northern Altus Plateau, many have heard ominous laughter from the locals, who dance as a part of their festival.\n\nDominula, Windmill Village is a Location in Elden Ring. The Dominula, Windmill Village  is found in Altus Plateau. East of the "Road of Iniquity Side Path" Site of Grace. Follow the main path southeast, then bend east. Despite the jolly portrayal of the Celebrants, this place holds a sinister secret. \n\nNorth Altus Plateau Map\nElden Ring Map\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Dominula, Windmill Village\n\nMillicent - Summonable cooperator. Most of them are generally not hostile but be prepared, because a group of them may change upon approach. They are brutal in packs so try to take them out slowly one by one or if you have slash weapons maybe 3 at a time. There is 1 Rotten Stray in the pack so be prepared for them. Be sure to check building tops for small paths leading to treasure. Clear these dancers to gain a chance to obtain their garments and equipment. Though Drop rate chances are slim, those attempting to farm in this location can note that the Festive Dancers wield the following equipment: \n\nCelebrant's Cleaver\nCelebrant's Sickle\nCelebrant's Rib-Rake\nCelebrant's Skull (they do not drop this)\nBlue Festive Hood\nBlue Festive Garb\nFestive Hood\nFestive Garb\nFestive Garb (Altered)\n\nGuaranteed Celebrant's Skull\nNorth of the Village is also a narrow walkable Cliffside. Follow this route to obtain the Celebrant's Skull. [Map Link] The Golden Seed can be found at a phantom tree southwest of Windmill Village Site of Grace (following the road). [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nBoss: Godskin Apostle\nFollow the path to the top to fight the Godkin Apostle. After the enemy falls, you can obtain Godskin Peeler and Scouring Black Flame from him. [Map Link] Millicent can also be found around this location at some point during her questline. This is known to be her fourth location, and the Godskin Apostle must be defeated for her to appear. [Map Link] In this area, players may also choose to take a rest at the Windmill Heights Site of Grace.\n\nFire's Deadly Sin\nAfter having seen the Flightless Bird Painting in Leyndell, Royal Capital, you can return and head south following the village houses along the cliffside. Just behind one of the houses you will find a seated spirit from whom you can obtain the spell, Fire's Deadly Sin. [Elden Ring Interactive Map link]\n\nElden Ring Dominula, Windmill Village Gallery QUESTION
Why do the locals in Dominula, Windmill Village dance as a part of their festival despite the ominous laughter heard from them?

ANSWER
The locals dance as part of the festival in Dominula, Windmill Village despite the ominous laughter as a cover for the sinister secret the village holds.
