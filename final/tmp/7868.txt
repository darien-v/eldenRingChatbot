CONTEXT
\nReapers are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Reapers have long reach and a unique sweeping moveset. These weapons are particularly useful for sneaking damage behind shields, when swung with proper spacing.\nReapers can be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, so play around with them and see what you like as you acquire more.\n\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, the speed of some attacks has been increased and the recovery time has been reduced.\n\nElden Ring Reaper Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Whips in Elden Ring. QUESTION
Why should players experiment with different ashes of war to modify the skills and damage type of reapers in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players should experiment with different ashes of war to modify the skills and damage type of reapers in Elden Ring to find what suits their playstyle due to the unique sweeping moveset and long reach of reapers, making them effective behind shields with proper spacing.
