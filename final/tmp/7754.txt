Bloodflame Blade spell enhances your weapon with festering bloodflame. After dealing damage, weapons imbued with bloodflame apply a debuff to their enemy that continues to build up onset of Hemorrhage for a very short time.\n\nA Blood Oath incantation, granted by the Lord of Blood.\nEngulfs armament held in the right hand with bloodflame.This incantation can be cast while in motion.\nAfter dealing damage, bloodflame continues to build up onset of blood loss for a very short time.\n\nWhere to find Bloodflame Blade\n\n Liurnia of the Lakes: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the marshes northwest to Rose Church, southwest of the lake. QUESTION
Why is the Bloodflame Blade spell most effective on weapons that already have the Hemorrhage effect?

ANSWER
Bloodflame Blade spell is most effective on weapons with the Hemorrhage effect because it can grant the Hemorrhage status effect to any weapon it is applied to, stacking bleed over time and boosting overall damage output.
