The Inseparable Sword scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity and Faith and is a good Weapon for Faith Builds. \n\nSword forged by compounding silver and gold.A sacred weapon to hunt Those Who Live in Death.Deals holy damage.\nThe inseparable twins found solace in the Golden Order, the only institution not to revile them as accursed beings.\n\nWhere to Find Inseparable Sword in Elden Ring\nThe Inseparable Sword weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nPrince of Death's Throne, after giving D's brother D's Twinned Armour. You must have killed Lichdragon Fortissax in Fia's Dream and received the Mending Rune of Death. [Map Link]\ncan be acquired slightly earlier if you kill D when he appears in Limgrave, close to the Summonwater Village Outskirts. Doing this allows you to give his armor to his brother in Siofra Aqueduct and after reloading the area, the brother will be hostile to you. Kill him to get the Inseparable Sword and Twinned Armor back. This does not break Fias questline.\n\nElden Ring Inseparable Sword Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Sacred Blade\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War\nCan't be enchanted with Magic nor boosted with Consumables.\nInseparable Sword can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones\nThis weapon, alongside the Banished Knight's Greatsword and Knight's Greatsword features an alternate Greatsword moveset with unique two-handed R1 attacks. This moveset is drawn from Dark Souls 2's Mirrah Greatsword and Dark Souls 3's Hollowslayer Greatsword. \nThe Inseparable Sword is the only greatsword that is placed on the hip when sheathed.\nDeals +20% damage to  Undead type enemies and prevents Skeletons from reviving\nAt 80 Faith has the highest Attack Rating of all Holy Damage dealing Greatswords, if you have not invested in Strength or Dexterity much.\nSell Value:   200\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
