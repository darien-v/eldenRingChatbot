QUESTION
What is the significance of Robustness Resistance in combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Robustness Resistance in Elden Ring is significant as it shows your resistance to hemorrhage and frostbite, reducing the chance of succumbing to such ailments and allowing you to stay in combat without needing to retreat for recovery.
