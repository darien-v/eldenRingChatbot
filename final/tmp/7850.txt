CONTEXT
\nSpirit Ashes in Elden Ring are companions, often mimicking the Lands Between's Enemies, summoned by the the Spirit Calling Bell to aid you in Combat. This page covers how they function, the spirit ashes available, summon costs, and a community-driven list of their relative strength.\n\nHow to use Spirit Ashes\nTo use Spirit Ashes, players must first obtain the Spirit Calling Bell from Renna at the Church of Elleh [Map Link].\nSimilar to Items, Spirit Ashes can be equipped to and used from your Quick Slots or Pouch. However, they can only be used while not in Multiplayer Coop and when near a Rebirth Monument.\nCan you Summon Two Ashes in Elden Ring?\nAfter summoning in a given area, you are not allowed to summon again in that area until you rest at any Sites of Grace or Fast Travel.\n\nPlayers may reuse the Spirit Ashes to dismiss a summon.\nLeaving the boundary of a valid summoning area will dismiss any active spirits.\nSpirits may only be summoned once per encounter, so you cannot re-summon them after they die during a boss fight.\nSpirits cannot be summoned while the effects of the Furlcalling Finger Remedy are active. Use the remedy again to cancel its effects.\nSummoning spirits require either FP or HP depending on the summon.\n\nMonument to Summon Spirits\n\nPlayers must be near a Rebirth Monument, stone obelisks found at many Points of Interest and nearly every boss arena, to use Spirit Ashes.\nA monument icon, depicted below, will appear on the left side of the screen when conditions are met:\n\nUnlocking Spirit Ashes Upgrades\nRoderika is the only NPC capable of upgrading Spirit Ashes. She offers her services after a short side quest:\n\nTalk to Roderika at the Stormhill Shack. [Map Link]\nHead to Stormveil Castle and collect Chrysalids' Memento. [Map Link]\nReturn to Roderika and return the momento.\nTravel to the Roundtable Hold, and talk to her and to Blacksmith Hewg several times until you exhaust their dialogue options.\nReload the area by leaving or other means.\nRoderika will now be sat across from Blacksmith Hewg and is able to upgrade your Spirit Ashes\n\nUpgrading Spirit Ashes\nSpirit Ash Upgrades universally increase the summons' Attack Power and Defenses.\nOf the 64 Spirit Ashes, there are two different types of Spirit Ashes, each with their own Upgrade Materials:\n\n42 Standard Spirit Ashes - Grave Gloveworts\n22 Renowned Spirit Ashes - Ghost Gloveworts\n\nGiant Rat Ashes are unique in that enhancing the spirit ashes will increase their numbers.\n\nAll Legendary Spirit Ashes in Elden Ring\nTo unlock the Legendary Ashen Remains Trophy and Achievement, you will need to acquire the following Spirit Ashes:\n\nLhutel the Headless\nBlack Knife Tiche\nRedmane Knight Ogha\nMimic Tear Ashes\nAncient Dragon Knight Kristoff Ashes\nCleanrot Knight Finlay Ashes\n\nSpirit Ashes in PVP\nWith the arrival of Patch 1.08, Spirit Ashes can be used for PVP, but ONLY at matches held at the Caelid Colosseum. QUESTION
How do Spirit Ashes in Elden Ring function in combat?

ANSWER
Spirit Ashes in Elden Ring function in combat by being companions that mimic Lands Between's Enemies, summoned using the Spirit Calling Bell to assist in battle, each with varying abilities, attack power, defenses, and summon costs.
