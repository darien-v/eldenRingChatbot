\n\n Carian Piercer \n\nSpell Type\nCarian Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 17\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Impales foes with magical greatsword \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 27 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nCarian Piercer is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Carian Piercer spell is cast to create an offensive magic sword that thrusts forward. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the sorceries of the Carian royal family.Conjures a magic greatsword to impale foes.Charging lengthens the initial step in.\nThis sorcery forms a counterpart to Carian Greatsword.Highly lethal when used against single foes riding alone.\n\nCarian Piercer Location in Elden Ring\nCarian Piercer can be found in the following location:\n\nCaria Manor: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in an area behind the gardens, to the east. [Map Link] Video Location\n\nElden Ring Carian Piercer Guide\n\nCarian Sorcery\n\nCan be boosted by the Carian Glintstone Staff\n\nStamina Cost: 25\nDeals (Sorcery Scaling x 2.87) Magic Damage uncharged and (Sorcery Scaling x 3.55) when charged\nWill knockdown enemies when fully charged.\nHas a lingering Hitbox that is useful for roll catching.\nCounts as 2 hits towards "Succesive Attack" Talismans. (i.e. Winged Sword Insignia, Godskin Swaddling Cloth, and Millicent's Prosthesis.)\nCan be used in conjunction with Carian Greatsword to expand the moveset, by performing a thrust after the horizontal slash.\nNote: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost has been reduced to 17 from 19, damage increased slightly and the stamina damage against guarding enemies has been increased.
