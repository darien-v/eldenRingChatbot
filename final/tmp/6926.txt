\n\n Smithing Stone [1] \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce armaments up to +3 \n\nSmithing Stone [1] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring used for reinforcing equipment to +1, +2, and +3.  Reinforced equipment boasts better stats.  Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nStone used to smith a variety of armaments. A shard found in plenty.\nStrengthens armaments up to +3.\nSmithing stone is found throughout the Lands Between, and mining galleries built to excavate it can be found everywhere.\n\nWhere to find Smithing Stone [1] in Elden Ring: All Locations Guide\nSmithing Stones [1] are exceptionally common, as they are found throughout the Lands Between.
