Bloodboon spell scatters bloodflame pools before caster to set area aflame. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSacred incantation of Mohg, Lord of Blood.\nThrust arm into the body of the Formless Mother, then scatter the bloodflame to set the area ablaze.\nThis incantation can be cast while in motion.\nThe mother of truth craves wounds. When Mohg stood before her, deep underground, his accursed blood erupted with fire, and besotted with the defilement that he was born into.\n\nWhere to find Bloodboon\nWhere to find Bloodboon:\n\nTrade Remembrance of the Blood Lord with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Bloodboon Guide\n\nBlood Incantation\nStamina Cost: 34\nDeals fire damage\nCan be used to spread blood flames in front of the caster and create Status-inflicting area of denial.\nThe trick to using this incantation is not to use target lock-on and free aim above the intended target, which will make the projectiles land ON the target instead of hitting their feet.\nThe lingering flames will cause Hemorrhage build-up (30/s) on enemies.\nThis build-up stacks with each instance of the blood pool, making this spell highly effective at dealing with crawlers like Magma Wyrms and other larger enemies.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 15 to 13, and attack power has been increased.\n\nElden Ring Bloodboon Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can Bloodboon be effectively used in combat situations in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Bloodboon in Elden Ring can spread blood flames to create an area of denial, deal fire damage, cause Hemorrhage build-up on enemies, and is best used without target lock-on to ensure projectiles land accurately.
