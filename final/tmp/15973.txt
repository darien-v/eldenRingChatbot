QUESTION
Why is the Golden Halberd considered a good weapon for dealing Holy Damage in addition to Physical Damage during combat?

ANSWER
The Golden Halberd is considered a good weapon for dealing Holy Damage because it scales primarily with Strength, Faith, and Dexterity, and is complemented with the Golden Vow Skill that increases attack power and defense stats, making it effective in combat.
