\n\n Marionette Soldier Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 67\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons two marionette soldier spirits \n\nMarionette Soldier Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Marionette Soldier Ashes summons two marionettes to fight for you.\nMarionette Soldiers can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information see Marionette Soldier.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirits of two marionette soldiers.\nSpirits of marionette soldiers created to serve a sorcerer.These marionettes are archers, each equipped with a pair of bows. On the verge of falling apart, they are ill—disciplined and attack without warning.\n\nWhere to find Marionette Soldier Ashes\nWhere to Find Marionette Soldier Ashes\n\nLocation: Raya Lucaria Academy\nNearest Site of Grace: Church of the Cuckoo\nWhen you reach the graveyard section, as you cross the other side of the bridge, make a hard left and drop down to find a path that curves to where the Marionette Soldiers are, kill the two soldiers and then loot the corpse that is hanging by the ledge to find this item.
