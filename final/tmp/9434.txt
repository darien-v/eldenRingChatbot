CONTEXT
\nKatanas are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Katanas specializes in mid-range combat and are capable of inflicting slash and thrust attacks, as well as the Hemorrhage Status Effect in many cases, which can deal significant damage. Many players choose this weapon type for its unique moveset.\nKatanas can be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, so play around with them and see what you like as you acquire more.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, Katanas poise damage has been reduced only on PVP.\n\nElden Ring Katana Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Katana in Elden Ring. QUESTION
Why would a player choose the Katana weapon type in Elden Ring over other weapon types?

ANSWER
Many players choose Katanas in Elden Ring for its unique moveset, mid-range combat specialization, ability to inflict slash and thrust attacks, Hemorrhage Status Effect, and flexibility through modification with Ashes of War.
