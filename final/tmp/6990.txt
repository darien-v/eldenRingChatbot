\n\n Lesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit \n\nLocations\n Stormveil Castle Leyndell, Royal Capital Grand Cloister Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree Leyndell, Ashen Capital Leyndell, Capital Outskirts \n\n Drops \n 4,736 ~ 26,460  RunesGolden Seed \n\nLesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit is a Monster Enemy in Elden Ring, the non-boss enemy of the Ulcerated Tree Spirit. Located within many later game areas, the Lesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit is a towering, snake-like creature that heavily features a body composed of tree-like structures and is covered is sweltering red pustules.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit:\n\nStormveil Castle\n\nLocated at the crypt of the castle.\nNearest Site of Grace: Liftside Chamber\n\nCapital Outskirts\n\nLocated south of Outer Wall Phantom Tree Site of Grace.\nEmerges next to the Carriage.\n\nOne can be encountered in Leyndell, Royal Capital.\nA rot variant is encountered at the bottom of the waterfall in the Grand Cloister.\nAnother rot variant encountered in the scarlet rot lake in Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree.\nThree can be encountered in Leyndell, Ashen Capital, at the location of Erdtree's Favor +2. \n\nDrops ~15000 runes (NG), killing all 3 will not grant additional items.\nCan be found by backtracking from the Forbidden Lands Site of Grace\n\nLesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit Combat Information\n\nHealth:  \n\n4,492 HP (Stormveil Castle)\n8,817 HP (Capital Outskirts)\n10,046 HP (Leyndell, Royal Capital)\n18,144 HP (Leyndell, Ashen Capital)\n19,880 HP (Miquella's Haligtree)\n11,191 HP (Ainsel River)\n\nPoise: 120\n\nABSORPTIONS\n\nPhy (Standard): 0\nPhy (Slash): 0\nPhy (Strike): 0\nPhy (Pierce): 0\n\nMagic: 20\nFire: -20\nLightning: 20\nHoly: 40\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if an absorption is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% is absorbed. Bigger number = less damage. An absorption of 100 means no damage goes through, and a resistance of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nRESISTANCES\n\nPoison: 517 - 1,463\nScarlet Rot: 517 - 1,188 (Miquella's Haligtree: IMMUNE)\nHemorrhage: 517 - 680\nFrostbite: 229 - 302\nSleep: IMMUNE\n\nThe resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it. For example, if a resistance is 100 you must deal 100 points of whatever aux buildup to trigger it. Note that these go down over time, and increase each time the effect procs.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit Drops\n\nRunes\n\n4,736  (Stormveil Castle)\n8,820  (Capital Outskirts)\n9,828  (Leyndell, Royal Capital)\n18,749  (Leyndell, Ashen Capital)\n26,460  (Miquella's Haligtree)\n11,193  (Ainsel River)\n\nGolden Seed (Only some encounters)\n\nLesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit Notes & Tips\n\nVery easy enemy if using ranged attacks.\nLeyndell, Royal Capital Encounter: Force the boss to spawn by entering the graveyard, then immediately turn around and run back across the bridge. Take a right and enter the room with the assassin's throwing lightning pots and climb the ladder.  \n\nLeave out through the golden double gate doors and immediately roll to the right to dodge a knights arrow. Jump on the railing in front of you and down onto the clay roof tiled building in front of you. \nFrom there ranged attacks can be targeted on him \n\nHighly highly recommend lorettas greatbow spell
