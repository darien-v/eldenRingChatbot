QUESTION
How can I find and access the Mistwood Outskirts in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Head to Mistwood in the Eastern part of Limgrave in Elden Ring, and locate Mistwood Outskirts. Speak to Kenneth Haight and the Nomadic Merchant in the north-west and southern sections, respectively, for guidance.
