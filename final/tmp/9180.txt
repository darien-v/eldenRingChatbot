[Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Blade of Calling Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Blade of Gold\nThis weapon cannot be infused with an Ashes of War.\nThis weapon can't be enchanted with Magic and can't be boosted by Consumables.\nBlade of Calling can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones\nWielded by Melina when summoned for the Morgott, The Omen King fight.\nSell Value:   1000\nThe Weapon Art Blade of Gold has a strong knockback, and can knock most small to medium sized enemies back, allowing for breathing room.\nBoth this and the Black Knife have substantial hyper-armor once the player is in the air, this hyper-armor DOES NOT activate while still on the ground.\nStealth attacks with Blade of Gold do additional damage.\nThe Blade is very similar to the Black Knife and some in the community speculate that the Black Knife is the Blade of Calling imbued with the power of Destined Death.\nPollyanna, one of Millicent's sisters, also wields the Blade of Calling, Both when summoned to fight against  Commander O'Neil and at the end of Millicent's quest line. \nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, the speed and distance of some attacks has been increased and the recovery time has been reduced.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
