ANSWER
Leyndell Foot Soldier Set in Elden Ring can be found by looting Leyndell Foot Soldiers in Altus Plateau and Mt. Gelmir, with Foot Soldier Gauntlets and Greaves dropping from Godrick Foot Soldiers.
