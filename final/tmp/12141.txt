Wrath of Gold spell knocks back nearby enemies when cast. Can be charged to increase range. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSuperior Erdtree Worship incantation.Produces a golden shockwave that knocks back nearby foes.Charging increases the size of the shockwave.This incantation was discovered when the Elden Ring was shattered, and it was feared as a sign of the Erdtree's wrath.\n\nWhere to find Wrath of Gold\nWhere to find Wrath of Gold:\n\n Found in the southernmost section of Woodfolk Ruins. It's the part directly northeast of Altus Tunnel. Among the enemies, there is a staircase that goes down and leads to a chest that contains this Incantation.
