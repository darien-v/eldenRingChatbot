QUESTION
What makes the Enchanted Knight class unique in the Elden Ring Closed Network Test?

ANSWER
The Enchanted Knight class in the Elden Ring Closed Network Test is unique for its ability to use sorceries before finishing the demo, having high Strength and Intelligence for heavy weapons and armor, and starting with the best shield with 100% physical mitigation.
