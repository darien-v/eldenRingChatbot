\n\n Glintstone Cometshard \n\nSpell Type\nGlintstone Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 17        \nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Fires a magic comet with a trailing tail \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 36 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nGlintstone Cometshard is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Glintstone Cometshard spell fires a comet that moves forward while leaving a trail. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nOne of the glintstone sorceries of the Academy of Raya Lucaria.Fires a magical comet with a trailing tail.This sorcery can be cast repeatedly and while in motion. Charging enhances potency.A sorcery of the Karolos Conspectus, the most venerable of the academy.\n\nWhere to find Glintstone Cometshard\nWhere to find Glintstone Cometshard:\n\n The spell can be purchased for 12,000 Runes after you deliver the Conspectus Scroll to Miriel Pastor of Vows, Sorceress Sellen, or Preceptor Seluvis. \n\nElden Ring Glintstone Cometshard Guide\n\nGlintstone Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 28\nDeals magic damage\nCan be charged for up to approximately 20-25% increased damage.\nCan be chain casted.\nDamage can be boosted by 10% by wearing Azur's Glintstone Crown.  This comes at the cost of a 15% increase in ALL sorcery costs\nThe projectile of this sorcery includes a comet tail (the outer dust of the projectile), dealing damage itself and thus adding a little bit of AoE-damage to the spell. Fired in a group of enemies, it will often hurt also the nearest bystanders of the target.\nNote: In Patch 1.07, FP cost was reduced from 19 to 17 and damage was increased slightly, as well as stamina damage against guarding enemies. In addition, while not mentioned in the patch notes, the stamina cost was reduced from 30 to 28. \nCompared to Glintstone Pebble, Glintstone Cometshard costs about 135% more FP, travels 15-20% farther and deals about 70-80% more damage.\nCompared to Great Glintstone Shard, Glintstone Cometshard costs about 45% more FP, and deals 20-25% more damage.\n\nBest Glintstone Cometshard Buffs\nUse these Buffs to maximize the performance of Glintstone Cometshard.
