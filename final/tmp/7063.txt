\n\n High Page \n\nLocation\nRaya Lucaria AcademyLeyndell Royal Capital\n\nDrops\n 508 - 1232 RunesHigh Page SetRed Branch ShortbowPerfumer's Bolt\n\nHigh Page is an Enemy in Elden Ring. High Pages are a tougher variant of common Pages, servants of the Nobility commited to keep them out of harm's way. High Pages wield an Estoc Thrusting Sword and a Crossbow, which they use to shoot up to 3 Perfumer's Bolts in a quick burst.\n\nStrong and dexterous servants of the Nobility, commited to keep them out of harm's way. Specially compared with their common counterparts.\n\nElden Ring High Page Locations\nWhere to Find High Page:\n\nRaya Lucaria Academy: once you have progressed far enough in the Academy to kick down the ladder in the Church of the Cuckoo, simply climb up the ladder, kill the Sorcerers, and head down the hall. The High Page is at the end of that hall.\nLeyndell Royal Capital: Start at the West Capital Rampart site of grace. head toward the large Gargoyle and drop down the stairs to the left. The High Page will be in this room. \n\nNOTE: After defeating Maliketh, the Black Blade this location will no longer be available.\n\nElden Ring High Page Combat Information\n\nHealth:\n\n1,318 HP (Academy of Raya Lucaria)\n2,155 HP (Leyndell, Royal Capital)\n\nPoise: 65\n\nABSORPTIONS\n\nPhy (Standard): 0\nPhy (Slash): 0\nPhy (Strike):10\nPhy (Pierce): 0\n\nMagic: 0\nFire: 0\nLightning: 0\nHoly: 0\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. QUESTION
Why are the High Pages stronger variants of the Pages and what significance does their commitment to keeping the Nobility out of harm's way hold in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
High Pages are stronger variants of Pages due to their strong and dexterous nature and their commitment to protecting the Nobility emphasizes their loyalty and importance in Elden Ring.
