\n\n Gurranq's Beast Claw \n\nSpell Type\nBestial Incantation\n\nFP Cost 21\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Creates beast claws to rend surroundings with shockwaves \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 15 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nGurranq's Beast Claw is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Gurranq's Beast Claw spell creates vertical waves around the caster that move outwards dealing damage in their wake. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSuperior incantation taught by Gurranq, the Beast Clergyman.Creates beast claws that rend surroundings with shockwaves.Long ago, Gurranq was a beast of such terrifying ferocity that hisformer name meant "Death of the Demigods."\n\nWhere to find Gurranq's Beast Claw\nWhere to find Gurranq's Beast Claw:\n\n Bestial Sanctum: Reward from Gurranq, Beast Clergyman after giving him the 8th Deathroot.
