The Carian Regal Scepter scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity, and Intelligence. It is a good Weapon only for characters with the highest intelligence, commonly used by sorcerers or any other magic build.\n\nMagic scepter of Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon. The glintstone is known as a Carian Blue, enhancing full moon sorceries.\nOnly those of the highest intelligence may wield this, the finest of all glintstone staves.\n\nWhere to Find Carian Regal Scepter in Elden Ring\nThe Carian Regal Scepter weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nObtained when unlocking the Remembrance of The Full Moon Queen's power by handing it over to Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Carian Regal Scepter Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Spinning Weapon\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War, nor enchanted with Magic.\nCarian Regal Scepter can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones.\nBoosts Full Moon sorceries by 10% which includes Rennala's Full Moon and Ranni's Dark Moon.\nThe Spinning Weapon skill on the Carian Regal Scepter appears to differ from other applications of the standard Spinning Weapon Ash of War in that it is projected more forward with spiraling blue particles behind. Deals magic damage that scales based on INT or sorcery scaling unlike the ash of war you can infuse on other staves. Affected by Magic Scorpion Charm.\n+10 gives Sorcery Scaling of 330 at 70 INT and 373 at 80 INT.\nThe second-most powerful staff, and the most powerful non-fp-cost-increasing staff, for players with 69 intelligence or above. \n\nLusat's Glintstone Staff always outdamages it, but increases fp costs by 50%.\nAcademy Glintstone Staff out-damages it from 60-68 intelligence.\n\nSell Value:   100\nWith 60 INT it starts being better than the Meteorite Staff at +7.
