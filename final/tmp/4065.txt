Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nShard of the late Alexander, a shattered warrior jar.\nGreatly boosts the attack power of skills.\nScraps of stewed flesh cling to the shard, and tatters of ornaments can be seen mingled within the slime. Relics of a red-haired champion, it would seem.\n\nShard of Alexander Effect in Elden Ring\nShard of Alexander greatly boosts the attack power of Skills by 15% (remains 15% in PVP).\n\nWhere to find Shard of Alexander in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Shard of Alexander:\n\nDrops from Iron Fist Alexander after completing his questline.
