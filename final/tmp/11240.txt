\n\n Lord of Blood's Favor Before soaking \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Pure white oath-cloth given by Varré. \n\n Lord of Blood's Favor After soaking \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Bloodied white oath-cloth, dyed in a maiden's blood \n\nLord of Blood's Favor is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Lord of Blood's Favor is used to progress White Mask Varre's questline. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nBefore soaking:\n\nPure white oath-cloth given by Varré. The final trial to be anointed a Knight of Mohg, Lord of Blood. "Soak the cloth with a maiden's blood... The blood of anyone's maiden will do. You are maidenless, after all."\n\nAfter soaking:\n\nFully reddened oath-cloth, dyed in a maiden's blood.\nThe final trial is complete. Luminary Mohg is sure to welcome you into his service, as a knight who will lay the foundations for his dynasty.\n\nWhere to find Lord of Blood's Favor in Elden Ring\nThe Lord of Blood's Favor is found by progressing White Mask Varre questline past Limgrave, and talking to him at the Rose Church in Liurnia of the Lakes. [Map Link] Players must get a Festering Bloody Finger from Varre and invade other players 3 times (You can invade and return to your world immediately). Talk to Varre again and accept to be knighted, and you will obtain a pure white oath-cloth and be charged with soaking it in the blood of a maiden. This can be done by going to the Church of Inhibition [Map Link] or Chapel of Anticipation. [Map Link] There will be a deceased maiden where you can obtain the bloodied white oath-cloth. Video Location\n\nElden Ring Lord of Blood's Favor Use\nLord of Blood's Favor can be used to progress White Mask Varre's questline. Once obtained, players must soak it in the blood of a maiden: The closest will be a Maiden corpse in the Church of Inhibition, north east of Liurnia. Possible is also the corpse at Chapel of Anticipation if you've unlocked the second belfry at the The Four Belfries. \nYou can also soak the cloth by killing Hyetta or Irina.\nBring the cloth back to Varre to progress the questline.\n\nElden Ring Lord of Blood's Favor Notes & Tips\n\nGo to the Four Belfries west of the Academy, you can find a portal that leads you back to the Chapel of Anticipation (be sure to pick the right portal, its name should involve the word Anticipation.) There is a dead maiden in there that you can use for the quest.\n\nElden Ring Key Items\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell Bearing  ♦  Academy Glintstone Key  ♦  Academy Scroll  ♦  Alexander's QUESTION
Why is soaking the Lord of Blood's Favor in the blood of a maiden necessary to progress the White Mask Varre's questline in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Soaking the Lord of Blood's Favor in a maiden's blood is necessary as the final trial required to be anointed a Knight of Mohg and to complete Luminary Mohg's quest for his dynasty's foundation.
