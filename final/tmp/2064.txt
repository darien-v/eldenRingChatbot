\n\n Vitality \n\nType\nDefensive Stat\n\nEffect\nIncreases your resistance to instant death from Blight buildup\n\nVitality is a Defensive Stat in Elden Ring. Vitality shows your resistance to death. Stats determine the strengths and weaknesses of your character. Defensive Stats are determined by your Attributes and Equipment. \n\nVitality Elden Ring Guide\n\nYour resistance to Death Blight buildup.\nThe higher the number, the lower your susceptibility to instant death.\nGoverned by your attributes, as well as protective gear, and active passive effects.\n\nElden Ring How to increase character Vitality\n\nVitality can be increased by adding points to Arcane.\n\nRelated Equipment & Items in Elden Ring for Vitality\nYou can use the following Equipment and Items to increase your Vitality:\n\nPrince of Death's Pustule (+90)\nPrince of Death's Cyst (+140)\nPillory Shield (+50)\nGrave Scythe (+35)\nFia's Set pieces have the highest innate Vitality per armor piece. The Deathbed Dress has comparable Vitality to Fia's Robe.\nLusat's Set has the highest combined Vitality.\n\nElden Ring Vitality Notes & Tips\n\nImmunity Increase by Rune Level and Arcane Level\nThe soft caps for Rune Level are 71, 111, and 161\nThe soft caps for Arcane are 15, 40, and 60\nThe formulas for calculating Vitality on Rune Level are as follows: \n\nLevel 1 - 71 --> 75 + 30*((Lvl+79 - 1) / 149)\nLevel 72 - 111 --> 105 + 40*((Lvl+79 - 150) / 40)\nLevel 112 - 161 --> 145 + 15*((Lvl+79 - 190) / 50)\nLevel 162 - 713 --> 160 + 20*((Lvl+79 - 240) / 552)\n\nThe formulas for calculating Vitality on Arcane are as follows: \n\nLevel 1 - 15 --> Lvl\nLevel 16 - 40 --> 15 + 15*((Lvl - 15) / 25)\nLevel 41 - 60 --> 30 + 10*((Lvl - 40) / 20)\nLevel 61 - 99 --> 40 + 10*((Lvl - 60) / 39)\n\nAfter adding both numbers together, the resulting number is rounded down for the UI menu. QUESTION
Why should one focus on increasing Vitality in Elden Ring and what are the most effective ways to do so?

ANSWER
Increasing Vitality in Elden Ring is crucial for boosting resistance to instant death from Blight buildup, achieved by adding points to Arcane, equipping items like Prince of Death's Pustule or Pillory Shield, and reaching soft caps for Rune Level and Arcane, ultimately enhancing overall survival chances.
