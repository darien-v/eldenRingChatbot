This huge, draconic humanoid guards an ancient throne beneath the eternal city of Nokstella. This Elden Ring Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella Guide features boss locations, tips, strategies and videos on how to defeat Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella easily, as well as boss stats and lore for Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella. Bosses are unique and challenging Enemies that can be encountered all over the Lands Between. Great Enemy bosses can drop unique Spirit Ashes, Weapons, Spells, and other various Items. This is an optional boss, as players don't need to defeat it in order to advance in Elden Ring. However, doing so yields useful items, as well as Runes.\nSee also Dragonkin Soldier and Lesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit).\n\n The Dragonkin were born in the Eternal City, where they knew no true sky, nor true lightning. Instead, ice lightning was their weapon.\n\nElden Ring Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Ainsel River Downstream\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella Location\nFrom the Ainsel River Downstream Site of Grace, drop down off the ledge into the river and follow it downstream into the western cave. You'll enter a cave full of Giant Ants. Head north and you'll eventually reach the Dragonkin Soldier's boss door. After this point, all of its attacks do additional lightning damage and many inflict Frostbite as well\nRun away once it starts charging itself with lightning\n\nFrozen Lightning Spear\nSummons a large blue lightning spear in its hand before tossing it forwards into the ground, creating a trail of electricity that blasts Frostbite lightning upwards a moment later. Used when you're far away\nRun to either side to avoid standing on the charged ground lightning. Jump or roll as you approach the edge to be sure you clear it. Try to move towards the soldier to keep it from throwing lightning over and over again\n\nLightning Leap\nJumps into the air either towards or away from the player while summoning a blue lightning spear in its hand, then tosses the lightning spear at the ground creating a trail of electricity that blasts Frostbite lightning upwards a moment later\nRun to either side to avoid standing on the charged ground lightning. Jump or roll as you approach the edge to be sure you clear it\n\nLightning Javelin\nSummons a large blue lightning spear in its hand, holds it in the air a couple seconds, then slams it into the ground creating a large shockwave that inflicts Frostbite\nWhen you see it raise the lightning bolt, run away and come back after the explosion\n\nDive Bomb\nJumps into the air and hovers there for a second as it pulls back its left arm, then dives forward to the ground while swinging its arm forward\nDodge to the right just as its arm becomes level with its head\n\nDragonkin Soldier of Nokstella QUESTION
What are the combat strategies and tips for defeating the Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Run away from Lightning Explosion, dodge Frozen Lightning Spear by moving to the sides, avoid charged ground lightning with Lightning Leap, retreat when Dragonkin Soldier uses Lightning Javelin, dodge Dive Bomb to the right to defeat Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella in Elden Ring.
