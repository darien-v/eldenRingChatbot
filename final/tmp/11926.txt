CONTEXT
\n\n Errant Sorcerer Wilhelm \n\nLocation\nLeyndell, Royal Capital\n\nDrops\nNothing\n\nErrant Sorcerer Wilhelm is an Enemy in Elden Ring. He is one of the assassination targets assigned to you by the Volcano Manor. You can find the summon sign to invade his world in Leyndell, Royal Capital.  Other Invadable NPCs can be found here.\n\nA silent seeker of sorcery, who had gone so far as to make contact with the primeval current, Wilhelm was led by guidance into confrontation with his friend Vargram, and it is said that he was then taken prisoner in Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Errant Sorcerer Wilhelm Locations\nWhere to Find Errant Sorcerer Wilhelm:\n\nHis invasion sign can be found close to the Fortified Manor, First Floor Site of Grace. Map Link\nHe will be assisted by Vargram the Raging Wolf.\nYou will be assisted by Recusant Bernahl.\nMake sure to invade him before both completing Crumbling Farum Azula and eliminating Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy, else his sign will be inaccessible!\n\nErrant Sorcerer Wilhelm Notes & Tips\n\nWears the Errant Sorcerer Set and the Hierodas Glintstone Crown, located in the Hermit Village in Mt. Gelmir. \nUses the Staff of Loss as his main weapon of choice.\nKnown spells are Ambush Shard, Night Shard, Night Comet, Crystal Burst and Stars of Ruin. \n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor
