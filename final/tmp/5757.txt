CONTEXT
\n\n Waterfowl Dance \n\n        Perform a series of one-footed leaps in the manner of a waterfowl to unleash a swift yet graceful slashing combo. Repeated inputs allow for up to two follow-up attacks \n\nWaterfowl Dance is a Skill in Elden Ring. Waterfowl Dance is a Unique skill that is only available for the Hand of Malenia, and cannot be added to any other weapon by Ashes of War.\n\nHow to get Waterfowl Dance in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill for the Hand of Malenia.\n\nElden Ring Waterfowl Dance Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 12 ( - 9)\nDeals 16 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nAll 3 separate flurries can be Parried\nOn use, you hover in the air for a split second before dashing forward in a flurry. Repeat inputs of the weapon skill button extend the skill for up to three flurries total.\nThe skill can be extended while in midair, and its forward momentum for each flurry lets you cross gaps that you normally cannot jump over.\nThe wind-up animation for this skill can be interrupted.\nThe skill is parryable.\nThe repeated attacks from this skill do scale with Winged Sword Insignia, Rotten Winged Sword Insignia, and Millicent's Prosthesis, and trigger Godskin Swaddling Cloth.\nBUG: using this skill while buffed by the Commander's Standard Rallying Standard ability will cause Waterfowl Dance to deal greatly reduced damage (reports show as low as 1% normal damage).\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nWaterfowl Warrior Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning
