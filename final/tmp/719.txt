\n\n Hermit Village \n\nA dilapidated village southeast of Mt. Gelmir. Now home to Demi-Humans.\n\nHermit Village is a Location in Elden Ring. The Hermit Village is found in Mt. Gelmir, southeast of the volcano. Players can reach this location by first traveling around the volcano starting from the ravine west of Wyndham Ruins, following it all the way around to the Seethewater Terminus site of grace in the northwest. From here, head south past the lake of magma until you reach the Craftsman's Shack. The village is just up the hill east of the shack.\n\nHermit Village Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Hermit Village\n\nPrimeval Sorcerer Azur - Silent NPC found in the north of the village, by the cliffs.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Hermit Village\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nGolden Rune (1) (Demi-Human Queen Maggie drop)\nSacramental Bud\nString\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials at this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nComet Azur\nErrant Sorcerer Boots\nErrant Sorcerer Manchettes\nErrant Sorcerer Robe\nHierodas Glintstone Crown\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nMemory Stone (Demi-Human Queen Maggie drop)\nPrattling Pate "You're Beautiful"\n\nElden Ring Hermit Village Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nDemi-Human\nLesser Demi-Human Chief\nAbductor Virgin\nGlintstone Sorcerer\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nDemi-Human Queen Maggie\n\nHermit Village Walkthrough\nThe Hermit Village southeast of Mt. Gelmir is a medium-sized town that has fallen into ruin. A band of Demi-Humans now roam its dilapidated streets, along with their queen. There are a few items of note in this village.\nCheck behind the southernmost house for a corpse with the Hierodas Glintstone Crown. Check the side of the house next to it for the Prattling Pate "You're Beautiful" tool. In the house in the northeast corner is a corpse with the entire Errant Sorcerer Set. The Hierodas Glintstone Crown is also part of this set. Continue northeast to fight the Demi-Human Queen Maggie who drops a Memory Stone upon defeat.\nContinue heading north and you will find the mysterious Primeval Sorcerer Azur. This NPC does not speak but you can interact with him to receive the powerful Comet Azur spell.\n\nElden Ring Hermit Village Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring
