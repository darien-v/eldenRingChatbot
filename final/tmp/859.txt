\n\n Forge of the Giants \n\nThe ever-burning Forge of the Fire Giants, said to hold the power to burn the Erdtree itself.\n\nForge of the Giants  is a Location in Elden Ring. The Forge of the Giants  is found in Mountaintops of the Giants. Come from the south and pay attention to the higher ground areas, you will see a giant cauldron next to a mountain shaped like a ramp. Climb that mountain up and use the frozen chain as a bridge to approach the top of the cauldron. Once there, you'll have reached the Forge. Map Link\n\nDo not attempt to explore the center down below, as it will result in Instant Death. There's a site of grace that can be found rounding the left edge of the forge at the end of it right before you reach the crack.\n[!] Accessing Farum Azula can lock you out of Brother Corhyn / Goldmask quest if you aren't far enough.\n\nForge of the Giants Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs in Forge of the Giants\n\nMelina - Will speak a few lines of dialogue before initiating a cutscene, if the player rests at the Site of Grace and has NOT taken the Frenzied Flame. \n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Forge of the Giants\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nSmoldering Butterfly\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo upgrade materials in this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nNo Equipment and Magic here\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nRemembrance of the Fire Giant (boss drop)\n\nElden Ring Forge of the Giants Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no enemies besides the Boss\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nFire Giant\n\nForge of the Giants Walkthrough\nThere's nothing to loot or explore in the Forge of the Giants other than reach the Site of Grace at the end of the left edge. (Except for a single smoldering butterfly partway around the edge to the right.) Once you reached the edge, don't attempt to explore the center of the forge as it will result in instant death. The only relevant thing to do here, is to talk to Melina in that Site of Grace. She will ask you if you are ready to commit to a sin, in which if you accept, after a few cinematics you will be teleported to Crumbling Farum Azula. Before accepting, it is recommended that your character is around Lvl 100+ otherwise this area might be a bit heavy for you.If you've inherited the Frenzied Flame, then Melina won't show up at this stage, instead there will be an option to "Listen to the sounds of flame" at the site of grace. After which you'll be teleported to Crumbling Farum Azula all the same.\nBe sure to finish or skip the cutscene. Quitting during the cutscene will interrupt the telportation process and you might not be able to progress to NG+. If the cutscene is interrupted, attempt to rest at the Forge of the Giants site of grace again. How do you reach the Forge of the Giants in Mountaintops of the Giants in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
