It's technically a boss fight against a Spirit-Caller Snail as it summons the spirit duo. However, the godskins represent the real difficulty of the fight, as the snail doesn't put up much of a fight without them.\nThis is an optional boss, as players don't need to defeat it in order to advance in Elden Ring. However, doing so yields useful Items and Runes. The three bosses don't fight together and instead appear one after the other. Upon entering the boss room, the Godskin Apostle spawns in, followed by the Godskin Noble after it has been defeated, and finally the Spirit-Caller Snail after both are dead. (Apostle & Noble)\nJumps and spins back in the air, and falls swinging his sword down on you.\nRoll and dodge back.\n\nSlam (Apostle)\nLevitates holding his scythe in front of him, charges and suddenly slams the ground deal area damage.\nPredict and keep distance.\n\nStretch Spin (Apostle)\nStretches and deforms like a serpent and performs a quick circular attack around himself.\nRoll twice as the attacks has 2 circular swings.\n\nSpinning Scythe (Apostle)\nExtends his arm spinning his Scythe on his hand very quickly like a helicopter and walks towards you.\nWalk back.\n\nTornado Scythe (Apostle)\nSpins his Scythe over his head casting wind performing area damage around him.\nRoll back, twice if you need to.\n\nStretch Walk (Apostle)\nStretches perform an attack, spins a bit around in the air and makes a long step forward to perform a second circular attack.\nYou may have time to attack depending on your build when he starts stretching, but be ready to dodge back once, and be ready to dodge again after he makes the long step towards you.\n\nHandle Hit (Noble)\nLifts his rapier and hits you or the ground with the handle to knock you down twice.\nDodge and walk backwards, or use shield.\n\nThrust (Noble)\nPuts himself in fencing position and thrusts at you.\nStay at range, he doesn't position himself very well to do it, so may not even need to dodge it.\n\nSwing and Thrust (Noble)\nPerforms 2 attacks as a combo, swinging to the right defensively stepping backwards and then thrusting at you. \nBe ready for the second attack as the first one is more random and may not even be close to hit you. Dodge it or shield it.\n\nDouble Swing (Noble)\nSwings his sword while spinning it first to his right and then to his left.\nIf you are at distance you may roll to him on his second attack and take the chance to hit him.\n\nRoll Attack (Noble)\nStarts rolling around, attacking you by squashing enemies with his body.\nDon't attack, dodge to the direction of his legs. (He steers slowly in that direction)\n\nCharged Thrust (Noble)\nCharges his rapier putting his rapier back  and suddenly thrusts towards you dashing a long distance for a melee attack.\nDon't understimate the range of this attack, dodge or use shield.\n\nExpansive Belly Blow (Noble)\nMakes and short expansive blow around him with his body.\nDodge back before he performs this attack and not while.\n\nFire field (Noble)\nCasts a ring of fire around him. But he will leave it in a few seconds.\nStay out of the ring, use this opportunity to perform ranged attack.\n\nJump and Slam (Noble)\nJumps and charges in the air to perfom a big area attack by slaming it.\nGain most of the distance before he performs the attack and dodge back right before he slams the ground.\n\nAggressive Fencing (Noble)\nHe starts thrust his rapier and swinging it rapidly like 3 times, and then performs an uppercut swing.\nRoll back and walk backwards to stay safe, or roll around him to get to his back and attack him.\n\nGodskin Apostle and Godskin Noble Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nThe Godskin Apostles' theme is not in Latin. QUESTION
Why is defeating the Godskin Apostle and Godskin Noble an optional boss fight in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Defeating the Godskin Apostle and Godskin Noble is an optional boss fight in Elden Ring because players do not need to defeat them in order to advance in the game, but doing so yields useful items and runes.
