\n\n Smithing Stone [5] \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce armaments up to +15 \n\nSmithing Stone [5] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Smithing Stone [5] is one of the rare smithing stones used to further boost the stats and acquire an even higher level of an equipment. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nStone used to smith a variety of armamemnts. A rare specimen, it is tinged with gold.\nStrengthens armaments up to +15.\nMined near the foot of the Erdtree, these stones are said to have been used to bolster the strength of the Royal forces.\n\nSmithing Stone [5] Location in Elden Ring\nSee Smithing Stone [6] for upgrades past +15.Where to find Smithing Stone [5]:\n\nTo buy from Twin Maiden Husks in the round table, Offer the Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing (3) to the Twin Maiden Husk.
