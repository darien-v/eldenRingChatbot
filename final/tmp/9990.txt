QUESTION
Why is the Heavy Crossbow in Elden Ring a good weapon for those who prefer ranged combat?

ANSWER
The Heavy Crossbow in Elden Ring is a good weapon for ranged combat due to its high attack power, long-range capabilities with a range of 42, and its passive Guard ability.
