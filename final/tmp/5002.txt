\n\n Serpent Arrow \n\nAttack Power \n\nPhy 30\n\nMag 0 \n\nFire 0 \n\nLigt 0 \n\nHoly 0 \n\nCrit 100 \n\nPassive\n\n        Causes heavy poison buildup (78) \n\nArrow\nPierce\n\nSerpent Arrow is an Arrow in Elden Ring. Serpent Arrow is a type of arrow that causes heavy poison buildup. Ammunition can be used in ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows, so players can deal ranged damage to Enemies and Bosses.\n\nArrow carved to resemble a flying snake. Used in tandem with the Serpent Bow. \nLoyal minions of the Formless Serpents, their fangs are daubed with deadly poison.\n\nWhere to Find Elden Ring Serpent Arrow\nSerpent Arrow can be found at the following location:\n\nSold by the Dragonbarrow's Isolated Merchant for 120 runes each.\nDropped by Serpent Snails in Nokstella The Eternal City (5 per drop).\nDropped by Serpent Snails in Volcano Manor (5 per drop).\nDropped by Skeletal Snails in the Royal Capital Outskirts, near the Hermit's Shack (5 per drop).\nDropped by Skeletal Snails in the Mountaintops of the Giants near where the Tibia Mariner is fought.\n\nElden Ring Serpent Arrow Notes & Tips\n\nCauses heavy (high) poison buildup.\n\nNot to mistake with applying deadly poison to the enemy. \n\nThese arrows only have high innate poison buildup, contrary to what their description implies.\n\nDeals Ranged Damage. \n\nBest paired with Serpent Bow.\n\nPierces enemies' armor\nSell Value: 10.\n\nHow to Craft Elden Ring Serpent
