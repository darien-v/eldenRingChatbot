\n\n Kaiden Sellsword \n\nLocation\nLimgraveWeeping PeninsulaConsecrated Snowfield\n\nDrops\n\n 282 - 4399 Runes\nKaiden Set\nDismounter\nRowa Raisin\nSweet Raisin\nFrozen Raisin\n\nKaiden Sellswords are Humanoid Enemies in Elden Ring. Kaiden Sellswords are heavily armored mercenaries, often seen mounting horses and wielding a Curved Greatsword for dismounting other riders. These warriors-for-hire are distinctly tough for novice fighters, specially because of their dexterous use of Dismounters and their War Cry Skill.\nA Kaiden Sellsword can be summoned for aid as a spirit by using the Kaiden Sellsword Ashes.\n\n These hulking, fearless mercenaries, said to hail from the mountaintops of the wintry north, are adept at fighting on horseback. Kaiden is the name of their home settlement.\n\nKaiden Sellsword Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Kaiden Sellsword:\n\nMultiple Kaiden Sellswords can be found northeast of Agheel North Site of Grace, a little way past the sword monument.\nOne can be found sleeping cross-legged next to a campfire at the northern end of the beach where the Coastal Cave is located, in Limgrave.\nOne Kaiden Sellsword can be found guarding the tower over Stormgate. The tower can be accessed by going all around Stormgate to the left and coming back through the field.\nSeveral Kaiden Sellswords can be found escorting processions and Carriages in Limgrave and the Consecrated Snowfield, usually comprised of Wandering Nobles and Trolls.\nOne Kaiden Sellsword can also be found leaning against the Consecrated Snowfield frozen river bank, south-by-southwest of Ordina, on the southwest side of the frozen river, accompanied by three Wandering Nobles.\n\nKaiden Sellsword Drops in Elden Ring\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 282 - 4399\n\nKaiden Helm\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nKaiden Armor\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nKaiden Gauntlets\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nKaiden Trousers\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nDismounter\n4.00%\nN/A\n\nRowa Raisin\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nSweet Raisin\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nFrozen Raisin\n6.00%\nN/A\n\nElden Ring Kaiden Sellsword Notes & Tips\n\nThese enemies attack with large curved weapons with great reach. QUESTION
How can Kaiden Sellswords be strategically approached and defeated in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Approach Kaiden Sellswords by focusing on dodging their attacks and utilizing well-timed blocks, while being cautious of their mounted maneuvers and War Cry Skill.
