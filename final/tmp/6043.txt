\n\n Ash-of-War Scarab \n\nDmg Negation \n\nPhy -5.82 \n\nVS Strike -5.6 \n\nVS Slash -5.82 \n\nVS Pierce -5.82 \n\nMagic -4.94 \n\nFire -4.94 \n\nLight -4.94 \n\nHoly -5.05 \n\nResistance \n\nImmunity 42 \n\nRobustness 22 \n\nFocus 27 \n\nVitality 26 \n\nPoise 6 \n\nHelm\nWgt. 5.1\n\nAsh-of-War Scarab is a Helm in Elden Ring. Ash-of-War Scarab isn't part of any set. It's a grey scarab that is worn directly on the head. Ash-of-War Scarab protects the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nGray scarab worn directly on the head.\nThese scarabs roll clumps of Ashes of War during their labors.\nSlightly reduces the FP cost of skills, but increases damage taken.\n\nWhere to find Ash-of-War Scarab in Elden Ring\nThe Ash-of-War Scarab Helm can be found at Stormveil Castle: Behind the waygate on the Limgrave Tower Bridge that takes you to the Divine Tower of Limgrave on a broken section of the bridge. Do not take the waygate after the golems, and instead drop down behind it to access this item and a Soft Cotton.
