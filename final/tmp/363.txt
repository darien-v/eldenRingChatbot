\n\n Malefactor's Evergaol \n\nAn evergaol overlooking the vast lakes. A flame-wielding thief resides within.\n\nMalefactor's Evergaol is a Location in Elden Ring. The Malefactor's Evergaol is found in Liurnia of the Lakes, on the long stretch of land in the southern landmass. To get to this location, players will need to approach the southern landmass from the southwestern side of the marshes and then travel east.\n\nMalefactor's Evergaol Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Malefactor's Evergaol\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Malefactor's Evergaol\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nThere are no gatherable items in this location.\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no materials in this locations\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nFlame of the Fell God (Adan, Thief of Flame Drop)\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this locations\n\nElden Ring Malefactor's Evergaol Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no regular enemies at this location.\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nAdan, Thief of Fire\n\nMalefactor's Evergaol Walkthrough\nUnlike most evergaols, this one does not require a Stonesword Key to unlock. The transporter mechanism is active as soon as you reach the area. Enter the portal to challenge the boss imprisoned within: Adan, Thief of Fire.\nAdan is a very straightforward fight. Despite his stout stature, he is quite agile and capable of quick dodge rolls. His main form of attack is his Flame of the Fell God spell, where he summons a large orb of fire that explodes after a short delay, dealing damage in a large radius and setting the area ablaze. This attack is easy enough to avoid due to the clear telegraph and slow animation. He will also use a combo of multiple swings with his flail, ending in a spinning attack. This move has a long animation and can easily be exploited by ranged players.\nAdan drops the Flame of the Fell God incantation upon defeat, allowing you to summon the same orb of fire that he QUESTION
Why is the Malefactor's Evergaol located in Liurnia of the Lakes and not in any other region in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Malefactor's Evergaol is located in Liurnia of the Lakes because it is situated on a long stretch of land in the southern landmass, requiring players to approach the area from the southwestern side of the marshes and then travel east.
