CONTEXT
\n\n Thundercloud Form \n\n        Temporarily transform into a red thundercloud and fly through the air, then plunge down with a lightning-infused blade. \n       Hold to increase the reach of the thundercloud form. \n\nThundercloud Form is a Skill in Elden Ring. Thundercloud Form is a special skill that is only available for the Dragon King's Cragblade, and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Thundercloud Form in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill for the Dragon King's Cragblade.\n\nElden Ring Thundercloud Form Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 28\nDeals 6 stance damage per tick while in cloud form, and the final hit deals either 11 poise damage if uncharged or 33 if charged. Up to 69 stance damage if the entire charged-attack chain hits.\nThis Skill is Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nFor some reason, if the Mimic Tear uses this skill, it never uses the charged version.\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Directional control has been increased.\n\n Thundercloud Form PVP Tips and How to Dodge It\n\nYou can dodge the uncharged version by going backwards to avoid the cloud damage and jumping to avoid the plunge. You can then follow up with a jumping attack to punish. No rolling or sprinting is necessary. Why is Thundercloud Form only available for the Dragon King's Cragblade in Elden Ring and not other weapons?

ANSWER
