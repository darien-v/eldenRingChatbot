CONTEXT
\n\n Black Flame Tornado \n\n        Spin armament overhead and then plunge it into the ground to summon a raging vortex of black flames. Hold to create an initial flame tornado while spinning the armament. \n\nBlack Flame Tornado is a Skill in Elden Ring. Black Flame Tornado is a regular skill that can be used by equipping the Godskin Peeler twinblade weapon. It can also be applied to weapons using the Ash of War: Black Flame Tornado.\n\nHow to get Black Flame Tornado\n\nThe default skill on the Godskin Peeler twinblade.\nAsh of War: Crumbling Farum Azula: Dropped by the Godskin Duo Boss inside the Dragon Temple area. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Black Flame Tornado Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Black Flame Tornado.\nFP Cost: 30\nThis Skill is Chargeable, but does not get a damage increase by the Godfrey Icon, charging also does not increase FP cost.\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nThis skill deals multiple hits in a row but does not add stacks towards the effects of the Winged Sword Insignia, Rotten Winged Sword Insignia, Millicent's Prosthesis, or Thorny Cracked Tear.\nThis counts as a ranged skill which scales purely via weapon level and faith. It will not apply status build up or actually hit with the weapon. This skill deals purely fire damage.\nRemoves any applied weapon buffs upon use, even Black Flame Blade.\nThe black flame DOT is applied as a debuff that deals roughly 1.9% hp damage over 2 seconds, this debuff is re-applied each time you hit with the skill. The charged version lasts longer and will apply more ticks.\n\nBlack Flame Tornado Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric QUESTION
How can I effectively use the Black Flame Tornado skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Hold to create an initial flame tornado while spinning the armament, removes weapon buffs, applies fire debuff over time.
