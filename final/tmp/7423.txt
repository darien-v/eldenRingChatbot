\n\n Ash of War: Spinning Weapon \n\nAffinity\nMagic\n\nSkill\nSpinning Weapon\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Spinning Weapon is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Spinning Weapon provides Magic affinity and the Spinning Weapon Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Magic affinity and the following skill:\n"Spinning Weapon: Defensive skill employed by Carian princesses. Lifts armament into mid-air, then makes it spin violently. Those it touches will suffer successive attacks."\nUsable on small and medium swords, axes, and hammers, as well as polearms and staves (great spears excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Spinning Weapon Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Spinning Weapon:\n\nSold by Sorcerer Rogier at Stormveil Castle or the Roundtable Hold for 1,000 . Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Rogier's Bell Bearing\n\nAsh of War: Spinning Weapon Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Spinning Weapon.\nProvides the Magic affinity, which adds Magic damage and Intelligence scaling, reduces base damage and Strength / Dexterity scaling.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Magic, Cold\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Curved Swords, Katanas, Twinblades, Hammers, Axes, Flails, Spears, Halberds, Reapers\n\nSpinning Weapon Demonstration\n\nMeteor Mage Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Spinning Weapon Notes & ANSWER
