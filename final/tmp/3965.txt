The Sacred Relic Sword scales primarily with Strength, Faith, and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for its additional holy damage. \n\nSword wrought from the remains of a god who should have lived a life eternal.\nThoughts on what the weapon portends are many and varied. Some consider it the mark of a great sin, or a sign of great devastation. Some think of it as the end of an age, while others; the beginning.\n\nWhere to Find Sacred Relic Sword in Elden Ring\nThe Sacred Relic Sword weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nTrade Elden Remembrance with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Sacred Relic Sword Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Wave of Gold\n\n2-second wind-up, then fires a long-reaching and gradually widening arc of Holy damage that damages everything in its path.\nProjectile scales entirely off Faith.\nDoes not benefit off any bonuses that augment consecutive hits like the Winged Sword Talisman and similar buffs.\nDoes not cause skeletal enemies to die without regenerating.\n\nThis weapon can't be infused with Ashes of War\nCan't be enchanted with Magic nor boosted by Consumables\nSacred Relic Sword can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones\nUpon reaching Mohgwyn Palace, one can fast travel to the Palace Approach Ledge-Road site of grace and use the Wave of Gold weapon skill to farm Albinaurics. Effective yield of around 6,000,000 runes per hour with the Golden Scarab Talisman. Look at Wave of Gold page for damage increase/breakdown.\nCan be powerstanced with the Blasphemous Blade or Sword of Milos for rapid healing or quick recuperation of the skill cost respectively.\nSell Value:   1000\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
