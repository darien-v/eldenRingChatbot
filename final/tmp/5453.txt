CONTEXT
\nNew Game Plus, or New Game+ (NG+), in Elden Ring is an unlockable feature upon completion of the story which allows you to begin a new journey while maintaining Stats and select pieces of Equipment. This page covers how to enter New Game Plus, what changes it brings to gameplay, and the equipment which carries over. In addition, there are community recommendations about what you may want to accomplish before entering your next journey.\nElden Ring New Game+ Helpful Links\n\nCheck our Walkthrough page for a detailed guide on reaching the end of the game.\nYou may also visit the Game Progress Route page for a quick overview of how to progress through the game.\nCheck our Endings page for the different conclusions to the game.\n\nElden Ring New Game Plus\nAfter defeating the final boss and watching the credits, the game will prompt you to "Begin Journey 2. If you do not start over now, you can choose to begin Journey 2 later at the Table of Lost Grace in Roundtable Hold."\n\n  What carries over to New Game Plus?\nThis is a list of items and discoveries QUESTION
What changes with NG+?

ANSWER
Enemies and bosses receive increased health and deal more damage, scaling based on enemy, location, and level of NG+, with differences in rune acquisition rate and multiplayer boss scaling.
