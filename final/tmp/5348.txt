QUESTION
Why do players need to find Aeonian Butterflies in the swamp of Aeonia in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players need to find Aeonian Butterflies in the swamp of Aeonia in Elden Ring to use as a crafting material for various items and weapons in the game.
