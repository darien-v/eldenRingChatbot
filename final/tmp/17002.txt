\n\n Assassin's Approach \n\nSpell Type\nTwo Fingers Incantations\n\nFP Cost 15\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Silences footsteps, reduces fall damage / sound \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 10 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nAssassin's Approach is an Incantation in Elden Ring. The Assassin's Approach spell can be cast to temporarily silence the footsteps of the caster, increasing their stealth. It also negates Fall Damage for non-lethal falls. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nIncantation of the Two Fingers' servants, who once served as the assassins of the Roundtable Hold.\nCompletely silences the footsteps of the caster. Additionally, reduces fall damage and the sound produced by falling.This incantation can be cast while in motion or crouching.\nThe assassins were charged with eliminating Tarnished who had strayed from guidance.\n\nWhere to find Assassin's Approach\nWhere to find Assassin's Approach:\n\nStarting Incantation for the Confessor Class\nPurchase from  Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows after giving either the Assassin's Prayerbook.\nCost = 2000\n\nElden Ring Assassin's Approach Guide\n\nTwo Fingers Incantation\nDuration: 30 seconds\nStamina Cost: 13\nAssassin's Approach, the Longtail Cat Talisman, and Soft Cotton are three items that prevent Fall Damage from being taken. However they do not change the height at which falls become lethal, which remains fixed at 20 meters. Using one of these means that the fall damage that is normally incurred from 16 m to ~19.9 m isn't taken at all.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 Duration has been extended from 20 to 30 seconds.\n\nElden Ring Assassin's Approach Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
