QUESTION
How do you initiate a duel in Elden Ring's Leyndell Colosseum and Caelid Colosseum?

ANSWER
Touch Marika's effigy at the Roundtable Hold to initiate the search for PVP mode, duels can only be held in Leyndell and Caelid Colosseums.
