\n\n Concealing Veil \n\nWeight\n0.9\n\nEffect \n\nConceals wearer while crouching away from foes \n\nConcealing Veil is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Concealing Veil conceals wearer while crouching away from foes. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nTalisman put together from dark cloth with a lustrous sheen.\nCompletely conceals the wearer's presence while crouching at a distance from foes.\nPart of one of the concealing veils used by the assassins on the Night of Black Knives.\n\nConcealing Veil Effect in Elden Ring\nConcealing Veil hides the wearer while crouching away from foes.\n Some info about Concealing Veil:\n\nDistance to trigger invisibility: About 5 medium weight rolls.\nThings that do break invisibility: removing the talisman (others seem to be fine, may depend on talisman), standing up, dropping from a platform/falling (causes you to stand up), any offensive action (such as shooting an arrow, spell, or weapon art), consuming an item, warping using a Phantom Bloody Finger.\nThings that don't break invisibility: moving (while crouching), rolling, changing equipment (other than this talisman).\nRe-applying invisibility in PvE: Once spotted, you must crouch again to trigger it.\n\nWhere to find Concealing Veil in Elden Ring\n\nAltus Plateau. Dropped by the Black Knife Assassin in Sage's Cave.
