\n\n Bloody Finger Ravenmount Assassin \n\nLocation\nRaya Lucaria Academy\n\nDrops\nAsh of War: Raptor of the Mists\n\nBloody Finger Ravenmount Assassin is a hostile NPC in Elden Ring. He is one of the Bloody Fingers and is targeted by Yura in the Academy of Raya Lucaria.\n\n The assassins of Ravenmount are killers by trade. They assail their victims while dressed as birds of prey.\n\nElden Ring Bloody Finger Ravenmount Assassin Locations\nWhere to Find Bloody Finger Ravenmount Assassin:\n\nAssassin can be found only during 2nd part of Bloody Finger Hunter Yura questline. Invasion sign is located North-east of Main Academy Gate site of grace (Raya Lucaria Academy), on a broken bridge. [Map link]\n\nElden Ring Bloody Finger Ravenmount Assassin Drops\n\nAsh of War: Raptor of the Mists\nSpeak to Yura to receive Smithing Stone [5] and progress his questline.\n\nBloody Finger Ravenmount Assassin Notes & Tips\n\nWears Raptor's Set and uses Raptor Talons infused with Raptor of the Mists Ash of war.\nDamage: Slash, Pierce\nInflicts: Hemorrhage\nCan heal once.\nCan throw Fan Daggers. Interestingly, he uses the Kukri throwing animation, so they are much more telegraphed\nTarnished-type enemy, therefore Status Effects - Madness including - are useful.\nCan easily be stunlocked to
