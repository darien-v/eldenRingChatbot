CONTEXT
\n\n Sacred Phalanx \n\n        Release the power of a hidden incantation to erect a palisade of golden spears frontwards. \n\nSacred Phalanx is a Skill in Elden Ring. Sacred Phalanx is a unique skill found on the Cleanrot Spear. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Sacred Phalanx in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on the Cleanrot Spear.\nDrops from the spear wielding Cleanrot Knights just south of the Heart of Aeonia by Commander O'Neil (very low drop rate).\nIt can also drop from the ghostly Cleanrot Knights in the War-Dead Catacombs.\nDrops from the spear wielding Cleanrot Knights in Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree. Closest is directly outside the "Prayer Room" Site of Grace (very low drop rate).\n\nElden Ring Sacred Phalanx Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 12\nDeals 22 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable.\nThis skill is very familiar to the Ritual Spear Fragment from Dark Souls 3.\nThe attack consists of two parts; A (normally-scaling) Spear attack, followed by a (skill-scaling) Holy Damage eruption of Spears from the ground\nThe damage scaling for the Spear eruption at each weapon upgrade level is as follows: \n\n+0:  0.47 * Faith + 128\n+1:  0.675 * Faith + 147\n+2:  0.831 * Faith + 172\n+3:  1 * Faith + 198\n+4:  1.27 * Faith + 222\n+5: 1.53 * Faith + 248\n+6:  1.73 * Faith +278\n+7:  2 * Faith + 307\n+8:  2.28 * Faith + 338\n+9:  2.58 * Faith + 370\n+10:  2.89 * Faith + 404\n\nNOTE: This skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: The Attack Power was Increased.\n\nLightning Dragoon Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden
