\n\n Claw Flick \n\n        Cause the finger to swell, then flex to build up strength before giving enemies an almighty flick. \n\nClaw Flick is a Skill in Elden Ring. Claw Flick is a unique skill that can be used on the Ringed Finger. It allows the player to cause the weapon's finger to swell then flick enemies away. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Claw Flick in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on the Ringed Finger.\n\nElden Ring Claw Flick Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 14\nDeals 30 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nAttack power and poise damage of the finger expansion have been increased.\n\nElden Ring
