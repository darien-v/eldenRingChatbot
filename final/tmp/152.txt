\n\n Uhl Palace Ruins \n\nA crumbling palace ruins now infested with foes.\n\nUhl Palace Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Uhl Palace Ruins is found underground in Ainsel River. The ruins are separated into two parts and each are reached from different entrances of Ainsel River. You can reach the southern section by heading south-west from the Ainsel River Well Depths Site of Grace when first arriving at Ainsel River from the Ainsel River Well. The northern section of the Uhl Palace Ruins can be found in Ainsel River Main by heading south from the Ainsel River Main Site of Grace after entering from Renna's Rise.\n\nUhl Palace Ruins Map\nSouthern Uhl Palace Ruins [Elden
