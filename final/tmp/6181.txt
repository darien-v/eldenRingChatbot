\n\n Lightning Slash \n\n        Call down a bolt of lightning into armament, then swing it down to create an explosive shock. The armament retains the lightning enchantment for a while. \n\nLightning Slash is a Skill in Elden Ring. Lightning Slash is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible weapons. Using this skill grants Lightning damage to the weapon and allows it to do an explosive attack. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Lightning Slash\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Slash - Dropped by a Teardrop Beetle in the middle of an enclosed area just outside the Capital Outskirts. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Lightning Slash Guide\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Lightning Slash.\nFP Cost: 10\nDuration: 40 seconds\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nAdds 85 lightning damage to the right hand weapon. This is a flat bonus and does not scale with anything.\nThe damage consists of three parts : a short distance stomp, weapon swing and a lightning bolt that strikes the ground after the weapon hits the ground. In some cases, it is possible for enemies to dodge the lightning bolt even though they were hit by the swing.\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nDuration has been increased from 20 to 40 Seconds.\nAttack power that grants the weapon a lightning attribute have been increased.\nDelay between the end of various actions (like using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\nDelay between using the skill and being able to attack has been reduced.\n\nLightning Slash Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Lightning Slash Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
