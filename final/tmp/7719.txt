Dragonfire spell is used to conjure a dragon head that exhales offensive flames.\n\nOne of the incantations of Dragon Communion.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to spew flaming breath. Charging extends duration.\nIncantation of those who have hunted dragons and feasted upon their hearts. Theirs is a pure and overwhelming power.\n\nDragonfire Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Dragonfire:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Church of Dragon Communion in Limgrave or at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 1 Dragon Heart. 2 seconds.\nCovers wide area and can occasionally ricochet from walls and non-destructible objects.\nDuration can be extended to breathe again, and you can swivel your character while casting to spew in different directions.\nFull cast requires at least 52 FP but will use up to 96 FP with no additional benefit before any FP-conservating buffs.\nIt takes on average 5 seconds including initial wind-up to complete the full cast.\nUnlike the named Breath spells, cannot be cast while jumping. \nCan be used while on horseback.\n\nBuilds with Dragonfire \n\nTemplar Written Guide\n\nElden Ring QUESTION
Why would someone choose to use the Dragonfire spell in Elden Ring over other incantations available?

ANSWER
Dragonfire spell in Elden Ring is chosen over other incantations because it transforms the caster into a dragon to spew flaming breath, covering a wide area and offering an overwhelming display of pure power.
