Battlemage Hugues is a Haima Battlemage and is found in Sellia Evergaol, in central Caelid north from Sellia. This is an optional boss as players don't need to defeat it to advance in Elden Ring.\nBattlemage is the normal enemy form of this boss. Battlemage Hugues can be summoned as spirits for aid in battle through Battlemage Hugues Ashes.\n\nHugues came from Sellia to study the Haima Conspectus at the academy, where he became a battlemage.\nHaima scholars seek to quell conflict with cannon fire and the gavel, but Hugues developed a longing for it.\n\nElden Ring Battlemage Hugues Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Dragonbarrow West\nMultiplayer is not allowed for this boss\nYou cannot summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Battlemage Hugues Location\nFrom the Dragonbarrow West Site of Grace, follow the crevasse east and then south. At the end of the cliffside, you'll find Sellia Evergaol which holds Battlemage Hugues. You can easily circle around the mage and continue performing backstab. If you manage to screw the rotation it's fine, wait for the mage to perform the Gavel of Haima and dodge roll through the slam, you can quickly get behind him and do a backstab, or you can just get a few swings in if you prefer that.\nIf you're using a colossal or heavy weapon, you'll easily stagger him with just a few swings, breaking his string of attacks. Once he's on his knees, you can then perform a crit attack.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nIf you are a magic and ranged user, it's best to have a shield that can highly negate magic damage like the Carian Knight's Shield, he will use the Glintstone Shard spell if you try to create some distance, so you can either time your dodge correctly to avoid the attack or block it. You can try baiting him in using some melee attacks then dodge roll away, then cast your spells/incantations or fire away some arrows.\nAlternatively you can just use Night Maiden's Mist. Get the tree between you and the boss and shoot the mist over the tree. Hughes will automatically retaliate as long as he takes damage, effectively pining himself into the mist but you should be safe with the trunk acting as a shield. Reapply the mist before it ends to kill him effortlessly.\n\nBattlemage Hugues Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nGavel of Haima\nWhen his target is at close-ranged, Hugues summons
