QUESTION
How can one efficiently farm Old Fang in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Old Fang can be efficiently farmed by repeatedly defeating Lion Guardians for a 100.00% drop rate, or by targeting specific Misbegotten enemies in Castle Morne for a higher drop rate.
