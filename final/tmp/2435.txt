QUESTION
How can the Royal Knight Set in Elden Ring be optimized for maximum magic defense?

ANSWER
Alter the Royal Knight Armor to the Royal Knight Armor (Altered) to remove the cape. Royal Knight Set has one of the highest magic defense, behind Lusat's Set, Azur's Glintstone Set, and Preceptor's Set.
