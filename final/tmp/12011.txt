\n\n Chief Guardian Arghanthy \n\nLocation\nGuardian's Garrison, in Mountaintops of the Giants\n\nDrops\n5,040  RunesOne-Eyed Shield\n\nChief Guardian Arghanthy is a Hostile Humanoid in Elden Ring, wearing the Ruler's Set and using fire magic to overwhelm those intruding within her area in theMountaintops of the Giants.\n\nElden Ring Chief Guardian Arghanthy Locations\nWhere to Find Chief Guardian Arghanthy:\n\nIn the highest section of Guardians' Garrison, in Mountaintops of the Giants. For example, if an absorption is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% is absorbed. Bigger number = less damage. An absorption of 100 means no damage goes through, and a resistance of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nRESISTANCES\n\nPoison: 112\nScarlet Rot: 112\nHemorrhage: 112\nFrostbite: 112\nSleep: IMMUNE\n\nThe resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it. Note that these go down over time, and increase each time the effect procs.\n\nElden Ring Chief Guardian Arghanthy Drops\n\n5,040  Runes\nOne-Eyed Shield\n\nChief Guardian Arghanthy Notes & Tips\n\nShe is a particularly strong flame caster. Using Fire Resistance equipment and consumables can help. She tends to either rush the player or defend with her shield. Her shield's skill can easily one-shot unaware players even with high Fire Damage Negation.\nUses the incantations Catch Flame, Burn, O Flame, and Surge, O Flame. She will also use the Greatshield’s skill to fire a powerful exploding fireball. 1. Catch Flame is a quick burst of flame, used when rushing the player. She will usually use this when within melee range.2. Burn O Flame is a strong move that releases several pillars of flame around her, but leaves them stationary while casting it. 3. Surge O Flame is a flaming stream that she uses to keep the player at a distance. It is her weakest spell, and can easily by tanked through.\nCan be made to drop down, making her easily killed with range from above.\n\nChief Guardian Arghanthy Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures
