The Nomadic Merchant's Set is a light armor set worn by the Nomadic Merchants found across the Lands Between. However, the chest colorization is different, with the colors being a faded pink/purple. The cape on the chest piece almost has a rainbow-like colorization, different from the basic grey and white colors the regular merchants around the world have. \n\nWhere to find Nomadic Merchant's Set in Elden Ring\n\nIn the hidden tunnels behind the altar in Mohg, the Omen's boss room at the Cathedral of the Forsaken Site of Grace in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds below Leyndell, Royal Capital. \n\nVideo Location\n\nAfter opening the secret passage behind the altar and taking the stairs down, after crossing the narrow fallen rafter, directly to the left is a tent and in the far corner you can find the full Nomadic Merchant's set.\nTo get access to the set, you need to destroy tent by either aggroing the Nomad close by, leading it next to the tent and baiting its frenzy attack onto it or quit-outing while standing very close to tent itself. \nSee the Location in the Elden Ring Map\n\nNomadic Merchant's Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nNomadic Merchant's Chapeau\nNomadic Merchant's Finery\nNomadic Merchant's Trousers\n\nNomadic Merchant's Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Nomadic Merchant's Finery can be altered to the Nomadic Merchant's Finery (Altered) to remove the cape.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
