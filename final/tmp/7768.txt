\n\n Ash of War: Barbaric Roar \n\nAffinity\nHeavy\n\nSkill\nBarbaric Roar\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Barbaric Roar is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Barbaric Roar provides Heavy affinity and the Barbaric Roar Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Heavy affinity and the following skill:\n"Barbaric Roar: Let loose a bestial roar to rally the spirit and increase attack power. While active, strong attacks change to savage combo attacks."\nUsable on melee armaments (daggers, thrusting swords, and whips excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Barbaric Roar Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Barbaric Roar:\n\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab in Ravine-Veiled Village, found by going as far northeast as possible in Lake of Liurnia (near but not accessible from Grand Lift of Dectus). Map LinkFrom the Ravine-Veiled Village Site of Grace, go down the stairs and follow the ravine northward. The scarab will be at the end, guarded by 3 Giant Bats.\n\nAsh of War: Barbaric Roar Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Barbaric Roar.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the
