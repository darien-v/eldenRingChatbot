QUESTION
Why is Varré's Bouquet considered a good weapon for blood loss buildup in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Varré's Bouquet is considered a good weapon for blood loss buildup in Elden Ring because it causes blood loss buildup (65) and is a hammer that scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity, and Arcane.
