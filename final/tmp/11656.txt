\n\n Lesser Sanguine Noble \n\nLocation\nRose ChurchSecond Church of MarikaMohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum\n\nDrops\n 470 - 6062 RunesBloodrose\n\nLesser Sanguine Noble is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Lesser Sanguine Nobles are an elite order of powerful blood mage assassins serving under Mohg, Lord of Blood. They are prominently found in the Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum. These are lesser versions of the Boss, Sanguine Noble.\nNot to be confused with the Sanguine Noble NPC that acts as an invader near the sending gate that is North of the Yelough Anix Ruins.\n\nElite assassins dressed in ornate garbs and wielding blood-caked rapiers. These nobles revel in blood sorcery, exhibiting a high level of proficiency in the dark arts as well as mastery of assassination techniques and swordplay.\n\nLesser Sanguine Noble Locations in Elden Ring\nLesser Sanguine Nobles can be found in the following locations:\n\nRose Church\nSecond Church of Marika, prior to invading Eleonora, Violet Bloody Finger\nMohgwyn Palace\nMohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum\n\nElden Ring Lesser Sanguine Noble Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 470 - 6062\n\nBloodrose\n15.00%\nN/A\n\nLesser Sanguine Noble Notes & Tips\n\nDeals Slash and Pierce Damage.
