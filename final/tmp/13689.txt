QUESTION
Why are the Astrologer Gloves considered to be lightweight gauntlets, and what specific defensive properties do they provide to the player when equipped?

ANSWER
The Astrologer Gloves are considered lightweight gauntlets, providing Dmg Negation, VS Strike 1, VS Slash 1, VS Pierce 1, Magic 3.2, Fire 3.1, Ligt 3.2, Holy 3.1 resistance, with immunity 14, robustness 7, focus 24, vitality 21, and poise 2.
