The Raging Wolf Set is a Medium Weight armor. Armor provides our character with protection and different resistance against all types of damage and negative status effects. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nArmor worn by Vargram the Bloody Wolf, one of the first Tarnished to visit the Roundtable Hold.\n\nWhere to find Raging Wolf Set in Elden Ring\nYou can find the Raging Wolf Set in:\n\nLeyndell, Royal Capital. After obtaining the second to last letter from Volcano Manor, you will be sent to assist Knight Bernahl with assassinating Vargram the Raging Wolf and Errant Sorcerer Wilhelm. On successful invasion, you will receive the full Raging Wolf Set. See it on the Elden Ring Map here. \n\nIt can no longer be acquired after defeating Maliketh (the boss of Crumbling Farum Azula) or Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy.\n\nVideo Location\n\nRaging Wolf Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\nIn Elden Ring, the Bloody Wolf Set is composed of 4 different armor pieces.
