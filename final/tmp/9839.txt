CONTEXT
\nFull Moon Sorceries are a group of Sorceries in Elden Ring that share a specific type of buff criteria. Full Moon Sorceries revolve around the ability to summon a full moon and hurl it towards foes. QUESTION
Why is it necessary to have a Glintstone Staff equipped in order to cast Full Moon Sorceries in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To cast Full Moon Sorceries in Elden Ring, a Glintstone Staff must be equipped because all Sorceries, including Full Moon Sorceries, require it for casting and they also require Intelligence to use.
