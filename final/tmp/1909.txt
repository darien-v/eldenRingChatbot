QUESTION
Why should players consider stacking the Spiked Cracked Tear with other buffs like the Roar Medallion for enhanced charged attacks in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Stacking the Spiked Cracked Tear with other buffs like the Roar Medallion enhances charged attacks by approximately 26%, providing a significant boost to combat effectiveness.
