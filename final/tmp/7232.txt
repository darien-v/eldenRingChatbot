\n\n Blackflame Monk \n\nLocation\nDivine Tower of CaelidVolcano Manor\n\nDrops\n 830 - 1764 RunesBlackflame Monk SetSmoldering ButterflyFire Blossom\n\nBlackflame Monk is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Blackflame Monks are hooded figures that wield swords resembling a roaring fire. Due to their betrayal, they use god-slaying Black Flame spells instead of the usual fire spells.\nThe spirit of a particular Blackflame Monk, Amon, can be summoned for aid in battle by using Blackflame Monk Amon Ashes.\n\n The Blackflame Monks, enthralled by the god-slaying black flame, became traitors, abandoning their posts as guardians.\nThe seduction of a taboo is never easily spurned.\n\nElden Ring Blackflame Monk Locations\nWhere to Find Blackflame Monk:\n\nThe Divine Tower of Caelid in Dragonbarrow. A monk can be found patrolling a corridor in the lower levels of the tower and one more can be found guarding the door to the Godskin Apostle boss' chamber. The latter one is an easy camp. From the Divine Tower of Caelid: Basement grace, run the short distance down the stairway and the monk will be right there.\nThe Volcano Manor, on the stairway outside the Godskin Noble boss chamber.
