QUESTION
How can players identify and utilize special weaknesses in Elden Ring to maximize damage against certain enemies?

ANSWER
Players can identify and utilize special weaknesses in Elden Ring by using weapons, skills, and spells that deal extra damage to specific enemy types listed in the game's mechanics.
