CONTEXT
\nIsolated Merchants in Elden Ring refer to several NPC Merchants that can be found in multiple locations. Isolated Merchants sell various items and equipment including Consumables, Materials, Weapons and Armor as well as notes containing hints for the player. Each Isolated Merchant has a different stock and they are usually found in or around an Isolated Merchant's Shack, complete with their own Sites of Grace. QUESTION
What is the purpose of the Isolated Merchants in Elden Ring and how do they benefit players in the game?

ANSWER
The purpose of Isolated Merchants in Elden Ring is to sell various items and equipment, and they benefit players by providing a convenient location to restock, dropping Bell Bearings when killed for Twin Maiden Husks to sell the same items.
