\n\n Godrick's Great Rune \n\nType\nGreat Rune\n\nEffect \n\n        Raises all attributes by +5 \n\nGodrick's Great Rune is a Great Rune in Elden Ring. Godrick's Great Rune Raises all attributes. Great Runes in Elden Ring are special items dropped by Demigod Bosses that can be equipped to acquire special passive bonuses.\n\nA Great Rune of the shardbearer Godrick.Its blessing raises all attributes\nThis Great Rune is known as the anchor ring, found in the center of the Elden Ring.\nThe first demigods were The Elden Lord Godfrey and his offspring, the golden lineage.\n\nWhere to Find Godrick's Great Rune\n\nDropped as a reward for defeating Godrick the Grafted located at Stormveil Castle. [Map Link]\nVideo Location\nVideo Location\n\nGodrick's Great Rune Use\nIf you want to use Godrick's Great Rune, you will need  travel to the Limgrave Tower Bridge site at Stormveil Castle. The path ahead is guarded by reanimated Collosal Statues, you can choose to fight them or just make a run for it until you reach the end where you'll find a waygate. There are some Warhawk enemies at the end as well as a flock of non-hostile Eagles. Keep going straight until you reach the large door of the tower, head inside, and step on the mechanism to activate the lift.\n\nAt the top, is another site of grace The Divine Tower of Limgrave. Continue upward until you reach the top where you'll find a glowing sigil at the center between a set of large fingers. Approach and interact with it to restore the power of the great rune. To activate and use Godrick's Great Rune you must activate the Great Rune at the Roundtable, then use a Rune Arc after completing the task above.
