\n\n Frenzied Flame Proscription \n\nHalf-buried catacombs, deep in the earth below Leyndell. The audience-chamber of the Three Fingers.\n\nFrenzied Flame Proscription is a Location in Elden Ring. The area is found beneath Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, underneath the Cathedral of the Forsaken Site of Grace.\n\nFrenzied Flame Proscription Map\n\n Reached upon defeating Mohg, the Omen, at Subterranean Shunning-Grounds. Elden Ring Interactive Map Link. The area is sealed until Morgott the Omen King has been defeated.\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Frenzied Flame Proscription\n\nHyetta\nThree Fingers\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Frenzied Flame Proscription\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nx1 Rune Arc (in chest behind illusory wall)\nx1 Golden Rune (11)\nYellow Ember\nx5 Eye of Yelough\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location.\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nFrenzied Flame Seal\nNomadic Merchant's Set\nFingerprint Stone Shield\nInescapable Frenzy\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere no key items in this location\n\nElden Ring Frenzied Flame Proscription Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nNomad\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses in this location\n\nFrenzied Flame Proscription Walkthrough\nBehind the altar at the Cathedral of the Forsaken is a hidden path, which reveals a labyrinth full of frenzied corpses and musically-inclined enemies. Three beams cross a pit with treacherous tombstone platforms sticking out from the walls. The Nomadic Merchant's Set, Eye of Yelough and Note: Miquella's Needle are inside three tents. They can only be reached by getting one of the frenzied Nomads to destroy them, or by standing right next to them, then quitting and reloading the game.\nThe alcove at one end of the middle beam has a Golden Rune (11), and the alcove at the end of the lowest beam has the Frenzied's Cookbook (2).\nThere is also a corpse with the Inescapable Frenzy incantation, and a treasure chest at the lowest floor before the bottom with a Fingerprint Stone Shield at the end of a hallway. Once you have collected everything, jump to the bottom of the pit; the ground will collapse beneath you, leaving you next to the Frenzied Flame Proscription Site of Grace.\nHyetta is also here, if you've progressed her questline to its conclusion. If you seek an audience with the Three Fingers, unequip all your armor and proceed to the locked door. This action will lock you into the Lord of Chaos ending unless remedied using Miquella's Needle. You can then return to Hyetta who will speak of the objectives of the Three Fingers. Exhaust her dialogue, reload the area and collect the Frenzied Flame Seal where she once stood.\nOn the opposite wall from the Site of Grace, in the middle arch, is an illusory wall that will lead you to a chest containing a Rune Arc. Behind this chest is another illusory wall that leads to the Root-Facing Cliffs Site of Grace in Deeproot Depths.\nElden Ring Frenzied Flame Proscription Gallery and Notes\nThis is one of the few locations with no enemies or bosses.
