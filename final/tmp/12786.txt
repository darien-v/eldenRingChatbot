\n\n Boltdrake Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.6\n\nEffect \n\n Reduces  \n       Lightning Damage taken by 13/17/20% \n\nBoltdrake Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Boltdrake Talisman reduces Lightning Damage taken by 13/17/20%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nTalisman depicting a yellow ancient dragon.Boosts lightning damage negation.\nThe ancient dragons, who ruled before the prehistoric era of the Erdtree, would protect their lord as a wall of living rock.And so it is that the shape of the dragon has become symbolic of all manner of protections.\n\nBoltdrake Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nBoltdrake Talisman reduces Lightning Damage taken by 13/17/20%. (Effect reduced to 4/5/6% in PvP.)\n\nWhere to find Boltdrake Talisman in Elden Ring\n\nLocated at Stormveil Castle. [Map link]\nWhen you reach the main castle grounds where you fight the Lion Guardian, head inside the room with the armored statues, and through the arch doorway, to reach the Limgrave Tower Bridge Site of Grace. Specifically, you'll find the on a body that you can loot, just at the top of the first set of stairs, resting against a pillar on the left side.\n\nBoltdrake Talisman +1: This more-powerful variant of the Boltdrake Talisman can be found in a storage shed inside the Old Altus Tunnel dungeon. [Map link]\nBoltdrake Talisman +2: Found on a corpse hanging off an opening in a tall tower south of the Dragon Temple Rooftop Site of Grace. Reaching this area is a bit complex: [Map link] \n\nYou will first need to head southeast to the large plaza guarded by a stationary Farum Azula Dragon, which is constantly raining red lightning all around the area.\nFrom here, look northeast, then drop down a series of cliffs to the east to reach the opening at the bottom of a large building.\nFollow the linear path here to a balcony with a ladder leading up to a terrace. Mind the Banished Knights patrolling the area.\nDirectly in front of the ladder is a lift building; to your left is a chest with the Dragon Towershield. Take the lift up to a series of slopes leading to a hallway underneath the same bridge you crossed to get to the southeast plaza.\nThe passage ahead has a Crucible Knight fighting a couple of Beastmen. Fight your way through the hallway (or just run past the enemies) to reach a ladder at the end.\nClimb up the ladder to reach an open window where the corpse holding the talisman can be found.\n\nBoltdrake Talisman +2 Video Location, Crumbling Farum Azula\n\nElden Ring Boltdrake Talisman Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  100\n+1 Variant Sell Value:  500\n+2 Variant Sell Value:
