CONTEXT
\nMaps for Elden Ring contains information that shows a layout for each location of the game that can help players through navigating their way around a specific area. These maps are generated by the community or created by Fextralife, and may not be reproduced without permission.\n\nPlease see our Interactive Map for a searchable version with locations, equipment, bosses, items and more.\nPlease see our Game Progress Route guide for a customized map that includes area progression and level recommendations.\n\nMap Fragments & Markers\nAt first the map is not complete, but you can extend it by acquiring Map Fragments. Map fragments are found next to a stele (large stone slabs). Steles are marked on the unexplored map, and they are generally easy to reach, not very far from the main roads. You can add up to 100 personal markers on your map.\n\nBeacons & Orientation\nOrientation is key when the game world is so open and vast, and Elden Rings offers Beacons to solve the mechanic of having to look at the map repeatedly. Using the Map, player will be able to place Beacons anywhere on the map, then the Beacons will appear on the world and they can be seen form very far without having to check on the map.\n\nElden Ring Why are Map Fragments located next to steles and how do they help players extend their maps in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
