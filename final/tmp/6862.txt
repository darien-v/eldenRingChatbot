\n\n Somber Smithing Stone [6] \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce special armaments to +6 \n\nSomber Smithing Stone [6] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Somber Smithing Stone [6] allows the user to further increase the stats and acquire a high level for special equipment. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nLarge gold smithing stone drained of color.\nStrengthens special armaments to +6.\nSpecial armaments with unique characteristics cannot be strengthened with colored smithing stones. QUESTION
How can one acquire the Somber Smithing Stone [6] in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Can be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold, dropped by the spear-wielding Lesser Crucible Knight in Siofra Aqueduct, found in Mt. Gelmir south of the First Mt. Gelmir Campsite, dropped by Magnus the Beast Claw in Altus Plateau, found on a corpse in southern Uhl Palace Ruins, dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in Nokstella, Eternal City, found in Lake of Rot, Forsaken Depths, inside Sellia Crystal Tunnel, Volcano Manor, Old Altus Tunnel, Deeproot Depths near second Site of Grace, in the Ants Nest in Deeproot Depths, in Leyndell, Royal Capital, and behind a shack in Altus Tunnel.
