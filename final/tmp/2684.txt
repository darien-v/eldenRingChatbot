The Highwayman Set is the armor worn by the Highwaymen found in Murkwater Cave, the first place Patches can be found. They have three pieces of Highwayman gear but are still wearing Foot Soldier Greaves from their former lives as soldiers. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Highwayman Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Highwayman Hood, Highwayman Cloth Armor, and Highwayman Gauntlets are dropped by the Highwaymen found in Murkwater Cave. \n\nIf you trigger the bell traps in the first room after the Murkwater Cave grace, multiple Highwaymen will come your way, allowing for a faster farm.\n\nThe Foot Soldier Greaves are dropped only by Godrick Foot Soldiers found across Limgrave.\n\nHighwayman Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nHighwayman Hood\nHighwayman Cloth Armor\nHighwayman Gauntlets\nFoot Soldier Greaves\n\nHighwayman Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
