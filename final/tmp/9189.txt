CONTEXT
\nLight Bows are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Light Bows are ranged weapons that can be used to inflict damage on hostile characters from afar. Similar to other ranged weapons, they grant no guard protection. However, Light Bows are unique in their ability to quickly notch and fire an arrow immediately after rolling, sprinting, or landing from a jump, allowing a more aggressive and mobile playstyle. QUESTION
How can Light Bows in Elden Ring be used to enable a more aggressive and mobile playstyle compared to other ranged weapons?

ANSWER
Light Bows in Elden Ring can quickly notch and fire an arrow immediately after rolling, sprinting, or landing from a jump, allowing for a more aggressive and mobile playstyle compared to other ranged weapons.
