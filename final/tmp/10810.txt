\n\n Loretta's Mastery \n\nSpell Type\nLoretta's Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 39\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Fires four great arrow from a magic greatbow at once \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 46 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nLoretta's Mastery is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Loretta's Mastery spell, unlike the Loretta's Greatbow that shoots one arrow from a magic greatbow, this Sorcery fires four great arrows at once. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSorcery used by Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree. Creates a magic greatbow and fires four great arrows simultaneously. Charging enhances potency. Hold to keep the great arrows nocked. Developed by Loretta after her long, bloody journey to seek out a place where the Albinaurics could live in peace. \n\nWhere to find Loretta's Mastery\nWhere to find Loretta's Mastery:\n\nDropped by Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree upon defeat. [Map Link] Video Location\n\nElden Ring Loretta's Mastery Guide\n\nLoretta's Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 33\nDeals magic damage\nWhen shooting at locked-on enemies or body parts close to the ground, the lower arrow generated has a tendency to hit the ground, reducing the potential damage output. It is recommended to either aim at a body part located higher up, or shoot from a higher elevation such as on horseback.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 43 to 39.\n\nElden Ring Loretta's Mastery Notes & Tips\n\nThis spell cannot be boosted by Arrow's Sting Talisman\nWhen fully charged, this spell is affected by Godfrey Icon.\nThe spell does no bonus damage from charging unless unless fully charged.\nIf fully charged, it does roughly 22% more damage.\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
