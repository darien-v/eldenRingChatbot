QUESTION
How can players interact with the Nomadic Merchant Castle Morne Rampart in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can buy various items and equipment from the Nomadic Merchant Castle Morne Rampart, including Consumables, Materials, Weapons, Armor, and notes; they can also attack and defeat the merchant, resulting in dropping Nomadic Merchant's Bell Bearing [4].
