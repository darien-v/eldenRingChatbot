CONTEXT
\n\n Earthshaker \n\n        Thrust armament into the ground, then gather strength to unleash an earth-shaking shockwave. Follow up with a strong attack to swing the armament in a sweeping strike. \n\nEarthshaker is a Skill in Elden Ring. Earthshaker is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, it can be used on Greataxes, Warhammers, and Colossal Weapons.\n\nHow to get Earthshaker in Elden Ring\n\nEarthshaker can be obtained killing an invisible scarab on the road after Grand Lift of Dectus (found near a statue at the first intersection, you can hear it rolling and see a little cloud of dust moving).\n\nElden Ring Earthshaker Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Earthshaker.\nFP Cost: 10 on use, 5 on follow up.\nDeals 36 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nUpon activation, the user holds their armament (weapon) with both hands above their head pointing to the ground, before thrusting it directly into the ground. Then they plunge the armament, creating a medium-sized shockwave around them that staggers enemies. \n\nAfter the shockwave, the user can input a Strong Attack to make a follow up attack that sweeps over a wide arc, which can be aimed in any direction.\nAll three instances (thrust, shockwave, and follow up) consume stamina separately.\nThe thrust and follow up attack deal the same damage type as the weapon while the shockwave deals standard damage.\n\nThe ground thrust has a hitbox and deals the same damage as a one handed light attack. \n\nThe shockwave deals approximately the same damage as a two handed light attack. \n\nSometimes it seems to deal about 30% more damage for unknown reasons.\n\nThe follow up attack deals approximately 10% more damage compared to a two handed light attack, and causes knockdown on some enemies.\nAll of these attacks provide above average poise/hyperarmor.\n\nFollow up attack can be done slightly later than the recovery animation suggests.\n\nThis can prove useful as a feint, or to delay the follow up attack when the enemy is in range.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nDoes not produce a shockwave.\nLoses aditional poise.\nDeals less damage (50% damage penalty).\nConsumes more stamina (50% more).\n\nEarthshaker Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric
