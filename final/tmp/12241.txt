This incantation can be cast while jumping.\nThese are the dying wails of the Dragonlord who once dwelled eternal beyond time.\n\nWhere to find Placidusax's Ruin\nWhere to find Placidusax's Ruin:\n\nTrade Remembrance of the Dragonlord with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Placidusax's Ruin Guide\n\nNote: This spell does not count as a Dragon Communion Incantation. Special buffs from Dragon Communion Seal will have no effect on this incantation.\nStamina Cost: 75\nRequires three slots to be equipped.\nThe initial explosion deals Lightning Damage, and the beam deals Fire Damage.\nIt takes on average 8 seconds to complete the cast.\nDeals total 70 stance damage\nCan be cast while jumping, and your character hovers slightly while using the spell.\nDespite the appearance, this incantation has 360⁰ control and can be aimed by using the directional movement during the cast. The entire beam can hit the same area using this technique.\nUnlike other Breath spells, cannot be used while on horseback, regardless of which hand the Sacred Seal is equipped in. \nThe initial cast comes out quickly and deals lots of damage, ideal for catching enemies close to the caster. Against enemies at a distance it will leave the caster wide open to retaliation.\nAs of  Patch 1.09, Roar Medallion increases the damage of the beams of this incantation by 5% in Player vs. Player and
