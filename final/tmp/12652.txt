\n\n Great-Jar's Arsenal \n\nWeight\n1.5\n\nEffect \n\nVastly raises maximum \n       Equip Load, by 19% \n\nGreat-Jar's Arsenal is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Great-Jar's Arsenal raises maximum Equip Load by 19%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA charm that resembles a great jar overflowing with weaponry.Vastly boosts maximum equipment load.\nThe great jar grants this talisman to their warriors. Carry as much as you can — grow big and strong.\n\nGreat-Jar's Arsenal Effect in Elden Ring\nGreat Jar's Arsenal raises maximum Equip Load by 19%.\n\nWhere to find Great-Jar's Arsenal in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Great-Jar's Arsenal: \n\nReward for completing the The Great-Jar's challenge of defeating three NPC duelists in Dragonbarrow (northern Caelid). [Map link]\nYou must kill all three NPC duelists in one life; dying will make it so you have to start from the first duelist again. You can access this area by coming up from Siofra Deep River Well in the underground. \n\nThis area can also be accessed by climbing the roots above the large crystal at the bottom of the valley. However, this is most likely unintentional.\n\nObtaining this talisman while playing online results in player's build being uploaded to the Elden Ring server to be used as randomized Knight of the Great Jar for other players.\nWhen you talk to the Great-Jar, he will only respond with "..."\nAfter talking to him, three red summon signs appear to summon three Knights of the Great-Jar. \n\nIn two of the four corners of the "arena", there are narrow rock outcroppings. Crouching or blocking in these spots will often cause the NPCs to fall off of the cliff as they attack. This is especially useful when sequence-breaking into this area with a low-level character.\nConsider killing the closer of the two archer golems, as he will shoot arrows at you and disrupt your fights.\n\nElden Ring Great-Jar's Arsenal Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  1,000\nIncompatible with Arsenal Charm.\nPatch 1.07 increases Armor's poise in general, so you no longer need this talisman to reach certain poise break point (such as Flame Dancer build etc.). QUESTION
Why is Great-Jar's Arsenal talisman considered valuable for players in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Great-Jar's Arsenal talisman is valuable for players in Elden Ring because it vastly raises the maximum Equip Load by 19%, allowing players to carry more items and gear.
