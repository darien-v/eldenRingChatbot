QUESTION
How can Frozen Armament in Elden Ring be utilized most effectively in combat situations?

ANSWER
Cast Frozen Armament while in motion, ensure to have a staff in one hand and the weapon in the other, cannot typically be used on weapons with secondary damage types like Magic, Holy, Fire, or Lighting, and recast if weapon is unequipped accidentally or two-handed.
