\n\n Grave Glovewort [1] \n\nEffect \n\n        Strengthen ashes to +1 \n\nGrave Glovewort [1] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Grave Glovewort [1] is the base material for upgrading ashes used at the lowest level. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nWhite flower that blooms in catacombs.Each a soundless bell used to summon spirits.\nStrengthens ashes to +1.\nThe art of strengthening ashes is known as spirit tuning. Only a spirit tuner may perform this deed.\n\nGrave Glovewort [1] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Grave Glovewort [1]:\n\n2x inside the Tombsward Catacombs at the Weeping Peninsula: One found in the room to the right of the boss door. A second one is found near a sarcophagus on the bottom level of the first room with a second floor. [Map Link]\n3x inside the Impaler's Catacombs at the Weeping Peninsula. The first is found on top of a sarcophagus in the room to the left of the boss door. A second one can be found in the second section of the sewers with Zombies, after dropping down underneath the Impaler Lift and one more can be found in the third section before climbing up the ladder. [Map Link]\n5x found in the Stormfoot Catacombs: The first one is found by a stine coffin in the room with the boss door. The second one is found in the next room, behind a pillar near the entrance to the narrow corridor. The third is found after climbing the ladder in the hallway where the second flamethrower pillar is. The fourth is found in a room west of where you got the third one. The final one is found to the left of the boss' door's lever. [Map Link]\n5x found in the Deathtouched Catacombs: The first one is located in the first large room left of the boss' door, right in front of the stairs. The fourth one is found by going through the doorway south of where you collected the previous one, sitting on an L-shaped ledge.
