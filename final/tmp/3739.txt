CONTEXT
\n\n Holy Ground \n\n        Raise shield to create an Erdtree-consecrated area that continuously restores HP and boosts defense for self and allies inside it. \n\nHoly Ground is a Skill in Elden Ring. Holy Ground is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, it can be used on all Shields. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Holy Ground in Elden Ring\n\nTogether with Tree Sentinel Armor set inside Auriza Hero's Grave.\n\nElden Ring Holy Ground Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Holy Ground.\nFP Cost: 30 on use.\nThis Skill not Chargeable\nUpon activation, the user raises their shield above their head, glowing with holy energy before releasing it, creating a medium-sized circle on the ground that provides some buffs if you stand in it. \n\nThe animation takes 4 seconds to perform, from start to finish. However, the user can perform another action after 3 seconds.\n\nThe buff provides a 20% boost to Damage negation (absorptions), and a HP regen effect restoring 600 HP over the duration (around 17 HP/second). \n\nThis buff lasts for 35 seconds.\nThis buff affects all allied entities, like player phantoms, friendly NPCs, and of course the user.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nA circle is not created on the ground, and no buff is provided.\nUser still has to go through the starting 3 seconds of the animation before performing another action.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: HP recovery amount has been increased.\n\nHoly Ground Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric
