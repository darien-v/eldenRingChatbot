\n\n Fire's Deadly Sin \n\nSpell Type\nFire Monk Incantation\n\nFP Cost 26\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Sets area and self ablaze with raging flames \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 19 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nFire's Deadly Sin is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Fire's Deadly Sin spell covers both the player and the area with flames\n\nIncantation originating from a deeply ominous prophecy.\nSets both the caster and the surrounding area ablaze with raging flames. The caster's body will remain engulfed in flame for a while, burning them and any who draw near. Charging enhances potency.\nThe prophet despaired, looking up at the Erdtree, for soon the kindling would burst into flame, bringing ruin.\n"The burning of the Erdtree is the first cardinal sin. That is not the domain of mere men."\n\nWhere to find Fire's Deadly Sin\nWhere to find Fire's Deadly Sin:\n\nAcquire the Flightless Bird Painting from Fortified Manor, Leyndell, Royal Capital.\nThen go southeast of Windmill Heights, directly East of where is says Dominula, Windmill Village on the map. You'll see a ghost in a chair who will drop it after disappearing.  [Elden Ring Interactive Map link]\n\nElden Ring Fire's Deadly Sin Guide\n Reward from completing the "Flightless Bird" painting puzzle.\n\nStamina Cost: 28\nDeals fire damage\nDuration: 40 Seconds (52 seconds with Old Lord's Talisman)\nContinually deals fire damage to both the caster and enemies that are at least within dagger range of the player.\nAs a continuous source of fire damage it can reliably reset the Frostbite status effect on enemies for build that inflict frost bite rapidly.\nWearing the Royal Remains Set ensures the caster will never be killed by the DoT this incantation inflicts. The Blessed Dew Talisman, Blessing's Boon/Blessing of the Erdtree incantation, Icon Shield or Crimsonburst Crystal Tear can also mitigate it.\nThis spell, along with  Catch Flame and  Flame Sling, doesn't have a sigil when casted, unlike other spells in the game, this may reference why Giant's Seal doesn't boosts the spells.\nThis is a Body Buff and will not stack with other buffs of the same
