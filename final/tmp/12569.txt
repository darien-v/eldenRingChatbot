[Map Link] He is very close to the grace.\n\nElden Ring Lazuli Glintstone Sword Notes & Tips\n\nThis weapon has a very unique heavy attack that can block attacks before its slash comes out. Charging the heavy attack will further extend the time the weapon can block before slashing. \n\nDespite the way it may appear, the animation does not utilize your left hand shield during the attack.  Only the block stats of the weapon itself will apply\n\nThis special attack does not work with the Curved Sword talisman, but is impacted by the Greatshield Talisman\nBlocking any attack with this weapons heavy attack will never put you into stun, unless the move you have blocked completely drains your stamina.\nThis weapon's unique charged heavy attack does significantly less stance damage than other straight swords (1h Charged R2 does 15 stance damage vs. the typical 30 of most straight swords).\n2-handing this weapon will increase its guard boost by 10%, just like any other weapon. This does include this weapons heavy attack and this does stack with the Greatshield Talisman.\nCarian Knight's Sword shares the unique heavy attack of this weapon. \n\nLazuli  Glintstone Sword boasts higher magic damage, lower stat requirements and a more reliable Ash of War.\nCarian Knight Sword has higher physical damage, higher overall damage, and better block performance (both damage reduction and guard boost)\nGenerally speaking, Carian Knight Sword is better for hybrid casters (with some amount of dex/str investment) while Lazuli Glintstone Sword performs better with a pure caster build (60-80 INT)\n\nAt +10 with 80 INT, 8 STR and 12 DEX (base Sorcerer stats), this weapon has an AR of 561, with 230+118 Magic damage and 193+19 Physical damage.\nAt +10 with 80 STR, 12 DEX and 18 INT (Srength Int build stats), this weapon has an AR of 595, with 193+126 Physical damage and 230+44 Magic damage.\nWeapon Skill: Glintstone Pebble\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War.\nCan't be enchanted with Magic and boosted by Consumables.\nLazuli Glintstone Sword can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones\nSell Value:   100\nBase drop rate is 2% (at 100 Discovery).\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, Straight Swords poise damage has been reduced only on PVP.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed. Please refer to the Upgrades Table of this weapon below. Additionally, the running attack speed, first attack speed and speed of guard counter of straight sword has increased.
