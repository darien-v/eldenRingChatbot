\n\n Iji's Mirrorhelm \n\nDmg Negation \n\nPhy 3.8 \n\nVS Strike 3.8 \n\nVS Slash 5.2 \n\nVS Pierce 5.2 \n\nMagic 6.2 \n\nFire 4.6 \n\nLigt 4.8 \n\nHoly 5.8 \n\nResistance \n\nImmunity 22 \n\nRobustness 42 \n\nFocus 26 \n\nVitality 39 \n\nPoise 7 \n\nHelm\nWgt. 7.5\n\nIji's Mirrorhelm is a Helm in Elden Ring. Iji's Mirrorhelm is a unique item and is not part of a set. It is worn by the troll blacksmith Iji. Iji's Mirrorhelm protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nHelm fashioned from a crystal looking-glass, said to have never left War Counselor Iji's head.Easily broken and weak against striking attacks.\nWorn by those committed to high treason, it wards off the intervention of the Greater Will and its vassal Fingers.\nIji was afraid. Terrified of his own treachery.\n\nWhere to find Iji's Mirrorhelm in Elden Ring\nThe Iji's Mirrorhelm Helm can be found at:\n\nAfter the player confronts Blaidd during Ranni's questline, War Counselor Iji will have new dialogue available. Upon exhausting that and returning to the area again, his corpse can be found near his usual spot at the Road to the Manor grace site. Upon his anvil, the player will find Iji's Mirrorhelm as well as Iji's Bell Bearing.
