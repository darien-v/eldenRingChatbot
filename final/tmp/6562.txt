\n\n Barricade Shield \n\n        Skill made famous by Sir Neidhardt. Focus your energy into the shield, temporarily hardening it to deflect greater blows. \n\nBarricade Shield is a Skill in Elden Ring. Barricade Shield is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible weapons. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Barricade Shield in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill of the Great Turtle Shell.\nAsh of War: Barricade Shield is dropped by Night's Cavalry at Weeping Peninsula. [Elden Ring Map Link].\n\nElden Ring Barricade Shield Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Barricade Shield.\nFP Cost: 12\nDuration: 10 Seconds\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nGives the shield Tier 4 Hardness\nHalf's the stamina cost when blocking attacks, irrespective of the shields "Guard Boost". It does not change the Guard Boost star.  \n\nI.e. If it costed 30 stamina to block an attack, now it costs 15 to block the same attack\n\nIf the skill is used in right hand, it will 2 hand the shield.\nNote: As of Patch 1.07, the effect duration increased from 6 to 10 seconds and the increase to guard boost differs depending on the shield.\n\nBarricade Shield Demonstration\n\nGolden Sword Sage Written Guide\n\nGolden Eye Written
