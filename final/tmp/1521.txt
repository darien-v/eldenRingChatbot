The Old Aristocrat Set is a light cloth set worn by the Old Nobles commonly found wandering the roads. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to Find Old Aristocrat Set in Elden Ring\n\nAll pieces of the Old Aristocrat Set are dropped by the Old Noble enemies, often found within larger groups of Wandering Nobles wandering the roads of most areas. The banner they hold makes them easily distinguishable from other Wandering Nobles.\nChurch of Vows - Three Old Nobles can be found close to the Site of Grace; one to the north, two to the east.\nThere are two Old Nobles within the Troll-pulled Carriage's caravan west of the Waypoint Ruins Cellar Site of Grace.\nAltus Plateau - There is an Old Noble immediately south of the Rampartside Path Site of Grace, in a small group and can be quickly farmed.\n\nOld Aristocrat Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nOld Aristocrat Cowl\nOld Aristocrat Gown\nOld Aristocrat Shoes\n\nOld Aristocrat Set Notes and Tips\n\nThese are not often dropped, due to the fact that these enemies aren't as numerous as other variations of Aristocrats.\nNot to be confused with the Aristocrat Set.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Old Aristocrat Set considered a light cloth set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Old Aristocrat Set is considered a light cloth set in Elden Ring due to its lighter weight compared to heavier armor sets.
