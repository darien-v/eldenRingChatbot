CONTEXT
\n\n Recusant Finger \n\nType\nReusable\n\nEffect \n\n        Attempts invasion of other player's world \n\nRecusant Finger is a Multiplayer Item in Elden Ring. Multiplayer Items allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.\n\nItem for online play. (Can also be used from the Multiplayer menu.)\nAttempts an invasion of another player's world. If successful, you will arrive as an invader (Recusant) with the objective of defeating the Host of Fingers of that world.\nThis furled finger is covered in snake scales. An heirloom of the lord of the Volcano Manor, bequeathed to Tarnished who have become family.\n\nWhere to find Recusant Finger in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Recusant Finger:\n\nIt can be found on a table in of the rooms of Volcano Manor. [Map Link]\nVideo Location\n\nRecusant Finger Guide for Elden Ring\n\nUsing the Recusant Finger attempts invasion of other player's world.\nAfter successfully invading, the goal of the invader is to eliminate the host of the world.\nIf the invader is successful in killing the host, or the host dies while the invader is present, the invader is rewarded with 1x Rune Arc and 1x Furlcalling Finger Remedy in addition to runes.\nIf the invader fails and dies during the invasion, they will drop their runes as if they died in their own world. ( Runes previously resting on the floor before the invasion will be lost)\n\nElden Ring Recusant Finger Notes & Tips\n\nIf they host enters a boss arena, the invader is sent back to their own world.\nThe invader does not need to strike the killing blow on the host of the world. If the invader is simply present in the world when the host dies, the invasion will be considered a success.\nInvaders are considered allies by normally hostile NPC enemies. This can be used to even out fights against hosts with many summoned players.\nIf the host dies shortly after dying as the invader, the invasion will be considered a success and the invader will be rewarded and not lose any runes.\nKilling any summoned players will grant additional runes.\n\nElden Ring Multiplayer Items\n\nBloody Finger  ♦  Blue Cipher Ring  ♦  Duelist's Furled Finger  ♦  Festering Bloody Finger  ♦  Finger Severer  ♦  Furlcalling Finger Remedy  ♦  Grace Mimic  ♦  Phantom Bloody Finger  ♦  Scriptstone  ♦  Small Golden Effigy  ♦  Small Red Effigy  ♦  Tarnished's Furled Finger  ♦  Tarnished's Wizened Finger  ♦  Taunter's Tongue  ♦  White Cipher Ring\n\n
