QUESTION
Why is the Hand of Malenia considered a good weapon for Samurai Build and Bleed Builds in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Hand of Malenia is considered a good weapon for Samurai Build and Bleed Builds in Elden Ring because it scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity, and it causes Blood loss Buildup.
