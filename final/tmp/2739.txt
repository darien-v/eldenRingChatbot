Godrick Knight Set is a Light Weight armor worn by knights loyal to Godrick The Grafted. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Godrick Knight Set in Elden Ring\nThere's a chance the Godrick Knight Set is dropped by defeating a Godrick Knight. You can find these at:\n\nGatefront Ruins: Dropped by the heavy Godrick Knight patrolling the outpost.\nFort Haight: Chance to drop from the Godrick Knight atop the fort walls (one time).\n\nGodrick Knight Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nGodrick Knight Helm\nGodrick Knight Armor\nGodrick Knight Gauntlets\nGodrick Knight Greaves\n\nGodrick Knight Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Godrick Knight Armor can be altered to the Godrick Knight Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
