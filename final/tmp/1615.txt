Bosses are unique and challenging Enemies that can be encountered all over the Lands Between. Great Enemy bosses can drop unique Spirit Ashes, Weapons, Spells, and other various Items. This is an optional boss, as players don't need to defeat it in order to advance in Elden Ring. However, doing so yields useful items, as well as Runes.\nGlintstone Dragon is the normal enemy form of this boss. Other bosses with the same basic attacks are Flying Dragon Agheel, Flying Dragon Greyll, Decaying Ekzykes, Glintstone Dragon Smarag, and Borealis the Freezing Fog. Lesser Dragon is a normal enemy with the same basic attacks.\n\n Adula, a devourer of sorcerers, was bested by Ranni and subsequently swore a knightly oath to her Dark Moon.\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dragon Adula Location\n\nThree Sisters [More Info] [Map Link] \n\nShe is impossible to kill at this location even if you deplete her HP bar in one hit, she is scripted to fly off instead.\n\nCathedral of Manus Celes [More Info] [Map Link]\n\nGlintstone Dragon Adula Combat information\n\nStance: 120\nParryable: No\nIs vulnerable to a  critical hit  after being stance broken\nDamage: Standard, Magic\n Dragon type\nWeak spot: head (+50% damage)\nSee Flying Dragon Agheel for a full boss guide\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nStandard: 35\nSlash: 35\nStrike: 35\nPierce: 10\n\nMagic: 80\nFire: 40\nLightning: 40\nHoly: 40\n\nThe negation numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nAdditional Fight Strategy\nThe Best Tips for Glintstone Dragon Adula:\n\nAdula has very high Magic resistance, so Intelligence builds should use spells like Rock Sling that deal physical damage.\nMeteorite of Astel with Cerulean Hidden Tear can easily trivialize the fight by raining meteorites that deal physical damage.\nWear armor with high Magic and Physical resistance, like the Banished Knight Set.\n\nAdditional Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nMost attacks are the same as Flying Dragon Agheel, except that Adula's flame does Magic damage\n\nSeeking Shards\nRears back and summons a giant crystal in front of its mouth. After a moment, the crystal will burst, sending several shards that will home in on your location. They track very well, and are difficult to dodge effectively. Used every 50 seconds or so.\nDuck behind something in the environment to block the projectiles, or use Eternal Darkness to consume them\n\nAdula's Moonblade\nFlies up and backwards, conjures a magical greatsword in its maw, then swoops down to slash it in a wide arc, loosing a wave of frost projectiles in its wake. Only used below 70% health and only once every 50 seconds\nWatch for its head to turn to its right and move back and to its left to dodge the main sweeping attack as well as many of the projectiles that fly out. Alternatively, if you are close enough, you can move under their body to avoid the sword entirely\n\nGlintstone Dragon Adula Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nAdula was a devourer of sorcerers, but was defeated by Ranni, after which they swore an oath to serve her, which explains why it appears in places relating to Ranni’s questline.\nThe name Adula is a reference to the scientific name, Adularia, of the semi-precious gem moonstone and the mountain in the Swiss Alps, Adula, where moonstone was first found.\n\nGlintstone Dragon Adula Combo Chart\n\n Glintstone Dragon Adula  Three Sisters  \n\nDrops\nCan't be killed here\n\nHP\n4,405\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Dragon Adula Boss (Three Sisters)\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Royal Moongazing Grounds\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nGlintstone Dragon Adula Location\nRoaming the Three Sisters area, usually near the door out of the Royal Moongazing Grounds. QUESTION
How can players effectively counter Glintstone Dragon Adula's "Seeking Shards" attack during combat?

ANSWER
Players can counter Glintstone Dragon Adula's "Seeking Shards" attack by either ducking behind environmental cover to block the projectiles or by using Eternal Darkness to consume them.
