Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. \n\nA young plant, crystalized before it could mature.Material used for crafting items. \nWidely found in Liurnia Lake.\n\nWhere to find Crystal Bud in Elden Ring\nCrystal Bud found in the overworld respawns after resting at a Site of Grace.\nIn the world\n\nGrows in Liurnia of the Lakes\n2 by Laskyar Ruins: One to the northeast of the Site of Grace and another to the southwest.\n5 by Debate Parlor Site of Grace in Raya Lucaria Academy: Exit into the courtyard, turn east, and jump over the fence. Climb up the ladder and go through the broken glass window to reach the second floor of the debate parlor. On a corpse to your left.
