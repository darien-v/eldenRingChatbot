QUESTION
What are the effects of using Briars of Punishment in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Wounds the caster to unleash a trail of bloodthorns, inflicts Hemorrhage buildup on hit, has long range and slight tracking, costs 50 HP to cast, and there is no known way to lower the health requirement.
