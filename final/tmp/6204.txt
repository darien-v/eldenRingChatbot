Enemy magic and holy damage negation will be reduced for all entities within the area, including the caster. \n\nKnowledge Above All is a Skill in Elden Ring. QUESTION
Why would reducing magic and holy damage negation be important in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Reducing magic and holy damage negation is important in Elden Ring to make spells and holy attacks more effective against enemies.
