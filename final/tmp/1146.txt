\n\n Bestial Sanctum \n\nThe Bestial Sanctum is a refuge for Gurranq, Beast Clergyman, who feeds on Deathroot. Offer him any in your possession, and he will grant you the strength of beasts.\n\nBestial Sanctum is a Location in Elden Ring found in Dragonbarrow. You can arrive at this location by exploring the northeastern portion of Dragonbarrow through Caelid or by using a teleporter in Limgrave, near the Third Church of Marika, after talking to D, Hunter of the Dead and obtaining directions.\n\nBestial Sanctum Map\nThe Bestial Sanctum is located at the northernmost tip of Dragonbarrow. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Bestial Sanctum\n\nGurranq, Beast Clergyman: Rewards the player for bringing Deathroot Items as follows:\n\n1st: Clawmark Seal + QUESTION
How can players access the Bestial Sanctum and interact with Gurranq, Beast Clergyman, in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can access the Bestial Sanctum in Elden Ring by exploring the northeastern portion of Dragonbarrow through Caelid or by using a teleporter in Limgrave near the Third Church of Marika after talking to D, Hunter of the Dead, and obtaining directions. Once there, they can interact with Gurranq, Beast Clergyman, by offering him Deathroot for various rewards.
