\n\n Haligdrake Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.6\n\nEffect \n\n Reduces  \n       Holy Damage taken by 13/17/20% \n\nHaligdrake Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Haligdrake Talisman reduces Holy Damage taken by 13/17/20%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nTalisman depicting a golden ancient dragon.Boosts holy damage negation.\nThe ancient dragons, who ruled in the prehistoric era before the Erdtree, would protect their lord as a wall of living rock.And so it is that the shape of the dragon has become symbolic of all manner of protections.\n\nHaligdrake Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nHaligdrake Talisman reduces Holy Damage taken by 13/17/20%. (Effect reduced to 4/5/6% in PvP.)\n\nWhere to find Haligdrake Talisman in Elden Ring\nBase talisman: Looted off a body in the Stranded Graveyard, on a ledge above the start of the tutorial. There are two ways to reach it:\n\nTravel to the Seaside Ruins Site of Grace. Walk south a short distance along the edge of the cliff (just past the crumbling archway). Look down toward the beach below and look for the windstream. Hop on Torrent and simply walk off the edge. Travel north until you see a small cave opening in the cliff face. [Map link]\nTravel to the The First Step Site of Grace. and make your way down the cliff to the east (should lead to an area with bats and crucifixes). Continue south east towards a bunch of crumbling pillars. Line yourself up with the pillar that is standing on the beach. Then, run or walk off of the edge of the cliff and land on a small ledge on said pillar (do not jump as you will be blocked by the ledge at the same level as the cliff). Then, safely hop down to the beach and the entrance to the cave is on the bottom of the cliff.\n\nHaligdrake Talisman +1:  Leyndell Catacombs: Found on a corpse next to a Lesser Spirit-caller Snail. From the Leyndell Catacombs site of grace, head up the elevator in the southeast to find a room with 3 spirit crossbowmen. Look under the arch next to the stairs for an illusory wall. Hit it to dispel the illusion, and then hit the wall on the left to dispel another illusion. The corpse and snail are found in here. [Map link]\nVideo Location\nHaligdrake Talisman +2: Looted off a body in Mohgwyn Palace, found in a small cluster of graves near a watchful Giant Crow perched up high.
