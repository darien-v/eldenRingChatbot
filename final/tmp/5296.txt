Crafting Materials are resources that can be obtained throughout The Lands Between and used to create a wide variety of items. \n\nA dusky-yellow flower that has started to fade and wilt. Material used for crafting items. Found throughout the Lands Between. Said to grow from the old fallen leaves of the Erdtree, these flowers serve as the base material for a number of sorcery techniques.\n\nErdleaf Flower Location in Elden Ring\nErdleaf Flower found in the overworld respawns after resting at a Site of Grace.\nIN THE WORLD\n\nFound in Limgrave, Weeping Peninsula, and Liurnia of The Lakes.\n\nFarming locations\n\nThe Four Belfries: Four are extremely close to the Site of Grace.Three are immediately next to the Site of Grace.
