QUESTION
Why is the Blue Silver Mail Skirt considered part of the Blue Silver Set Armor Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Blue Silver Mail Skirt is considered part of the Blue Silver Set Armor Set in Elden Ring because it is one of the pieces that, when worn together, offers enhanced defensive properties and shares a thematic design.
