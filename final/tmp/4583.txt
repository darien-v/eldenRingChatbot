\n\n Ash of War: Glintstone Pebble \n\nAffinity\nMagic\n\nSkill\nGlintstone Pebble Skill\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Glintstone Pebble is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Glintstone Pebble provides Magic affinity and the Glintstone Pebble Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Magic affinity and the following skill:\n"Glintstone Pebble: Skill that employs the glintstone sorcery of the same name. Follow up with a strong attack to chain this skill into a lunging thrust, performed while the armament is still imbued with glintstone."\nUsable on swords as well as polearms capable of thrusting (colossal weapons excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Glintstone Pebble Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Glintstone Pebble:\n\nSold by Sorcerer Rogier at Stormveil Castle or the Roundtable Hold for 1,500 .\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Rogier's Bell Bearing.\n\nAsh of War: Glintstone Pebble Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Glintstone Pebble Skill.\nProvides the Magic affinity, which adds Magic damage and Intelligence scaling, reduces Base damage and Strength / Dexterity Scaling.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Magic, Cold.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds.\n\nGlintstone Pebble Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Glintstone Pebble Notes & Tips\n\n The damage of Glintstone Pebble scales with Int, upgrade level, and weapon affinity for the projectile, as well as weapon's Int scaling value and weapon's AR for the magic-imbued thrust.  \n This Ash of War deals the most damage with the "Magic" affinity, deals less damage with the "Cold" affinity, and deals the least damage with "Heavy/Keen/Quality/Standard" affinities. \nC to B Int scaling increase averages 5-6% added damage on the enhanced thrust portion of the combo. \n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric
