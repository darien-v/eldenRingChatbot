\n\n Broken Statue \n\nLocation\nLeyndell Royal CapitalDeeproot Depths\n\nDrops\n 3332 - 3517 Runes\n\nBroken Statue is an Enemy in Elden Ring. Broken Statues appear to be broken-down Gargoyles, but are much smaller. They are immobile, so they spit exploding powder from their fixed position, which is often out of range of melee attacks.\n\nElden Ring Broken Statue Locations\nWhere to Find Broken Statue:\n\nLeyndell, Royal Capital\nDeeproot Depths\n\nElden Ring Broken Statue Drops\n\n 3332 - 3517 Runes\n\nBroken Statue Notes & Tips\n\nLow resistance to Strike, Standard and Magic Damage\nMedium resistance to other types of damage\nImmune to Status Effect\nImmobile enemy, that is helpless on long range or when encountered from back\nDamage: Fire\nTheir exploding breath can easily stun players on horseback, leading them to an unfortunate fall.\nThey can serve as a relatively easy Rune farm for the mid-game. Teleport to the “Across the Roots” Site of Grace, then simply run back and kill every Statue without them firing at you. Just be careful not to fall off if they do aggro onto you.\nTheir exploding breath appears to be similar to Spark Aromatics, used by Perfumers.\n\nElden Ring Broken Statue Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring
