\n\n Lesser Alabaster Lord \n\nLocation\nSeaside Ruins Yelough Anix Tunnel Ailing Village\n\nDrops\n 311 - 4852 Runes Alabaster Lord’s SwordAsh of War: Gravitas\n\nLesser Alabaster Lord is a Humanoid Creature in Elden Ring. Lesser Alabaster Lords are rare enemies, most commonly found in caves. Their look is that of a very tall and slim humanoid with stone skin and long hair. They wield a rather long Greatsword, as opposed to the Onyx Lords, who use curved greatswords.\n\n A race of ancients with skin of stone who were said to have risen to life when a meteor struck long ago.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Alabaster Lord Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Alabaster Lord:\n\nYou can find one of these creatures at the shores of South Limgrave. It is settled under a large, arched structure, warming up to a bonfire. This Lesser Alabaster Lord drops Ash of War: Gravitas when defeated. [Map Link]\nOne appears out of a floating purple sphere in the Yelough Anix Tunnel.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Alabaster Lord Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 311 - 4852\n\nAsh of War: Gravitas\n100%\nLooted from a Alabaster Lord that uses the Gravitas skill as one of its attacks when it fights. You can find the enemy roaming by the beach side at the stone arch that has a bonfire near the Seaside Ruins Site of Grace in south of Limgrave. From the site of grace, simply jump all the way to the beachside using your mount and defeat the enemy.\n\nAlabaster Lord's Sword\n100%\n Awarded upon killing an killing an Alabaster Lord in the Lake of Rot. You have to raise the platforms to get up to him by stepping on the buttons around the swamp. There are two buttons that are next to small columns or obelisks. Once you get raised on the platform, take on the long and narrow path, after a few meters you will be greeted by this enemy. Kill him to obtain the Alabaster Lord's Sword. Note: If Alabaster Lord dies after you die the sword will not drop. The Alabaster Lords in the Yelough Anix Tunnel can also drop their swords upon defeat.  \n\nGravity Well\n100%\nDropped by the Alabaster Lord enemy that appears from a gravity portal. The section is near the graveyard and the large rotating lift of Raya Lucaria Academy.\n\nLesser Alabaster Lord Notes & Tips\n\nLesser Alabaster Lord's are the co-founders of gravity sorceries, as seen in sorceries description (along with the weapon they wield) \nThey also have a darker counter part that wields a curved greatsword known as the Onyx Lord.\nThe Alabaster Lords are a race of ancients with skin of stone who were said to have risen to life when a meteor struck long ago.\nOne of the Alabaster Lords became a mentor of General Radahn and taught him Gravity Magic, with Collapsing Stars, Gravity Well and Rock Sling being three of the gravitational techniques studied by the young Radahn.\n\nLesser Alabaster Lord Image Gallery\n\nAll Elden Ring Creatures &
