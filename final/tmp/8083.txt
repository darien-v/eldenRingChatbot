\n\n Depraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 124\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons spirit of Depraved Perfumer Carmaan \n\nDepraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Depraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes are used to summon the spirit of Carmaan, who throw Spark Aromatics and can heal when damaged.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirit of Carmaan, the depraved perfumer.  Depraved perfumers practice their art for themselves alone. Carmaan was a notably formidable perfumer whose strength rivaled that of heroes, and it is said that he was in search of a secret physick of revivification.\n\nWhere to find Depraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes\nWhere to Find Depraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes\n\nLocated on a corpse in Volcano Manor that is near the top of Mt. Gelmir, west of Altus Plateau. Finding these ashes involves beginning the Volcano Manor questline. Once inside the manor talk to Lady Tanith and agree to receive the drawing-room key which opens the doors in the hallway to the right. In the room opposite to a bolted shut door is an illusionary wall that hides the secret passage leading you around the manor's perimeter into the locked room that hides the corpse holding the ashes.
