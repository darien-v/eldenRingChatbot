\n\n Alabaster Lord's Pull \n\n        Thrust the armament into the ground to create a gravity well. In \n       addition to dealing damage, this attack pulls enemies in. \n       Has a greater area of effect than Gravitas. \n\nAlabaster Lord's Pull is a Skill in Elden Ring. Alabaster Lord's Pull creates a powerful gravity well, that pulls enemies in while inflicting damage.
