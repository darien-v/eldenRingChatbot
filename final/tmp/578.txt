Its base is home to the spirits of the Minotaur-like Ancestral Followers.\n\nMinor Erdtree (Liurnia Northeast) is a Location in Elden Ring. The Minor Erdtree (Liurnia Northeast) is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. This Minor Erdtree can be reached by heading north along the cliffside road on the eastern landmass of Liurnia.\n\nMinor Erdtree (Liurnia Northeast) Map\nThis Minor Erdtree is located in the northeast of Liurnia, on a plateau past the Church of Vows.
