[Map Link]\nAfter gaining access to Leyndell, Royal Capital, he relocates to the colosseum in Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Map Link]\nHe then relocates to the Mountaintops of The Giants.\nHis corpse can then be found near his original location at the bottom of the colosseum in the Ashen Capital.\n\nGoldmask Questline: How to complete Goldmask's Quest\nGoldmask is part of Brother Corhyn's Questline.\n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: Goldmask\nThis NPC has no dialogue other than "..."\n\nElden Ring Goldmask Notes & Trivia\n\nGives Mending Rune of Perfect Order in Leyndell, Ashen Capital at the end of Brother Corhyn's Questline. Head south-southwest from the Ashen Capital Site of Grace past the round staircase on your left and cross the tree log bridge, heading up the stairs to the south opposite the Giant Miranda Sprout. Goldmask's corpse will be uphill just around the bend, lying in light along the right wall.\nIf you go back to his corpse after you get the Mending Rune of Perfect Order, he will drop Goldmask's Set (excluding the Radiant Gold Mask, which can be picked up in the Altus Plateau). QUESTION
How can players complete Goldmask's Quest in Elden Ring?


ANSWER
Players can complete Goldmask's Quest in Elden Ring by finishing Brother Corhyn's Questline, leading to acquiring the Mending Rune of Perfect Order from Goldmask's corpse and returning to receive Goldmask's Set.
