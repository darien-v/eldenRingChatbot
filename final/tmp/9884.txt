QUESTION
Why do Depraved Perfumer enemies wear the Depraved Perfumer Headscarf in Elden Ring and what significance does it hold for their character development and lore within the game's world?

ANSWER
The Depraved Perfumer enemies in Elden Ring wear the Depraved Perfumer Headscarf as a symbol of their heresy-inclined nature, signifying their slow descent into self-destruction due to altering their body and mind with spices, thus reflecting their character development and lore within the game's world.
