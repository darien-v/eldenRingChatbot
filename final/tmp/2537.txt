Nox Swordstress Set is a cloth set worn by the Nox Swordstresses of Nokron and Nokstella. They share the Nox Monk Bracelets and Nox Monk Greaves with the Nox Monk Set and Night Maiden Set. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Nox Swordstress Set in Elden Ring\n\nPieces of the Nox Swordstress Set are dropped by Nox Swordstress enemies found in Nokron and Nokstella.\nThree can be found in relatively close proximity to the Nokstella, Eternal City site of grace. One up the stairs in a side room, and 2 in the river riding Giant Ants.\n\nNox Swordstress SetArmor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nNox Swordstress Crown\nNox Swordstress Armor\nNox Bracelets\nNox Greaves\n\nNox Swordstress Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Nox Swordstress Crown can be altered to the Nox Swordstress Crown (Altered) to remove the hat.\nThe Nox Swordstress Armor can be altered to the Nox Swordstress Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
