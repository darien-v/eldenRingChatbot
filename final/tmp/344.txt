\n\n Seluvis's Rise \n\nA tower bequeathed unto Preceptor Seluvis. Serves as his personal study as he delves into the sorcerous arts.\n\nSeluvis's Rise is a Location in Elden Ring. Seluvis's Rise is one of three towers found in the Three Sisters sub-region of Liurnia of the Lakes. This tower is found in the southwest section of Three Sisters. The entrance is initially blocked until you speak to Ranni at Ranni's Rise for the first time.\n\nSeluvis's Rise Map\nSeluvis's Rise is the tower located in the southwest of the Three Sisters sub-region. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Seluvis's Rise\n\nPreceptor Seluvis\n\nSeluvis's Rise Walkthrough\nWhen first arriving at the Three Sisters, the entrance to Seluvis's Rise is blocked by a barrier. It is unlocked only after speaking to Ranni the Witch atop Ranni's Rise in the northwest and subsequently being invited by Preceptor Seluvis.\nOnce invited, you will find Seluvis at the ground floor who hands you Seluvis's Potion and tasks you with finding a woman named Nepheli.\nYou can climb the ladder behind Seluvis to reach the top floor of his tower where you will find a chest with a Memory Stone inside.\nOutside, in a ruin between Ranni's Rise and Renna's Rise, there is an illusory wall / floor on the ground, that can be opened by attacking or rolling on it. This leads to a bunch of frozen characters and a note that says "Seluvis' Puppet. Do not touch". There is another Illusory Wall just to the left of this that reveals what appears to be a puppet of Sorceress Sellen.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Seluvis's Rise\n\nGather & Farm
