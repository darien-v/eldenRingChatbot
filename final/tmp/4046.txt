(Effect reduced to 4/5/6% in PvP.)\n\nWhere to find Spelldrake Talisman in Elden Ring\n\nBase talisman: Acquired by defeating Runebear at the end of the Earthbore Cave in the Weeping Peninsula. [Map Link]\nSpelldrake Talisman +1: Sellia, Town of Sorcery: Found in a small room in the northwest section of town, right under the balcony containing the Staff of Loss. This room is initially sealed by a barrier that can be dispelled by lighting a brazier atop a tower in the southwest of town. [Map Link]\nSpelldrake Talisman +2: Found in the secret catacombs underneath the Hidden Path to the Haligtree by turning right just before the Stray Mimic Tear's boss door, following the corridor up and taking the lift. The talisman can be found on a corpse in an alcove at the top. QUESTION
What formula can be used to calculate magic damage negation after equipping the Spelldrake Talisman +2 in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
MNWT = 100 - [(100 - MNWOT) * 0.8], MNWT: Magic Negation With Talisman, MNWOT: Magic Negation With Out Talisman.
