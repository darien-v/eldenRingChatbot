QUESTION
Why is Lightning Grease a valuable consumable item in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Lightning Grease is valuable in Elden Ring as it coats armament, adding 85 additional Lightning Damage to attacks for 60 seconds, crafting only requires Root Resin and Fulgurbloom, and it can be found in various locations, enabling players to easily enhance their combat capabilities.
