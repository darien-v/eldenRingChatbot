How can one effectively utilize Death Lightning in combat situations within Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Utilize Death Lightning by channeling Fortissax's power, inflicting Death Blight on foes, charging to extend the storm duration, and trading Remembrance of the Lichdragon with Enia at Roundtable Hold to obtain the spell in Elden Ring.
