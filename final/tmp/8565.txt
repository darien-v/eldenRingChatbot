Guardian Garb (Full Bloom) is part of the Guardian Set Armor Set, and  is the armor worn by the Guardians found around various Minor Erdtrees. Guardian Garb (Full Bloom) is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nEngraved golden garb. QUESTION
Why does the Guardian Garb (Full Bloom) in Elden Ring greatly lower fire damage negation while raising the HP recovery effects of the Flask of Crimson Tears?

ANSWER
The Guardian Garb (Full Bloom) in Elden Ring greatly lowers fire damage negation while raising the HP recovery effects of the Flask of Crimson Tears because it is said that the blood-red flowers blooming on their backs mark the senescence of their ancient pact, possibly indicating that the guardians are part tree already.
