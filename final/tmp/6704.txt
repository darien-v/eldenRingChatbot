\n\n Map (Liurnia, West) \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Map of Liurnia's western region. \n\nMap (Liurnia, West) is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Map (Liurnia, West) reveals the western portion of the Liurnia region on the map. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nMap of Liurnia's western region.\nThe majority of Liurnia, known for its vast forests and ever-present fog, is sinking into a lake. The western coast has always been closely associated with Carian royalty.\n\nMap (Liurnia, West) Location in Elden Ring\nMap (Liurnia, West) can be found in the following location:\n\nOn a monolith in the open field northeast of the North Liurnia Lake Shore Site of Grace in northern Liurnia. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Map (Liurnia, West) Use\nMap (Liurnia, West) can be used to reveal the western portion of the Liurnia region.\n\nElden Ring Map (Liurnia, West) Notes & Tips\nFAQ: Where is Elden Ring West map?\n\nMaps in Elden Ring are collected in parts.
