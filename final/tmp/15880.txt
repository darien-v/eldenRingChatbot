CONTEXT
\nHeavy Thrusting Swords  are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Heavy Thrusting Swords focus on piercing attacks and are best used to counter or follow-up an enemy's attack. Their extra weight packs some extra physical attack power and still allows players to use this weapon with a Shield.\nHeavy Thrusting Swords can be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, so play around with them and see what you like as you acquire more. These weapons also have Feints, similar to regular thrusting swords and curved swords.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, the speed of Heavy Thrusting Swords crouching attacks has been reduced. Also, the stagger caused by double-handed Heavy Thrusting Swords jumping attacks has been reduced. And finally, Poise damage has also been reduced, but only for PVP.\n\nElden Ring Heavy Thrusting Sword Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Heavy Thrusting Swords in Elden Ring.
