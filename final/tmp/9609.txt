\n\n Lazuli Glintstone Crown \n\nDmg Negation \n\nPhy 4.4 \n\nVS Strike 3.1 \n\nVS Slash 5.2 \n\nVS Pierce 4.8 \n\nMagic 4.4 \n\nFire 4.5 \n\nLigt 3.4 \n\nHoly 3.8 \n\nResistance \n\nImmunity 16 \n\nRobustness 26 \n\nFocus 10 \n\nVitality 10 \n\nPoise 6 \n\nHelm\nWgt. 5.1\n\nLazuli Glintstone Crown is a Helm in Elden Ring. Lazuli Glintstone Crown is part of the Lazuli Sorcerer Set, and is a Medium Weight armor that increase intelligence and dexterity to the detriment of HP. Lazuli Glintstone Crown protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nOne of the glintstone crowns bestowed upon Raya Lucaria scholars whose pursuits were deemed worthy.\nIncreases intelligence and dexterity to the detriment of HP.\nScholars of the Lazuli Conspectus study Carian sorceries - a heterodox pursuit that views the moon as equal to the stars.\n\nWhere to find Lazuli Glintstone Crown in Elden Ring\nThe Lazuli Glintstone Crown Helm can be found at:\n\nFound at Raya Lucaria Academy. [Map Link]\nClosest Site of Grace: Debate Parlor.\nFrom the Site of Grace, go North. Upon reaching the courtyard, turn left and climb the ruined staircase, then climb the second staircase where 2 mages are shooting at you. When you reach the door, turn left and jump over the railing - behind you is a mage and a shortcut 1-way door. Climb the stairs and turn left, then jump over the railing again. Follow the roof and climb the ladder. After the mage ahead of you, turn right and follow the path until you see a lower roof top. Jump on it, then jump again on the second one. Turn left and go to the edge, there's a gap. Jump it and follow the roof forward and to the right. You will enter a tower with a wooden floor and a hole in it. There are wooden platforms to drop down. The Crystallized Crab is on one of these platforms. Look for the Crystallized Crab inside the hole and kill it, and it will reward you with the crown.\n\nLazuli Glintstone Crown Set in Elden Ring\n\nLazuli Glintstone Crown is a piece of the Lazuli
