\n\n Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nBone Peddler's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing allows you to buy items from the Two Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story. \n\nBell bearing of an unfortunate bone peddler who was slain by the Bell Bearing Hunter.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Rountable Hold to gain access to the following items:\n\nThin Beast Bones\nHefty Beast Bone\n\nWhere to find Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing in Elden Ring\nDropped by the Bell Bearing Hunter Boss invasion in Warmaster's Shack, Stormhill (Limgrave). This invasion only triggers at night.
