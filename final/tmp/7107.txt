Giant Rats are hostile creatures even if not attacked, and are usually seen in abandoned areas or within caves. They vary in size, ranging from small normal-sized Rats, to large human-sized Rats. These rodent creatures may very well attack alone, but will often charge in groups, therefore making them a considerable threat.\nA group of Giant Rats can be summoned for aid as spirits by using the Giant Rat Ashes.\n\n[They] swarm upon their victims... QUESTION
How can players effectively deal with groups of Giant Rats in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can effectively deal with groups of Giant Rats in Elden Ring by being cautious not to get mobbed, using slash and fire damage, while remembering that these creatures do not inflict plague status effects and are not parryable.
