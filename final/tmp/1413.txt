As long as you move when you see the Miranda's Rain of Light attack starting up, you can fight the Omenkiller the same way you would one-on-one.\nMagic and Ranged Users\nStay away from the Miranda and move when you see its Rain of Light attack. Since the Omenkiller has higher elemental damage negation, it's a good idea to use spells that inflict physical damage like Rock Sling or Bestial Incantations.\n\nOmenkiller and Miranda Flower Attacks
