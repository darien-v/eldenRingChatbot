CONTEXT
\n\n Cragblade \n\n        Skill that manipulates gravity. Bury the armament in the ground, pulling rocks from the earth to reinforce it. Increases attack power and makes it easier to break enemy stance. \n\nCragblade is a Skill in Elden Ring. Cragblade is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, can be used on melee armaments (Whips excepted). Updated to Patch 1.08.\n\nHow to get Cragblade in Elden Ring\n\nObtained by killing a Teardrop Scarab in Caelid, located to the west of Impassable Greatbridge Site of Grace, near the sword monument. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Cragblade Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Cragblade.\nFP Cost: 16 on use.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nBoth of this Skill's attacks can be Parried\nUpon activation, the user slams their armament (weapon) to the ground, then briefly uses gravity magic to encase the striking end of the armament in stone, before pulling it out from the ground. \n\nThis provides a temporary weapon buff.\nThe user is able to turn and "aim" when slamming the weapon to the ground, and when pulling it out. \nBoth motions (Slam and Pull) deal damage if an enemy is close enough to the user.\n\nThey also consume stamina separately.\n\nThe buff increases physical attack power by 15%, Stance damage by 10%, and stamina damage against a blocking enemy by 50% \n\nThe Weapon Buff lasts for 60 seconds.\nBuff is only applied to the weapon when pulling it out of the ground. If the user is interrupted before that, the FP will be consumed, but the buff will not be applied.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nThe weapon buff is not applied.\nDeals reduced damage.\nConsumes 30% more stamina on use.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nEffect duration has been extended from 30 to 60 seconds.\nPhysical Attack Power buff has been increased from 10% to 15%.\nStamina Attack Power against guarding enemies has been increased from 20% to 50%.\nStance damage buff has been increased from 10% to 20%.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.08 as follows:\n\nPoise Damage has been reduced for Weapon Arts, dropping this buff back down to 10%.\n\nBuilds & Videos for Cragblade in Elden Ring\n\nCragblade Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Tower Knight
