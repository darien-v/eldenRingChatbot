\n\n Ash of War: Poison Moth Flight \n\nAffinity\nPoison\n\nSkill\nPoison Moth Flight\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Poison Moth Flight is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Poison Moth Flight provides Poison affinity and the Poison Moth Flight Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Poison affinity and the following skill:\n"Poison Moth Flight: Slash with a poison-infused blade. If the follow-up strike lands on a poisoned foe, it will deal significant damage."\nUsable on small and medium swords (twinblades excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Poison Moth Flight Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Poison Moth Flight:\n\nDropped by Night's Cavalry in Caelid. The Night's Cavalry, as usual, only spawns at night. It will start to walk westward on the road north of the Cathedral of Dragon Communion. [Map Link]\n\nAsh of War: Poison Moth Flight Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Poison Moth Flight.\nDeals 9 stance damage\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Poison, Blood, Occult\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Curved Swords, Katanas, Thrusting Swords\n\nNight Cavalry Video Guide\n\nPoison Moth Flight Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Poison Moth Flight Notes & Tips\n\nNOTE: Received a buff in Patch 1.07 that increased; Poison Status Buildup, duration, damage of poison, as well as damage inflicted against poisoned enemies.\nOnly the mist part of the attack builds up poison. Status on the weapon from weapon buffs, infusions or otherwise will not change this ash of war in any way.\nThe type of poison or how long the poison has been active does not affect the damage of this ash. And it does not 'pop' scarlet rot.\nCan be used in 2 ways: as a way to inflict deadly poison or deal burst damage working similarly to blood loss. \nSynergizes well with Kindred of Rot's Exultation Exultation, Shard of Alexander, Golden Vow (Spell), etc. Will only affect the weapon-hit portion of the ash, not the poison 'pop'.\nNotably useful with weapons, that have innitate Poison/Scarlet Rot QUESTION
Why is the Ash of War: Poison Moth Flight specifically dropped by Night's Cavalry in Caelid and only spawns at night?

ANSWER
The Ash of War: Poison Moth Flight is specifically dropped by Night's Cavalry in Caelid and only spawns at night due to the thematic connection between the poison moth motif and the nighttime setting, enhancing the immersive experience for players seeking this particular ash.
