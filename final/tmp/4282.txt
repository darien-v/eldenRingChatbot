The player can use the Cerulean Hidden Tear in the Flask of Wondrous Physick and the Comet sorcery to just burn down both the archers and all the Direwolves from horseback (any strong, longer range single target attack should work though), loot, ride back to the grace, rinse, repeat.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed. Describe how the Albinauric Bow can be acquired in Elden Ring.
