QUESTION
Why is the effect of Crimson Bubbletear in Elden Ring limited to occurring only once and expiring after a certain duration?

ANSWER
The effect of Crimson Bubbletear in Elden Ring is limited to occurring only once and expiring after a certain duration to prevent it from preventing death when faced with damage that would otherwise kill the player.
