Updated to Patch 1.10.\n\nSuperior incantation of Dragon Communion.\nChannels the power of Borealis, the Freezing Fog.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to spew icy breath from above.Charging extends the duration. This incantation can be cast whilejumping.\nThe ice dragons were once lords of the mountaintops long ago,until they were defeated by the Fire Giants and chased from thepeak.\n\nWhere to find Borealis's Mist\nWhere to find Borealis's Mist:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 2 Dragon Hearts. Players need to defeat Borealis the Freezing Fog in order to unlock this Incantation. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Borealis's Mist Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 60\nDeals magic damage\nInflicts Frostbite (110 per tick) on opponents.\nLike other named Breath incantations, this spell has a long wind-up time of 2 seconds.\nDeals 18% more damage than Dragonice, with a slightly longer range but a narrower spread.\nDuration can be extended to breathe again, and you can swivel your character while casting to spew in different directions.\nThe full cast requires at least 90 FP but will use up to 156 FP with no additional benefit before any FP-conservating buffs.\nIt takes on average 5 seconds including the initial wind-up to complete the full cast.\nCan be cast while jumping, and your character hovers slightly while using the spell.\nCannot be used on horseback.\nAs of Patch 1.09, Roar Medallion increases the damage of this incantation by approx. 5%. See Patch Notes for details.\nIn the Colosseum, if the opponent is spacing the dragon breath out of its range or putting pressure by interrupting your dragon breath, use Swarm of Flies. Most opponents will run away from the attack, causing them to lose stamina and give you space to do a dragon breath. \nTo prevent the opponent from constantly interrupting your dragon breath, use Endure. With all dragon breaths having a 2.5-second wind-up and Endure having a 3-second duration it will give you enough time to do a dragon breath in the air. However, you will not have enough time to swap into a Dragon Communion Seal on both hands if you want the full wind-up duration to be protected by Endure. \nOther notes and player tips go here.\n\n

QUESTION
Where can Borealis's Mist be found and how can it be unlocked?

ANSWER
Can be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 2 Dragon Hearts, Players need to defeat Borealis the Freezing Fog to unlock.
