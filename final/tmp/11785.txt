\n\n Haligtree Knight \n\nLocation\nElphael, Brace of the Haligtree\n\nDrops\n 5928 RunesHaligtree Knight SetKnight's GreatswordGreatbowHaligtree Crest GreatshieldGreat ArrowMiquella's Lily\n\nHaligtree Knight is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring Haligtree Knights are the elite defenders of Miquella's Haligtree. They are the heavier counterparts of the Haligtree Soldiers and are typically found in smaller numbers but are nonetheless more deadly.\n\nKnights who have vowed to serve Miquella's Haligtree. They are skilled in using both magical and holy skills, loyally protecting the rotted Haligtree to the very end.\n\nHaligtree Knight Locations in Elden Ring\nHaligtree Knights can be found in Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree at the following locations:\n\nTwo standing on either side of the Putrid Avatar  guarding the passage near the Elphael Inner Wall Site of Grace. Neither of these drop Haligtree Crest Greatshields, despite one of the knights using one. See the Haligtree Crest Greatshield page for more information.\nSeveral in the graveyard next to the church, below the Drainage Channel Site of Grace.\n\nElden Ring Haligtree Knight Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 5928\n\nHaligtree Knight Armor\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nHaligtree Knight Gauntlets\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nHaligtree Knight Greaves\n3.00%\nAll Variants.\n\nKnight's Greatsword\n4.00%\nGreatsword & Shield Variant.\n\nHaligtree Crest Greatshield\n4.00%\nGreatsword & Shield Variant.\n\nPartisan\n4.00%\nPartisan Variant.\n\nGreatbow\n4.00%\nGreatsword & Greatbow Variant.\n\n5x Great Arrow\n15.00%\nGreatsword & Greatbow Variant.\n\nMiquella's Lily\n8.00%\nAcross all Variants, but not every Knight.\n\nHaligtree Knight Notes & Tips\n\nHaligtree Knights have the typical knight moveset, but with a few additional tactics: \n\nA single Flask of Wondrous Physick that will heal them fully. They have high poise even when using the flask.\nThe Ash of War: Glintstone Pebble skill, which fires a Glintstone Pebble and follows up with a magic-imbued lunge.\nThe Ash of War: Sacred Order skill, which imbues their weapon with holy affinity after performing a salute. \n\nAdditionally, causes them to start regenerating health.\n\nThey can switch to Greatbows to shoot at the player from a far distance. \n\nThe Haligtree Knight Helm they wear is not dropped by these knights, and can be found as an item by climbing a ladder near the Elphael Inner Wall Site of Grace.\n\nHaligtree Knight Image
