QUESTION
Why would the Isolated Merchant Weeping Peninsula be willing to fight against customers if they attempt to attack him?

ANSWER
The Isolated Merchant Weeping Peninsula would fight against customers if they attempt to attack him because he considers them highwaymen and views their aggression as a descent into savagery.
