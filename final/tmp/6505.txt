\n\n Glintstone Pebble \n\nSpell Type\nGlintstone Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 7\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Fires a magic projectile from glintstone \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 10 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nGlintstone Pebble is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Glintstone Pebble spell is cast to produce offensive magic projectiles that attacks enemies from range. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nThe most basic glintstone sorcery of the Academy of Raya Lucaria.\nThe glintstone serves as a conduit, launching magical projectiles at foes.This sorcery can be cast repeatedly and while in motion.\nThis is a universal first step on the journey to true knowledge of sorcery.\n\nGlintstone Pebble Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Glintstone Pebble:\n\nStarting Spell for the Astrologer\nCan be purchased from Sorceress Sellen for  1,000 Runes at the Limgrave: Waypoint Ruins. [Map Link]\nCan be purchased from Thops at the Church of Irith for  1,000 Runes. [Map Link]\nCan be purchased from the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold for 1,000 Runes, after giving them Thops's Bell Bearing.\n\nElden Ring Glintstone Pebble Guide\n\nGlintstone Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 20\nInflicts (Sorcery Scaling x 1.52) magic damage before resistances.\nThis is the basic go-to sorcery for any player starting out on their journey to become a sorcery caster. It has a low Intelligence requirement (10 INT), a low casting cost (7 FP), tracks its target slightly, and has a comfortable range. All these traits make it a fine starting sorcery to obtain until stronger options can be acquired later on.\nSince Patch 1.06, the range of Glintstone Pebble has been increased, but the damage and stager power have been decreased.\nNOTE: This Glintstone Sorcery was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: The Attack Power was increased.\n\nPVP Tips and How to Dodge Glintstone Pebble\n\nCan be dodged on its own by moving directly perpendicular to the attacker.
