Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Blackflame Monk Set in Elden Ring\nAll four pieces of the Blackflame Monk Set drop off of Blackflame Monk enemies. There are two spots to easily farm the set:\n\nHead down the stairs from the Divine Tower of Caelid: Basement site of grace. There is one Blackflame Monk that can be handled alone and reset easily.\nHead northwest from the Temple of Eiglay site of grace, go outside, and walk down the temple stairs.
