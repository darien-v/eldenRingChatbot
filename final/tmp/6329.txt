QUESTION
Who can craft the Ancient Dragonbolt Pot and what materials are needed for crafting it?

ANSWER
The Ancient Dragonbolt Pot can be crafted by players and requires the Ancient Dragon Apostle's Cookbook, 2 Mushrooms, 1 Fulgurbloom, 1 Gravel Stone, and 1 Empty Ritual Pot as crafting materials.
