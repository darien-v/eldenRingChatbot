Most Consumables grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects or applying temporary buffs and debuffs, while others can be thrown at enemies to deal damage. When used, the foot boosts runes gained by 30% for 3 minutes. The effect is not removed when resting at a site of grace. However, the effect is removed after fast-traveling to a site of grace.\n\nFour-toed foot of a fowl, pickled in a golden medicinal solution. QUESTION
Where can you find Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
