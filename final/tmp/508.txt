\n\n Forsaken Ruins \n\nThe Forsaken Ruins consists of a group of broken buildings and abandoned campsites. It is located in Caelid and is guarded by a Giant Crow.\n\nForsaken Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Forsaken Ruins is found in Caelid below the cliff South East of the Rotview Balcony site of grace.\n\nForsaken Ruins Map\nThe Forsaken Ruins are found a short distance east of the Shack of the Rotting, below the cliffs. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Forsaken Ruins\n\nNo NPCs and Merchants in this location.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Forsaken Ruins\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nFaded Erdleaf Flower\nTrina's Lily\nGolden Rune [5]\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSmithing Stone [4]\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nSword of St. Trina\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nNo Uniques, Ashes or Keys at this location\n\nElden Ring Forsaken Ruins Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nGiant Crow\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nNo Bosses in this location.\n\nForsaken Ruins Walkthrough\nAround the destroyed structures, you can find items such as Faded Erdleaf Flower, Trina's Lily, Golden Rune [5], and Smithing Stone [4]. Be careful here since there are two Giant Crow enemies guarding this location.\nOnce you've cleared the area, look on the western portion of the ruins to find an Imp Statue here that you can use a Stonesword Key to dispel the fog of the cellar. Beyond the white fog is a treasure chest containing x1 Sword of St. Trina.\n\nElden Ring Forsaken Ruins Gallery and Notes\nOverlooking the Forsaken Ruins from the Rotview Balcony site of grace.\n\nAll
