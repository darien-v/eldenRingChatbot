\n\n Cursemark of Death \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Cursemark carved into Lunar Princess Ranni's discarded flesh \n\nCursemark of Death is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPC which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nCursemark carved into the discarded flesh of Ranni the Witch. Also known as the half-wheel wound of the centipede.\nThis cursemark was carved at the moment of Death of the first demigod, and should have taken the shape of a circle.\nHowever, two demigods perished at the same time, breaking the cursemark into two half-wheels.\nRanni was the first of the demigods whose flesh perished, while the Prince of Death perished in soul alone.\n\nWhere to find Cursemark of Death in Elden Ring\n\nFound on a body lying at the top of the Divine Tower of Liurnia.
