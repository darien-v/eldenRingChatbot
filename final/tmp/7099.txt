CONTEXT
\n\n Giant Skeleton (Spirit) \n\nLocation\nMohgwyn PalaceMountaintops of the Giants\n\nDrops\nN/A\n\nGiant Skeleton (Spirit) is an Enemy in Elden Ring. They are summoned by Tibia Mariners and red Second-Generation Albinaurics, and encountered in a few locations without a summoner. Their appearance is the upper half of a colossal skeleton, and can shoot a powerful blast of what appears to be ghostflame out of its mouth at the player.\n\nElden Ring Giant Skeleton (Spirit) Locations\nWhere to Find Giant Skeleton (Spirit):\n\nSummoned by the Tibia Mariner boss found at Wyndham Ruins.\nSummoned by the red Second-Generation Albinaurics in the forested area of the Mohgwyn Palace.\nThey spawn without any summoner in the graveyards of the Mountaintops of the Giants.\n\nElden Ring Giant Skeleton (Spirit) Drops\n\nDrops no runes nor items.\n\nGiant Skeleton (Spirit) Notes & Tips\n\nCan be summoned by the Tibia Mariner in the Wyndham Ruins, and the large topless red-skinned Second-Generation Albinaurics using their Curved Great Club as a giant horn. Once these latter summoners are attacked, the Giant Skeleton (Spirit) they summoned disappears.\nThe Giant Skeletons in the graveyards of the Mountaintops of the Giants don't have a summoner, and will continue to spawn after the nearby Tibia Mariner is defeated.\nLike Inaba (Spirit) and Lesser Dragonkin Soldier (Spirit), they drop no Runes upon death.\nIt is based off of the mythological yōkai, Gashadokuro, and is referred to as such in the game files.
