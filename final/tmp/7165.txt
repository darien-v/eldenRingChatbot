\n\n Duelist \n\nLocation\nLeyndell, Royal Capital\n\nDrops\nDuelist SetDuelist GreataxeBattle Hammer\n\nDuelist is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. The Duelists either wield a giant axe with an extended chain, or two Battle Hammers attached to chains, which they can swing around with ease.\nIn the Consecrated Snowfield, there are two Rotten Duelist variants.\n\nFormer gladitorial fighters who were exiled from the colloseum. They wield chain axes and hammers, and attack with a frenzied fervor.\n\nElden Ring Duelist Locations\nWhere to Find Duelist:\n\nLeyndell, Royal Capital, near the large colosseum.\n\nElden Ring Duelist Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nDuelist Helm\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nGravekeeper Cloak\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nDuelist Greaves\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nDuelist Greataxe\n4.00%\nOnly drops from the Duelist Greataxe Variant\n\nBattle Hammer\n4.00%\nOnly drops from the Battle Hammer Variant.\n\nDuelist Notes & Tips\n\nThere are several boss versions, such as the Grave Warden Duelist, the Frenzied Duelist and the Putrid Grave Warden Duelist, as well as one darkness-wrapped one that must be brought into a circle of light in the Sainted Hero's Grave (does not respawn after death).\nDamage: Standard (Axe), Strike (flail, hammer) \nParriable: Yes\nIs suspectible to a critical hit after stance break or parry.
