The Morgott's Cursed Sword scales primarily with Arcane and Dexterity, with reduced scaling in Strength, and is a good Weapon for melee combat. Its unique skill, Cursed-Blood Slice, allows players to charge forward and deliver a downward slice that causes an additional Blood Loss build up effect.\n\nWarped blade of shifting hue used by Morgott, the Omen King. \nThe accused blood that Morgott recanted and sealed away reformed into this blade. \n\nHow to Acquire Morgott's Cursed Sword in Elden Ring\nThe Morgott's Cursed Sword weapon can be acquired through these methods:\n\nCan be purchased from Finger Reader Enia found at the Roundtable Hold with the Remembrance of the Omen King.\n\nElden Ring Morgott's Cursed Sword Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Cursed-Blood Slice\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War\nCannot be enchanted with Magic nor buffed by Consumables\nMorgott's Cursed Sword can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones\nCauses Blood loss Buildup:  (60)\nSell Value:   1000\nUnlike  Bloodflame Blade, this weapon does not apply the gradual Blood Loss build up debuff.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
