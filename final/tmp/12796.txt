[Map Link]\n\nBuilds that use Blue Dancer Charm\n\nBlack Arrow Written Guide\n\nNoble Swordsman Build Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Blue Dancer Charm Notes & Tips\n\nSell Value:  500\nThe talisman only affects physical scaling damage. The physical base damage and other non-physical damage types will remain the same. Purely non-physical weapons like Cipher Pata and Coded Sword will therefore gain no benefit from this talisman.\nThe damage bonus increases in inverse proportion to the weight of your equipment (the lower your total equipment load, the greater the damage bonus). \nThe damage bonus has soft caps at 8(1.135x), 16(1.09x) and 20(1.0375x) equip weight. Once equip weight is 30 or higher the talisman has no effect.\nThe maximum physical attack power multiplier is 1.15x, however this is unobtainable due to the weight of the talisman, the maximum obtainable multiplier is 1.1483125x\nLighter weapons receive higher percent damage bonus than heavier weapons due to them being lower weight. \nThe damage scaling shown on your equipment screen while wearing the talisman for unequipped weapons is not accurate until you actually equip the weapon. \nIncreases Bow Damage.  Affects Sorceries and Incantations that deal physical damage.\nDue to this talisman not being based on equip load %, only flat amount, the boost from this talisman is not increased by the Winged Crystal Tear.\nBlue Dancer Charm formula\n\nif EquipWgt < 8, then EquipWgt * QUESTION
Why does the Blue Dancer Charm only affect physical scaling damage and not other non-physical damage types?

ANSWER
The Blue Dancer Charm only affects physical scaling damage and not other non-physical damage types because it specifically raises Physical Damage with lower Equip Load, as indicated in its formula and in-game description.
