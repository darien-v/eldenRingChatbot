CONTEXT
\n\n Golden Retaliation  \n\n        Ready the shield to unleash its protective incantation. \n       This will dispel sorceries and incantations, retaliating with golden power. \n\nGolden Retaliation is a Skill in Elden Ring. Golden Retaliation is a special skill only available for an individual Shield, and cannot be applied to other Shields via Ashes of War.\n\nHow to get Golden Retaliation in Elden Ring\n\nThis Skill can only be acquired by equipping Erdtree Greatshield.\n\nElden Ring Golden Retaliation Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 4\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nIntercepts a single magical projectile and fires a homing projectile in return. The projectile deals holy damage and scales with the upgrade level of the shield and marginally with the player's Faith stat, but not any other stats.\n\nTalismans like Shard of Alexander and Sacred Scorpion Charm increase the damage of the projectile.\nThe projectile fired by Golden Retaliation cannot be intercepted by another Golden Retaliation.\nThe player's own spells/incantations can be reflected by this, so Discus of Light and Triple Rings of Light, which automatically return to the caster after reaching their max travel distance can set this skill up. Since all of them deal holy damage, you get great value out of the Sacred Scorpion Charm and Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear with this set up.\nThe projectile deals reduced damage the farther it travels due to severe damage dropoff, but the Arrow's Reach Talisman can alleviate this. (In this case, the talisman only reduces the damage dropoff penalty, leaving the actual range of the projectile unchanged.) \n\nIf multiple projectiles are approaching in quick succession, this skill will only intercept one of the projectiles. The rest will still connect.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric Roar  ♦  Barrage  ♦  Barricade QUESTION
What is the effect of the Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear when combined with the Golden Retaliation skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear increases the damage output of the homing projectile fired by Golden Retaliation in Elden Ring.
