\n\n Pidia's Bell Bearing \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nPidia's Bell Bearing is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Pidia's Bell Bearing can be offered to the Twin Maiden Husks to gain access to new shop items. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nServant Pidia's bell bearing, found upon his perished flesh.\nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to new items.\n\nWhere to find Pidia's Bell Bearing in Elden Ring\n\nDropped by Pidia, Carian Servant upon death in Caria Manor.  Drop into the top layer of the church by jumping down from the three sisters external area, descending along the manors' church tower.
