QUESTION
What strategies can be employed to effectively defeat the Demi-Human Queen in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To effectively defeat the Demi-Human Queen in Elden Ring, focus on dodging her Crystal Burst spell, use ranged attacks to chip away her health, and be prepared for her larger size and staff attacks.
