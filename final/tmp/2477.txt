QUESTION
Why is the Radahn Soldier Set considered the standard soldier attire for soldiers in General Radahn's army in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Radahn Soldier Set is considered the standard soldier attire for soldiers in General Radahn's army in Elden Ring due to its high Resistance, Immunity, Robustness, Focus, Vitality, and Poise stats.
