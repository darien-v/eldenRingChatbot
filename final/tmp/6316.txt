\n\n Blasphemous Claw \n\nEffect \n\n        Deflects the power of the Black Blade \n\nScaling \n\n        - \n\nFP Cost\n-\n\nBlasphemous Claw is a Tool in Elden Ring. Tools are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.\n\nA slab of rock engraved with traces of the Rune of Death.  Can deflect the power of the Black Blade.  On the night of the dire plot, Ranni rewarded Praetor Rykard with these traces. Should the coming trespass one day transpire, they would serve as a last-resort foil, allowing Rykard to challenge Maliketh the Black Blade, the black beast of Destined Death.\n\nWhere to find Blasphemous Claw in Elden Ring\nThe Blasphemous Claw is dropped by Recusant Bernahl upon defeat at Crumbling Farum Azula. [Map Link]  Video Location\n\nBlasphemous Claw use in Elden Ring\n\nThe Blasphemous Claw can be used to deflect the power of the Black Blade, acting similarly to a Parry.\nCan parry specific melee attacks from Maliketh. His sword will briefly glow yellow before attacks that can be parried. \n\nHis sword also glows white before his projectile attacks, but these do not seem to be parriable.\n\nIf timed correctly against Maliketh's glowing attacks, stuns him temporarily, giving a massive window to attack.\nDoes not seem like the Blasphemous Claw can be used in PVP against the Black Blade incantation or Maliketh's Black Blade weapon. Has been tested a bit and did not seem to work against them, but more testing would be helpful.\n\nElden Ring Blasphemous Claw Notes & Tips\n\nThe Blasphemous Claw can be used indefinitely and costs no FP to use.\nYou can hold up to 1 Blasphemous Claw.\nYou can store up to 600 Blasphemous Claw.\nThis item cannot be sold.\n\nElden Ring Consumables\n\nAcademy Magic Pot  ♦  Acid QUESTION
Why is the Blasphemous Claw specifically effective against the Black Blade wielded by Maliketh?

ANSWER
The Blasphemous Claw is specifically effective against the Black Blade wielded by Maliketh due to its ability to deflect the power of the Black Blade, acting similarly to a Parry and stunning Maliketh temporarily when timed correctly against his glowing attacks.
