Dogs are hostile and agile creatures, and will become hostile even if not attacked by players. Dogs are often found roaming the fields of Limgrave, and similar to Wolves, they will chase their prey to great lengths. They are dangerous when alone, but even deadlier in packs.\n\nDog Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Dog:\n\nFive Dogs can be found in the Dragon-Burnt Ruins.\nFour Dogs (including two large ones) can be found in the encampment northeast of the Warmaster's Shack in Stormhill.\nA Dog can be found at the foot of The Four Belfries. Another Dog, larger, can be found uphill a little ways.\nTwo Dogs can be found on the beach bordering Liurnia Lake, northwest of the Gate Town Bridge Site of Grace and east of the Academy Gate Town Site of Grace.\nOne Dog can be found next to a Carriage in the Consecrated Snowfield. It is located almost halfway between the Consecrated Snowfield Site of Grace and the Consecrated Snowfield Catacombs.\nTwo Dogs can be found just south of the Inner Connsecrated Snowfield Site of QUESTION
Why are dogs in Elden Ring hostile and become aggressive even if not attacked by players?

ANSWER
Dogs in Elden Ring are hostile and aggressive because they have a moveset similar to previous Souls entries, similar to Strays, occasionally larger with more health, and chase prey in packs, making them dangerous.
