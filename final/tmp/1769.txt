Battlemage Set is a cloth spellcaster armor set worn by Battlemage enemies that uses the Haima Glintstone Crown. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Battlemage Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe entire Battlemage Set drops from a particular Battlemage enemy, located east of West Windmill Pasture or west of East Windmill Pasture, on a hill near the northern end of the collapsed bridge.
