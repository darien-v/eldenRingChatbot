Three Sisters is a sub-region of Liurnia of the Lakes. It is found west of Caria Manor and is accessible only after completing the manor and defeating its boss Royal Knight Loretta.\n\nA secluded land located west of Caria Manor, barred from entry by the estate's walls and its eternal guardian.
