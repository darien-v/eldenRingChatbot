\n\n Depraved Perfumer \n\nLocation\nVillage of the AlbinauricsShaded Castle\n\nDrops\n 385 - 880 RunesDepraved Perfumer SetPerfumer's ShieldMiranda PowderBudding Cave MossAltus Bloom\n\nDepraved Perfumer is a humanoid enemy in Elden Ring. Depraved Perfumers are the corrupted cohorts of the Perfumers of Leyndell. They will attack the player with a mixture of physical and perfume attacks.\n\n These heresy-inclined perfumers imbibe their own spices to alter body and mind. Their slow descent into self-destruction is what earned them their name.\n\nElden Ring Depraved Perfumer Locations\nWhere to Find Depraved Perfumers:\n\nMany are found in The Shaded Castle.\nOne is always found in the Village of the Albinaurics.\n\nElden Ring Depraved Perfumer Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 385 - 880\n\nDepraved Perfumer Headscarf\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nDepraved Perfumer Robe\n 3.00%\nN/A\n\nDepraved Perfumer Gloves\n 3.00%\nN/A\n\nDepraved Perfumer Trousers\n 3.00%\nN/A\n\nPerfumer's Shield\n2.00%\nOnly dropped by Depraved Perfumers that wield it.\n\nBudding Cave Moss (x5)\n15.00%\nN/A\n\nAltus Bloom (x5)\n8.00% - 15.00%\nN/A\n\nMiranda Powder (x3)\n8.00% - 15.00%\nN/A\n\nDepraved Perfumer Notes & Tips\n\nDepraved Perfumers in The Shaded Castle have approximately 1485HP and award  880 Runes\nThey can heal up to three times.\nThey do not drop the Erdsteel Dagger they carry.\nA good place to farm: head to the Shaded Castle Ramparts site of grace, go down the stairs behind you and head left, continue on the road and kill the Depraved Perfumer ahead.
