\n\n Waypoint Ruins \n\nWaypoint Ruins consists of a group of broken buildings and abandoned campsites, now overrun with Miranda sprouts and their crazed caretaker.\n\nWaypoint Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Waypoint Ruins is found in Western Limgrave. It can be reached by either going southeast from Agheel Lake North site of grace or going north from Agheel Lake South site of grace.\n\nWaypoint Ruins Map for Elden Ring\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Waypoint Ruins\n\nSorceress Sellen - Sorceries shop\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Waypoint Ruins\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nRowa Fruit\nMushroom\nRuin Fragment\nx1 Immunizing Cured Meat\nx2 Glowstone\nx4 Trina's Lily\nx1 Golden Rune [1]\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location. \n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nNo Unique Items, Ashes and Keys\n\nWaypoint Ruins Creatures, Enemies and Bosses in Elden Ring\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nMiranda Sprout\nGiant Miranda Sprout\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nMad Pumpkin Head\n\nWalkthrough for Waypoint Ruins in Elden Ring\nThis small area is towards the east side of the map. Players will want to bring poison resistance as the Miranda Sprouts in the area can inflict the Poison Status Effect. Defeat the Giant Miranda Sprout to gain Immunizing Cured Meat. Consuming this item will temporarily boost Immunity. There will be other Miranda Sprouts in the area to farm Poisonbloom and Miranda Power. Other lootable materials in the area include Ruin Fragment found atop the large topped stone structure, and Trina's Lily x4, looted off a draped body on one of the taller ruined walls of this location. \nAfter clearing the area, use Torrent to double-jump into the central and blocked area that features a staircase leading to the cellar. Within the cellar, you will find a Boss: Mad Pumpkin Head.\nAfter defeating the boss, Waypoint Ruins Cellar site of grace becomes accessible and the player can interact with Sorceress Sellen, starting her questline and giving you access to her shop. Completing her questline will reward you with a Sellian Sealbreaker, Sellen's Primal Glintstone, Eccentric's Hood, Eccentric's Armor, Eccentric's Manchettes, Eccentric's Breeches, Shard Spiral, Glintstone Kris, Witch's Glintstone Crown. \n\nElden Ring Waypoint Ruins Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy Crystal Cave  ♦  Academy
