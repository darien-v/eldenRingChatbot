Map Link\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Letter from Volcano Manor Tutorial Guide\nThe Letter from Volcano Manor is a unique info item in that it is a quest item. This letter outlines your assassination target after pledging your services to the Volcano Manor. You'll get a total of two Letters from Volcano Manor, each assigning you a different target.\n\nThe first letter assigns you to assassinate Old Knight Istvan and marks a red marker on your map to mark his location.\nThe second letter assigns you to assassinate Rileigh the Idle after completing the first assignment and marks a red marker on your map to mark his location.\nThe final letter you receive is the Red Letter.\n\nElden Ring Letter from Volcano Manor Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 2 Letter from Volcano Manor.\nYou cannot store Letter from Volcano Manor.\nSell
