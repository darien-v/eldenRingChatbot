CONTEXT
\nTorches are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Torches deal little damage, but can be used to ward off unwanted beasts while dealing some Fire Damage on them.\nTorches can't be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, except to apply the "No Skill" skill.\nTorches do not receive a unique moveset as a result of dual-wielding (AKA "power stancing").\nElden Ring Torch Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Torches in Elden Ring. QUESTION
How do the base values and maximum standard values of different torches in Elden Ring compare in terms of physical damage, fire damage, and other attributes?

ANSWER
Base values and maximum standard values of different torches in Elden Ring vary widely in physical and fire damage, with each torch having unique attributes and scaling, such as Beast-Repellent Torch excelling in fire damage, Steel-Wire Torch in physical damage, Sentry's Torch in stamina, and Torchpole having the highest physical damage but lower fire damage.
