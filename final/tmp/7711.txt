\n\n Dragonmaw \n\nSpell Type\nDragon Communion Incantations\n\nFP Cost 34\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Channels dragon to bite foes before caster \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 24 \n\nArcane 16 \n\nDragonmaw is an Incantation in Elden Ring. The Dragonmaw spell channels a dragon head to bite foes in front of the caster. Updated to Patch 1.10.\n\nOne of the incantations of Dragon Communion.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to bite enemies before them.\nIncantations of those who have hunted dragons and feasted upon their hearts. Theirs is a pure and overwhelming power.\n\nWhere to find Dragonmaw\nWhere to find Dragonmaw:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Church of Dragon Communion in Limgrave for 1 Dragon Heart.\n\nElden Ring Dragonmaw Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 50\nDeals physical damage\nYour character will slightly float in the air and lunge forward during the full animation\nYou can swivel your character while casting to strike in a different direction.\nThe full cast takes on average 4 seconds, with the bite being registered around the 2.5-second mark. \nThis spell grants hyper-armor, but only during the mid-air animation.\nNote: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost was reduced significantly, from 46 to 34. Additionally, stamina damage against guarding enemies has been increased and the hitbox around the dragon's neck enlarged to make it easier to hit an enemy at close range.\nAs of Patch 1.10, Dragonmaw has a hyper armor damage resistance of 7.5% during the full animation. \nAs of Patch 1.10, Dragonmaw does 45 stance damage. \n\nDragon Warrior Written Guide\n\nDragon Dancer Build\n\nElden Ring Dragonmaw Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to Patch 1.10. See Patch Notes for details.\nIf you quickly follow up with any dragon breath while standing on the ground you gain the float and lunge property of Dragonmaw, so as long as the pink fire effects around your character do not expire. \nYou can use the float and lunge property with Theodorix's Magma to close the gap on your enemy. Theodorix's Magma will do double the damage at point-blank range. \nDeals more damage and has more hyper-armor than Dragonclaw, but the latter has a follow-up attack and can be chained into itself when power-stancing seals.\nA well-timed Dragonmaw can dodge enemies with ground attacks such as Godfrey and Radagon. \nOther notes and player tips go here. \n\nElden Ring Incantations\n\nAgheel's
