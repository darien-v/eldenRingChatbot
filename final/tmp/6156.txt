CONTEXT
\n\n Mighty Shot \n\n        Archery skill performed from an oblique stance. Ready the bow, then pull the bowstring to its limit to enhance the power of the shot, penetrating the enemy's guard. \n\nMighty Shot is a Skill in Elden Ring. Mighty Shot is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to Light bows and Longbows.\n\nHow to get Mighty Shot\n\nDefault skill of Composite Bow.\nDefault skill of all Bows except Black Bow.\nAsh of War: Mighty Shot - Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab on the path from Limgrave towards Weeping Peninsula. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Mighty Shot Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Mighty Shot.\nFP Cost: 0 on activation (stance), 6 per shot.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nOn activation, the user assumes a stance, preparing the bow. When inputting light or heavy attacks, the user pulls the bowstring all the way to fire the respective arrow from their slot, consuming FP (and stamina while in combat). \n\nWhen the stance is being held, stamina regen is reduced by approximately 80%.\n\nThe arrows fired from this Weapon Skill have the following properties: \n\nTravel longer distances before falling off, effectively increasing their range.\nHave increased damage on impact.\nCan partially ignore blocking from shields (and weapons).\nDeal increased status buildup, if applicable.\n\nThe main appeal of this Weapon Skill is excelling at saving up arrows (specially status-inflicting ones) in exchange for FP, thanks to the increased range and damage of arrows. \n\nWorks specially well with the Serpent Bow (which has the skill innately and can't be changed) for poison, bleed and sleep arrows, even at low arcane levels.\n\nMighty Shot Demonstration\n\nBuilds with Mighty Shot\n\nMagic Archer Written Guide\n\nGolden Eye QUESTION
What are the properties and effects of the Mighty Shot skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The properties and effects of the Mighty Shot skill in Elden Ring include longer arrow distances, increased damage, partial shield ignoring, increased status buildup, and excelling at saving arrows in exchange for FP.
