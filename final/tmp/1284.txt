Messmer the Impaler is a boss added in the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC and, can only be challenged by players who possess the DLC. He is accompanied by a snake and uses fire based Spells to attack the player, as well as a large spear. Not much information is available besides his clear resemblance to other Red Haired Demigod and a connection to Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy through the red snakes he carries.\n\nMother, wouldst thou truly Lordship sanction, in one so bereft of light?\n\nElden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree DLC Gameplay\n\nMESSMER THE IMPALER INFORMATION ELDEN RING\nIn one of the main pieces of artwork of Messmer the Impaler he is shown to be sitting on the throne of Morgott the Omen King, in the words of game director Hidetaka Miyazaki this symbolizes he is at least on equal terms to other demigods and children of Queen Marika. Part of the DLC will be exploring why the Lands of Shadow exists, as well as their relationship with this mysterious antagonist.\n\nAnother thing of note is that he carries the same eyes as dragons and runebears, this could mean he is one of the many characters that communes with dragons in the game. \n\nElden Ring Messmer the Impaler Boss\nMessmer the Impaler is a BOSS found in Shadow of the Erdtree\n\nIs this an optional boss\nWhat kind of Foe is it\nClosest Site of Grace:\nIs multiplayer allowed for this boss?\nCan you summon Spirit ashes for this boss?\n\nElden Ring Messmer the Impaler Location\nMessmer the Impaler can be found in the Land of Shadow in the Shadow of the Erdtree\n\nMessmer the Impaler Combat Information\n\nHealth: NG (??), NG+ (??), NG++ (??), NG+3 (??), NG+4 (??), NG+5 (??), NG+6 (??), NG+7 (??)\nAbsorptions: ??\nDefenses do not change between phases - a firebomb will do the same damage in phase 1 as in phase 2.\nDeals ??
