QUESTION
What are the defensive properties and set bonuses of the White Reed Greaves in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The White Reed Greaves in Elden Ring have defensive properties of Dmg Negation Phy 5.4, VS Strike 5.8, VS Slash 6.5, VS Pierce 5, Magic 6.2, Fire 6.2, Ligt 6.8, Holy 5.8, along with set bonuses of Immunity 41, Robustness 26, Focus 34, Vitality 31, and Poise 10.
