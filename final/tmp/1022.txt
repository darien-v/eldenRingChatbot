For a faster defeat, players can choose to use Spirit Summons, however this isn't necessary. She can be staggered very easily and will pause to cover her face, giving you a longer opportunity to deal more hits and keep her from her attack sequences. Along with this, her poise can be broken. Continue with these attacks while dodge or blocking her lunges until she is defeated.  \nMagic and Ranged Users\nUse any heavy physical damage Spell such as Rock Sling to keep her staggered and break her poise. To have an even smoother,  quick encounter, use Spirit summons to double attacks and have almost the the entire fight open to your magic or ranged attack as she focuses on the summon. Continue dealing attacks and moving back to quickly defeat this boss.\n\nDemi-human Queen Gilika Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\n Sceptre Plunge\nShe uses her sceptre and plunges it into the ground. \nIt will be easy to predict around where this attack will go based on the direction she is facing so dodge roll away from the area to avoid it or block it.\n\nRoar\nLets out a roar with a radial effect\n This action has a short range.
