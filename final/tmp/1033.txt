\n\n Church of Inhibition \n\nThe remnants of an old church in the north of Liurnia, just before the Altus Plateau.\n\nChurch of Inhibition is a Location in Elden Ring. The Church of Inhibition is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. Head north from the Frenzied Flame Village and at the top of the hill is a broken stone building, remnants of the Church of Inhibition.\n\nChurch of Inhibition Map\nThe Church of Inhibition is located on the northernmost section of Liurnia's eastern landmass. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Church of Inhibition\n\nA dead Finger Maiden is sitting near the grace. Her blood can be used to sully the lord's token given to you by White Mask Varre as part of his questline.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Church of Inhibition\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nEye of Yelough\nGold-Tinged Excrement\nRowa Fruit\nErdleaf Flower\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSacred Tear\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nFinger Maiden Fillet\nFinger Maiden Robe\nFinger Maiden Shoes\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this location. \n\nElden Ring Church of Inhibition Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nFestering Fingerprint Vyke (Invader)\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nnone\n\nChurch of Inhibition Walkthrough\nChurch found at the top of the hill, the body of a finger maiden can be found on a chair by the statue where you can obtain their armor (Finger Maiden Set) During your first arrival be sure to reach the Site of Grace first, as soon after you will be attacked by an invader. \n\nElden Ring Church of Inhibition Gallery and Notes\n\nCaution should be taken upon approaching the church's perimeter, Festering Fingerprint Vyke will invade. They're a relatively tough enemy and will inflict madness build-up with ranged, AOE and physical spear attacks. The fight is made worse should the player stray too close to the Frenzy-Flaming Tower east of the church.
