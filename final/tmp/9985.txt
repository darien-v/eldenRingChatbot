QUESTION
Why is the Light Crossbow in Elden Ring a good weapon for those who prefer ranged combat?

ANSWER
The Light Crossbow in Elden Ring is a good weapon for those who prefer ranged combat due to dealing good damage, simplicity of use, and not scaling with any primary attribute but requiring both Strength and Dexterity.
