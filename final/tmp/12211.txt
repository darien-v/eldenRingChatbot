\n\n Rotten Breath \n\nSpell Type\nDragon Communion Incantations\n\nFP Cost 36 (4)\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Channels dragon to spew scarlet rot breath \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 15 \n\nArcane 12 \n\nRotten Breath is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Rotten Breath spell allows the caster to conjure a cloud of rot and decay. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the incantations of Dragon Communion.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to spew scarlet rot breath.Charging extends duration.\nThose extraordinary individuals who perpetuate DragonCommunion are called the dragon-hearted.\n\nWhere to find Rotten Breath\nWhere to find Rotten Breath:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 1 Dragon Heart. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Rotten Breath Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 48\nDeals standard physical damage\nInflicts Scarlet Rot (65 buildup per tick, upto 10 ticks on fully charged cast) on opponents.\nScarlet Rot inflicted by this spell deals (0.33% Max HP) + 13 per second for 90 seconds, for a total of 29.7% Max HP + 1170 damage over its full duration. \n\nScarlet Rot on weapons inflicts (0.18% Max HP) + 15 per second for 90 seconds, for a total of 16.2% Max HP + 1350 damage over its full duration.\n\nLike other generic Breath incantations, this spell has long wind-up time of 2 seconds.\nCovers wide area and can occasionally ricochet from walls and non-destructible objects.\nDuration can be extended to breathe again, and you can swivel your character while casting to spew in different directions.\nFull cast requires at least 60 FP but will use up to 104 FP with no additional benefit from any FP-conserving buffs.\nIt takes on average 5 seconds including initial wind-up to complete the full cast.\nUnlike the named Breath spells, cannot be cast while jumping.\nCan be used while on horseback.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.07, the Scarlet Rot buildup this spell causes has been reduced from 110 per tick to 65 per tick.\n\nBuilds with Rotten Breath\n\nBlood Dragon Written Guide\n\nBlood Dancer Written Guide\n\nDragon Kinght Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Rotten Breath Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
