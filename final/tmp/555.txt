\n\n Divine Tower of Liurnia \n\nA long bridge leads to the nearly empty Tower of Liurnia where a corpse lies at the peak.\n\nDivine Tower of Liurnia is a Location in Elden Ring. It is one of the six Divine Towers. The Divine Tower of Liurnia is found in Liurnia of the Lakes and is reached by taking the lift down at the secret area of Carian Study Hall.\n\nDivine Tower of Liurnia Map\nThe Divine Tower of Liurnia can only be accessed by inverting the Carian Study Hall using the Carian Inverted Statue. This statue is acquired by progressing Ranni's questline up to the point where you hand her the Fingerslayer Blade.\nOnce inverted, players need to reach the lift in the center of the Study Hall by careful platforming and then go through the large doors after descending the lift. This leads to the long bridge to the tower. Beware of the Godskin Noble enemy guarding the center of the bridge. QUESTION
When can the Divine Tower of Liurnia in Elden Ring be accessed and what items can be obtained from it?

ANSWER
The Divine Tower of Liurnia in Elden Ring can be accessed by inverting the Carian Study Hall after progressing Ranni's questline. Items that can be obtained from the tower include the Stargazer Heirloom talisman and the Cursemark of Death key item.
