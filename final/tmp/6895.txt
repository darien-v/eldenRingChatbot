\n\n Smithing Stone [8] \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce armaments up to +24 \n\nSmithing Stone [8] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Smithing Stone [8] is a rare composition of the common smithing stone that is mainly used to forge and strengthen greater equipment level. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nStone used to smith a variety of armaments. Found in a polar region, it is semi-vitrified.\nStrengthens armaments up to +24.\nThought to have been used to hone the weapons of the champions of the War against the Giants at the birth of the Erdtree.\n\nSmithing Stone [8] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Smithing Stone [8]:\n\nCaelid:\n\nUsing the Spirit Spring located to the north of the Church of the Plague will allow players to leap onto a rock formation with several corpses lying around and propped up on chairs.
