CONTEXT
\nCookbooks in Elden Ring are Key Items with records of crafting techniques which automatically expand the player's crafting repertoire, often with recipes for Ammunition and Consumables. This page covers their names, items they unlock for crafting, and Locations.\nCrafting requires a Crafting Kit (purchased from Merchant Kalé at Church of Elleh) and can only be done outside of combat.\nCookbooks are kept in New Game+.
