\n\n Oath of Vengeance \n\n        Swear an oath upon the greatsword to avenge the clan, temporarily raising all attributes for a certain duration. While the oath's effects are active, poise will also be increased.  \n\nOath of Vengeance is a Skill in Elden Ring. Oath of Vengeance is a unique skill directly equipped with the Grafted Blade Greatsword, which can be obtained from Castle Morne. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Oath of Vengeance\n\nDefault skill on the Grafted Blade Greatsword.\n\nElden Ring Oath of Vengeance Guide\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 20\nRaises all Attributes by 5 for 60 seconds and Poise for 5 seconds, counted from the beginning of the buff animation.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable.\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nThis is a Unique buff. See Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking buffs.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nEffect has been extended from 30 to 60 seconds.\nAn effect that prevents staggers caused by blood loss and frostbite status effects has been added.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Oath of Vengeance Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
