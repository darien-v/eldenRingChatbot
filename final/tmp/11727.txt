\n\n Lazuli Sorcerer \n\nLocation\nCaria ManorRaya Lucaria AcademyAcademy Crystal CaveSellia, Town of Sorcery\n\nDrops\n 128 - 201 Runes Lazuli Sorcerer SetLazuli Glintstone Sword\n\nLazuli Sorcerer is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Lazuli Sorcerers are scholars of the Lazuli Conspectus at the Raya Lucaria Academy, who study Carian Sorceries. These pupils wield a small shield and a sword which deals magic damage.\n\n Scholars of the Lazuli Conspectus study Carian sorceries - a heterodox pursuit that views the moon as equal to the stars.\n\nElden Ring Lazuli Sorcerer Locations\nWhere to Find Lazuli Sorcerer:\n\nLazuli Sorcerers are found in most areas of Raya Lucaria Academy. They often serve as melee support to their glintstone spell slinging cohorts.\nThey are also in Caria Manor.\nThey are also found in Sellia, Town of Sorcery.\n\nElden Ring Lazuli Sorcerer QUESTION
Why do Lazuli Sorcerers wield an Ice Crest Shield and wear specific sorcerer gear, but do not drop them as loot?

ANSWER
Lazuli Sorcerers do not drop their Ice Crest Shield and specific sorcerer gear as loot. They only wield them but do not provide them as drops.
