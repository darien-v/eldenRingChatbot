CONTEXT
\nCurved Swords are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Curved Swords specialize in slashing attacks, and are capable of inflicting damage on hostile creatures without sacrificing mobility. They can also attack more quickly than many other melee weapons in Elden Ring, making them excellent against fast-moving opponents. Curved Swords, like Thrusting Swords, have a back-step feint. While charging up a heavy attack, pressing Dodge will cancel the attack and instead perform a back-step attack.\nCurved Swords can use most Ashes of War, but not those that require Pierce Damage, since they cannot deal that damage type.\nNote: Since Patch 1.06, the time required to roll after performing an attack has been reduced, the motion speed of strong attack and charge attack has been increased and the motion speed of Guard Counter has been increased.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, poise damage has been reduced, but only for PVP.\nNOTE: Patch 1.09, Additionally, the running attack speed, first attack speed of straight sword has increased.
