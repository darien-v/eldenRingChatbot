\n\n Ghostflame Ignition \n\n        Thrust out the barbed rod and set its tip alight with ghostflame. \n       Follow up with a normal attack to set the ground ablaze with ghostflame, or a strong attack to trigger a massive explosion. \n\nGhostflame Ignition is a Skill in Elden Ring. Ghostflame Ignition is a unique skill that can be used on Death's Poker. It allows the player to thrust out the barbed rod and set its tip alight with ghostflame.\n\nHow to get Ghostflame Ignition in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill on the Death's Poker.\n\nElden Ring Ghostflame Ignition Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 15 (10 - 10)\nDeals 4 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill's main thrust, as well as the Light and Heavy attacks, can be Parried.\nDamage type is Magic Damage and has Frostbite buildup \n\n"Ghostflame" does not deal any Fire Damage.\n\nWearing SpellBlade Set increases the damage\nDamage scales with weapon upgrade level and Intelligence\nParticually useful against large enemies and bosses, especially when paired with weapon/skill dealing Fire Damage.\n\nElden Ring Skills QUESTION
Why is Ghostflame Ignition considered particularly useful against large enemies and bosses in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ghostflame Ignition is particularly useful against large enemies and bosses in Elden Ring because the skill can be paired with weapons or skills dealing Fire Damage, and can deal Magic Damage with Frostbite buildup.
