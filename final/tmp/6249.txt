CONTEXT
\n\n Ground Slam \n\n        Jump up high into the air and crash down on the ground ahead. The resulting pratfall sends a powerful shockwave in all directions. \n\nGround Slam is a Skill in Elden Ring. Ground Slam is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and can be applied to all melee armaments. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Ground Slam\n\nAsh of War: Ground Slam - Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab found rolling down the hill west of the Minor Erdtree in Mistwood. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Ground Slam Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Ground Slam.\nFP Cost: 14 on use.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nUpon activation, the user briefly crouches before leaping into the air, falling down and creating a shockwave at the point of impact.\nDeals 15 Stance damage with the shockwave and an addtional 30 from a direct hit. Rounding adds another point for 46 total.\nHas a high poise modifier, allowing the player to not be staggered by most attacks\nIf used without FP: \n\nDoesn't produce shockwave on impact.\nDeals less damage on hit\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed in Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nThe time between using the skill and being able to roll have been reduced.\nAttack Power has been increased.\n\nGround Slam QUESTION
Why is Ground Slam considered a powerful skill in Elden Ring and how does it enhance gameplay?

ANSWER
Ground Slam is considered powerful in Elden Ring due to its high poise modifier, ability to not be staggered by most attacks, and dealing significant damage through a shockwave and direct hit, thus enhancing gameplay by providing a strong offensive option with defensive utility.
