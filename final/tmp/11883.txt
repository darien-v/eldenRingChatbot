\n\n Giant Bat \n\nLocation\nAll across the Lands Between.\n\nDrops\n 47 - 887 RunesGolden Runes\n\nGiant Bat is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. Giant Bats are hostile creatures with large wings that allow them to fly. They are most commonly found at nighttime, in the open fields or in caves.\n\nGiant Bat Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Giant Bat:\n\nPlayers can encounter Giant Bat near the cliffs on Western Limgrave.\nMany can be found in Fort Faroth in Caelid.\nCan also be found on mines or caves.\n\nElden Ring Giant Bat Combat Information\n\nHealth: \n\n143 HP (Highroad Cave)\n161 HP (Weeping Peninsula)\n208 HP (Stormveil Castle)\n285 HP (Stillwater Cave)\n303 HP (Ruin-Strewn Precipice)\n409 HP (Capital Outskirts)\n519 HP (Volcano Manor)\n303 HP (Caelid - Gowry's Shack)\n842 HP (Greyoll's Dragonbarrow - Fort Faroth)\n610 HP (Mountaintops of the Giants)\n\nPoise: 30\n\nABSORPTIONS\n\nPhy (Standard): -10\nPhy (Slash): -40\nPhy (Strike): -10\nPhy (Pierce): -10\n\nMagic: -20\nFire: -20\nLightning: -20\nHoly: -20\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked.
