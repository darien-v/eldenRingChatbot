Headless soldiers that use various armor and weapons capable of vanishing in and out of existence. Sometimes found in groups alongside a Mausoleum Knight leading them. They are often found roaming around Walking Mausoleums, though a majority of them will despawn once the Mausoleum has been downed and made accessible.\nThe spirit of five (5) Mausoleum Soldiers can be summoned for aid in battle by using the Mausoleum Soldier Ashes.\n\n...Soldiers who confound foes by disappearing and reappearing at will.\nThe mausoleum is where the bodies of soulless demigods are lain to rest, and these soldiers followed their masters into Death by severing their own heads from their bodies.\n\nElden Ring Mausoleum Soldier Locations\nWhere to Find Mausoleum Soldier:\n\nThere QUESTION
Why do Mausoleum Soldiers sever their own heads from their bodies to follow their masters into Death?

ANSWER
Mausoleum Soldiers sever their heads to follow their masters into Death due to serving soulless demigods whose bodies are laid to rest in the mausoleum.
