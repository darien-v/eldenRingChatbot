\n\n Perfumer's Ruins \n\nA set of ruins found in Altus Plateau, far to the west and below Mt. Gelmir.\n\nPerfumer's Ruins  is a Location in Elden Ring. The Perfumer's Ruins  is found in Altus Plateau, far to the west. The ruins are found by heading northwest from the Abandoned Coffin site of grace.\n\nPerfumer's Ruins Map\nThe Perfumer's Ruins are found far to the west in Altus Plateau. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Perfumer's Ruins\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Perfumer's Ruins\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nNascent Butterfly\nAltus Bloom\nBudding Cave Moss\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials at this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nPerfumer's Talisman\nOmenkiller Set\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nPerfume Bottle\nPerfumer's Cookbook [1]\n\nElden Ring Perfumer's Ruins Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nPerfumer\nOmenkiller\nMiranda Sprout\nGiant Miranda Sprout\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no Bosses at this location\n\nPerfumer's Ruins Walkthrough\nThis guide assumes you're entering the ruins from the east, which is where you'll likely come from. Recommended to prepare poison curing items or to be sure you can handle poison. When approaching, you will likely be attacked by Perfumer enemies. They are easy to beat and there are 3 of them in the ruins. They throw explosive bottles at you. In the ruins, there are yellow Poison Flower pretty much everywhere. It can be a good idea to clear them to make sure you have safe terrain to fight the more dangerous enemies.\nIn the middle of the ruins is an open place with a table, guarded by an Omenkiller (strong enemy using two big weapons). The Omenkiller Set can be looted in this location as wellYou can sneak behind him and stealth attack him. He's pretty dangerous and has a fire breathing attack. Once killed, you can safely loot the Perfume Bottle on the floor near the table, and the Perfumer's Cookbook in the chest.\nIn another room near the back of the ruins is a yellow Giant Poison Flower. It will easily poison you. Killing it will reveal a passage behind it to find a tiny room with a Nascent Butterfly crafting item. There is another perfume bottle on the ground here and stairs going down to a door, inside of which is a chest containing Perfumer's Talisman.\nThere is nothing else to do in these ruins, but just south-west/below them are the Unsightly Catacombs locked by an imp statue.\n\nElden Ring Perfumer's Ruins Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned
