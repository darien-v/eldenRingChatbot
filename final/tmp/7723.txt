QUESTION
How can one effectively utilize Dragonbolt Blessing in combat situations in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
One can utilize Dragonbolt Blessing in combat by increasing resistance to ailments, deflecting enemy attacks easier, reducing lightning damage negation, increasing all resistances by 30, and pairing it with Raptor of the Mists for an effective counter jump attack, ensuring uninterrupted aerial combat.
