Gelmir Leyndell Royal Capital (Sewers)Mountaintops of the Giants\n\nDrops\n 418 - 1911 RunesSomber Smithing Stones\n\nThe Fingercreeper is a type of Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. Fingercreepers are hostile grotesque creatures comprised of gigantic disembodied hands, fused together and given life. Their behavior and method of traversal are highly reminiscent of a spider.\n\nFingercreeper Locations in Elden Ring\nFingercreepers can be found in the following locations:\n\nCaria Manor: prominently found around the gardens along with their Lesser counter parts.\nMt. Gelmir: by the pillar next to the Road of Iniquity Site of Grace.\nCan be encountered in the sewage section of Leyndell, Royal Capital.\nMountaintops of the Giants. They are accompanied by Lesser Fingercreepers, and there are two Colossal Fingercreepers at Flame Peak, as well.\n\nElden Ring Fingercreeper Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 418 - 1911\n\nSomber Smithing Stone (2)\n100%\nOne time only\n\nSomber Smithing Stone (4)\n100%\nOne time only\n\nSomber Smithing Stone (7)\n100%\nOne time only\n\nFingercreeper Notes & Tips\n\nWhen hit with any weapon/spell dealing Fire Damage, Fingercreepers are stunned for a few seconds, with an animation of them "dusting off", which deals additional damage to them.\nIt's recommended to maneuver yourself to their “backs,” as their attacks come mainly from the front.\nThey can sometimes fire off a purple orb that tracks the player down, which will explode and immobilize them. They will often follow up with a Grab attack.\nThose found in Caria Manor often hide underneath items, grabbing the player if caught. They can be distinguished by their fingernails protruding from the ground.\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nPhy (Standard): -10\nPhy (Slash): -40\nPhy (Strike): -10\nPhy (Pierce): -10\n\nMagic: 0\nFire: 0\nLightning: 0\nHoly: 0\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. QUESTION
Why do Fingercreeper creatures in Elden Ring exhibit vulnerability to fire damage, leading to a stunning effect and additional damage?

ANSWER
Fingercreepers in Elden Ring exhibit vulnerability to fire damage, leading to a stunning effect and additional damage, due to their reaction mechanism when hit by fire-related attacks.
