\n\n Prophecy Painting \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Reminiscence of the painting "Prophecy". \n       Examine using the corresponding button. \n\nProphecy Painting is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs\n\nWork of a wandering artist.Reminiscence of a painting titled "Prophecy."\nThis painter is said to have captured the landscapes seen during the last moments of those welcomed into death's embrace.\nThe soul of the painter, and vestiges of the dead's last moments can be discovered by visiting the location depicted even now.\n\nWhere to find Prophecy Painting in Elden Ring\n\nLocation: Stormveil Castle. [Map Link]\nFound inside a study room located at the heavily guarded courtyard where the Omen ogre is. The nearest checkpoint is the Liftside Chamber site of grace. [Video Location]\n\nElden Ring Prophecy Painting Tutorial Guide\n\n The location is directly north of the Church of Pilgrimage at the northern tip of Weeping Peninsula, next to a broken pillar. [Map Link] The item you get is Warhawk Ashes. You summon a Hawk similar to the ones in Stormveil Castle and it costs 52 FP. It attacks both with the swords attached to its talons and its beak. The summon is also able to breathe fire. It can be upgraded with Grave Glovewort. \n\nElden Ring Prophecy Painting Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 Prophecy Painting.\nYou cannot store Prophecy Painting.\nThis item cannot be sold.\nNotes
