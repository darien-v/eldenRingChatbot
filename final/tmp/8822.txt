QUESTION
Why is the Eye of Yelough considered a dangerous intoxicant despite its pain-relieving properties?

ANSWER
The Eye of Yelough is considered a dangerous intoxicant despite its pain-relieving properties due to its effects on causing intoxication when consumed.
