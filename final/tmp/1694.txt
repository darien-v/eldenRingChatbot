You can even roll towards her and deal a number of hits underneath. The main attack to look out for is her grab attack which can kill players easily if they don't manage to escape. As she approaches 50% health she enters phase 2 and will transition to weaponless combat after tossing away her staff. Instead, she starts to show aggressive slaming behavior. You can still use the same strategy by rolling back from some of these attacks to avoid damage or continue to roll toward or underneath her to gain closer and better position to deal numerous melee attacks.  \n\nMagic and Ranged Users\nRecommend using summons to keep agro of boss while using magic spells. Mimic Tear Ashes are pretty effective due to the high health bar. Rock Sling is a pretty effective spell. Summon your spirit ash once you get in the room then doge the hits until your summon starts doing damage and the boss aggros your summon(s). From there, throw your spells at it, be sure to keep an eye on if the boss starts to agro you so you can be prepared to dodge as needed.\n\nDemi-Human Queen Margot Attacks & Counters\n\nAttack\nDescription\nCounter\n\nStaff Strike / Drag\nThe Demi-Human Queen does a series of melee staff attacks that can be dealt in a combo. This consists of slamming, strike and dragging her staff.\nRoll through these attacks but be prepared to counter each direction and follow up attack. \n\nPhase 2 (Enraged)\n\nStaff Throw\nShe will stand in place, shaking, before growling and throwing her staff at her target. She will then roar and become more aggressive.\nThe staff itself does pitiful damage, but this indicates her phase transition.\n\nChomp\nShe lunges at the area directly in front of her\nSide step this as she lunges in the area directly in front of her. Stay to her side to avoid contact\n\n Slam\nThis boss takes its fists and slams the area in front of her, affecting a larger range than her regular attacks. \nThis attack animation takes a while so once shes up on her hind likes take a step back or roll towards her for a better attack position. \n\nDemi-Human Queen Margot Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nLike Gilika, she will enrage upon being QUESTION
What are the best tips for defeating Demi-Human Queen Margot in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Use any tanky or multi-summon Spirit Ashes, utilize Hoarfrost Stomp skill, cast Rock Sling Spell, for melee users dodge attacks and exploit openings, for magic/ranged users summon and use spells, watch for boss aggro and dodge accordingly.
