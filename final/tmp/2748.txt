General Radahn Set is a Heavyweight armor that boasts superior Robustness Resistance. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find General Radahn Set in Elden Ring\n\nPurchased from Enia at Roundtable Hold for 36,000 runes after defeating Starscourge Radahn.\n\nGeneral Radahn Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nRadahn's Redmane Helm\nRadahn's Lion Armor\nRadahn's Gauntlets\nRadahn's Greaves\n\nGeneral Radahn Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Radahn's Lion Armor can be altered to the Radahn's Lion Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nThis set has very similar stats to the Banished Knight Set, only slightly heavier (if you are using the altered chest and helmet, otherwise the weight is identical) and provides slightly more protection in some areas, and slightly less protection in other areas. The chest piece offers better protection overall compared to the altered chest piece of the banished knight set that you can get much earlier, but not substantially more (same is true for the altered Banished Knight Helm, but there are two banished knights in Stormveil that you can farm for that).\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the General Radahn Set considered a Heavyweight armor with superior Robustness Resistance in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The General Radahn Set is considered a Heavyweight armor with superior Robustness Resistance due to its high Robustness, Immunity, and overall protection offered compared to other armor sets.
