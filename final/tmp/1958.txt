Crystal Tears can be used and mixed to enhance the effects of the Flask of Wondrous Physick.\n\nA crystal tear formed slowly over the ages where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground.\nCan be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick.The resulting concoction temporarily raises one's maximum stamina.\n\nGreenspill Crystal Tear Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Greenspill Crystal Tear:\n\nMistwood: Found on an altar next to the Minor Erdtree in Mistwood along with the Spiked Cracked Tear. QUESTION
What are the effects and location of the Greenspill Crystal Tear in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Greenspill Crystal Tear temporarily boosts stamina in mixed physick and can be found in Mistwood next to the Minor Erdtree.
