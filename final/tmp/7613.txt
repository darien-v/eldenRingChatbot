\n\n Flock's Canvas Talisman \n\nWeight\n1.0\n\nEffect \n\nGreatly raises potency of \n       incantations (8%) \n\nFlock's Canvas Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Flock's Canvas Talisman raises the potency of Incantations by 8%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman bearing an icon that depicts a mass of masked figures.Greatly raises potency of incantations.\nThe figures represent the flock at prayer, their firm belief in the intangible inspiring even the solitary founder of their religion.\nWhat is faith if not an affirmation?\n\nFlock's Canvas Talisman Effect\nFlock's Canvas Talisman raises the potency of Incantations by 8%.\n\nWhere to find Flock's Canvas Talisman in Elden Ring\n\nDrops from Gowry after you kill him, only if he is crying about Millicent's death.\nCompleting Millicent's entire quest line, by slaying or helping her at the end, will put Gowry in the "crying" state.Killing her earlier will also get Gowry into this state.\n\nFlock's Canvas Talisman Notes & Tips\n\nStacks multiplicatively with other incantation buffs, including Faithful's Canvas Talisman.\nOnly affects damage; has no effect on support incantations.\nSell value:  2,000\nSometimes, after finishing Millicent's questline with the "good" ending and returning to Gowry, upon attacking him he will continuously turn into a bug, making it impossible to kill him to get Flock's Canvas Talisman until starting your next journey.\n\nFlock's Canvas Talisman Builds\n\nCrusader Written Guide\n\nMagic Dragonknight Written Guide\n\nGolden Sword Sage
