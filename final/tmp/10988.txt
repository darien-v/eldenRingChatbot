\n\n Adula's Moonblade \n\nSpell Type\nCold Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 22\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Sweeping slash followed by cold blade projectile \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 32 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nAdula's Moonblade is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Adula's Moonblade spell allows you to slash and finishes the blow with a cold blade ranged attack. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSorcery of Adula, the Glintstone Dragon.\nConjures a cold magic greatsword, then delivers a sweeping blowthat launches a blade-like projectile of frost.\nThis sorcery can be cast repeatedly.\nAdula, a devourer of sorcerers, was bested by Ranni andsubsequently swore a knightly oath to her Dark Moon.\n\nWhere to find Adula's Moonblade\nWhere to find Adula's Moonblade:\n\nDropped by the Glintstone Dragon Adula located at the Cathedral of Manus Celes. Elden Ring Interactive Map Link\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Adula's Moonblade Guide\n\nConsidered a Carian Sword Sorcery and a Cold Sorcery. \n\nThis means that the damage is boosted by both the Carian Glintstone Staff and the Snow Witch Hat.\n\nStamina Cost: 22\nDeals magic damage: \n\nThe initial slash inflicts (Sorcery Scaling x 1.33) magic damage before resistances.\nThe projectile inflicts (Sorcery Scaling x 1.89) magic damage before resistances.\n\nInflicts Frostbite buildup: \n\nThe initial slash inflicts 90, while the projectile inflicts 35.\n\nThis sorcery has a chain animation that slightly increases its activation speed for repeated uses.\nIt's possible to hit an enemy with both the slash of the blade and the projectile, maximizing damage and frostbite buildup. However, this only works on the first cast. Subsequent chain casts can't apply both sources of damage.\nNote: This Spell was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nThe amount of frostbite buildup that is applied to a guarding enemy has been increased.\nFP consumption was reduced from 26 to 22. \n\nBest Adula's Moonblade Buffs\nUse these Buffs to maximize the performance of Adula's Moonblade spell.
