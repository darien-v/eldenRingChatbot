QUESTION
How can equipping the Ruler's Mask helm in Elden Ring benefit a player's character in terms of defensive properties and appearance?

ANSWER
Equipping the Ruler's Mask helm in Elden Ring provides damage negation, physical damage resistance vs strike, slash, and pierce, high magic, fire, lightning, and holy resistance, immunity 20, robustness 10, focus 33, vitality 33, poise 3, and changes the player's appearance.
