CONTEXT
\n\n Spinning Wheel \n\n        Strike the wheel against the ground to set it spinning at top speed. Hold to keep the wheel spinning. Can be used while walking to push the armament into enemies. \n\nSpinning Wheel is a Skill in Elden Ring. Spinning Wheel is a unique skill on Ghiza's Wheel. Damage enemies with the quick spinning blades on the wheel and continue to move into enemies. Updated for Patch 1.09\n\nHow to get Spinning Wheel\n\nDefault skill on the Ghiza's Wheel.\n\nElden Ring Spinning Wheel Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 3\nDeals 4 stance damage per spin\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nSkill starts up with a lunge that revs up the wheel. Holding weapon skill button keeps the wheel spinning for continuous damage and bleed buildup. Maintaining this skill will drain FP but not Stamina.\nIt can be used continuously by using Cerulean Hidden Tear.\nDamage from the wheel spinning is not boosted by the Winged Sword Insignia, Rotted Winged Sword Insignia, or Millicent's Prosthesis.\n\nPatch Changes\nPatch 1.09\n\nIncreased attack generation speed.\nAdded a roll & heavy attack cancel timing while using the skill.\n\nElden Ring
