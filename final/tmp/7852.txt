Putrid Corpse Ashes are slow and erratic undead beings able to induce some level of poison to Enemies when approached.\nPutrid Corpses can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Putrid Corpse.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirits of four putrid corpses.\nThese spirits appear some distance from the summoner. Though their movements are dull and confused, their low moans make them effective at drawing the attention of enemies.  True death does not come easily for them.\n\nWhere to find Putrid Corpse Ashes\nWhere to Find Putrid Corpse Ashes\n\nDrops from Frenzied Duelist, boss of Gaol Cave.
