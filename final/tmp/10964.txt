\n\n Carian Greatsword \n\nSpell Type\nCarian Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 12\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Performs sweeping slash using magical greatsword \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 24 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nCarian Greatsword is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Carian Greatsword spell creates a magic sword that slice enemies from the side. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the sorceries of the Carian royal family.Conjures a magic greatsword and then delivers a sweeping blow.This sorcery can be cast repeatedly.Armed with this spell, sorcerers gain the strength of knights, their loyalty sworn to the moon.\n\nWhere to find Carian Greatsword\nWhere to find Carian Greatsword:\n\nCan be purchased from Miriel, Pastor of Vows at the Church of Vows for  10,000 Runes. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Carian Greatsword Guide\n\nCarian Sorcery\n\nCan be boosted by the Carian Glintstone Staff\n\nStamina Cost: 22\nInflicts (Sorcery Scaling x 2.68) magic damage.\nAlthough niche, has a very useful purpose against Black Knife Assassin type enemies. Their side leaps are unable to evade the Greatsword's arc and staggers them on hit. With proper timing and enough FP/Stamina for chain casting, it's possible for a mage to stun-lock assassins.\nCounts as 2 hits towards "Succesive Attack" Talismans. (i.e. Winged Sword Insignia, Godskin Swaddling Cloth, and Millicent's Prosthesis.)\nCancels out of ending animation faster if chain casting another spell afterwards. Chain casting into another spell cancels the animation faster than rolling out, jumping, back stepping, and attacking. Does not make casting time faster for other spells unless it is a "swift" spell (i.e. Carian Slicer, Swift glintstone shard, and Night shard.)\nCan be used in conjunction with Carian Piercer to expand the moveset, by performing a thrust after the horizontal slash.\nOn successive casts, it looks like swings 3 into 4 (and any more afterwards) swing faster than swings 1 into 2\nNote: In Patch 1.07, FP cost was reduced from 14 to 12 and damage was increased slightly, as well as stamina damage against guarding enemies.\n\nElden Ring Carian Greatsword Moveset and Videos\n\nSpellblade Written Guide\n\nMagus Written Guide\n\nEnchanted Knight Written Guide\n\nMoonveil Samurai Written Guide\n\nBattlemage Written Guide\n\nWarrior Wizard Build\n\nElden Ring Carian Greatsword Note and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
