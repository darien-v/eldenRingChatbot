Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nGloves worn by traveling maidens.\nMaidens traversed the Lands Between for different reasons. Some to seek audience with the Fingers. Others, to find the Tarnished who they were destined to guide.\n\nTraveling Maiden Gloves Location in Elden Ring\nThe Traveling Maiden Gloves can be found in the following location:\n\nOn a corpse inside the Shack of The Lofty in the northern Mountaintops of the Giants. The rest of the set can also be looted here. QUESTION
Why are Traveling Maiden Gloves a part of the Traveling Maiden Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Traveling Maiden Gloves are a part of the Traveling Maiden Set in Elden Ring because they offer various defensive properties, protect both arms, and are worn as a glove accessory by traveling maidens.
