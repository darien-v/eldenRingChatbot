CONTEXT
\n\n Great Rune of the Unborn \n\nType\nGreat Rune\n\nEffect \n\n        Used to perfect rebirth \n\nGreat Rune of the Unborn is a Great Rune in Elden Ring. Great Rune of the Unborn provides the perfect rebirth ability. Great Runes in Elden Ring are special items dropped by Demigod Bosses that can be equipped to acquire special passive bonuses.\n\nAmber egg clutched by Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon.Great Rune of unborn demigods.\nPerfects those who have been born anew.\nChildren born anew by Rennala are all frail and short-lived.Imperfect beings, each and all\n\nWhere to find Great Rune of the Unborn in Elden Ring\nDropped as a reward for defeating Rennala Queen of the Full Moon located at Raya Lucaria Academy in Liurnia of the Lakes. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Great Rune of the Unborn Use\n\nThis Great Rune does not need to be activated, nor can it be equipped.\nThis rune gives you access to Rebirth (character respec) in the Raya Lucaria Grand Library, after defeating Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon.\nA Larval Tear is also required each time you want to respec\n\nElden Ring Great Runes\n\nGodrick's Great Rune  ♦  Malenia's Great Rune  ♦  Mending Rune of Perfect Order  ♦  Mending Rune of the Death-Prince  ♦  Mending Rune of the Fell Curse  ♦  Mohg's Great Rune  ♦  Morgott's Great Rune  ♦  Phantom Great Rune  ♦  Radahn's Great Rune  ♦  Rykard's Great Rune\n\n

QUESTION
Why is the Great Rune of the Unborn dropped as a reward for defeating Rennala Queen of the Full Moon in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Great Rune of the Unborn is dropped as a reward for defeating Rennala Queen of the Full Moon in Elden Ring because it provides the perfect rebirth ability in the game.
