QUESTION
How can I locate Nomadic Merchant Mt. Gelmir in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
From Primeval Sorcerer Azur Site of Grace, go east across a stone pillar, then north across the next stone pillar bridge, climb up 2 ladders to find the Mt. Gelmir Nomadic Merchant, or from Ninth Mt. Gelmir Campsite Site of Grace, cross the rope bridge east, climb up rocks to find a ladder leading down to the merchant.
