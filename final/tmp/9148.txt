\n\n Skeletal Bandit \n\nLocation\nDungeons and graveyards of the Lands Between\n\nDrops\n\n 35 - 682 RunesBandit's Curved Sword\nHuman Bone Shard\n\nSkeletal Bandits are Humanoid Enemies in Elden Ring. Skeletal Bandits are bare-boned creatures skilled in ambushing their preys in groups, often alongside Skeletal Militiamen, Skeleton Mages, and several other Skeletons. These light-armored pirates often act as lifeless corpses in order to surprise-attack their targets, but will mostly attack from beneath the ground. Just like all other Skeletons, Skeletal Bandits will not die upon defeat, making them able to re-take their previous shape and keep on fighting. In order to avoid this, players should also attack their remains even after these creatures are defeated.\nThe spirit of a Skeletal Bandit can be summoned for aid in battle by using Skeletal Bandit Ashes.\n\nThe remains of former bandits that will continue to rise again until properly finished off.After the night of the plot, Deathroot appeared in the Lands Between, and Those Who Live in Death soon followed.\n\nSkeletal Bandit Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Skeletal Bandits:\n\nCaelid. At the cemetery past Summonwater Village.\nWeeping Peninsula.
