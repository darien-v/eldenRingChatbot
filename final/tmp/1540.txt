Mushroom Set is a Light Weight armor with mushrooms growing all over that that does not offer much in the way of protection but boosts superior Immunity, Focus, and Vitality Resistances. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Mushroom Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Mushroom Crown is found on a corpse hanging off of a pillar ruin in the south-eastern corner of Lake of Rot. [Elden Ring Map here]\nThe rest of the set is found in Seethewater Cave, in a cavern with a Giant Poison Flower and multiple mushroom people. QUESTION
How can one acquire the complete Mushroom Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The complete Mushroom Set in Elden Ring can be acquired by finding the Mushroom Crown in the south-eastern corner of Lake of Rot and the rest of the set in Seethewater Cave.
