Putrid Grave Warden Duelist is an aggressive strength-based boss whose attacks inflict bleed, and is found behind the locked door in Consecrated Snowfield Catacombs. This is an optional boss as players don't need to defeat it to advance in Elden Ring.\nRotten Duelist is the normal enemy form of this boss. Grave Warden Duelist and Frenzied Duelist are other variants of this boss.
