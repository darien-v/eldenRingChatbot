\n\n Volcano Manor Spirit \n\nLocation\nVolcano Manor \n\nRole\nBenevolent\n\nThe Volcano Manor Spirit is an NPC in Elden Ring. He rests in the Volcano Manor.\n\nSomeone, please, kill him...\n\nVolcano Manor Spirit Location in Elden Ring\nThe Volcano Manor Spirit can be found outside the Drawing Room in the Volcano Manor.\n\nThis NPC does not move and can only be talked to.\nThis spirit can't be fought\n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: Volcano Manor Spirit\n\n"Someone, please, kill him..."\n"Mm, you're Tarnished. Here to put the demigods to the sword.Then please. Kill the great serpent.The one that devoured Praetor Rykard.I left the serpent-slaying spear in the Lord's Chamber.Worthy Tarnished, brandish the spear, and run him through.The great serpent...that unspeakable monstrosity."\n"Praetor Rykard's ambitions, though blasphemous, marked him a worthy sovereign.But they were reduced to gluttonous depravity, once he gave himself to the serpent.Whatever that thing is, it is no longer Praetor Rykard.Someone must kill him.To spare him, and his ambitions, from further dishonour."\n"Worthy Tarnished. I left the serpent-slaying spear in the Lord's Chamber.It is the only thing that can kill that horror.Run it through. The great serpent...that unspeakable monstrosity."\n\nElden Ring Volcano Manor
