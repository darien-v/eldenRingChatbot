\n\n Carian Phalanx \n\nSpell Type\nCarian Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 24\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Forms a defensive arch of numerous magic glintblades \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 34 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nCarian Phalanx  is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Carian Phalanx spell creates numerous swords over your head which attack enemies when they get close enough. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the sorceries of the Carian royal family.\nCreates a defensive arch of numerous magic glintblades overhead, which automatically attack nearby foes.This sorcery can be used cast while in motion.\nAn enhanced version of the glintblade sorcery used by enchanted knights.\n\nWhere to find Carian Phalanx\nWhere to find Carian Phalanx :\n\nSold by Preceptor Seluvis at Seluvis's Rise, after completing steps of his questline (at least after the handing of Amber Starlight to him, Phalanx appears in his store).\nCan be obtained post-mortem via Seluvis's Bell Bearing. (from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold)\n\nElden Ring Carian Phalanx Guide\n\nCarian Sorcery\n\nCan be boosted by the Carian Gintblade Staff.\n\nStamina Cost: 16\nDeals magic damage \n\nEach individual gintblade deals (Sorcery Scaling x 0.48) magic damage.\nFires 9 gintblades for a total of (Sorcery Scaling x 4.32) magic damage.\n\nFeatures an additional four blades over the base Glintblade Phalanx spell, offering more damage, as well as damage to an enemy's stance.\nPhalanx spells are a good way for mages to whittle down an enemy's stance.\nThe blades will break upon coming into contact with any geometry, including enemies. This unfortunately means one cannot prep themselves with phalanx before passing through a boss fog gate, and must take care after casting to not accidentally break the blades on any surfaces to not waste any damage.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 26 to 24.\n\nElden Ring Carian Phalanx Note and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can the Carian Phalanx spell be used effectively in combat situations in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Carian Phalanx spell can be effectively used in combat situations by creating numerous swords overhead that automatically attack nearby foes, deal magic damage, break enemy stances, and are good for mages to weaken enemies' defenses.
