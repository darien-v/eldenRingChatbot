\n\n Ranni's Dark Moon \n\nSpell Type\nFull Moon Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 57\nSlots Used 2\n\nEffect \n\n        Incarnate a cold, dark moon and launch it at foes. \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 68 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nRanni's Dark Moon is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Ranni's Dark Moon spell launches a dark moon at foes. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nLegendary sorcery associated with the Carian queen. \nUses the caster as a vessel to incarnate a cold, dark moon, then sends it floating toward foes. The dark moon dispels all sorcery that touches it, and temporarily reduces magic damage negation for those it strikes.\nThis moon was encountered by a young Ranni, led by the hand of her mother, Rennala. What she beheld was cold, dark and veiled in occult mystery.\n\nWhere to find Ranni's Dark Moon\nWhere to find Ranni's Dark Moon:\n\nChelona's Rise: Found in a chest at the top of the tower after unlocking the seal. See the Chelona's Rise page for details on how to unseal the tower. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nNote: The location of this spell is accessible only after the player has made significant progress in Ranni's questline, which will grant the player access to the upper plateau in southwest Liurnia.\n\nElden Ring Ranni's Dark Moon Guide\n\nFull Moon Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 32\nThis sorcery is boosted by the Carian Regal Scepter (+10%)\nThis sorcery is boosted by the Snow Witch Hat (+10%)\nThis sorcery deals Magic damage, and will build Frostbite (270) on vulnerable enemies. It is possible to apply it in one cast.\nApplies a debuff which reduces the target's magic negation by 10% for one minute.  \n\nIf this manages to trigger Frostbite on the target, it reduces damage negation by a further 20%. The total reduction is 32% due to multiplicative stacking.\nThis debuff reduces damage negation multiplicatively. Therefore it will always increase Magic damage by 10%, regardless of the target's base negation.\n\nRanni's Dark Moon draws in other sorcery in its path and cancels them out.  \n\nFurther testing may be required to determine exactly when this property begins. \n\nWhile it may have incredibly high damage, Ranni's Dark Moon has a long casting animation and can be canceled if struck by an enemy before the projectile launches.\nThis is a Power Shred Debuff and will not stack with other debuffs of the same type. \n\nSee Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking debuffs.\n\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 62 to 57.\n\nBest Ranni's Dark Moon Buffs\nUse these Buffs to maximize the performance of Ranni's Dark Moon.
