\n\n Rejection \n\nSpell Type\nTwo Fingers Incantations\n\nFP Cost 9\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Produces a shockwave that pushes away foes. \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 12 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nRejection is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Rejection spell is cast to create a shockwave that pushes enemies away. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nIncantation of the Two Fingers' faithful.\nProduces a shockwave that knocks back nearby foes.Charging increases the size of the shockwave.\nHark, Tarnished! If you truly walk in faith, you must be prepared to reject all else.\n\nRejection Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Rejection:\n\nSold by Brother Corhyn at the Roundtable Hold for 1,500 \n\nElden Ring Rejection Guide\n\nTwo Fingers Incantation\nStamina Cost: 24\nOne of offensive spells, that deals 0 damage to target\nDeals 15 stance damage\nCan be charged for a stronger push or vs shielded enemies \n\nCharging seems to affect the range of spell, not stance damage (testing needed)\n\nMay break enemy stance \n\nThis makes Rejection a good spell against Crystalians, as it knocks them even before 1st stance break.\n\nCan be chain-casted as long as player has enough  FP\nAffects a 360º AoE\nDoes not seem to awaken/alert sleeping or unaware enemies.
