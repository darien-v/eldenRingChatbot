CONTEXT
\n\n Chilling Mist \n\n        Coat armament in frost, and then slash, spreading frigid mist forwards. The armament retains its frost for a while. \n\nChilling Mist is a Skill in Elden Ring. Chilling Mist is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War that can be used on many melee armaments. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Chilling Mist\n\nCan be found in the Three Sisters sub-region. Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the ruins south of Renna's Rise. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Chilling Mist Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Chilling Mist.\nFP Cost: 14 on use.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nUpon activation, the user raises their armament (weapon) and coats it with frost, before performing a sweep in front of them that releases a cold mist in a small area. \n\nThe frost coating counts as a weapon buff. \nThis skill consumes FP before the sweep and mist are performed. If the user is interrupted but has consumed FP, only the weapon buff will be performed.\nConsumes approximately 30 stamina.\n\nThe weapon is buffed with a 60 Frost buildup per hit effect, after consuming FP. \n\nThis buff lasts for 40 seconds after use.\nThis buff is not reflected in the weapon's stat screen.\nThis buff stacks with the innate status buildup the weapon has (if any), including frost.\nThe buff only applies to the weapon in the right hand.\n\nThe sweep attack deals aproximately 100% more damage than a light attack. \n\nIt also applies 120 frostbite on hit.\nThis sweep can be aimed in any direction before its release.\n\nAfter the sweep, the weapon leaves a stationary mist that continues to apply 120 more frost buildup per tick. \n\nThe mist lingers for 5 seconds upon appearing.\nHas a small area of effect that can affect multiple enemies if clustered together.\n\nIf used without FP: \n\nNo weapon buff is added.\nThe sweep deals the same damage as a light attack, and doesn't have additional buildup.\nNo mist is released.\n\nNOTES: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nMotion speed has been increased.\nDuration of the weapon's frostbite effect has been increased from 20 to 40 seconds.\n\nChilling Mist Demonstration\n\nRime Ronin Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's QUESTION
How does the Chilling Mist skill in Elden Ring impact gameplay?

ANSWER
The Chilling Mist skill in Elden Ring coats the weapon with frost, providing a weapon buff, increased damage, frost buildup per hit, and releases a sweeping mist that applies frost buildup and frostbite, affecting multiple enemies.
