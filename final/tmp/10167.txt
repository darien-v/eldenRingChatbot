CONTEXT
\nMagma Sorceries or Lava Sorceries are a group of Sorceries in Elden Ring that share a specific type of buff criteria. Magma Sorceries draw upon the power of molten lava from Mt Gelmir. QUESTION
What are the requirements for using Magma Sorceries in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Magma Sorceries in Elden Ring require a Glintstone Staff equipped, as well as Intelligence and Faith to use.
