\n\n Freezing Mist \n\nSpell Type\nCold Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 20\nSlots Used: 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Releases cold mist before caster \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 21 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nFreezing Mist is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. The Freezing Mist spell releases a cold mist that inflicts Frostbite buildup on enemies. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nSorcery said to have been used by the old snow witch.\nReleases a cold mist before the caster.This sorcery can be cast while in motion. Charging enhances range of the mist.\nThe snowy crone taught the young Ranni to fear the dark moon as she imparted her cold sorcery.\n\nWhere to find Freezing Mist\n\nCan be bought from Preceptor Seluvis for 6,000 Runes. Seluvis is located in Seluvis's Rise, inside the Three Sisters region of Liurnia of the Lakes. [Map link] \n\nBefore Seluvis will sell you Sorceries, you must progress in his quest line. The choices you make do not matter.\nAlternatively, giving Ranni the Fingerslayer Blade causes Seluvis's death, or the player could simply attack him. He drops Seluvis's Bell Bearing, and the spell can be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at Roundtable Hold after offering the bearing to them.\n\nFreezing Mist Guide\n\nCold Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 13\nInflicts Frostbite buildup (120) on opponents.\nDepending on resistance, for most enemies, it takes about three seconds of being affected by the mist to cause Frostbite.\nThe spell will not alert unaware enemies, even when it triggers the Frostbite effect, similar to Poison Mist.\nUnlike Night Maiden's Mist, it does not affect the caster or friendly targets.\nThe spell can be charged to enlarge the affected area. (Charging does not affect the rate of effect buildup.)\nThe area expands from the point of "impact", reaching its full size in about half a second, or about one second if the spell is charged.\nIt has a short range, even when free-aimed into the air.\nWhile standing in or walking through the mist is sufficient to trigger Frostbite on most enemies, running through it (once) is usually not.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 Frostbite Status Buildup has been increased from 90 to 120.\n\nSoul Samurai Guide\n\nFreezing Battlemage Written Guide\n\nCarian Cavalier Written Guide\n\nFreezing Mist Note and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
