\n\n Elden Throne \n\nThe seat of reigning Elden Lord\n\nElden Throne is a Location in Elden Ring.\nThe Elden Throne is found in Leyndell, Royal Capital. You can reach the Elden Throne through the Queen's Bedchamber. Here the battle with Morgott, The Omen King takes place. After the fight, you can attempt to enter the Erdtree only for access to be denied, causing the Site of Grace to spawn in the center of the arena. Melina tells you that in order to unlock the entrance to the Erdtree you need to travel to Mountaintops of the Giants.\nAfter completing Crumbling Farum Azula, the player is directed back to the Elden Throne from Leyndell, Ashen Capital to realize their ambition of becoming the Elden Lord. Godfrey, First Elden Lord now appears in Morgott's arena and must be defeated to proceed. The Elden Throne Site of Grace becomes available again, and is the final checkpoint before the final bosses located in the Erdtree.\nElden Throne Map\n[overall map goes here]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Elden Throne\n\nMelina - Appears if you activate the Site of Grace after unsucessfully attempting to enter the Erdtree. After defeating him, you can interact with the Erdtree, but the thorns won't allow you to pass. Activate the newly appeared Site of Grace, to have a small conversation with Melina. She will offer to journey with you again to try and get rid of the thorns, handing you the Rold Medallion which lets you access the Mountaintops of the Giants.\nAfter completing Crumbling Farum Azula, you will be directed back to the Elden Throne from Leyndell, Ashen Capital. Godfrey, First Elden Lord will be your next challenge. Defeat him and the Site of Grace will appear in the arena. You can now travel past the burned-away thorns in the Erdtree and touch the light at the end of the tunnel to face the final bosses of this game.\nElden Ring Elden Throne Gallery and Notes\n[other QUESTION
What is the sequence of events that lead the player to become the Elden Lord in Elden Throne?

ANSWER
Defeat Morgott, receive Rold Medallion from Melina, complete Crumbling Farum Azula, defeat Godfrey, proceed to Erdtree for final bosses.
