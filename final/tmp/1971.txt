QUESTION
Why is it important to boost one's faith temporarily in mixed physick in Elden Ring using the Faith-knot Crystal Tear?

ANSWER
Boosting one's faith temporarily in mixed physick using the Faith-knot Crystal Tear is important in Elden Ring because it enhances the effectiveness of healing and support abilities for a limited duration.
