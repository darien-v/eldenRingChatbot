CONTEXT
\nTorches are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Torches deal little damage, but can be used to ward off unwanted beasts while dealing some Fire Damage on them.\nTorches can't be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, except to apply the "No Skill" skill.\nTorches do not receive a unique moveset as a result of dual-wielding (AKA "power stancing").\nElden Ring Torch Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Torches in Elden Ring.
