The Smoldering Shield is a Shield that scales primarily with Strength and Faith. Small Shields usually weigh very little and give the least protection from attacks. They are often used by players who prefer parrying rather than blocking. Its lighter weight also makes it ideal for quick, agile characters with low equipment weight capacity.\n\nSmall shield made upon Mt. Gelmir forged from uncooling lava.Resists frost with its heat, and can even be used to deal fire damage if wielded as a weapon.\n\nWhere to Find Smoldering Shield\n\nFrom the Prison Town Church site of grace, exit the building and turn right, walking west until dropping down onto the first rooftop, just behind the long-necked serpent. Immediately turn left, face south and head toward the corner of the adjacent rooftop, and safely drop down onto the rocky ground below. Once there, continue downhill to the east, and drop down to the big roof to the southwest (NOT the small hexagonal rooftop). From there, make a running jump to the rocky ground to the northwest, and continue onward to the graveyard. QUESTION
Why would a player choose to use the Smoldering Shield in Elden Ring over other shields available in the game?

ANSWER
A player might choose the Smoldering Shield in Elden Ring over other shields due to its fire damage capabilities, low weight for agility, robustness boost, and scaling mainly with Strength and Faith.
