\n\n About Monument Icon \n\nType\nInfo Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Information about Monument Icon \n\nAbout Monument Icon is an Info Item in Elden Ring. Tutorial items provide information about how to add Skills to your Weapons, Crafting advices, Combat tips, and more. They can be obtained through exploration and looting, dropped by specific Enemies or Bosses, or purchased from NPCs.\nSee Rebirth Monuments for detailed information.\n\nWhere to find About Monument Icon in Elden Ring\n\nAutomatically added to inventory after concept introduction. The concept introduction can be triggered in Elden Ring version 1.0, when you see the first Monument Icon after you receive the Spirit Calling Bell. It can no longer be triggered in version 1.6. If you really want to to obtain this item, you can purchase a physical version of the game and thus play version 1.0 of the game.\n\nElden Ring About Monument Icon Tutorial Guide\n\nYou must be near a rebirth monument to summon spirits from ashes.\nWhen you are close to a rebirth monument and able to summon, a monument icon will appear on the left side of the screen.\n\nElden Ring About Monument Icon Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 1 About Monument Icon.\nYou cannot store About Monument Icon.\nYou cannot sell About Monument Icon.\n\nElden
