\n\n The Shaded Castle Spirit \n\nLocation\nInside The Shaded Castle\n\nRole\nBenevolent\n\nThe Shaded Castle Spirit is an NPC in Elden Ring. The Shaded Castle Spirit lays defeated within The Shaded Castle.\n\nHouse Marais is ruined... Just deserts, for falling for that severed harpy. No surprise that guilty cretin took the castle and our storied sword...\n\nThe Shaded Castle Spirit Location in Elden Ring\nThe Shaded Castle Spirit can be found heading directly North of the Shaded Castle Inner Gate grace and then turning East when you reach the wall. [Map Link]\n\nThis NPC does not move and can only be talked to.\nThis spirit can't be fought\n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: The Shaded Castle Spirit\n\n "... House Marais is ruined... Just deserts, for falling for that severed harpy. No surprise that guilty cretin took the castle and our storied sword..." \n\nElden Ring The Shaded Castle Spirit Notes & Trivia\n\nThe Shaded Castle Spirit speaks of the fall of the House of Marais. \n\nThe severed harpy comment is a reference to Malenia. A painted portrait of her can be found inside Castellan's Hall. [Map Link]\nThe guilty cretin in question is Elemer of the Briar. He stole the Marais sword and is waiting inside Castellan's Hall. [Map Link]\nThe storied sword is the Marais Executioner's Sword. QUESTION
What historical event is the Shaded Castle Spirit referring to regarding the fall of House Marais and the stolen Marais sword?

ANSWER
The fall of House Marais and the stolen Marais sword are referring to Elemer of the Briar stealing the Marais Executioner's Sword from the Shaded Castle.
