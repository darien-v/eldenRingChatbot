CONTEXT
\nFrenzied Flame Incantations are a group of Incantations in Elden Ring that share a specific type of buff criteria. Frenzied Flame Incantations originate from the maddening Three Fingers, and revolve around chaos and madness. Frenzied Flame Incantations require Faith in order to be casted.\nThere are a total of 5 Frenzied Flame Incantations available for players. Frenzied Flame Incantations can be found throughout the Land Betweens.\n\nBestial\n\nBlood\n\nDragon Communion\n\nDragon Cult\n\nErdtree\n\nFire Giant\n\nFire Monks\n\nFrenzied Flame\n\nGodskin Apostles\n\nGolden Order\n\nServants of Rot\n\nTwo Fingers\n\nFrenzied Flame Incantations info\n\nObtaining Frenzied Flame Incantations\n\nThere isn't a Book related to Frenzied Flame Incantations\nCheck the individual Incantations pages to learn about their locations.\n\nFrenzied Flame Incantations Requirements\n\nAs is the case with all Incantations, you must have a Sacred Seal equipped in order to be able to cast Frenzied Flame Incantations.\nFrenzied Flame Incantations require Faith to use.\n\nBoosting Frenzied Flame Incantations\n\nFrenzied Flame Seal that provides a 20% boost in damage for all Frenzied Flame Incantations, but does NOT buff the amount of Madness buildup they cause.\nCertain Frenzied Flame Incantations deal fire damage and receive damage buffs from the Fire Scorpion Charm (12%) and the Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear (20%) (Does not buff Howl of Shabriri or Inescapable Frenzy).\nAll Frenzied Flame Incantations cause Madness buildup, but their madness dealt only scales with the Arcane scaling from the Dragon Communion Seal\nFrenzied Flame incantations also inflict Madness on the user, which allows one to activate the effect of the Black Dumpling and increase ALL damage by 10% for 60 seconds\n\nAll Frenzied Flame Incantations\n\nFrenzied Burst\n\nHowl of Shabriri\n\nInescapable Frenzy\n\nThe Flame of Frenzy\n\nUnendurable Frenzy \n\n

QUESTION
Why do Frenzied Flame Incantations require Faith in order to be casted?

ANSWER
Frenzied Flame Incantations require Faith in order to be casted because they originate from the maddening Three Fingers and revolve around chaos and madness.
