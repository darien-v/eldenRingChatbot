Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the sorceries of the Carian royal family.\nCreates a defensive arch of magic glintblades overhead, whichautomatically attack nearby foes.This sorcery can be cast while in motion.\nBestowed upon the enchanted Carian knights, who combined thisart with their swordsmanship to maintain pressure upon theirfoes, striking in waves of steel and sorcery.\n\nWhere to find Glintblade Phalanx\nWhere to find Glintblade Phalanx:\n\nThe spell can be purchased for  2,500 Runes, after giving the Royal House Scroll to Miriel Pastor of Vows, Sorceress Sellen, or Preceptor Seluvis.\nThe Ash of War: Glintblade Phalanx is obtained by defeating Godrick the Grafted in Stormveil Castle and speaking to Rogier in Roundtable Hold. It's on his Rapier. Simply slot another Ashes of War there or select it and Remove Enchantment to free it up to use on a different Weapon.\n\nElden Ring Glintblade Phalanx Guide\n\nCarian Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 12\n Deals magic damage \n\n Each individual gintblade deals (Sorcery Scaling x 0.60) magic damage. \n Fires 5 gintblades for a total of (Sorcery Scaling x 3.00) magic damage. \n\nAble to track invisible enemies as well, what makes Phalanx a good choice to track invisible Teardrop Scarabs.\nPhalanx spells are a good way for mages to whittle down an enemy's stance.\nThis spell is generally outperformed by the Ash of War of the same name, but it can be useful in builds that use a weapon that can not be infused with Ashes of War\nThe blades will break upon coming into contact with any geometry, including enemies. This unfortunately means one cannot prep themselves with phalanx before passing through a boss fog gate, and must take care after casting to not accidentally break the blades on any surfaces to not waste any damage.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 20 to 18.\n\nMoonveil Shinobi Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Glintblade Phalanx Note and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is Glintblade Phalanx generally outperformed by the Ash of War of the same name in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Glintblade Phalanx is generally outperformed by the Ash of War variant in Elden Ring due to its utility in builds limited by weapon infusion options.
