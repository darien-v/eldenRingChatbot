The Godslayer's Seal scales primarily with Faith and Strength. The Godslayer's Seal is a good Weapon for any character that wishes to use Incantations. It also has a passive effect that gives a 10% boost to Godslayer Incantations.\n\nSacred seal of the Godskin Apostles, inlaid with obsidian.\nSaid to represent the manipulation of black flame, this catalyst enhances godslayer incantations.\n\nWhere to Find Godslayer's Seal in Elden Ring\nThe Godslayer's Seal weapon can be found at Stormveil Castle\n\nLocation: Stormveil Castle\nNearest Site of Grace: Rampart Tower  \nFrom the Rampart Tower grace, take the elevator down to the feasting hall where the Grafted Scion was roaming. Exit into the courtyard with all of the waiting archers. Make a hard left into a small stairway that goes underground. You'll find a room that is guarded by four Rats, and ahead is a doorway that is blocked by a white fog. To dispel the fog, you will need to use a Stonesword Key on the Imp Statue. You'll find a stockroom on the other side; the room has a treasure chest containing the seal. At 69+ Faith, the Erdtree Seal has a higher Spellbuff.\n\nGodslayer Incantations boosted by this seal include Black Flame, Black Flame Ritual, and Scouring Black Flame.\nThe Godslayer Incantations are very strong on a Faith build. They possess good scaling, availability, and good base damage, while having a DoT effect that scales with enemy HP. If the enemy is weak to fire, these incantations will make short work of them. QUESTION
Why is the Godslayer's Seal considered a good weapon for characters who wish to use Incantations in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Godslayer's Seal is considered a good weapon for characters who wish to use Incantations in Elden Ring due to its good scaling primarily with Faith and Strength, passive 10% boost to Godslayer Incantations, and strong incantations that possess good base damage and scaling.
