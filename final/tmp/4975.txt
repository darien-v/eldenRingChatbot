QUESTION
Why does the Bone Ballista Bolt drop much quicker than standard Ballista Bolts, making it harder to use at longer ranges?

ANSWER
The Bone Ballista Bolt drops much quicker than standard Ballista Bolts due to its design, making it harder to use at longer ranges.
