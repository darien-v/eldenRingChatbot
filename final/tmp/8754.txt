QUESTION
Why is it important to craft and use the Freezing Pot in Elden Ring, and how does it compare to other throwing pots in terms of effectiveness and attributes?

ANSWER
Crafting and using the Freezing Pot in Elden Ring is important due to its ability to cause 380 frost buildup, always inflicting the same amount regardless of attributes, and it requires an empty Ritual Pot to craft, distinguishing it from other throwing pots.
