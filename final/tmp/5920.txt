\n\n Spinning Strikes \n\n        Polearm skill that performs continuous spinning attacks. Hold to continue the attack. Can be followed up with a normal or strong attack. Nullifies projectiles such as arrows while spinning. \n\nSpinning Strikes is a Skill in Elden Ring. Spinning Strikes is a is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, it can be used on Polearms (Great Spears excepted). Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Spinning Strikes in Elden Ring\n\nAfter completing Irina's questline, Edgar will invade at the Revenger's Shack where he upon death will drop a Banished Knight's Halberd +8 with the Spinning Strikes Ash of War attached. The ash of war can be extracted from the weapon.\n\nElden Ring Spinning Strikes Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Spinning Strikes.\nFP Cost: 2\nStance damage: 6 (spin loop) 12 (Light attack finish) 16 (Heavy attack finish)\nLight Attack FP Cost: 10 \nHeavy Attack FP Cost: 15 \nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nFunctions similarly to Wild Strikes, but useable on Polearms. \n\nWild Strikes has higher poise, while Spinning Strikes has greater range and can attack in all directions\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: \n\nTime between various actions and activation of skills have been shortened.\nAttack Power has been slightly increased.\n\nSpinning Strikes Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient
