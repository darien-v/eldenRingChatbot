The Nox Monk Set is similar in appearance to the Nox Swordstress Set, with some pieces of the set being shared between them. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Nox Monk Set in Elden Ring\nGloves and greaves can be looted from the Nox Swordstress, Nox Priest as well as Nox Monk common enemies in Nokstella, Eternal City as they share the same armor pieces among each other. Nox Monk Hood and chestpiece however can only drop from Nox Monk enemies:\n\nTwo can be found in the ruins northwest of Nokstella, Eternal City site of grace.\nTwo can be found going up the stairs from Nokstella, Eternal City grace inside the imp statue locked room on your left and before the bridge connecting the buildings.\n\nNote that the Nox Swordstress & Nox Priest  boss found in Sellia, Town of Sorcery will not drop the set. Elden Ring Interactive Map link\n\nNox Monk Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nNox Monk Hood\nNox Monk Armor\nNox Bracelets\nNox Greaves\n\nNox Monk Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Nox Monk Hood can be altered to the Nox Monk Hood (Altered) to remove the hood.\nThe Nox Monk Armor can be altered to the Nox Monk Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nSee Nox Swordstress Set for a similar armor.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
