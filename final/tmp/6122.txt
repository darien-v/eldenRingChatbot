\n\n Onyx Lord's Repulsion \n\n        Thrust the armament into the ground to create a gravity well. In addition to dealing damage, this attack sends enemies flying away. \n\nOnyx Lord's Repulsion is a Skill in Elden Ring. Onyx Lord's Repulsion is a Unique skill found on the Onyx Lord's Greatsword, a powerful weapon with an added Magic Damage stat to complement this skill. This not only deals damage to nearby enemies, but also allows the user to distance himself when overwhelmed with enemies by manipulating gravity. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Onyx Lord's Repulsion\n\nDefault skill on the Onyx Lord's Greatsword.\n\nElden Ring Onyx Lord's Repulsion Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 27\nDeals 24 stance damage.\nThis Skill  is not Chargeable.\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nWearing Spellblade Set increases the damage of the explosion only as it deals 100% Magic Damage\nNote:
