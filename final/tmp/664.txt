\n\n Lakeside Crystal Cave \n\nHidden on the southwestern cliffs of Liurnia of the Lakes, a winding cave carved by large crystals.\n\nLakeside Crystal Cave is a Location in Elden Ring. The Lakeside Crystal Cave is found within the southern region of Liurnia of the Lakes, directly south from the Scenic Isle Site of Grace.\n\nLakeside Crystal Cave Map\nThe cave is located on South-Western Liurnia, carved among the cliffs by the shore. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Lakeside Crystal Cave\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nArteria Leaf\nCave Moss\nBudding Cave Moss\nCrystal Cave Moss\nSilver Firefly\nSoft Cotton\nHefty Beast Bone\nThrowing Dagger\nLump of Flesh\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nSmithing Stone (4)\nSmithing Stone (2)\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nSpear Talisman\nThrowing Dagger\nCerulean Amber Medallion (boss drop)\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no uniques or ashes here\n\nElden Ring Lakeside Crystal Cave Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nCrystal Snail\nDemi-Human\nLesser Demi-Human Chief\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nBloodhound Knight  \n\nLakeside Crystal Cave Walkthrough\nAfter entering the cave you will find yourself in a large open area and in front of the grace is a chest containing an Arteria Leaf. Stick to your left and jump up the hill to clear out the Demi-Humans. Follow the pathway along the cliff as normal however, don't drop down into the chasm. Enter the room at the end of the cliff, kill the Demi-Human Chieftain inside the room and open the chest containing the Spear Talisman. Afterwards, head back and to the middle of the cliff to find an area to drop down to. Once you are at the bottom, you will enter a room containing 3 Crystal Snails and another room with many more, but these will take some time to attack. Drop down again to find Soft Cotton x2 on a body on the ledge and then drop down to grab a Smithing Stone (2), Smithing Stone (4) looted from two more corpses, and a Lump of Flesh. Once you proceed, you will get ambushed by three more snails. Turn left to fight the Boss, Bloodhound Knight. He is very agile and can close gaps quickly. Upon his Death he drops the Cerulean Amber Medallion.\nAfter defeating the boss, continue through the arena to find the Slumbering Wolf's Shack. You can meet Latenna here and there is a Rune Arc behind the shack.\n\nElden Ring Lakeside Crystal Cave Gallery and Notes\n\nAfter defeating the Bloodhound Knight boss, players may proceed to QUESTION
Why is the Lakeside Crystal Cave in Elden Ring a significant location for players to explore?

ANSWER
The Lakeside Crystal Cave in Elden Ring is significant for players to explore because it houses unique items like the Cerulean Amber Medallion, offers challenges with various enemies like Crystal Snails and the Bloodhound Knight boss, and leads to the Slumbering Wolf's Shack site of Grace through which players can meet NPC Latenna and find the Rune Arc.
