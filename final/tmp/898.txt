\n\n East Windmill Pasture \n\nEast Pasture Outskirsts of Windmill Village. True to its name, it can be identified by the still-working windmill placed in the center of the pasture. \n\n East Windmill Pasture is a Location in Elden Ring. The East Windmill Pasture is found in the Altus Plateau, in the North-most limits of the area. It is very close to the West Windmill Pasture, just slightly north-east of it.\n\n East Windmill Pasture Map\nThe East Windmill Pasture is found in the Altus Plateau, in the North-most limits of the area. QUESTION
How can players effectively clear the enemies in the East Windmill Pasture in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can clear the enemies in the East Windmill Pasture by defeating Celebrants and Burning Putrid Corpses, being cautious of the latter's exploding ability, and looting the bodies for valuable items like Noble's Traveling Garb and Navy Hood.
