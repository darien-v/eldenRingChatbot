\n\n Tombsward Ruins \n\nThe ruins of a former burial ground. Next to nothing remains here, yet squires still roam the dark tunnels beneath. Perhaps protecting the graves of their former noble masters...\n\nTombsward Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Tombsward Ruins is found in the western landmass of the Weeping Peninsula, towards the north. These ruins are easily accessible on foot or on horseback as it is tucked in an open field just past the center of the peninsula.\n\nTombsward Ruins Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Tombsward Ruins\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Tombsward Ruins\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nBeast Liver\nErdleaf Flower\nRowa Fruit\nTrina's Lily\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location \n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nWinged Scythe\nPage Hood\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this location\n\nElden Ring Tombsward Ruins Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nRat\nPage\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no Bosses at this location\n\nTombsward Ruins Walkthrough\nThere isn't much to see at these ruins. Above ground, there are a couple of Rats feasting on corpses, one of which contains a Beast Liver crafting material. At the center of the ruins, there is a set of stairs that leads underground to a dark hall protected by two Page enemies. Be sure to bring a Torch or lead the Pages outside. Watch out for their quick two-hit thrust and two-hit slash attacks. They can also shoot at you from a distance using their crossbows. At the end of the dark hall is a small room containing a chest. Inside, you will find the Winged Scythe weapon.\n\nElden Ring Tombsward Ruins Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy Crystal
