CONTEXT
\n\n Wave of Gold \n\n        Imbue the sword with bygone golden glory, then fire it at foes. \n       A wide, golden wave fans out forwards, sweeping through all enemies caught in its path. \n\nWave of Gold is a Skill in Elden Ring. Wave of Gold is a special skill that is only available for the Sacred Relic Sword, and it cannot be applied on other weapons via Ashes of War.\n\nHow to get Wave of Gold in Elden Ring\n\nDefault skill for the Sacred Relic Sword.\n\nElden Ring Wave of Gold Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 42\nDeals 0 stance damage.\nThis Skill is not Chargeable.\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus\n\nDeals 100% Holy Damage, which scales only on Faith (not Strength nor Dexterity).\n2 second charge, Long range, widens with range, and deals slightly less damage around 20% at longer ranges.\nThe following effects will buff the damage of this skill: \n\nGolden Vow Skill/Ash of War increases damage by 12% for 45 seconds, the Golden Vow spell increases damage by 15% for 90 seconds.\nHoly-Shrouding Cracked Tear increases damage by 20% for 3 minutes.\nSacred Scorpion Charm increases damage by 12%.\nWarrior Jar Shard increases damage by 10%, the higher version Shard of Alexander increases damage by 15%.\nRitual Sword Talisman increases damage by 10%.\nThe damage buffs above stack multiplicatively for a total of 1.15 * 1.2 * 1.12 * 1.15 * 1.1 = 195.5%, almost doubling the base damage.\n\nRotten Winged Sword Insignia and similar items are not affected, as the attacks are not counted as “consecutive”.\nThis list of damage enhancing talismans/skills/spells may not be exhaustive.\nUseful for farming Albinaurics upon reaching Mohgwyn Palace, giving an excellent runes/minute rate. \nNote: As of Patch 1.09, the damage and poise damage decreases when hitting an opponent over a certain distance in PVP only\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nSword Saint Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient QUESTION
Why is Wave of Gold only available for the Sacred Relic Sword and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Wave of Gold is only available for the Sacred Relic Sword and cannot be applied to other weapons via Ashes of War in Elden Ring because it is a special skill uniquely tied to that sword.
