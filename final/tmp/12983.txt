QUESTION
Why is Beast Claw considered to work well in low-Faith, high-Strength builds in conjunction with the Clawmark Seal as an efficient ranged attack option?

ANSWER
Beast Claw deals slash damage, has efficient ranged attacks, and is best suited for low-Faith, high-Strength builds.
