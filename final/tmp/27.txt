CONTEXT
\n\n Morgott's Great Rune \n\nType\nGreat Rune\n\nEffect \n\n        Greatly raises maximum HP \n\nMorgott's Great Rune is a Great Rune in Elden Ring. Morgott's Great Rune raises maximum HP. Great Runes in Elden Ring are special items dropped by Demigod Bosses that can be equipped to acquire special passive bonuses.\n\nA Great Rune of the shardbearer Morgott.Its blessing greatly raises maximum HP.\nThis Great Rune is the anchor ring that houses the base, and proves two things:\nThat the Omen King was born of the golden lineage, and that he was indeed the Lord of Leyndell.\n\nWhere to find Morgott's Great Rune in Elden Ring\nDropped as a reward for defeating Morgott, the Omen King located at found in Leyndell, Royal Capital. Map Link\n\nElden Ring Morgott's Great Rune Use\nIf you want to use Morgott's Great Rune, you will need to bring it to the Divine Tower of East Altus, found after the Forbidden Lands elevator in the Capital Outskirts.\n\nElden Ring Morgott's Great Rune Notes & Tips\n\nIncreases health by 25%. This multiplier gets applied after all other health increasing buffs, equipment, and spells. \n\nGodrick's rune will provide more health if your base vigor is between 6-14, inclusive. \n\nElden Ring Great Runes\n\nGodrick's Great Rune  ♦  Great Rune of the Unborn  ♦  Malenia's Great Rune  ♦  Mending Rune of Perfect Order  ♦  Mending Rune of the Death-Prince  ♦  Mending Rune of the Fell Curse  ♦  Mohg's Great Rune  ♦  Phantom Great Rune  ♦  Radahn's Great Rune  ♦  Rykard's Great Rune\n\n

QUESTION
How can I efficiently utilize Morgott's Great Rune in Elden Ring to maximize its effect on my character's maximum HP?

ANSWER
Bring Morgott's Great Rune to the Divine Tower of East Altus to maximize its effect on maximum HP, increasing it by 25% after all other health buffs, with Godrick's rune providing more health if base vigor is between 6-14,inclusive.
