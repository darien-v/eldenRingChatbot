CONTEXT
\n\n Phantom Slash \n\n        Skill inspired by the fond remembrances of the Night's Cavalry. Creates an apparition of the knights' former instructor who guides a joint lunging upward swing. Additional input allows for a follow-up attack. \n\nPhantom Slash is a Skill in Elden Ring. Phantom Slash is a is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, it can also be used on Polearms and Twinblades (Great Spears excepted). Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Phantom Slash in Elden Ring\n\nDropped by a Night's Cavalry northeast the Forbidden Lands Grace in the Forbidden Lands. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Phantom Slash Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Phantom Slash.\nCan barely change direction when used.\nFP Cost: 8\nDeals 9+9 (Total 18) stance damage\nThis Skill is Not Chargeable\nBoth of this Skill's attacks can be Parried (the Phantom cannot be Parried)\nThe range of both the phantom's and your own attacks will always be the same, regardless how close or far an enemy is. Due to this, the player must place themselves at the optimal range to land both hits. Better used on slower targets. \nThe phantom wears the Night's Cavalry Set and uses the Nightrider Glaive.\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nDirectional control has been improved.\nDelay between the end of various actions (such as using items or attack animations) and being able to perform the skill has been reduced.\n\nBlackflame Bushido Written Guide\n\nPhantom
