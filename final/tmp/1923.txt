CONTEXT
\n\n Opaline Hardtear \n\nType\nCrystal Tear\n\nEffect \n\n        Temporarily boosts all damage negations in physick \n\nOpaline Hardtear is a Crystal Tear in Elden Ring. Crystal Tears can be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick to enhance its effect.\n\nA crystal tear formed slowly over ages where the Erdtree's bounty falls to the ground.\nCan be mixed in the Flask of Wondrous Physick.The resulting concoction temporarily boosts all forms of damage negation.\n\nWhere to find Opaline Hardtear\n\nDropped by Putrid Avatar next to the Minor Erdtree north of Fort Faroth in Caelid. [Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Opaline Hardtear Notes & Tips\n\nIncreases all damage negation types by 15% for 3 minutes. \n\nThe bonus is reduced to 10% in PvP.\n\nCan stack with other sources of protections (talismans, consumables) \n\nWhen paired with Boiled Crab and either Dragoncrest Shield Talisman or Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman can grant - with proper armor - even 60% damage negation towards physical-type damage.\n\nYou can find Crystal Tears at the bases of Minor Erdtrees, and elsewhere throughout the Lands Between.\nAt sites of grace, you can mix two crystal tears in the Flask of Wondrous Physick. QUESTION
Why is Opaline Hardtear's damage negation bonus reduced to 10% in PvP in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The damage negation bonus of Opaline Hardtear is reduced to 10% in PvP in Elden Ring to balance its effectiveness in player versus player combat.
