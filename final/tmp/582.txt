This Evergaol does not require a Stonesword Key to unlock. The portal mechanism in the center is active as soon as you enter the area. Enter the portal to challenge the boss within: Bols, Carian Knight.\nBols is a Troll Knight clad in Carian armor, wielding a massive greatsword. Due to his size, his movements are rather slow and his attacks have a hefty weight behind them. He compensates for this by frequently roaring, which not only increases his damage, but releases a burst of pressure that staggers players. Be careful as he tends to combo into his attacks after a roar. Bols is tall enough that you can easily and safely dodge under his legs to avoid most of his attacks, but never take your eyes off him as he can quickly turn around for a devastating follow-up.\nBols drops the Greatblade Phalanx spell upon defeat.\n\nElden Ring Cuckoo's Evergaol Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy Crystal
