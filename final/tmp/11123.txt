\n\n Miniature Ranni \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Doll resembling Ranni the Witch \n\nMiniature Ranni is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Miniature Ranni is a Quest Item. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nA doll resembling Ranni the Witch.From head to toe every detail is perfect.\nThis unresponsive doll seems pleasantly cool.\n\nAfter defeating Baleful Shadow the icon and description of Miniature Ranni will change:\n\nA doll resembling Ranni the Witch.From head to toe every detail is perfect.\nThe chilliness is gone, feeling now like an empty husk.\nThere is no response.\n\nWhere to find Miniature Ranni in Elden Ring\n\nFound in an open stone coffin in the waters of Ainsel River Main. [Map Link]\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Miniature Ranni Use\nMiniature Ranni can be spoken to at Ainsel River Main Site of Grace, she will respond after talking 3 times. After persisting on speaking to it, Ranni speaks back through the doll and asks you to eliminate a Baleful Shadow, which is located just beyond the Nokstella Waterfall Basin grace point. After doing so, you will be given the Discarded Palace Key.\nYou can also talk to her at the Nokstella, Eternal City and Nokstella Waterfall Basin Sites of Grace before defeating the Baleful Shadow. Note that it is possible to begin the conversation with her at any of these sites of grace. Once you've talked to her at a new grace, her dialogue will remain the same as the most recent grace's dialogue, even if you return to the previous graces you talked to her at.\nAt the end of Ranni's questline, she can be spoken to again at Ranni's Chamber Site of Grace in Ranni's Rise.\n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: Miniature Ranni\nSecond conversation at Nosktella Waterfall Basin\n\nAch, this form hath loosened my tongue.I've let slip too much.Forget what thou'st heard. Forget.\n\nElden Ring Miniature Ranni Notes & Tips\n\nYou may have to have explored Nokstella, Eternal City and be sitting at the Nokstella Waterfall Basin Site of Grace to talk to her.\nPer anonymous
