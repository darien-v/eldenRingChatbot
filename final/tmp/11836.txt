QUESTION
Why do Giant Skeletal Slimes in Elden Ring remain hostile even if not attacked?

ANSWER
Giant Skeletal Slimes in Elden Ring remain hostile even if not attacked due to their nature, despite their intimidating size, as they do not move around much and do not deal significant damage.
