\n\n Dragoncrest Shield Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.8\n\nEffect \n\n Reduces  \n       Physical Damage taken 10/13/17% \n\nDragoncrest Shield Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Dragoncrest Shield Talisman reduces Physical Damage taken by 10/13/17%. Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman is the direct upgrade to this talisman. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nWrought iron talisman depicting a trio of ancient dragons.Boosts physical damage negation.\nThe ancient dragons, who ruled in the prehistoric era before the Erdtree, would protect their lord as a wall of living rock.And so it is that the shape of the dragon has become symbolic of all manner of protections.\n\nDragoncrest Shield Talisman in Elden Ring\nDragoncrest Shield Talisman reduces Physical Damage taken by 10/13/17%. (Effect reduced to 2/3/4%, respectively, in PvP.)\n\nWhere to find Dragoncrest Shield Talisman in Elden Ring\n\nBase talisman: Bestial Sanctum: Found on the bottom level of the structure below the surrounding cliffs. [Map link]\n+1 Variant: Sainted Hero's Grave: Can be found in a locked imp's room, behind a Stonesword Key fog wall (not the first one you encounter; the Crimson Seed Talisman is located behind that one). When you reach the area with the big axes stomping the ground, you need to get on the first one and jump on the ledge on the left wall. There are multiple Putrid Corpses along this path and in the room with the fog doors, alongside a pair of Wraith Callers. Once you've dealt with them and opened the fog door, there are a final pair of Putrids watching over the talisman.   [Map link]\n+2 Variant: Crumbling Farum Azula: Looted from a corpse on floating chunk of debris in the northern section, between Dragon Temple Lift and Dragon Temple Rooftop. holding a large number of runes is not recommended.\nLook down over the edge of the cliffs to the west of the Bestial Sanctum building to find tree roots that lead you down below the Sanctum.\nBefore descending from the cliffs, you can benefit greatly from a moderate supply of Soft Cotton, as without them several of the falls required will leave you vulnerable to the flying enemies in the
