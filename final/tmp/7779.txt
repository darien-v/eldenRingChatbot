CONTEXT
\nThrusting Swords are a type of Weapon in Elden Ring. Thrusting Swords strike a balance between moderate damage, fast attacks, and good reach, while piercing and slashing Enemies.\nThrusting Swords can be modified by a number of Ashes of War to change their Skills or Damage Type, so play around with them and see what you like.\nThrusting Swords and Curved Swords have a built-in feint. Press dodge shortly after beginning the charge-up for a heavy attack, and another animation will happen. Some weapons (such as the Frozen Needle) shoot a projectile during this animation, allowing for additional methods of play.\n\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08, the speed of Thrusting Swords' crouching attacks has been reduced. Poise damage has also been reduced, but only for PVP.\n\nElden Ring Thrusting Sword Range Comparison\n\nWeapon Range Data for Whips in Elden Ring.
