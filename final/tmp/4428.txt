\n\n Ash of War: Sword Dance \n\nAffinity\nKeen\n\nSkill\nSword Dance\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Sword Dance is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Sword Dance provides Keen affinity and the Sword Dance Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Keen affinity and the following skill:\n"Sword Dance: Quickly close in to perform a series of spinning upward slashes. Follow up with an additional input to finish with a downward slash."\nUsable on swords, axes, and polearms capable of slashing (colossal weapons and great spears excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Sword Dance Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Sword Dance:\n\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab found northwest of the Minor Erdtree in southwest Liurnia, below the cliffs leading to a path where several jellyfish are located. From the Revenger's Shack site of grace, head slightly east until you reach a dirt road. Take that road south. When you see the cliff with the Minor Erdtree on it, the dirt road will fork into two directions. Take the road leading west and it will take you by a statue and bend around the cliff. You will see jellyfish on the road and the scarab will be just a little further down in the middle of the road. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nAsh of War: Sword Dance Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Sword Dance.\nProvides the Keen affinity, which increases Dexterity scaling, decreases Strength and Base damage.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Axes, Greataxes, Spears, Halberds, Reapers\n\nSword Dance Demonstration\n\nBuilds with Ash of War: Sword Dance \n\nBlackflame Spellblade Written Guide\n\nCrusader Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Sword Dance Notes & Tips\n\nThe initial skill input will make the user spin forward a moderate distance and hit twice. An aditional input will slam the armament to the ground, hitting again.\nDistance covered is the same, regardless of weapon class.\nThe speed of both inputs in relation to the attacks can vary. This is based on the weapon class of the armament in question that has the skill. \n\n"Small" armaments such as Daggers, Straight Swords, Curved Swords, Katanas and Axes have overall fast speed on both inputs. There's almost no delay between the input and the attacks.\n"Large" armaments such as Greatswords, Twinblades, Greataxes, Spears, Halberds and Scythes (Reapers) have a slight attack delay on the first input, and a longer, considerable delay, on the second input.\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric Roar  ♦  Ash of War: Barrage  ♦  Ash of War: QUESTION
Why might someone want to acquire the Ash of War: Sword Dance in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To acquire the Ash of War: Sword Dance in Elden Ring, someone might want it because it provides the Keen affinity which increases Dexterity scaling, grants the Sword Dance skill usable with various weapon categories, and offers a unique series of spinning slashes attack.
