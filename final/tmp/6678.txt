\n\n Memory Stone \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Increases memory slots \n\nMemory Stone is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Memory Stone increases Memory Slots. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nA black, lightly beguiling stone. Prized by the sorcerers who produce them.\nIncreases memory slots.\nSaid to be a fragment of the black moon that once hung above the Eternal City.\n\nWhere to find Memory Stones in Elden Ring\nYou can get up to 8 Memory Stones.\nWhere to find:\n\nFound in a chest at the top of Oridys's Rise in Weeping Peninsula. QUESTION
Why are Memory Stones in Elden Ring passive and do not require the player to equip them to increase available memory slots?

ANSWER
Memory Stones in Elden Ring are passive and do not require equipping because once obtained, they passively increase available memory slots, unlike the Moon of Nokstella Talisman.
