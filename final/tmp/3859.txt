\n\n Sleep Pot \n\nEffect \n\n        Uses \n       FP. Throw to cause buildup of  \n       Sleep\n\nScaling \n\n        Arc C \n\nFP Cost\n9\n\nSleep Pot is a Consumable Item in Elden Ring. It can be thrown at enemies to inflict Sleep, and requires an empty Cracked Pot to craft.\n\nCraftable item prepared using a cracked pot. Enchanted by the incantations of St. Trina.  Consumes FP. Throw enemies to cause buildup of sleep.  Like a lullaby, or a quagmire, its light-purple haze irresistibly draws its victims down into sleep. Sweet dreams.\n\nSleep Pot use in Elden Ring\nSleep Pot uses FP. Throw to inflict Sleep.\n\nHas a base of 29 sleep build-up that scales with Arcane. Build-up is applied per tick for 15 ticks over three seconds, and will accumulate as the opponent stands within the sleep mist. Note that PvE enemies will regenerate about 15 sleep build-up from the beginning to the end of the pot's duration.\n10 Arcane: 30 sleep build-up per tick, for 450 total.\n30 Arcane: 38 sleep build-up per tick, for 570 total.\n60 Arcane: 40 sleep build-up per tick, for 600 total.\n99 Arcane: 42 sleep build-up per tick, for 630 total.\n\nSleep Pot Crafting Guide\nTo craft Sleep Pots, you need the Fevor's Cookbook [1] as well as the following Crafting Materials:\n\nMushroom x1\nTrina's Lily x1\nEmpty Cracked Pot x1\n\nSleep Pot Notes & Tips\n\nYou can hold up to 20 Sleep Pots, depending on how many Cracked Pots you have.\nYou can store up to 600 Sleep Pots.\nSell value: Sleep Pots cannot be sold.\nOnce a Sleep Pot is thrown, its Cracked Pot will be available for crafting another throwing pot.\n\nElden Ring Consumables\n\nAcademy Magic Pot  ♦  Acid Spraymist  ♦  Aeonian Butterfly  ♦  Albinauric Pot  ♦  Alluring Pot  ♦  Altus Bloom  ♦  Ancient Dragonbolt Pot  ♦  Baldachin's
