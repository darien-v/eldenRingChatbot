QUESTION
Why is the Black Bow considered a good weapon for Bow Builds in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Black Bow in Elden Ring is considered a good weapon for Bow Builds due to its scaling primarily with Strength and Dexterity, and its versatility in allowing quick shooting after rolling and sprinting.
