QUESTION
Why does the Mushroom Crown increase attack power when something nearby suffers from poison or rot in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Mushroom Crown increases attack power when something nearby suffers from poison or rot in Elden Ring because it raises damage by +10% for 20 seconds after you or an enemy experience Poison or Scarlet Rot, stacking with other poison-induced buffs.
