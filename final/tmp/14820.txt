\n\n Grape Spirit \n\nLocation\nStormveil Castle\n\nRole\nBenevolent\n\nThe Grape Spirit is an NPC in Elden Ring. Located in Stormveil Castle. He is looking for a maiden to give his grapes to and seems to have lost one, since a Shabiri Grape can be found beside him.\n\n    ... Maiden, dear maiden, where are you? Please, take my grapes.\n\nGrape Spirit Location in Elden Ring\nThe Grape Spirit can be found after the Throne Room once Godrick has been defeated.\n\nThis NPC does not move and can only be talked to.\nThis spirit can't be fought\n\nDialogue in Elden Ring: Grape Spirit\n\n "...Maiden, dear maiden, where are you?Please, take my grapes." \n\nElden Ring Grape Spirit Notes & Trivia\n\nJudging from the position he is found in and, the way he is holding his hands over his eye sockets, it can be assumed that this spirit has removed his own eyes. This would explain why he can't find the maiden and why there is a Shabiri Grape next to him.\nThe maiden he is searching for could be Hyetta. Since she can be found just outside the door (providing
