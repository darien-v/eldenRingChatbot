Why, O guidance of grace, will this door not open?" \n\nLimgrave Colosseum is a Location in Elden Ring. The Limgrave Colosseum is located in Limgrave and can be reached by following the path north of Warmaster's Shack.\n\nAs of December 7th, 2022, the closed Limgrave Colosseum can be opened after installing the Colosseum Update\n\nLimgrave Colosseum Map \n\nIt can be reached by following the path north of Warmaster's Shack. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nUnlocking the Limgrave Colosseum\nThe Location should be easy for players to find as it is not far from the start of the game, so it is likely that this would be the first colosseum players will find.\nPlayers will need to head up through Limgrave up Stormhill and to the Warmaster's Shack Site of Grace. From there, players will need to head north and the Colosseum on an empty plateau. Access to Combat Ordeal and United Combat, as well as the Limgrave arena venue will be given to players upon reaching the Colosseum.\n\nNPCs and Merchants in Limgrave Colosseum\n\nFrustrated Spirit can be found at the front of the Colosseum.\n\nLimgrave Colosseum PvP\nThe Limgrave Colosseum features two modes: United Combat and Combat Ordeal.\n\nIn United Combat, combatants will be divided into two teams to fight in timed battles with the ability to respawn. Each death and respawn grants points to the adversaries, offering victory to the team with the higher score.\nIn Combat Ordeal, it’s every Tarnished for themselves. Combatants in this mode can also respawn until the timer hits zero, and the one with the most points will prevail as victor.\n\nALL ITEMS IN Limgrave COLOSSEUM\n\nGATHER & FARM ITEMS\n\nItems go QUESTION
How can players unlock the closed Limgrave Colosseum in Elden Ring after installing the Colosseum Update?

ANSWER
Players can unlock the closed Limgrave Colosseum in Elden Ring after installing the Colosseum Update by heading north from Warmaster's Shack in Limgrave, reaching the Colosseum on an empty plateau, and accessing Combat Ordeal and United Combat modes.
