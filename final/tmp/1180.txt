\n\n Apostate Derelict \n\nA ruined church lying in the frozen north-westof the Consecrated Grounds\n\nApostate Derelict is a Location in Elden Ring. The Apostate Derelict is found in Consecrated Snowfield. It can be reached by following the frozen river in the region northwest, taking the hill on the west side just before it ends and going past the Wandering Mausoleum. Players should approach with caution, as the nearby Wandering Mausoleum fires magical artillery into the air, which while not explicitly aimed or homing, can be dangerous to the player.\n\nApostate Derelict Map\nThe Apostate Derelict is an old church found past the Wandering Mausoleum in the northwest tip of the Consecrated Snowfield. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Apostate Derelict\n\nPhillia, Towering Little Sister - This woman serves as the final location for Latenna's questline.
