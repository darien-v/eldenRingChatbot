QUESTION
Why is the Godfrey Icon in Elden Ring specifically designed to only affect charged Spells and Skills by 15% but not Stance damage or Bow attacks?

ANSWER
The Godfrey Icon in Elden Ring is specifically designed to only affect charged Spells and Skills by 15% but not Stance damage or Bow attacks to maintain balance and game mechanics.
