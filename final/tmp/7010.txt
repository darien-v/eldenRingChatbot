These creatures are Non-Boss variants of the Mad Pumpkin Head, a mutated large humanoid wearing a Pumpkin Helm covering its head.\nMad Pumpkin Heads can be summoned as spirits for aid by using the Mad Pumpkin Head Ashes.\n\n Though he has stifled his panic within the dark confines of his helmet, he rampages as if driven mad when agitated by bloodshed or the humming of insects.\nThis mad soldier is all that is left of a broken gladiator.\n\nElden Ring Lesser Mad Pumpkin Head Locations\nWhere to Find Lesser Mad Pumpkin Head:\n\nCastle Morne\nAltus Plateau - By a grave marker overlooking the cliffs northeast of the Bridge of Iniquity.\nRaya Lucaria Academy\nFort Haight - Guarding the entrance of the fort.\nLimgrave. The one with flail can inflict blood loss.\nRarely, the one wielding standard may use it like a throwing spear, dealing Pierce Damage. Usually, they stick it into the ground and switch to regular hammer.\nStrong point: Pumpkin Head's - attacks there are heavily (80%) reduced in power, plus some weapons will even bounce off if not two-handed. Use your weapon's moveset wisely to avoid hitting the helmet.\nLike their boss counterparts, the Lesser Pumpkin Heads will enter a frenzy state upon being awoken from the Sleep  effect by a non-repost attack. The Pumpkin Head will appear to stare skywards before pounding its chest three times and proceeding to attack its allies for a limited time.
