\n\n Avionette Soldier Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 67\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summon the spirits of two avionette soldiers \n\nAvionette Soldier Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Avionette Soldier Ashes summon the spirits of two avionette soldiers equipped with long-hafted scythes, that can also execute aerial attacks.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of two avionette soldiers. Spirits of marionette soldiers with avian features that were created to serve a sorcerer.Equipped with long-hafted scythes, they also attack from the skies by lobbing fire pots. Can sometimes malfunction when damaged. \n\nWhere to find Avionette Soldier Ashes\nWhere to Find Avionette Soldier Ashes\n\nLocated on a corpse after taking the rotating lift down in Raya Lucaria Academy. From the Schoolhouse Classroom site of grace, go back outside to the large lift and ride it down. Face north and jump off the lift early onto a rocky platform.
