\n\n Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater \n\nLocation\n Smoldering Church, Limgrave Corpse-Stench Shack, Mt. Gelmir Consecrated Snowfield \n\nAnastasia, Tarnished-Eater is an NPC Invader Enemy in Elden Ring. Anastasia first reveals herself as players approach the Smoldering Church and becomes a recurring adversary as players progress.\n\n The knife-wielding Ogress Anastasia, known to have eaten countless Tarnished while disguised as a Finger Maiden.\n\nElden Ring Anastasia Locations\nWhere to Find Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater:\n\nRight in front of the Smoldering Church in Caelid. [Map Link]\nAs you approach the Corpse-Stench Shack, at the end of the road leading to Mt. Gelmir. [Map Link]\nIn the Consecrated Snowfield, along the frozen river southwest of Ordina, Liturgical Town, on the straight-line path between Ordina and Yelough Anix Ruins. The best landmark is a corpse sitting in a chair on the river's south bank holding a Stonesword Key. QUESTION
Who is Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater and what landmarks are associated with her locations?

ANSWER
Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater, an NPC Invader in Elden Ring, is a knife-wielding Ogress who disguises as a Finger Maiden and invades at the Smoldering Church, Corpse-Stench Shack at Mt. Gelmir, and Consecrated Snowfield near a corpse with a Stonesword Key landmark.
