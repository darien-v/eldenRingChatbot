Black Blade spell throws a drill attack emitting a wave of light. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nPower gleaned from the remembrance of Maliketh.\nCaster creates an illusory black blade, then leaps forward to deliver a spinning slash that emits a wave of light. This can be followed up with one additional attack.\nThis blade was once imbued with Destined Death. In addition to dealing damage, it reduces foes' maximum HP and continues to sap their current HP for a very short time.\n\nWhere to find Black Blade\nWhere to find Black Blade:\n\nTrade Remembrance of the Black Blade with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Black Blade Guide\n\nErdtree Incantation\nStamina Cost: 44\nNote: In Patch 1.07, the stamina cost has been reduced to 44 from 51. Improved turning performance and shortened recovery time. The sword and the wave attack have been changed so that they hit at the same time. Attack power, stamina attack power and poise damage of each part have been decreased. Decreased the number of times the wave part hits large enemies.(This incantation is currently missing its follow up attack when cast from the left hand. This issue will be corrected in a future update.)\nReduces enemy max HP by 10% for 15 seconds.\nSaps enemy HP for 2 seconds\nDeals 10 stance damage\nThe damage type is 100% Holy Damage. The sapping damage can be increased by Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear and Sacred Scorpion Charm, as well as other generic attack buffs like Golden Vow, etc.\nDebuff stacks with either the Destined Death OR Blade of Death but NOT both.\nBlack Blade performs a spinning slash that emits a vertical wave that passes through enemies. The follow-up spin and its wave is angled diagonally. \n\nSee Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking debuffs.\n\nCrusader Written Guide\n\nBlack Blade Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Black Blade Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can players effectively incorporate Black Blade into their builds in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can effectively incorporate Black Blade into their builds by trading Remembrance of the Black Blade with Enia at Roundtable Hold and utilizing its powerful spinning slash attack with Holy Damage and debuff stacking capabilities.
