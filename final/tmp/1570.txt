Godskin Apostle is not as aggresive as Godskin Noble, and you can notice it more when you fight him alone. He can be staggered which spells giving you lots of chances to perform comfortable melee attacks. At the next stage, he starts casting lots of area spells, and it's convinient to have a good spirit ash ally tanking him out so you can keep distance. Godskin Noble appears after defeating the Apostle, and also appears in spirit form. On it's first stage, The Noble is not very good at ranged attacks, but it is very awkward to fight him at melee distance. He walks towards you very quickly and aggresively all the time. You can usee the trees around the arena to pivot around and gain more space. Godskin alos gets staggered and kneels down to take a breath, giving you a chance to perform a critical attack on him. At the next stage, it starts moving awkwardly for the player, it's very hard to predict his movement when it starts rolling around. The magic area attacks are very predictable and you only have to dodge back and stay away until the move finishes. QUESTION
What are the best strategies for defeating the Godskin Apostle & Godskin Noble bosses in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
For Godskin Apostle, stagger and use melee attacks, while for Godskin Noble, use the environment for spacing and dodge magic area attacks to defeat them efficiently.
