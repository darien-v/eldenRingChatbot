QUESTION
Why should players use Sentry's Torch when fighting invisible Black Knife Assassin and Lesser Black Knife Assassin in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players should use Sentry's Torch when fighting invisible Black Knife Assassin and Lesser Black Knife Assassin in Elden Ring to make the assassins visible with the light cast from the torch.
