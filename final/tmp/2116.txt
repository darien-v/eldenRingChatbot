\n\n Magic Defense \n\nType\nDefensive Stat\n\nEffect\nShows your current defense against Magic attacks\n\nMagic Defense is a Defensive Stat in Elden Ring. Magic Defense shows your defense power and damage negation against magical attacks. Stats determine the strengths and weaknesses of your character. Defensive Stats are determined by your attributes and Equipment. \nMagic is very commonly used in PvP, so increasing Magic Defense is key for PvP builds.\n\nMagic Defense Elden Ring Guide\n\nYour defense power and damage negation against magical attacks.\nThe former is calculated via attributes, while the latter is calculated via defensive gear and active passive effects.\n\nElden Ring How to Increase Character Magic Defense\n\nMagic Defense can be increased by assigning points to Intelligence.\n\nRelated Equipment & Items in Elden Ring for Magic Defense\nYou can use the following Equipment and Items to increase your Magic Damage Negation:\n\nSpelldrake Talisman (13%/17%/20% based on variant).\nPearldrake Talisman (5%/7%/9% based on variant).\nLusat's Set has the best total Magic Damage Negation.\nRoyal Knight Set has one of the best total Magic Damage Negation while having high Poise.\n\nElden Ring VS Magic Resistance Notes & Tips\n\nDefense increase by Rune Level and Intelligence level: \n\nThe soft caps for Rune Level are 71, 91, and 161\nThe soft caps for Intelligence are 20, 35, and 60\nThe formulas for calculating Magic Defense by Rune Level are as follows: \n\nLevel 1 - 71: 40 + 60*((Lvl+79 - 1) / 149)\nLevel 72 - 91: 100 + 20*((Lvl+79 - 150) / 20)\nLevel 92 - 161: 120 + 15*((Lvl+79 - 170) / 70)\nLevel 162 - 713: 135 + 20*((Lvl+79 - 240) / 552)\n\nThe formulas for calculating Magic Defense by Intelligence are as follows: \n\nLevel 1 - 20: 40*(Lvl / 20)\nLevel 21 - 35: 40 + 10*((Lvl - 20) / 15)\nLevel 36 - 60: 50 + 10*((Lvl - 35) / 25)\nLevel 61 - 99: 60 + 10*((Lvl - 60) / 39)\n\nAfter adding both numbers together, the resulting number is rounded down for the UI menu.\n\nStats in Elden Ring\n\nArcane  ♦  Dexterity  ♦  Discovery  ♦  Endurance  ♦  Equip Load  ♦  Faith  ♦  Fire Damage Negation  ♦  Fire Defense  ♦  Focus  ♦  Focus Resistance  ♦  FP  ♦  Holy Damage Negation  ♦  Holy Defense  ♦  HP  ♦  Immunity  ♦  Immunity Resistance  ♦  Intelligence  ♦  Level  ♦  Lightning Damage Negation  ♦  Lightning Defense  ♦  Magic Damage Negation  ♦  Memory Slots  ♦  Mind  ♦  Physical Defense  ♦  Poise  ♦  Robustness  ♦  Robustness Resistance  ♦  Runes Held  ♦  Stamina  ♦  Strength  ♦  Vigor  ♦  Vitality  ♦  VS Pierce Damage Negation  ♦  VS Pierce Defense  ♦  VS Slash Damage Negation  ♦  VS Slash Defense  ♦  VS Strike Damage Negation  ♦  VS Strike Defense  ♦  Weight\n\n

QUESTION
When should I prioritize increasing Magic Defense in Elden Ring and how can I effectively achieve this?

ANSWER
Increasing Magic Defense in Elden Ring should be prioritized for PvP builds due to the common use of magic in PvP. This can be achieved by assigning points to Intelligence and utilizing specific Equipment and Items such as Spelldrake Talisman, Pearldrake Talisman, Lusat's Set, and Royal Knight Set for increased Magic Damage Negation.
