\n\n Spirit Calling Bell \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Bell that summons various spirits from ashen items \n\nSpirit Calling Bell is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Spirit Calling Bell Bell that summons various spirits from ashen items. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nA bell capable of summoning various spirits from ashen remains. It usually requires FP to use.\nSpirits can only be summoned in the vicinity of a rebirth monument. When summoning is possible, the monument icon will be visible on the left side of the screen.\nOnly one type of spirit may be summoned at once, and none may be summoned during multiplayer.\n\nWhere to find Spirit Calling Bell in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Spirit Calling Bell:\n\nGiven to players by Renna at the Church of Elleh in Limgrave. Return to the church after acquiring the ability to summon your Spectral Steed Whistle from Melina. You will find Renna sitting by the ruins at night time. [Map Link]\nIf you do not get the Spirit Calling Bell from Renna, she will eventually stop appearing at the Church of Elleh. However, you can try waiting until nightfall at the Church, then open the map and Travel to Stormhill Shack then Travel back and then she should be there again. Otherwise, you can buy the bell for 100 runes, as well as the Lone Wolf Ashes from the Twin Maiden Husks in the Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Spirit Calling Bell Use\n\nSpirit Calling Bell is used to summon Spirits from ashes.\nUsing Spirit Calling Bell will usually consume FP.\nSpirit Calling Bell may only be used to summon Spirits in specific locations (Points of Interest on the World Map and almost every boss arena). \nIt cannot be used during multiplayer.\n\nElden Ring Spirit Calling Bell Notes & Tips\n\nNotes & Tips go here\n\nElden Ring Key Items\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell
