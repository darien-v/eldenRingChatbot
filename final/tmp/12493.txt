QUESTION
How can players effectively stack buffs by dual-wielding Sacred Seals in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can effectively stack buffs by dual-wielding Sacred Seals in Elden Ring by passively applying incantation type buffs when held, allowing for buffs by casting lightning incantations using a second seal held.
