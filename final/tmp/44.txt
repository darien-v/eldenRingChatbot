CONTEXT
\n\n Mending Rune of the Death-Prince \n\nType\nGreat Rune\n\nEffect \n\n        Can be used to mend the shattered Elden Ring \n\nMending Rune of the Death-Prince is a Great Rune in Elden Ring. Unlike most other Great Runes, it can't be equipped. Its only effect is to alter the ending of the game.\n\nRune gestated by Fia, the Deathbed Companion. Used to restore the fractured Elden Ring when brandished by the Elden Lord.\nFormed of the two hallowbrand half-wheels combined, it will embed the principle of life within Death into Order.\nThe Golden Order was created by confining Destined Death. Thus, this new Order will be one of Death restored.\n\nWhere to find Mending Rune of the Death-Prince in Elden Ring\nAwarded to the player by Fia after completing her questline: \nAfter obtaining the Cursemark of Death from Divine Tower of Liurnia, bring to Fia in the Deeproot Depths. Reload the area to find her sleeping, then interact with her to trigger the Lichdragon Fortissax boss fight. Defeat it, and return to find the Rune on Fia's corpse. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Mending Rune of the Death-Prince Use\nGrants access to an alternative ending after defeating the final boss. See Endings page.  \n\nElden Ring Mending Rune of the Death-Prince Notes & Tips\nObtaining this item will not affect any other ending. \n\nElden Ring Great Runes\n\nGodrick's Great Rune  ♦  Great Rune of the Unborn  ♦  Malenia's Great Rune  ♦  Mending Rune of Perfect Order  ♦  Mending Rune of the Fell Curse  ♦  Mohg's Great Rune  ♦  Morgott's Great Rune  ♦  Phantom Great Rune  ♦  Radahn's Great Rune  ♦  Rykard's Great Rune\n\n

QUESTION
Where can the Mending Rune of the Death-Prince be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Awarded to the player by Fia after completing her questline in the Deeproot Depths.
