QUESTION
How can Ruin Fragments be obtained in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ruin Fragments can be obtained in Elden Ring by finding them near the remains of fallen ruins, purchasing from Gatekeeper Gostoc or Twin Maiden Husks, and farming at Liurnia Tower Bridge Site of Grace.
