\n\n Greatshield Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.9\n\nEffect \n\n Reduces stamina damage taken while blocking by 20% \n\nGreatshield Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Greatshield Talisman reduces the stamina damage taken while blocking by 20%. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats. \n\nTalisman depicting a knight holding a greatshield.Boosts guarding ability.\nThe knights of Leyndell once modelled themselves after the Tree Sentinels. Their purpose is to protect that which deserves protection, and thus the shield always comes before the sword.\n\nGreatshield Talisman Effect\nGreatshield Talisman reduces the stamina damage taken while blocking by 20% (applied after Guard Boost; shields with higher Guard Boosts in the first place will see less benefit from this effect).\n\nWhere to find Greatshield Talisman\n\nFound in a Carriage chest, east of the Erdtree-Gazing Hill site of grace. Watch out for the three fire-arrow balistae guarding the carriage. [Map link]\n\nElden Ring Greatshield Talisman Notes & Tips\n\nSell value:  500 Runes\nThis talisman does not increase damage reduction from blocking; it only reduces how much stamina you lose while blocking.\nThis talisman did previously grant a scaling level of Guard Boost, this however was changed so as to not make some shields have 100 Guard Boost which would reduce stamina damage by 100%.\nStacks multiplicatively with Scholar's Shield or Shield Grease to provide a 48% reduction to stamina damage taken (after Guard Boost)\n\nFrost Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Tower Knight QUESTION
Why is it important for players in Elden Ring to reduce stamina damage taken while blocking by using the Greatshield Talisman?

ANSWER
Reducing stamina damage taken while blocking with the Greatshield Talisman is important in Elden Ring to maintain better stamina management and sustain blocking attacks efficiently.
