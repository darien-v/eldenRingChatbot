Deathbed Dress is a lightweight chest piece that slowly replenishes the HP of nearby allies, but not that of the wearer. Deathbed Dress is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance.\n\nExtremely thin and sheer white dress worn by the Deathbed Companion as she embraces the dead.\nSlowly replenishes the HP of nearby allies, but not that of the wearer.\nThe touch of the fabric is exceedingly soft, so as not to harm even the most withered corpse, while still sharing her warmth. \n\nWhere to find Deathbed Dress in Elden Ring\n\nFound on the bed in the building with the Lower Capital Church site of grace in Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Map link] [Video location]\n\nDeathbed Dress Set in Elden Ring\n\nDeathbed Dress is an individual piece and doesn't belong to any set.\nIt is the inner dress part of Fia's Robe.\n\nElden Ring Deathbed Dress Notes & Tips\n\nHeals nearby allies (both summoned players and Spirit Ashes) by 2 HP per second.\nSell value: 500\nWill become unobtainable after a certain point in the game.\nPatch 1.06 fixed a bug which caused the player to become more easily noticed by the enemy when wearing Deathbed Dress, even when crouching.
