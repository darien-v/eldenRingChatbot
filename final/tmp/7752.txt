Bloodflame Talons spell lacerates the air and creates bloodflame bombs that explode after a short delay. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nA Blood Oath incantation, granted by the Lord of Blood.Creates bloodflame lacerations before the caster, which explode in an instant.This incantation can be cast repeatedly.After dealing damage, bloodflame continues to build up onset of blood loss for a very short time.\n\nWhere to find Bloodflame Talons\nWhere to find Bloodflame Talons:\n\nDropped by Mohg, the Omen in Forsaken Depths, Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, under Leyndell. QUESTION
How can the Bloodflame Talons spell in Elden Ring be effectively chain-cast in combat?

ANSWER
Chain-cast Bloodflame Talons in Elden Ring by ensuring you have enough FP and/or Stamina to repeatedly create bloodflame lacerations that explode, inflicting Hemorrhage and dealing fire damage.
