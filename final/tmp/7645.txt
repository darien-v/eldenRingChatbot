\n\n Ash of War: Glintblade Phalanx \n\nAffinity\nMagic\n\nSkill\nGlintblade Phalanx\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament\n\nAsh of War: Glintblade Phalanx is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Glintblade Phalanx provides Magic affinity and the Glintblade Phalanx Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\nPlease visit Glintblade Phalanx for the Spell variant.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Magic affinity and the following skill:\n"Glintblade Phalanx: Skill used by the enchanted knights who served the Carian royal family. Form an arch of magic glintblades overhead, which will attack foes automatically. Follow up with a strong attack to chain this skill into a lunging thrust."\nUsable on swords as well as polearms capable of thrusting (colossal weapons excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Glintblade Phalanx Location in Elden Ring\n\nAcquired from Rogier's Rapier.\nAfter defeating Godrick the Grafted in Stormveil Castle, Rogier can be found in Roundtable Hold, by the balcony. After speaking to him, and receiving his rapier, the skill may be removed from the rapier in order to be applied to other armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Glintblade Phalanx Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Glintblade Phalanx.\nProvides the Magic affinity, which adds Magic damage and Intelligence scaling, reduces base damage and Strength / Dexterity scaling.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Magic, Cold.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds.\n\nMoveset & Videos in Elden Ring for Ash of War: Glintblade Phalanx\n\nGlintblade Phalanx Demonstration\n\nMage Written Guide\n\nMagus Written Guide\n\nNotes & Tips\n\nThis skill does 40 poise damage, 10 for each magical sword.\nUnlike its spell counterpart, this variant of Glintblade Phalanx deals substantial damage to an enemy's stance; assuming all four swords land on the desired target, most enemies' stance can be broken with merely one or two casts.\nAlso unlike Glintblade Phalanx spell counterpart, the caster gets rooted in place for the duration of the cast animation.\n\nYou can manipulate the projectiles in a way to change your desired enemy. There are 2 ways: 1) Move your camera in another direction (not comfortable as you can barely see your enemies and surrounding area).2) Simply make your character face another direction. \n\nElden QUESTION
Why would someone choose to apply the Ash of War: Glintblade Phalanx to their armament in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Someone would choose to apply the Ash of War: Glintblade Phalanx to their armament in Elden Ring because it grants the Magic affinity and the skill Glintblade Phalanx, which can deal substantial damage to an enemy's stance with its magical swords and offers the ability to chain into a lunging thrust.
