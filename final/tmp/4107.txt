\n\n Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone 古竜岩の喪色鍛石 \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce special armaments to +10 \n\nSomber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone strengthens special armaments to +10. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nAncient dragonrock smithing stone drained of color. A scale of the Ancient Dragonlord, and hidden treasure of Farum Azula.\nStrengthens special armaments to +10. \nThe Ancient Dragonlord's seat is said to lie beyond time. This stone lightly twists time, allowing the creation of a weapon capable of slaying a god. \n\nSomber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone Locations in Elden Ring\nEight (8) Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone can be found:\n2 in Consecrated Snowfield:\n\nReceived from completing Latenna's quest line upon delivering her to Apostate Derelict.\nDropped by Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater after her third invasion, on the the frozen river southwest of Ordina, Liturgical Town.Note: This invasion's conditions were thought to be bugged, but the bug was fixed in 1.05.\n\n1 in Mohgwyn Palace:\n\nFound in a chest at the feet of the massive statue by the Dynasty Mausoleum Midpoint.\nVideo Location\n\n3 in Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree:\n\nFound in a chest hidden between columns near a roof with a corpse carrying an item, after the Prayer Room site of grace in the Haligtree. To reach it, go to the end of the first walkway and jump down to the gazebo with the red scarab, then run up the diagonal support on the other side.  The chest is in a small room. Over the railing to the right is a gargoyle next to a corpse with the stone.
