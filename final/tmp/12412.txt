QUESTION
How can Flame Fortification in Elden Ring be obtained and what are its effects in terms of increasing fire damage negation?

ANSWER
Flame Fortification can be obtained by purchasing it from Brother Corhyn at the Roundtable Hold for 3,000 Runes in Elden Ring. Its effect is increasing fire damage negation by +35% (+10% in PvP).
