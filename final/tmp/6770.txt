\n\n Golden Order Principia \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Given to a cleric to gain access to: \n\n        Radagon's Rings of Light \n       Law of Regression \n\nGolden Order Principia is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Golden Order Principia can be given to a cleric to gain access to new spells. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nPrayerbook of the Golden Order fundamentalists. A dense and complex academic treatise that contains the Order's fundamental principles.\nCan be given to a learned cleric to gain access to the following incantations:\n\nRadagon's Rings of Light\nLaw of Regression\n\nWhere to find Golden Order Principia in Elden Ring\n\nLocated in a chair hanging from the ceiling directly above the Erdtree Sanctuary site in Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Map Link]\nFrom the Erdtree Sanctuary site in Leyndell, Royal Capital, exit the east side of the room. Once outside, climb up the root to the left that circles back into the building's 2nd floor. Once inside, exit the only door and immediately turn left to jump down to a nearby roof. Once on the roof, a broken window can be used to get back inside. After entering the broken window, climb the root crossing the room to reach the Golden Order Principia, which is located in the chair hanging from the ceiling.\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Golden Order Principia Use\nGolden Order Principia can be given to a cleric to gain access to:\n\nRadagon's Rings of Light\nLaw of Regression\n\nElden Ring Golden Order Principia Notes, Tips & Trivia\n\nThere is also an item chest on the second floor containing the Erdtree Bow.\nIt is a good idea to give the Principia to Brother Corhyn. The book plays a role in his and Goldmask's questline.\nBe aware that the incantations from this book require Intelligence. \n\nElden Ring Key Items\n\nAbandoned Merchant's Bell Bearing  ♦  Academy
