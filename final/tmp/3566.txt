\n\n Soul Stifler \n\n        Raise the greathorn's wings to summon a soul-sapping miasma. Enemies in the affected area will temporarily suffer from reduced defense.  \n\nSoul Stifler is a Skill in Elden Ring. Soul Stifler is a unique skill on the Winged Greathorn that can temporarily increase the damage against enemies within its area of effect. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Soul Stifler\n\nDefault skill on the Winged Greathorn.\n\nElden Ring Soul Stifler Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is a Unique Skill\nFP Cost: 12\nThis Skill is NOT Chargeable.\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nOn skill use - Creates a zone that: \n\nStays where the caster used the skill\nLasts for ~10 seconds\nPlaces a debuff on any enemy within the zone during its duration\nRefreshes the debuff timer on any enemies within the zone if the enemy is already affected by the debuff, and the skill is cast again. or re-enters the zone.\nAs of  Patch 1.07, the Debuff lasts for 60s after the target is first affected by the debuff.Note: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: The effect time has been extended.\n\nDamage against affected targets is increased by 15%.\nDoes NOT Stack with Shriek of Milos which ever is applied second will be used\nDoes Stack with Frostbite, increasing damage against affected targets by a total of 38%.\nCan be used on a sleeping enemy without waking them up.  However, it is not silent and will draw enemy attention if you're sneaking behind them.\nThis is a Power Shred Debuff and will not stack with other debuffs QUESTION
What are the specifics of the debuff caused by using the Soul Stifler skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The debuff caused by using the Soul Stifler skill lasts for 60 seconds on affected targets after first application, can be refreshed by recasting the skill, increases damage against targets by 15%, does not stack with certain other debuffs but stacks with Frostbite for a total of 38%, and is a Power Shred Debuff.
