Scouring Black Flame spell creates fire in a wide area in front of the user. Can be charged for increased range. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nA black flame incantation of the Godskin Apostles.Sweeps a wide area before the caster with black flame.Charging enhances range.The black flame could once slay gods. But when Maliketh sealed Destined Death, the true power of the black flame was lost.\n\nWhere to find Scouring Black Flame\nWhere to find Scouring Black Flame:\n\n Dropped by Godskin Apostle.
