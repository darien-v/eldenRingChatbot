\n\n Smarag's Glintstone Breath \n\nSpell Type\nDragon Communion Incantations\n\nFP Cost 36 (6)\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Spews magic breath of Glintstone Dragon Smarag from above \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 23 \n\nArcane 15 \n\nSmarag's Glintstone Breath is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Smarag's Glintstone Breath spell conjures head of Smarag to drown enemies in primordial magic.\n\nSuperior incantation of Dragon Communion.Channels the power of Smarag, the Glintstone Dragon.\nTransforms caster into a dragon to spew magic breath from above.Charging extends duration. This incantation can be used while jumping.\nSmarag was a devourer of sorcerers, and over time, his body became corrupted by their glintstones.\n\nWhere to find Smarag's Glintstone Breath\nWhere to find Smarag's Glintstone Breath:\n\nCan be purchased at the Dragon Communion Altar at the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid for 2 Dragon Hearts. Players need to defeat Glintstone Dragon Smarag in order to unlock this Incantation. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Smarag's Glintstone Breath Guide\n\nDragon Communion Incantation\nStamina Cost: 60\nDeals magic damage\nLike other named Breath incantations, this spell has long wind-up time of 2 seconds.\nDeals 18% more damage than Glintstone Breath, with slightly longer range but narrower spread.\nDuration can be extended to breathe again, and you can swivel your character while casting to spew in different directions.\nFull cast requires at least 78 FP but will use up to 144 FP with no additional benefit before any FP-conservating buffs.\nIt takes on average 5 seconds including initial wind-up to complete the full cast.\nCan be cast while jumping, and your character hovers slightly while using the spell.\nCannot be used on horseback.\nAs of Patch 1.09, Roar Medallion increases the damage of this incantation by approx. 5% .Tested in PVP.\n\nDragon Kinght Written Guide\n\nMagic Dragonknight Written Guide\n\nELDEN RING SMARAG'S GLINSTONE BREATH NOTES AND TIPS\n\nUpdated to Patch 1.10. With all dragon breaths having a 2.5-second wind-up and Endure having a 3-second duration it will give you enough time to do a dragon breath in the air. QUESTION
Why is Smarag's Glintstone Breath considered a superior incantation of Dragon Communion in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
It channels the power of Smarag, the Glintstone Dragon, transforming the caster into a dragon to spew magic breath from above, with the ability to extend duration by charging and use it while jumping.
