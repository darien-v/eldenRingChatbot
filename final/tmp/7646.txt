\n\n Ash of War: Giant Hunt \n\nAffinity\nQuality\n\nSkill\nGiant Hunt\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Giant Hunt is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Giant Hunt provides Quality affinity and the Giant Hunt Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Quality affinity and the following skill:\n"Giant Hunt: Skill developed for confronting gigantic foes. Step forward from a low stance, carrying the momentum into a sudden upward thrust."\nUsable on large and colossal weapons capable of thrusting, spears, and twinblades.\n\nAsh of War: Giant Hunt Location in Elden Ring\n\nPreviously dropped by the lone wandering Troll Knight (no longer as of 1.03.02). [Elden Ring Map Link]\nDropped by area boss in northern Liurnia, Night's Cavalry, between Bellum Church and East Raya Lucaria Gate sites of grace. Only appears at night. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAsh of War: Giant Hunt Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Giant Hunt.\nProvides the Quality affinity, which balances Strength and Dexterity scaling, decreases base damage.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Twinblades, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Spears, Great Spears, and Halberds.\nDeals Pierce damage, and works with the Spear Talisman, for increased counter-attack damage.\nDespite its seemingly lengthy animation, this skill cannot be charged.\n\nGiant Hunt Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Giant Hunt Notes & Tips\n\nGiant Hunt provides the highest singular strike damage out of all the thrust Ashes of War.\nCan send medium-sized humanoid enemies flying up in the air, as long as they have no hyperarmor active, forcing long combat recovery.\nGiant Hunt lowers the user's hitbox mid-animation, allowing them to dodge certain enemy attacks.\nThis Ash of War is a potent option for roll-catching PvP opponents.\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric Roar  ♦  Ash of
