Blaidd's Gauntlets is an armor piece that protect both of the player's arms by applying various defensive properties, It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nWell-worn black gauntlets of the man-wolf Blaidd.\nBlaidd, who served as Ranni's shadow, was a loyal ally who would defy destiny itself if it would have him turn upon his Lady.\n\nWhere to find Blaidd's Gauntlets in Elden Ring\nThe Blaidd's Gauntlets Armor Gauntlets piece can be found at the following location:\n\nFollowing Ranni's questline, after she is given the Dark Moon Ring, return to Ranni's Rise and you will hear Blaidd talking to himself in front of the tower.
