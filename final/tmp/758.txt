CONTEXT
\n\n Grand Lift of Dectus \n\nA massive lift platform found between Liurnia and the Altus Plateau. The mechanism that operates the lift is barred by four Guardian Statues, requiring the brandishing of a special artifact for them to release their hold.\n\nGrand Lift of Dectus is a Location in Elden Ring. The Grand Lift of Dectus is found between Liurnia of the Lakes and the Altus Plateau (see Ruin-Strewn Precipice for an alternative way between both areas). This location can be reached by following the eastern landmass in Liurnia, heading north through the Bellum Highway.\n\nGrand Lift of Dectus Map\nThe Grand Lift of Dectus is found in the northernmost tip of Liurnia's eastern landmass. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Grand Lift of Dectus\n\nThere are no NPCs or Merchants at this location.\n\nGrand Lift of Dectus Walkthrough\nThe Grand Lift of Dectus is a massive platform with its entrance found at the end of the Bellum Highway in northern Liurnia, along the eastern landmass. This colossal structure joins the regions of Liurnia and the Altus Plateau. However, on your first visit, the lift is sealed by four Guardian Statues. It requires a special artifact in order to activate. This artifact is the Dectus Medallion which is made up of two halves found in separate locations:\n\nThe Dectus Medallion (Left) can be found in Mistwood, inside a chest atop the tallest tower in Fort Haight to the east. [Map Link]\nThe Dectus Medallion (Right) can be found in Dragonbarrow, inside a chest atop the eastern tower in Fort Faroth to the east. [Map Link]\n\nOnce both halves are obtained, approach the platform once more to hoist the whole medallion and raise the lift, taking you to the Altus Plateau.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Grand Lift of Dectus\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nThere are no Gatherable items in this location\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location \n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nGreat Mace ( Found in a chest in the soldier camp, southwest of the Grand Lift of Dectus.)\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nStonesword Key ( Found on a corpse located on a camp northeast from the Grand Lift of Dectus. Elden Ring Map here)\n\nElden Ring Grand Lift of Dectus Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no enemies in this location\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses in this location\n\nElden Ring Grand Lift of Dectus Gallery and Notes\nCollecting both Dectus Medallion halves and taking the Grand Lift of Dectus will trigger the Radahn Festival. QUESTION
How can one obtain and activate the special artifact needed to activate the Grand Lift of Dectus in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Obtain the Dectus Medallion by finding its two halves in chests atop towers in Mistwood and Dragonbarrow, then approach the lift to hoist the whole medallion and raise the lift.
