QUESTION
Who might drop the Crimsonburst Crystal Tear in Elden Ring, and where can it be found?

ANSWER
The Erdtree Avatar boss drops the Crimsonburst Crystal Tear in Elden Ring, and it can be found beneath the Minor Erdtree in the Weeping Peninsula.
