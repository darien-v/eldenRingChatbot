\n\n Boilprawn Shack \n\nSmall run-down shack in the swamps of Liurnia. The Blackguard is said to reside here, cooking prawns.\n\nBoilprawn Shack is a Location in Elden Ring. The Boilprawn Shack is found in Liurnia of the Lakes. You can reach it by travelling southwest from the Academy Gate Town Site of Grace. You can rest at the Site of Grace found on the west side of the building.\nThis is the initial location of the NPC Blackguard Big Boggart and also a mandatory location for Rya's questline.\n\nBoilprawn Shack Map\nThe Boilprawn Shack can be found in the southern half of the lake. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Boilprawn Shack\n\nBlackguard Big Boggart - Merchant who stole the necklace from Rya.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Boilprawn Shack\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nBoiled Prawn (Blackguard Big Boggart)\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nNo Upgrade Materials at this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nNo Equipment or Magic at this location\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nRya's Necklace\nSpread Out Gesture\n\nElden Ring Boilprawn Shack Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no regular enemies at this location\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses at this location\n\nBoilprawn Shack Walkthrough\nThis location is needed for Rya's questline. After speaking with Rya (Elden Ring Map link), you will get a quest to receive this stolen necklace.\nSpeak to Blackguard to haggle Rya's Necklace and to be able to purchase Boiled Prawn and Boiled Crab from him. Continue speaking to him to eventually obtain a new Gesture: Spread Out. \nAn alternative to paying 1000 runes for the necklace is to kill him, but it is unnecessary.\n\nElden Ring Boilprawn Shack QUESTION
Why is it necessary to haggle with Blackguard for Rya's necklace at the Boilprawn Shack in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
To receive Rya's stolen necklace, you need to haggle with Blackguard at the Boilprawn Shack in Elden Ring, before being able to purchase Boiled Prawn and Boiled Crab from him, and finally obtain the new Gesture: Spread Out by continuing to speak with him.
