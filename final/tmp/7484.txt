\n\n Ash of War: Royal Knight's Resolve \n\nAffinity\nQuality\n\nSkill\nRoyal Knight's Resolve\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Royal Knight's Resolve is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Royal Knight's Resolve provides Quality affinity and the Royal Knight's Resolve Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Quality affinity and the following skill:\n"Royal Knight's Resolve: Skill of the knights who once served the Elden Lord. Hold the flat of the armament to your face and pledge your resolve, greatly powering up your next attack."\nUsable on all melee armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Royal Knight's Resolve Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Royal Knight's Resolve:\n\nFrom the Temple of Eiglay Grace, exit the northeast door that opens to the west. Enter the small building that only contains a lift, and ride it up. Continue straight across the big hallway northwest. (This requires having unlocked the lift. Take the "long way" from the Temple to the big hallway, past the lava pools, if the lift isn't unlocked.) Continue down into the building to the north; the first room is empty, and the room you enter through a doorway, facing east, looks like a throne room. Go up one flight of stairs on either side, and turn around and continue going up a second flight of stairs to the western door. There will be an imp statue in the next room. Carefully jump down the cages, and be wary of the sorcerer enemy halfway down, and the crawling enemies at the bottom. The Ashes of War are in the room past the door to the northeast.\n Video Location tl;dr: Behind the Imp Seal in the hall where various nobles are praying. You can access this area after kiling the Godskin Noble.\n\nAsh of War: Royal Knight's Resolve Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Royal Knight's Resolve.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan QUESTION
How can I obtain the Ash of War: Royal Knight's Resolve in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
From the Temple of Eiglay Grace, exit the northeast door that opens to the west, enter a small building with a lift, ride it up, continue northwest to a room with an imp statue, carefully jump down the cages, and reach the room northeast to find the Ash of War: Royal Knight's Resolve in Elden Ring.
