\n\n Ash of War: War Cry \n\nAffinity\nHeavy\n\nSkill\nWar Cry\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: War Cry is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: War Cry provides Heavy affinity and the War Cry Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Heavy affinity and the following skill:\n"War Cry: Give a war cry to rally the spirit and increase attack power. While active, strong attacks change to charging attacks."\nUsable on melee armaments (daggers, thrusting swords, and whips excepted).\n\nAsh of War: War Cry Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: War Cry:\n\nSold by Knight Bernahl at the Warmaster's Shack for 800 . Elden Ring Map Link.\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them Bernahl's Bell Bearing.\n\nAsh of War: War Cry Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill War Cry.\nProvides the Heavy affinity, increases Strength scaling, decreases all other scaling types.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Straight Swords, Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds, Reapers, Fists, Claws, Colossal Weapons\nDuration: 20 seconds.\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: War Cry Notes & Tips\n\nWhen this ash is active, holding down R2 will give you a charging attack that will stagger most enemies multiple times before landing in a heavy swing.\nCharging in this context does not mean powering up your swing but rather charging as in moving forward unstoppably.\nCounts as a weapon buff despite the appearance of a red aura around the player model; using a weapon grease or weapon-enhancing incantation will remove the War Cry effect.\nBuff stacks with Stormhawk Deenh summon's war cry AoE buff.\n\nBuilds with Ash of War: War Cry\n\nWar Cry Demonstration\n\nBarbarian Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Ashes of QUESTION
Why should players consider equipping the Ash of War: War Cry in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players should consider equipping the Ash of War: War Cry in Elden Ring because it provides the Heavy affinity, increases Strength scaling, and decreases all other scaling types, while granting the War Cry skill that boosts attack power and transforms strong attacks into charging attacks.
