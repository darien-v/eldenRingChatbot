QUESTION
Why is the Hermit Merchant's Bell Bearing [3] considered a key item in Elden Ring and what role does it play in accessing new shop items?

ANSWER
Hermit Merchant's Bell Bearing [3] is considered a key item in Elden Ring as it can be offered to Twin Maiden Husks at Roundtable Hold to access new shop items such as Gravity Stone Fan, Gravity Stone Chunk, Lost Ashes of War, Celestial Dew, Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook, Perfumer's Cookbook, Prisoner Iron Mask, Prisoner Clothing, and Prisoner Trousers.
