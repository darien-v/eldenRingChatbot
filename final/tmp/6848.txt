\n\n Somber Smithing Stone [9] \n\nEffect \n\n        Reinforce special armaments to +9 \n\nSomber Smithing Stone [9] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Somber Smithing Stone [9] a colorless smithing stone that is quite rare to acquire, it is mainly used to reinforce special equipment to increase its level and stats. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nAncient smithing stone drained of color\nStrengthens special armaments to +9.\nSpecial armaments with unique characteristics cannot be strengthened with colored smithing stones. Number of steps and amount strengthened differ from standard equipment.\n\nSomber Smithing Stone [9] Location in Elden Ring\nSee Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone for upgrades past +9.Where to find Somber Smithing Stone [9]:\n\nSomberstone Miner's Bell Bearing [5]: Found on a body in the abandoned temple on the North side of Crumbling Farum Azula, down the lift from the Beside The Great Bridge Site of Grace in front of the altar.\nFound on a corpse on the raised platform in Lake of Rot where there's a Lesser Alabaster Lord. QUESTION
Why are Somber Smithing Stones [9] considered quite rare to acquire in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Somber Smithing Stones [9] are considered quite rare to acquire in Elden Ring because they are mainly used to reinforce special armaments to increase their level and stats, require specific exploration, looting, enemy drops, NPC gifts, or merchant purchase to obtain, and are colorless ancient smithing stones that can only strengthen special armaments to +9.
