QUESTION
Where can someone find the Aspects of the Crucible: Breath spell?

ANSWER
By Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy Site of Grace in Volcano Manor: Progress Tanith's quest to the point where she's feasting on Rykard's corpse, then kill her. Tanith's Knight will invade shortly thereafter, and will drop this Aspects of the Crucible: Breath when killed.
