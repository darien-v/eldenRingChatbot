\n\n Assassin's Cerulean Dagger \n\nWeight\n0.8\n\nEffect \n\n Critical attacks restore 15  \n       FP\n\nAssassin's Cerulean Dagger is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Assassin's Cerulean Dagger causes critical attacks to restore 15 FP. Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nAn assassin's dagger, misshapen and stained in cerulean.Critical hits restore FP.\nThis charm is modelled after the darkly gleaming blades used in the Night of Black Knives. Those which gave the demigods their first taste of death.\n\nAssassin's Cerulean Dagger Effect in Elden Ring\nAssassin's Cerulean Dagger causes critical attacks to restore 15 FP.\n\nWhere to find Assassin's Cerulean Dagger in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Assassin's Cerulean Dagger:\n\nDropped by the Black Knife Assassin boss found in Black Knife Catacombs in the northeast of the Liurnia of the Lakes region. [Map Link] \n\nThis boss is a secret second boss within the Black Knife Catacombs that is found behind an illusory wall. From the room with the falling blades, stand on top of one after it drops to be lifted up, giving you access to a hallway heading south. QUESTION
How do critical attacks with the Assassin's Cerulean Dagger interact with large enemies in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Critical attacks with the Assassin's Cerulean Dagger change to a double hit animation on large enemies in Elden Ring, activating the talisman's effect twice.
