\n\n Ash of War: Through and Through \n\nAffinity\nStandard\n\nSkill\nThrough and Through\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Through and Through is an Ash of War  in Elden Ring. Through and Through provides no affinity and is usable on Greatbows only. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:\n"Through and Through: Powerful archery skill using a greatbow held in an oblique stance. Ready the greatbow, then twist the bowstring to fire a mighty greatarrow that can penetrate through enemies."\nUsable on greatbows.\n\nThrough and Through Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Through and Through:\n\nThrough and Through can be found on an Invisible Scarab just outside Volcano Manor in the Altus Plateau. Strike the Scarab when the footsteps are near and you will acquire this Ash of War. Map Link\n\nThrough and Through Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Through and Through.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Greatbows\nThe Scarab will follow a set pathway in a loop, so standing on one place and waiting for it to come to you is the easiest option rather than to chase it down. \n\nThrough and Through Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Through and Through Notes & Tips\n\nThrough and Through also provides substantial damage buff of around 64% over the standard fully charged shot.\nIf targets are lined up, will penetrate and deal damage to all targets. Doesn't break shield guards. \nSignificantly increases firing rate when fired back-to-back (holding L2 throughout multiple shots), but destroys your stamina when you do this, as you can't regen any when holding down L2 \nCan be buggy and not trigger if you active the Ash of War when already firing, or when aiming down sights normally. Much more consistent when used prior to aiming or shooting. \n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric Roar  ♦  Ash of War: Barrage  ♦  Ash of War: Barricade How can Through and Through Ash of War be obtained in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
