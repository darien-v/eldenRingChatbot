\n\n Sacred Ring of Light \n\n        Skill used by the commanders of the Cleanrot Knights. Gather a sacred ring of light in the armament, and then fire it forwards. Can be fired in rapid succession. \n\nSacred Ring of Light is a Skill in Elden Ring. Sacred Ring of Light is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War, this skill can also be used on polearms (Great Spears excepted). Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Sacred Ring of Light in Elden Ring\n\nThe Ash of War: Sacred Ring of Light grants the Sacred affinity and Sacred Ring of Light skill. The armament gathers a sacred ring of light and then fires it forward, the skill can be fired in rapid succession.\nDropped by a Teardrop Scarab found in the Aeonian Swamps in Caelid. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Sacred Ring of Light Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Sacred Ring of Light.\nFP Cost: 9 per use.\nDeals 6 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nUpon activation, the user makes a low sweep upward with their armament (weapon), creating a discus (ring) of light in the air that travels to an enemy, dealing holy damage on impact.\n\nThe user can repeatedly press the Skill button to continue firing more rings of light.\n\nThe player can change directions with each sweep.\nScales with Weapon Level and Faith.\n\nNOTE: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nRange and speed of the projectile halo have been increased.\nDamage detection to the weapon part has been added.\n\nSacred Ring of QUESTION
How can the Sacred Ring of Light skill be effectively used in combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Sacred Ring of Light skill in Elden Ring can be effectively used by gathering a sacred ring of light in the armament and firing it forwards in rapid succession, dealing holy damage on impact, and continuously pressing the Skill button to fire more rings of light, while being able to change directions with each sweep.
