\n\n Starcaller \n\nLocation\nWeeping Peninsula Ainsel RiverAltus Plateau\n\nDrops\n 57 - 512 RunesGravity Stone FanGravity Stone Chunk\n\nStarcallers are a Humanoid Creature in Elden Ring. Starcallers are humanoids clad in black robes that cast gravity Sorceries, they have some sort of relationship with Alabaster Lords. They are found near craters left by meteorites, scavenging for stones imbued with magic.\n\n Scavengers desperately digging around craters found in the wake of meteorite strikes, they dub themselves Starcallers.\n\nElden Ring Starcaller Locations\nWhere to Find Starcallers:\n\nNear the craters in Northeastern Weeping Peninsula.\nSouth of Stormcaller Church in the Altus Plateau.
