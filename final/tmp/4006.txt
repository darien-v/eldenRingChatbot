\n\n Winged Sword Insignia \n\nWeight\n1.4\n\nEffect \n\n Raises attack power with successive attacks (3%,5%,10%) \n\nWinged Sword Insignia is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Winged Sword Insignia increases attack power with each successive attack. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a raised prosthetic blade.An honor bestowed upon the knights who fought alongside Malenia the Severed.\nRaises attack power with successive attacks.\nThe wings symbolize Malenia and her undefeated prowess. Though she never knew relief from the accursed rot she was born into, her blade was forever beautiful - and relentless.\n\nWinged Sword Insignia Effect in Elden Ring\nWinged Sword Insignia increases attack power with each successive attack.\n\nIncreases attack power relative to the number of attacks you land. Has three tiers of attack power increase. Tiers decay to lower tiers when not attacking fast enough. Number of hits needed to activate effect varies based on attacks used.\nDecay duration: 1.5 seconds.\nFor each tier, attack power increases by 3%, 5%, and then 10%.\nThe attack power boost also affects the damage of certain Spells.\n\nWhere to find Winged Sword Insignia in Elden Ring\n\nStillwater Cave: Dropped by the Cleanrot Knight boss upon defeat. QUESTION
How does the Winged Sword Insignia in Elden Ring increase attack power with successive attacks, and what are the specific tiers and decay durations associated with this effect?

ANSWER
The Winged Sword Insignia increases attack power by 3%, 5%, and 10% with each successive attack, with tiers decaying after 1.5 seconds if not attacking fast enough.
