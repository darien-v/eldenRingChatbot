\n\n Elden Stars エルデの流星 \n\nSpell Type\nErdtree Incantations\n\nFP Cost 41\nSlots Used 2\n\nEffect \n\n        Creates a stream of golden shooting stars that assail the area. \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 50 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nElden Stars is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Elden Stars spell conjures a stream of projectiles that devastate the area. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nThis legendary incantation is the most ancient of those that derive from the Erdtree.\nCreates a stream of golden shooting stars that assail the area.\nIt is said that long ago, the Greater Will sent a golden star bearing a beast into the Lands Between, which would later become the Elden Ring.\n\nWhere to find Elden Stars\nWhere to find Elden Stars:In the Deeproot Depths, head west from the Great Waterfall Crest grace until you reach some roots. From there look to the north-west to see a root going up to a cave and follow it. Note that this is a different entrance than the one where the remains containing the spell is visible (with the ledge below it with two ants).\n\nEnter the cave and fight through the packs of Giant Ants until the end and  you'll find Elden Stars on a body facing the cave exit. QUESTION
How can one effectively utilize Elden Stars in combat scenarios in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Utilize Elden Stars by summoning slow-moving gold projectiles that shoot off smaller ones, allowing movement and other actions, dealing holy and stance damage with reduced FP consumption post Patch 1.07.
