Tools are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.\n\nProof that one is a glorious knight of the new Dynasty of Mohgwyn that the Lord of Blood will inaugurate.\nUse to be granted audience with Mohg. The new dynasty is nigh.\n\nPureblood Knight's Medal Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Pureblood Knight's Medal:\n\nWhite Mask Varre will give you this item once you have completed the 3 invasions required for his questline to advance. He will give you the Medal after the reusable Bloody Finger, exhausting his dialogue is necessary. [Elden Ring Map Here]\nVideo Location\n\nPureblood Knight's Medal use in Elden Ring\n\nUsed to grant a Audience with Mohg. Teleports the player to just outside the steps of Mohgwyn Palace near the Dynasty Mausoleum Entrance Site of Grace.\nCan be infinitely reused to teleport back to this grace, allowing the player to teleport to safety. \n\nWorks while in combat or inside boss arenas, unlike normal teleportation.\nCannot be used when the player is teleport-blocked.\n\nPureblood Knight's Medal Notes & Tips\n\nThis item is used from the inventory.\nWhite Mask Varre's advice to "not use it just yet" is a misdirection. He is awaiting the Mohg Dynasty, which will never come and is a fool's hope (according to Gideon Ofnir) - there is no later prompt to use it\nThis item is not kept on New Game+\n\nElden Ring
