\n\n Warmaster's Shack \n\nSmall shack in a remote area near north-central Stormhill.\n\nWarmaster's Shack is a Location in Elden Ring. Warmaster's Shack is found in Stormhill. Players can acquire Ashes of War and find some respite at this location's Site of Grace, before venturing forth into the storm.\n\nWarmaster's Shack Map for Elden Ring\n\nIt is in front of the woods at Stormhill [Elden Ring Map here].\n\nElden Ring NPCs and Merchants in Warmaster's Shack\n\nKnight Bernahl - A Tarnished Knight longing to pass on his battle arts. Sells Ashes of War.\nBell Bearing Hunter spawns at night. At the Site of Grace, pass time until night. Then rest again at the site (without passing time). Finally, enter the shack to spawn the hunter.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Warmaster's Shack\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nHerba\nErdleaf Flower (Infinite Farm by resting at Grace)\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials in this location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nThere are no equipment in this location\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\nKnight Bernahl's stock:\n\nAsh of War: Stamp (Upward Cut)\nAsh of War: Kick\nAsh of War: Endure\nAsh of War: War Cry\nAsh of War: Spinning Slash\nAsh of War: Impaling Thrust\nAsh of War: Quickstep\nAsh of War: Storm Blade\nAsh of War: Parry\nAsh of War: No Skill\n\nWarmaster's Shack Creatures, Enemies and Bosses in Elden Ring\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nThere are no Creatures or Enemies at this location\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nBell Bearing Hunter (night)\n\nElden Ring Warmaster's Shack Walkthrough\nYour main point of interest in this location is the merchant, Knight Bernahl who will sell you various Ashes of War. Beware not to visit this location at night as the NPC is replaced by a Boss Fight encounter with the Bell Bearing Hunter. This Boss spawns only at night. The quickest way to spawn the boss is to rest at the Warmaster's Shack, advance time to night, then rest again. You must enter the shack to make him spawn. He drops the Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing. There is also an Erdleaf Flower next to the Site of Grace that can be infinitely farmed by resting.\n\nElden Ring Warmaster's Shack Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring
