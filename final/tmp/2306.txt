Warhawks are large bird creatures commonly found in Stormveil Castle. They settle on tree branches and often scavenge on dead corpses, acting just like crows. Warhawks are always hostile once they've spotted the player.\nThe spirit of a Warhawk can be summoned for aid in battle by using Warhawk Ashes. Alternatively, the spirit of Stormhawk Deenh, a fierce hawk that once rendered lifelong service to the old king of Stormveil, can be summoned by using Stormhawk Deenh Ashes.\n\n Stormhawks that have had their talons sliced off, so that razor-fine swords could be grafted in their place. They react with extreme aggression, using their mobility in combination with their deadly talons.\n\nWarhawk Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Warhawks:\n\nCastle Sol\nCrumbling Farum Azula\nDivine Tower of Limgrave\nMountaintops of the Giants\nStormveil Castle\n\nElden Ring Warhawk Combat Information\n\nHealth:\n\n256 - 500 HP (Stormveil Castle)\n176 HP (Divine Tower of Limgrave)\n1017 HP (Crumbling Farum Azula)\n750 HP (Mountaintops of the Giants - West Zamor Ruins, Grand Lift of Rold)\n1656 HP (Mountaintops of the Giants - South Castle Sol, Snow Valley Ruins Overlook)\n\nPoise: 30 - 50\n\nNegations (or Absorptions)\n\nStandard: -10\nSlash: -10\nStrike: -10\nPierce: -10\n\nMagic: -20\nFire: -20 - (-40)\nLightning: -20\nHoly: -20\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. Having a good shield can make a difference\nAlternatively, use range weapons/skill/spells like Lightning Spear or Ice Spear.\nParryable: Yes (melee attack). Successful parry knocks warhawk, but without chance for riposte.\nGuard Counter and heavy attack a long quick weapon like Short Spear devastates the warhawks\nThe Whip (the Strike damage of standard Whip, not the slash whip) cause stagger to the warhawks even with weak R1 attack. Whip also has good range and speed and can therefore stun-lock the birds.\nCan be poise broken, but not riposted afterwards.\nBug: If Warhawk from Castle Sol manage to survive direct hit from weapon that can knock him off from spot he resides, he will become stuck in the wall.\nLike many other enemies, Warhawk have variants. In this case, there's one type of Warhawk wearing a helmet with spikes, capable of using a close ranged fire blast. \n\nWarhawk Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor
