\n\n Death Flare \n\n        Set the lusterless sun ablaze with the Prince of Death's flames, inflicting the death ailment upon foes. \n       Follow up with an additional input to bring down the armament, triggering an explosion. \n\nDeath Flare is a Skill in Elden Ring. Death Flare is the unique skill of the Eclipse Shotel, which can inflict the Death Blight ailment upon foes. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nHow to get Death Flare\n\nDefault Skill on the Eclipse Shotel.\n\nDeath Flare Guide\n\nThis is a unique skill.\nFP cost: 16.\nThe explosion deals 30 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nThe buff applies 65 holy attack power that scales on Faith and 30 Death Blight build-up.\nThe follow-up explosion deals 70 Death Blight to opponents that it makes contact with.\nThe buff lasts for 45 seconds.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nDuration has been increased from 30 to 45 seconds.\nAttack power of the effect that grants the weapon a holy attribute has been increased from 35 to 65.\n\nElden Ring Skills Demonstration\n\nDeath Flare Notes & Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
