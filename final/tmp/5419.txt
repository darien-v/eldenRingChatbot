QUESTION
Why did the Kingsrealm Spirit ask for forgiveness from War Counselor Iji for the lost battle at the Kingsrealm Ruins?

ANSWER
The Kingsrealm Spirit asked for forgiveness from War Counselor Iji for the lost battle at the Kingsrealm Ruins because the royal grounds were entrusted to them but they ultimately failed to defend them.
