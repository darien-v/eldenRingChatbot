The Golden Order Greatsword scales primarily with Dexterity and Faith, with minor scaling in Strength. It is a versatile Weapon that can deal considerable amount of Holy damage both in melee and at medium range.\n\nGreatsword made of light, modeled after the Elden Ring itself. Forged by King Consort Radagon to proudly symbolize the tenets of the Golden Order. One of the legendary armaments. Telltale signs betray that this was once the greatsword bequeathed to him by his first wife, Rennala. \n\nWhere to Find Golden Order Greatsword in Elden Ring\nThe Golden Order Greatsword weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by Misbegotten Crusader in the Cave of the Forlorn.
