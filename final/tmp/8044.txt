\n\n Godrick Soldier Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 54\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons two Godrick soldier spirits \n\nGodrick Soldier Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Godrick Soldier Ashes summons two Godrick Soldiers to fight by your side.\nGodrick Soldiers can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Godrick Soldier.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirits of two of Godrick's soldiers.\nA duo of spirits, consisting of a wielder of devastating charged hammer attacks in the vanguard and a crossbowman bringing up the rear.The soldiers who serve Godrick the Grafted are what remains of the army that fled the royal capital of the Erdtree.\n\nWhere to find Godrick Soldier Ashes\nWhere to Find Godrick Soldier Ashes\n\nFound at the Cemetery at the west of Stormhill that is filled with Spirit Jellyfish, next to a grave. There are two red jellyfish nearby. Check both sides of each grave to find it. [Map Link]\nFrom the Stormhill Shack site of grace, head south and then west, following the cliff around until you can head up some a rocky path  northward.
