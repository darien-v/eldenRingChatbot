\n\n Wraith Caller \n\nLocation\nLiurnia of the LakesAltus PlateauThe Shaded CastleSainted Hero's GraveLunar Estate RuinsSubterranean Shunning-Grounds\n\nDrops\n 72 - 477 RunesGrave Violet\n\nWraith Caller is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Wraith Callers are strange, hunched-over humanoids that loiter in the ruins of the Liurnia region, often accompanying Revenants. They are much less aggressive than the Revenants, but most can call forth wraiths to chase down the player using their bell.\n\nWraith Caller Locations in Elden Ring\nWraith Callers can be found in the following locations:\n\nLiurnia Lake\nLaskyar Ruins\nBellum Highway: A large group of them spawn south of Frenzied Flame Village Outskirts site of grace guarding an Eye of Yelough.\nOne spawns underneath the natural bridge that leads from the Grand Lift of Dectus to the Altus Highway Junction Site of Grace.\nThe Shaded Castle\nSainted Hero's Grave\nLunar Estate Ruins\nSubterranean Shunning-Grounds\n\nElden Ring Wraith Caller Drops\n\n 72 - 477 Runes\nGrave Violet\n\nWraith Caller Notes & Tips\n\nWraith Callers are extremely weak against Holy Damage.\nBeing a type of Revenant, Wraith Callers are damaged by the area of effect from  Heal, Great Heal, Lord's Heal, and Erdtree Heal for 50% of their max HP + 300 HP. In addition, the area of effect from Blessing's Boon and Blessing of the Erdtree damages them for 3% + 10 HP per second for 30 seconds. The area of effect of these healing incantations also deal significant Poise damage. Revenants are the only enemies in the game that are damaged by healing incantations.\nSome ride erratic, nearly-skeletal horses.\nSome do not wield a Wraith Calling Bell, and will simply rush and attack the player physically.\n\nWraith Caller Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone Cluster  ♦  Albinauric  ♦  Albinauric Wolfback Archer  ♦  Anastasia QUESTION
What strategy can be used to effectively defeat Wraith Callers in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Wraith Callers in Elden Ring can be effectively defeated using Holy Damage and area of effect healing incantations which damage them.
