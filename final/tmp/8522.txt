QUESTION
Where can Dwelling Arrow be found in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Dwelling Arrow can be found by being dropped by Ancestral Followers, purchased from Nomadic Merchant in Siofra River, or purchased from the Imprisoned Merchant in Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum in Elden Ring.
