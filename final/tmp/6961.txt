QUESTION
Why do Lone Wolves in Elden Ring often ambush their targets by dropping from trees or high places, despite their name suggesting they are solitary creatures?

ANSWER
Lone Wolves in Elden Ring often ambush targets by dropping from trees or high places because, despite their name, they are commonly found in packs, displaying pack behavior.
