QUESTION
Why is Night's Cavalry Armor described as pitch-black armor thinly painted with dried blood?

ANSWER
Worn by the Night's Cavalry, the Night's Cavalry Armor is described as pitch-black armor thinly painted with dried blood because it was worn by the infamous Night's Cavalry who rode funeral steeds, wander the dim roads at night, and were deliverers of death for great warriors, knights, and champions.
