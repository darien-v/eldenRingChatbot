\n\n Deep Siofra Well \n\nA second lift to the Siofra River\n\nDeep Siofra Well  is a Location in Elden Ring. The Deep Siofra Well  is found in Dragonbarrow. You can reach it by heading up the lift in the north-eastern side of the Siofra River. From the Siofra River, this is found past the fire lit pillars and has its own platform that requires 2 Stonesword Keys to activate.
