\n\n Rykard's Rancor \n\nSpell Type\nMagma Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 23\nSlots Used 2\n\nEffect \n\n        Releases searing spirits that repeatedly explode after delay \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 40 \n\nFaith 18 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nRykard's Rancor is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Rykard's Rancor is a Remembrance spell gained with the Blasphemous Blade and is powered with the Remembrance of the Blasphemous. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nThe terrible power of Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy. \nSummons searing spirits that leave a trail of delayed explosions in their wake.\nThese spirits manifest from the rancor of heroes who met a violent end. The lord granted them an audience, whereupon they were welcomed by the maw of the great serpent -- and within the serpent's bowels, they became the lord's kin. \n\nWhere to find Rykard's Rancor\nWhere to find Rykard's Rancor:\n\n Obtained when unlocking the Remembrance of the Blasphemous power by handing it over to Enia at Roundtable Hold. \n\nElden Ring Rykard's Rancor Guide\n\nMagma Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 36\nDeals fire damage\nOut of the Magma Sorceries, this is easily the best, as it deals consistent damage over time and has great tracking. It's main downside is the 18 Faith investment it takes to cast this spell, as the Prisoner and Astrologer classes start with very low Faith. This can be alleviated by the Two Fingers Heirloom, Marika's Scarseal and/or Faith boosting armor pieces like the Imp Head (Corpse).\nHowever, if the player desires only a cost efficient way to reset the Frostbite status on enemies for an Intelligence build, Magma Shot is more straightforward an cheaper. both in requirements and FP/Stamina consumption than this spell, but deals less damage.\nAs it deals fire damage, Rykard's Rancor can be used to reset an enemy's frost buildup, which can be useful for quickly proccing Frostbite multiple times. Note that multiple Frostbite procs will only result in more damage, and will not stack Frostbite's reduced damage negation.\nNote: FP cost was significantly reduced as of Patch 1.07, from 35 to 23\n\nCarian Spellknight Written Guide\n\nDeath Mage Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Rykard's Rancor Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Faith investment required to cast Rykard's Rancor a potential downside for certain classes in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Faith investment required for Rykard's Rancor is a potential downside for some classes in Elden Ring because classes like the Prisoner and Astrologer start with very low Faith, making it challenging to meet the Faith requirements to cast the spell efficiently.
