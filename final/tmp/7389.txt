CONTEXT
\n\n Ash of War: Storm Wall \n\nAffinity\nStandard\n\nSkill\nStorm Wall\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Storm Wall is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Storm Wall provides Standard affinity and the Storm Wall Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants no affinity to an armament, but imparts the following skill:\n"Storm Wall: Swing the shield to create a wall of storm winds in front of you, deflecting arrows and other such physical projectiles. Can also be used in the same way as a regular parry."\nUsable on small and medium shields.\n\nAsh of War: Storm Wall Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Storm Wall:\n\nFrom Stormhill Shack, head directly north, ignoring the road that bends toward the castle, until you reach the end of a collapsed bridge. Drop down onto an outcropping to the west to find a Teardrop Scarab that drops this Ash of War.
