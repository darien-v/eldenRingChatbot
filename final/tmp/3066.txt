QUESTION
What are the defensive properties and location details of the Sorcerer Leggings in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The defensive properties of the Sorcerer Leggings in Elden Ring are Dmg Negation Phy 2.3, VS Strike 3, VS Slash 3, VS Pierce 0.3, Magic 7.3, Fire 7.1, Ligt 6.8, Holy 7.2, Resistance Immunity 24, Robustness 7, Focus 39, Death 37, Poise 3. They can be found by defeating Glintstone Sorcerers and Twinsage Sorcerers in Raya Lucaria Academy.
