QUESTION
How can you rescue the Morne Spirit locked inside the cage behind the castle in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Head southwest from the Behind the Castle grace, down the cliff, then head northwest onto the roof, continue northwest and jump over the gap to the other roof, fall into the open gap in the roof grate to rescue the Morne Spirit.
