\n\n Primeval Sorcerer Azur \n\nLocation\nHermit Village\n\nDrops\nAzur's Glintstone Set\n\nPrimeval Sorcerer Azur is an NPC in Elden Ring.  Azur can be found near the cliffs north of the Hermit Village in Mt. Gelmir.\n\n Once a grand master at the Academy of Raya Lucaria, now exiled and residing near the Hermit Village.\n\nPrimeval Sorcerer Azur Location in Elden Ring\nPrimeval Sorcerer Azur can be found in the north of Hermit Village in Mt. Gelmir, near the edge of the cliffs. [Map Link]\n\nThis NPC does not move.\nPrimeval Sorcerer Azur cannot be fought, all attempts at physical and magical attacks simply will not register. As such, he cannot be killed.\n\nPrimeval Sorcerer Azur Questline: How to complete Primeval Sorcerer Azur's Quest\n\nPrimeval Sorcerer Azur is part of Sorceress Sellen Questline.\nAzur can be interacted with but he does not speak. He simply hands over the Comet Azur sorcery and the prompt to interact with him further disappears.\n\nElden Ring Primeval Sorcerer Azur Notes & Trivia\n\nAfter Sorceress Sellen transforms (by completing her questline and siding with her in the fight against Witch Hunter Jerren) you can return to Primeval Sorcerer Azur's location, where Azur's Glintstone Set will be in place of his body.\nWhat is most likely Azur's face can be seen in portraits found throughout Raya Lucaria Academy, as well as the Karolos Glintstone Crown, worn by Glintstone Sorcerers that are a part of the Karolos Conspectus, which Azur founded.\n\nElden Ring NPCs\n\nAbandoned Merchant Siofra River  ♦  Aeonian Spirit  ♦  Aeonian Swamp Spirit  ♦  Albinauric Village QUESTION
Who exiled Primeval Sorcerer Azur and why did he end up residing near the Hermit Village?

ANSWER
Primeval Sorcerer Azur was exiled by the Academy of Raya Lucaria and ended up near the Hermit Village as a consequence of his exile.
