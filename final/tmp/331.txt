QUESTION
Why is the Minor Erdtree in the Weeping Peninsula guarded by an Erdtree Avatar that attacks those who approach?

ANSWER
The Minor Erdtree in the Weeping Peninsula is guarded by an Erdtree Avatar as a defense mechanism to deter any potential threats from approaching and safeguard the area.
