The Scorpion's Stinger scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity. It is a good Weapon for quickly applying the rot effect and dealing powerful damage-over-time. Particularly great for defeating dragons. \n\nDagger fashioned from a great scorpion's tail, glistening with scarlet rot.\nA ceremonial tool used by heretics, crafted from the relic of a sealed outer god.\n\nWhere to Find Scorpion's Stinger in Elden Ring\nThe Scorpion's Stinger weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nFound in the Lake of Rot. Cross the swamp and go through the entrance with large statues on either side to reach the Grand Cloister Site of Grace. Use the ledges to drop down to the bottom, and head straight ahead (westward) and you'll find the dagger in a chest. QUESTION
How can players find Scorpion's Stinger in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can find Scorpion's Stinger in Elden Ring by locating it in the Lake of Rot, crossing the swamp to reach the Grand Cloister Site of Grace, then dropping down to find it in a chest.
