CONTEXT
\n\n Ice Spear \n\n        Skill of the warriors who served Lunar Princess Ranni. Spin armament to release cold magic, then channel it into a piercing spear of ice. \n\nIce Spear is a Skill in Elden Ring. Ice Spear is a regular skill found in Ashes of War and can be applied to polearms capable of thrusting, as well as twinblades. This is an offensive skill that conjures a spear of ice to pierce enemies.\n\nHow to get Ice Spear in Elden Ring\n\nDropped by a Night's Cavalry boss in the eastern Liurnia landmass, near the broken Gate Town Bridge. Only appears at night. [Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nElden Ring Ice Spear Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Ice Spear.\nFP Cost: 15 on use.\nDeal 25 stance damage\nThis Skill not Chargeable\nScales primarily with Dexterity (only that of the weapon), secondarily with Intelligence (intrinsic scaling plus the weapon's scaling), and does not scale with Strength to any extent.  \n\nIn other words, Heavy infusions inflict the least damage and can only slightly improve with Intelligence investment, while a Cold infusion with very strong Dexterity scaling and decent or better Intelligence scaling will inflict the most damage. \n\nUpon use, the user twirls their armament (weapon) before unleashing a projectile that inflicts frost buildup. \n\nBoth parts of the animation deal damage and frost buildup.\n\nRange of the projectile is only moderate and will not be able to hit distant enemies.\nWorks exceptionally well on Clayman's Harpoon with Magic and Cold infusion due to the scaling being primarily applied to magic damage, the former for pure projectile output and the latter for inflicting Frostbite status effect buildup.\nDamage can be further increased by wearing the Spellblade Set\nDespite having dexterity scaling, Ice Spear exclusively deals magic damage.\n\nIce Spear Demonstration\n\nFrost Knight Written Guide\n\nRoyal Knight Written Guide\n\nElden Ring QUESTION
What are the characteristics and attributes of the Ice Spear skill in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ice Spear is an offensive skill that conjures a spear of ice dealing magic damage, primarily scaling with Dexterity and secondarily with Intelligence, inflicting frost buildup, and is not chargeable in Elden Ring.
