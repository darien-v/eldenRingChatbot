Cookbooks are records of crafting techniques that players can use to learn how to craft a wide variety of items.\n\nA record of crafting techniques left by an armorer who served the great general Radahn.Contains knowledge of dealing with rot; the application of fire in particular.\nAcquire the knowledge to craft the following:\n- Redmane Fire Pot\n\nArmorer's Cookbook [4] Location in Elden Ring\n\nCan be found in Redmane Castle, in the western gate house.
