The Dragon Halberd scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and it is a good Weapon for those users with high Dexterity builds who can use the Dragon Halberd to slash Enemies while spinning. \n\nHalberd shaped like a dragon, enwreathed with both ice and lightning.\nAlas, the Dragonkin Soldiers never attained immortality, and perished as decrepit, pale imitations of their skyborn kin.\n\nWhere to Find Dragon Halberd in Elden Ring\nThe Dragon Halberd weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by the Dragonkin Soldier boss in Siofra River. This Dragonkin Soldier can be accessed by a portal directly south on the map of the Worshipper's Woods on ruins being led up to by a collapsed pillar with an archer sitting atop. [Map link] [Video location]\n\nElden Ring Dragon Halberd Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: Spinning Slash\nSpinning Slash on this weapon functions similarly to the standard version, but has an unstated additional effect of imbuing the weapon with additional lightning damage and frostbite build-up on hit for the next 45 seconds. This version of the skill will be faster than the regular Ash of War: Spinning Slash on all other Halberds.\nDespite scaling better to STR at +10, a more even spread will result in better AR than leaning more into STR for the same level of stat investment (56 STR / 34 DEX = 647AR vs 70 STR / 20 DEX = 639AR, 55 STR / 55 DEX = 694 AR vs 80 STR / 30 DEX = 690 AR).\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War.\nDragon Halberd can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones.\nDeals +10% damage against  Dragon-type enemies.\nDeals +5% damage against  Ancient Dragon-type enemies\nSpinning Slash buffs this weapon with Ice Lightning, adding 160 Lightning Damage and 80 Frost build up for 45 seconds, much like the Dragonscale Blade.\nSell value:   200\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
