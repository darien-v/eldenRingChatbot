QUESTION
Why is the Page Hood in Elden Ring described as providing more magical defenses than physical protection?

ANSWER
Page Hood in Elden Ring is described as providing more magical defenses than physical protection because its stats show higher values for Magic, Fire, Lightning, and Holy resistance compared to Strike, Slash, and Pierce damage negation.
