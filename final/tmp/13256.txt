Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nGauntlets of a knight banished form their motherland.Dirty and battered after enduring a lengthy vagabond journey.Metal armor is heavy, but also sturdy, offering significant damage negation. \n\nWhere to find Vagabond Knight Gauntlets in Elden Ring\nThe Vagabond Knight Gauntlets Armor Gauntlets piece can be found at the following location:\n\nA Starting Equipment Piece for the Vagabond Class.\nSold by the Nomadic Merchant located in eastern Mountaintops of the Giants for 1,000  Runes.
