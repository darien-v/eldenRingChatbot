The Erdtree Seal scales with Faith, and is a good Weapon for any high-faith character that wishes to use Incantations.\n\nA formless sacred seal decorated with an Erdtree crest, once the focus of religion in the Lands Between.Even though the Elden Ring is shattered, and the Erdtree has dulled from its former radiance, earnest faith continues to hold the answers.\n\nWhere to Find Erdtree Seal in Elden Ring\nThe Erdtree Seal weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nFound on a corpse in a cell near the Omenkiller enemy in the town below the Prison Town Church Site of Grace in Volcano Manor. [Map link]\nVideo Location\n\nElden Ring Erdtree Seal Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: No Skill\nThis weapon cannot be infused with Ashes of War  or buffed with Magic and Consumables.\nErdtree Seal can be upgraded by using Somber Smithing Stones.\nFaith scaling increases from C at +0 to B at +1, to an A at +3, and an S at +5.\nWhen both are fully upgraded, this seal grants equal incant scaling as the Finger Seal at 66 Faith, and begins to exceed it at 67.\nIncant scaling of 273 at +10 with 60 Faith; 353 at +10 with 80 faith; 367 at +10 with 99 faith.\nDespite the incantation category existing in the lore, Erdtree Seal does not provide a boost to Erdtree Incantations.\nErdtree Seal is the best sacred seal for casting instant healing spell, as they only benefit from faith-based scaling, and Erdtree Seal has the best faith scaling in the game.\nSell value:   200\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.08 a bug that prevented the follow-up attack from occurring when the Black Sword Incantation was activated with a Sacred Seal in the left hand has been fixed.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
