Guilty Set is a three piece, light cloth armor set. It can be seen on the bodies of dead humanoids crucified on wooden crucibles in Limgrave, and in Leyndell, Royal Capital. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are typically composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Guilty Set in Elden Ring\n\nThe Cloth Garb [Map Link] and Cloth Trousers [Map Link] can be found in Murkwater Cave.
