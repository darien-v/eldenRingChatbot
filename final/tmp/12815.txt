\n\n Assassin's Crimson Dagger \n\nWeight\n0.8\n\nEffect \n\n Critical attacks restore 10% + 85  \n       HP\n\nAssassin's Crimson Dagger is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Assassin's Crimson Dagger causes critical attacks to restore 10% +85 HP. Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nAn assassin's dagger, misshapen and stained in crimson.Critical hits restore HP.\nThis charm is modelled after the darkly gleaming blades used in the Night of Black Knives. Those which gave the demigods their first taste of death.\n\nAssassin's Crimson Dagger Effect in Elden Ring\nAssassin's Crimson Dagger causes critical attacks to restore 10% +85  HP.\n\nWhere to find Assassin's Crimson Dagger in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Assassin's Crimson Dagger:\n\nCan be found inside the Deathtouched Catacombs, dropped by the Black Knife Assassin.
