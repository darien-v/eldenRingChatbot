\n\n Ash of War: Golden Vow \n\nAffinity\nSacred\n\nSkill\nGolden Vow\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Golden Vow is an Ash of War in Elden Ring. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\nAsh of War: Golden Vow provides Sacred affinity and the Golden Vow Skill.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Sacred affinity and the following skill:\n"Golden Vow: Skill passed down from antiquity among the knights of the capital. Raise armament aloft and pledge to honor the Erdtree in battle, granting self and nearby allies increased attack power and defense."\nUsable on all melee armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Golden Vow Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ashes of War: Golden Vow:\n\nLimgrave: dropped by a mounted Godrick Knight on the cliff southwest above the Deathtouched Catacombs when defeated. He's at the east side hill of the Limgrave colosseum.[Elden Ring Map Link]\n\nAsh of War: Golden Vow Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants skill Golden Vow.\nProvides the Sacred affinity, which adds Holy Damage and Faith scaling, decreases physical damage, most damage negation.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Lightning, Sacred\nCan be applied to the
