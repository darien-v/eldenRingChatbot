QUESTION
How can I effectively utilize Sacred Tears to upgrade my Sacred Flask potency in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Sacred Tears can be used to upgrade the potency of Crimson and Cerulean Flasks to +12, found mostly in churches throughout Elden Ring, allowing for improved restorative effects.
