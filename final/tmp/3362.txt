QUESTION
How can one obtain Fire Monk Greaves in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Fire Monk Greaves can be obtained by farming Fire Monks in camps in western Liurnia of the Lakes, around Fort Laiedd in Mt. Gelmir, and around Guardians' Garrison in the Mountaintops of the Giants.
