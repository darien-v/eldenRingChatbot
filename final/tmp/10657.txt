\n\n Unseen Blade \n\nSpell Type\nNight Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 13\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Makes right-hand armament completely invisible \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 12 \n\nFaith 0 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nUnseen Blade is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Unseen Blade spell provides a buff that makes your weapon invisible. Specially useful for PVP. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the night sorceries of Sellia, Town of Sorcery.\nEnchants armament held in right hand, making it completely invisible.This sorcery can be cast while in motion.\nUsed by Sellian assassins to present themselves as unarmed sorcerers.\n\nWhere to find Unseen Blade\nWhere to find Unseen Blade:\nThis Sorcery can be found at the top of the Tower after completing the Mirage Rise puzzle. When you reach at the location, although your map marks there is a tower, you will notice that there is nothing there! However, you will receive a map, called Mirage Riddle. You will have to interact with three Phantom crests.\n\nThe first one is next to that initial position, hard to miss.\nThe second one can be found by continuing to the west, near the rock wall. Enemies will spawn.\nThe final one, is near the Bower of Bounty site of grace. Starting at the Bower of Bounty site of grace, head directly south to the edge of a cliff. Here a small enemy will spawn. Once you see this enemy, head east along the cliffside. On the left side you will see a strange looking stone which you have to hit in order to reveal the phantom crest. The stone lies before the bridge and will likely have messages surrounding it..\n\nOnce you found all three, head to where the tower was supposed to be, to actually find it this time. Climb all the way to the top to find this sorcery, along with Unseen Form and x5 Slumbering Egg.\n\nElden Ring Interactive Map Link\n\nElden Ring Unseen Blade Guide\n\nNight Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 10\nDuration: 45 seconds\nActs as a weapon buff and will remove/be removed by any other buff. \nCannot be used on element infused or unique weapons.\nNOTE: Since Patch 1.07 FP Consumption has been Decreased from 18 to 13.\n\nElden Ring Unseen Blade Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Unseen Blade spell particularly useful for PVP in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Unseen Blade spell is particularly useful for PVP in Elden Ring because it makes your weapon completely invisible, allowing players to deceive opponents and appear as unarmed sorcerers.
