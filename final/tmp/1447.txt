Sage Set is a lightweight armor of the wise sages who were deemed heretical. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Sage Set in Elden Ring\n\nCan be found in Stillwater Cave - Liurnia of the Lakes, just ahead of and below the Lake Facing Cliffs. To reach it you will have to follow the upper path around and then jump across to where two Servants of Rot are standing. There will be a tunnel path to follow. The set is all sitting on a poison submerged corpse near the entrance, guarded by two Giant Bats and a Chanting Winged Dame that will attack you when you take it. They can all be hit with ranged attacks before triggering aggro. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nSage Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nSage Hood.\nSage Robe.\nSage Trousers.\n\nSage Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nCompared to the Astrologer starting gear, this Set offer an overall lighter load on the cost of lower overall resistances.\nWorn by Sage Gowry and Necromancer Garris.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
Why is the Sage Set armor considered a lightweight option in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Sage Set armor is considered a lightweight option in Elden Ring due to its overall lighter load compared to other armor sets, sacrificing overall resistances for lower weight.
