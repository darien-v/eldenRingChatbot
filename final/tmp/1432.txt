QUESTION
Why does the Spellblade Set in Elden Ring boost the effectiveness of Glintstone sorcery skills specifically, and how does it achieve this compared to other sets in the game?

ANSWER
The Spellblade Set in Elden Ring boosts Glintstone sorcery skills specifically by increasing the damage of Magic-dealing ashes of war/skills by 2% per piece, not boosting any sorcery spells, such as Ice Spear, magic beam skill of Sword of Night and Flame, or any Ash of War on Horn Bow.
