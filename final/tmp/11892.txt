As hostile NPCs, Flame Guardians will chase and attack you on sight. Their name is displayed in bright red above their health bar, similar to NPC Invaders.\n\nFlame Guardian Location in Elden Ring\nFlame Guardians can be found at the following location:\n\nGuardians'
