\n\n Eagle \n\nLocation\nEverywhere in the Lands Between\n\nDrops\n\n 4 - 27 RunesFour-Toed Fowl FootFlight Pinion\n\nEagle is a Wildlife Creature in Elden Ring. Eagles are not hostile and will flee on approach. They are often perched near cliffs. Their materials are quite useful for making Arrows and other consumable items.\n\nEagle Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Eagle:\n\nPlayers can encounter Eagle near cliffs and mountainsides in the Lands Between.\nMany of them are found at the foot of the Limgrave Divine Tower on the other side of the bridge, past the teleporter. Beware of the large Stormhawks that will attack you if approached. \n\nElden Ring Eagle Combat Information\n\nHealth: 27 HP\nPoise: 0\n\nABSORPTIONS\n\nPhy (Standard): -40\nPhy (Slash): -40\nPhy (Strike): -40\nPhy (Pierce): -40\n\nMagic: -40\nFire: -100\nLightning: -40\nHoly:-40\n\nThe absorption numbers are the % of your damage that gets blocked. For example, if an absorption is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% is absorbed. Bigger number = less damage. An absorption of 100 means no damage goes through, and a resistance of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nRESISTANCES\n\nPoison: 63\nScarlet Rot: 63\nHemorrhage: 63\nFrostbite: 63\nSleep: 63\n\nThe resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it.
