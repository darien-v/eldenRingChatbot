It is found around the western side of the tree. This particular Avatar can be difficult to fight due to the small space to maneuver around its attacks, especially the spears of light. You can try to lure it further west where the area is slightly more open. It drops the Greenburst Crystal Tear and the Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear for your Flask of Wondrous Physick. Head northwest of tree and look for a tree branch extending from the cliffs. Walk along the tree branch to pick up a Cracked Pot at the end.\nCircling around the tree to the southeast, you can find the entrance to the Minor Erdtree Catacombs dungeon by the cliff face.\n\nElden QUESTION
Why is the Erdtree Avatar boss difficult to fight due to the small space to maneuver around its attacks, especially the spears of light?

ANSWER
The Erdtree Avatar boss is difficult to fight due to the small space to maneuver around its attacks, especially the spears of light, because it can limit your ability to dodge effectively and can quickly corner you.
