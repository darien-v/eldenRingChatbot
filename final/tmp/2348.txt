It is the cloth set worn by White Mask Varré. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find War Surgeon Set in Elden Ring\n\nDropped by one of three Nameless White Mask NPC invaders in the Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum area of Siofra River, west of the Palace Approach Ledge-Road site of grace, in the blood filled lake. The easiest way to get there is starting going down the hill from the site of grace and taking the first on the left to get to the blood lake. The needed invader spawns behind the massive stone pillar beside the Blistered Giant Crow. Elden Ring Interactive Map Link\nVideo Location\n\nWar Surgeon Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nWhite Mask\nWar Surgeon Gown\nWar Surgeon Gloves\nWar Surgeon Trousers\n\nWar Surgeon Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe White Mask piece increases the players attack power by 10% for 20 seconds once blood loss has been inflicted on an enemy.\nThe War Surgeon Gown can be altered to the War Surgeon Gown (Altered) to remove the cloak. This chest piece is initially dropped in its (altered) form, removing the neck drape and lowering its defensive properties. This can be undone at any site of grace so long as you have Tailoring Tools, or for free at Boc the Seamster.\nKilling Mohg, Lord of Blood will stop the Nameless White Mask invaders from spawning, locking the player out of obtaining this set.\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
