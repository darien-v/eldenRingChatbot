The Dragonclaw Shield is a Shield that scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity. Greatshields weigh a decent amount and provide the best protection in the game. Greatshields provide a greater value of blocking and protecting one's self from enemy and boss attacks, but greatly increases the weight and reduces the player's mobility. This type of shield works best at reducing damage or guarding instead of parrying.\n\nGreatshield said to have been whittled from the claw of a great, ancient dragon, wielded by grotesque Tree Sentinels who yet serve the Erdtree.\nImbued with lightning.\n\nWhere to Find Elden Ring Dragonclaw Shield\nThe Dragonclaw Shield Shield can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by the Draconic Tree Sentinel, fought at Capital Outskirts. [Elden Ring Map Link] Video Location\n\nElden Ring Dragonclaw Shield Notes & Tips\n\nShield Skill: Shield Bash\nThis shield cannot be infused with Ashes of War \nCannot be enchanted with Magic or buffed by Consumables\nDeals +30% damage against  Dragon type enemies\nDeals +15% damage against  Ancient Dragon type enemies\nSell Value: 500.\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
