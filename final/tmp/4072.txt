The Dragon Greatclaw scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity and is a good Weapon for going against dragon-like creatures and enemies. In addition to general Physical Damage, what makes this weapon great is that it does Lightning Damage which can be especially effective towards specific enemy types. \n\nWeapon said to have been whittled from the claw of a great, ancient dragon, wielded by grotesque Tree Sentinels who yet serve the Erdtree.\nThe claw is enwreathed with lightning, and tears through the dragons' feeble descendants with ease.\n\nWhere to Find Dragon Greatclaw in Elden Ring\nThe Dragon Greatclaw weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nDropped by the Draconic Tree Sentinel, fought at Capital Outskirts.
