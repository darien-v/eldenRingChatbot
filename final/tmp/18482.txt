QUESTION
Why is the Wailing Dunes considered a significant battleground in Elden Ring's lore?

ANSWER
The Wailing Dunes is a significant battleground in Elden Ring's lore because it was where the inconclusive battle between General Radahn and Malenia took place during the Shattering, along with being the site where the player and other warriors challenge General Radahn during Radahn Festival.
