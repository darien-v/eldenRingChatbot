\n\n Malformed Star \n\nLocation\nAinsel River Altus Tunnel/Perfumer's Grotto\n\nDrops\n 3844 - 7995 Runes\n\nSomber Smithing Stone [7]\n\nMalformed Star is an Enemy in Elden Ring. Malformed Stars are strange insectoid creatures with rock-like debris comprising their bodies and human skulls for heads. They hang from the ceiling and are able to shoot multiple rocks at players from a far distance. When at close-range, they will bite any player that gets too close. They are special enemies in that they do not respawn when killed.\n\nElden Ring Malformed Star Locations\nWhere to Find Malformed Star:\n\nUhl Palace Ruins\nAinsel River Main. Guarding the entrance to northern Uhl Palace Ruins\nAltus Tunnel and Perfumer's Grotto (connected dungeon)\n\nElden Ring Malformed Star Drops\n\n 3844 - 7995 Runes\nSomber Smithing Stone [7], guaranteed drop from only the one in Ainsel River Main specifically. \n\nMalformed Star Notes & Tips\n\nThese are a similar in appearance to Astel, Naturalborn of the Void. According to the description of the Remembrance of the Naturalborn, Astel is a part of this group, likely being a fully-grown specimen.\nThe encounter in the northern section of the Uhl Palace Ruins has plenty of cover, allowing you to get close enough to lock on to its head. In this instance, using Kukri will deal significant damage.\nThey resemble antlions, having the body and wings of the adult and the large and deadly mouthparts of the larva.\nThe way the creatures hang upside down is similar to how certain insect larva spin a cocoon to await maturity, or how nymphs hang onto branches to molt into their adult forms. This could assume that these creatures are immature juveniles, and are immobile due to being in metamorphosis.\nDespite their size, is very easy to block with a shield.\nHas very low health.\n\nMalformed Star Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone Cluster  ♦  Albinauric  ♦  Albinauric Wolfback Archer  ♦  Anastasia
