\n\n Ash of War: Lightning Ram \n\nAffinity\nLightning\n\nSkill\nLightning Ram\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Ram is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Lightning Ram provides Lightning affinity and the Lightning Ram Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Lightning affinity and the following skill:\n"Lightning Ram: Skill inspired by tumbling rams. Let out a bleat, then tumble forwards, clad in lightning. Tumbles can be repeated in rapid succession."\nUsable on all melee armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Ram Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Lightning Ram:\n\nIt can be found at Altus Plateau, just south of Sainted Hero's Grave. It drops from a Teardrop Scarab in the area. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nAsh of War: Lightning Ram Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Lightning Ram.\nDeals 6 stance damage\nProvides the Lightning affinity, which adds Lightning damage and increases Dexterity scaling, decreases Base damage and Strength scaling.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Lightning, Sacred\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds, Reapers, Fists, Claws, Whips, Colossal Weapons\nHas an FP cost of 5 initially and 5 more to sustain per additional cast.\n\nLightning Ram Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Lightning Ram Notes & Tips\n\nDamage influenced by weapons(also using Lightning Scorpion Charm, Shard of Alexander, and Blue Dancer Charm with 0 equip load)  seems to have an unbuffed "cap" of 653. I have been unable to find anything that makes it hit harder than that, I've spent  spent over an hour testing a bunch of different weapons... the 3 weapons I used to get this result were Keen Urumi(whip), Keen Pest's Glaive, and Keen Guardian's Swordspear, Keen for the extra Dex scaling. If you wanna get REALLY spicy offhand Commander's Standard and in another offhand Jellyfish Shield, buff with Commander's then Jellyfish shield and go crazy with Lightning Ram for ~30sec(duration of the shorter buff), which will make it hit for 783-1050ish+.  \n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric Roar  ♦  Ash of War: Barrage  ♦  Ash of War:
