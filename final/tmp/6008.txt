CONTEXT
\n\n Sacred Blade \n\n        Grants armament's attacks holy essence and fires off a golden blade projectile. The armament retains its holy essence for awhile. \n\nSacred Blade is a Skill in Elden Ring. Sacred Blade is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War that can be used on many melee armaments. Using this skill grants holy essence to the weapon and fires off a golden blade projectile, great to use against Those Who Live in Death. Updated to Patch 1.07\n\nHow to get Sacred Blade\n\nAsh of War: Sacred Blade - Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab North of the Third Church of Marika. Its location is next to a Spiritspring. [Elden Ring Map Link]\nInnate skill of the Miquellan Knight's Sword and Inseparable Sword.\n\nElden Ring Sacred Blade Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Sacred Blade.\nFP Cost: 19\nDeals 6 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried (the Holy projectile can be Parried with Carian Retaliation) \n\nThis Skill can be Parried if performed with no Focus.\n\nAfter windup it throws a holy arc to a single target with medium range.\nIt will also buff the weapon in your right hand with additional 90 holy attack power for 40 sec. Doesn't scale with Faith nor change with weapon kind and upgrades. This buff takes place right before the projectile is fired.\nTakes roughly 1.3 seconds to cast as of Patch 1.04.\nDeals 100% Holy Damage and an extra +100% damage to undead.\nDeals extra damage when hitting the weak spots of enemies, such as the head of a death bird.\nIf you are close enough to the target, you will hit the target with the weapon itself and deal additional weapon damage instead of just the projectile.\nEven at low levels of faith, the projectile deals a lot of damage to undead. A +9 sacred morning star with 15 faith averages nearly 1000 damage against skeletons.\nSkeletons that would usually reanimate will stay dead if killed by the projectile, even if a necromancer is nearby.\n\nNote: This Skill was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows:\n\nMotion speed and range of the blade have been increased.\nDamage detection to the weapon part has been added.\nEffect duration increased from 20 to 40 seconds and holy attack power increased from 85 to 90.\n\nSacred Blade Demonstration\n\nPerfect Paladin Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning QUESTION
What are the stats and effects of the Sacred Blade skill in Elden Ring after Patch 1.07?

ANSWER
Sacred Blade skill stats in Elden Ring after Patch 1.07 include: FP Cost: 19, Deals 6 stance damage, Adds 90 holy attack power for 40 sec, 100% Holy Damage, +100% vs undead, Extra damage on weak spots, Faster motion speed and increased range, Damage detection, Extended effect duration.
