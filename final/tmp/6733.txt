The Troll's Hammer scales primarily with Strength, Faith, and Dexterity, and is a good Weapon for dealing Physical Damage, but also can deal Fire Damage. This weapon is best used when surrounded by multiple foes due to its accompanying Skill, Troll's Roar.  This allows the user to distance themself away from nearby enemies, by generating a shockwave that blows them back. \n\nMining tool of stonedigger trolls used to crack bedrock. \nTrolls are descended from the giants, and these were supposedly once used as ceremonial smithing tools. In the distant past, smithing was considered divine. \n\nWhere to Find Troll's Hammer in Elden Ring\nThe Troll's Hammer weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nFound in a storage shed inside the Old Altus Tunnel dungeon.
