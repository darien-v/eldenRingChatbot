Black Knife Set is a special heavy armor used by assassins due to its stealth-enhancing materials. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Black Knife Set in Elden Ring\nThe full set is found on a corpse under an archway behind Ordina, Liturgical Town in Consecrated Snowfield. [Map here]\n\nBlack Knife Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nBlack Knife Hood\nBlack Knife Armor\nBlack Knife Gauntlets\nBlack Knife Greaves\n\nBlack Knife Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nThe Black Knife Armor can be altered to the Black Knife Armor (Altered) to remove the cape.\nThe chest armor piece of this set muffles the sound of the wearer's footsteps, similar to the effect of the Crepus's Vial talisman and the Assassin's Approach incantation. The chest armor piece needs to be unaltered, with the cape intact, to receive this bonus. The cape only adds 0.2 weight, so leaving it on is highly advised. This silence effect also affects the wearer's rolls and jumps.\nThe chest piece comes with an almost fully transparent cloak, including a hood. \n\nThe hood is worn over the following helms and other headwear: \n\nBlack Knife Hood\nBloodsoaked Mask\nAstrologer Hood\nPerfumer Hood\nChain Coif\nFingerprint Helm\nEccentrics Hood (Altered)\nNox Swordstress Crown (Altered)\nNox Monk Hood (Altered)\n\nPoise updated to patch 1.07.
