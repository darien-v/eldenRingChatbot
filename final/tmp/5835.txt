\n\n Thops's Barrier \n\n        Erect a magical forcefield while swinging the shield to deflect sorceries and incantations. Can also be used in the same way as a regular parry. \n\nThops's Barrier is a Skill in Elden Ring. Thops's Barrier is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War that can be used on shields.\n\nHow to get Thops's Barrier\n\nIn Church of Irith: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab after delivering the spare Academy Glintstone Key to Thops. Reload the area after delivering the key to spawn the scarab.
