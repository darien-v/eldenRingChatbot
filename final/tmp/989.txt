\n\n Converted Fringe Tower \n\nA tower found on the fringe in the northeastern tip of Liurnia.\n\nConverted Fringe Tower is a Location in Elden Ring. The Converted Fringe Tower is found in Bellum Highway. The tower is found at the edge of the cliffs in the very northeast of Liurnia of the Lakes, head east from the Frenzied Flame Village Outskirts Site of Grace.\n\nConverted Fringe Tower Map\nThe Converted Fringe Tower can be found on our interactive map by clicking on [Elden Ring Map here].\n\nAll NPCs and Merchants in Converted Fringe Tower\n\nNo NPCs are in this tower.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Converted Fringe Tower\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nEye of Yelough\nMiquella's Lily\nMushroom\nCrystal Dart\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no Upgrade Materials in this Location\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nCannon of Haima\nGavel of Haima\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no key items in this location\n\nElden Ring Converted Fringe Tower Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nSchool of Graven Mages\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nThere are no bosses in this location\n\nConverted Fringe Tower Walkthrough\nThe top of the tower is sealed by a puzzle. Read the message at the foot of the statue is a hint to solve to puzzle:\n"May erudition light the way!"\nTo clear the puzzle, perform the Erudition gesture in front of the statue while wearing a Glintstone Crown that lights up when you perform the gesture.\nThese crowns are:\n\nKarolos Glintstone Crown\nLazuli Glintstone Crown\nOlivinus Glintstone Crown\nTwinsage Glintstone Crown\nWitch's Glintstone Crown\n\nNote: The Azur, Lusat, Haima and Hierodas Glintstone Crowns do not work for this.\nThe room at the top of the stairs will summon a School of Graven Mages, this is the only enemy in the tower, after the fight take the elevator up to get to the chest at the top and retrieve the Gavel of Haima and Cannon of Haima.\n\nElden Ring Converted Fringe Tower Gallery and Notes\n[other
