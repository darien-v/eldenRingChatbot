\n\n Storm Stomp \n\n        A Skill brought to Stormveil by knights from lost lands. Stomp the ground hard to kick up a temporary storm. \n       It can be used on all melee armaments. \n\nStorm Stomp is a Skill in Elden Ring. Storm Stomp is a regular skill that can be found in Ashes of War and applied to compatible weapons.\n\nHow to get Storm Stomp in Elden Ring\n\nAsh of War: Storm Stomp - Found in the southeastern area of Gatefront Ruins. There is a set of stairs that leads to a small room underground where a treasure chest can be found. Inside the chest is the Ash of War: Storm Stomp and Whetstone Knife key item. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nElden Ring Storm Stomp Guide, Notes & Tips\n\nThis is an Ash of War Skill, associated with the Ash of War: Storm Stomp.\nFP Cost: 6\nDeals 6 stance damage\nThis Skill is not Chargeable\nThis Skill cannot be Parried\nEquip the weapon that you have bound Storm Stomp to and press L2/LB to activate the ability.\nThe skill creates a large AOE via a stomp animation that lasts about 1 second. \nDoesn't deal much damage, but has a lot of hitstun when it lands. This enables it to have great combo potential.\n\nElden Ring Skills\n\nAncient Lightning Spear  ♦  Angel's Wings  ♦  Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Barbaric Roar  ♦  Barrage  ♦  Barricade Shield  ♦  Bear
