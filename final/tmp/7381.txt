\n\n Ash of War: Stormcaller \n\nAffinity\nQuality\n\nSkill\nStormcaller\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Stormcaller is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Stormcaller provides Quality affinity and the Stormcaller Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Quality affinity and the following skill:\n"Stormcaller: One of the skills that channel the tempests of Stormveil. Spin armament to create surrounding storm winds. Repeated inputs allow for up to two follow-up attacks."\nUsable on swords capable of slashing, axes, hammers, and polearms (small and colossal weapons excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Stormcaller Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Stormcaller:\n\nStormveil Castle: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in the northern section of the castle, past the courtyard and just before the Secluded Cell Site of Grace. QUESTION
How can the Ash of War: Stormcaller be applied to different weapon categories in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Ash of War: Stormcaller can be applied to Straight Swords, Greatswords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds, and Reapers, as long as the weapon allows skill replacement.
