\n\n Howl of Shabriri \n\nSpell Type\nFrenzied Flame Incantations\n\nFP Cost 21\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Shrieks, building up madness in nearby foes \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 33 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nHowl of Shabriri is an Incantation in Elden Ring. The Howl of Shabriri spell creates madness buildup in nearby foes. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nIncantation originating from the maddening Three Fingers.\nReleases a maddening shriek that causes madness buildup in foes nearby.This incantation also causes madness buildup in the caster and makes enemies more likely to target them.\nIt is said that the sickness of the flame of frenzy began with Shabriri, the most reviled man in all history.\n\nHowl of Shabriri Location in Elden Ring\nThe Howl of Shabriri spell can be found in the following location:\n\nFound in a chest on the second level of the Frenzy-Flaming Tower. [Elden Ring Map Link] \n\nFrom the Grand Lift of Dectus grace site, move South through a mountain path and follow the path. You will come upon a small incline going towards a tall stone tower, use the nearby rocks as cover from the flaming orb above the tower as you make your way towards it. Take a left to climb onto a set of rocks, to your left will be the tower with some small rats and a large rat at the base. Climb 2 ladders and turn around to see a chest containing the Howl of Shabriri incantation.\n\nElden Ring Howl of Shabriri Guide\n\nFrenzied Flame Incantation\nStamina Cost: 38\nDeals Fire damage\nCauses severe Madness buildup (35 per tick) in enemies in an area of effect around the caster, as well as slight (6 per tick) madness buildup in the caster. \n\nThe amount of Madness dealt does not appear to change with incant scaling or the seal used.\nNote that all but a handful of enemies in PVE are immune to Madness.\n\nDeals slight damage, but not enough for the spell to be useful offensively against enemies immune to Madness. \n\nHowever, this damage can be useful to prevent low-poise enemies from attacking.\n\nWhen cast, the caster receives simultaneous buffs and debuffs: \n\nDamage +25%\nDamage Taken +30%\nEnemies are more likely to attack the caster\nDuration: 40 secs. Increased to 52 secs. with Old Lord's Talisman\n\nThis is a Body Buff and will not stack with other buffs of the same type. \n\nSee Buffs and Debuffs for more information on stacking buffs.\n\nNote: This Spell was changed with Patch 1.07 as follows: Effect duration has been extended from 20 to 40 seconds.\nAs of Patch 1.09, Roar Medallion increases the damage of the howl area of effect about approx. 2% in Player vs. Player.  No change in the increase of the damage buff from this incantation.\n\nElden Ring Howl of Shabriri Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can the Howl of Shabriri spell be strategically used in combat scenarios within Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Howl of Shabriri spell can be used to cause madness buildup in enemies and make them more likely to target the caster, while simultaneously receiving a damage buff and debuff to manage combat encounters effectively in Elden Ring.
