QUESTION
How can one obtain Dragon Hearts in Elden Ring and what are their uses in the game?

ANSWER
Dragon Hearts in Elden Ring can be obtained by defeating dragons like Borealis, the Freezing Fog and Decaying Ekzykes, and are used for trading at the Church of Dragon Communion for Dragon-based Incantations.
