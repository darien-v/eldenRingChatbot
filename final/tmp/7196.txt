GelmirVolcano Manor\n\nDrops\n 1312 - 3181 Runes\n\nAbductor Virgin or Iron Virgin is an Enemy in Elden Ring.\n\nElden Ring Abductor Virgin Locations\nWhere to find Abductor Virgin:\n\nCaria Manor\nRaya Lucaria Academy\nRedmane Castle\nSealed Tunnel\nMt Gelmir\nVolcano Manor\n\nAbductor Virgin Appearance\nThe Iron Virgin is a large metal construct resembling a metal statue of a saintly woman cradling a small silver baby to its chest, with several snakes winding around her upper body. From its shoulders hang a pair of long, spindly arms ending in one of two deadly weapons- a pair of pendulum blades on chains, or a pair of massive circular sawblades attached to long, rigid forearms. Its "dress" features a set of doors that swing open to reveal a hollow chamber full of blood-red fog and several metal snakes coiled up around a central rod hanging from below the Virgin's torso. Six of these metal snakes form a pair of long, tentacle-like "arms", each made of three snakes wound together and ending in three-fingered "hands", with the snakes' heads forming the fingers. Instead of legs, the Iron Virgin rolls around on a spherical wheel, allowing it to move in any direction horizontally without having to turn its whole body around.\n\nAbductor Virgin Behavior\nIron Virgins can be found slowly patrolling their territory on the lookout for intruders, or simply standing still on guard. When attacking, the Iron Virgin will immediately rush over and attack their target, spinning their arms in a wide circle to cut down enemies from every direction. The sickle-wielding variety can launch their blades from far away with deadly accuracy, even leading their blade in whatever direction their target is running so as not to miss, and then pull it back via its chain. Sawblade-wielding iron virgins prefer to charge forward at their targets with sawblades spinning full-tilt, mowing down opponents like a bulldozer.\nAll types of Iron Virgin have a special attack using their torture chambers- when an opponent gets too close, the doors in their front swing open, the snake-arms inside unfold, and they will attempt to grab anyone who gets too close. Once ensnared, the Iron Virgin's victim will be dragged inside, the doors will shut, and they will be helplessly attacked by the metal snakes inside before being unceremoniously thrown back out, if their victim doesn't outright die while inside the torture chamber. Abductor Virgins, which are visually indistinguishable from Iron Virgins, will teleport whoever they capture to another location far away.\n\nElden Ring Abductor Virgin Drops\n\n 1312 - 3181 Runes\n\nAbductor Virgin Notes & Tips\n\nDeals Standard Damage.\nWeak to Lightning Damage (Lightning Spear is especially useful for this)\nNeutral to Strike Damage, low resistance to other types of damage. \n\nLone Abductor Virgin met at lava part of Volcano Manor is immune to Fire Damage\n\nImmune to Status Effects.\nParryable: No\nCan be stance-broken, but not riposted afterwards\nWeak spot: Innards, exposed both after stance-break and during a charge attack.\nTakes extra stance damage from lightning damage. 5 casts of Thunderbolt  are sufficient to stance break them, but repeated casts are unlikely to hit the innards as the head usually blocks the innards from the thunderbolt.\nOne full cycle of Dragon Halberd skill breaks their stance. Other full cycle targeted at their innards should be enough to eliminate them.\n\nWith Dragon Halberd it is recommended to bait their ranged attack, as it gives enough time to use said skill.\n\nBeware of getting face-to-face in melee range - you will take massive damage if caught by the hands in a grab attack.\nThe Swinging Sickle blades can and will easily pass through walls, so hiding behind a corner will do you no good. However, the Iron Virgin cannot jump, and it has difficulty attacking targets on higher ground, so the terrain can still be used to your advantage.\nThe one you encounter at the bottom of the lift in the Academy can be easily beaten by standing underneath the wheel of the lift. It can't reach you from there.  Beware, as dying by its grab attack will trap you in the Volcano Manor.\nA Headless  Wheel variant can be seen in Mt. Gelmir, near the village filled with Demi-Humans. It behaves erratically, often rushing all over the place, killing anything that gets in its way. \n\nHowever, if this enemy is under the effect or "rune bonus", sparks will be where regular Virgin has eyes.\n\nThe one at Redmane Castle has one pendulum blade, and one sawblade, utilizing both to its advantage.\nOne can use the Wheel of the Abductor Virgin, called Ghiza's Wheel, dropped by Inquisitor QUESTION
Why is the Abductor Virgin immune to fire damage in the lava part of Volcano Manor?

ANSWER
The Abductor Virgin is immune to fire damage in the lava part of Volcano Manor because it is a special Lone variant encountered in that specific location.
