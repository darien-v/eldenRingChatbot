\n\n Ash of War: Seppuku \n\nAffinity\nBlood\n\nSkill\nSeppuku\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament for 60 seconds.\n\nAsh of War: Seppuku is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Seppuku provides Blood affinity and the Seppuku Skill for 60 seconds. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Blood and the following skill:\n"Seppuku: A forbidden technique used by swordsmen from the Land of Reeds. Plunge the blade into your stomach to stain it with blood. Increases attack power and improves ability to inflict blood loss."\nUsable on swords as well as polearms capable of thrusting (small and colossal weapons not included).\n\nAsh of War: Seppuku Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Seppuku:\n\nOn the frozen lake east of the Freezing Lake site of grace in the Mountaintops of The Giants, dropped by an invisible scarab. [Map Link]\n\nAsh of War: Seppuku Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Seppuku.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Poison, Blood, Occult\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Straight Swords, Greatswords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds\n\nAsh of War: Seppuku Videos\n\nSeppuku Demonstration\n\nThrusting Sword Build Guide\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Seppuku Notes & Tips\n\nConflicts with (both cannot affect a weapon simultaneously): Bloodflame\nThe self-damage caused by this skill doesn't trigger the damage negation from Opaline Bubbletear, meaning both can be used together.\nCan effectively be used to counter blood loss caused by enemies or in PvP. When used, the weapon art will trigger blood loss, dispelling the blood loss gauge no matter how much it has been built up, sacrifcing 15% +100 of your HP for using the weapon art instead of losing a larger chunk because of blood loss being procced by enemies or another players.\nThe blood loss inflicted upon yourself can activate the effects of the White Mask and Lord of Blood's Exultation, granting you a very powerful 30% damage increase immediately.\nSeppuku's buff scales with Arcane, providing a higher bleed buildup with weapons that have either innate scaling, or gain said scaling (via Occult infusion). \n\nA 80 Arcane Occult-infused weapon with innate bleed will gain more bleed buildup from Seppuku than a Blood-infused weapon with Seppuku.\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of QUESTION
Why is the Ash of War: Seppuku beneficial for both PvP and countering blood loss caused by enemies?

ANSWER
The Ash of War: Seppuku is beneficial for both PvP and countering blood loss as it enables the user to dispel blood loss gauge, activate damage increase effects, and gain higher bleed buildup scaling with Arcane compared to Blood-infused weapons.
