\n\n Crucible Feather Talisman \n\nWeight\n0.8\n\nEffect \n\nImproves dodge rolling but increases damage taken \n\nCrucible Feather Talisman is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Crucible Feather Talisman improves the effectiveness of dodge rolls, but increases damage taken. Players can use Talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman fashioned from feathers that embody the aspects of various creatures.Said to have grown on the human body long ago.\nImproves the effectiveness of dodge rolls, but increases damage taken at all times.\nA vestige of the crucible of primordial life. Born partially of devolution, it was considered a signifier of the divine in ancient times, but is now increasingly disdained as an impurity as civilization has advanced.\n\nCrucible Feather Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nCrucible Feather Talisman improves the effectiveness of dodge rolls, but increases damage taken. Frame data assumes 60 FPS:\n\nLight/Medium Roll Invulnerability Duration +23% (+6 iframes, 32 Total)\nHeavy (Fat) Roll Invulnerability Duration +25% (+6 iframes, 30 Total)\nDamage Negation -30%\n\nDodge rolling effectiveness does not stack with the Windy Crystal Tear.\n\nWhere to find Crucible Feather Talisman in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Crucible Feather Talisman:\n\nFound on a corpse inside the Auriza Hero's Grave [Elden Ring Map Link]:\n\nFrom the first Chariot Hall, drop down through the opening on either side just before the second ramp.\nIn the room below, head west and up the steps, then turn right into the next corridor that leads to a large hall with two Chariots running almost side by side, with a short delay between them. Head all the way down towards the pit behind the pillars.\nLooking down, you will see several wooden beams that you can drop onto. Navigate your way to the lowest beam and then head south towards the archway with a ladder at the back. Climb the ladder to reach a third Chariot Hall with a single Chariot.\nHead all the way down this hall, using the alcove on the left about halfway through to avoid the Chariot (unless you've previously destroyed it). At the bottom, turn left into a corridor and head straight to find a small room guarded by an Omen. The talisman can be found on a corpse behind the Omen, sitting by a statue.\n\nCrucible Feather Talisman Builds\n\nSlumbering Swordstress  Guide\n\nElden Ring Crucible Feather Talisman Notes &
