Alberich's Set. Set with red glintstones said to be formed by the blood of sacrifices. Strengthens Thorn Sorceries. Mad Tongue Alberich was an aloof yet disturbed heretical sorcerer said to have been driven mad by jeering tongues during his service to the Roundtable Hold long ago. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Alberich's Set in Elden Ring\nThe entire Alberich's Set can be found in the main hall of the Fortified manor in Leyndell, Royal Capital, near the Fortified Manor, first floor Site of Grace. See it on the Elden Ring Map here.
