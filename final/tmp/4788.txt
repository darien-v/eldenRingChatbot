\n\n Mausoleum Soldier Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 75\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons five mausoleum soldier spirits \n\nMausoleum Soldier Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Mausoleum Soldier Ashes summons five mausoleum soldier spirits.\nMausoleum Soldiers can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Mausoleum Soldier.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell. Use to summon the spirits of five mausoleum soldiers. Spirits of five headless soldiers who confound foes by disappearing and reappearing at will. The mausoleum is where the bodies of soulless demigods are lain to rest, and these soldiers followed their masters into Death by severing their own heads from their bodies. \n\nWhere to find Mausoleum Soldier Ashes\nWhere to Find Mausoleum Soldier Ashes\n\nLocated at the base of a small dead tree at the edge of a cliff in the Deeproot Depths.From the The Nameless Eternal City site of grace located in the Deeproot Depths, go north until you reach the Mausoleum. From there, go north toward the cliff and follow the edge until you reach the northmost location possible.
