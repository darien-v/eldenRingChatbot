\n\n Great Wyrm Theodorix \n\nLocation\nConsecrated Snowfield\n\nDrops\n180,000  Runes (NG)135,000  Runes (NG, Co-op Host)45,000  Runes (NG, Co-op Phantom)Dragon Heart x3\n\nHP\n25,649\n\nStronger VS\n\nFire\n\nFrostbite\n\nWeaker to\n\nStandard\n\nStrike\n\nPierce  \n\nSleep\n\nGreat Wyrm Theodorix is a Great Enemy Boss in Elden Ring. Theodorix was once a troll, but was transformed into a lesser dragon for the sin of Dragon Communion and now lives on the frozen river in the Consecrated Snowfield. This is an optional boss, as players don't need to defeat it in order to advance in Elden Ring.\nSee also Magma Wyrm and Magma Wyrm Makar.\n\nThe name of the ancient troll warrior Theodorix lives on — as a hero of the War against the Giants.\n\nElden Ring Great Wyrm Theodorix Boss\n\nThis is an optional boss\nClosest Site of Grace: Cave of the Forlorn\nMultiplayer is allowed for this boss\nYou can summon  Spirit Ashes  for this boss\n\nElden Ring Great Wyrm Theodorix Location\nHead north from the Inner Consecrated Snowfield Site of Grace to the river of ice, then follow it east and you'll run into Theodorix. Consider opening the Cave of the Forlorn with a Stonesword Key to get a closer Site of Grace, but beware of the Giant Land Octopus guarding its entrance. For example, if a negation is 60, 40% of that damage by that type will go through and 60% will be negated. Bigger number = less damage. A negation of 100 means no damage goes through, and a negation of -100 mean the enemy takes 2x damage from that source. 0 means damage goes pretty much as is.\n\nResistances\n\nPoison: 653 / 943 / 1400\nScarlet Rot: 653 / 943 / 1400\nHemorrhage: 653 / 943 / 1400\nFrostbite: 1406 / 1863\n\nSleep: 290 / 332 / 430 / 720\nMadness: Immune\n\nThe resistance numbers are the buildup amount to trigger it. For example, if a resistance is 100 you must deal 100 points of the given buildup to trigger it. Note that these go down over time, and increase each time the effect procs. The values after the "/"s indicate the increased resistances after each successive proc.\n\n NG+ and Beyond (click to reveal)\n\nAdditional Fight Strategy\n\nGoad the nearby Giant Land Octopuses into fighting Theodorix. They can take and deal a lot of damage before going down.\nRun past him and climb on the ledge that goes behind his resting spot for a safe zone to snipe him from. This ledge also has an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone.\n\nGreat Wyrm Theodorix Lore, Notes & Other Trivia\n\nTheodorix was once a troll warrior, a veteran of the War Against the Giants, and became a Magma Wyrm upon indulging the Dragon Communion.\nTheodorix is automatically hostile towards Large Land Octopuses, Lesser Crabs, and Fire Slugs.
