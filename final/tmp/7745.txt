\n\n Burn O Flame! 火よ、焼き尽くせ！ \n\nSpell Type\nFire Giant Incantations\n\nFP Cost 26\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Raises a series of flame pillars around caster \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 27 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nBurn O Flame!  is an Incantation in Elden Ring. Burn O Flame! spell creates fire pillars around your character. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nOne of the incantations that draws directly from the power of the Fire Giants.\nRaises a series of flame pillars around the caster.\nCharging further increases the number of flame pillars.\nThe Fire Giants borrowed from the power of a fell god, and still they were defeated. Yet their failure released them from their solitary curse: to serve as keepers of the Flame for eternity.\n\nWhere to find Burn O Flame!\nWhere to find Burn O Flame! :\n\nTrade Remembrance of the Fire Giant with Enia at Roundtable Hold.\n\nElden Ring Burn O Flame! Guide\n\nFire Giant Incantation\nStamina Cost: 36\nDeals fire damage and 47 stance damage\nThis enchantment allows users to raise a series of pillars of flame around themselves.\nDamage can be extremely high against large enemies when multiple pillars hit, though even a single pillar deals a significant amount of damage.\nUncharged, this spell has a fairly quick startup, making it a very useful crowd control option if you can afford the high FP cost.\nNote: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost reduced from 30 to 26, and shortened the time it takes for a flame pillar to be generated.\n\nPyromancer Written Guide\n\nElden Ring Burn O Flame! Notes and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07.
