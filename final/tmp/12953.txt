Black Flame's Protection Incantation increases physical damage negation.\n\nA black flame incantation of the Godskin Apostles.\nSummons black fire within, increasing physical damage negation. However, sacred flasks and other such forms of HP restoration are impaired.\nThe Apostles were all embraced by the Gloam-Eyed Queen, and the black flame was their armor within.\n\nWhere to find Black Flame's Protection\nWhere to find Black Flame's Protection:\n\nReceived from Gideon Ofnir, after selecting the "about the secret medallion" dialogue option. In order for this question to appear, you will first need Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left) and Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right). The dialogue option is unlocked upon reaching Miquella's Haligtree.\nIf not received by Gideon Ofnir, it can be purchased at Twin Maiden Husks after reaching Leyndell, Ashen Capital.\n\nElden Ring Black Flame's Protection Guide\n\nGodskin Apostle Incantation\nStamina Cost: 13\nIncreases physical damage negation by 35%.\nReduces all healing by 20%.\nLasts 70 seconds.\nStacks QUESTION
How can one counteract the reduction in healing caused by Black Flame's Protection Incantation in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
By equipping the Crimson Seed Talisman, the reduction in healing from Black Flame's Protection Incantation in Elden Ring can be entirely negated.
