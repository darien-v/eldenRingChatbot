QUESTION
Why is the Lesser Demi-Human Chief more aggressive and deals more damage during nighttime as indicated by red eyes?

ANSWER
The Lesser Demi-Human Chief is more aggressive and deals more damage during nighttime as indicated by red eyes because it signifies their heightened aggression and increased combat prowess under the cover of darkness.
