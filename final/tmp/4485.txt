\n\n Ash of War: Sacred Order \n\nAffinity\nSacred\n\nSkill\nSacred Order\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Sacred Order is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Sacred Order provides Sacred affinity and the Sacred Order Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Sacred affinity and the following skill:\n"Sacred Order: Skill of the Golden Order fundamentalist knights. Perform a salute and grant the armament holy essence. Highly effective against Those Who Live in Death."\nUsable on all melee armaments.\n\nAsh of War: Sacred Order Location in Elden Ring\nAsh Of War: Sacred Order is dropped by a Teardrop Scarab in Altus Plateau, North-West of the Abandoned Coffin Site Of Grace. Map Link\n\nAsh of War: Sacred Order Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Sacred Order.\nProvides the Sacred affinity, which adds Holy damage and Faith scaling, decreases physical damage, most damage negation.\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Lightning, Sacred\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Daggers, Straight Swords, Greatswords, Colossal Swords, Curved Swords, Curved Greatswords, Katanas, Twinblades, Thrusting Swords, Heavy Thrusting Swords, Hammers, Great Hammers, Axes, Greataxes, Flails, Spears, Great Spears, Halberds, Reapers, Fists, Claws, Whips, Colossal Weapons\n\nSacred Order Demonstration\n\nBuilds using Sacred Order\n\nLightning Lancer Written Guide \n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Sacred Order Notes & Tips\n\nDuration: 60 seconds (78s with Old Lord's Talisman). It's canceled when the weapon it's equipped on stops being held.\nBoosts all damage caused by the player by 10%.\nBoosts Weapon, Ash of War and Spell (not Consumable) damage by 100% to Undead type enemies and prevents Skeletons from reviving.\nIt's one way to get low Faith investment Holy damage on weapons. Order's Blade Incantation provides a higher increase in Holy damage overall, and other Ashes Of War can provide the same access to Sacred weapon affinity.\nIn practice, it functions kind of like a single target Golden Vow, but the description somehow completely forgets to mention it.\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's Gambit  ♦  Ash of War: Barbaric Roar  ♦  Ash of War: QUESTION
How can Ash of War: Sacred Order be used effectively in combat situations in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Ash of War: Sacred Order can be effectively used to grant the Sacred affinity for Holy damage and Faith scaling, decrease physical damage, boost damage against Undead enemies by 100%, and provide a 10% damage increase overall when equipped on melee armaments.
