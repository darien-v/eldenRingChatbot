The Staff of Loss scales primarily with Strength, Dexterity, and Intelligence and is a good Weapon for those looking to play an ambusher type of caster. \n\nStaff missing its glintstone. Wielded by sorcerers who believe that discovery comes through acts of asceticism.\nThis staff only distinguishes itself when casting invisibility sorceries, but that is reason enough for some to wield it.\n\nWhere to Find Staff of Loss in Elden Ring\nThe Staff of Loss weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nSellia, Town of Sorcery: Found on a corpse leaning over a building's balcony in the west. Accessible by jumping across the rooftops. See the Sellia page for details on how to get it. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring Staff of Loss Notes & Tips\n\nWeapon Skill: This armament has no skill. If the armament in the other hand has a skill, that skill will be used instead.\nThis weapon can be infused with Ashes of War\nStaff of Loss can be upgraded by using Smithing Stones\nDespite the description, this staff does not block the casting of non-Invisibility spells.\nBoosts attack power of Invisibility sorceries by 30%.\n\nInvisibility sorceries include Night Shard and Night Comet. Also note that night sorceries are not dodged by NPC characters, making this an excellent staff to fight enemies that tend to dodge other ranged attacks\nAs of Patch 1.03.2, this 30% damage buff will be applied to appropriate spells regardless if it is the staff being used to cast. It only needs to be visibly equipped on the player character to apply.\nDespite seeming like they belong in the category of Invisibility, the following sorceries do not have increased damage or duration according to testing: Ambush Shard, Night Maiden's Mist, and Eternal Darkness.\nDespite being categorized as sorceries of invisibility internally, the following sorceries are unaffected because they do not deal damage: Unseen Blade and Unseen Form.\n\nWith 80 INT, this staff has: 179 sorcery scaling at +0 // 339 sorcery scaling at +25\nAt ~80 INT, this staff will outperform any staff in the game in terms of raw damage, so long as you limit yourself to only night sorceries (which is quite limiting).\nUsing 2 staffs simultaneously will cause the 30% boost to stack multiplicatively, creating the highest boost that can be attained for a single magic school (Gravity sorceries can be boosted similarly, but lack the ability to upgrade the staff) \n\nThis can only be done in NG+ or by getting someone to drop the second staff for you, as only one can be obtained per playthrough\n\nThis staff has the same handle as the Carian Glintblade Staff, though it's a different color, and lacks a glintstone (or perhaps, it is just invisible).\nSell Value:   200 Runes\nNOTE: As of Patch 1.09, the scaling values of this weapon were changed.
