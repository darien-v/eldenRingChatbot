CONTEXT
\n\n Slash Damage \n\nCategory\nPhysical Damage\n\nSlash Damage is one of the Damage Types in Elden Ring. It is one of the eight main Damage Types and belongs to the Physical Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt in Combat and are affected by many factors including your equipment, skills and temporary buffs.\n\nElden Ring Slash Damage Guide\nSlash Damage is a type of Physical Damage that is often associated with sharp weapons such as Curved Swords, Daggers and Katanas. Weapons that primarily deal the other damage types can also possess attacks that deal Slash Damage within their movesets. Slash damage is effective against unarmored or lightly armored enemies as well as fleshy creatures.\n\nSlash Damage and Players\nThis section lists information pertaining to Slash Damage and its QUESTION
How can players improve their resistance to Slash Damage in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can improve their resistance to Slash Damage in Elden Ring by equipping specific armor pieces like Lionel's Helm, using skills such as Golden Vow, and consuming items like Boiled Crab and Aromatic Ironjar.
