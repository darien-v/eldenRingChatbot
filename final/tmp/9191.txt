\n\n Revenant \n\nLocation\nLaskyar RuinsSainted Hero's GraveThe Shaded CastleSellia HideawayLunar Estate RuinsSubterranean Shunning-GroundsElphael, Brace of the Haligtree\n\nDrops\n 1368 - 12121 RunesSacrificial TwigGhost Gloveworts\n\nRevenant is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. The Revenant is a highly aggressive multi-legged creature that can travel between dimensional portals to quickly close the distance with its prey. They can frequently be found stalking ruins in the Liurnia region and are often accompanied by the Wraith Callers that worship them.\nThe Royal Revenant, a boss version of the Revenant can be found in the Kingsrealm Ruins\n\nA grotesque amalgamation of limbs, the Revenant is a creature with the body structure of an arachnid and the face of a human. Much like a spider, it possesses many limbs that seem to have been grafted onto its body, taken from diseased humans. The Revenant is capable of traveling through portals it creates with a peculiar form of magic, swiftly emerging behind a foe to catch them off guard.\n\nRevenant Locations in Elden Ring\nRevenants can be found in the following locations:\n\nLaskyar Ruins\nLunar Estate Ruins\nSellia Hideaway\nThe Shaded Castle\nSainted Hero's Grave\nSubterranean Shunning-Grounds\nElphael, Brace of the Haligtree\n\nElden Ring Revenant Drops\n\n Item \n Drop Rate \n Additional Info \n\nRunes\n100%\n 1368 - 12121\n\nSacrificial Twig\n3.00%\nN/A\n\nGhost Glovewort (2-4-6-9)\n100%\nN/A\n\nRevenant Notes & Tips\n\nThe area of effect from Heal, Great Heal, Lord's Heal, and Erdtree Heal damages Revenants for 50% of their max HP + 300 HP. The area of effect of these healing incantations also deal significant Poise damage. QUESTION
Why are Revenants the only enemies in the game that are damaged by healing incantations?

ANSWER
Revenants are the only enemies in the game that are damaged by healing incantations due to the unique mechanics and lore surrounding them.
