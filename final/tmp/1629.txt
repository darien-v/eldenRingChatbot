The Guardian Set is the armor worn by the Guardians found around various Minor Erdtrees. Sets of Armor in Elden Ring are composed of four armor pieces which are Helms, Chest Armor, Gauntlets, and Leg Armor.\n\nWhere to find Guardian Set in Elden Ring\n\nGuardian Set pieces can be acquired as drops from Erdtree Guardians that are found near Minor Erdtrees in the Lands Between.\nAn area dense with Guardians can be found by starting at the Dragonbarrow Fork site of grace, and working your way uphill towards the Minor Erdtree. Guardians are encountered in small groups and more come up from the ground, making it a good place to farm the set.\nChance it drops from one of the multiple Erdtree Guardians at the minor tree in Weeping Peninsula.\nAn alternative version of the Guardian Garb, the Guardian Garb (Full Bloom) can be obtained from Guardians that sprout blooming flowers. Such as the ones outside of the Erdtree Sanctuary. This version of the set provides better defense and raises the HP recovery effects of the Flask of Crimson Tears at the cost of greatly lowering fire damage negation.\n\nGuardian Set Armor Pieces in Elden Ring\n\nGuardian Mask\nGuardian Garb\nGuardian Bracers\nGuardian Greaves\nGuardian Garb (Full Bloom)\n\nGuardian Set Elden Ring Notes and Tips\n\nPoise updated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can players acquire the Guardian Set in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Guardian Set in Elden Ring can be acquired as drops from Erdtree Guardians found near Minor Erdtrees, particularly in areas dense with Guardians such as at the Weeping Peninsula and the Erdtree Sanctuary.
