It is a good Weapon for those who prefer ranged combat. \n\nLarge crossbow made from composite materials. One of the largest of all bolt firing devices.\nIts limbs are more pliable than metallic ones, allowing bolts fired from this weapon to pierce even forged of steel.\n\nWhere to Find Arbalest in Elden Ring\nThe Arbalest weapon can be found at the following location:\n\n In Stormveil Castle, On a rampart past the courtyard filled with Exile Soldiers.
