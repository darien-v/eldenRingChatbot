The Crystal Staff scales primarily with Strength and Intelligence and is a good Weapon for players that use Crystal Magic.  \n\nStaff fashioned from pure crystal; a deed impossible for a human. Enhances Crystalian sorceris.\nThe Crystalian's faint cognition is know as the "wisdom of stone." This staff can only be weilded by those of intellect high enough to grasp such wisdom. \n\nWhere to Find Crystal Staff in Elden Ring\nThe Crystal Staff weapon can be found at the following location:\n\nAcademy Crystal Cave. The Staff is behind an illusory wall in the room right before the boss gate. The wall is to the left of the stone archway that leads to a side-room nearby. Screenshot here. QUESTION
Why does the Crystal Staff scale primarily with Strength and Intelligence?

ANSWER
The Crystal Staff scales primarily with Strength and Intelligence in order to cater to players who use Crystal Magic.
