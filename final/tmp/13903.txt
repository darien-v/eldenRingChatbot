Redmane Surcoat is part of the Radahn Soldier Set Armor Set. It is the rust-worn armor of the soldiers loyal to General Radahn. Redmane Surcoat is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped. Some armor pieces may be available to both genders but may be slightly different for male and female characters.\n\nArmor worn by soldiers who fought by General Radahn's side.\nThe surcoat depicts a red-maned lion raising a sword in the image of Radahn. Thus, they were redmanes, and all proved they were worthy of the name.\n\nWhere to find Redmane Surcoat in Elden Ring\nThere is a chance that Radahn Soldier Surcoat drops from Radahn Soldiers when defeated.
