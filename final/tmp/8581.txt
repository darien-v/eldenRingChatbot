QUESTION
Why is the Godskin Noble Robe described as having an unusually rotund appearance despite being a light armor in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Godskin Noble Robe is described as unusually rotund due to its sewn smooth skin patches with subcutaneous fat, exuding an intimidating mass despite its light build.
