When provoked, the snails will peek out of their shells while being able to cast a crystal breath cone attack at a medium range or shoot a small glintstone-like projectile at longer ranges. They can also bite or jump surprisingly quickly at the player.\n\nSnails with crystals growing from their shell. QUESTION
What attacks does the Crystal Snail have when provoked in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Crystal Snail can cast a crystal breath cone attack at medium range, shoot a small glintstone-like projectile at longer ranges, bite or jump surprisingly quickly at the player, two Frostbite build-up breath attacks, one similar to Hoarfrost Stomp and a quick frost ball, a green Poison build-up spit attack and is found in Elden Ring.
