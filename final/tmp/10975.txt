\n\n Briars of Sin \n\nSpell Type\nAberrant Sorceries\n\nFP Cost 6\nSlots Used 1\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons thorns from a whorl of your own blood. \n       Requires \n\nIntelligence 0 \n\nFaith 24 \n\nArcane 0 \n\nBriars of Sin is a Sorcery in Elden Ring. Briars of Sin spell is cast to produce thorns of blood that surround the player in a medium AoE. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nAn aberrant sorcery discovered by exiled criminals.Theirs are the sorceries most reviled by the academy.\nWounds the caster with thorns of sin, creating a spiral of bloodthorns.This sorcery can be cast repeatedly, up to three times.\n\nBriars of Sin Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Briars of Sin:\n\nLiurnia of the Lakes: Dropped by a Thorn Sorcerer directly north-west from the Artist's Shack grace. Cross the natural stone bridge, there will be by a group of Burning Slugs close to the sorcerer. [Map Link]. If you see only 2 slugs and no sorcerer, just head west to the other side of the large rocks. The sorcerer is located just south of the encampment.\n\nElden Ring Briars of Sin Guide\n\nAberrant Sorcery\nStamina Cost: 26\nDeals (Sorcery Scaling x 2.00) magic damage on the first cast, followed by (Sorcery Scaling x 2.32) and (Sorcery Scaling x 2.33)  for the second and third cast respectively\nLow Cost Sorcery (6 Focus Points per cast)\nUses only 1 Memory Slot\nRequires Faith (24)\nDeals Magic Damage\nCosts 80 HP to cast\nBuilds up the Hemorrhage effect (95) on hit. This buildup is affected by the Arcane stat, if the staff has Arcane scaling (tested with Albinauric Staff) Using the Albinauric staff with Arcane investment increases the bleed build up of the thorn sorceries, but only by a tiny amount. \nModerate cast speed\nCan be chain-casted as long as player has enough Health and FP\nThorns are created in a medium AoE around the player\nNote: As of Patch 1.07, FP cost was reduced from 9 to 6, and the spell is also faster to cast while also now inflicting more bleed buildup: from 90 to 95.\n\nSanguine Spellblade Build Guide\n\nElden Ring Briars of Sin Note and Tips\n\nUpdated to patch 1.07. QUESTION
How can players effectively utilize the Briars of Sin spell in Elden Ring combat situations?

ANSWER
To effectively utilize the Briars of Sin spell in combat situations in Elden Ring, players should focus on chaining its casts for increased bleed buildup, ensuring they have sufficient Health and FP, and taking advantage of its medium AoE effect around the player.
