\n\n Forlorn Hound Evergaol \n\nThe southern gaol of Limgrave holds a lone knight, who had abandoned his master, now forced to rot inside his cell.\n\nForlorn Hound Evergaol is a Location in Elden Ring. The Forlorn Hound Evergaol is found in Limgrave. It can be found at the very south of Main Limgrave\n\nForlorn Hound Evergaol Map\n[Elden Ring Map here]\n\nAll NPCs in Forlorn Hound Evergaol\n\nBlaidd - Requests an end to Darriwil's tale as part of his questline. Therefore he will be available as a summon when facing Darriwil in this location. He can only be summoned once the player is inside the Evergaol itself. He will appear as a golden summon sign.\n\nAll Items in Elden Ring's Forlorn Hound Evergaol\n\nGather & Farm Items\n\nThere are no Materials here\n\nUpgrade Materials\n\nThere are no upgrade materials here\n\nEquipment and Magic\n\nBloodhound's Fang\n\nUnique, Ashes and Keys\n\nThere are no Keys here\n\nElden Ring Forlorn Hound Evergaol Creatures, Enemies, and Bosses\n\nRegular Creatures and Enemies\n\nAbnormal Stone Cluster\n\nField Bosses and Bosses\n\nBloodhound Knight Darriwil\n\nForlorn Hound Evergaol Walkthrough\nYou can summon the NPC Blaidd to help you in this fight. Go to Mistwood Ruins and hear his howl, then travel back to Church of Elleh and speak to the merchant there ''About the howling in the woods'' He will give you a gesture to be used under the tower so that Blaidd jumps down. You can then speak to him and summon him in the fight. His summoning sign will appear after you have entered the Evergaol.\nUpon entry of the Evergaol, you will face the Bloodhound Knight Darriwil. For combat notes and tips visit his page. Defeat him to obtain Bloodhound's Fang.\nAfter entering into Ranni's service, Blaidd will become trapped in this location if it is still uncleared. You'll be able to hear howling in the area. He will start speaking with you when you reach the center of the evergaol. You will then be prompted to open the evergaol. \n\nElden Ring Forlorn Hound Evergaol Gallery and Notes\n[other images go here]\n\nAll Elden Ring Locations\n\nAbandoned Cave  ♦  Academy Crystal
