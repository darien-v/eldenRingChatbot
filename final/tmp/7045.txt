CONTEXT
\nUpgrade Materials, or Bolster Materials, in Elden Ring are responsible for strengthening Weapons, Shields, and Sacred Flasks with crucial Upgrades. This page covers their names and effects. An exhaustive list of Locations for each can be found on their respective pages.\nWhile all Upgrade Materials can be scavenged while exploring the Lands Between, certain Bell Bearings allow players to purchase an unlimited quantity of materials for weapon, shield, and spirit ash upgrades. QUESTION
How can players obtain an unlimited quantity of upgrade materials for weapons, shields, and sacred flasks in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Players can obtain an unlimited quantity of upgrade materials for weapons, shields, and sacred flasks in Elden Ring by using specific Bell Bearings to purchase them.
