Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman depicting a dagger and a surgeon.Enhances critical hits.\nThe white-garbed field surgeons come to the aid of friend and foe alike by dealing a final deadly thrust to spare them from the prolonged agony of a mortal wound. A sense of mercy is a catalyst for bloodlust.\n\nDagger Talisman Effect in Elden Ring\nDagger Talisman increases Critical Damage by 17%.\n\nWhere to find Dagger Talisman in Elden Ring\nStart from the waygate that teleports you to the Audience Pathway before Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy. Head back and head upstairs. Use two Stonesword Keys and pass thorough the fog gate. After entering the hanging cage room, turn right and drop down. Follow the path and you will find the Dagger Talisman on a corpse. QUESTION
Where can I find the Dagger Talisman in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Start from the waygate that teleports you to the Audience Pathway before Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy. Head back and head upstairs. Use two Stonesword Keys, pass through the fog gate. After entering the hanging cage room, turn right and drop down. Follow the path and you will find the Dagger Talisman on a corpse.
