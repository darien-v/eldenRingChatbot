\n\n Ghost Glovewort [6] \n\nEffect \n\n        Strengthen renowned ashes to +6 \n\nGhost Glovewort [6] is an Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ghost Glovewort [6] can be used to upgrade Elite Spirits Ashes. Upgrade Materials can be acquired through exploration, looting it from specified areas of a Location, dropped by a specific Enemy or Boss, given by an NPC, or sold by a Merchant.\n\nWhite flower that blooms in catacombs.A spirit nestles close to it.\nStrengthens renowned ashes to +6.\nAshes which keep their renown even after death cannot be strengthened by merely corporeal glovewort.\n\nGhost Glovewort [6] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ghost Glovewort [6]:\n\nCan be purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold after giving them the Ghost-Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing (2).\nWyndham Catacombs: Found in the sewer area amongst the Putrid Corpses. [Map Link]\nAuriza Hero's Grave: In the first Chariot Hall, drop down the openings on either side just before the second ramp. In the room below, head west and up the steps and then turn right into the next corridor. Follow the path to a second Chariot Hall where two Chariots are running side by side with a short delay between them. As you exit into this hall, turn left to find two ramps. In between them in the back is a small opening where the glovewort can be found.
