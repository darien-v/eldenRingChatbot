Ancient Dragons' Lightning Spear spell summons a red lightning spear to stab enemies from above. Updated to Patch 1.07.\n\nA secret incantation of the capital's ancient dragon cult.\nCreates a spear of red lightning and stabs it into the ground fromabove. On impact, the spear will burst into trails of lightningcovering the area.\nSpoken of in legend, red lightning is the weapon wielded by theancient dragons.\n\nWhere to find Ancient Dragons' Lightning Spear\nWhere to find Ancient Dragons' Lightning Spear:\n\nPurchase from Brother Corhyn or Miriel, Pastor of Vows after giving the Ancient Dragon Prayerbook\n\nElden Ring Ancient Dragons' Lightning Spear Guide\n\nStamina Cost: 31\nOne of the Dragon Cult Incantations and is boosted by the Gravel Stone Seal  \nDeals lightning damage and 22 stance damage\nReceived a poise boost, which gives it hyper armor during the cast. \nThis spell is a close ranged AOE, with many lightning waves spreading out, dealing damage.\nA single hit of this spell will out perform a single hit of Fortissax's Lightning Spear, but the latter spell hits two total times, and has a much higher Damage/FP cost ratio in total.  \nNote: In Patch 1.07, FP cost was reduced from 30 to 25 and the amount of stamina
