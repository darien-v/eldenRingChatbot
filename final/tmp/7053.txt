\n\n Juno Hoslow, Knight of Blood \n\nLocation\nMountaintops of the Giants\n\nDrops\n158  RunesRune ArcFurlcalling Finger RemedyHoslow's Petal WhipHoslow's HelmHoslow's ArmorHoslow's GauntletsHoslow's Greaves\n\nJuno Hoslow, Knight of Blood is a Humanoid Enemy in Elden Ring. Juno Hoslow is a Hostile NPC that you can invade as part of the final contract of the Volcano Manor questline. He is the older brother of Diallos and a knight decked in black and silver armor, equipped with dual whips. Other Invadable NPCs can be found here.\n\nJuno Hoslow had a younger brother who was all talk and no trousers. His inability to commit to action was such that Juno inherited the Hoslow legacy without resistance, granting him the freedom to shower his little brother with adoration.\n\nElden Ring Juno Hoslow, Knight of Blood Locations\nWhere to Find Juno Hoslow, Knight of Blood:\n\nMountaintops of the Giants: East of Shack of the Lofty. Touch the red Summon Sign on the ground to invade his world. Only available after progressing the Volcano Manor questline. [Elden Ring Map here]\n\nElden Ring Juno Hoslow, Knight of Blood Drops\n\n158  Runes\nRune Arc\nFurlcalling Finger Remedy\nHoslow's Petal Whip\nHoslow's Helm\nHoslow's Armor\nHoslow's Gauntlets\nHoslow's Greaves\n\nPerforms the Hoslow's Oath gesture, giving it to the player once completed.\n\nJuno Hoslow, Knight of Blood Notes & Tips\n\nCan heal only once.\nUses dual Hoslow's Petal Whip, dealing high Strike Damage and inflicting Blood Loss build-up. He also uses Bloodhound's Step to weave in and out of attacks.\nAssuming correct timing, all of Juno's right hand whip attacks - including his double whip attack - can be dodged by rolling towards him. Ensure the Tarnished remains just about striking distance away from Juno to bait out an attack.\nIf rolling towards Juno and immediately attacking, he will either take the hit or use Bloodhound's Step to dodge away. It is best to simply commit to one attack lest he immediately counters with a whip attack of his own.\nIf Juno uses Bloodhound's Step to dodge away from the follow up attack, he himself will either follow up with another attack or do nothing. However, Juno's idle is often short enough for him to use another attack. Thus, it is simply best to bait out another attack while he is idle.\nWhile it is possible to dodge Juno's left hand whip attack, this is almost impossible as the whip hitbox is confusingly large. It is simply best to back away from Juno if he uses his left hand whip attacks.\nLike any human encounter, status effects are very effective.\nIt is possible to use Inescapable Frenzy to grab him while is busy performing his gesture. QUESTION
Why is Juno Hoslow, Knight of Blood invadable only after progressing the Volcano Manor questline in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Juno Hoslow, Knight of Blood is invadable only after progressing the Volcano Manor questline in Elden Ring because his world can be accessed by touching the red Summon Sign in the Mountaintops of the Giants, east of Shack of the Lofty, as part of the final contract of the questline.
