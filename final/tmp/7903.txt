\n\n Skeletal Bandit Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 22\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons skeletal bandit spirit \n\nSkeletal Bandit Ashes  is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Skeletal Bandit Ashes allows players to summon the aid of skeletal bandit spirits.\nSkeletal Bandits can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Skeletal Bandit.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirits of a skeletal bandit.\nThese are the spirits of bandits who live in Death, and will continue to rise again until properly finished off.After the night of the plot, Deathroot appeared in the Lands Between, and Those Who Live in Death soon followed.\n\nWhere to find Skeletal Bandit Ashes\nWhere to Find Skeletal Bandit Ashes \n\nObtained when defeating the Tibia Mariner Field Boss in Liurnia of the Lakes.
