QUESTION
Why is the Eccentric Set in Elden Ring inspired directly from the armor once worn by German Landsknecht, elite mercenaries under the Holy Roman Empire?

ANSWER
The Eccentric Set in Elden Ring is inspired by the armor of German Landsknecht elite mercenaries under the Holy Roman Empire, reflecting a historical connection to the real-life profession and lifestyle of the Landsknechts.
