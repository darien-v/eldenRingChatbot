\n\n Ash of War: Phantom Slash \n\nAffinity\nQuality\n\nSkill\nPhantom Slash\n\nEffect\nGrants affinities and skills to an armament.\n\nAsh of War: Phantom Slash is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Phantom Slash provides Quality affinity and the Phantom Slash Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Quality affinity and the following skill:\n"Phantom Slash: Skill inspired by the fond remembrances of the Night's Cavalry. Creates an apparition of the knights' former instructor who guides a joint lunging upward swing. Additional input allows for a follow-up attack."\nUsable on polearms and twinblades (great spears excepted).\n\nAsh of War: Phantom Slash Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ash of War: Phantom Slash:\n\nDropped by a Night's Cavalry northeast the Forbidden Lands Grace in the Forbidden Lands. Elden Ring Map Link.\n\nAsh of War: Phantom Slash Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Phantom Slash.\nCan be used with any affinity Upgrade.\nCan be applied to the following weapon categories, so long as the weapon allows skill replacement: Twinblades, Spears, Halberds, Reapers\n\nPhantom Slash Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ash of War: Phantom Slash Notes & Tips\n\nLocking on and sprinting sideways or backwards before using the skill will send the phantom towards the target while moving you to the side or back. Useful for hitting enemies during lengthy combos/abilities (e.g. Rivers of Blood's Corpse Piler, Waterfowl Dance, Bloodblade Dance, etc.)\n\nSprinting sideways (left/right) will send the phantom towards the target, and send you left or right.\nSprinting backwards will send the phantom towards the target, and send you back.\nSprinting diagonal forward (left/right + forward) will send the phantom to the target, and send you right/left of the phantom.\nSprinting diagonal backward (left/right + back) will send the phantom to the target, and send you backwards to the left/right.\n\nElden
