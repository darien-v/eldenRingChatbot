\n\n Lone Wolf Ashes \n\nType\nSpirit Summon\n\nFP Cost 55\nHP Cost -\n\nEffect \n\n        Summons three lone wolf spirits. \n\nLone Wolf Ashes is a Spirit and Summon in Elden Ring. Lone Wolf Ashes summons a pack of three wolves with independent life bars that can move and attack enemies at melee range.\nDespite being called Lone Wolf, these ashes spawn 3 wolves that work as a team. Wolves have decent amount of HP, they move quickly enough and they inflict a decent amount of damage. Wolves are better as DPS rather than Tank companions. Wolves are not very useful against Bosses whose attacks have a great AoE. Wolves may not resist many attacks, but they move a lot around enemies, and they dodge a lot, attacking as a team.\nLone Wolves can also be found as Enemies in Elden Ring. For more information, see Lone Wolf.\n\nAshen remains in which spirits yet dwell.Use to summon the spirits of three lone wolves.\nSpirits of wolves chased from their pack. They later encountered a nameless Tarnished, who welcomed them as hunting companions. The wolf spirits overwhelm enemies with their agility, aiding the summoner in combat.\n\nLone Wolf Ashes Location in Elden Ring\nThe Lone Wolf Ashes can be found at the following location:\n\nGiven to players by Renna at the Church of Elleh in Limgrave. Return to the Church after getting the ability to summon your Spectral Steed Whistle from Melina. You will find Renna sitting by the ruins of the church. QUESTION
What strategies can be utilized to effectively use Lone Wolf Ashes in combat in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Effective use of Lone Wolf Ashes in combat in Elden Ring involves keeping enemies engaged, utilizing their high damage, quick movement, and teamwork, while ensuring they are not overwhelmed due to their low health.
