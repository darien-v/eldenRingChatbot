(Except for disappearing)\nThis NPC cannot be talked to and will disappear when the player gets too close. After he disappears, he will leave an item. \nThis spirit can't be fought.\n\nElden Ring Wandering Artist Spirit Locations\nWhere to Find the Wandering Artist Spirit:Note that he will not appear at these locations unless you have picked up the painting beforehand. QUESTION
What items do you receive as drops from the various paintings when interacting with the Wandering Artist Spirit in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The drops from the paintings when interacting with the Wandering Artist Spirit are: Incantation Scarab, Warhawk Ashes, Juvenile Scholar Cap, Juvenile Scholar Robe, Larval Tear, Ash of War: Rain of Arrows, Harp Bow, Fire's Deadly Sin, Greathood.
