\n\n Laskyar Ruins \n\nRuins of a sunken structure beset by fog, found amidst the marshes of south Liurnia.\n\nLaskyar Ruins is a Location in Elden Ring. The Laskyar Ruins is found in Liurnia of the Lakes, in the southern portion of the shallows. This location is easily accessible on foot or on horseback.\n\nLaskyar Ruins Map\nThe Laskyar Ruins are situated on the southern tip of the lake. It is sitting in front of a set of pillars.\nGo to the northwestern section of the ruins to find a set of stairs leading to a dark underground hall. There is a room at the end of this hall with a chest containing the Wraith Calling Bell.\nThere is a gazebo in the center of the ruins with a Waygate that transports users to the entrance of the Raya Lucaria Academy, you will need a Glintstone Key to proceed through the gates. There is a Smithing Stone [3] on the corpse outside the gazebo. Map Link.\nGlintstone Craftsman's Cookbook [2] can be looted from a corpse amongst the ruins in the southern part of this location.
