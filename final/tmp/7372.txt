Click here for the sorcery Thops's Barrier (Spell)\nAsh of War: Thops's Barrier is an Ash of War and Upgrade Material in Elden Ring. Ash of War: Thops's Barrier provides Magic affinity and the Thops's Barrier Skill. Ashes of War can be equipped on Weapons and Shields to modify the Skill or an equipment, or to apply affinities that modify scaling values.\n\nThis Ash of War grants an armament the Magic affinity and the following skill:\n"Thops's Barrier: Erect a magical forcefield while swinging the shield to deflect sorceries and incantations. Can also be used in the same way as a regular parry."\nUsable on small and medium shields.\n\nAsh of War: Thops's Barrier Location in Elden Ring\n\nIn Church of Irith: Dropped by a Teardrop Scarab after delivering the spare Academy Glintstone Key to Thops. Reload the area after delivering the key to spawn the scarab. [Map Link]\n\nAsh of War: Thops's Barrier Elden Ring Guide\n\nGrants the skill Thops's Barrier.\nProvides the Magic affinity, which adds Magic damage and Intelligence scaling, reduces Base damage and Strength / Dexterity scaling\nCan be used with the following affinity Upgrades: Standard, Heavy, Keen, Quality, Magic, Cold\nCan be applied to Small Shields and Medium Shields.\nThops's Barrier has 11 start-up frames, 9 active frames, and 31 recovery frames (at 60 FPS).\nSee Parrying for detailed information on the mechanic and a comparison of different parry skills.\nFormerly dropped by a teleporting scarab on a small island in Liurnia, it has since been patched to coincide with Thops’s questline.\n\nThops's Barrier Demonstration\n\nElden Ring Ashes of War\n\nAsh of War: Assassin's QUESTION
How can I obtain Ash of War: Thop's Barrier in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
