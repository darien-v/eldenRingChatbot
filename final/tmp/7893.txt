QUESTION
How can players optimize the Warrior class in Elden Ring in terms of build and playstyle?

ANSWER
Players can optimize the Warrior class in Elden Ring by focusing on dexterity, utilizing two Scimitars for dual attacks, and considering unique abilities like glintstone pebble or bestial constitution for build and playstyle variations.
