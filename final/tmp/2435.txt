\n\n Silver Scarab \n\nWeight\n1.2\n\nEffect \n\n Raises  \n       Item Discovery by 75 \n\nSilver Scarab is a Talisman in Elden Ring. Silver Scarab raises Item Discovery by 75. Players can use talismans in Elden Ring to boost a variety of Stats.\n\nA talisman facsimile of a scarab, the carrier of treasures and precious things.\nThis silver scarab enhances item discovery.\n\nSilver Scarab Effect in Elden Ring\nSilver Scarab raises Item Discovery by 75.\n\nWhere to find Silver Scarab in Elden Ring\n\nFound in a chest in Hidden Path to the Haligtree. In the large central room, there is a broken section of railing. There, you can drop down to an invisible platform, and walk to a small room. The chest is past an Illusory Wall, behind the Grave Glovewort (9). [Map link]\nTip for finding the way on the invisible bridge: Hoarfrost Stomp will easily reveal the invisible walkway. Alternatively, use glowing stones, throwing knives, or arrows (fire or shining work the best).\n\nElden Ring Silver Scarab Notes & Tips\n\nSell value:  500\n\nElden Ring Talismans\n\nAncestral
