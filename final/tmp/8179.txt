Those who study the Twinsage Conspectus are the academy's elite, capable of mastering the Glintstone Cometshard and Crystal Burst sorcereries.\n\nElden Ring Twinsage Sorcerer Locations\nTwinsage Sorcerers can be found all around the Raya Lucaria Academy and surrounding areas.
