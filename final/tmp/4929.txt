\n\n Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing [4] \n\nType\nKey Item\n\nEffect \n\n        Offer to Twin Maiden Husks for new item access \n\nSmithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing [4] is a Key Item in Elden Ring. Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing [4] can be offered to the Two Maiden Husks for new item access. Key Items in Elden Ring include a wide variety of items found in specific locations or are given by a related NPCs which are used to unlock areas, quests, and to further progress the game's story.\n\nThe bell bearing of a miner who once dug for smithing stone. \nOffer to the Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold to gain access to the following items: \n- Smithing Stone [7]- Smithing Stone [8]- Ruin Fragment\n\nSmithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing [4] Location in Elden Ring\nThe Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing [4] can be found in the following location:\n\nCrumbling Farum Azula: Dropped by the Godskin Duo inside the Dragon Temple area upon defeat.
