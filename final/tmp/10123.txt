Cookbooks are records of crafting techniques that players can use to learn how to craft a wide variety of items.\n\nA record of crafting techniques taught by the capital's ancient dragon cult.Details techniques that harness lightning, the weapon of the ancient dragons.\nAcquire the knowledge to craft the following:\n- Ancient Dragonbolt Pot- Lightningproof Dried Liver\n\nAncient Dragon Apostle's Cookbook [4] Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to find Ancient Dragon Apostle's Cookbook [4]\n\nCan be acquired at Crumbling Farum Azula. From the Dragon Temple Lift site of Grace, do not take the lift. Instead head out into the undead courtyard and keep right. There is a series of collapsed archways and crumbled tower pieces stretching to the west to traverse. Platform over the tower pieces, and then pick up this book inside an enclosed area at the end. Two undead beastmen will attack you when you pick up the cookbook. QUESTION
Why is the Ancient Dragon Apostle's Cookbook [4] required for crafting the Ancient Dragonbolt Pot and Lightningproof Dried Liver in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
The Ancient Dragon Apostle's Cookbook [4] is required for crafting the Ancient Dragonbolt Pot and Lightningproof Dried Liver in Elden Ring because it provides the crafting techniques needed to harness lightning, the weapon of the ancient dragons.
