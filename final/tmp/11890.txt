They are often found in long-abandoned villages and around large bonfires where there are high concentrations of the influence of the maddening Three Fingers.\n\nFrenzied Villager Locations in Elden Ring\nFrenzied Villagers can be found in the following location(s):\n\nAiling Village at the Weeping Peninsula.\nFrenzied Flame Village in Liurnia.\nFrenzy-Flaming Tower in Liurnia.\nYelough Anix Ruins in Consecrated Snowfield.\n\nElden Ring Frenzied Villager Drops\n\n 48 - 1020 Runes\nWeathered Straight Sword\nGlass Shard\nYellow Ember\nEye of Yelough\n\nFrenzied Villager Notes & Tips\n\nThey are frenzied Commoners.\nLike other types of Commoners, some carry a Torchpole or a Spear, but they do not drop them. These two weapons can only be dropped by the Exile Soldier that wield them.\n\n Frenzied Villager Image Gallery\n\nElden Ring Creatures & Enemies\n\nAbductor Virgin  ♦  Abnormal Stone
