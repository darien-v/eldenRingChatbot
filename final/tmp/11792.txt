Guillemots are not hostile and will flee on approach.\n\nGuillemot Location in Elden Ring\nWhere to Find Guillemot:\n\nPlayers can encounter Guillemots roaming the beaches of Western Limgrave.\nThere are several on the west beach of the Weeping Peninsula.\nThere are eight located east and northeast of the Church of Dragon Communion grace.  \n\nThis is the best farming spot, but you have to use a Bow. Crossbows and Spells scare away the surrounding Guillemots.\n\nThere are at least three located northeast of the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid, near a hooded Banished Knight.\n\nElden QUESTION
Why are Guillemots considered a better option for farming Four-Toed Fowl Feet compared to Eagles in Elden Ring?

ANSWER
Guillemots are a better option for farming Four-Toed Fowl Feet than Eagles due to dropping them far more often (25% vs. 8%).
